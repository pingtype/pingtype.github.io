1 Â-lùn Hien-chi To thi-pat ngit, Mô-sî seu-si̍p Â-lùn lâu kì ke-têu lai-é, pin Yî-set-lie̍t ke-têu chóng-ló lòi.2 Kì lâu Â-lùn kóng: "Ngì oi tho̍k yit-chak mò khiet-tiám ke se ngiù-kú lòi-cho su̍k-chhui-chi, yit-chak mò khiet-tiám ke mièn-yòng-kú cho sêu-fa-chi hien-pûn Song-chú.3 Yèn-heu, ngì oi tui Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn kóng: ‘Ngì-têu oi tho̍k yit-chak sân-yòng-kú lòi-cho su̍k-chhui-chi, yu tho̍k yit-se thai, mò khiet-tiám ke se ngiù-kú lâu se mièn-yòng-kú kok yit-chak cho sêu-fa-chi,4 yu tho̍k yit-chak ngiù-kú lâu yit-chak mièn-yòng-kú cho phìn-ôn-chi. Ngì-têu oi chiông liá-têu lâu kiâu kám-lám-yù ke su-chi chhiòn-phu hien-pûn Song-chú, yîn-vi kîm-pû-ngit Song-chú oi hiong ngì-têu hién-hien.’ "5 Só-yî, kì-têu chiông Mô-sî fûn-fu kì-têu ke chhiòn-phu tai-to sṳn-chong-phùng chhièn; chhiòn-thí ngìn-mìn chhi-si̍p chhai ke-vi, khî chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn.6 Mô-sî kóng: "Song-chú min-lin ngì-têu cho liá só-yû ke sṳ, he oi hiong ngì-têu hién-hien Kì ke yùng-kông."7 Chiap-tén, Mô-sî tui Â-lùn kóng: "Ngì oi to chi-thàn chhièn hien ngì ke su̍k-chhui-chi lâu sêu-fa-chi, vi ngì chhṳ-kâ lâu ngìn-mìn su̍k-chhui; ngì yu oi hien ngìn-mìn ke chi, vi kì-têu su̍k-chhui, chhiòn-phu cheu Song-chú só min-lin ke."8 Só-yî, Â-lùn hàng-to chi-thàn chhièn, chhṳ̀-sí se ngiù-kú cho kì chhṳ-kâ ke su̍k-chhui-chi.9 Kì ke-têu lai-é chiông hiet tai-to kì mien-chhièn, kì chhiu yung sú-chṳ́ vun-hiet, chiông hiet kò chhai chi-thàn ke si-kok táng, chhûn-tó ke hiet chhiòn-phu tó chhai chi-thàn ke kiok ke-vi.10 Chiap-tén, kì cheu Song-chú thûng-ko Mô-sî siên-pu ke min-lin chiông su̍k-chhui-chi ke yù, lióng-chak yêu-é, lâu kôn ke mióng-yù chhiòn-phu piong chhai chi-thàn táng sêu-hien.11 Kì yu chiông ngiù ke ngiuk lâu phì tai-to yàng-ngoi sêu-thet.12 Â-lùn ya chhṳ̀-sí vi chhṳ-kâ hien sêu-fa-chi ke thèu-sâng. Kì ke-têu lai-é chiông hiet tai-to kì mien-chhièn, kì chhiu chiông hiet hia chhai chi-thàn si chû-vì.13 Yèn-heu, kì-têu chiông sêu-fa-chi ke ngiuk yit-te yit-te, lièn thèu kâu-pûn kì, kì chhiu chiông liá-têu piong chhai chi-thàn táng sêu.14 Kì yu sé-chhiang puk-nui lâu heu-thúi, pài chhai sêu-fa-chi táng sêu-hien.15 Chiap hâ-hi, Â-lùn vi ngìn-mìn hien-chi. Kì chiông vi ngìn-mìn su̍k-chhui ke sân-yòng-kú khiên ko-lòi, chhṳ̀-sí, yèn-heu cheu kì vi chhṳ-kâ hien su̍k-chhui-chi ke fông-fap hien ke-chak sân-yòng-kú.16 Kì yu khiên sêu-fa-chi ke thèu-sâng ko-lòi, cheu thiàu-li chiông kì fung-hien.17 Kì ya hien su-chi, tui chûng-kiên yá yit-kha̍k piong chhai chi-thàn táng sêu. (Chhù-liáu tak chêu-sṳ̀n ke sêu-fa-chi yî-ngoi, chai hien liá-chi.)18 Kì chhṳ̀-sí ngiù-kú lâu mièn-yòng-kú, vi ngìn-mìn hien phìn-ôn-chi. Â-lùn ke-têu lai-é chiông hiet tai-to kì mien-chhièn, kì chhiu chiông hiet hia chhai chi-thàn si chû-vì.19 Kì-têu chiông ngiù-kú lâu mièn-yòng-kú ke yù, pâu-hàm phì ke mî, pâu-tén puk-nui ke yù, lióng-chak yêu-é, lâu kôn ke mióng-yù,20 chhiòn-phu piong chhai sên-ngì ke hiûng-phù táng, tai-to kì mien-chhièn. Â-lùn chhiu chiông liá-têu yù sêu chhai chi-thàn táng,21 yu chûn-cheu Mô-sî ke min-lin chiông hiûng-phù-ngiuk lâu yu heu-thúi yèu kí-ha cho yèu-chi hien-pûn Song-chú.22 Â-lùn hien su̍k-chhui-chi, sêu-fa-chi, lâu phìn-ôn-chi yî-heu, chhiu khià-sú chuk-fuk ngìn-mìn, yèn-heu tui chi-thàn táng hâ-lòi.23 Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn ngi̍p-hi sṳn-chong-phùng, chai tui ke-vi chhut-lòi, chuk-fuk ngìn-mìn. Liá-sṳ̀, Song-chú ke yùng-kông hiong chhiòn-thí mìn-chung hién-hien.24 Yû fó tui Song-chú mien-chhièn chhut-lòi, sêu-thet sêu-fa-chi lâu chi-thàn táng ke yù. Ngìn-mìn yit khon-tó chhiu chhiòn-phu fôn-hí to thai-sâng sê, phu̍k chhai thi-song.
