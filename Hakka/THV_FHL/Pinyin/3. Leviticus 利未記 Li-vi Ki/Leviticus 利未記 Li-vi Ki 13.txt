1 Yû-koân Phì-fû-phiang ke Thiàu-li Song-chú hiong Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn siên-pu yî-ha ke thiàu-li.2 Na yû-ngìn ke phì-ngiuk hí-pheu, pot-sién, ya-he fat-yàm, tông-chhiong he pot thái-kô( 9 13:2 "thái-kô" ya-he yi̍t-cho "mà-fûng-phiang" ya-he "phì-fû-phiang". Ngièn-vùn yû kha fat ke yi-sṳ; chhai 13:47 khiung yit-chak sṳ fân-cho "sâng-kû". Li-vi Ki 13–14 chông chhut-hien chhin tô pái.), chhiu-oi tai kì to Â-lùn-chhu̍k ke chi-sṳ̂ ke-vi hi.3 Chi-sṳ̂ oi kiám-chhà kì pot tûng-sî ke vi-só; na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só ke mô pien pha̍k-set, pot tûng-sî ke hien-siong chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, liá chhiu-he thái-kô, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu liá-ke ngìn chhai lî-ngì song mò chhiang-li.4 Na-he liá-ke ngìn pot tûng-sî ke vi-só pien-cho pha̍k-set, chúng-he mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, mô ya mò-pien pha̍k-set, chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.5 Thi-chhit ngit, chi-sṳ̂ oi kâ kiám-chhà yit-pái, na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só mò pien kha ngiàm-chhung, ya-mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chhiu-oi ki-siu̍k chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.6 Thi-chhit ngit, chi-sṳ̂ oi chai kiám-chhà kì; na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só pien-cho mò án-pha̍k, mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chi-sṳ̂ chhiu-oi siên-pu liá-ke ngìn he chhiang-li ke; kì pot ke he sién thin-thin. Liá-ke ngìn oi sé-chhiang kì ke sâm-fu. Án-ngiòng, kì chhai lî-ngì song chhiu chhiang-li lé.7 Chúng-he, chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà kì, siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song chhiang-li heu, na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só yu kín-chhe kín thai-ta̍p, kì chhiu-oi chón-to chi-sṳ̂ ke-vi.8 Chi-sṳ̂ oi chhùng-sîn kiám-chhà kì; na-he fat-hien pot tûng-sî ke vi-só yû-yáng chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chhiu-oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song he mò chhiang-li ke. Liá he thái-kô.9 Na-he yû-ngìn pot thái-kô, oi tai kì to chi-sṳ̂ ke-vi;10 chi-sṳ̂ oi kiám-chhà kì. Na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só chúng hí-lòi, phì-fû pien pha̍k-set, mô ya pien-pha̍k, yu mièn-to khon tet-tó ngiuk,11 liá he man-sin thái-kô ke phiang-chṳn. Chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li, m̀-sṳ́ chiông kì kak-lì, yîn-vi yí-kîn tî kì he mò chhiang-li ke.12 Na-he thái-kô chhe-to chhiòn-sṳ̂n tak-vi he, tui thèu-nà to kiok chhiòn-phu yû,13 chi-sṳ̂ chhiu-oi chai kiám-chhà kì; chhiu-son fat-hien kì chhiòn-sṳ̂n sûi-yèn pot thái-kô, hàn-he oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke; yîn-vi chhiòn-sṳ̂n ke phì-ngiuk pien-pha̍k, kì chhai lî-ngì song ya he chhiang-li ke.14 Chúng-he, yit fat-hien yû fùng-chúng ke ngiuk, kì chhiu-son he mò chhiang-li ke.15 Chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà kì, yit fat-hien yû fùng-chúng ke ngiuk, chhiu-oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li, he thái-kô ke phiang-chṳn.16 Chúng-he fùng-chúng ke vi-só na-he hó chón-lòi, pien-cho pha̍k-set, kì chhiu-oi chón-to chi-sṳ̂ ke-vi;17 chi-sṳ̂ oi chhùng-sîn kiám-chhà kì. Na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só khok-sṳ̍t pien-pha̍k, kì chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke; chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì chhiang-li.18 Na yû-ngìn ke phì-fû pot lia̍p-é, yí-kîn hó lé,19 ngièn-lòi pot lia̍p-é ke vi-só yu chúng hí-lòi pien-cho pha̍k-set, ya-he yû fùng-pha̍k pân-tiám chhut-hien, kì oi-hi pûn chi-sṳ̂ khon;20 chi-sṳ̂ oi kiám-chhà kì. Na-he yû chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk ke chhìn-hìn, mô yu pien pha̍k-set, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li; liá he tui lia̍p-é pien-cho thái-kô ke chṳn-thèu.21 Na-he chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà ke sṳ̀, fat-hien mô mò pien-pha̍k, pot lia̍p-é ke hien-siong ya-mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, set ya mò án-pha̍k, chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.22 Na-he pot lia̍p-é ke vi-só chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li; liá he thái-kô ke chṳn-thèu.23 Na-he pot lia̍p-é ke vi-só mò pien kha ngiàm-chhung, ya-mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, ke chhiu-he lia̍p-é ke pâ thin-thin. Chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke.24 Na yû-ngìn pûn fó sêu-sông, sông-héu pien-pha̍k ya-he yû fùng-pha̍k pân-tiám,25 chi-sṳ̂ chhiu oi kiám-chhà. Na-he sông-héu ke mô pien-pha̍k, sông-héu chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, liá he thái-kô tui fó sêu-sông ke sông-héu pot chhut-lòi ke chṳn-thèu, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li.26 Na-he sông-héu ke mô mò pien-pha̍k, sông-héu mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, set ya mò án-pha̍k, chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.27 Thi-chhit ngit, chi-sṳ̂ oi chai kiám-chhà kì. Na-he sông-héu chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, liá he thái-kô ke chṳn-thèu, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì mò chhiang-li.28 Na-he sông-héu mò pien ngiàm-chhung, ya-mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, set ya mò án-pha̍k, liá chhiu-he yîn-vi fó sêu-sông chúng hí-lòi ke. Chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song chhiang-li, yîn-vi liá he sêu-sông ke pâ thin-thin.29 Mò-lun nàm-ńg, thèu-nà táng ya-he hâ-ngâm na-yû pot tûng-sî,30 chi-sṳ̂ chhiu oi kiám-chhà. Na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, ke vi-só ke thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî pien vòng-set, yu pien kha-séu, liá he thái-kô ke chṳn-thèu, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu liá-ke ngìn mò chhiang-li.31 Chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà ke sṳ̀, na-he fat-hien pot tûng-sî ke hien-siong mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, chúng-he màng sâng vû-set ke thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî, chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.32 Thi-chhit ngit, chi-sṳ̂ oi chai kiám-chhà pot tûng-sî ke hien-siong; na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî mò pien-vòng, pot tûng-sî ke vi-só ya-mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk,33 liá-ke ngìn chhiu-oi chiông pot tûng-sî ke vi-só chû-vì ke thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî thi-thet. Yèn-heu, chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì chai kak-lì chhit-ngit.34 Thi-chhit ngit, chi-sṳ̂ oi chhùng-sîn kiám-chhà pot tûng-sî ke vi-só; na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, ya-mò chhṳ̂m-ngi̍p phì-ngiuk, chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke. Kì oi sé-chhiang sâm-fu, án-ngiòng kì chhiu chhiang-li lé.35 Chúng-he, chhiang-li heu, pot tûng-sî ke vi-só na kín-chhe kín thai-ta̍p,36 chi-sṳ̂ chhiu-oi chai kiám-chhà kì. Na-he pot tûng-sî ke vi-só yû chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chhiu m̀-sṳ́ chai chhìm pien-vòng ke thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî, kì he mò chhiang-li ke.37 Na-he ngin-vì pot tûng-sî ke vi-só mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, vû-set ke thèu-nà-mô ya-he fù-sî ya sâng chhut-lòi, pot tûng-sî ke vi-só chhiu-son hó lé; chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke.38 Mò-lun nàm-ńg, na-he phì-fû yû pha̍k-set ke pân-tiám,39 chi-sṳ̂ chhiu oi kiám-chhà. Na pân-tiám he am fôi-set, liá he phì-fû pot-sién; liá-ke ngìn chhai lî-ngì song he chhiang-li ke.40 Na yû-ngìn lut-thet thèu-nà-mô, mò-lun he thèu-nà ke thèu-chhièn ya-he heu-poi kông-thèu, kì he chhiang-li ke.41 併於上節42 Chúng-he lut-mô ke vi-só na chhut-hien pha̍k-chûng tai-fùng ke chṳn-thèu, liá he thái-kô ke phiang-chṳn.43 Chi-sṳ̂ oi kiám-chhà kì; na-he kông-thèu ke vi-só chṳn-kîn yû pha̍k-chûng tai-fùng ke chṳn-thèu,44 chi-sṳ̂ oi siên-pu kì he mò chhiang-li ke, yîn-vi kì ke thèu-nà pot thái-kô.45 Pot thái-kô ke ngìn yit-thin oi chok chhá-lan ke sâm, thèu-nà-mô oi chông pûn kì nùng-nùng, êm-tén choi-sùn, hêm-kóng: "Mò chhiang-li, mò chhiang-li!"46 Chhai thái-kô màng yî-hó yî-chhièn, kì he mò chhiang-li ke; kì yit-thin oi he̍t chhai yàng-ngoi, lâu phe̍t-ngìn kak-lì. Yû-koân Sâng-kû ke Thiàu-li47 Mò-lun he yòng-mô sâm ya-he mà-sâ sâm, he chhṳ̍t-sâ ya-he vàng-sâ ke, he phì ke ya-he phì ke sán-phín, na-he sâng-kû( 10 13:47-48 "sâng-kû": Khon 13:2.),48 併於上節49 liá sâng-kû na-he chhiâng-set ya-he fùng-set, chhiu-he voi chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p ke kû, yit-thin oi kâu-pûn chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà.50 Chi-sṳ̂ oi siòng-se kiám-chhà, chiông kì kak-lì chhit-ngit.51 Thi-chhit ngit, oi chai kiám-chhà kì. Na-he sâng-kû chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, liá tûng-sî he mò chhiang-li ke.52 Chi-sṳ̂ oi chiông kì sêu-thet, yîn-vi kì he voi kín-chhe kín thai-ta̍p ke kû, yit-thin oi yung fó sêu-thet.53 Chúng-he, chi-sṳ̂ kiám-chhà heu na fat-hien liá sâng-kû mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p,54 kì oi ham-ngìn sé ke tûng-sî, chiông kì chai kak-lì chhit-ngit.55 Yèn-heu, chi-sṳ̂ oi chai kiám-chhà kì; na-he sâng-kû mò chhe-to kha thai-ta̍p, chúng-he hàn-he mò pien-set, liá tûng-sî hàn-he mò chhiang-li ke. Mò-lun sâng-kû he chhai chṳn-mien ya-he poi-mien, ke tûng-sî yit-thin oi yung fó sêu-thet.56 Chúng-he, chi-sṳ̂ chai kiám-chhà kì ke sṳ̀, na-he fat-hien sâng-kû yí-kîn mò-thet, chhiu-oi chiông ke phu-fun chhá hâ-lòi.57 Yî-heu, sâng-kû na yu chhut-hien, yu kín-chhe kín thai-ta̍p, ngì chhiu oi yung fó chiông ke tûng-sî sêu-thet.58 Na-he ngì chiông ke tûng-sî sé-ko, sâng-kû chhiu mò-thet hi, ngì oi chai sé yit-pái, án-ngiòng ke tûng-sî chhai lî-ngì song chhiu chhiang-li lé.59 Yî-song he yû-koân sâm-fu sâng-kû ke thiàu-li. Mò-lun he yòng-mô sâm ya-he mà-sâ sâm, he chhṳ̍t-sâ ya-he vàng-sâ ke, ya-he phì ke tûng-sî, só-yû sâng-kû ke, chhiòn-phu oi on-cheu liá thiàu-li lòi kiet-thin chhiang-li ya-he mò chhiang-li.
