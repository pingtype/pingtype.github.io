1 Su-chi ke Thiàu-li Na-he yû-ngìn hien su-chi pûn Song-chú, kì oi yung yu-mien-fún. Kì oi tó-têu kám-lám-yù chhai mien-fún tî-poi, chiông nen-hiông piong chhai táng-kô,2 tai-to Â-lùn-chhu̍k ke chi-sṳ̂ mien-chhièn. Chi-sṳ̂ oi yá yit-kha̍k yu-mien-fún lâu kám-lám-yù, pin só-yû ke nen-hiông piong chhai chi-thàn táng lòi sêu, thoi-péu hien chhiòn-phu. Liá fó-chi ke hiông-mi he Song-chú só fôn-hí ke.3 Chhûn-tó ke su-chi oi kûi-pûn Â-lùn lâu kì ke chṳ́-sûn; liá he chṳ-sṳn ke, yîn-vi he tui hien-pûn Song-chú ke fó-chi chûng liù hâ-lòi ke.4 Na-he ngì hien ke he yung lù-è phoi ke su-chi, oi hien yung yu-mien-fún kiâu kám-lám-yù cho-sṳ̀n ke vù-kau phûn-piáng, ya-he kò kám-lám-yù ke vù-kau pho̍k-piáng.5 Na-he ngì hien ke he yung chiên-phàn chiên ke su-chi, oi hien yung yu-mien-fún kiâu kám-lám-yù cho ke vù-kau-piáng.6 Hien-piáng ke sṳ̀, oi chiông kì pak-cho yit-te yit-te, táng-kô lìm-têu yù. Liá he su-chi.7 Na-he ngì hien ke he chhién vo̍k-thèu pet ke su-chi, oi-yung yu-mien-fún kiâu kám-lám-yù cho ke.8 Ngì oi chiông liá-têu tûng-sî cho ke su-chi tai-to Song-chú mien-chhièn, kâu-pûn chi-sṳ̂ tai-to chi-thàn thèu-chhièn.9 Chi-sṳ̂ oi nâ su-chi ke yit phu-fun piong chhai thàn-táng lòi sêu, thoi-péu hien chhiòn-phu. Liá fó-chi ke hiông-mi he Song-chú só fôn-hí ke.10 Chhûn-tó ke su-chi oi kûi-pûn Â-lùn lâu kì ke chṳ́-sûn; liá he chṳ-sṳn ke, yîn-vi he tui hien-pûn Song-chú ke fó-chi chûng liù hâ-lòi ke.11 Ngì-têu hien-pûn Song-chú ke su-chi chhiòn-phu cho-m̀-tet kiâu kau-mà; ngì-têu chhie̍t-tui cho-m̀-tet chiông kau ya-he phûng-thòng cho fó-chi hien-pûn Song-chú.12 Ngì-têu cho-tet chiông liá-têu hien-pûn Song-chú cho mî-ngièn sîn-chhut ke chi-vu̍t, chúng-he cho-m̀-tet piong chhai chi-thàn táng sêu cho chhîn-hiông ke chi-vu̍t.13 Ngì só-hien ke kok-chúng su-chi chhiòn-phu oi kiâu-yàm, yîn-vi yàm thoi-péu Song-ti lâu ngì-têu só-li̍p ke yok. Ngì yit-thin oi yung yàm kiâu kok-chúng chi-vu̍t.14 Na-he ngì hien sîn-chhut ke ńg-kuk pûn Song-chú cho su-chi, oi-hien phoi-ko ke ńg-kuk nga̍t-cho chhû-fún.15 Hàn-oi kâ-têu kám-lám-yù, chiông nen-hiông piong chhai táng-kô; liá he su-chi.16 Chi-sṳ̂ oi nâ yit phu-fun ke ńg-kuk chhû-fún, yù lâu só-yû ke nen-hiông, thoi-péu hien chhiòn-phu, cho fó-chi hien-pûn Song-chú.
