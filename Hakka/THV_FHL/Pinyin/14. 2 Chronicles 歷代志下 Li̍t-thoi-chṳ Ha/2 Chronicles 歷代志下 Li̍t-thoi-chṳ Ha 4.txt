1 Sṳn-thien ke Yung-phín( Lie̍t-vòng Ki-song 7:23-51 ) Só-lò-mùn-vòng ham-ngìn chu-chho yit-chak thùng chi-thàn, kiú kûng-chhak si-fông, kô si kûng-chhak pan.2 Kì ya chu-chho yit-chak yèn ke thùng-hói, chhṳ̂m lióng kûng-chhak lióng kûng-chhun, tui liá-phién to ke-phién si kûng-chhak si kûng-chhun, chû-vì sṳ̍p-sâm kûng-chhak lióng kûng-chhun.3 Thùng-hói piên-sùn ke ngoi-poi yû lióng-phài tiâu-khiet, yit-phài chhai táng-kô, yit-phài chhai hâ-poi, khat-cho ngiù-kú ke yong-sṳt. Liá-têu thùng-ngiù lâu thùng-hói chhiòn-phu he yung kûi-te thùng chu-chho ke.4 Thùng-hói ôn chhai sṳ̍p-ngi chak thùng-ngiù poi-nòng táng, ngiù-thèu hiong-ngoi, sâm-chak hiong-tûng, sâm-chak hiong-sî, sâm-chak hiong-nàm, sâm-chak hiong-pet.5 Thùng-hói phûn chhit kûng-fûn pan, piên-sùn chhiong pî-é ke piên-sùn, khat yû pak-ha̍p-fâ ke fâ-phan. Thùng-hói cho-tet chông liuk-van kûng-sṳ̂n.6 Kì yu chu-chho sṳ̍p-chak thùng-phùn, ńg-chak piong chhai sṳn-thien nàm-phién, nang-ngoi ńg-chak piong chhai pet-phién. Liá-têu thùng-phùn he yung lòi sé sêu-fa-chi ke tûng-sî. Thùng-hói ke súi he pûn chi-sṳ̂ yung lòi sé ke.7 Kì cheu kûi-thin ke yong-sṳt cho sṳ̍p-chak kîm tên-thòi, sṳ̍p-chông chok-è, piong chhai thai-thien ke lióng-phién, tak-phién yû ńg-chak kîm tên-thòi lâu ńg-chông chok-è. Kì ya cho yit-pak chak kîm-vón.8 併於上節9 Kì vi chi-sṳ̂ hí nui-yen, ya hí ngoi-yen. Lióng-yen chûng-kiên ke mùn chhiòn-phu pâu thùng.10 Thùng-hói piong chhai sṳn-thien ke tûng-nàm kok.11 Fu-làn ya chu-chho vo̍k-thèu, vo̍k-chhán lâu vón. Kì vi Só-lò-mùn-vòng vàn-sṳ̀n kì só fu-chit Song-ti sṳn-thien ke kûng-chhàng. Kì só chu-chho ke yung-phín lie̍t chhai hâ-poi:Lióng-kî thùng-chhû,ôn chhai thùng-chhû táng-kô ke lióng-chak vón-hìn chhû-thèu,chhû-táng piên-chṳt ke lien-é ke thù-hìn,si-pak chak thùng sa̍k-liù — tak-chak chhû-thèu thù-hìn khiêu-tén lióng-phài sa̍k-liù, yit-phài yit-pak chak,sṳ̍p-chak( 5 4:14 "sṳ̍p-chak" khon Lie̍t-vòng Ki-song 7:43, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "kì chho lé". Hâ-poi "sṳ̍p-chak" khiung-yong.) thùng-phùn-chho,sṳ̍p-chak thùng-phùn,thùng-hói,chhang-tén thùng-hói ke sṳ̍p-ngi chak thùng ngiù-kú,vo̍k-thèu, vo̍k-chhán lâu chhâ-é. Sṳ̂-fu Fu-làn vi Só-lò-mùn-vòng chu-chho Song-chú sṳn-thien ke yung-phín, chhiòn-phu he yung thùng chu-chho nò-to kîm-kîm ke.12 併於上節13 併於上節14 併於上節15 併於上節16 併於上節17 Liá-têu tûng-sî chhiòn-phu he cheu vòng ke min-lin, chhai Yok-tan phìn-ngièn, Sû-kot lâu Sat-lâ-tan tûng-ông ke chu-chho-chhòng chu-chho ke.18 Yîn-vi liá-têu thùng-hi tông-tô, mò ngìn son-ko to-tái yung-thet kí-tô thùng.19 Só-lò-mùn yu ham-ngìn vi Song-ti ke sṳn-thien chṳ-chho tak-chúng kîm-hi: Yû kîm-thàn lâu pài-sat kiûng-piáng ke chok-è;20 piong chhai chṳ-sṳn-só thèu-chhièn cheu-mìn yung ke sùn-kîm tên lâu tên-thòi;21 sùn-kîm fâ, tên, fó-khiàm;22 sùn-kîm tên-sîm chién-tô, vón, piong-hiông ke thia̍p-é, lâu chông fó-than ke phùn-è. Liá-têu yung-phín chhiòn-phu he yung sùn-kîm chṳ-chho ke. Sṳn-thien ngoi-poi ke mùn lâu chṳ-sṳn-só ke mùn chhiòn-phu pâu kîm-é.
