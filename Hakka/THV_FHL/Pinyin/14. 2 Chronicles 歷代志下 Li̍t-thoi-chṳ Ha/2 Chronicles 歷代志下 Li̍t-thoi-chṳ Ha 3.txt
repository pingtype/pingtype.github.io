1 Só-lò-mùn ke â-pâ Thai-ví yí-kîn vi sṳn-thien chún-phi yit-ke só-chhai, chhiu-he chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Mô-li-â sân-táng — Song-chú sṳt hiong Thai-ví hién-hien ke só-chhai, chhiu-he Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn Â-lò-nâ ke tá-ma̍k-chhòng.2 Só-lò-mùn cho vòng ke thi-si ngièn ngi-ngie̍t chhû-ngi khôi-kûng hí sṳn-thien.3 Só-lò-mùn hí Song-ti sṳn-thien ke thi-kî, yû ngi-sṳ̍p chhit kûng-chhak chhòng, kiú kûng-chhak fat.4 Ngi̍p mùn-héu ke mùn-lòng lâu sṳn-thien phiàng-fat, fat kiú kûng-chhak, kô kiú kûng-chhak. Mùn-lòng nui-phu chhiòn-phu tap sùn-kîm.5 Thai-thien ke piak tâng yit-phài hiông-pak-su piông, chai tap sùn-kîm, táng-kô tiâu-khiet chûng-su lâu lien-é ke thù-on.6 Vòng yung chhin-chiâng ke pó-sa̍k lâu tui Pâ-ngá-yîm chin-khiéu ke kîm-é chông-fòng sṳn-thien.7 Kì yung kîm-é tap sṳn-thien ke piak, liòng, mùn lâu mùn-khiâm. Sṳ̂-fu chhai piak-táng tiâu-khiet kî-lu-pak.8 Chṳ-sṳn-só ke nui-thien yû kiú kûng-chhak chhòng, kiú kûng-chhak fat, lâu sṳn-thien phiàng-fat. Chṳ-sṳn-só piak-táng só-tap ke kîm-é yû ngi-sṳ̍p kûng-tún.9 Kîm tâng-é yung-thet ńg-pak chhit-sṳ̍p kûng-khiet. Lèu-fòng ke piak ya yû tap kîm-é.10 Vòng yu min-lin sṳ̂-fu cho lióng-ke kî-lu-pak, ngoi-poi pâu-tén kîm-é, piong chhai chṳ-sṳn-só tî-poi.11 Liá lióng-ke kî-lu-pak phiàng-phiàng khî-tén, mien-tui ngi̍p-héu; tak-ke kî-lu-pak yû lióng-chak yi̍t-kap, tak-chak yi̍t-kap yû lióng kûng-chhak lióng kûng-chhun chhòng, lióng-tui yi̍t-kap tá-khôi, chhai thien ke chûng-kiên fu-siông chiap-tó, lióng-phién tu̍t-tó piak, thai-yok kiú kûng-chhak fat.12 併於上節13 併於上節14 Chṳ-sṳn-só ke pu-mok he yung mà-sâ lâu khì-thâ chhòi-liau cho ke, ngiam-cho làm-set, chṳ́-set, fùng-set, yû kî-lu-pak ke thù-on chhiak chhai táng-kô. Lióng-kî Thùng-chhû15 ( Lie̍t-vòng Ki-song 7:15-22 ) Vòng ham-ngìn cho lióng-kî thùng-chhû, tak-kî kô sṳ̍p-ńg kûng-chhak pan, tun chhai sṳn-thien thèu-chhièn. Tak-kî yû yit-chak chhû-thèu, kô lióng kûng-chhak lióng kûng-chhun.16 Thùng-chhû thèu ôn-tén yû piên-chṳt ke lien-é thù-hìn, lien-é táng yû yit-pak chak thùng sa̍k-liù.17 Liá lióng-kî thùng-chhû tun chhai sṳn-thien ngi̍p-héu ke lióng-phién, nàm-phién ke-kî ham-cho Ngâ-kîn( 3 3:17 "Ngâ-kîn" yû "Kì (Song-ti) kien-li̍p" ke yi-sṳ.), pet-phién ke-kî ham-cho Pô-â-sṳ̂( 4 3:17 "Pô-â-sṳ̂" yû "yîn-vi Kì (Song-ti) ke nèn-li̍t" ke yi-sṳ.).
