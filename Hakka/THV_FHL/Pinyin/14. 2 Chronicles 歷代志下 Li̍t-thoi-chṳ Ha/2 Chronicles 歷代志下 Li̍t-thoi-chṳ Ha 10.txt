1 Pet-phu Kî-chhu̍k ke Chho-fán( Lie̍t-vòng Ki-song 12:1-20 ) Lò-pô-ôn to Sṳ-kiam hi; Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhiòn-phu chhi-si̍p chhai ke-vi, oi sat-li̍p kì cho vòng.2 Nì-pat ke lai-é Yâ-lò-pô-ôn yîn-vi sám Só-lò-mùn-vòng thò-to Âi-khi̍p hi; kì yit thâng-tó Lò-pô-ôn cho vòng ke sêu-sit, chhiu tui Âi-khi̍p kón chón-lòi.3 Pet-phu kî-chhu̍k ke ngìn-mìn phai-ngìn hi chhiáng kì lòi, yèn-heu lâu kì khiung-ha hi kien Lò-pô-ôn, tui kì kóng:4 "Ngiâ-pâ Só-lò-mùn sṳ́ ngài-têu pà chhûng-tâm. Na-he ngì kám-khiâng ngài-têu ke fu-tâm, pûn ngài-têu ke sên-fa̍t ko-tet hi, ngài-têu chhiu fu̍k-sṳ ngì."5 Lò-pô-ôn kóng: "Ngì-têu sâm-ngit yî-heu chai-lòi kien ngài." Só-yî kì-têu chhiu lì-khôi.6 Lò-pô-ôn-vòng mun kí-ke ngièn-ló ke yi-kien; kì-têu sṳt cho-ko kiâ-pâ Só-lò-mùn-vòng ke ku-mun. Kì mun kì-têu: "Ngì-têu sióng ngài yin-kôi ngióng-pân fì-tap liá-têu ngìn nè?"7 Kì-têu kóng: "Na-he ngì sióng-oi hó khoán-thai liá-têu ngìn, sṳ́ kì-têu fôn-hí, fì-tap kì-têu ke sṳ̀ oi kóng-têu hó-fa, kì-têu chhiu voi yún-yén cho ngì ke nù-phu̍k."8 Chúng-he, Lò-pô-ôn m̀-tap liá-têu ngièn-ló ke khien-va, fán-chón hi chhìm ke-têu lâu kì khiung-ha thai ke heu-sâng ngìn; kì-têu liá-ha cho kì ke ku-mun.9 Lò-pô-ôn mun kì-têu: "Ngì-têu yû má-ke kien-ngi? Ke-têu ngìn yêu-khiù ngài kám-khiâng kì-têu ke fu-tâm, ngài yin-kôi ngióng-pân fì-tap kì-têu nè?"10 Kì-têu kóng: "Ngì cho-tet án-ngiòng lâu kì-têu kóng: ‘Ngài ke sú-chṳ́-mî pí ngâ-pâ ke yêu-sṳ̂n hàn-kha chhû!11 Ngâ-pâ pûn ngì-têu chhûng-tâm, ngài oi pûn ngì-têu hàn-kha chhûng ke tâm-thèu. Ngâ-pâ yung phì-piên tá ngì-têu, ngài oi yung tai kiêu-é ke phì-piên tá ngì-têu!’ "12 Sâm-ngit yî-heu, Yâ-lò-pô-ôn lâu só-yû ngìn-mìn cheu Lò-pô-ôn-vòng ke chṳ́-sṳ, yu to kì mien-chhièn.13 Lò-pô-ôn-vòng m̀-thâng ngièn-ló ke kien-ngi, fán-chón cheu heu-sâng ngìn ke kien-ngi tui ngìn-mìn kóng tông-ngiàm ke fa. Kì kóng: "Ngâ-pâ pûn ngì-têu chhûng-tâm, ngài oi pûn ngì-têu hàn-kha chhûng ke tâm-thèu. Ngâ-pâ yung phì-piên tá ngì-têu, ngài oi yung tai kiêu-é ke phì-piên tá ngì-têu!"14 併於上節15 Vòng m̀-tap ngìn-mìn ke yêu-khiù; chúng-he liá he Chú Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi, oi sṳ̍t-hien Kì thûng-ko Sṳ-lò-ngìn siên-tî Â-hî-ngâ tui Nì-pat ke lai-é Yâ-lò-pô-ôn só-kóng ke fa.16 Yî-set-lie̍t ngìn-mìn khon-tó vòng m̀ chiap-na̍p kì-têu ke yêu-khiù chhiu thai-sâng hêm-kóng:"Chhai Thai-ví-kâ ngài-têu son má-ke?Yâ-sî ke lai-é tui ngài-têu yû má-ke hó-chhu?Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, ngài-têu lòi-hi chón!Thai-ví-kâ â, chhṳ-kâ pó-chhung ô!" Só-yî, Yî-set-lie̍t ngìn-mìn chho-fán, chón vuk-hâ hi.17 Chúng-he he̍t chhai Yù-thai thi-khî ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, Lò-pô-ôn hàn-he cho kì-têu ke vòng.18 Lò-pô-ôn-vòng phai khú-kûng ke chúng-kón Â-tô-làn to Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn ke-vi hi, Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn sa yung sa̍k-thèu chiông kì te̍p-sí. Liá-sṳ̀, Lò-pô-ôn-vòng kón-kín sông chan-chhâ, sia chón-hi Yâ-lu-sat-lâng.19 Tui án-ngiòng, pet-koet Yî-set-lie̍t ke ngìn-mìn yit-chhṳ̍t fán Thai-ví ke kâ-chhu̍k.
