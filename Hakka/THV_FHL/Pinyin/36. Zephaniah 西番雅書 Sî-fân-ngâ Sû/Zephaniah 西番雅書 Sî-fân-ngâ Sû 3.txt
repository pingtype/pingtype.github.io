1 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Chhui-ok lâu Kiu-su̍k Liá phoi-ngia̍k, fú-phai, khî-fu ngìn ke sàng chhám lé!2 Kì m̀ thâng-chhiùng Song-chú ke min-lin, ya m̀ chiap-su Kì ke kón-kau. Kì m̀ yí-kho Song-chú, ya m̀ chhîn-khiun kì ke Song-ti.3 Kì ke-têu cho-kôn ke chhiong ho-ho-kún ke sṳ̂-é; kì ke-têu sṳ́m-phan-kôn thâm-sîm to chhiong am-pû-sṳ̀ tông-yâu ke sài-lòng, lièn yit kî kut-thèu me-mò liù-to thiên-kông.4 Kì ke-têu siên-tî mò fu chit-ngim, yu kúi-cha; kì ke-têu chi-sṳ̂ tá-lá-sap sṳn-vu̍t, khiuk-kié Song-ti ke fap-li̍t.5 Chúng-he Song-chú hàn-he chhai liá sàng-tú; Kì hàng kûng-ngi, chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi cho put-ngi ke sṳ. Tak chêu-sṳ̀n, Kì hiong Kì ke chṳ́-mìn hién-mìn kûng-ngi, chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt thìn-chṳ́; chúng-he put-ngi ke ngìn-mìn ki-siu̍k fam-chhui, m̀-tî kien-seu.6 Song-chú kóng: "Ngài sêu-me̍t lie̍t-koet, chhio-tha̍p kì-têu ke sàng-sṳ, chiông kì-têu ke sàng-lèu pien-cho sa̍k-tôi, lu-hong mò-ngìn sông-hâ. Ngài sṳ́ kì-têu ke sàng-sṳ fông-liòng, kiê-lu khûng-khûng, mò liù pan-sà.7 Ngài láu-tó ke-sṳ̀ Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn yit-thin voi chûn-kin Ngài, chiap-su kón-kau, yún-yén ki-tet Ngài pûn kì-têu ke kau-hiun! Chúng-he ko mò kí-kiú, kì-têu yu hàn-kha li-hoi cho fái-sṳ lé!"8 Song-chú kóng: "Ngì-têu thin-chho̍k oi tén, tén Ngài hong-hí-lòi ko lie̍t-koet ke-ngit. Ngài yí-kîn kiet-thin seu-si̍p lie̍t-koet van-pâng chhièn-lòi, chiông Ngài ke thai fat-khién chhiòn-phu tó chhai kì-têu sṳ̂n-song. Ngài ngie̍t-chhìn ke fó oi sêu-me̍t chhiòn sṳ-kie.9 "Ke-sṳ̀, Ngài oi kói-pien van-mìn, pûn kì-têu chhiòn-phu yung chhiang-li ke fa lòi khì-khiù Ngài, thùng-sîm ha̍p-yi sṳ-fung Ngài.10 Ngài só fûn-san ke chṳ́-mìn, lièn chhai yén-yén ke Kú-sṳ̍t hò-pa ke-vi ke, ya oi to-chón-lòi hiong Ngài hien-chi.11 To ke-sṳ̀, Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn â, ngì-têu m̀-sṳ́ yîn-vi phoi-ngia̍k Ngài ke sṳ kám-kok to kien-seu; yîn-vi Ngài oi tui ngì-têu chûng-kiên chhù-thet só-yû kiêu-ngau ke ngìn. Chhai Ngài ke sṳn-sân, ngì-têu m̀-voi chai chhṳ-kô chhṳ-thai.12 Ngài oi chhai ngì-têu tông-chûng liù-hâ khiâm-pî, khun-khú ke ngìn; kì-têu voi to Ngài mien-chhièn lòi khiù Ngài pó-fu.13 Só-chhûn ke ke-têu Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, m̀-voi chai cho fái-sṳ, m̀-voi kóng fâ-liâu, me mò kúi-cha. Kì-têu voi ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p, m̀-voi chai kiâng má-ngìn." Khoai-lo̍k ke Kô14 Siak-ôn ke chṳ́-mìn â, yin-tông chhong-kô!Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, yin-tông thai-sâng hêm!Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke pak-siang â,yin-tông mân-sîm fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.15 Song-chú yí-kîn kié-chhù ngì ke chit-fa̍t,yí-kîn kiuk-chéu ngì só-yû ke sù-thi̍t.Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Vòng lâu ngì khiung-ha,ngì m̀-sṳ́ chai kiâng voi yû má-ke châi-fo.16 Chhai ke-ngit voi yû-ngìn tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng kóng:Siak-ôn â, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng!M̀-sṳ́ kiâng-to sú-ngiôn kiok-lài!17 Song-chú — ngì ke Song-ti lâu ngì khiung-ha;Kì ke thai-nèn sṳ́ ngì tet-sṳn.Kì oi chhai ngì tông-chûng fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k;Kì ke thung-siak oi su ngì sîn ke sâng-miang( 7 3:17 "oi su ngì sîn ke sâng-miang" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ya yû hien-thoi yi̍t-pún fân-cho "oi sṳ́ ngì ôn-chhin".).Kì oi yîn ngì fôn-hí chhong-kô,18 chhiong ngìn chhai ko-chiet ke sṳ̀ án fôn-hí( 8 3:18 "chhiong ngìn ... fôn-hí" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).Song-chú kóng: Ngài yí-kîn thìn-thet châi-fo ke vî-hia̍p,chhù-thet ngì ke kien-seu( 9 3:18 "Ngài yí-kîn thìn-thet ... kien-seu": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).19 Sṳ̀-chiet to lé!Ngài oi chit-fa̍t yit-chhiet khî-fu ngì ke ngìn;Ngài oi chṳ́n-kiu ngiôn-ngio̍k, sṳt-san ke ngìn,sṳ́ liù-mòng ke ngìn to-chón chhṳ-kâ ke kâ-yèn.Ngài oi sṳ́ kì-têu ke kien-seu pien-cho yùng-yeu;kì-têu chhai chhiòn sṳ-kie voi yû hó miàng-sâng.20 Sṳ̀-chiet to lé!Ngài oi chiông ngì-têu si-san ke chṳ́-mìn thu chón-lòi;Ngài oi chhai ngì-têu ngién-chhièn fî-fu̍k ngì-têu ke sán-ngia̍p,sṳ́ ngì-têu ke miàng-sâng chhòn-to thiên-ha van-mìn.Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.
