1 "Ke-ngit, Ngài oi vi Thai-ví ke heu-thoi lâu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-ngìn khôi yit-ke chhièn-ngièn, chiông kì-têu ke chhui-ok lâu lá-sap sé-to chhiang-chhiang." Song-chú Chhù-thet Ngiéu-siong lâu Ká Siên-tî2 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi kóng: "Ke-ngit, Ngài oi chiông ngiéu-siong ke miàng tui liá-ke thi-fông chhù-thet, mò ngìn voi chai ki-ngiam. Ngài ya oi tui liá-ke thi-fông chhù-me̍t ká siên-tî lâu sià-lìn.3 Na-he hàn yû-ngìn ngang-kiáng kín kóng ká yi-ngièn, kiung kì ke yà-ôi oi lâu kì kóng: ‘Ngì kôi-sí lé, yîn-vi ngì chia Song-chú ke miàng kóng yi-ngièn, sṳ̍t-chi-song he phien-ngìn.’ Kì kóng-tén yi-ngièn ke sṳ̀, kì ke yà-ôi oi chiông kì chhiuk-sí.4 "To ke-ngit, tak-ke ká siên-tî voi vi chhṳ-kâ só-kóng ke yi-siong kám-kok kien-seu, ya m̀-voi chai chok phì-mô ke sâm lòi khî-phien ngìn.5 Kì voi kóng: ‘Ngài m̀-he siên-tî; ngài he kâng-thièn-ngìn, chhiùng-se chhiu kâng-thièn( 28 13:5 "kâng-thièn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "cho ngìn ke nù-chhòi".).’6 Na yû-ngìn mun kì: ‘Ngì poi-nòng( 29 13:6 "poi-nòng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "hiûng-phù".) ke sông-héu he ngióng-pân lòi ke?’ Kì voi en-kóng: ‘He chhai phèn-yû vuk-hâ su-sông ke.’ " Kûng-tá Mu̍k-chá7 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi kóng: "Tô-kiam â, hong hí-lòi! Kûng-kit Ngài ke mu̍k-chá — Ngài ke thùng-phân! Tá mu̍k-chá, yòng-khiùn chhiu si-san. Ngài ya oi chhut-sú tá ke-têu ngiôn-ngio̍k ke yòng.8 Chhiòn-thi sâm-fûn chṳ̂-ngi ke ngìn voi mò-miang, sâm-fûn chṳ̂-yit ke ngìn voi sâng-tén.9 Ngài oi pûn liá sâm-fûn chṳ̂-yit ke ngìn kîn-ko kháu-ngiam, sṳ́ kì-têu sùn-chhiang, chhiong ngiùn-è kîn-ko fó. Ngài oi sṳ-lien kì-têu, chhiong lien kîm-é yit-yong; yèn-heu kì-têu voi hiong Ngài khì-tó, Ngài chhiu voi thâng. Ngài oi lâu kì-têu kóng, kì-têu he Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn; kì-têu oi sṳ̀n-ngin, Ngài — Song-chú he kì-têu ke Song-ti."
