1 Ôi-kô Song-ti ham ngài vi Yî-set-lie̍t ke chung vòng-chṳ́ cho liá-sú ôi-kô:2 Ngiâ-mê he sṳ̂-mà, phu̍k chhai kûi-khiùn kûng-sṳ̂ chûng;kì chhai seu-chong sṳ̂-é tông-chûng hiuk se sṳ̂-é.3 Kì hiuk-thai yit-chak se sṳ̂-é sàng-cho seu-chong sṳ̂-é;seu-chong sṳ̂-é ho̍k-voi tá-lia̍p, ho̍k-voi sṳ̍t ngìn.4 Lie̍t-koet thâng-tó, chiông kì koái-ngi̍p chhṳ̂m-hâng;kì-têu yung kiêu-é chiông kì thô-to Âi-khi̍p.5 Sṳ̂-mà tén-to sîm chho̍k-kip chhie̍t-mong;kì yu nang-ngoi hiuk-thai yit-chak se sṳ̂-é sàng-cho seu-chong sṳ̂-é.6 Seu-chong sṳ̂-é thèn-tén kûi-khiùn sṳ̂-é tak só-chhai chéu-lòi chéu-hi;kì ya ho̍k-voi tá-lia̍p, ho̍k-voi sṳ̍t ngìn.7 Kì pho-fái sàng-pó( 24 19:7 "pho-fái sàng-pó" ya-he yi̍t-cho "hoi-sí koá-fu", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò chhîn-chhú.), tá-fái sàng-sṳ;kì thai-sâng hô, chhiòn-thi ke ngìn kiâng-to pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat.8 Lie̍t-koet tui si-mien pat-fông lòi vì kì;kì-têu tá-khôi lò-mióng, chiông kì koái-ngi̍p chhṳ̂m-hâng.9 Kì-têu yung kiêu-é chiông kì thô-ngi̍p lûng-é, tai-to Pâ-pí-lùn-vòng ke-vi;kì-têu chiông kì koân-ngi̍p kâm-ngiu̍k, Yî-set-lie̍t ke khiùn-sân mò chai thâng-tó kì chhin-sê ke sâng.10 Ngiâ-mê chhiong yit-thèu chung chhai hâi-súi sùn ke phù-thò-su,yîn-vi súi fûng-fu, kî-ya̍p cha̍t-cha̍t, kó-chṳ́ tông-kiet.11 Kì ke su-khoá chhin-ngang, hó cho vòng ke chhóng-è;kì ke su-sṳ̂n kô-thai, chhêu-ko khì-thâ su-é ke kî-ya̍p;kì ke su-mî kô-to tù-thiên, yén-yén chhiu khon tet-tó.12 Chúng-he, yû fat-khién ke sú chiông kì lièn-kîn pâng-hí,vok chhai thi-song, pûn tûng-fûng chhôi-châu,su-khoá áu-thôn, kó-chṳ́ châu-thet, pûn fó-sêu chhiang-chhiang.13 Liá-ha kì pûn-ngìn chung chhai khóng-yâ,tông-châu mò-súi ke só-chhai.14 Su-koáng chho̍k-fó, su-khoá lâu kó-chṳ́ chhiòn-phu sêu-thet;kì sa mò chhin-ngang ke su-khoá, hó cho vòng ke chhóng-è. Liá he yit-sú ôi-kô; liá-sú ôi-kô yit-chhṳ̍t liù-chhòn.
