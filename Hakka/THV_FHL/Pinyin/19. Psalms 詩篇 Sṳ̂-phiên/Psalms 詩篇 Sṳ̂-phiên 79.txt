1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂.Vi Koet-kâ Khì-tó Song-ti â, ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn chhîm-ngi̍p Ngì ke thú-thi.Kì-têu tá-lá-sap Ngì ke sṳn-thien,sṳ́ Yâ-lu-sat-lâng pien-cho fông-liòng.2 Kì-têu nâ Ngì phu̍k-ngìn ke sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ pûn ên-tiâu sṳ̍t;kì-têu chiông Ngì sṳn-mìn ke sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ fit-pûn yâ-chhu.3 Kì-têu sṳ́ Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn hiet-liù sṳ̀n-hò,hiet chhiong súi án-ngiòng yim-tén Yâ-lu-sat-lâng,mò liù-hâ yit-ke ngìn hó mài-chong sṳ̂-thí.4 Lìn-koet vú-yu̍k ngài-têu;chû-vì ke ngìn seu ngài-têu, sâu-nat ngài-têu.5 Song-chú â, hàn-oi kí-kiú nè?Ngì oi fat-khién to yún-yén mâ?Ngì ke nu-hi oi chhiong fó sêu-to kí-sṳ̀ nè?6 Khiù Ngì chiông nu-hi chón-hiong m̀ kin-vi Ngì ke koet-kâ,piang chhai ke-têu m̀ khiù-ham Ngì miàng ke ngìn-mìn sṳ̂n-song;7 yîn-vi kì-têu thûn-me̍t Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi,sṳ́ kì-têu ke hiông-thú pien-cho fông-liòng.8 Khiù Ngì mo̍k ki-tén ngài-têu chú-siên ke chhui,m̀-hó tui ngài-têu thó kì-têu ke chhui-chai.Khiù Ngì kiak-kiak sṳ̂-ên lòi lìn-mén ngài-têu,yîn-vi ngài-têu yí-kîn mò pan-si̍t hî-mong.9 Chṳ́n-kiu ngài-têu ke Song-ti â,khiù Ngì vi-tó Ngì yùng-kông ke miàng lòi pông-chhu ngài-têu;khiù Ngì vi-tó Ngì ke miàng théu-piong ngài-têu,sa-miên ngài-têu ke chhui.10 Cho-má-ke pûn lie̍t-koet kín-mun:"Ngì-têu ke Song-ti chhai nai-vi nò?"Pûn ngài-têu khon-tó Ngì chit-fa̍t lie̍t-koet,yîn-vi kì-têu liù Ngì phu̍k-ngìn ke hiet.11 Pûn-ngìn chok-hi koân ke, ngien Ngì chhùi-thâng kì-têu ke chhên-sâng;pûn-ngìn thin sí-chhui ke, ngien Ngì yung thai-nèn théu-piong kì-têu.12 Chú â, lìn-koet ngióng-pân siû-yu̍k Ngì,khiù Ngì yung chhit-phi vàn-pûn kì-têu.13 Án-ngiòng, ngài liá-têu cho Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn, Ngì mu̍k-yông ke yòng-khiùn,ngài-têu oi yún-yén kám-chhia Ngì;ngài-têu oi siung-chan Ngì, chhṳ̍t-to sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi!
