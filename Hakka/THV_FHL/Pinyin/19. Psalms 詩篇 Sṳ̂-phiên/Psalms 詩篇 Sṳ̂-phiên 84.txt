1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung kâ-thi̍t ngo̍k-hi.Hot-mu Song-ti ke Sṳn-só Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi â,Ngì ke sṳn-só sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-hó!2 Ngài sióng-oi hi Song-chú ke sṳn-thien, sióng-to fûn-thet;ngài oi yung chhiòn-phu ke sṳ̂n-sîm thai-sâng siung-chan yún-sên ke Song-ti.3 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi,ngài ke Vòng, ngài ke Song-ti â,chhai Ngì ke chi-thàn sùn-thèu,lièn vuk-yàm-tiâu ya vi chhṳ-kâ cho-teu,yen-é ya chhìm-tó phu-chṳ́ ke só-chhai.4 He̍t chhai Ngì sṳn-thien tú ke ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!Kì-têu yit-chhṳ̍t chhai ke-vi chan-mî Ngì.5 Yí-kho Ngì tet-tó hi-li̍t,ngie̍t-sîm oi hi Siak-ôn-sân chhèu-sṳn ke ngìn,kì-têu sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-yû fuk-hi ô!6 Kì-têu kîn-ko châu-châu ke sân-lak,kì chhiu pien-cho chhièn-súi chṳ̂-thi;lièn chhiû-yí ya lo̍k hâ-lòi,sṳ́ sân-lak to-chhu sàng-cho pî-thòng.7 Kì-têu ye̍t-hàng ye̍t-yû hi-li̍t,chui-heu voi to Siak-ôn chhèu-kien chṳ-chûn ke Song-ti.8 Chú Song-ti — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi â,khiù Ngì thâng ngài ke khì-tó!Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti â, khiù Ngì chet-ngí lòi-thâng!9 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì khon-ku ngài-têu ke tún-phài;khiù Ngì cheu-ku Ngì só sién-li̍p ke vòng!10 Chhai Ngì ke sṳn-thien he̍t yit-ngit,yàng-ko chhai phe̍t-vi he̍t chhiên-ngit;ngài kâm-ngien khî chhai ngài Song-ti sṳn-thien ke mùn-héu( 77 84:10 "khî chhai ... mùn-héu" yi-sṳ he, tén chi-sṳ̂ chún kì ngi̍p-hi kin-pai Song-ti. Ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhai ngài Song-ti sṳn-thien ke mùn-héu chóng-mùn".),ya mò-oi he̍t chhai sià-ok ngìn ke chong-phùng tú.11 Yîn-vi Chú Song-ti he ngit-thèu, he tún-phài;Kì voi su ên-fi lâu chûn-yùng pûn ngài-têu.Song-chú m̀-voi chiông chui-hó ke liù-ténm̀ pûn ke-têu hàng-vì chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.12 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi â,sin-kho Ngì ke ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!
