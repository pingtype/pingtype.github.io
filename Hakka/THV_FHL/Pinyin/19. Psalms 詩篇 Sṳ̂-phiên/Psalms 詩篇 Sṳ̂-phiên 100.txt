1 Kám-chhia ke sṳ̂.Van-mìn Oi Siung-chan Song-chú Phû-thiên-ha oi hiong Song-chú fôn-fû!2 Oi fôn-fôn hí-hí lòi kin-pai Song-chú!Oi chhong khoai-lo̍k ke sṳ̂-kô lòi-to Kì ke mien-chhièn!3 Oi tî Song-chú he Song-ti!Ngài-têu he Kì chhóng-chho ke,he su̍k Kì ke;ngài-têu he Kì ke chṳ́-mìn,he Kì mu̍k-yông ke yòng-khiùn.4 Oi yung kám-chhia ke sîm lòi chin-ngi̍p Kì ke sṳn-thien!Oi yung siung-chan lòi chin-ngi̍p Kì ke sṳn-só!Oi kám-chhia Kì, siung-chan Kì ke miàng!5 Yîn-vi Song-chú he chṳ-san;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn;Kì ke sin-sṳ̍t van-thoi put-pien.
