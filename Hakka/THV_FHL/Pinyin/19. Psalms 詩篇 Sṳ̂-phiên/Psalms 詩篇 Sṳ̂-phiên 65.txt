1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Siung-chan lâu Kám-ên Song-ti â, ngài-têu yin-tông chhai Siak-ôn chan-mî Ngì;ngài-têu yin-tông tui Ngì vàn só-hí ke ngien.2 Thâng ngìn khì-tó ke Chú â,ngìn-ngìn yin-tông chhiu-khiun Ngì.3 Chhui-ok yàng-ko ngài-têu( 58 65:3 "ngài-têu" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "ngài".) ke sṳ̀,Ngì sa-miên ngài-têu ke ko-fam.4 Ngì só kién-sién, tai-lòi he̍t chhai Ngì sṳn-só ke ngìn,sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!Ngài-têu voi hióng-su Ngì sṳn-só ke fûng-sṳn to sîm-mân yi-chiuk,chhiu-he Ngì sṳn-thien ke fuk-hi!5 Chṳ́n-kiu ngài-têu ke Song-ti â,Ngì yung khì-meu ke chok-vì lòi en ngài-têu ke khì-tó, pûn ngài-têu sṳn-li.Sṳ-kiên ke van-mìn lâu thiên-ngài hói-kok ke ngìn chhiòn-phu sin-kho Ngì!6 Ngì chông-hién Ngì ke thai-nèn,yung li̍t-liong ôn-thin khiùn-sân.7 Ngì phìn-chhin thai-hói ke ho-sâng,pô-long ke chhàu-nau,lâu van-mìn ke sâu-thung.8 Chhiòn sṳ-kie yîn-vi Ngì só sṳ̀n-chhiu ke thai-sṳ lòi kin-vi Ngì;tui ngit-chhut to ngit-lo̍k chṳ̂ thi ke ngìn yîn-vi Ngì ke chok-vì lòi fôn-fû.9 Ngì khon-ku thú-thi, lo̍k kâm-lìm lòi chṳ̂-yun kì;Ngì sṳ́ thú-thi tet-tó chṳ̂-yông, pûn kì phì hí-lòi.Ngì só-su ke hò-pa, yí-súi mân-mân;Ngì pûn thai-thi ki-siu̍k chhut-sán ńg-kuk.10 Ngì sṳ́ kâng-thi yí-súi chhûng-chiuk,yung súi chṳ̂-yun thièn-thi;Ngì lo̍k-yí pûn châu-nài sûng-ngiôn,sṳ́ nùng-chok-vu̍t tet-tó sên-chóng.11 Ngì ke ên-tién sṳ́ ngài-têu ngièn-ngièn fûng-sû;yû Ngì kiok-chiak ke só-chhai chhiu yû fu-chiuk.12 Mu̍k-chhòng chûng ngiù-yòng sṳ̀n-khiùn;sân-liâng kiên fôn-sâng mân-phien.13 Ngiù-yòng mân-sân, ńg-kuk mân-hâng;van-vu̍t yit-thùng fôn-fû kô-chhong!
