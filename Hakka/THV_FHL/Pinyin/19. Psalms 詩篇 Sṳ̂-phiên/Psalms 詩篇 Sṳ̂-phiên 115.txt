1 Thu̍k-yit Chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t ke Song-ti Song-chú â, yùng-kông m̀-hó kûi-pûn ngài-têu,m̀-hó kûi-pûn ngài-têu!Yùng-kông oi yîn-vi Ngì ke chhṳ̀-oi lâu sin-sṳ̍t kûi chhai Ngì ke miàng-hâ!2 Cho-má-ke pûn lie̍t-koet kín-mun ngài-têu:"Ngì-têu ke Song-ti chhai nai-vi nè?"3 Ngài-têu ke Song-ti chhai thiên-táng;Kì cheu chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-yi hàng-sṳ.4 Kì-têu ke ngiéu-siong he kîm-ngiùn cho ke,he ngìn ke sûng-sú chṳ-chho chhut-lòi ke.5 Kì-têu yû choi, ya m̀-voi kóng-fa;sûi-yèn yû muk-chû, me m̀-voi khon;6 yû ngí-kûng, ya m̀-voi thâng;yû phi-kûng, me m̀-voi phi;7 yû sú, chúng-he m̀-voi miâ;yû kiok, ya he m̀-voi hàng;hèu-lièn, me m̀-voi chhut-sâng.8 Ngien yit-chhiet( 105 115:8 "Ngien yit-chhiet" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Yit-chhiet".) chṳ-chho lâu sin-kho ngiéu-siong ke ngìn,lûng-chúng thùng kì-têu só-cho ke ngiéu-siong khiung-yong.9 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, oi sin-kho Song-chú!Kì he ngì-têu ke pông-chhu lâu tún-phài.10 Â-lùn-kâ ke chung chi-sṳ̂ â, oi sin-kho Song-chú!Kì he ngì-têu ke pông-chhu lâu tún-phài.11 Kin-vi Song-chú ke â, oi sin-kho Kì!Kì he ngì-têu ke pông-chhu lâu tún-phài.12 Song-chú yit-chhṳ̍t ku-ngiam ngài-têu;Kì hàn-oi su-fuk pûn ngài-têu;Kì oi su-fuk pûn Yî-set-lie̍t-kâ ke ngìn-mìn;Kì oi su-fuk pûn Â-lùn-kâ ke chung chi-sṳ̂.13 Song-chú oi su-fuk pûn kin-vi Kì ke ngìn,mò-lun thi-vi kô-tâi lûng-chúng yû-fun.14 Ngien Song-chú kâ-thiâm chṳ́-ńg pûn ngì-têu,pûn ngì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn ye̍t-lòi ye̍t-tô.15 Ngien Song-chú su-fuk pûn ngì-têu;Kì he chhóng-chho thiên-thi ke Chú!16 Thiên, chhiu-he Song-chú ke thiên;thi, Kì chhiu su-pûn ngìn-lui.17 Sí-ngìn m̀-voi chan-mî Song-chú;lo̍k yîm-fú ke ya he khiung-yong.18 Chúng-he, ngài-têu sâng-sâng ke ngìn oi siung-chan Song-chú,tui liá-ha chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
