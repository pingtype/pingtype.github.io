1 Siung-chan Song-chú ke Chṳ-san Hâ-li-lu-yá!Song-chú ke phu̍k-ngìn â, oi chan-mî,ngì-têu oi chan-mî Song-chú ke miàng!2 Song-chú ke miàng yin-kôi su siung-chan,tui liá-ha chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén!3 Tui ngit-chhut to ngit-lo̍k ke só-chhai,Song-chú ke miàng yin-kôi su chan-mî!4 Song-chú chhêu-ko thiên-ha van-koet,Kì ke yùng-kông pí thiên kha-kô.5 Má-ngìn chhiong Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti nè?Kì ke pó-chho sat chhai chṳ-kô ke só-chhai,6 chúng-he, Kì tâi-thèu kôn-chhat,khon-ku chû-thiên lâu thai-thi.7 Kì tui nài-fún chûng kô-sṳ̂n khiùng-khú ngìn,Kì tui sṳ́-tôi tú khiên-hí khun-khú ngìn;8 Kì sṳ́ kì-têu thùng chung vòng-chṳ́ cho phèn-yû,thùng kì-têu pún-koet ke vòng-chṳ́ chhô khiung-ha.9 Kì sṳ́ m̀-voi kiung ke fu-ńg chhai kâ-chûng su chûn-chhung,su-pûn kì chṳ́-ńg, pûn kì fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.Hâ-li-lu-yá!
