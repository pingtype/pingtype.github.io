1 s THI-ŃG KIÉNSiung-chan Song-ti ke Chṳ-san "Oi kám-chhia Song-chú, yîn-vi Kì he chṳ-san;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn!"2 Song-chú só kiu-su̍k ke ngìn â,ngì-têu oi án-ngiòng kín-kóng!Kì kiu ngì-têu thot-lì sù-thi̍t ke sú;3 Kì tui tûng, sî, nàm( 97 107:3 "nàm": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "hói".), pet,tai ngì-têu tui kok thi-fông to-chón-lòi.4 Yû-têu ngìn mì-sṳt chhai mò-ngìn he̍t ke khóng-yâ,chhìm m̀-tó lu hó-hi yû-ngìn he̍t ke sàng-sṳ.5 Kì-têu tú-kî yu choi-châu,chhiòn-mò pan-si̍t ke hî-mong.6 Chhai kip-nan chûng, kì-têu khiù-ham Song-chú,Kì chhiu chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì khú-nan.7 Kì tai-liâng kì-têu hàng chhṳ̍t-lu,hi-to yû-ngìn he̍t ke sàng-sṳ.8 Kì-têu yin-tông kám-chhia Song-chú put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi,siung-chan Kì vi-tó sṳ-kiên-ngìn só-hàng ke khì-sṳ.9 Kì pûn choi-châu ke ngìn tet-tó mân-chiuk,pûn tú-kî ke sṳ̍t chhin-hó yu chhin-páu.10 Yû-têu ngìn sên-fa̍t chhai vû-am lâu sí-yîm chûng,kì-têu su-chhin thung-khú yu pûn thiet-lien khún-tén,11 yîn-vi kì-têu phoi-ngia̍k Song-ti ke fa,méu-sṳ Chṳ-kô-chá ke chṳ́-yi.12 Song-ti yung lò-khú hòng-fu̍k kì-têu ke sîm;kì-têu vang-tó, chúng-he mò-ngìn lòi fù.13 Chhai kip-nan chûng, kì-têu khiù-ham Song-chú,Kì chhiu chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì khú-nan.14 Kì tai kì-têu tui vû-am lâu sí-yîm chhut-lòi,yu pâng-thôn ke-têu khún-tén kì-têu ke só-lien.15 Kì-têu yin-tông kám-chhia Song-chú put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi,siung-chan Kì vi-tó sṳ-kiên-ngìn só-hàng ke khì-sṳ.16 Kì tá-lan thùng-mùn,chám-thôn thiet mùn-chhôn.17 Yû-têu ngìn yîn-vi chhṳ-kâ ke ko-fam pot-phiang( 98 107:17 "pot-phiang": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "pien-ngong".),yu yîn-vi chhṳ-kâ ke chhui-ok su-khú;18 kì-têu tui sṳ̍t-vu̍t chhiòn-mò vi-khiéu,kín-lòi chhiu kín-khiûn sí-mòng ke mùn.19 Chhai kip-nan chûng, kì-têu khiù-ham Song-chú,Kì chhiu chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì khú-nan.20 Kì kóng yit-ki fa chhiu yî-hó kì-têu,kiu kì-têu thot-lì sí-mòng.21 Kì-têu yin-tông kám-chhia Song-chú put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi,siung-chan Kì vi-tó sṳ-kiên-ngìn só-hàng ke khì-sṳ.22 Kì-têu yin-tông oi tui Kì hien kám-chhia ke chi,yung fôn-hí ke sṳ̂-kô kóng-sot Kì ke chok-vì!23 Yû-têu ngìn chhô-sòn chhut-hói,chhai thai-hói tông-chûng mèu-sên.24 Kì-têu khon-tó Song-chú ke chok-vì,Kì chhai chhṳ̂m-hói só-hàng ke khì-sṳ.25 Kì yit min-lin, chhiu hí khòng-fûng,sṳ́ hói-long kie̍p-to pan-thiên-kô.26 Yit-ha chhûng-to kô-kô, yit-ha lo̍k-to chhṳ̂m-chhṳ̂m,ngùi-kip chṳ-khi̍t, kì-têu kiâng-to sṳ̀n-fùn sêu-san.27 Kì-têu vang-lòi vang-hi, chhiong sṳ̍t chiú-chui;kì-têu ke chhòi-thiau vàn-chhiòn tién m̀-chhut.28 Chhai kip-nan chûng, kì-têu khiù-ham Song-chú,Kì chhiu chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì khú-nan.29 Kì sṳ́ khòng-fûng phìn-chhin,pûn hói-long tiâm hâ-lòi.30 Kì-têu fôn-hí na-thiên, yîn-vi fûng-phìn long-chhin;Kì chhiu thu kì-têu to só-oi hi ke hói-kóng.31 Kì-têu yin-tông kám-chhia Song-chú put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi,siung-chan Kì vi-tó sṳ-kiên-ngìn só-hàng ke khì-sṳ.32 Kì-têu yin-tông chhai chóng-ló ke ngi-fi chûng chan-mî Kì,chhai ngìn-mìn ke thai-fi chûng chhòn-yòng Kì ke ví-thai.33 Song-chú sṳ́ hò-pa pien-cho khóng-yâ,sṳ́ chhièn-súi châu-thet sàng-cho hôn-thi,34 sṳ́ chhin phì ke thú-thi pien-cho yàm-thi,yîn-vi he̍t chhai ke-vi ke ngìn chhin fái.35 Kì sṳ́ sâ-mo̍k tak-vi yû pî-thòng,sṳ́ hôn-thi sàng-cho chhièn-súi chṳ̂ thi.36 Kì pûn tú-kî ke ngìn he̍t chhai ke-vi;ke-têu ngìn chhiu hí yit-chho sàng lòi-he̍t.37 Kì-têu kâng-thièn ve-chúng, chung phù-thò,tet-tó fî-sòng fûng-fu ke sû-sṳ̀n.38 Kì su-fuk pûn kì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn kín-tô;Kì sṳ́ kì-têu ke thèu-sâng yû-kâ mò-kám.39 Heu-lòi yîn-vi pûn-ngìn khî-fu, su-khú su-nan,Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn chhiu kín kám-séu, kín chhôi-ko.40 Song-ti sṳ́ thúng-chhṳ-chá tù-tó vú-yu̍k,pûn kì-têu phêu-liù chhai mò-ngìn he̍t ke khóng-yâ.41 Chúng-he, Kì chṳ́n-kiu khiùng-khú-ngìn thot-lì khú-nan,pûn kì-têu ke kâ-chhu̍k chhiong yòng-khiùn án hîn-vong.42 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn khon-tó chhiu chhin fôn-hí;chúng-he fái-ngìn chhiòn-phu á-khiéu vù-ngièn.43 Ngien yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn chu-yi liá-têu sṳ-chhìn;ngien kì-têu sṳ̂-ngiam Song-chú put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi.
