1 Sông-kîn ke sṳ̂.Sun-chhiùng Song-chú Tet Chióng-sóng Kin-vi Song-chú, chûn-hàng Kì tho-lu ke,liá-chúng ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!2 Ngì lò-luk tet-tó ke la ngì yung;ngì chhiu voi khoai-lo̍k, fàm-sṳ sun-li.3 Ngì ke pû-ngiòng chhai ngì vuk-hâ,chhiong kiet-chṳ́ tô-tô ke phù-thò-su;ngì ke chṳ́-ńg vì-tén ngì ke chok-è,chhiong se-thèu ke kám-lám-su.4 Kin-vi Song-chú ke ngìn,tit-khok voi án-ngiòng tet-tó fuk-hi!5 Ngien Song-chú tui Siak-ôn su-fuk pûn ngì!Ngien ngì yit-sên-ngìn khon-tó Yâ-lu-sat-lâng hîn-vong!6 Ngien ngì khon-tet-tó ngì ke chṳ́-chṳ́ sûn-sûn!Ngien Yî-set-lie̍t hióng-su thai-phìn!
