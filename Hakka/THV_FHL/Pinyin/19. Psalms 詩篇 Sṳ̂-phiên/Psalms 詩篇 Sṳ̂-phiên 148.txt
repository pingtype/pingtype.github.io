1 Chhiòn Yí-chhiu Oi Siung-chan Song-ti Hâ-li-lu-yá!Ngì-têu oi tui thiên-táng chan-mî Song-chú!Ngì-têu oi chhai chṳ-kô ke só-chhai chan-mî Kì!2 Kì ke chung thiên-sṳ́ â, ngì-têu oi chan-mî Kì!Kì ke chung thiên-kiûn â, ngì-têu oi chan-mî Kì!3 Ngit-thèu â, ngie̍t-kông â, oi chan-mî Kì!Kín ngiap ke sên-sṳ̀n â, oi chan-mî Kì!4 Thiên-táng ke chû-thiên â, oi chan-mî Kì!Thiên-táng ke chung-súi â, oi chan-mî Kì!5 Ngien liá-têu chan-mî Song-chú ke miàng!Kì yit ha-lin, chhiu chho-chhut yit-chhiet.6 Kì chiông liá-têu kok-chhiu kok-vi, yún-yén kiên-ku;Kì thin-liáu kie-han, put-nèn chhêu-ko( 130 148:6 "Kì thin-liáu ... chhêu-ko" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kì ha-liáu min-lin, chhiu m̀-voi kói-pien".).7 Ngì-têu oi tui thi-song chan-mî Song-chú!Thai-hói-koai â, chhṳ̂m-yên â,8 ngiap-lang, pên-pho̍k, siet lâu yùn-vu â,chûn-hàng Kì min-lin ke thai-fûng â,9 só-yû ke thai-sân lâu se-sân â,kó-chṳ́-su lâu kok-chúng ke su â,10 yâ-chhu lâu thèu-sâng â,phà-chhùng lâu fî-khìm â,ngì-têu lûng-chúng oi chan-mî Kì!11 Sṳ-song ke kiûn-vòng â, thiên-ha van-mìn â,thai-sṳ̀n â, sṳ-song yit-chhiet chóng-khièn ke â,12 se â-kô-é â, se â-moi-é â,ló-ngìn-kâ â, se-ngìn-è â,ngì-têu lûng-chúng oi chan-mî Kì!13 Ngien chhiòn sṳ-kie ke yit-chhiet lûng-chúng chan-mî Song-chú ke miàng!Kì ke miàng he chṳ-chûn ke;Kì ke yùng-kông chhêu-ko thiên-thi.14 Kì sṳ́ Kì ke chṳ́-mìn khiòng-sṳn( 131 148:14 "Kì sṳ́ Kì ke chṳ́-mìn khiòng-sṳn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kì vi-tó Kì ke chṳ́-mìn hîn-hí yit-ke vòng".),pûn Kì sîm só-oi ke chṳ́-mìn,chhiu-he chûng-sîm ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn,lûng-chúng chan-mî Kì!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
