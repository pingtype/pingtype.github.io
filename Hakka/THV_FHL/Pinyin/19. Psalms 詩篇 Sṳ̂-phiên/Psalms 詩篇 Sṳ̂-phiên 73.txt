1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂.Song-ti ke Kûng-ngi Song-ti khok-sṳ̍t ên-thai Yî-set-lie̍t,hó khoán-thai sîm-kôn chhiang-li ke ngìn.2 Chúng-he ngài hiám-hiám tá-thong-thiu,ngài ke kiok hiám-hiám khî m̀-chhai;3 yîn-vi ngài chhit-tu kiêu-ngau ke ngìn hîn-vong,khon-tó fái-ngìn fu-kui ngài chhin m̀ kâm-ngien.4 Kì-têu mò tù-tó má-ke thung-khú;kì-têu ke sṳ̂n-thí ya chhin yúng-khien.5 Kì-têu m̀-sṳ́ chhiong phe̍t-ngìn án sîn-khú;kì-têu mò chhiong phe̍t-ngìn tù-tó châi-nan.6 Só-yî, kì-têu chhiu chiông kiêu-ngau chún pha̍t-lien tai;kì-têu ya chiông phau-li̍t chún sâm-fu chok.7 Kì-têu ke muk-chû hién-chhut thâm-sîm;kì-têu ke yi-ngiam chhûng-mân kúi-cha.8 Kì-têu sâu-nat phe̍t-ngìn, kóng ngìn ke fái-fa;kì-têu chhṳ-kô chhṳ-thai, thù-mèu ap-chṳ ngìn.9 Kì-têu yung choi siet-thu̍k song-thiên,yung sa̍t-mà kâu-sô sṳ-kiên-ngìn;10 chṳ-to lièn Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn ya thèn kì-têu,yit-sîm yit-yi sin-chhiùng kì-têu só-kóng ke fa( 67 73:10 Pún-chiet chhai Hî-pak-lòi-vùn yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).11 Kì-têu kóng: "Song-ti ngióng voi tî?Chṳ-kô-chá kám-yû chṳ-sṳt mâ?"12 Khon â, fái-ngìn chhiu-he án-ngiòng;kì-têu tông hó-chhói, kín-lòi kín-pot.13 Ngài kiên-chhṳ̀ sùn-kiet, m̀-kám fam-chhui;liá sṳ̍t-chhai he long-fi chîn-sṳ̀n ke!14 Yîn-vi ngài kûi-ngit tù-tó châi-nan;tak-ngit chêu-sṳ̀n chhiu su-tó chit-fa̍t!15 Ngài na-he kóng:"Ngài oi ki-siu̍k án-ngiòng kóng",ngài chhiu m̀-he Ngì chûng-sṳ̍t ke chṳ́-mìn.16 Ngài chhin sióng-oi liáu-kié liá-ke sṳ,chúng-he ngài sông nó-kîn ya mò-yung;17 chhṳ̍t-to ngài chin-ngi̍p Song-ti ke sṳn-thien,ngài chang mìn-pha̍k fái-ngìn ke kiet-khiu̍k.18 Ngì sṳ̍t-chhai chiông kì-têu piong chhai va̍t thiu-thiu ke só-chhai;Ngì sṳ́ kì-têu vang-lo̍k me̍t-mòng chṳ̂-chûng.19 Kì-têu voi chhai ngiap-muk chṳ̂-kiên me̍t-thet;kì-têu voi pûn thai châi-nan so chhiang-chhiang.20 Chhiong ngìn pot-mung, yit-siáng ko-lòi, mung chhiu mò-thet hi,Chú â, Ngì yit-siáng, kì-têu chhiu mò-chiûng mò-chiak lé.21 Ngài pún-lòi sîm-fài put-phìn,ngài ke sîm-kôn su sông-hoi.22 Ngài chhiong khìm-chhu án-ngong;ngài tui Ngì mò liáu-kié.23 Chúng-he, ngài yit-chhṳ̍t thùng Ngì khiung-ha,yîn-vi Ngì khiên-tén ngài ke yu-sú.24 Ngì he yung khien-va lòi phú-thô ngài,to-mî Ngì ya voi tai ngài ngi̍p yùng-kông.25 Chhù-liáu Ngì, chhai thiên-táng ngài hàn yû má-ngìn?Ngài yû Ngì, chhai sṳ-kiên ngài mò-chai yû-hó hiûn-mu ke.26 Ngài ke ngiuk-thí lâu sîm-sṳ̀n kín-lòi kín fú-phai;chúng-he Song-ti chhiu-he ngài sîm-nui ke li̍t-liong,yu he ngài ke fuk-fun chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén.27 Ke-têu lì-khôi Ngì ke ngìn yit-thin voi me̍t-mòng;só-yû poi-phan Ngì ke ngìn voi pûn Ngì sêu-me̍t.28 Lun-tó ngài, chhîn-khiun Song-ti chhiu-he ngài ke fuk-hi!Ngài kho Chṳ-kô ke Song-chú cho ngài ke phit-nan-só;ngài oi án-ngiòng lòi siên-yòng Ngì yit-chhiet ke chok-vì.
