1 Yî-tûng-ngìn Tô-yit po-ko Só-lò-vòng kóng: "Thai-ví hi-to Â-hî-mí-le̍t vuk-hâ." Ke-sṳ̀, Thai-ví cho liá-sú kau-hiun-sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Song-ti ke Sṳ́m-phan lâu Ên-tién Yû khièn-sṳ ke ngìn â, ngì cho-má-ke khoâ-khiéu só-cho ke fái-sṳ nè?Song-ti ke chhṳ̀-oi he yún-yén m̀-voi kói-pien ke.2 Ngì ke sa̍t-mà chhiong nò-li ke thi-thèu-tô;ngì yit-chhṳ̍t chṳ-chho sṳ-fî sông-hoi phe̍t-ngìn.3 Ngì hau sià-ok, nâu liòng-san;ngì oi hî-ká, hen chṳ̂n-lî.4 Ngì liá-ke fâ-liâu-kú â,ngióng án-oi chung-sông phe̍t-ngìn!5 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, Song-ti oi chha̍t-tái sêu-me̍t ngì;Kì oi chok ngì, chiông ngì tui vuk-hâ khiú chhut-lòi,chiông ngì tui sṳ-ngìn chûng lièn-thèu tai-kîn pâng-thet.6 Ngi-ngìn khon-tó chhiu voi chho̍k-kiâng;kì-têu voi seu ngì, kóng:7 "Khon â, liá chhiu-he mò yí-kho Song-ti ke ngìn;kì yí-kho chhṳ-kâ ke chhièn tô, cho fái-sṳ kiên-li̍p chhṳ-kâ."8 Chúng-he ngài, ngài chhiong chung chhai Song-ti vuk-hâ meu-sṳn ke kám-lám-su;ngài yún-yún yén-yén yí-kho Song-ti put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi.9 Song-ti â, ngài oi yún-yén kám-chhia Ngì só-cho ke yit-chhiet;ngài oi chhai Ngì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn chûng chhòn-yòng Ngì ke hó miàng-sâng.
