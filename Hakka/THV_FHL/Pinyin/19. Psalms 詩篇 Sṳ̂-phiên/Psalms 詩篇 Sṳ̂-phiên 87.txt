1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂-kô.Siung-chan Yâ-lu-sat-lâng Song-chú chhai sṳn-sân-tángkien-chho chhṳ-kâ ke sàng.2 Kì tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke thung-siak,yàng-ko Yî-set-lie̍t phe̍t ke sàng-sṳ.3 Song-ti ke sàng â, chhiáng thâng Kì só-kóng,koân-he ngì ke-têu kông-yùng ke sṳ-chhìn:4 "Ngài oi chiông Âi-khi̍p lâu Pâ-pí-lùnki chhai sun-fu̍k Ngài ke miàng-tân chûng;Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn, Thai-ngì-ngìn lâu Kú-sṳ̍t-ngìn,Ngài oi son kì-têu cho Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-ngìn."5 Lun-tó Siak-ôn, oi án-ngiòng kóng:"Van-koet oi cho kì ke kûng-mìn";yîn-vi Chṳ-kô-chá oi pûn kì khiòng-sṳn.6 Song-chú oi tên-ki van-mìn ke fu-khiéu,son kì-têu cho Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke kûng-mìn.7 Kì-têu lûng-chúng voi thiau-vú chhong-kô, kóng:"Ngài-têu hen-fuk ke chhièn-ngièn chhai Siak-ôn!"
