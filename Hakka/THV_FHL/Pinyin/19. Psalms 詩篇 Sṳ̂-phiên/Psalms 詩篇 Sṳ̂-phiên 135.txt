1 Siung-chan ke Sṳ̂-kôHâ-li-lu-yá!Oi chan-mî Song-chú ke miàng!Song-chú ke phu̍k-ngìn â,chhai Song-chú ke sṳn-thien tông-pân ke â,chhai ngài-têu Song-ti ke sṳn-thien ngoi-yen sṳ-fung ke ngìn â,ngì-têu oi chan-mî Kì!2 併於上節3 Ngì-têu oi chan-mî Song-chú, yîn-vi Song-chú he chṳ-san;oi kô-siung Kì ke miàng, yîn-vi Kì he chhṳ̀-oi ke( 118 135:3 "Kì he chhṳ̀-oi ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "liá he khi̍t-hó ke".).4 Song-chú vi chhṳ-kâ kién-sién Ngâ-kok,tho̍k Yî-set-lie̍t cho Kì pó-pi ke chṳ́-mìn.5 Ngài tî Song-chú he chṳ-thai ke;ngài-têu ke Chú chhêu-ko van-sṳ̀n.6 Chhai thiên, chhai thi, chhai thai-hói, chhai chhṳ̂m-yên,Song-chú cheu chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-yi hàng-sṳ.7 Kì sṳ́ yùn-vu tui thi-khi̍t sṳ̂n-sông hi,sṳ́ ngiap-lang thùng phau-yí khiung-ha lòi,sṳ́ fûng tui Kì ke chhông-khu chhôi-chhut.8 Kì chhai Âi-khi̍p chhṳ̀-sí só-yû thèu-thôi ke,mò-lun he ngìn, he thèu-sâng, chhiòn-phu chhṳ̀-thet.9 Chhai ke-vi, Kì sṳ̂-hàng sṳ̀n-chiak khì-sṳ,chit-fa̍t Âi-khi̍p-vòng lâu kì ke phu-ha.10 Kì sêu-me̍t chhin-tô koet,chhṳ̀-sí tông li-hoi ke vòng:11 Yû Â-mô-li-vòng Sî-fèn,Pâ-sân-vòng Ngo̍k, lâu só-yû Kâ-nàm ke vòng.12 Kì chiông kì-têu ke thú-thi su-pûn Kì chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-mìn,su-pûn Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn cho sán-ngia̍p.13 Song-chú â, Ngì ke miàng yún-yén chhòng-chhùn!Van-sṳ van-thoi lûng-chúng oi ki-ngiam Ngì!14 Song-chú oi vi Kì ke chṳ́-mìn sṳ̂n-yên;Kì tui Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn chhṳ̀-pî mân-mân.15 Lie̍t-koet ke ngiéu-siong he kîm-ngiùn cho ke,he ngìn ke sûng-sú chṳ-chho chhut-lòi ke.16 Kì-têu yû choi, ya m̀-voi kóng-fa,yû muk-chû, ya m̀-voi khon,17 yû ngí-kûng, ya m̀-voi thâng,yû phi-khûng, ya m̀-voi théu-hi.18 Ngien yit-chhiet( 119 135:18 "Ngien yit-chhiet" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Yit-chhiet".) chṳ-chho lâu sin-kho ngiéu-siong ke ngìn,lûng-chúng thùng kì-têu só-cho ke ngiéu-siong khiung-yong.19 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-chú!Â-lùn-kâ ke chung chi-sṳ̂ â, oi siung-chan Kì!20 Li-vi-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-chú!Kin-vi Song-chú ke ngìn â, oi siung-chan Kì!21 Ngì-têu chhai Siak-ôn ke â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!Oi siung-chan he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Song-chú!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
