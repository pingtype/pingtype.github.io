1 Khién-khiù Kûng-ngi Song-chú â, Ngì cho-má-ke khî-to án-yén nè?Chhai fam-nan ke sṳ̀, Ngì cho-má-ke m̀ chhut-mien?2 Fái-ngìn yîn-vi kiêu-ngau pet khiùng-khú-ngìn to mò-lu hó-chéu;ngien kì-têu ham-lo̍k chhai chhṳ-kâ só-sat ke lò-mióng tî-poi.3 Fái-ngìn khoâ-khiéu chhṳ-kâ sià-ok ke yu̍k-mong;thâm-sîm ke ngìn khî-chhie̍t pin méu-sṳ Song-chú.4 Fái-ngìn chiông kì ke mien ngau-to kô-kô, kóng:"Song-chú m̀-voi chhìm ngài son-sṳ,sṳ-sṳ̍t-song me-mò Song-ti â!"Kì sîm-chûng só-sióng ke chhiu-he án-ngiòng.5 Fái-ngìn ke hàng-sṳ sòng-sòng sun-li.Kì mò liáu-kié Song-ti ke sṳ́m-phan;kì sâu-nat kì só-yû ke tui-thi̍t.6 Kì sîm-kôn sióng: "Ngài yún-yén m̀-voi sṳt-phai;ngài sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi m̀-voi tù-tó châi-nan."7 Kì mân-choi chu-ma, kúi-cha, hot-hot tot-tot,sa̍t-mà hau-kóng yen-hen lâu sià-ok ke fa.8 Kì mài-fu̍k chhai chhûn-chông ke tî-poi;kì tén kî-fi sat-hoi mò-chhui ke ngìn.Kì ke muk-chû thêu-khon mò yí-kho ke ngìn,9 kì chhiong sṳ̂-é mài-fu̍k chhai chhó-teu tú;kì tén kî-fi oi chok khiùng-khú-ngìn,mióng-tó yî-heu chhiu chiông kì chok-hi.10 Khiùng-khú-ngìn chhai kì ke ap-pet chṳ̂-ha,chṳ́-yû tâi-thèu hòng-fu̍k kì ke phau-li̍t.11 Fái-ngìn sîm-kôn sióng: "Song-ti m̀ ki-tet lé;Kì êm-tén muk-chû, yún-yén khon m̀-tó ngài!"12 Song-chú â, khiù Ngì hong-hí-lòi!Song-ti â, hó chhut-sú lé!Mo̍k m̀ ki-tet su-khú ke ngìn!13 Fái-ngìn cho-má-ke kám méu-sṳ Song-ti,sîm-kôn kóng: "Kì m̀-voi chhìm ngài son-sṳ"?14 Khì-sṳ̍t, yit-chhiet chhai Ngì ke kam-chhat chûng;Ngì chhut-sú koân-sîm khú-nan lâu yù-sèu.Khiùng-khú mò yí-kho ke ngìn thèu-kho Ngì;Ngì yit-chhṳ̍t chhiu-he pông-chhu kû-yì ke.15 Khiù Ngì sêu-me̍t ok-ngìn lâu fái-ngìn ke sṳ-li̍t;khiù Ngì chit-fa̍t kì-têu ke chhui, chha̍t-tái son-sṳ.16 Song-chú yún-yún yén-yén cho Vòng chóng-khièn;ngoi-pâng voi tui Kì ke thú-thi sêu-me̍t.17 Song-chú â, Ngì yí-kîn thâng-tó khiùng-khú-ngìn ke sîm-ngien;Ngì voi kâ-thiâm kì-têu ke yúng-hi, thâng kì-têu khì-tó.18 Ngì voi thoi kû-yì lâu su ap-pet ke sṳ̂n-yên,pûn sṳ-kiên-ngìn m̀-voi chai-ko khiúng-hak phe̍t-ngìn.
