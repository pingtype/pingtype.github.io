1 Siung-chan Chṳ́n-kiu-chá Song-ti Hâ-li-lu-yá!Ngài ke sîm â, ngì oi chan-mî Song-chú!2 Ngài hàn sâng ke sṳ̀-chiet oi chan-mî Song-chú!Ngài yit-sên-ngìn oi kô-siung ngài ke Song-ti!3 Ngì-têu m̀-hó yí-kho yû khièn-sṳ ke ngìn,m̀-hó yí-kho sṳ-kiên-ngìn;kì yit-si̍t-é tû put-nèn pông-chhu.4 Kì-têu ke hi yit-thôn chhiu kûi-thú;kì-têu ke kie-va̍k tông-ngit chhiu mò-yung.5 Tet-tó Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti lòi pông-chhu ke ngìn, chṳ̂n-yû fuk-hi ô!Ngióng-mong Song-chú — kì ke Song-ti,liá ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!6 Song-ti chhóng-chho thiên, thi, hói lâu khì-chûng ke van-vu̍t;Kì chhṳ̀-sú sin-sṳ̍t chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.7 Kì vi-tó ke-têu su khî-ap ke chú-chhṳ̀ kûng-tho;Kì pûn tú-kî ke ngìn yû-hó sṳ̍t.Song-chú sṳ́ ke-têu pûn-ngìn koân ke tet-tó chhṳ-yù;8 Song-chú pûn mô-muk ke ngìn khon-tet-tó.Song-chú fù-chhṳ̀ ke-têu su ap-chṳ ke ngìn;Song-chú thung-siak chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.9 Song-chú pó-fu chhut-ngoi-ngìn,fù-chhṳ̀ kû-yì koá-fu;chúng-he Kì oi pho-fai ok-ngìn ke kúi-kie.10 Song-chú oi cho Vòng to yún-yén!Siak-ôn â, ngì ke Song-ti oi chóng-khièn to van-thoi!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
