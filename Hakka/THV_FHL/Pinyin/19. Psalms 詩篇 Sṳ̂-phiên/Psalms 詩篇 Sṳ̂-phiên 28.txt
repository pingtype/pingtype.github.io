1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂.Khiù Pông-chhu ke Khì-tó Song-chú â, ngài khiù-ham ke chhiu-he Ngì!Ngài ke phàn-sa̍k â, khiù Ngì mo̍k set-tén ngí-kûng m̀-thâng ngài!Ngì na-he ki-siu̍k tiâm-tiâm m̀-tap ngài,ngài voi chhiong phe̍t-ngìn khiung-yong lo̍k yîm-kiên.2 Khiù Ngì thâng ngài ke khiù-ham lòi pông-chhu ngài!Ngài hiong Ngì ke chṳ-sṳn-só khià-sú khì-tó,ke-sṳ̀, khiù Ngì lìn-mén ngài, thâng ngài khién-khiù!3 Mo̍k chiông ngài thùng fái-ngìn khiung-ha thin-chhui,ya-he thùng chok-ok ke ngìn khiung-ha chhù-thet;kì-têu lâu lìn-sa kóng-fa chhin fò-hi,chúng-he kì-têu ke sîm chhûng-mân kiên-cha.4 Khiù Ngì chit-fa̍t kì-têu ke hàng-vì,cheu kì-têu ke sià-ok tui-thai kì-têu;khiù Ngì cheu kì-têu sûng-sú só-cho ke,cheu kì-têu yin-tet ke po-yin kì-têu.5 Yîn-vi kì-têu méu-sṳ Song-chú ke chok-vì,yu khon-khiâng Kì ke sú só-cho ke kûng-chok;só-yî, Song-chú oi fí-fai kì-têu,pûn kì-têu yún-yén hîn m̀ hí-lòi.6 Song-chú he yin-tông tet-tó siung-chan ke,yîn-vi Kì thâng ngài khiù lìn-mén ke sâng.7 Song-chú he ngài ke li̍t-liong, ngài ke tún-phài;ngài chhiòn-sîm sin-kho Kì, chhiu tet-tó pông-chhu.Ngài ke sîm fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k,ngài oi chhong-kô kám-chhia Kì!8 Song-chú he Kì chṳ́-mìn ke li̍t-liong;Kì pí-yu chṳ́n-kiu Kì só sién-li̍p ke vòng.9 Song-chú â, chṳ́n-kiu Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn,su-fuk pûn su̍k Ngì chhṳ-kâ ke ngìn.Khiù Ngì mu̍k-yông kì-têu,fù-chhṳ̀ kì-têu, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.
