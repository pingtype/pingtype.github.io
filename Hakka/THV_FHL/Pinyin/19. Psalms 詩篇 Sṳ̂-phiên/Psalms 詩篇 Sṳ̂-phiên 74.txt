1 Â-sat ke kau-hiun-sṳ̂.Vi Koet-nan Khì-tó Song-ti â, Ngì cho-má-ke fit-thet ngài-têu án-kiú?Ngì oi yún-yén tui Ngì mu̍k-yông ke yòng-khiùn khién-to fat-fó mâ?2 Oi ki-tet Ngì kú sṳ̀-thoi kién-sién ke chṳ́-mìn,chhiu-he Ngì só-su̍k, kûi Ngì chhṳ-kâ ke mìn-chhu̍k;khiù Ngì ki-ngiam Ngì sṳt he̍t-ko ke Siak-ôn-sân.3 Ngài-têu ke sù-thi̍t yí-kîn chiông sṳn-thien fí-me̍t thet lô;khiù Ngì sái kiok-phu ko-lòi khon liá fông-liòng ke só-chhai.4 Ngì ke sù-thi̍t chhai Ngì ke sṳn-thien tú thai-sâng hêm;kì-têu chiông kì-têu tet-sṳn ke khì-è khià hí-lòi.5 Kì-têu chhiong chám su-é ke ngìn,nâ-tén pú-thèu kín-phiak kín-chám.6 Sṳn-thien-tú só-yû tiâu-khiet ke chhû-tún,chhiòn-phu yung pú-thèu lâu thiet-chhùi khau-thet.7 Kì-têu hàn piong-fó sêu Ngì ke sṳn-thien;kì-têu siet-thu̍k kin-pai Ngì ke só-chhai, chhak-to tap-tap.8 Kì-têu kiet-sîm kóng: "Ngài-têu lòi chiông kì-têu chha̍t-tái sêu-me̍t";kì-têu chhiu chiông liá thi-fông tak-kiên sṳn-só sêu chhiang-chhiang.9 Sṳ̀n-sṳn ke ki-ho chhiòn-phu m̀ kien-thet lé;ya mò yit-ke siên-tî lé;ngài-têu tông-chûng mò-ngìn tî liá châi-nan oi-to kí-sṳ̀.10 Song-ti â, sù-thi̍t seu Ngì oi-to kí-sṳ̀ nè?Kám-kóng thi̍t-ngìn voi yún-yén siet-thu̍k Ngì ke miàng he-mò?11 Ngì cho-má-ke m̀ chhûn yu-sú pông-chhu ngài-têu?Khiù Ngì tui fài-chûng chhûn-sú sêu-me̍t kì-têu.12 Song-ti â, chhṳ-kú yî-lòi Ngì he ngài ke vòng;chhai liá sṳ-kiên, sṳ̂-hàng chṳ́n-kiu ke chhiu-he Ngì.13 Ngì yung thai-nèn fûn-khôi hói-súi;Ngì tá-lan hói-koai ke thèu-nà.14 Ngì chiông thai hói-koai( 68 74:14 "thai hói-koai": Kú sṳ̀-thoi Khiun-tûng chhòn-sot chûng ke thai koai-vu̍t, thoi-péu fun-lon lâu sià-ok. Liá koai-vu̍t yû kí-chak thèu-nà.) ke thèu-nà tá-to mièn-mièn,chiông kì ke ngiuk pûn-pûn khóng-yâ ke thung-vu̍t( 69 74:14 "khóng-yâ ke thung-vu̍t" ya-he yi̍t-cho "khóng-yâ ke ngìn-mìn" ya-he "chhô sòn-è ke ngìn".) sṳ̍t.15 He Ngì sṳ́ chhièn-súi kín yúng-tén chhut-lòi;he Ngì sṳ́ thai hò-pa ke súi châu-thet.16 Ngit-ya ke pien-fa he Ngì ke chṳ́-yi;ngit-thèu lâu ngie̍t-kông he Ngì phài-lie̍t ke.17 Thai-thi ke kie-han he Ngì sat-li̍p ke;ngie̍t-thiên lâu hòn-thiên he Ngì ôn-phài ke.18 Song-chú â, m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet Ngì ke sù-thi̍t sâu-nat Ngì;m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet mò kin-khièn ke ngìn ngióng-pân méu-sṳ Ngì ke miàng.19 M̀-hó chiông Ngì sùn-liòng ke chṳ́-mìn( 70 74:19 "Ngì sùn-liòng ke chṳ́-mìn": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Ngì ngie̍t-kap-è ke sin-miang".) kâu-pûn chhàn-phau ke thi̍t-ngìn;m̀-hó yún-yén m̀ ki-tet Ngì liá-têu su ap-pet ke chṳ́-mìn.20 Khiù Ngì ki-ngiam Ngì thùng ngài-têu só-li̍p ke khie-yok,yîn-vi thi-song vû-am ke só-chhai chhûng-mân phau-li̍t.21 Khiù Ngì mo̍k pûn su ap-pet ke ngìn tù-tó vú-yu̍k;oi pûn khiùng-khú mò-ngìn pông-chhu ke ngìn siung-chan Ngì.22 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì hong-hí-lòi chên-chhí Ngì chhṳ-kâ ke chṳn-ngi;khiù Ngì m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet mò kin-khièn ke ngìn kûi-ngit seu Ngì.23 M̀-hó m̀ ki-tet Ngì ke sù-thi̍t tet-yi ke seu-sâng;m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet Ngì ke thi̍t-ngìn mò-thìn ke chhàu-nau.
