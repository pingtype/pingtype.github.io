1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Song-ti Chhóng-chho ke Yùng-kông Phû-thiên chhòn-yòng Song-ti ke yùng-kông;kiûng-chhông hién-mìn Kì sú só-cho ke.2 Ngit-ngit kóng-hí,ya-ya chhòn-yòng;3 m̀-sṳ́ ngièn-ngî,m̀-sṳ́ yîm-hióng.4 Kì-têu ke sin-sit( 16 19:4 "sin-sit" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "sok-è".) chhòn-to ngìn-kiên;kì-têu ke fa-ngî chhòn-phien thiên-ha.Song-ti chhai thiên-táng vi-tó ngit-thèu tap chong-phùng;5 ngit-thèu chhiong sîn-lòng fôn-fôn hí-hí chhut thung-fòng,yu he chhiong yúng-sṳ khoai-khoai lo̍k-lo̍k chéu-siông-kiuk.6 Kì tui thiên ke liá-phién chhut-lòi,tîn-to thiên ke ke-phién hi;van-vu̍t chhiòn-phu tet-tó kì ke ngie̍t-hi.7 Song-chú ke Kau-sṳ Song-chú ke kau-sṳ vàn-chhiòn,voi sṳ́ ngìn ke sâng-miang von-sîn;Song-chú ke fap-lin sin-sṳ̍t,voi pûn tân-sùn-ngìn tet-tó chṳ-fi;8 Song-chú ke kau-hiun kûng-chṳn,voi sṳ́ ngìn ke sîm yû khoai-lo̍k;Song-chú ke kie-min chhîn-mìn,voi pûn ngìn ke muk-chû kông-mìn.9 Kin-vi Song-chú he sùn-kiet ke sṳ( 17 19:9 "Kin-vi Song-chú he sùn-kiet ke sṳ": Yû-têu Chûng-vùn yi̍t-pún nang-ngoi fân-cho "Song-chú ke tho-lî he sùn-kiet ke".),yún-yén chhùn-liù;Song-chú ke fap-chet chṳn-chhṳ̍t,lûng-chúng kûng-tong.10 Liá-têu pí kîm-é kha pó-kui,yàng-ko song-tén ke kîm-é;pí phûng-thòng kha-thiàm,yàng-ko chui sùn ke phûng-thòng.11 Liá-têu pûn Ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn mìn-pha̍k( 18 19:11 "mìn-pha̍k" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhṳ̂m-su kín-kie".);séu-sîm chûn-sú, hó-chhu vù-han.12 Chúng-he má-ngìn voi khon-tó chhṳ-kâ ke m̀-chho̍k â?Khiù Ngì sa-miên ngài vù-yi-chûng só-fam ke ko-sṳt.13 Khiù Ngì sṳ́ Ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn mo̍k ku-yi fam-chhui,mo̍k pûn chhui-ok khúng-chṳ ngài.Án-ngiòng, ngài chang voi cho yit-ke mò-hó hiàm ke ngìn,miên-tet fam phoi-ngia̍k ke thai-chhui.14 Song-chú — ngài ke phàn-sa̍k, ngài ke kiu-su̍k-chá â,ngien ngài ke ngièn-hàng sṳ̂-sióng tet-tó Ngì ke kam-na̍p.
