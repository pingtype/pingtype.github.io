1 Thai-ví chhai Yù-thai khóng-yâ ke sṳ̀ só-cho ke sṳ̂.Hot-mu Song-ti Song-ti â, Ngì he ngài ke Song-ti;ngài pet-chhiet chhìm-khiù Ngì!Chhiong thiên-hôn, châu-châu, mò-súi ke fông-thi,ngài ke sîm-sṳ̀n sióng-ngiam Ngì, hot-mu Ngì.2 Ngì sṳt pûn ngài chhai sṳn-só chhèu-kien Ngì,pûn ngài khon Ngì ke thai-nèn lâu yùng-kông.3 Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi pí sâng-miang kha-hó;só-yî ngài ke choi oi chhong-kô siung-chan Ngì.4 Ngài yit-sên-ngìn lûng-chúng oi kám-chhia Ngì;ngài oi khià-hí sûng-sú tui Ngì khì-tó.5 Ngài ke sîm-sṳ̀n voi chhin mân-chiuk, chhiong sṳ̍t phông-phai;ngài ke choi oi tui Ngì ngìm-chhong fôn-lo̍k siung-chan ke sṳ̂-kô.6 Ngài chhai mìn-chhòng hong sṳ̂-ngiam Ngì;ngài kûi am-pû kín sióng-ngiam Ngì.7 Yîn-vi Ngì sṳ̀-sòng pông-chhu ngài,ngài chhai Ngì ke yi̍t-kap hâ fôn-hí chhong-kô.8 Ngài ke sîm kiâm-kiâm thèn-tén Ngì;Ngì ke yu-sú ya fù-chhṳ̀ ngài.9 Ke-têu chhìm kî-fi sat-hoi ngài ke,kì-têu lûng-chúng voi lo̍k yîm-kiên hi.10 Kì-têu voi sí chhai tô-kiam hâ,sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ pûn yâ-kiéu khie chhiang-chhiang.11 Chúng-he, vòng voi yîn-vi Song-ti fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k;tui Song-ti hí-ngien ke lûng-chúng voi chan-mî Kì;ke-sṳ̀, kóng fâ-liâu ke choi yit-thin voi set-he̍t.
