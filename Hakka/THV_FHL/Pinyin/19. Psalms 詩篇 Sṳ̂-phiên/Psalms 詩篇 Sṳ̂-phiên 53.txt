1 Thai-ví ke kau-hiun-sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung Pî-ôi-khìm thiau.Ngìn ke Sià-ok( Sṳ̂-phiên 14 ) Ngong-ngìn sîm-nui kóng:"Mò Song-ti!"Kì-têu chhin fú-phai, chôn-mùn cho khó-vu ke sṳ;kì-têu mò yit-sà hàng-san.2 Song-ti tui thiên-táng khon sṳ-kiên-ngìn,oi khon kám-yû mìn-lî ke ngìn,yû chhìm-khiù Song-ti ke ngìn mò.3 Chúng-he, kì-têu chhiòn-phu hàng phiên-chhâ,lûng-chúng khiung-yong fú-phai.Kì-têu mò-ngìn hàng-san,lièn yit-ke me-mò.4 Song-ti kóng: "Cho fái-sṳ ke ngìn ngióng-pân án-mò chṳ-sṳt?Kì-têu thûn-sṳ̍t Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn, chhiu-chhiong sṳ̍t-fan yit-yong;kì-têu chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt khiù-mun Ngài."5 Chúng-kóng, kì-têu voi kiâng-to pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat,kîn-li̍t chhiùng-lòi màng-sṳt yû ke chho̍k-kiâng;yîn-vi Song-ti oi tá-san Kì chṳ́-mìn sù-thi̍t ke hài-kut.Kì oi hi-chhie̍t kì-têu, sṳ́ kì-têu sṳt-mong lau-khui.6 Ngien Yî-set-lie̍t ke chṳ́n-kiu tui Siak-ôn chhut-lòi!Song-ti fì-fu̍k Kì chṳ́-mìn ke sán-ngia̍p ke-sṳ̀,Ngâ-kok ke chṳ́-sûn oi thai fôn-hí,Yî-set-lie̍t ke ngìn-mìn oi khoai-lo̍k.
