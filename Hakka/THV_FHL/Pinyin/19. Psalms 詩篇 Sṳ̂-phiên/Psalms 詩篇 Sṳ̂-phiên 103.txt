1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂.Song-ti ke Thung-siak Ngài ke sîm â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!Ngài ke sṳ̂n-sîm-lìn oi siung-chan Kì ke sṳn-miàng!2 Ngài ke sîm â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!M̀-hó m̀ ki-tet Kì yit-chhiet ke ên-fi!3 Kì sa-miên ngài só-yû ke chhui-ok;Kì yî-hó ngài yit-chhiet ke phiang-thung.4 Kì kiu ngài thot-lì sí-mòng;Kì yung chhṳ̀-oi lâu lìn-mén cho ngài ke miên-liù.5 Kì yung chui-hó ke tûng-sî lòi mân-chiuk ngài,pûn ngài yit-chhṳ̍t chhiâng-chhûn, yúng-khien chhiong yîn-tiâu.6 Song-chú thoi su ap-pet ke sṳ̂n-yên;Kì vi-tó kì-têu sṳ̂n-chông kûng-ngi.7 Kì tui Mô-sî khí-sṳ Kì ke kie-va̍k,pûn Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn tî Kì ke chok-vì.8 Song-chú yû lìn-mén lâu ên-tién,mò sùi-phien fat-khién, mân-yû put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi.9 Kì m̀-voi yit-chhṳ̍t chit-phi;Kì m̀-voi yún-yén pot-at.10 Kì mò cheu ngài-têu ke chhui-ko chit-fa̍t ngài-têu;Kì ya mò yîn-vi ngài-têu ke chhui-ok po-yin ngài-têu.11 Thiên lì thi yû kí-kô,tui kin-vi Kì ke ngìn, Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi ya yû kí-thai!12 Tûng lì sî yû kí-yén,Kì sṳ́ ngài-têu ke ko-fam lì-khôi ngài-têu ya yû án-yén!13 Â-pâ he ngióng-pân thung-siak chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-ńg,Song-chú ya ngióng-pân thung-siak kin-vi Kì ke ngìn.14 Kì tî ngài-têu ke pún-chṳt;Kì ki-tet ngài-têu put-ko he nài cho ke.15 Lun-tó sṳ-ngìn, kì-têu ke sâng-miang chhiu-chhiong chhó.Kì-têu chhiong yâ-thi ke fâ, sâng-to án-chiâng;16 pûn fûng yit chhôi chhiu mò-thet hi,mò-ngìn chai chhìm tet-tó kì.17 Chúng-he Song-chú ke chhṳ̀-oi kûi-pûn kin-vi Kì ke ngìn, tui song-kú to yún-yén;Kì ke kûng-ngi ya kûi-pûn kì-têu ke chṳ́-chṳ́ sûn-sûn,18 chhiu-he ke-têu sṳ̀n-sîm sú Kì ke khie-yok,chûng-sṳ̍t chûn-hàng Kì kie-min ke ngìn.19 Song-chú chhai thiên-táng sat pó-chho;Kì cho vòng thúng-chhṳ só-yû ke yit-chhiet.20 Thai-yû nèn-li̍t ke thiên-sṳ́ â,thâng Kì min-lin, sun-chhiùng Kì chṳ́-yi ke sṳ́-chá â,ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-chú!21 Thiên-táng yit-chhiet yû khièn-nèn,chûn-hàng Kì chṳ́-yi ke nù-phu̍k â,ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-chú!22 Yit-chhiet Kì só chhóng-chho ke â,oi siung-chan Song-chú!Oi chhai Kì thúng-chhṳ ke tak só-chhai siung-chan Kì!Ngài ke sîm â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!
