1 Thai-ví chhai Â-pí-mí-le̍t mien-chhièn cha-tiên, pûn kì kiuk-chéu heu só-cho ke sṳ̂.Siung-chan Song-chú ke Mî-san Ngài oi sòng-sòng siung-chan Song-chú;ngài ke choi oi yit-chhṳ̍t chan-mî Kì.2 Ngài ke sîm oi yîn-vi Song-chú lòi khoâ-khiéu;ngien su pet-hoi ke ngìn thâng-liáu chhiu fôn-hí.3 Lòi thùng ngài khiung-ha chhòn-yòng Song-chú ke ví-thai;ngài-têu khiung-ha lòi siung-chan Kì chṳ-kô ke miàng.4 Ngài chhìm-khiù Song-chú, Kì chhiu en ngài,kiu ngài thot-lì yit-chhiet ke chho̍k-kiâng.5 Ngióng-mong Kì ke ngìn chhiu yû kông-mìn;kì-têu m̀-voi chai mân-mien kien-seu.6 Liá khun-khú-ngìn khiù-ham, Song-chú chhiu chhùi-thâng,kiu kì thot-lì yit-chhiet ke fam-nan.7 Song-chú ke sṳ́-chá pó-yu kin-vi Kì ke ngìn,chhai kì-têu chû-vì chat-yàng lòi chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu.8 Song-chú ke mî-san ngì-têu oi sòng khon-é!Yí-kho Kì ke ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-yû fuk-hi!9 Song-chú ke sṳn-mìn â, oi kin-vi Song-chú!Kin-vi Kì ke ngìn mò-khiam pan-hong.10 Chhiu-he sṳ̂-é ya yû khiam-khiet lâu tú-kî ke sṳ̀-chiet,chúng-he chhìm-khiù Song-chú ke ngìn má-ke hó-chhu tû m̀-voi khiam.11 Heu-sâng-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi lòi thâng ngài kóng!Ngài oi kâu ngì-têu ngióng-pân kin-vi Song-chú.12 Yû-ngìn oi-siak sâng-miang mò?Chûi-khiù chhòng-su fuk-lo̍k mò?13 Mo̍k pûn ngì ke sa̍t-mà chhut ok-ngièn;mo̍k pûn ngì ke choi-sùn kóng kúi-cha.14 Oi hi-chhie̍t sià-ok, cho hó-sṳ;oi chûi-khiù fò-phìn, sat-mâng hàng.15 Song-chú ke muk-chû khon-ku ngi-ngìn;Kì ke ngí-kûng thâng kì-têu khiù-ham.16 Song-chú tui cho fái-sṳ ke ngìn pien-mien,oi tui sṳ-kiên chhù-thet kì-têu ke miàng.17 Ngi-ngìn khiù-ham Song-chú, Kì chhiu thâng,kiu kì-têu thot-lì yit-chhiet fam-nan.18 Song-chú chhîn-khiun fôi-sîm sṳt-chṳ ke;Kì chṳ́n-kiu sîm-lìn su-khú ke ngìn.19 Ngi-ngìn yû chhin-tô khú-nan,chúng-he Song-chú oi kiu kì thot-lì liá yit-chhiet.20 Song-chú oi pó-chhiòn kì ke kut-thèu,lièn yit-kî ya m̀-voi pûn-ngìn tá-thôn.21 Chhui-ok voi hoi-sí sià-ok ngìn;hen ngi-ngìn ke oi su chit-fa̍t.22 Song-chú oi kiu-su̍k Kì phu̍k-ngìn ke sin-miang;yí-kho Kì ke ngìn chhiòn-phu m̀-voi su chit-fa̍t.
