1 Yî-sṳ̂-lâ-ngìn Yî-tham ke kau-hiun-sṳ̂.Koet-nan chûng ke Sṳ̂-kô Song-chú â, ngài oi yún-yén kô-chhong Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi;ngài oi khôi-choi chhòn-yòng Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t chhṳ̍t-to van-thoi.2 Ngài kóng, Ngì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn;Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t kiên-thin, chhiong thiên yit-yong.3 Ngì kóng: "Ngài sṳt thùng Ngài só kién-sién ke ngìn li̍p-yok;Ngài sṳt yin-hí Ngài ke phu̍k-ngìn Thai-ví kóng:4 ‘Ngài oi sṳ́ ngì ke heu-thoi yún-yén cho vòng;Ngài oi kiên-ku ngì ke vòng-vi to van-thoi.’ "5 Song-chú â, chû-thiên siung-chan Ngì khì-meu ke chok-vì;sṳn-chá chhai thiên-thìn chhong-kô chan-mî Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t.6 Thiên-táng kám yû thùng Song-chú pí et-tet ke?Sṳ̀n ke chung-chṳ́( 81 89:6 "Sṳ̀n ke chung-chṳ́" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Thiên-sṳ́" ya-he "Khì-thâ ke sṳ̀n".) tông-chûng mò yit-ke cho-tet thùng Song-chú pài khiung-ha.7 Chhai sṳn-chá ke chhi-fi chûng, Song-ti chui su chûn-chhung;kì-têu chhai Kì ke chû-vì, chhiòn-phu kin-vi Kì.8 Chú Song-ti — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi â,má-ngìn chhiong Ngì án-yû khièn-nèn?Song-chú â, Ngì chhai só-yû ke sṳ-chhìn song hién-chhut Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t.9 Ngì kón-chṳ thai-hói;Ngì phìn-chhin pô-long.10 Ngì tá-sui hói-koai Lâ-ha̍p-pak( 82 89:10 "Lâ-ha̍p-pak": Kú sṳ̀-thoi Khiun-tûng chhòn-sot chûng ke hói-koai; kì thoi-péu fun-lon lâu sià-ok ke li̍t-liong.), chhṳ̀-sí kì;Ngì yung thai khièn-nèn ke sú tá-san Ngì ke sù-thi̍t.11 Thiên he Ngì ke, thi ya khiung-yong he Ngì ke;sṳ-kie lâu khì-chûng ke van-vu̍t he Ngì chhóng-chho ke.12 Tui nàm-khi̍t to pet-khi̍t, chhiòn-phu he Ngì chho ke;Thâ-phok-sân, Het-mùn-liâng lûng-chúng tui Ngì fôn-fû.13 Ngì ke sú-kut ngióng-pân yû-li̍t!Ngì ke khièn-nèn ngióng-pân khiòng-thai!14 Ngì ke koet he yung chṳn-ngi lâu kûng-tong cho kîn-kî;Ngì só-cho ke yit-chhiet hién-chhut chhṳ̀-oi lâu sin-sṳ̍t.15 Yung sṳ̂-kô kin-pai Ngì ke ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi!Song-chú â, kì-têu sên-fa̍t chhai Ngì ke kông-mìn chûng.16 Yîn-vi Ngì, kì-têu kûi-ngit fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k;kì-têu só siung-chan ke he Ngì ke kûng-ngi.17 Ngì he kì-têu ke yùng-kông lâu li̍t-liong;Ngì sṳ̂-ên, ngài-têu chhiu tet-tó sṳn-li.18 Ngài-têu ke tún-phài sṳ̍t-chhai he Song-chú kién-sién ke;ngài-têu ke vòng he Yî-set-lie̍t ke Sṳn-chá sat-li̍p ke.19 Song-ti Pûn Thai-ví ke Yin-hí Ke-sṳ̀, Ngì chhai yi-siong chûng tui Ngì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn kóng:"Ngài yí-kîn chiông miên-liù su-pûn yit-ke chhut-miàng ke yúng-sṳ;Ngài chiông vòng-vi su-pûn Ngài tui ngìn-mìn chûng sién chhut-lòi ke ngìn.20 Ngài yí-kîn kién-sién Ngài ke phu̍k-ngìn Thai-ví,yung sṳn-yù kò kì, li̍p kì cho ngì-têu ke vòng.21 Ngài ke nèn-li̍t oi sṳ̀-sòng thùng kì khiung-ha;Ngài ke khièn-nèn voi sṳ́ kì pien chhin khiòng-chong.22 Kì ke sù-thi̍t yún-yén mò phan-fap yàng kì;sià-ok ke ngìn ya mò fap-thu hoi-tó kì.23 Ngài oi tá-sui thùng kì chok-tui ke ngìn;Ngài oi tá-vang yit-chhiet hen kì ke ngìn.24 Ngài oi yung sin-sṳ̍t lâu chhṳ̀-oi khoán-thai kì;kho Ngài ke miàng, kì chhiu voi tet-tó sṳn-li.25 Ngài oi sṳ́ kì ke koet-thú kín-lòi kín-thai,tui Thi-chûng-hói to Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò.26 Kì voi tui Ngài kóng:‘Ngì he ngâ Pâ, ngài ke Song-ti;Ngì he chṳ́n-kiu ngài ke phàn-sa̍k!’27 Ngài oi sat-li̍p kì cho chóng-chṳ́,cho sṳ-kiên chui ví-thai ke vòng.28 Ngài tui kì ke chhṳ̀-oi m̀-voi kói-pien;Ngài thùng kì li̍p ke yok yún-yén yû-háu.29 Ngài oi sṳ́ kì ke heu-thoi yún-yén chóng-khièn;kì ke pó-chho voi chhiong thiên án-ngiòng chhòng-chhùn.30 "Kì ke heu-thoi na hi-chhie̍t Ngài ke kau-sṳ,hàng-sṳ vì-ngìn m̀ chûn-cheu Ngài ke fap-chet,31 kì-têu na vì-phoi Ngài ke kûi-chet,kì-têu na m̀ sú Ngài ke kie-min,32 Ngài chhiu oi yung kun-é lòi chit-fa̍t kì-têu ke ko-fam;Ngài ya oi yung piên-é lòi chit-fa̍t kì-têu ke chhui-ngiak.33 Chúng-he, Ngài tui Thai-ví ke chhṳ̀-oi m̀-voi sû-fì;Ngài tui kì ke sin-sṳ̍t yún-yén m̀-voi pien-chṳt.34 Ngài m̀-voi fi-thet thùng kì só-li̍p ke yok,ya m̀-voi kói-pien Ngài pûn kì ke yin-hí.35 "Ngài chṳ́ chhṳ-kâ ke sṳn-miàng fat-sṳ,Ngài fat-sṳ yit-pái yún-yén yû-háu:Ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi khî-phien Thai-ví!36 Kì ke heu-thoi voi yit-thoi chhòn yit-thoi, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén;Ngài oi khon-ku kì ke pó-chho, pûn kì chhiong ngit-thèu chhòng-chhùn.37 Kì ke pó-chho voi chhiong ngie̍t-kông án kiên-thin;ngie̍t-kông he thiên-khûng chûng-sṳ̍t ke kien-chṳn-chá."38 Than-sit Vòng ke Sṳt-phai Kiet-kó Ngì sa khî-chhie̍t kì, piong-thet kì;Ngì fî-sòng khién Ngì só sién-li̍p ke vòng.39 Ngì chhí-sêu thùng Ngì phu̍k-ngìn só-li̍p ke yok;Ngì chiông kì ke miên-liù fit-lo̍k nam-nài tú.40 Ngì chhak-thet kì só-yû ke sàng-chhiòng,sṳ́ kì ke sàng-pó sàng-cho fông-thi.41 Ko-lu ke ngìn chhiòn-phu chhióng kì;lìn-koet ya siông-châng vú-yu̍k kì.42 Ngì sṳ́ kì ke sù-thi̍t lûng-chúng tá-yàng kì;Ngì pûn kì ke thi̍t-ngìn chhiòn-phu chṳ̂n fôn-hí.43 Ngì sṳ́ kì ke vú-hi sṳt-háu,pûn kì chhai chan-chhòng khî m̀-he̍t.44 Ngì chhióng-chéu kì ke khièn-chhóng( 83 89:44 "khièn-chhóng": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "kông-fî".),chiông kì ke pó-chho súng-vang.45 Ngì sṳ́ kì vi-ló siên-sôi;Ngì pûn kì su-chhin yit-chhiet ke siû-yu̍k.46 Khién-khiù Song-ti Théu-piong Song-chú â, Ngì khong-tén m̀-tap ngài-têu, oi to yún-yén mâ?Ngì ke nu-hi chhiong fó tông-chho̍k, oi to kí-sṳ̀ nè?47 Khiù Ngì oi ki-tet ngài ke ngìn-sên he kí-tón;Ngì só chhóng-chho ke ngìn-lui he ngióng-pân hî-khûng!48 Nai yû-ngìn chhòng-sên put-ló nè?Má-ngìn thò tet-ko yîm-kiên ke khièn-sṳ nè?49 Chú â, Ngì yung sin-sṳ̍t hiong Thai-ví só yin-hí ke,Ngì siên-chhièn ke chhṳ̀-oi chhai nai-vi?50 Chú â, khiù Ngì m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet Ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn só sṳ̀n-su ke siû-yu̍k;khiù Ngì m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet ngài ngióng-pân ngiûn-su ngoi-pâng ke khî-fu( 84 89:50 "khî-fu": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chung-ngìn".).51 Song-chú â, Ngì ke sù-thi̍t vú-yu̍k Ngì sat-li̍p ke vòng;mò-lun kì hi-to nai-vi, kì-têu chúng-he vú-yu̍k kì.52 Ngien Song-chú yún-yén tet-tó siung-chan!Â-mén! Â-mén!
