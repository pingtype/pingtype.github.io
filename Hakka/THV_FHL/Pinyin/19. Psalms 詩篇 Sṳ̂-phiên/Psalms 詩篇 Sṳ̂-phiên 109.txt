1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Fam-nan chûng ke Khiù-ham Ngài só siung-chan ke Song-ti â,khiù Ngì m̀-hó yit-chhṳ̍t tiâm-tiâm.2 Sià-ok kúi-cha ke ngìn kûng-kit ngài.Kì-têu kóng fâ-liâu lòi fí-pong ngài,3 yung ok-thu̍k ke fa lòi chung-sông ngài;kì-têu mò-yèn mò-ku kûng-kit ngài.4 Ngài siak kì-têu, vi kì-têu khì-tó,chúng-he, kì-têu sa thùng ngài chok-tui.5 Kì-têu yung-ok po-san,yung yen-hen po oi-sîm.6 Khiù Ngì phai mò kûng-chṳn ke fap-kôn lòi sṳ́m-phan ngài ke sù-thi̍t,yung yit-ke thùng kì chok-tui ke ngìn lòi ko kì.7 Ngien kì su sṳ́m-phan, pûn-ngìn thin-chhui;ngien kì ke khì-tó ya son fam-chhui!8 Ngien kì ke su-miang suk-tón,phe̍t-ngìn chhióng kì ke chṳt-vi!9 Ngien kì ke chṳ́-ńg pien kû-yì,kì ke pû-ngiòng sàng-cho koá-fu!10 Ngien kì ke chṳ́-ńg liù-long cho khiet-sṳ̍t,tui khiet-sṳ̍t-liàu tî-poi pûn-ngìn kiuk chhut-lòi( 100 109:10 "pûn-ngìn kiuk chhut-lòi" he cheu yit-pún kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chhut-hi chhìm".)!11 Ngien chai-chú nâ-chéu kì só-yû ke chhòi-sán,sâng-fun-ngìn chhióng-chéu kì lò-luk só-tet ke!12 Ngien kì tet m̀-tó ngìn ke chhṳ̀-pî,kì ke kû-yì tet m̀-tó lìn-mén!13 Ngien kì ke heu-thoi chhiòn-phu chhie̍t-thet,kì ke siang chhòn m̀-to thi-ngi thoi!14 Ngien Song-chú ki-tén kiâ chú-siên ke chhui-ok,mo̍k m̀ ki-tet kiâ mê ke ko-fam!15 Ngien Song-chú yún-yén ki-tén kì-têu ke chhui,sṳ́ sṳ-kiên-ngìn vàn-chhiòn m̀ ki-tet kì-têu!16 Yîn-vi kì chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt sióng-tó chhṳ̀-pî,chṳ́-yû tî ap-pet chhôi-ko ke khiùng-khú-ngìn,sṳm-chṳ sat-hoi sông-sîm mò yí-kho ke ngìn.17 Kì chhin-hau chu-chú, ngien chu-chú lìm-to kì!Kì m̀-hén chuk-fuk, ngien chuk-fuk yén-lì kì!18 Kì yung chu-chú lòi chông-phi chhṳ-kâ, chhiong chok ngoi-phàu;ngien chu-chú chhiong súi yit-yong liù-ngi̍p kì ke sṳ̂n-thí,chhiong yù án-ngiòng yit-chhṳ̍t theu-ngi̍p kì ke kut-thèu!19 Ngien chu-chú chhiong sâm án-ngiòng ngiàm-tén kì,chhiong yêu-tai yit-yong sòng-sòng khún-tén kì!20 Ngien Song-chú án-ngiòng chit-fa̍t ngài ke-têu tui-thi̍t,po-yin ke-têu kóng fái-fa kûng-kit ngài ke ngìn.21 Chúng-he, Chṳ-kô ke Song-chú â,khiù Ngì yîn-vi Ngì ke miàng lòi pông-chhu ngài,cheu Ngì chṳ-san ke chhṳ̀-oi lòi chṳ́n-kiu ngài!22 Yîn-vi ngài khiùng-khú mò yí-kho;ngài ke nui-sîm sông-thung chṳ-khi̍t.23 Ngài chhiong lìm-am ke ngit-yáng chhiam-chhiam sêu-sṳt,chhiong yit-chak chhó-máng yit-yong pûn-ngìn fin-thet.24 Ngài kim-sṳ̍t to chhit-thèu sôn-ngiôn,sṳ̂n-thí chheu-to chhûn phì-pâu-kut.25 Sù-thi̍t khon-tó ngài chhiu seu ngài;kì-têu kín yèu-thèu vú-yu̍k ngài.26 Song-chú — ngài ke Song-ti â, khiù Ngì pông-chhu ngài;khiù Ngì cheu Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi chṳ́n-kiu ngài!27 Pûn ngài ke sù-thi̍t tî liá he Ngì ke thai-nèn,liá-khien sṳ he Ngì — Song-chú chhṳ-kâ só-hàng ke.28 Chhiu-son kì-têu chu-chú ngài,Ngì hàn-he oi chuk-fuk ngài.Ngien pet-hoi ngài ke ngìn kien-seu( 101 109:28 "Ngien pet-hoi ngài ke ngìn kien-seu" he cheu yit-pún kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Kì-têu pet-hoi ngài fán-chón kien-seu".),Ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn sa tet-tó fôn-hí.29 Ngien ngài ke-têu sù-thi̍t chok-tén siû-yu̍k,pûn kien-seu chhiong ngoi-phàu án-ngiòng pâu-tén.30 Ngài oi chhut-sâng thai-thai kám-chhia Song-chú;ngài oi chhai thai chhi-fi chûng chan-mî Kì,31 yîn-vi Kì vi khiùng-khú ke ngìn sṳ̂n-yên,kiu kì thot-lì thin kì sí-chhui ke ngìn.
