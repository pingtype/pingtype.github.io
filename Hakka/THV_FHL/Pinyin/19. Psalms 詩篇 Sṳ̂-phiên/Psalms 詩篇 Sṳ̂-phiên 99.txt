1 Song-ti he Chṳ-kô ke Vòng Song-chú chóng-khièn!Van-mìn oi pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat!Kì chhô chhai kî-lu-pak pó-chho song,thai-thi oi yèu-thung!2 Song-chú chhai Siak-ôn thai-yû nèn-li̍t;Kì he chṳ-kô, chhêu-ko thiên-ha van-koet.3 Van-mìn oi siung-chan Kì ke miàng;Kì ke miàng ví-thai yu vî-ngiàm.Kì he chṳ-sṳn ke!4 Thai-nèn ke Vòng( 89 99:4 "Thai-nèn ke Vòng": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Vòng ke thai-nèn".) â, Ngì fôn-hí kûng-ngi;Ngì kien-li̍p kûng-phìn;Ngì chhai Yî-set-lie̍t-kâ sṳ̂n-chông kûng-ngi, vì-fu kûng-tho.5 Oi siung-chan Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti!Oi chhai Kì ke pó-chho chhièn khúi-pai!Kì he chṳ-sṳn ke!6 Chhai Kì ke chi-sṳ̂ chûng yû Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn,chhai khiù-ham Kì miàng ke ngìn chûng yû Sat-mû-ngí;kì-têu khiù Song-chú, Kì chhiu en kì-têu.7 Kì chhai yùn-chhû ke tî-poi tui kì-têu kóng-fa;kì-têu fu̍k-chhiùng Kì pân-pu ke fap-lin lâu kûi-chet.8 Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti â,Ngì thâng Ngì chṳ́-mìn ke khiù-ham;Ngì he sa-miên kì-têu ke Song-ti,chúng-he Ngì cheu kì-têu ke ko-fam po-yin kì-têu.9 Oi siung-chan Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti!Oi chhai Kì ke sṳn-sân táng kin-pai Kì!Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti he chṳ-sṳn ke!
