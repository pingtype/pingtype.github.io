1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Tet-sṳn ke Sṳ̂-kô Ngien Song-ti hong-hí-lòi, sṳ́ Kì ke sù-thi̍t si-san;ngien ke-têu yen-hen Kì ke tui Kì mien-chhièn thò-chéu.2 Ngien kì-têu pûn Song-ti kiuk-chéu,chhiu-chhiong yên pûn fûng chhôi-san yit-yong;ngien fái-ngìn chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn sêu-me̍t,chhiong la̍p tù-tó fó chhiu yùng-thet yit-yong.3 Chúng-he ngi-ngìn oi fôn-hí chhong-kô,chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.4 Oi tui Song-ti chhong-sṳ̂, siung-chan Kì ke miàng;oi vi-tó ke chhô-yùn ke( 60 68:4 "ke chhô-yùn ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sṳ́-chhâ ko khóng-yâ ke".) yi-phi tho-lu.Kì ke miàng he ôn-to Song-chú;ngì-têu oi chhai Kì mien-chhièn fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k!5 Song-ti he̍t chhai Kì ke sṳn-thien tú;Kì cho kû-yì ke Â-pâ, vi koá-fu sṳ̂n-yên.6 Song-ti sṳ́ kû-tân ke ngìn yû vuk-hâ hó-chón,sṳ́ pûn-ngìn koân ke tet-tó chhṳ-yù khoai-lo̍k;chúng-he phoi-ngia̍k ke he̍t chhai châu-hip ke thi.7 Song-ti â, Ngì chhai Ngì chṳ́-mìn ke thèu-chhièn hàng,Ngì tai-liâng kì-têu hàng-ko khóng-yâ ke sṳ̀-chiet,8 thai-thi chṳ́n-thung, thiên ya lo̍k-yí,yîn-vi Sî-nai ke Song-ti( 61 68:8 Song-ti chhṳ̂n-cho "Sî-nai ke Song-ti", yîn-vi Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhut Âi-khi̍p oi hi Kâ-nàm ke sṳ̀, Song-ti chhai Sî-nai-sân tui kì-têu khí-sṳ chhṳ-kâ (Chhut Âi-khi̍p Ki 19:16-25).) chhut-hien,Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti kong-lìm lé.9 Song-ti â, Ngì kong-hâ thai-yí;Ngì sṳ́ châu-châu ke thú-thi tet-tó chṳ̂-yun.10 Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn chhiu he̍t chhai ke-vi;Song-ti â, Ngì yung chhṳ̀-pî khoán-thai khiùng-khú-ngìn.11 Chú yit ha-lin,kûi-khiùn ke fu-ńg chhiu chhòn-kóng:12 "Ke-têu vòng lâu kiûn-chhui thò-chéu lé!"Chhai vuk-hâ ke fu-ńg pûn-tó chan-li-phín;13 yû pâu pha̍k-ngiùn ke ngie̍t-kap-è ke tiâu-siong,kì-têu ke yi̍t-kap yû vòng-kîm kín sám-kông.(Chan-chên chûng ngióng yû-ngìn khong chhai yòng-khiên tú?)14 Chhiòn-nèn ke Song-ti kiuk-san ke-têu vòng ke sṳ̀,Kì ham( 62 68:14 "Kì ham" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhiu-chhiong".) thai-siet lo̍k chhai Sat-mùn-sân táng-kô.15 Pâ-sân ke sân sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n chông-ngiàm;Pâ-sân-sân yû chhin-tô chho sân-fûng.16 Ngì-têu tô-fûng tô-liâng ke sân â,cho-má-ke chhit-tu Song-ti só-sién só-he̍t ke Siak-ôn-sân?Song-chú oi yún-yén he̍t chhai Siak-ôn sân-táng!17 Song-ti ke chan-chhâ yû chhiên-chhiên van-van;Chú tai kì-têu tui Sî-nai ngi̍p sṳn-só( 63 68:17 "Chú tai ... sṳn-só" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Chú chhai kì-têu tông-chûng, Sî-nai chhai sṳn-só".).18 Kì sṳ̂n-sông-hi to kô-kô ke só-chhai,tai-tén chhin-tô chok-lòi ke ngìn;Kì chiap-su ngìn hien ke lî-vu̍t,sṳm-chṳ phoi-ngia̍k-chá ke lî-vu̍t.Chú Song-ti oi he̍t chhai ke só-chhai.19 Oi siung-chan Chú, chhiu-he kiu ngài-têu ke Song-ti,yîn-vi Kì tak-ngit pà-tén ngài-têu ke chhûng-tâm.20 Ngài-têu ke Song-ti he chṳ́n-kiu ke Song-ti;Song-chú he kiu ngài-têu thot-lì sí-mòng ke Chú.21 Song-ti yit-thin oi tá-lan Kì sù-thi̍t ke thèu-nà,ke-têu ngang-ngang oi fam-chhui ke ngìn ke thèu-nà-hok.22 Chú kóng-ko:"Ngài oi chiông ngì-têu ke sù-thi̍t tui Pâ-sân tai-lòi;Ngài oi tai kì-têu tui chhṳ̂m-hói ke tông-chûng ko-lòi,23 hó-pûn ngì-têu tui kì-têu ke hiet-chûng hàng-tén ko,ya hó-pûn ngì-têu ke kiéu lòi sê kì-têu ke hiet."24 Song-ti â, thai-kâ lûng-chúng khon-tó Ngì tet-sṳn ke hòng-lie̍t;kì-têu khon-tén ngài ke Song-ti — ngài ke Vòng chin-ngi̍p sṳn-só.25 Sṳ̂-pân chhai-chhièn, ngo̍k-chhui chhai-heu,tá lìn-kú ke thùng-ńg chhai tûng-ông.26 Ngì-têu oi chhai thai chhi-fi chûng siung-chan Song-ti;Yî-set-lie̍t ke chṳ́-sûn â, ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-chú!27 Chui-se ke kî-chhu̍k Phien-ngâ-mén hàng chui thèu-chhièn,yèn-heu, Yù-thai ke chung liâng-chhiu lâu sùi-chhiùng thèn-tén,chiap-tén he Sî-pu-lùn lâu Nâ-fut-thâ-li ke chung liâng-chhiu.28 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì hién-chhut Ngì ke thai-nèn,chhiu-he Ngì vi-tó ngài-têu yung-ko ke nèn-li̍t!29 Yîn-vi Ngì chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke sṳn-thien,lie̍t-vòng oi tai lî-vu̍t hien-pûn Ngì.30 Khiù Ngì chit-fa̍t Âi-khi̍p — ke-chak lù-chuk chûng ke khìm-chhu;chit-fa̍t lie̍t-koet — ke-khiùn ngiù-kú lâu kì-têu ke se-ngiù,chhṳ̍t-to kì-têu tâi-thèu hòng-fu̍k, tui Ngì hien-song ngiùn-è.Khiù Ngì chiông ke-têu chhin hau siông-chhṳ̀ ke mìn-chhu̍k tá-san!31 Voi yû thai-sṳ́ tui Âi-khi̍p chhièn-lòi;Kú-sṳ̍t-ngìn voi khià-sú hien lî-vu̍t pûn Song-ti.32 Thi-song van-koet â, oi kô-siung Song-ti;ngì-têu oi chhong sṳ̂-kô lòi siung-chan Chú!33 Oi kô-siung ke chhô chhai van-kú kô-thiên song ke Chú!Thâng â, Kì fat-chhut ke sâng-yîm mân-yû khièn-nèn.34 Oi chhòn-yòng Song-ti ke thai khièn-nèn;Kì ke vî-ngiàm thai-ko Yî-set-lie̍t;Kì ke thai khièn-nèn chhûng-mân kiûng-chhông.35 Song-ti â, Ngì chhai Ngì ke sṳn-só chûng he chṳ̂n tet-ngìn kin-vi ke!Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti su li̍t-liong lâu khièn-nèn pûn Kì ke chṳ́-mìn.Ngì-têu oi siung-chan Song-ti!
