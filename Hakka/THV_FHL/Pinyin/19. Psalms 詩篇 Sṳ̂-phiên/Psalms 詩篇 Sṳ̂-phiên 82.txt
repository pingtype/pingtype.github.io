1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂.Song-ti He Chui-kô ke Thúng-chhṳ-chá Song-ti chú-chhṳ̀ thiên-thìn ke chhi-fi,chhai ke-têu sṳ̀n tông-chûng cho kiet-chhài.2 Kì kóng: "Ngì-têu oi thìn-chṳ́ mò kûng-phìn ke sṳ́m-phan!Ngì-têu khon fái-ngìn ke chhìn-mien oi to kí-sṳ̀ nè?3 Oi vì-fu khiùng-khú-ngìn lâu kû-yì ke khièn-li;tui-thai khun-khú lâu su ap-pet ke ngìn oi kûng-tong.4 Oi yên-chhu khiùng-khú lâu mò yí-kho ke ngìn,oi théu-piong kì-têu thot-lì fái-ngìn ke khî-fu.5 "Liá-têu sṳ̀n chhiòn-mò chṳ-sṳt ya mò mìn-pha̍k!Kì-têu chhai vû-am chûng hàng-lòi hàng-hi;sṳ-kiên chṳn-ngi ke kîn-kî chhiu yèu-thung.6 "Ngài láu-tó ngì-têu he sṳ̀n,chhiòn-phu he Chṳ-kô-chá ke lai-é.7 Chúng-he ngì-têu thùng sṳ-kiên-ngìn khiung-yong voi sí;ngì-têu voi chhiong tak-ke vòng-chṳ́ án-ngiòng vang-thet."8 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì hong-hí-lòi sṳ́m-phan sṳ-kie,yîn-vi van-koet chhiòn-phu he su̍k Ngì ke.
