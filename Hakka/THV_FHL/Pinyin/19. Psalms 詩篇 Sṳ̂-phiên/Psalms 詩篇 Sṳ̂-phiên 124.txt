1 Thai-ví sông-kîn ke sṳ̂.Song-ti he Chṳ́n-kiu-chá Yî-set-lie̍t â, ngì kóng khon-é!Song-chú na mò pông-chhu ngài-têu,ngì kóng, ngài-têu voi ngióng-pân nè?2 "Na m̀-he Song-chú pông-chhu ngài-têu,án-ngiòng, tông sù-thi̍t kûng-kit ngài-têu,3 tui ngài-têu thai fat-khién ke sṳ̀-chiet,chó chhiu chiông ngài-têu sâng-sâng thûn-thet;4 thai-súi chó chhiu chiông ngài-têu tá-chéu,pô-long chó chhiu chiông ngài-têu yim-ko,5 kip-liù chó chhiu yim-sí ngài-têu lô!"6 Ngài-têu oi kám-chhia Song-chú!Kì mò yù-chhai ngài-têu pûn sù-thi̍t sṳ̍t-thet.7 Ngài-têu chhiong tiâu-é tui lia̍p-ngìn ke lò-mióng thò-chéu;mióng-è lie̍t-thet, ngài-têu chhiu tet-tó chhṳ-yù.8 Ngài-têu ke pông-chhu tui Song-chú lòi;Kì he chhóng-chho thiên-thi ke Chú-chái.
