1 Kám-ên ke Sṳ̂-kô Oi kám-chhia Song-chú, yîn-vi Kì he chṳ-san;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.2 Oi kám-chhia van-sṳ̀n ke Song-ti;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.3 Oi kám-chhia van-chú ke Chú-chái;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.4 Vì-yû Kì nèn hàng thai sṳ̀n-chiak;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.5 Kì yung chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ-fi chhóng-chho chû-thiên;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.6 Kì chhai chhṳ̂m-súi ke táng-kô phû-sat thai-thi;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.7 Kì chhóng-chho ngit-thèu lâu ngie̍t-kông;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.8 Kì chho ngit-thèu kón-lî ngit-sṳ̀-thèu;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.9 Ngie̍t-kông lâu sên-sṳ̀n kón-lî am-pû-sṳ̀;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.10 Kì chhṳ̀-sí Âi-khi̍p só-yû thèu-thôi ke;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.11 Kì tai-liâng Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhut Âi-khi̍p;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.12 Kì chhûn-chhut thai-nèn ke sú lâu sú-pí;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.13 Kì fûn-khôi Fùng-hói ke súi;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.14 Kì tai-liâng Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn hàng ko-hi;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.15 Kì chiông Âi-khi̍p-vòng lâu kì ke kiûn-chhui yim-sí chhai Fùng-hói;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.16 Kì tai-liâng Kì ke chṳ́-mìn kîn-ko khóng-yâ;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.17 Kì chhṳ̀-sí ke-têu yúng-chong ke vòng;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.18 Kì chhṳ̀-sí ke-têu chhut-miàng ke vòng;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.19 Kì chhṳ̀-sí Â-mô-li-vòng Sî-fèn;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.20 Kì chhṳ̀-sí Pâ-sân-vòng Ngo̍k;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.21 Kì chiông kì-têu ke thú-thi su-pûn Kì ke chṳ́-mìn cho sán-ngia̍p;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.22 Kì chiông kì-têu ke thú-thi su-pûn Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn Yî-set-lie̍t;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.23 Ngài-têu sṳt-phai ke sṳ̀, Kì mò piong-thet ngài-têu;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.24 Kì kiu ngài-têu thot-lì sù-thi̍t ke sú;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.25 Kì su sṳ̍t-vu̍t pûn ngìn lâu thung-vu̍t;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.26 Oi kám-chhia thiên-táng ke Song-ti;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.
