1 Siung-chan Song-chú Hâ-li-lu-yá!Oi chhai Song-ti ke sṳn-só chan-mî Kì!Oi chhai hién-chhut Kì thai-nèn ke kiûng-chhông chan-mî Kì!2 Oi chan-mî Kì thai-nèn ke chok-vì!Oi chan-mî Kì ke chṳ-chûn vù-tui!3 Oi phùn yòng-kok lòi chan-mî Kì!Oi thàn-khìm cheu-sit chan-mî Kì!4 Oi tá-kú thiau-vú chan-mî Kì!Oi âi-hièn phùn-sêu chan-mî Kì!5 Oi yung tông-hióng ke thùng-lò chan-mî Kì!Oi yung thai-sâng ke chhê-é chan-mî Kì!6 Yit-chhiet yû sâng-miang ke â,ngì-têu oi chan-mî Song-chú!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
