1 Siung-chan ke Sṳ̂-kô Hâ-li-lu-yá!Ngì-têu oi chhong yit-sú sîn-kô lòi chan-mî Song-chú,chhai Kì chûng-sîm chṳ́-mìn ke chhi-fi chûng chan-mî Kì!2 Yî-set-lie̍t â, oi yîn-vi ngì-têu ke chhóng-chho Chú fôn-hí!Siak-ôn ke chṳ́-ńg â, oi yîn-vi ngì-têu ke Vòng khoai-lo̍k!3 Oi thiau-vú lòi chan-mî Kì ke miàng!Oi tá-kú thàn-khìm lòi kô-siung Kì!4 Song-chú fî-sòng fôn-hí Kì ke chṳ́-mìn;Kì chṳ́n-kiu khiâm-pî ke ngìn, su kì sṳn-li.5 Ngien Kì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn yîn-vi tet-sṳn khoai-lo̍k;ngien kì-têu theu-ya thai-sâng fôn-hí chhong-kô.6 Ngien kì-têu thai-sâng fôn-fû siung-chan Song-ti,kì-têu ke sú khià-tén lióng-piên li ke kiam,7 hó tui lie̍t-koet po yên-sù,hìn-fa̍t van-mìn,8 chiông kì-têu ke vòng yung thiet-lien khún hí-lòi,yung thiet sú-khau só-tén kì-têu ke thai-sṳ̀n,9 cheu Song-ti só-thin ke lòi hìn-fa̍t lie̍t-koet.Liá he cho Song-ti chûng-sîm chṳ́-mìn ke kông-yùng.Hâ-li-lu-yá!
