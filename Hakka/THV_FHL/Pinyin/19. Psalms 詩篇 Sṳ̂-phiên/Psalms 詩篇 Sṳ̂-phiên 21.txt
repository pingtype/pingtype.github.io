1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Kô-siung Sṳn-li Song-chú â, vòng yîn-vi Ngì su ke li̍t-liong fôn-hí;kì yîn-vi Ngì só-su ke sṳn-li fî-sòng khoai-lo̍k.2 Kì ke sîm-ngien, Ngì vi kì sṳ̀n-chhiòn;choi só-khiù ke, Ngì ya tap-yin kì.3 Ngì su-pûn kì khi̍t-thai ke hen-fuk chiap-thai kì,yu chiông kîm miên-liù tai chhai kì ke thèu-nà-táng.4 Kì tui Ngì khiù su-ngièn, Ngì chhiu su-pûn kì;Ngì sṳ́ kì chhòng-su, yèn-to yún-yún yén-yén.5 Kì yîn-vi Ngì só-su ke sṳn-li mân-yû yùng-kông;Ngì yu chiông chûn-yùng lâu vî-ngiàm kâ-pûn kì.6 Ngì su kì ke thai fuk-hi vù-khiùng vù-chhin;Ngì lâu kì thùng-chhai pûn kì chhûng-mân khoai-lo̍k.7 Yîn-vi vòng yû sin-kho Song-chú;thûng-ko Chṳ-kô-chá ke chhṳ̀-oi,kì ke vòng-khièn m̀-voi yèu-thung.8 Ngì oi chhut-sú tui-fu Ngì yit-chhiet ke sù-thi̍t;Ngì ke yu-sú oi chok ke-têu hen Ngì ke ngìn.9 Ngì yit chhut-hien, oi sṳ́ kì-têu chhiong lo̍k chhai fó-lù tî-poi.Song-chú oi thai fat-khién lòi thûn-me̍t kì-têu;lie̍t-fó oi chiông kì-têu sêu-to chhiang-chhiang.10 Ngì oi tui thi-song me̍t-chhin kì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn;Ngì oi tui ngìn-kiên chhṳ̀-thet kì-têu ke heu-thoi.11 Chhiu-son sù-thi̍t sat kúi-kie oi tui-fu Ngì,kì-têu ke yîm-mèu ya mò khó-nèn sṳ̀n-kûng.12 Ngì yit lâi-kiûng tui-chún kì-têu ke thèu-nà,kì-têu chhiu voi chón-sṳ̂n chéu-to chhiang-chhiang.13 Song-chú â, ngien Ngì hién-mìn Ngì ke li̍t-liong!Ngài-têu oi ngìm-sṳ̂ kô-siung Ngì ke khièn-nèn.
