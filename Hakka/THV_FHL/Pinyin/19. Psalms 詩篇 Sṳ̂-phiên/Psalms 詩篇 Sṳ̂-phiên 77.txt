1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂, cheu Yâ-thu-tun ke chhong-fap, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Khú-nan chûng ke Ôn-ví Ngài tui Song-ti chhut-sâng khiù-ham;ngài thai-sâng khiù Song-ti, khiù Kì chhùi-thâng.2 Fam-nan ke sṳ̀, ngài chhìm-khiù Chú;ngài kûi am-pû khià-sú khì-tó,ngài ke sîm m̀-hén su ngìn ke ôn-ví.3 Ngài sṳ̂-ngiam Song-ti chhiu kín than-sit;ngài tiâm-tiâm kín-sióng chhiu kín sông-sîm.4 Ngì sṳ́ ngài kûi am-pû soi m̀-lo̍k kau;ngài yù-sèu chhâu-fàn to kóng m̀-chhut fa.5 Ngài sióng-ngiam ko-hi ke ngit-è,sióng-hí siên-chhièn ke-têu ngièn-ngit.6 Ngài theu-ya kín-sióng( 72 77:6 "sióng" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chhong-kô".);ngài sîm-kôn kín-mun:7 "Kám-kóng Chú oi yún-yén fit-thet ngài-têu?Kì yún-yén mò-oi chai sṳ̂-ên he-mò?8 Kì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi chhiu yún-yén thìn-thet mâ?Kì ke yin-hí chhiu van-thoi lo̍k-khûng he-mò?9 Kám-kóng Song-ti yí-kîn m̀ ki-tet ngióng-pân sṳ̂-ên?Kám-kóng Kì ke lìn-mén pûn nu-hi set-thet he-mò?"10 Ngài chhiu kóng: "Liá he ngài chui sông-sîm ke sṳ-chhìn,Chṳ-kô-chá ke yu-sú yí-kîn mò khiung-yong lé."11 Song-chú â, ngài oi sióng-ngiam Ngì ví-thai ke chok-vì;ngài oi sióng-hí Ngì vông-sṳ̀ só sṳ̀n-chhiu ke khì-sṳ.12 Ngài oi mén-sióng Ngì yit-chhiet ke só-cho;ngài oi kín-ngiam Ngì thai-nèn ke chok-vì.13 Song-ti â, Ngì só-cho ke yit-chhiet he sṳn-kiet ke;khì-thâ ke sṳ̀n-mìn, nai yit-ke thùng Ngì pí-et-tet!14 Ngì he hàng sṳ̀n-chiak khì-sṳ ke Song-ti;Ngì hiong lie̍t-koet hién-chhut Ngì ke thai-nèn.15 Ngì yung thai-li̍t kiu-su̍k Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn,chṳ́n-kiu Ngâ-kok lâu Yok-sit ke heu-thoi.16 Song-ti â, hói-súi khon-tó Ngì chhiu chho̍k-kiâng;chhṳ̂m-yên kien-tó Ngì, khiung-yong ya kín-chhat.17 Vû-yùn kong-hâ thai-yí;thiên-khûng kín hióng lùi-kûng;ngiap-lang sa-lòi sa-hi.18 Ngì ke lùi-kûng lung-lung-kún;ngiap-lang cheu-kông chhiòn sṳ-kie;thai-thi kiâng-to kín-chûn kín-chhat.19 Ngì hàng-ko thai-hói, Ngì ye̍t-ko chhṳ̂m-yên,chúng-he mò-ngìn khon-tó Ngì ke kiok-chiak.20 Ngì sṳt thûng-ko Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn ke sú,chhiong mu̍k-chá án-ngiòng tai-liâng Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn.
