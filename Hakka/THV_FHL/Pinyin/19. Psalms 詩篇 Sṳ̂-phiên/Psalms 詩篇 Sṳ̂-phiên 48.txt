1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂-kô.Siak-ôn — Song-ti ke Sàng Song-chú chṳ-thai!Chhai ngài-têu Song-ti ke sàng-nui,chhai Kì ke sṳn-sân-táng,Kì yin-tông su chui-thai ke chan-mî!2 Song-ti ke Siak-ôn-sân chhin-chiâng chhin-kô;thai kiûn-vòng ke sàng sṳ́ van-mìn fôn-hí.3 Song-ti chhai sàng ke kiûng-thien tî-poi;Kì hién-mìn chhṳ-kâ he phit-nan-só.4 Lie̍t-pâng ke vòng chhi-si̍p khiung-ha,siông-khiung lòi kûng-tá Siak-ôn-sân.5 Kì-têu yit khon-tó liá-sàng chhiu thai chho̍k-kiâng;kì-têu kiâng-to mò kat-sat, kiak-kiak thò-chéu.6 Chhai ke-vi kì-têu yu chho̍k-kiâng yu thung-khú,chhiong fu-ngìn-kâ kiung-chṳ́ ke sṳ̀ án kân-khú,7 chhiong Thâ-sṳ̂ ke fo-sòn pûn tûng-fûng tá-lan án-chhám.8 Chhai Song-chú, Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi — ngài-têu Song-ti ke sàng-nui,thèu-pái ngài-têu só thâng-kóng ke, liá-ha ngài-têu khon-tó lé;Song-ti yit-thin voi pó-fu liá-sàng, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.9 Song-ti â, chhai Ngì ke sṳn-thien chûng,ngài-têu sióng-ngiam Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi.10 Song-ti â, mân thiên-ha ke ngìn siung-chan Ngì;Ngì ke miàng chhòn-phien chhiòn sṳ-kie!Ngì yung chṳn-ngi thúng-chhṳ;11 ngien Siak-ôn-sân fôn-hí!Ngien Yù-thai ke sàng-sṳ khoai-lo̍k,yîn-vi Ngì ke sṳ́m-phan kûng-chṳn!12 Ngì-têu oi tîn-tén Siak-ôn lòi-hàng,son kì ke sàng-lèu khon yû kí-tô;13 oi siòng-se khon sàng-chhiòng lâu sàng-pó,hó lâu ngì-têu ke heu-thoi chhòn-kóng:14 Liá Song-ti yún-yún yén-yén he ngài-têu ke Song-ti;Kì yit-thin voi sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi tai-liâng ngài-têu.
