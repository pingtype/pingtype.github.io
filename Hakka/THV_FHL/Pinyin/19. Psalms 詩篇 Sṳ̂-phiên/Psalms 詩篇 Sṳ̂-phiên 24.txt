1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂.Yùng-kông ke Kiûn-vòng Sṳ-kie lâu khì-chûng ke yit-chhiet,chhiòn-phu he Song-chú ke;thai-thi lâu thi-song ke van-vu̍t,ya he Kì ke.2 Kì chiông thai-thi kien-li̍p chhai chhṳ̂m-hói ke táng-kô,chiông thi ke kîn-kî ôn chhai chhṳ̂m-súi ke tî-poi.3 Má-ngìn cho-tet sông-hi Song-chú ke sân?Má-ngìn cho-tet chin-ngi̍p Kì ke sṳn-thien?4 Chhiu-he sûng-sú chhîn-kiet, sîm-kôn chhiang-li,mò pai ngiéu-siong, fat-sṳ mò cho-ká ke ngìn.5 Liá-chúng ngìn, Song-chú oi su-fuk pûn kì;chṳ́n-kiu kì ke Song-ti oi phan kì mò-chhui.6 Liá-têu ngìn oi lòi chhèu-kien Song-chú,lòi-to Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti mien-chhièn.7 Sàng-mùn â, tá khôi-lòi!Yún-kiú ke mùn â, tá khôi-lòi!Yùng-kông ke kiûn-vòng oi ngi̍p-lòi!8 Liá yùng-kông ke kiûn-vòng he má-ngìn?Kì chhiu-he kiên-khiòng yû thai nèn-li̍t ke Song-chú,chhiu-he chhai chan-chhòng tak-pái tá-yàng ke Song-chú!9 Sàng-mùn â, tá khôi-lòi!Yún-kiú ke mùn â, tá khôi-lòi!Yùng-kông ke kiûn-vòng oi ngi̍p-lòi!10 Liá yùng-kông ke kiûn-vòng he má-ngìn?Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi,Kì chhiu-he yùng-kông ke kiûn-vòng!
