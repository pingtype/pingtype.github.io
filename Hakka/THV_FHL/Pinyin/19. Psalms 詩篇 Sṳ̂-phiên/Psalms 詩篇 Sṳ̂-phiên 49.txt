1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Yí-kho Chhòi-sán ke Ngì-ngong Van-mìn â, ngì-têu oi thâng-hó!Sṳ-kiên-ngìn â, oi liù-sîm thâng!2 Mò-lun he phìn-chhien fu-kui,lûng-chúng oi chet-ngí lòi-thâng!3 Ngài ke choi oi kóng chṳ-fi ke fa;ngài ke sîm oi sióng thûng-tha̍t ke sṳ.4 Ngài ke ngí-kûng oi se-ngi thâng pí-yi;ngài oi thàn-khìm kié-mìn ngài ke liang-é.5 Chhai châi-nan lìm-to ke-têu ngit-è,sù-thi̍t vì-khun tén ngài ke-yông-sṳ̀,ngài cho-má-ke oi-sṳ́ chho̍k-kiâng nè?6 Kì-têu put-ko he kho chhièn-chhòi ke ngìn,só khoâ-khiéu ke he chhòi-sán tô thin-thin.7 Ngìn mò fap-thu su̍k-chón chhṳ-kâ ke hiûng-thi,ya mò-fap thoi kì na̍p su̍k-ka pûn Song-ti.8 Sâng-miang ke su̍k-ka khi̍t-kui,chhie̍t-tui mò-ngìn fu tet-chhut.9 Sióng-oi yún-yén sâng-sâng, m̀-sṳ́ sí,liá he chhie̍t-tui mò khó-nèn ke sṳ.10 Oi khon chhîn-chhú, yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn voi sí,ngong-ngong tong-tong ke ngìn khiung-yong voi me̍t-mòng;kì-têu lûng-chúng chiông chhièn-chhòi liù-pûn phe̍t-ngìn.11 Sûi-yèn kì-têu sṳt yû chhṳ-kâ ke thú-thi,chúng-he yún-yén ke kâ he chhúng-pû( 44 49:11 "chhúng-pû" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "nui-sîm".) thin-thin;kì-têu oi yit-chhṳ̍t liù chhai chhúng-pû tî-poi.12 Ngìn sûi-yèn he̍t chhai chûn-kui chûng hàn-he voi sí;kì lâu voi sí ke khìm-chhu mò má-ke fûn-phe̍t.13 Khon â, yí-kho chhṳ-kâ ke ngìn, kì-têu ke miang-yun ngióng-pân;khon ke-têu khoâ-khiéu tet-tó chhòi-sán ke ngìn kiet-khiu̍k ngióng-pân( 45 49:13 "khon ke-têu ... ngióng-pân" ya-he yi̍t-cho "hàn-yû ke-têu sùi-chhiùng kì-têu ke ngìn ke kiet-khiu̍k".).14 Kì-têu lâu yòng-è yit-yong chu-thin lo̍k yîm-kiên;"Sí-mòng" thin-chho̍k voi sàng-cho kì-têu ke mu̍k-chá.Kì-têu ke sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ ya oi chhai chhúng-pû tî-poi mièn-thet;thiên-kông ke sṳ̀, ke-têu ngi-ngìn chhiu oi yàng-ko kì-têu.15 Chúng-he Song-ti voi kiu-su̍k ngài ke lìn-fùn;Kì voi kiu ngài thot-lì yîm-kiên ke khièn-sṳ.16 Khon-tó ngìn fat-chhòi, kín-lòi kín yû-chhièn,m̀-sṳ́ pot-tok-ngok;17 kì sí ke sṳ̀ má-ke ya tai m̀-chéu;chhièn-chhòi ya m̀-voi thèn kì hâ-hi.18 Chhiu-son kì yit-sên-ngìn chhin tet-yi,miàng-sâng su-tó phe̍t-ngìn ke chhṳ̂n-chan,19 kì chui-heu hàn-he oi thèn chú-siên hi,yún-yén mò fap-thu chai khon-tó kông-mìn.20 Ngìn sûi-yèn he̍t chhai chûn-kui chûng, na mò kok-ngu,kì lâu voi sí ke khìm-chhu mò má-ke fûn-phe̍t.
