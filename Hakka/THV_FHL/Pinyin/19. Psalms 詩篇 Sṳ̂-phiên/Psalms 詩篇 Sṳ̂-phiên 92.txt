1 Ôn-sit-ngit ke sṳ̂-kô.Siung-chan ke Sṳ̂-kô Song-chú â, tui Ngì hien-song kám-chhia sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-hó!Chṳ-kô-chá â, kô-siung Ngì ke miàng sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-hó!2 Ngài tak chêu-sṳ̀n oi chhòn-yòng Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi,ngài tak am-pû oi kóng-sot Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t,3 oi yung sṳ̍p-hièn-khìm lòi phan-cheu,yung su-khìm mî-meu ke sâng-yîm lòi-fò.4 Song-chú â, Ngì ke chok-vì pûn ngài tet-tó fôn-hí;ngài oi yîn-vi Ngì ke sú só-cho ke lòi fôn-fû.5 Song-chú â, Ngì só-hàng ke chṳ̂n ví-thai!Ngì ke yi-ngiam sṳ̍t-chhai fî-sòng chhṳ̂m!6 Nga-ta ke m̀-voi liáu-kié,ngong-é ya m̀-voi mìn-pha̍k liá-khien sṳ:7 sûi-yèn fái-ngìn chhiong yâ-chhó án voi sâng,hàng-sṳ chit-ok ke hîn-vong yit-sṳ̀,kì-têu chui-heu voi vàn-chhiòn sêu-me̍t;8 chúng-he, Song-chú â,Ngì yún-yén he chṳ-kô vù-song ke!9 Song-chú â, ngài-têu chhîn-chhú tî:Ngì ke-têu sù-thi̍t thin-chho̍k voi me̍t-mòng;hàng-sṳ chit-ok ke chhiòn-phu voi si-san.10 Ngì pûn ngài chhiong yâ-ngiù án khiòng-chong;Ngì su-pûn ngài khi̍t-thai ke hen-fuk.11 Ngài chhîn-muk khon-tó ngài ke sù-thi̍t sṳt-phai;ngài chhîn-ngí thâng-tó kûng-kit ngài ke fái-ngìn kieu-chṳ̂.12 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn voi chhiong chûng-su án meu-sṳn,chhiong Lì-pâ-nun ke hiông-pak-su án kô-thai.13 Kì-têu chhiong chung chhai Song-chú sṳn-thien tú ke su-é,chhai ngài-têu Song-ti ke thìn-yen tú kín-lòi kín-vong,14 to ló hàn-he voi kiet kó-chṳ́,kî-ya̍p cha̍t-cha̍t, yún-yén chhiâng-chhui.15 Liá chṳn-mìn Song-chú he kûng-chṳn ke,ngài ke phàn-sa̍k chhiòn-mò put-ngi.
