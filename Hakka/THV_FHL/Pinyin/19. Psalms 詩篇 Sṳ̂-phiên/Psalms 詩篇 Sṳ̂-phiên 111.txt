1 Siung-chan Song-chú Oi chan-mî Song-chú!Chhai chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke chhi-fi lâu ngi-fi chûng,ngài oi chhiòn-sîm chhiòn-yi lòi kám-chhia Song-chú.2 Song-chú ke chok-vì sṳ̍t-chhai ví-thai;fàm hí-oi ke chhiu me̍t-sióng ngiên-kiu.3 Kì ke chok-vì mân-yû yùng-kông, vî-ngiàm;Kì ke kûng-ngi chhòng-chhùn, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.4 Song-chú hàng khì-sṳ, sṳ́ ngìn ki-ngiam;Kì mân-yû ên-fi, yu yû lìn-mén.5 Kì su sṳ̍t-vu̍t pûn kin-vi Kì ke ngìn;Kì yún-yén ki-tén Kì só-li̍p ke yok.6 Kì tui Kì ke chṳ́-mìn hién-sṳ thai-nèn,chiông ngoi-pâng ke thú-thi su-pûn kì-têu.7 Kì ke sú só-cho ke chhiòn-phu sin-sṳ̍t kûng-chṳn;Kì yit-chhiet ke kau-hiun khok-sṳ̍t khó-kho.8 Kì-têu yún-yén yû-háu,he cheu sin-sṳ̍t lâu kûng-ngi sat-li̍p ke.9 Kì vi Kì ke chṳ́-mìn sṳ̂-hàng kiu-su̍k;Kì thùng kì-têu li̍p yún-yén ke khie-yok.Kì ke miàng he sṳ̀n-sṳn tet-ngìn-vi ke!10 Kin-vi Song-chú he chṳ-fi ke khôi-thèu( 103 111:10 "Kin-vi ... khôi-thèu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Chṳ-fi ke chhung-tiám he kin-vi Song-chú".);chûn-hàng Kì kie-min ke chhiu yû chhûng-mìn.Oi chan-mî Song-chú, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén!
