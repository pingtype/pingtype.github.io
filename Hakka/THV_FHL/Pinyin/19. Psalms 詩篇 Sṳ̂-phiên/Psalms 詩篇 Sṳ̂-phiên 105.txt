1 Song-ti he Li̍t-sṳ́ ke Chú-chái( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 16:8-22 ) Ngì-têu oi kám-chhia Song-chú, khiù-ham Kì ke miàng;oi hiong van-koet chhòn-yòng Kì ke chok-vì!2 Ngì-têu oi chhong-kô lòi siung-chan Kì!Oi kóng-sot Kì khì-meu ke chok-vì!3 Ngì-têu oi yîn-vi Kì ke sṳn-miàng khoai-lo̍k!Ngien yit-chhiet chhìm-khiù Song-chú ke ngìn fôn-hí!4 Oi khiù Song-chú pông-chhu;oi sṳ̀-sòng kin-pai Kì.5 Song-ti ke phu̍k-ngìn Â-pak-lâ-hón ke heu-thoi â,Ngâ-kok ke chṳ́-sûn, Kì só kién-sién ke chṳ́-mìn â,ngì-têu oi ki-tet Kì só-cho khì-meu ke chok-vì,Kì só-hàng ke sṳ̀n-chiak lâu só kóng-hí ke sṳ́m-phan.6 併於上節7 Kì he Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti;Kì ke sṳ́m-phan chhòn-phien chhiòn sṳ-kie.8 Kì voi yún-yén sú Kì ke yok;Kì só-kóng ke yin-hí chhṳ̍t-to chhiên-thoi.9 Kì ki-tet thùng Â-pak-lâ-hón só-li̍p ke yok,hàn-yû Kì fat-sṳ su-pûn Yî-sat ke yin-hí.10 Song-chú thùng Ngâ-kok kiên-thin liá-yok cho thiàu-li,chhiu-he Kì pûn Yî-set-lie̍t yún-kiú ke khie-yok.11 Kì kóng: "Ngài oi chiông Kâ-nàm-thi su-pûn ngì,chhiòn-phu oi cho ngì-têu ke sán-ngia̍p."12 Ke-sṳ̀, Song-ti chṳ́-mìn ke ngìn-tên yû-han, su-muk chhin-séu,chhai Kâ-nàm-thi chhiam-sṳ̀ he̍t.13 Kì-têu tui liá-chhu̍k phêu-liù to ke-chhu̍k,tui liá-koet liù-long to ke-koet.14 Chúng-he Song-ti put-chún ngoi-ngìn khî-fu kì-têu;vi-tó kì-têu, Kì kín-ko ngoi-chhu̍k ke-têu vòng.15 Kì kóng: "Cho-m̀-tet vì-nàn Ngài só kién-sién ke;ngì-têu cho-m̀-tet sông-hoi Ngài ke siên-tî!"16 Song-chú sṳ́ ke thi-fông tù-tó kî-fông;Kì chhiet-thôn kì-têu liòng-sṳ̍t ke lòi-ngièn.17 Chúng-he Kì chhâi Yok-sit vi kì-têu siên-hàng;Yok-sit pûn-ngìn mai-cho nù-chhòi.18 Kì ke sûng-kiok pûn kiok-liàu só-tén,kì ke kiáng-kîn tai-tén thiet-kâ-só.19 Song-chú ke fa kín sṳ-lien kì,chhṳ̍t-to kì só yi-ngièn ke sṳ sṳ̍t-hien.20 Âi-khi̍p ke vòng chhiu phai-ngìn hi chiông kì sṳt-fong;thúng-chhṳ ngìn-mìn ke chhiu fî-fu̍k kì ke chhṳ-yù,21 phai kì cho vòng-kiûng ke chóng-kón,ham kì chhṳ-lî kì só-yû ke yit-chhiet,22 pûn kì chhṳ-yù kón-lî vùn-vú pak-kôn,yû-khièn chiông chṳ-fi kâu ke-têu ngièn-ló.23 Heu-lòi Yî-set-lie̍t hi-to Âi-khi̍p,Ngâ-kok chhiam-he̍t chhai Hàm ke thi-fông.24 Song-chú sṳ́ Kì ke chṳ́-mìn chen-kâ,pûn kì-têu pí thi̍t-ngìn kha khiòng-sṳn.25 Kì sṳ́ Âi-khi̍p-ngìn yen-hen Kì ke chṳ́-mìn;kì-têu yung kúi-kie tui-fu Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn.26 Kì chhiu chhâi Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn Mô-sî,yu phai Kì só kién-sién ke Â-lùn.27 Kì-têu hién-chhut Song-ti ke sṳ̀n-chiak,chhai Hàm ke thi-fông hàng thai khì-sṳ.28 Song-ti sṳ́ ke-vi chhûng-mân vû-am,chúng-he Âi-khi̍p-ngìn m̀-thâng Kì ke min-lin( 92 105:28 "chúng-he ... min-lin" he cheu yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chúng-he kì-têu mò vì-poi Kì ke fa".).29 Kì chiông kì-têu ke hò-súi pien-cho hiet,sṳ́ kì-têu ke ǹg chhiòn-phu sí chhiang-chhiang.30 Kì-têu koet-nui tak-vi he hà-mà,lièn vòng-kiûng tî-poi ya he khiung-yong.31 Song-ti ha-lin, vû-yìn kûi-khiùn pî-lòi,set-mà ya he khiung-yong, phien-mân chhiòn-thi.32 Kì kong pên-pho̍k chhiong lo̍k-yí,sṳ́ ngiap-lang to-chhu kín-ngiap.33 Kì tá-fái phù-thò-su lâu vù-fâ-kó-su,tá-thôn kì-têu koet-nui só-yû ke su-muk.34 Kì yit chhut-sâng, chhó-máng chhiu pî-lòi,chhiên-chhiên van-van, mò phan-fap hó son.35 Kì-têu chiông koet-nui ke chhiâng-chhoi khie chhiang-chhiang,chiông thièn-yèn só-yû ke ńg-kuk sṳ̍t kông-kông.36 Kì chhṳ̀-sí koet-nui só-yû ke chóng-chṳ́,chhiu-he ke-têu ngìn thèu-thôi ke lai-é.37 Yèn-heu, Kì chhiu tai-liâng Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhut-lòi;kì-têu tai-tén kîm-ngiùn, kok kî-chhu̍k sà-sà yúng-khien.38 Kì-têu lì-khôi ke sṳ̀, Âi-khi̍p-ngìn fî-sòng fôn-hí,yîn-vi kì-têu kiâng Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn kiâng-to voi sí.39 Song-ti yung yùn châ-tén Kì ke chṳ́-mìn,am-pû-sṳ̀ yung fó lòi cheu-kông kì-têu.40 Kì-têu( 93 105:40 "Kì-têu" he cheu yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Kì".) yêu-khiù, Kì chhiu sṳ́ êm-thùn pî-lòi;yu tui thiên-táng kong liòng-sṳ̍t mân-chiuk kì-têu.41 Kì tá-khôi phàn-sa̍k, pûn chhièn-súi yúng chhut-lòi,súi chhiong hò-pa liù chhai châu-châu ke khóng-yâ.42 Yîn-vi Kì ki-tet Kì sṳ̀n-sṳn ke yin-hí,chhiu-he tui Kì phu̍k-ngìn Â-pak-lâ-hón só-kóng ke.43 Kì tai Kì ke chṳ́-mìn fôn-fôn hí-hí chhut-lòi;Kì só kién-sién ke chṳ́-mìn fôn-fû yu chhong-kô.44 Kì chiông lie̍t-koet ke thú-thi su-pûn kì-têu,pûn kì-têu sṳ̀n-su ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn ke thièn-yèn,45 hó-pûn Kì ke chṳ́-mìn sú Kì ke kûi-chet,pin chûn-hàng Kì yit-chhiet ke kau-sṳ.Oi chan-mî Song-chú!
