1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂.Song-chú lâu Kì Sién-li̍p ke Vòng Song-chú lâu ngài ke chú kóng:"Ngì chhô chhai Ngài ke yu-phién,tén Ngài sṳ́ ngì ke sù-thi̍t phu̍k chhai ngì ke kiok-hâ."2 Song-chú oi tui Siak-ôn khok-chán ngì ke vòng-khièn.Ngì oi chhai ngì ke thi̍t-ngìn chûng chóng-khièn.3 Ngì thùng thi̍t-ngìn chok-chan ke sṳ̀-chiet,ngì ke ngìn-mìn voi chhṳ-ngien chhiùng-kiûn;ngì ke heu-sâng-ngìn voi to sṳn-sân lòi-chhiu ngì,chhiu-chhiong chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu ke kâm-lu án-tô( 102 110:3 Chhai Hî-pak-lòi-vùn pún-chiet ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).4 Song-chú yí-kîn fat-sṳ, chhie̍t-tui mò fán-fí, Kì kóng:"Ngì oi cheu Ma̍k-kî-sé-tet ke thí-chṳ,yún-yén cho chi-sṳ̂."5 Chú chhai ngì ke yu-phién;Kì fat-khién ke-ngit oi tá-kit lie̍t-vòng.6 Kì oi sṳ́m-phan van-koet, chiông sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ tôi hí-lòi;Kì oi tá-kit chhiòn-thi ke thúng-chhṳ-chá.7 Ngài ke vòng voi chhai lu-sùn lîm hâi-súi,nèn-li̍t chhiu fî-fu̍k, tet-tó thai sṳn-li.
