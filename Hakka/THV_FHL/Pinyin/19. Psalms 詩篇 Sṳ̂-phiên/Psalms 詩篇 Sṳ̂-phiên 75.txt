1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, thiau yung Mo̍k Phai-fai.Sṳ́m-phan ke Chú Song-ti Song-ti â, ngài-têu kám-chhia Ngì,ngài-têu kám-chhia Ngì!Ngài-têu siên-yòng Ngì ke ví-thai,kóng-sot( 71 75:1 "ngài-têu siên-yòng Ngì ke ví-thai, kóng-sot" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Ngì ke miàng chhîn-khiun; ngìn kóng-sot".) Ngì khì-meu ke chok-vì!2 Song-ti kóng: "Ngài yí-kîn thin-hó ngit-è;Ngài oi yung kûng-tong lòi sṳ̂-hàng sṳ́m-phan.3 Sûi-yèn thi lâu he̍t chhai thi-song ke ngìn yèu-thung,Ngài hàn oi pûn thi ke kîn-kî tet-tó kiên-ku.4 Ngài ham kiêu-ngau ke ngìn mo̍k kiêu-ngau;Ngài ham hiûng-ok ke ngìn mo̍k hau-teu;5 mo̍k chhṳ-thai thèu ngo-ngo,mo̍k kóng-fa kiáng vàng-vàng."6 Ngìn tet-tó kô-sṳ̂n m̀-he tui tûng-fông ya-he sî-fông lòi ke,ya m̀-he tui khóng-yâ lòi ke;7 vì-yû Song-ti ton-thin yit-chhiet;Kì sṳ́ yit-sà kong-hâ, yit-sà kô-sṳ̂n.8 Song-chú ke sú-chûng nâ-tén yit-chak pî-é,tî-poi chông-mân Kì thai fat-khién ke ngiàm-chiú;Kì tó chhut-lòi,sṳ-song sià-ok ke ngìn lûng-chúng oi lîm,yit-chhṳ̍t lîm-to chui-heu yit-tit.9 Chúng-he ngài oi siên-yòng Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti;ngài oi chhong-kô siung-chan Kì chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.10 Kì oi tá-sui fái-ngìn ke khièn-sṳ;Kì oi kâ-thiâm ngi-ngìn ke li̍t-liong.
