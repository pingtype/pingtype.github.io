1 Thai-ví ke kau-hiun-sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung sṳ̂-hièn ke ngo̍k-hi.Ngìn pûn Phèn-yû Chhut-mai heu ke Khì-tó Song-ti â, khiù Ngì chet-ngí thâng ngài ke khì-tó;m̀-hó m̀-tap ngài, yù-chhai ngài kín-khiù.2 Khiù Ngì liù-sîm thâng ngài, en ngài;ngài fàn-chhau to mò-kat mò-sat.3 Sù-thi̍t khiúng-hak ngài,fái-ngìn ap-pet ngài;kì-têu chṳ-chho châi-nan hoi ngài;kì-têu thai fat-khién, sióng-oi mèu-hoi ngài.4 Ngài ke sîm chhûng-mân thung-khú;kiâng-sí ke kám-kok kín pet ngài.5 Ngài kûi-sṳ̂n kiâng-to pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat,chhiong-he voi pûn chho̍k-kiâng chim-sí.6 Ngài kóng: "Chṳ̂n hî-mong ngài yû yi̍t-kap, chhiong ngie̍t-kap-è yit-yong!Ngài chhiu cho-tet pî-hi chhìm hiû-sit ke só-chhai.7 Ngài yit-thin voi pî-to yén-yén ke thi-fông,hi khóng-yâ hiet.8 Ngài voi kiak-kiak chhìm yit-ke phit-nan-só,hó chhai ke-vi sám-phit khòng-fûng phau-yí."9 Chú â, khiù Ngì pien-lon thi̍t-ngìn ke khiông-khiéu;yîn-vi ngài khon-tó sàng-nui yû phau-thung lâu siông-châng.10 Kì-têu ngit-ya chhai sàng-chhiòng táng-kô sùn-lòi sùn-hi;sàng-nui chhûng-mân chhui-ok lâu châi-nan.11 Tak só-chhai yû pho-fai ke hàng-thung;kiê-hong chhûng-mân khî-ap lâu kiên-cha.12 Na-he tui-thi̍t met-sa ngài,ngài hàn-yû phan-fap ngiûn-su;na-he thi̍t-ngìn kûng-kit ngài,ngài hàn cho-tet sám-phit kì.13 Mò sióng-tó he ngì — ngài ke thùng-phân,ngài ke tî-kí, ngài chhîn-me̍t ke phèn-yû!14 Ngài-têu sṳt fu-siông kâu-thàm to chhin-yû tap-toi,ngài-têu sṳt thùng chung-ngìn khiung-ha chhai sṳn-thien kin-pai kâu-phòi.15 Ngien ngài ke sù-thi̍t fut-yèn sí-thet!Ngien kì-têu sâng-sâng tiet-lo̍k yîm-kiên!Yîn-vi kì-têu ke vuk-hâ lâu sîm-kôn lûng-chúng chhûng-mân sià-ok.16 Chúng-he, ngài oi khiù-ham Song-ti;Song-chú yit-thin voi chṳ́n-kiu ngài.17 Mò-lun chêu-sṳ̀n, tông-chu, am-pû-thèu,ngài kín khiù-ham, yu kín théu-thai-hi;Kì yit-thin voi thâng ngài ke chhên-sâng.18 Sûi-yèn yû yit thai-khiùn ke thi̍t-ngìn thùng ngài siông-chhṳ̀,Kì voi chṳ́n-kiu ngài phìn-ôn tui chan-chhòng to-chón-lòi.19 Song-ti tui kú-chó chhiu yit-chhṳ̍t he chóng-khièn-chá;Kì yit-thin voi thâng-tó, sṳ́ kì-têu hòng-fu̍k,yîn-vi kì-têu m̀-hén kói-pien, ya m̀-kiâng Song-ti.20 Ngài ke thùng-phân kûng-kit phèn-yû;kì vì-poi chhṳ-kâ ke khie-yok.21 Kì ke choi pí nen-yù kha-va̍t,chúng-he sîm-tú sióng ke he chên-teu.Kì ke fa pí yù hàn-kha ngiôn,khì-sṳ̍t he pâng-chhut ke li-kiam.22 Ngì yin-tông chiông chhûng-tâm kâu-pûn Song-chú,Kì chhiu voi fù-chhṳ̀ ngì;Kì yún-yén m̀-voi pûn ngi-ngìn sṳt-phai.23 Chúng-he Song-ti â, Ngì oi chiông hiûng-ok ke ngìn fit-lo̍k yîm-kiên;Ngì oi sṳ́ hàng-sṳ kúi-cha ke ngìn yêu-chat.Lun-tó ngài, ngài oi chhiòn-sîm sin-kho Ngì.
