1 Só-lò-mùn ke sṳ̂.Vi Kiûn-vòng Khì-tó Song-ti â, khiù Ngì kau-thô koet-vòng,pûn kì cheu Ngì ke kûng-ngi lòi sṳ́m-phan;khiù Ngì chiông Ngì ke kûng-ngi su-pûn vòng-chṳ́.2 Án-ngiòng kì chhiu voi yung chṳn-ngi chhṳ-lî Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn,cheu kûng-tong tui-thai ke-têu su̍k Ngì ke khiùng-khú-ngìn.3 Ngien thai-sân se-sân yîn-vi kûng-ngi,sṳ́ chhiòn-koet ngìn-mìn hióng-su thai-phìn.4 Ngien vòng cheu kûng-tong pí-yu khiùng-khú-ngìn,yên-chhu yû khiam-khiet mò-ngìn pông-chhu ke,thùng-sṳ̀ tá-kit khî-ap kì-têu ke ngìn.5 Chhiu-son ngit-thèu hàn fat-kông, ngie̍t-kông ya hàn-chhôi,ngien Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi kin-vi Ngì!6 Ngien vòng chhiong yí-súi lo̍k chhai chhó-phû;ngien vòng chhiong kâm-lìm chṳ̂-yun thai-thi.7 Ngien kì chhai-vi ke ngit-è chṳn-ngi sṳ̂n-chông;ngien koet-thai mìn-ôn chhiu-chhiong ngie̍t-kông chhòng-chhùn.8 Kì ke koet tui liá-chak hói theu-to ke-chak hói,tui Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò yit-chhṳ̍t theu-to thi-khi̍t.9 He̍t chhai khóng-yâ ke voi hiong kì hàng-lî;kì ke sù-thi̍t voi phu̍k-hâ hòng-fu̍k kì.10 Thâ-sṳ̂ lâu lie̍t-tó ke-têu vòng voi tui kì hien-song kung-vu̍t;Sṳ-pâ lâu Sî-pâ( 66 72:10 Thâ-sṳ̂, Sṳ-pâ lâu Sî-pâ khó-nèn chhai hien-thoi ke Sî-pân-ngà, Â-lâ-pak lâu Yî-sok-phit-â ke-têu thi-fông.) ke chung vòng ya tai lî-vu̍t lòi.11 Lie̍t-vòng voi khúi-pai kì;van-koet voi fu̍k-sṳ kì.12 Kì théu-piong tui kì khiù-ham ke khiùng-khú-ngìn,théu-piong su ap-pet lâu mò-ngìn pông-chhu ke ngìn.13 Kì lìn-mén ngiôn-ngio̍k khiùng-khú ke ngìn;kì chṳ́n-kiu khiùng-khú-ngìn ke sin-miang.14 Kì kiu kì-têu thot-lì ap-pet lâu khiòng-phau;kì khon kì-têu ke sâng-miang cho chhin pó-kui.15 Ngien vòng van-soi!Ngien kì tet-tó Sṳ-pâ ke kîm-é;ngien yû-ngìn sṳ̀-sòng kín thoi kì khì-tó,khiù Song-ti yit-chhṳ̍t su-fuk pûn kì!16 Ngien thú-thi ńg-kuk fûng-tên,yû fûng-fu ke sû-sṳ̀n;sân-thèu kó-chṳ́ mân-phien,chhiu-chhiong Lì-pâ-nun yit-yong;ngien sàng-sṳ ke ngìn hîn-vong,chhiong yâ-thi sâng-mân chhiâng-chhó.17 Ngien kì ke miàng yún-yén pûn-ngìn ki-ngiam;ngien kì ke miàng-sâng chhiong ngit-thèu chhòng-chhùn.Ngien van-mìn tui kì tet-tó fuk-hi;ngien van-koet chhṳ̂n kì chṳ̂n-yû fuk-hi.18 Oi siung-chan Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti!Vì-yû Kì chang hàng tet-chhut khì-meu ke sṳ.19 Oi yún-yén siung-chan Kì yùng-yeu ke miàng!Ngien Kì ke yùng-kông chhûng-mân chhiòn sṳ-kie!Â-mén! Â-mén!20 Yâ-sî ke lai-é Thai-ví ke khì-tó kiet-suk.
