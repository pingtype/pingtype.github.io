1 Khun-khú-ngìn sṳt-chṳ ke sṳ̀, chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn chha̍t-tái kóng-chhut khú-chhìn ke khì-tó.Fam-nan chûng ke Khì-tó Song-chú â, khiù Ngì thâng ngài ke khì-tó;khiù Ngì thâng ngài khiù-ham ke sâng!2 Ngài ham chhai fam-nan chûng ke sṳ̀-chiet,khiù Ngì m̀-hó êm-tén mien m̀-tap ngài!Khiù Ngì chet-ngí lòi-thâng ngài ke khì-tó;ngài khiù Ngì ke sṳ̀-chiet, kiak-kiak en ngài!3 Ngài ke ngièn-ngit chhiong yên án-ngiòng sêu-sṳt;ngài ke kut-thèu chhiong chhai lù-chûng sêu-tén ke fó-than.4 Ngài ke sîm su-sông, chhiong chhó châu-thet;ngài sṳm-chṳ lièn sṳ̍t-fan ya m̀ ki-tet.5 Ngài théu thai-hi kín-chhên,kûi-sṳ̂n chṳ́-yû phì-pâu-kut.6 Ngài chhiong khóng-yâ ke tiâu-é,chhiong yâ-thi ke meu-thèu-tiâu.7 Ngài yit-chhṳ̍t soi m̀ lo̍k-kau;ngài chhiong vuk-táng kû-tân ke tiâu-é.8 Ngài ke sù-thi̍t chhiùng-chó to-am yu̍k-ma ngài;met-sa ngài ke ngìn chṳ́-tén ngài lòi chu-chú.9 Yîn-vi Ngì ke fat-khién lâu fun-nu,ngài sṳ̍t fó-fôi, chhiong sṳ̍t-fan yit-yong,yung muk-chṳp chhâm-tén yím-liau lòi lîm.Ngì chiông ngài khià hí-lòi yu phiâng-thet.10 併於上節11 Ngài ke ngièn-ngit chhiong lìm-am ke sṳ̂n-yáng,ya chhiong chhó chón-vòng châu-thet.12 Chúng-he, Song-chú â, Ngì yún-yén chóng-khièn;Ngì ke miàng pûn-ngìn ki-ngiam chhṳ̍t-to van-thoi.13 Ngì yit-thin voi hong-hí-lòi lìn-mén Siak-ôn,yîn-vi Ngì lìn-mén kì ke sṳ̀-chiet to lé,Ngì só-thin ke sṳ̀-kiên yí-kîn sṳ̀n-su̍k lé.14 Ke-têu sa̍k-thèu he Ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn só fôn-hí ke;lièn sàng-chûng ke nài ya he kì-têu só thung-siak ke.15 Thiên-ha van-koet thin-chho̍k voi kin-vi Song-chú ke miàng;thi-song van-vòng voi yîn-vi Kì ke yùng-kông chho̍k-kiâng.16 Song-chú chhùng-kien Siak-ôn ke sṳ̀-chiet,Kì ke yùng-kông voi hién-mìn chhut-lòi.17 Kì oi chhùi-thâng khiùng-khú-ngìn ke khiù-ham;Kì m̀-voi méu-sṳ kì-têu ke khì-tó.18 Oi vi heu-thoi chiông Song-chú ke chok-vì ki-liu̍k hâ-lòi,pûn ke-têu hàn-màng chhut-se ke ngìn hiáu-tet siung-chan Kì.19 Song-chú tui chṳ-kô ke sṳn-só khon hâ-lòi;Kì tui thiên-táng kôn-chhat ngìn-kiên ke yit-chhiet.20 Kì thâng ke-têu fam-ngìn ke chhên-sâng;Kì théu-piong pûn-ngìn phan sí-hìn ke.21 Án-ngiòng, van-mìn chhi-si̍p khiung-ha,van-koet kin-pai Song-chú ke sṳ̀,ngìn voi chhai Siak-ôn chhòn-yòng Song-chú ke miàng;kì-têu voi chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng siung-chan Kì.22 併於上節23 Song-chú suk-tón ngài ke ngièn-ngit,sṳ́ ngài vi-ló siên-sôi.24 Ngài kóng: "Ngài ke Song-ti â,m̀-hó sṳ́ ngài chûng-ngièn ko-sṳ̂n."Song-chú â, Ngì van-sṳ yún-chhùn!25 Ngì chhai song-kú thin thai-thi ke kîn-kî;chû-thiên ya he Ngì chhîn-sú chhóng-chho ke.26 Thiên-thi voi sêu-me̍t, chúng-he Ngì yún-yén chhòng-chhùn;kì-têu voi chhiong sâm-fu lan-thet án-ngiòng sêu-sṳt.Ngì oi kói-pien thiên-thi, chhiong von-sâm yit-yong;kì-têu chhiu voi sêu-sṳt.27 Chúng-he, Ngì yún-yén m̀-voi kói-pien;Ngì ke su-ngièn vù-khiùng vù-chhin.28 Ngì liá-têu phu̍k-ngìn ke chṳ́-sûn voi ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p;kì-têu ke heu-thoi voi kiên-li̍p chhai Ngì mien-chhièn.
