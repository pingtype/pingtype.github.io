1 Siung-chan ke Sṳ̂-kô Ngi-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi yîn-vi Song-chú fôn-hí chhong-kô;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn â, ngì-têu siung-chan Kì he yin-tông ke.2 Oi thàn-khìm kám-chhia Song-chú,yung sṳ̍p-hièn-khìm kô-siung Kì.3 Oi chhong sîn ke sṳ̂-kô chan-mî Kì;oi thàn-to chhin-hó, thai-sâng lòi chhong.4 Yîn-vi Song-chú ke fa chṳn-chhṳ̍t;Kì só-cho ke chhiòn-phu sin-sṳ̍t.5 Song-chú fôn-hí chṳn-ngi lâu kûng-phìn;Kì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi chhûng-mân chhiòn-thi.6 Chû-thiên he thûng-ko Song-chú ke min-lin lòi chhóng-chho ke;ngit, ngie̍t, sên-sṳ̀n ya he thûng-ko Kì ke fa chho-sṳ̀n ke.7 Kì chiông hói-súi chhi-si̍p khiung-ha;Kì yung thai-hói lòi cho súi-khu.8 Ngien chhiòn sṳ-kie kin-vi Song-chú!Ngien sṳ-kiên ke van-mìn kiâng Kì!9 Kì yit chhut-sâng chhiu yû sṳ-kie;Kì yit min-lin van-yû chhiu khî-chhai.10 Song-chú pho-fai van-koet ke yîm-mèu;Kì sṳ́ van-mìn ke kie-mèu sṳt-háu.11 Song-chú ke kie-va̍k yún-yén chhòng-chhùn;Kì ke chṳ́-yi van-thoi put-pien.12 Chûn Song-chú cho Song-ti ke koet-kâ sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi!Kì só kién-sién kûi-su̍k Kì ke chṳ́-mìn chṳ̂n-yû fuk-hi!13 Song-chú tui thiên-táng khon hâ-lòi;Kì khon-tó chhiòn sṳ-kie ke ngìn.14 Kì tui chhṳ-kâ ke pó-chhokhon-tén sṳ-kiên ke van-mìn.15 Ngìn ke sîm chhiòn-phu he Kì chho ke;ngìn ke hàng-vì Kì chhiòn-phu kam-chhat.16 Kiûn-vòng tet-sṳn m̀-he kho pîn-tô;chan-sṳ tá-yàng m̀-he kho li̍t-thai.17 Kho chan-mâ lòi tet-sṳn he nong-sióng;mâ ke li̍t-thai mò phan-fap kiu ngìn.18 Song-chú chhîn-ngién khon-ku kin-vi Kì ke ngìn;Kì khon-ku ngióng-mong Kì chhṳ̀-oi ke ngìn.19 Kì kiu kì-têu, pûn kì-têu thot-lì sí-mòng,thiên-hôn ke sṳ̀, pó-liù kì-têu ke sin-miang.20 Ngài-têu sîm só ngióng-mong ke chhiu-he Song-chú;Kì pông-chhu ngài-têu, cho ngài-têu ke tún-phài.21 Ngài-têu ke sîm oi yîn-vi Kì fôn-hí,yîn-vi ngài-têu sin-kho Kì ke sṳn-miàng.22 Song-chú â, ngài-têu ngióng-mong Ngì;ngien Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi lâu ngài-têu thùng-chhai!
