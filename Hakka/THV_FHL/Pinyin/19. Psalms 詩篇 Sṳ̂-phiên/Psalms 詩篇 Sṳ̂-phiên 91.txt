1 Song-ti he Ngài-têu ke Pó-fu-chá Mò-lun má-ngìn, tui Chṳ-kô-chá chhìm-khiù ôn-chhiòn,ya-he he̍t chhai Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke pí-yu chṳ̂-ha,2 chhiòn-phu cho-tet tui Song-chú kóng:"Ngì he ngài ke phit-nan-só lâu sàng-pó;Ngì he ngài ke Song-ti, ngài sin-kho Ngì."3 Kì voi kiu ngì thot-lì yit-chhiet ke lò-mióng,pin thot-lì yit-chhiet chṳ-miang ke chhòn-ngiam-phiang.4 Kì voi yung chhṳ-kâ ke yi̍t-kap pí-yu ngì;tet-tó Kì ke khon-ku, ngì yit-thin ôn-chhiòn;Kì ke sin-sṳ̍t voi chhiong tún-phài pó-fu ngì.5 Am-pû-sṳ̀, ngì m̀-sṳ́ kiâng má-ke ngùi-hiám,ngit-sṳ̀-thèu, ngì m̀-sṳ́ kiâng tù-tó kûng-kit;6 ngì m̀-sṳ́ kiâng vû-am chûng liù-hàng chṳ-miang ke chhòn-ngiam-phiang,ya m̀-sṳ́ kiâng tông-chu-thèu fí-me̍t-sin ke châi-hoi.7 Sûi-yèn yû kûi-chhiên ngìn vang chhai ngì chó-phién,yû kûi-van ngìn sí chhai ngì ke yu-phién,liá-ke châi-nan ya mò khó-nèn lìm-to ngì.8 Ngì tá-khôi muk-chû chhîn-ngién lòi khon,chhiu voi khon-tó fái-ngìn su chit-fa̍t.9 Yîn-vi ngì kho Song-chú cho ngì ke phit-nan-só,ki-yèn Chṳ-kô-chá chhiu-he ngì he̍t ke só-chhai,10 châi-nan yit-thin m̀-voi lìm-to ngì,châi-fo m̀-voi chiap-khiun ngiâ vuk-hâ.11 Song-ti voi chhâi-phai thiên-sṳ́ khon-ku ngì,chhai ngì só-hàng ke lu-song pó-fu ngì.12 Kì-têu voi yung sûng-sú lòi fù-tén ngì,pûn ngì ke kiok m̀-voi chhong-tó sa̍k-thèu.13 Ngì voi chiông sṳ̂-é lâu thu̍k-sà tha̍p chhai kiok-hâ;ngì voi tém-sí hiûng-ok ke sṳ̂-é lâu thai-sà.14 Song-ti kóng: "Ngài oi chṳ́n-kiu ke-têu chôn-sîm kin-oi Ngài ke ngìn;Ngài oi pó-fu chûn Ngài cho Chú ke ngìn.15 Kì-têu khiù-ham Ngài ke sṳ̀-chiet,Ngài voi en kì-têu;kì-têu chhai fam-nan chûng,Ngài oi thùng kì-têu khiung-ha.Ngài oi chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu,pûn kì-têu tet-tó kông-yùng.16 Ngài oi pûn kì-têu hióng-su chhòng-su,pûn kì-têu khon-tó Ngài ke kiu-ên."
