1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Vi Koet-kâ ke Fu̍k-hîn Khì-tó Song-chú â, Ngì yit-chhṳ̍t ku-ngiam Ngì ke thú-thi;Ngì sṳt pûn Yî-set-lie̍t chhùng-kien kâ-yèn.2 Ngì sa-miên Ngì chṳ́-mìn ke chhui,ngièu kì-têu yit-chhiet ke ko-fam.3 Ngì mò chai tui kì-têu fat-khién;Ngì sû-chón Ngì ke thai fat-khién.4 Kiu ngài-têu ke Song-ti â, fu̍k-hîn ngài-têu;khiù Ngì thìn-chṳ́ Ngì tui ngài-têu ke fun-nu!5 Kám-kóng Ngì oi yún-yén tui ngài-têu fat-khién?Kám-kóng Ngì oi khién-to sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi mâ?6 Khiù Ngì chai yit-pái pûn ngài-têu khiòng-sṳn hí-lòi;Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn chhiu voi siung-chan Ngì.7 Song-chú â, tui ngài-têu hién-chhut Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi;khién-khiù Ngì chiông Ngì ke chṳ́n-kiu su-pûn ngài-têu.8 Ngài oi thâng Chú Song-ti só-kóng ke fa.Kì yin-hí oi su phìn-ôn pûn Kì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn,chúng-he kì-têu cho-m̀-tet to-chón-hi hàng ngong-lu.9 Kì thin-chho̍k voi kiu ke-têu kin-vi Kì ke ngìn;Kì ke yùng-kông voi chhûng-mân ngài-têu ke thú-thi.10 Chhṳ̀-oi lâu chûng-sîm( 78 85:10 "Chhṳ̀-oi lâu chûng-sîm" chṳ́ Song-ti tui ngìn ke chhṳ̀-oi, lâu ngìn tui Song-ti ke chûng-sîm.) fu-siông ngiàng-chiap;kûng-ngi lâu fò-phìn pí-chhṳ́ siông-chhîn.11 Ngìn ke chûng-sîm tui thi-song sâng-chhut;Song-ti ke kûng-ngi tui thiên-táng hién-hien.12 Song-chú oi sṳ́ ngài-têu chhin fu-chiuk;ngài-têu ke thú-thi voi yû fûng-sû.13 Kûng-ngi voi hàng chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn,vi Kì yi-phi oi hàng ke tho-lu.
