1 Phàn-ko-chiet ke Sṳ̂-kô Yî-set-lie̍t ke ngìn-mìn yit lì-khôi Âi-khi̍p,Ngâ-kok ke chṳ́-sûn yit chhut ngoi-pâng ke thi,2 Yù-thai chhiu sàng-cho Song-ti ke sṳn-thi,Yî-set-lie̍t sàng-cho Kì chhṳ-lî ke koet.3 Fùng-hói yit khon-tó, súi chhiu thò-chéu;Yok-tan-hò ke súi ya tiên-to liù.4 Thai-sân chhiong kûng-yòng kín-thiau;se-sân chhiong se-yòng kín-thiàu.5 Fùng-hói â, ngì cho-má-ke oi thò-chéu?Yok-tan-hò â, ngì ngióng-pân tiên-to liù?6 Thai-sân â, ngì cho-má-ke chhiong kûng-yòng kín-thiau?Se-sân â, ngì ngióng-pân chhiong se-yòng kín-thiàu?7 Thai-thi â, Chú to ke sṳ̀-chiet ngì oi kín-chûn,chhai Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti mien-chhièn ngì oi kín-chhat!8 Kì sṳ́ phàn-sa̍k sàng-cho pî-thòng,sṳ́ ngàm-sa̍k chhûng-chhut chhièn-súi lòi.
