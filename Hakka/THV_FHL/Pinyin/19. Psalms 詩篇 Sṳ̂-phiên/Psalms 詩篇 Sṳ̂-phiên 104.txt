1 Siung-chan Chhóng-chho ke Chú Ngài ke sîm â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!Song-chú — ngài ke Song-ti â, Ngì chṳ̂n ví-thai!Ngì chok-tén yùng-kông lâu vî-ngiàm;2 Ngì yung kông cho chhòng-phàu phî-tén.Ngì ôn-sat kiûng-chhông, chhiong tap chong-phùng yit-yong;3 Ngì chhai kô-thiên ke súi mien-táng hí chhu-só.Ngì yung yùn cho chan-chhâ,chia fûng ke yi̍t-kap lòi-pî.4 Ngì yung fûng cho sṳ́-chá,yung ngiap-lang cho phu̍k-ngìn.5 Ngì chiông thai-thi ôn chhai kiên-ku ke kîn-kî táng-kô,pûn kì yún-yén m̀-voi yèu-thung.6 Ngì yung thai-hói chhiong ngoi-phàu pâu-tén thi-mien;thai-súi chhiu chiông khiùn-sân yim-tén.7 Ngì thai-sâng yit-tot, súi chhiu kón-kín thui-chéu;Ngì thai-sâng yit-hot, súi chhiu sia-chéu.8 Thai-súi ye̍t-ko sân-liâng, liù-ngi̍p sân-lak,to Ngì vi kì-têu sat-thin ke só-chhai.9 Ngì va̍k-thin kie-han, m̀-chún súi yê ko-lòi,ham kì-têu m̀-hó chai chiông thai-thi yim-tén.10 Ngì sṳ́ chhièn-súi chhai sân-lak yúng-chhut,chhai khiùn-sân chûng-kiên liù-tén hâ.11 Chéu-chhu yû súi hó-lîm,yâ-lì yû hó chṳ́-hot.12 Fî-niâu chhai súi-piên cho-teu,chhai su-kî ke chûng-kiên chhong-kô.13 Ngì tui thiên-táng lo̍k-yí chhai sân-liâng-táng;yîn-vi Ngì kûng-chok ke kiet-kó, thai-thi chhiu fûng-chiuk.14 Ngì sṳ́ chhiâng-chhó sâng chhut-lòi,pûn thèu-sâng sṳ̍t;Ngì sṳ́ chhiâng-chhoi chóng-thai,pûn ngìn cho sṳ̍t-vu̍t;Ngì pûn ngìn chhai thú-thi song kâng-chung ńg-kuk:15 kiuk-chiú lòi pûn ngìn-sîm fôn-hí,chṳ-chho yù lòi chṳ̂-yun mien-mau,cho liòng-sṳ̍t lòi kâ-thiâm thí-li̍t.16 Song-chú chung chhai Lì-pâ-nun ke hiông-pak-su,chhiòn-phu tet-tó fûng-fu ke yí-súi chṳ̂-yun.17 Tiâu-é chhai ke-têu su-táng cho-teu;chhiùng-su-táng ya yû pha̍k-ho̍k ke teu.18 Yâ-sân-yòng he̍t chhai kô-sân-táng;sa̍k-fôn khong chhai sa̍k-lùng tî-poi.19 Ngì chho ngie̍t-kông lòi-thin chiet-lin;ngit-thèu tî kí-sṳ̀ oi lo̍k-sân.20 Ngì sṳ́ vû-am lìm-to, sàng-cho am-pû-thèu,su-nà chûng só-yû ke yâ-chhu chhiu chhut-lòi.21 Sṳ̂-é ho-ho-kún, chhut-lòi tá-lia̍p,oi chhìm Song-ti só-su ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t.22 Ngit-thèu yit chhut, kì-têu chhiu sám-chéu,to-chón sân-lùng tî-poi hi soi-muk.23 Yèn-heu, ngìn chhut-hi cho-se,lò-lò luk-luk to thôn-vû.24 Song-chú â, Ngì ke chhóng-chho sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n-tô!Kì-têu chhiòn-phu he Ngì yung chṳ-fi chho ke;thai-thi chhûng-mân Ngì só chhóng-chho ke van-vu̍t.25 Ke-phién yû hói, yu-thai yu-fat,khì-chûng yû vù-su ke sâng-vu̍t,thai-ke se-ke chhiòn-phu tû yû;26 hói-mien yû chhin-tô sòn lòi-lòi hi-hi,Ngì chho ke thai hói-koai chhai hói-tú káu-súi.27 Kì-têu chhiòn-phu he yí-kho Ngì,kho Ngì cheu-sṳ̀ kiûng-kip sṳ̍t-vu̍t.28 Ngì su má-ke pûn kì-têu, kì-têu chhiu liâng-su;Ngì ke sú yit tá-khôi, kì-têu chhiu tet-tó páu-chiuk.29 Ngì na-he êm-mien m̀-tap kì-têu,kì-têu chhiu chho̍k-kiâng;Ngì sû-chón kì-têu ke hi,kì-têu chhiu sí-thet, kûi-thú.30 Ngì tui kì-têu phùn-hi,kì-têu chhiu su-chho;Ngì sṳ́ thai-thi sṳ̀-sòng von-sîn.31 Ngien Song-chú ke yùng-kông yún-yén chhòng-chhùn!Ngien Song-chú fôn-hí chhṳ-kâ só-chho ke!32 Kì khon thai-thi, thi chhiu chṳ́n-thung;Kì miâ khiùn-sân, sân chhiu chhut-yên.33 Ngài yit-sên-ngìn oi chhong-kô lòi chan-mî Song-chú!Ngài yû-sên chṳ̂-ngit oi kô-siung ngài ke Song-ti!34 Ngien Kì yîn-vi ngài ke sṳ̂-kô fôn-hí,yîn-vi ngài ke khoai-lo̍k he tui Kì lòi.35 Ngien chhui-ngìn tui sṳ-kiên sêu-me̍t;ngien fái-ngìn m̀-voi chai chhut-hien.Ngài ke sîm â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!Hâ-li-lu-yá!
