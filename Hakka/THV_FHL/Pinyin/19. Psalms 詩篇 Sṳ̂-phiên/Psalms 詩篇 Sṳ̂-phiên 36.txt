1 Song-chú ke phu̍k-ngìn Thai-ví ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng.Ngìn ke Sià-ok lâu Song-chú ke Liòng-san Chhui chhai fái-ngìn ke sîm-tái kín-kóng;chhai kì khon-lòi, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng Song-ti.2 Chhui-ngìn chhṳ-kô chhṳ-thai,láu-tó Song-ti m̀-voi fat-kok, m̀-voi chit-fa̍t kì ke chhui( 33 36:2 "láu-tó ... chhui" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chṳ-to mò fat-hien chhṳ-kâ ke chhui, lòi fí-kói".).3 Kì mân-choi he sià-ok lâu kúi-cha;kì chó chhiu mò chṳ-fi, mò hàng-san.4 Kì sông-chhòng heu hàn-he thù-mèu fái-sṳ,só-oi hàng ke chhiòn-phu m̀-he hó-sṳ;kì chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt khî-chhie̍t chhui-ok.5 Song-chú â, Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi kô-to tù-thiên;Ngì ke sin-sṳ̍t tha̍t-to kiûng-chhông.6 Ngì ke kûng-ngi chhiong kô-sân vún-ku;Ngì ke phan-ton chhiong chhṳ̂m-yên nàn-chhet.Mò-lun he ngìn ya-he thèu-sâng,Song-chú â, Ngì chhiòn-phu khon-ku!7 Song-ti â, Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi sṳ̍t-chhai pó-kui!Sṳ-kiên-ngìn chhai Ngì ke yi̍t-kap hâ chhìm-tó pí-yu.8 Kì-têu chhai Ngì ke vuk-hâ hióng-su yèn-si̍t;Ngì pûn kì-têu lîm Ngì khoai-lo̍k ke chhièn-súi.9 Ngì chhiu-he yit-chhiet sâng-miang ke ngièn-thèu;chhai Ngì ke kông chûng, ngài-têu khon-tó kông.10 Khiù Ngì yit-chhṳ̍t thung-siak ngin-sṳt Ngì ke ngìn;khiù Ngì ki-siu̍k pûn chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn kûng-tho.11 Mo̍k pûn kiêu-ngau ke ngìn châu-tha̍p tó ngài;mo̍k pûn sià-ok ke ngìn chiông ngài kiuk-chéu.12 Ngì khon, chok-ok ke ngìn vang lo̍k-hi lé;yí-kîn súng-vang lé, hong m̀ hí-lòi lé!
