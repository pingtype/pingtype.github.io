1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, cheu Yâ-thu-tun ke chhong-fap.Sin-kho Song-ti Ngài sîm-sṳ̀n tiâm-tiâm tén-heu Song-ti;ngài ke chṳ́n-kiu he tui Kì lòi ke.2 Vì-yû Kì he ngài ke phàn-sa̍k, ngài ke kiu-chú;Kì he ngài ke sàng-pó, ngài chhiu m̀-voi yèu-thung.3 Ngì-têu chhiòn-thí kûng-kit yit-ke ngìn, oi-to kí-sṳ̀ nè?Kì chhiong chhià-chéu ke vì-chhiòng, voi vang ke lì-pâ thin-thin.4 Thai-kâ yit-sîm yit-yi sióng-oi chiông kì tui thai-vi lâi hâ-lòi;kì-têu hau-kóng fâ-liâu, mân-choi chuk-fuk, chúng-he sîm chu-ma kì.5 Ngài ke sîm â, tiâm-tiâm tén-heu Song-ti,yîn-vi Kì he ngài vì-yit ke hî-mong.6 Vì-yû Kì he ngài ke phàn-sa̍k, ngài ke kiu-chú;Kì he ngài ke sàng-pó, ngài chhiu m̀-voi yèu-thung.7 Ngài ke chṳ́n-kiu lâu kông-yùng chhiòn-phu he chhai Song-ti;Kì he ngài kiên-ku ke phàn-sa̍k, ngài ke phit-nan-só.8 Chung pak-siang â, oi sṳ̀-sòng sin-kho Song-ti,chiông sîm-yi chhiòn-phu pài chhai Kì ke mien-chhièn,yîn-vi Song-ti he ngài-têu ke phit-nan-só.9 Pî-chhien ke ngìn chhin hî-khûng;fu-kui ke ngìn tông hî-ká.Yung chhṳn-é lòi-chhṳn, chhiòn-mò pan-tiám chhûng;kì-têu lûng-chúng pí khûng-hi hàn-kha khiâng.10 Ngì-têu thin-chho̍k m̀-hó yí-kho ok sṳ-li̍t,ya m̀-hó nong-sióng kho chhióng-ngìn lòi fat-chhòi;chhiu-son pot hí-lòi,ngì-têu ke sîm ya m̀-hó yí-kho chhièn-chhòi.11 Ngài chai-sâm thâng Song-ti kóng-ko,yit-chhiet khièn-nèn he su̍k Kì ke;12 Chú â, put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi ya he su̍k Ngì ke.Ngì yit-thin voi cheu tak-sà só-hàng ke po-yin kì-têu.
