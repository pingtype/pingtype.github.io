1 Siung-chan Chhiòn-nèn ke Song-ti Hâ-li-lu-yá!Chhong-kô siung-chan ngài-têu ke Song-ti he ngióng-pân mî-san!Chan-mî Kì he chhin khoai-lo̍k, ya he chhin sṳt-tông ke sṳ!2 Chiông Yâ-lu-sat-lâng chhùng-sîn hí hí-lòi ke chhiu-he Song-chú;seu-si̍p liù-mòng ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chón-lòi ke ya he Kì.3 Kì yî-hó sîm-kôn pho-sui ke ngìn,chiông kì-têu ke sông-héu pâu hí-lòi.4 Kì chṳ-thin sên-sṳ̀n ke su-muk,vi kì-têu mî-yit-ke ôn-miàng.5 Ngài-têu ke Chú chui-thai, mân-yû nèn-li̍t;Kì ke chṳ-fi mò-ngìn nèn chhûi-chhet.6 Song-chú fù-chhṳ̀ pî-mì ke ngìn;Kì sṳ́ fái-ngìn vang-to tap-tap.7 Oi yung kám-chhia ke sîm chhong-kô siung-chan Song-chú,thàn su-khìm cheu-ngo̍k kô-siung ngài-têu ke Song-ti.8 Kì sṳ́ vû-yùn châ-thiên,lo̍k-yí chṳ̂-yun thai-thi,sṳ́ khiùn-sân sâng chhiâng-chhó.9 Kì su sṳ̍t-vu̍t pûn chhu-lui,lâu kín-kieu ke se vû-â.10 Kì mò chung-yi mâ-é ke li̍t thai,mò kak-yi ngìn ke kiok chhin voi chéu.11 Song-chú fôn-hí kin-vi Kì ke ngìn,hí-oi ngióng-mong Kì chhṳ̀-oi ke ngìn.12 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng â, oi siung-chan Song-chú!Siak-ôn â, oi chan-mî ngì ke Song-ti!13 Kì sṳ́ ngì ke sàng-mùn kiên-ku;Kì su-fuk pûn ngì ke chṳ́-ńg.14 Kì pó-sú ngì ke piên-kie phìn-chhin;Kì pûn ngì sṳ̍t song-tén ke ma̍k-é to páu-páu.15 Kì hiong thai-thi fat-chhut min-lin;Kì ke fa ngiàm-sṳ̀ chhiu chhòn-phien.16 Kì kong ke siet chhiong yòng-mô;Kì ve ke sông chhiong chhṳ̀n-fôi.17 Kì sṳ́ pên-pho̍k chhiong sa̍k-é lo̍k-hâ;Kì chhôi hòn-fûng, má-ngìn khî tet-he̍t?18 Kì yit min-lin, sông-siet pên-pho̍k chhiu yùng-thet;Kì yung fûng yit-chhôi, súi chhiu liù-thûng.19 Kì chiông Kì ke fa khí-sṳ pûn Ngâ-kok,chiông Kì ke kûi-thin lâu fap-chet chhòn-pûn Yî-set-lie̍t.20 Kì mò án-ngiòng tui-thai phe̍t ke koet-kâ;kì-têu chhiòn mò liáu-kié Kì ke fap-chet.Hâ-li-lu-yá!
