1 Thai-ví sông-kîn ke sṳ̂.Siung-chan Yâ-lu-sat-lâng Yû-ngìn tui ngài kóng:"Ngài-têu lòi-hi Song-chú ke sṳn-thien!"Ke-sṳ̀, ngài sṳ̍t-chhai tông fôn-hí!2 Liá-ha, ngài-têu lòi-to liá-vi,sûng-kiok khî chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke sàng-mùn-nui.3 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng yí-kîn hí-hó lé,he yit-chho lièn-kiet chṳ́n-chhè ke sàng.4 Kok kî-chhu̍k, chhiu-he su̍k Song-chú ke kok kî-chhu̍k, lûng-chúng sông-hi,cheu Yî-set-lie̍t só liâng-su ke fap-lin siung-chan Song-chú ke miàng.5 Liá-vi yû sat sṳ̂n-chông chṳn-ngi ke pó-chho,chhiu-he Thai-ví-kâ ke pó-chho.6 Oi vi Yâ-lu-sat-lâng khiù fò-phìn, kóng:"Ngien oi-siak ngì ke ngìn lûng-chúng hîn-vong!7 Ngien ngì ke sàng-tú yû fò-phìn!Ngien ngì ke kiûng-nui chhin hîn-vong!"8 Vi-tó ngài ke chhîn-chhit phèn-yû,ngài oi tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng kóng:"Ngien ngì phìn-ôn!"9 Vi-tó Song-chú — ngài-têu Song-ti ke sṳn-thien,ngài oi vi ngì ke hen-fuk khì-tó!
