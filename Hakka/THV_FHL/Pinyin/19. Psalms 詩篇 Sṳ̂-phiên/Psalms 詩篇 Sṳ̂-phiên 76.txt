1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung sṳ̂-hièn ke ngo̍k-hi.Tet-sṳn ke Chú Song-ti Song-ti chhai Yù-thai, tak-ke ngìn ngin-sṳt Kì;chhai Yî-set-lie̍t, ngìn-ngìn chûn-chhung Kì ke miàng.2 Kì chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng yû sṳn-chong-phùng;Kì chhai Siak-ôn-sân yû he̍t ke só-chhai.3 Chhai ke-vi, Kì áu-thôn kiûng-chien,fí-fai tún-phài, tô-kiam lâu vú-hi.4 Song-ti â, Ngì sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n kông-yùng!Ngì tá-yàng thi̍t-ngìn, tui khiùn-sân chón-lòi ke sṳ̀, sṳ̍t-chhai chṳ̂n vî-ngiàm!5 Kì-têu ke yúng-sṳ só chhióng-tó ke, yu pûn-ngìn chhióng-chéu;kì-têu ke chan-sṳ soi-to sí-sí, mò-li̍t hó tí-khong.6 Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti â, Ngì tot yit-sâng,kì-têu ke mâ lâu khì-pîn chhiu thôn-hi.7 Song-ti â, chṳ́-yû Ngì he tet-ngìn kin-vi ke!Ngì yit fat-khién, má-ngìn chhai Ngì mien-chhièn khî tet-he̍t nè?8 Ngì tui thiên-táng siên-pu phan-kiet;Ngì hong-hí-lòi sṳ̂-hàng sṳ́m-phan,oi chṳ́n-kiu yit-chhiet su ap-pet ke ngìn,ke-sṳ̀ chhiòn-thi kin-vi, m̀-kám chhut-sâng.9 併於上節10 Ngìn ke fun-nu voi pien-cho tui Ngì ke siung-chan;Ngì voi phìn-fu̍k chhûn hâ-lòi ke nu-hi.11 Oi tui Song-chú — ngì-têu ke Song-ti hí-ngien, pin oi vàn-ngien;lìn-koet ya lûng-chúng oi chhièn-lòi chin-kung, kin-pai khó-vi ke Chú.12 Kì me̍t ke-têu vòng-chṳ́ ke vî-fûng;Kì sṳ́ thi-song ke kiûn-vòng chho̍k-kiâng.
