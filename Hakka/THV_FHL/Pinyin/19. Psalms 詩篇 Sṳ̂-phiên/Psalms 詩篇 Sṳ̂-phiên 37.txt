1 Thai-ví ke sṳ̂.Hó-ngìn lâu Fái-ngìn ke Miang-yun M̀-sṳ́ yîn-vi chok-ok ke ngìn pot-at;ya m̀-sṳ́ chhit-tu hàng put-ngi ke ngìn.2 Kì-têu voi chhiong chhó tông-kiak khû-thet;kì-têu voi chhiong chhṳ̍t-vu̍t chhin-kiak châu-thet.3 Oi sin-kho Song-chú, chhin-li̍t hàng-san;oi chhai liá thi-song ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p.4 Oi yîn-vi Song-chú fôn-fôn hí-hí;Kì chhiu chiông ngì sîm só-oi ke su-pûn ngì.5 Oi chiông van-sṳ kâu-thok Song-chú;sin-kho Kì, Kì chhiu voi sṳ̀n-chhiòn.6 Kì voi pûn ngì ke chṳn-ngi chhiong kông án-ngiòng fat-chhut;Kì voi pûn ngì ke kûng-chṳn chhiong tông-chu ke ngit-thèu.7 Oi tiâm-tiâm chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn, nai-sîm tén-heu Kì;m̀-hó yîn-vi phe̍t-ngìn hàng-sṳ sun-li chhiu pot-at,mo̍k yîn-vi fái-ngìn ke kie-mèu sṳ̀n-kûng lòi put-phìn.8 M̀-hó fat-khién, ya m̀-hó nâu-ngìn;mo̍k pot-at, miên-tet ngì sa cho-fái.9 Cho-fái ke ngìn yit-thin voi su chién-chhù;chúng-he, ngióng-mong Song-chú ke ngìn chhiu nèn sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi( 34 37:9 "sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ôn-kî chhai liá-phién thú-thi song", hâ-poi chhut-hien kí-pái.).10 Yit-ngiap-muk, fái-ngìn chhiu sêu-me̍t thet-hi;ngì sióng-oi chhìm kì-têu, ya chhìm m̀-tó.11 Khiâm-pî ke ngìn chang nèn sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi;kì-têu voi hióng-su vù-khiùng ke hen-fuk!12 Fái-ngìn mèu-hoi ngi-ngìn,hiong kì ngâu-ngà chhiet-chhṳ́.13 Chúng-he Chú oi seu fái-ngìn,yîn-vi Kì tî kì-têu ke ma̍t-ngit voi-to lé.14 Fái-ngìn pâng-chhut tô-kiam, lâi-khôi kì-têu ke kiûng-chien,oi tá-vang khiùng-khú ke ngìn, sat-hoi chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.15 Chúng-he, kì-têu ke tô-kiam voi chhiuk-chhôn chhṳ-kâ ke sîm;hàn-yû, kì-têu ke kiûng-chien voi pûn-ngìn áu-thôn thet-hi.16 Ngi-ngìn só-yû ke sûi-yèn séu,yàng-ko fái-ngìn chhòi-sán chhin-tô.17 Yîn-vi fái-ngìn ke khièn-sṳ voi pho-me̍t;chúng-he Song-chú oi fù-chhṳ̀ ngi-ngìn.18 Song-chú tî vàn-chhiòn-ngìn ke su-ngièn;kì-têu ke sán-ngia̍p yún-yén chhòng-chhùn.19 Kì-têu chhai fam-nan ke ngit m̀-voi sṳt-mong,kî-fông ke sṳ̀-chiet hàn-he yû-hó páu-chiuk.20 Chúng-he, chok-ok ke ngìn chui-heu voi me̍t-mòng.Song-chú ke tui-thi̍t voi chhiong yâ-fâ chhia-thet;kì-têu voi chhiong yùn-yên khiung-yong sêu-san thet.21 Fái-ngìn chia-chhièn m̀-vàn;chúng-he ngi-ngìn thai-fông sṳ̂-sá.22 Song-chú su-fuk ke ngìn voi sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi;Kì só chu-chú ke yit-thin voi su chién-chhù.23 Song-chú na fôn-hí má-ngìn só-hàng ke lu,chhiu sṳ́ kì ke kiok-phu vún-tong;24 van-yit tiet-tó, ya m̀-voi tiet-to tap-tap,yîn-vi Song-chú fù-tén kì ke sú.25 Ngài chhiùng-se to liá-ha, yí-kîn sṳ̍t-to án-ló,màng-sṳt khon-ko yû ngi-ngìn pûn Song-chú piong-thet,m̀-sṳt khon-tó ngi-ngìn ke chṳ́-sûn cho khiet-sṳ̍t.26 Kì yit-chhṳ̍t kiu-chi ngìn, lo̍k-yi chia-pûn ngìn;kì ke chṳ́-sûn ya chhiòn-phu tet-tó fuk-hi!27 Ngì-têu oi lì-ok hàng-san,chhiu cho-tet yún-yén ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p.28 Yîn-vi Song-chú só-oi ke chhiu-he kûng-ngi;Kì m̀-voi piong-thet Kì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn.Kì-têu voi yún-yén tet-tó Kì ke pó-yu;chúng-he, fái-ngìn ke chṳ́-sûn voi su chién-chhù.29 Yit-chhiet ke ngi-ngìn voi sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi,chṳ́-chṳ́ sûn-sûn yún-kiú he̍t chhai liá-vi.30 Ngi-ngìn ke choi fat-chhut chṳ-fi ke fa;kì ke sa̍t-mà kóng-lun kûng-ngi ke sṳ.31 Kì ke sîm-chûng yû Song-ti ke kau-sṳ;kì ke kiok-phu yit-thin m̀-voi thiu-tó.32 Fái-ngìn kín thêu-khon ngi-ngìn,oi chhìm kî-fi sat-hoi kì;33 chúng-he Song-chú m̀-voi piong-thet ngi-ngìn,m̀-voi pûn kì lo̍k chhai sù-thi̍t sú-chûng,su sṳ́m-phan ya m̀-voi chṳ-to thin-chhui.34 Oi ngióng-mong Song-chú, chûn-hàng Kì ke tho-lu!Kì voi kô-sṳ̂n ngì, pûn ngì sṳ̀n-chiap thú-thi;ngì voi khon-tó chok-ok ke ngìn su chién-chhù.35 Ngài sṳt khon-ko yit-ke tông-ok ke fái-ngìn,chhiong Lì-pâ-nun ke hiông-pak-su( 35 37:35 "chhiong Lì-pâ-nun ke hiông-pak-su" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún; Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.) án kô-thai;36 heu-lòi ngài kîn-ko ke sṳ̀, kì sa m̀ kien-thet( 36 37:36 "heu-lòi ... m̀ kien-thet" ya-he yi̍t-cho "heu-lòi kì ko-sṳ̂n, tui ngìn-kiên sêu-sṳt thet-hi".);ngài chhìm-lòi yu chhìm-hi, chúng-he chhìm kì m̀-tó.37 Chu-yi vàn-chhiòn-ngìn, khon chṳn-chhṳ̍t-ngìn;oi fò-phìn ke ngìn chṳ́-sûn hîn-vong;38 chúng-he phoi-ngia̍k ke ngìn voi chhiòn-phu sêu-me̍t;fái-ngìn ke heu-thoi voi su chién-chhù!39 Song-chú he ngi-ngìn ke chṳ́n-kiu-chá,kì-têu fam-nan sṳ̀ ke phit-nan-só.40 Song-chú pông-chhu kì-têu, chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu;Kì kiu kì-têu thot-lì sià-ok ke ngìn,yîn-vi kì-têu chhìm-khiù Kì ke pí-yu.
