1 Song-ti he Vòng Song-chú chóng-khièn, Kì yung vî-ngiàm cho sâm;Song-chú chok-hó, yung nèn-li̍t cho yêu-tai.Sṳ-kie chhiu kiên-ku, m̀-voi yèu-thung.2 Song-chú â, Ngì ke pó-chho chhṳ-kú chhiu-yû;chhai song-kú yî-chhièn, Ngì chó chhiu chhùn-chhai.3 Song-chú â, chhṳ̂m-yên fat-chhut sâng-yîm;thai-súi ho-ho-kún, pô-long thâu-thiên.4 Song-chú chhai thiên-táng, chṳ-chûn vù-tui,khièn-piang yàng-ko thai-súi ke ho-sâng,nèn-li̍t thai-ko hói-song ke pô-long.5 Song-chú â, Ngì ke fap-lin kiên-thin;Ngì ke sṳn-thien khok-sṳ̍t he sṳ̀n-sṳn,chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén!
