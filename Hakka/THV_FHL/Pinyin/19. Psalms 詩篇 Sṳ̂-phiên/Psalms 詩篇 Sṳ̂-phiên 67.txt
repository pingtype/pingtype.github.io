1 Yit-phiên sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung sṳ̂-hièn ke ngo̍k-hi.Kám-ên ke Sṳ̂-kô Song-ti â, lìn-mén ngài-têu;khiù Ngì su-fuk pûn ngài-têu,yung chhṳ̀-oi tui-thai ngài-têu;2 hó pûn chhiòn sṳ-kie tî Ngì ke chṳ́-yi,van-koet lûng-chúng chhîn-chhú Ngì ke kiu-ên.3 Song-ti â, ngien van-mìn siung-chan Ngì;ngien sṳ-kiên-ngìn lûng-chúng siung-chan Ngì!4 Ngien van-pâng fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k lòi chhong-kô;yîn-vi Ngì yung kûng-chṳn sṳ́m-phan van-mìn,Ngì tai-liâng sṳ-kiên ke van-pâng van-koet.5 Song-ti â, ngien van-mìn siung-chan Ngì;ngien sṳ-kiên-ngìn lûng-chúng siung-chan Ngì!6 Thú-thi yû chhut-sán ńg-kuk;Song-ti — ngài-têu ke Song-ti yû su-fuk pûn ngài-têu( 59 67:6 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Ngien thú-thi chhut-sán ńg-kuk; ngien Song-ti — ngài-têu ke Song-ti su-fuk pûn ngài-têu".).7 Ngien Song-ti su-fuk pûn ngài-têu;ngien thiên-ha van-mìn kin-vi Kì!
