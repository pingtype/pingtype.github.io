1 Thai-ví sông-kîn ke sṳ̂.Khiâm-pî Sin-kho ke Khì-tó Song-chú â, ngài ke sîm m̀-kám kiêu-ngau,lièn ngài ke muk-chû ya m̀-kám chhṳ-kô;ngài m̀-kám thet chhṳ-sin nèn cho thai-sṳ,ya m̀-kám khoâ-khiéu oi hàng thai khì-sṳ.2 Ngài ke sîm phìn-chhin yu chhṳ-chhai,chhiong tón-nen ke ô-nga-é chhai kiâ-mê fài-chûng tiâm-tiâm;ngài ke sîm ya chhiong tón-nen ke ô-nga-é ôn-nèn chhṳ-chhai.3 Yî-set-lie̍t â, oi ngióng-mong Song-chú,tui liá-ha chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén!
