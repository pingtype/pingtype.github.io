1 Thai-ví ke chan-mî sṳ̂.Siung-chan ke Sṳ̂-kô Ngài ke Song-ti, ngài ke Vòng â,ngài oi chhòn-yòng Ngì ke ví-thai!Ngài oi siung-chan Ngì ke miàng,chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén!2 Ngài oi tak-ngit siung-chan Ngì;ngài oi chan-mî Ngì ke miàng chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén!3 Song-chú chṳ-thai, chui khâm-tet su chan-mî;Kì ke ví-thai, mò-ngìn chhet-liòng tet-chhut.4 Sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi oi siên-yòng Ngì ke chok-vì;kì-têu oi chhòn-kóng Ngì thai-nèn ke kûng-chok.5 Kì-têu oi kóng-hí Ngì ke yùng-kông lâu chûn-ngiàm;ngài oi kín-ngiam Ngì khì-meu ke chok-vì.6 Kì-têu oi kóng-lun Ngì tet-ngìn-kiâng ke chok-vì ngióng-pân yû nèn-li̍t;ngài oi siên-yòng Ngì ke ví-thai.7 Kì-têu oi chhòn-yòng Ngì fûng-sṳn ke liòng-san,fôn-hí chhong-kô lòi siung-chan Ngì ke kûng-ngi.8 Song-chú yû ên-tién lâu lìn-mén,mò sùi-phien fat-khién, mân-yû put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi.9 Song-chú ên-thai van-yû,lìn-mén Kì só-chho ke yit-chhiet.10 Song-chú â, Ngì só chho ke yit-chhiet oi kám-chhia Ngì;Ngì chûng-sîm ke chṳ́-mìn oi siung-chan Ngì.11 Kì-têu oi kóng-hí Ngì vòng-khièn ke yùng-kông,thàm-lun Ngì ke thai-nèn;12 kì-têu oi pûn sṳ-kiên-ngìn tî Ngì thai-nèn ke chok-vì,ngin-sṳt Ngì koet-thu ke yùng-kông lâu chûn-ngiàm.13 Ngì ke koet-thu yún-yén chhòng-chhùn;Ngì ke vòng-khièn chhṳ̍t-to van-thoi.Song-chú ke yin-hí chhiòn-phu sin-sṳ̍t khó-kho;Kì só-cho ke yit-chhiet hién-chhut Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi( 129 145:13 "Song-chú ... chhṳ̀-oi" he kîn-kí yit-pún chhâu-pún lâu yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún.).14 Voi tiet-tó ke, Song-chú fù-tén kì-têu;pûn-ngìn ap-chṳ ke, Kì fù-chhṳ̀ kì-têu.15 Chung-sên ke muk-chû chhiòn-phu ngióng-mong Ngì;Ngì cheu sṳ̀-chiet su sṳ̍t-vu̍t pûn kì-têu.16 Ngì ke sú yit-ha tá-khôi,chung-sên chhiu tet-tó páu-chiuk.17 Song-chú só-hàng ke chok-vì chhiòn-phu he kûng-ngi;Kì só-cho ke yit-chhiet hién-chhut Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi.18 Song-chú chhîn-khiun só-yû khiù-ham Kì ke ngìn,chhiu-he yit-chhiet sṳ̀n-sîm khiù-ham Kì ke ngìn.19 Kì oi sṳ̀n-chhiòn kin-vi Kì ke ngìn ke sîm-ngien;Kì oi thâng kì-têu ke khiù-ham, chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu.20 Song-chú pó-fu yit-chhiet kin-oi Kì ke ngìn;chúng-he Kì oi sêu-me̍t só-yû sià-ok ke ngìn.21 Ngài oi khôi-choi chan-mî Song-chú;ngien yit-chhiet su-chho ke siung-chan Kì ke sṳn-miàng,chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén.
