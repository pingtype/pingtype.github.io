1 Â-sat ke sṳ̂-kô.Khién-khiù Tá-thui Yî-set-lie̍t ke Sù-thi̍t Song-ti â, khiù Ngì m̀-hó tiâm-tiâm m̀ khôi-choi!Song-ti â, khiù Ngì m̀-hó tiâm-tiâm m̀ chhut-sâng!2 Khon â, Ngì ke sù-thi̍t liá-ha tông chho-fán;yen-hen Ngì ke ngìn thèu-nà ngo-to kô-kô.3 Kì-têu yîm-mèu oi thùng Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn chok-tui;kì-têu sat-kie ham-hoi Ngì só pí-yu ke ngìn.4 Kì-têu kóng: "Lòi â, lòi sêu-me̍t kì-têu ke koet-kâ,sṳ́ Yî-set-lie̍t ke miàng yún-yén mò-ngìn chai ki-ngiam!"5 Kì-têu thùng-sîm khiung-ha chhâm-siòng,pí-chhṳ́ kiet-mèn oi fán-khong Ngì:6 Chhiu-he Yî-tûng-ngìn, Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li-ngìn,hàn-yû Mô-ap-ngìn lâu Ha-kap-ngìn,7 yû Kâ-pâ-le̍t-ngìn, Â-mùn-ngìn, Â-mâ-li̍t-ngìn,thùng-sṳ̀ hàn-yû Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn lâu Thai-ngì-ngìn.8 Â-sut-ngìn ya thùng kì-têu kiêu-kiet,cho Lò-tet heu-thoi ke lièn-mèn.9 Khiù Ngì tui-fu kì-têu, chhiong tui-fu Mí-thien-ngìn yit-yong,chhiong chhai Kî-sun hò-sùn tui-fu Sî-sî-lâ lâu Yâ-pîn.10 Ngì chhai Yún-Tô-ngí chiông kì-têu sêu-me̍t;kì-têu ke sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ chhai thi-song mièn-thet.11 Khiù Ngì tui-fu kì-têu ke chiông-kiûn,chhiong tui-fu Ngò-li̍p lâu Sî-yî-pak;ya sêu-me̍t kì-têu ke thúng-chhṳ-chá,chhiong sêu-me̍t Sî-pâ lâu Sat-mu-nâ;12 yîn-vi kì-têu kóng: "Ngài-têu lòi-hi chhióng,lòi-hi cham ke su̍k Song-ti ke thú-thi."13 Ngài ke Song-ti â, tá-san kì-têu, chhiong chhôi-san chhṳ̀n-fôi;khiù Ngì sṳ́ kì-têu chhiong lùng-hông pûn fûng kién-chéu yit-yong.14 Chhiong fó sêu-kông su-lìm,fó-yam sêu-phien sân-liâng,15 cheu-yong, khiù Ngì yung khòng-fûng lòi tûi kì-têu,yung phau-yí lòi pûn kì-têu kiâng-to voi sí.16 Song-chú â, khiù Ngì sṳ́ kì-têu mân-mien kien-seu,án-ngiòng, kì-têu chang voi sṳ̀n-ngin Ngì ví-thai ke miàng.17 Ngien kì-têu yún-yén sṳt-phai chho̍k-kiâng!Ngien kì-têu chhai siû-yu̍k chûng me̍t-mòng!18 Ngien kì-têu tî: Chṳ́-yû Ngì — Song-chú,he chhiòn-thi ke Chṳ-kô-chá!
