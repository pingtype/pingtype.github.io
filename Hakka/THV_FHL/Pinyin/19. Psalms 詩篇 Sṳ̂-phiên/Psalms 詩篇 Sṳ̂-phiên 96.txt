1 Song-ti he Chṳ-kô ke Vòng( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 16:23-33 ) Oi chhong sîn ke sṳ̂-kô chan-mî Song-chú!Chhiòn sṳ-kie tû oi lòi kô-siung Song-chú!2 Oi tui Song-chú chhong-kô, oi siung-chan Kì ke miàng!Oi tak-ngit chhòn-yòng Kì chṳ́n-kiu ke fuk-yîm!3 Oi tui van-koet siên-chhòn Kì ke yùng-kông!Oi tui van-mìn kóng-hí Kì ví-thai ke chok-vì!4 Song-chú he chui ví-thai ke, yin-tông su chui-thai ke chan-mî;Kì yin-tông pí só-yû ke sṳ̀n-mìn tet-tó kha-tô ke kin-vi.5 Lie̍t-koet só-yû ke sṳ̀n-mìn put-ko he ngiéu-siong;chúng-he chhóng-chho chû-thiên ke chhiu-he Song-chú.6 Yùng-kông, vî-ngiàm chhai Kì ke mien-chhièn;thai-nèn, yùng-fà chhûng-mân Kì ke sṳn-só.7 Thi-song ke van-chhu̍k â, yin-tông siung-chan Song-chú!Ngì-têu oi siung-chan Kì ke yùng-kông lâu thai-nèn!8 Oi siung-chan Song-chú yùng-kông ke miàng;oi tai chi-vu̍t lòi chin-ngi̍p Kì ke sṳn-thien.9 Oi yung sṳn-kiet ke tá-pan lòi khúi-pai Song-chú( 86 96:9 "Oi yung ... Song-chú" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Tông Song-chú hién-sṳn ke sṳ̀, oi lòi khúi-pai Kì" ya-he "Oi khúi-pai sṳn-kiet vî-ngiàm ke Song-chú".);chhiòn-thi oi chhai Kì ke mien-chhièn pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat!10 Oi hiong van-koet siên-pu: "Song-chú chóng-khièn!Thai-thi kiên-ku, m̀-voi yèu-thung;Kì oi yung kûng-chṳn lòi sṳ́m-phan van-mìn."11 Chû-thiên oi fôn-hí, thai-thi oi khoai-lo̍k!Hói lâu khì-chûng ke sâng-vu̍t â, oi thai-sâng fôn-fû!12 Thièn-yèn lâu khì-chûng ke chhut-sán â, oi fôn-hí hí-lòi!Ke-sṳ̀, su-nà chûng ke su-muk oi chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn fôn-hí chhong-kô;13 yîn-vi Song-chú oi lòi,Kì oi lòi chhṳ-lî thiên-ha.Kì oi yung kûng-ngi thúng-chhṳ sṳ-kie;Kì oi yung sin-sṳ̍t tui-thai van-mìn.
