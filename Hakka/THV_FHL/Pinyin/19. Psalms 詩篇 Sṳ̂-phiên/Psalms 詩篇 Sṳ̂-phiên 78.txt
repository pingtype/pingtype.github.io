1 Â-sat ke kau-hiun-sṳ̂.Song-ti lâu Kì ke Chṳ́-mìn Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn â, oi liù-sîm thâng ngài ke kau-thô;ngì-têu oi chet-ngí thâng ngài só-kóng ke fa.2 Ngài oi yung pí-yi lòi sot-mìn;ngài oi kóng li̍t-sṳ́ ke o-meu:3 chhiu-he ngài-têu só thâng-ko só-tî ke sṳ,he ngài-têu ke chú-siên lâu ngài-têu kóng ke.4 Ngài-têu mò-oi tui chṳ́-sûn khong-tén liá-têu sṳ-chhìn;ngài-têu yit-thin oi tui ha yit-thoi ke ngìn sot-mìn:Song-chú ke mî-san, Kì ke thai-nèn,lâu Kì yit-chhiet khì-meu ke chok-vì.5 Kì pân-pu fap-lin pûn Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi;Kì chṳ-thin kie-min pûn Yî-set-lie̍t ngìn-mìn.Kì ha min-lin pûn ngài-têu ke chú-siên,oi chiông Kì ke fap-li̍t chhòn-pûn chṳ́-sûn,6 pûn heu-thoi ngìn yû-hó ho̍k-si̍p,yu chhòn-pûn kì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn.7 Án-ngiòng, kì-têu ya voi sin-kho Song-ti,m̀-voi m̀ ki-tet Song-ti só-cho ke sṳ,fán-chón voi chûn-sú Kì ke kie-min.8 Kì-têu chang m̀-voi chhiong chú-siên yit-yong;kì-têu he ngang-kiáng phoi-ngia̍k ke pak-siang,tui Song-ti mò kiên-thin ke sin-sîm,tui Kì ya mò yún-kiú ke chûng-sîm.9 Yî-fap-lièn ke chṳ́-sûn sûi-yèn yû kiûng-chien,chúng-he khôi-chan ke-ngit chhiu chón-sṳ̂n thò-chéu.10 Kì-têu mò chûn-sú Song-ti ke yok;kì-têu m̀ fu̍k-chhiùng Kì ke kau-sṳ.11 Kì-têu m̀ ki-tet Kì só-cho ke-têu sṳ-chhìn,m̀ ki-tet Kì hàng pûn kì-têu khon ke sṳ̀n-chiak.12 Song-ti chhai Só-ôn ke phìn-ngièn, chhai Âi-khi̍p,chhai kì-têu chú-siên ke mien-chhièn hàng sṳ̀n-chiak.13 Kì chiông hói fûn-khôi, tai-liâng kì-têu ko-hi;Kì sṳ́ hói-súi chhiong chhiòng-piak án-ngiòng khî-tén.14 Ngit-sṳ̀-thèu, Kì yung yùn-chhái tai-liâng kì-têu;am-pû-sṳ̀, Kì yung fó-kông yîn-thô kì-têu.15 Kì chhai khóng-yâ sṳ́ phàn-sa̍k lie̍t-khôi,pûn kì-têu yû chhiong chhṳ̂m-yên án-tô ke súi hó-lîm.16 Kì sṳ́ phàn-sa̍k phun-chhut chhin-tô súi,chhiong hò-pa súi án-ngiòng liù-tén hâ.17 Chúng-he, kì-têu hàn ki-siu̍k tet-chhui Song-ti,chhai khóng-yâ phoi-ngia̍k Chṳ-kô-chá.18 Kì-têu ku-yi sṳ-tham Song-ti,sùi-sîm só-oi ke thó sṳ̍t-vu̍t.19 Kì-têu siet-thu̍k Song-ti, kóng:"Song-ti nèn chhai khóng-yâ phan chok-si̍t mâ?20 Yû-yáng, Kì sṳ̍t-chhai yû tá phàn-sa̍k,pûn súi phun-chhut, chhiong hò-pa súi liù-tén hâ;chúng-he Kì hàn-yû phan-fap pûn ngài-têu liòng-sṳ̍t mâ?Kì yû fap-thu vi Kì ke chṳ́-mìn yi-phi ngiuk mâ?"21 Song-chú thâng-tó liá-ke fa chhiu thai fat-khién.Kì yung fó kûng-kit Kì ke chṳ́-mìn Ngâ-kok;Kì tui Yî-set-lie̍t khién-to fó-hi tông-chho̍k;22 yîn-vi kì-têu m̀ siông-sin Song-ti,ya m̀ yí-kho Kì ke chṳ́n-kiu.23 Chúng-he, Kì hàn-he hiong thiên ha-lin,fûn-fu tá-khôi thiên-mùn;24 Kì tui thiên-táng su kì-têu liòng-sṳ̍t,kong-hâ mâ-nâ lòi yông-yuk kì-têu.25 Ngìn chhiu sṳ̍t-tó thiên-sṳ́ ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t;he Song-ti pûn kì-têu tet-tó páu-chiuk.26 Kì siên pûn thiên-khûng chhôi-hí tûng-fûng,yu yung khièn-nèn ham nàm-fûng chhôi-lòi.27 Kì sṳ́ ngiuk chhiong chhṳ̀n-fôi án-ngiòng kong hâ-lòi,sṳ́ fî-niâu chhiong hói-sâ án-tô su-pûn kì-têu;28 Kì sṳ́ fî-niâu lo̍k chhai yàng-fòng chûng,chhai kì-têu pu-phùng ke si chû-vì.29 Kì-têu chhiòn-phu yû-hó sṳ̍t, sṳ̍t-to páu-pi̍t-pi̍t;Song-ti cheu kì-têu só thâm-oi ke su-pûn kì-têu.30 Chúng-he kì-têu hàn tông thâm-sṳ̍t,kì-têu ke choi hàn chheu-tén ke sṳ̀,31 Song-ti chhiu tui kì-têu thai fat-khién,chhṳ̀-sí kì-têu tông-chûng ke hó-hon,sû-thet Yî-set-lie̍t chui yû-siu ke heu-sâng-ngìn.32 Sûi-yèn kîn-li̍t liá yit-chhiet,kì-têu hàn-he ki-siu̍k fam-chhui;chhiu-son fat-sên liá-têu khì-sṳ,kì-têu hàn-he m̀-hén siông-sin.33 Só-yî Kì sṳ́ kì-têu ke ngièn-ngit pien-cho hî-khûng,sṳ́ kì-têu ke sâng-miang fut-yèn-kiên tù-tó châi-nan.34 Kì yit chhṳ̀-ngìn ke sṳ̀, kì-têu chhiu khiù-ham Kì;kì-têu fí-kói, pet-chhiet chhìm-khiù Song-ti.35 Kì-têu sióng-hí Song-ti he kì-têu ke phàn-sa̍k,Chṳ-kô ke Song-ti he kì-têu ke kiu-su̍k-chá.36 Chúng-he kì-têu kóng fâ-liâu phien Kì;kì-têu tui Kì kóng hî-ká ke fa.37 Kì-têu tui Kì chhin mò chûng-sîm,ya mò chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t sú Kì ke yok.38 Sûi-yèn án-ngiòng, Song-ti thung-siak Kì ke chṳ́-mìn:Kì sa-miên kì-têu ke chhui, mò sêu-me̍t kì-têu;yit-pái yu yit-pái, Kì ngiûn-tén mò thai fat-khién.39 Kì thí-sit kì-têu put-ko he ngiuk-sṳ̂n thin-thin,chhiong yit-chhṳn fûng, chhôi ko-hi chhiu mò to-chón-lòi.40 Kì-têu chhai khóng-yâ chai-sâm phoi-ngia̍k Song-ti;kì-têu yit-chhṳ̍t chhai sâ-mo̍k sṳ́ Kì sông-sîm!41 Kì-têu yit-pái yu yit-pái sṳ-tham Song-ti,ngiâ Yî-set-lie̍t ke Chṳ-sṳn-chá thai fat-khién.42 Kì-têu m̀ ki-tet Kì vù-khiùng ke thai-nèn,pin Kì kiu kì-têu thot-lì sù-thi̍t ke ngit-è.43 Kì-têu m̀ ki-tet Kì chhai Âi-khi̍p hàng ke sṳ̀n-chiak,pin Kì chhai Só-ôn phìn-ngièn só-cho ke khì-sṳ:44 Kì chiông hò-pa ke súi pien-cho hiet,sṳ́ Âi-khi̍p-ngìn lîm m̀-tet.45 Kì sṳ́ vû-yìn kûi-khiùn lòi khiàm-tuk kì-têu,sṳ́ hà-mà châu-tha̍p kì-têu ke thièn-thi.46 Kì sṳ́ kì-têu ke ńg-kuk pûn se chhó-máng sṳ̍t-thet,ham thai chhó-máng chiông kì-têu ke thièn-yèn sún chhiang-chhiang.47 Kì yung pên-pho̍k fí-me̍t kì-têu ke phù-thò-su,lo̍k sông lòi tá-fái kì-têu ke vù-fâ-kó-su.48 Kì yung pên-pho̍k te̍p-sí kì-têu ke ngiù-khiùn,yu yung ngiap-lang( 73 78:48 "Kì yung pên-pho̍k te̍p-sí ... yu yung ngiap-lang" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kì yung chhi̍t-phiang sat-sí ... yu yung pot-vûn".) sông-hoi kì-têu ke yòng-khiùn.49 Kì hiong kì-têu fat-chhut lie̍t-nu, su-hi, châi-fo;Kì ke thai fat-khién chhiong sí ke sṳ́-chá yit-yong tá-kit kì-têu.50 Kì mò chai ngiûn-tén Kì ke nu-hi;Kì m̀-hén ngièu kì-têu ke sin-miang;Kì sṳ́ kì-têu pot-vûn lòi sí-thet.51 Kì chhṳ̀-sí Âi-khi̍p tak-kâ ke chóng-chṳ́,chhiu-he Hàm vuk-hâ yúng-chong ke thèu-thôi.52 Heu-lòi, Kì chhiu-chhiong mu̍k-chá yit-yong,chiông chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-mìn tai chhut-lòi,ya tai-liâng kì-têu kîn-ko khóng-yâ.53 Chhai Kì ke tai-liâng chṳ̂-ha, kì-têu chhin phìn-ôn, mò-kiâng mò-sèu;chúng-he kì-têu ke thi̍t-ngìn chhiu pûn hói-súi chim-sí chhiang-chhiang.54 Kì thu kì-têu to Kì ke sṳn-thi,lòi-to Kì chhîn-chhṳ chṳ̂n-fu̍k ke sân.55 Kì chhai Kì chṳ́-mìn ke mien-chhièn chiông ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn kiuk-chéu,chiông ke thú-thi fûn-phi pûn Kì ke chṳ́-mìn,chiông ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn ke kâ-yèn kâu-pûn Yî-set-lie̍t ke kok kî-chhu̍k.56 Chúng-he, kì-têu sṳ-tham Chṳ-kô ke Song-ti,phoi-ngia̍k Kì, m̀-hén fu̍k-chhiùng Kì ke kie-min.57 Kì-têu thùng chú-siên yit-yong phoi-ngia̍k, mò chûng-sîm;kì-têu chhiong sûng-thet ke kiûng yit-yong kho m̀-tet.58 Kì-têu hí ngoi-pâng sṳ̀n-mìn ke chi-thàn lòi ngiâ Kì fat-khién;kì-têu yung tiâu-khiet ke ngiéu-siong lòi yîn-hí Kì ke chhit-tu.59 Song-ti yit thâng-tó chhiu fat-khién,chiông Yî-set-lie̍t vàn-chhiòn hi-chhie̍t.60 Kì fit-thet ke hí chhai Sṳ-lò( 74 78:60 "Sṳ-lò" he Thai-ví-vòng yî-chhièn, Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn kin-pai Song-ti ke chûng-sîm sṳn-só.) ke sṳn-só,chhiu-he Kì chhai ngìn-kiên só tap ke chong-phùng.61 Ke chông-hién Kì ke khièn-nèn lâu yùng-kông ke yok-khui,Kì yù-chhai kì pûn thi̍t-ngìn chhióng-chéu.62 Kì tui chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-mìn fat-khién,pûn kì-têu sí chhai tô-kiam chṳ̂-ha.63 Kì-têu ke heu-sâng pûn chan-fó sêu-me̍t;kì-têu ke moi-é chhìm-mò ngìn hó-ka.64 Kì-têu ke chi-sṳ̂ pûn tô-kiam chhṳ̀-sí;kì-têu chûng-kiên ke koá-fu mò-fap kieu.65 Chui-heu, Chú chhiong soi-siáng yit-yong,chhiong yúng-sṳ chiú-heu chhîn-siáng.66 Kì tá-thui Kì ke thi̍t-ngìn,pûn kì-têu yún-yén kien-seu.67 Kì hi-chhie̍t Yok-sit ke heu-thoi,mò kién-sién Yî-fap-lièn kî-chhu̍k.68 Kì kién-sién ke he Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k,hàn-yû Kì só-siak ke Siak-ôn-sân.69 Kì chhai ke-vi hí sṳn-thien, kô-to tù-thiên,chhiong thai-thi khî-to chhai-chhai, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén.70 Kì kién-sién Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn Thai-ví,tui yòng-làn tông-chûng tho̍k kì chhut-lòi,71 pûn kì m̀-sṳ́ chai chóng yòng-khiùn,li̍p kì cho Yî-set-lie̍t ke vòng,cho Song-ti chṳ́-mìn ke mu̍k-chá.72 Thai-ví chhiòn-sîm mu̍k-yông kì-têu;kì chhin chîn-mìn tai-liâng kì-têu.
