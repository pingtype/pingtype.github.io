1 Song-ti he Ngìn-lui ke Sṳ́m-phan-chá Song-chú â, Ngì he sṳ̂n-yên ke Song-ti;sṳ̂n-yên ke Song-ti â, Ngì hó fat-fó lé!2 Ngì he chhiòn ngìn-lui ke sṳ́m-phan-chá;khiù Ngì hong-hí-lòi po-yin kiêu-ngau ke ngìn!3 Song-chú â, fái-ngìn ke ngit-è yû kí-tô?Kì-têu yòng-yòng tet-yi oi to kí-sṳ̀ nè?4 Chok-ok ke ngìn khoâ-khiéu oi to kí-sṳ̀?Kì-têu hàn-oi kiêu-ngau to kí-sṳ̀ nè?5 Song-chú â, kì-têu ap-chṳ Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn;kì-têu pet-hoi ke-têu su̍k Ngì ke ngìn.6 Kì-têu sat-hoi koá-fu lâu kû-yì,yu mèu-sat chhiam-he̍t ke sâng-fun-ngìn.7 Kì-têu kóng: "Song-chú m̀-voi khon-tó;Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti m̀-voi koân-sîm."8 Ngì-têu yâ-màn-ngìn â, ngióng-pân án nga-ta?Ngì-têu ngong-tong ngìn â, kí-sṳ̀ chang mìn-pha̍k?9 Song-ti chho ngài-têu ke ngí-kûng,Kì kám m̀-voi thâng?Song-ti chho ngài-têu ke muk-chû,Kì kám m̀-voi khon?10 Kì kón-lî van-koet,kám m̀-voi chit-fa̍t ngìn?Kì he ngìn-lui ke thó-sṳ̂,kám voi mò chṳ-sṳt?11 Song-chú chhîn-chhú ngìn ke sîm-sṳ̂;kì-têu só-sióng chhiòn-he hî-khûng.12 Song-chú â, Ngì só kón-kau ke ngìn yû fuk-hi,chhiu-he Ngì yung kau-sṳ lòi chṳ́-thô ke ngìn!13 Kì chhai fam-nan chûng, Ngì sṳ́ kì phìn-ôn,chhṳ̍t-to fái-ngìn tiet-lo̍k chhṳ̂m-hâng me̍t-mòng.14 Song-chú m̀-voi fit-thet Kì ke chṳ́-mìn;Kì m̀-voi piong-thet su̍k Kì ke ngìn.15 Kûng-ngi thin-chho̍k voi chhai fap-thìn chai chhut-hien;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn lûng-chúng voi vì-fu chṳn-ngi.16 Má-ngìn vi-tó ngài hong-hí-lòi kûng-kit fái-ngìn?Má-ngìn khî chhai ngài liá-phién tui-khong chok-ok ke ngìn?17 Song-chú na mò pông-chhu ngài,ngài chó chhiu lo̍k yîm-kiên( 85 94:17 "lo̍k yîm-kiên": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "he̍t chhai tiâm-chhin ke só-chhai".) lô!18 Tak-pái, ngài kóng: "Ngài ke kiok tá-thong-thiu lé!"Song-chú â, Ngì put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi chhiu fù-chhṳ̀ ngài.19 Ngài chhâu-fàn yù-sèu ke sṳ̀-chiet,Ngì ôn-ví ngài, pûn ngài khoai-lo̍k.20 Ngì thùng fú-phai ke kôn-yèn mò lòi-vông;kì-têu sṳ́ put-ngi ke sàng-cho ha̍p-fap.21 Kì-têu kiêu-kiet mèu-hoi ngi-ngìn,chiông mò-chhui ke ngìn phan sí-hìn.22 Chúng-he Song-chú he ngài ke yeu-sai;ngài ke Song-ti he ngài phit-nan ke phàn-sa̍k.23 Kì oi yîn-vi kì-têu ke sià-ok po-yin kì-têu,cheu kì-têu só-fam ke chhui lòi sêu-me̍t kì-têu;Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti oi sêu-me̍t kì-têu.
