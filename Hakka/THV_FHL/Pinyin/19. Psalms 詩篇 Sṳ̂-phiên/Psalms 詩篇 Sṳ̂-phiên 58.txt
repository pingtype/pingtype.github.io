1 Thai-ví só khat ke sṳ̂( 51 58:1 "Thai-ví só khat ke sṳ̂": Yi-sṳ chṳ́ thi̍t-phe̍t khat chhai sa̍k-thèu fe̍t khì-thâ hi-vu̍t song ke sṳ̂, ya-he yi̍t-cho "Thai-ví ke Kîm-sṳ̂".), kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, thiau yung Mo̍k Phai-fai.Khiù Song-ti Chit-fa̍t Chok-ok ke Ngìn Thúng-chhṳ-chá( 52 58:1 "Thúng-chhṳ-chá" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Sṳ̀n-mìn".) â, ngì-têu chṳn-kîn kóng chṳn-ngi he-mò?Ngì-têu chṳn-kîn cheu kûng-tong sṳ́m-phan ngìn-mìn he-mò?2 M̀-he! Ngì-têu yit-sîm sióng-oi chok-ok;ngì-têu chhai sṳ-kiên hàng khiòng-phau ke sṳ.3 Chok-ok ke ngìn yit chhut-se chhiu mì-sṳt fông-hiong;kóng fâ-liâu ke lì-khôi mû-thôi chhiu hàng chhâ-lu.4 Kì-têu fî-sòng ok-thu̍k, chhiong thu̍k-sà yit-yong;set-tén ngí-kûng, chhiong ngí-lûng ke fan-sṳ̀-chhung,5 thâng m̀-tó káu-sà-ngìn phùn tha̍k-é ke sâng,ya thâng m̀-tó mô-su̍t-sṳ̂ ngiam-chu ke sâng.6 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì khau-thôn kì-têu choi-tú ke thu̍k-ngà!Song-chú â, khiù Ngì pâng-thet ke-têu sṳ̂-é ke thai-ngà!7 Ngien kì-têu chhiong hò-pa-súi châu-thet,chhiong se-lu ke chhó pûn-ngìn chien-tha̍p( 53 58:7 "chhiong se-lu ... chien-tha̍p": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).8 Ngien kì-têu chhiong yè-lò án-ngiòng mièn-thet,chhiong lau-thet ke thôi mò kien-tó ngit-thèu.9 Ngì-têu yung net-su lòi sêu-fó, vo̍k-é hàn-màng sêu,thai-fûng chiông chhiâng-chhiâng màng châu ke lâu sêu-tén ke khiung-ha chhôi-chéu;chhiong án-ngiòng, ngien kì-têu kiak-kiak su sêu-me̍t( 54 58:9 Pún-chiet chhai Hî-pak-lòi-vùn yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).10 Ngi-ngìn khon-tó chhui-ngìn su po-yin chhiu fôn-hí na-thiên;kì-têu oi chhai chok-ok ke ngìn hiet-hò chûng hàng to-chón-lòi.11 Án-ngiòng, ngìn oi kóng: "Ngi-ngìn chṳn-kîn yû san-po;ton-chṳ̂n yû yit-vi Song-ti chhai sṳ́m-phan sṳ-kie!"
