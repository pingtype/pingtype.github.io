1 Song-ti he Chṳ-kô ke Chú-chái Song-chú chóng-khièn!Thai-thi â, oi fôn-hí hí-lòi!Só-yû ke hói-tó â, oi fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k!2 Yùn-tôi lâu vû-am chhai Kì chû-vì;chṳn-ngi lâu kûng-tong he Kì koet-thu ke kî-chhú.3 Yû fó hàng chhai Kì ke thèu-chhièn,sêu-me̍t Kì chû-vì ke sù-thi̍t.4 Kì ke ngiap-lang cheu-liong sṳ-kie;thai-thi khon-tó kín-chûn kín-chhat.5 Khiùn-sân chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn,chhai chhiòn-thi ke Chú mien-chhièn,chhiu-chhiong la̍p án-ngiòng yùng-thet.6 Chû-thiên siên-yòng Kì ke kûng-ngi;van-mìn khon-tó Kì ke yùng-kông.7 Yit-chhiet kin-pai ngiéu-siong ke ngìn voi kien-seu;kho ká-sṳ̀n lòi khoâ-khiéu ke ngìn voi lau-khui;van-sṳ̀n lûng-chúng oi phu̍k( 87 97:7 "van-sṳ̀n lûng-chúng oi phu̍k" ya-he yi̍t-cho "van-sṳ̀n â, ngì-têu lûng-chúng oi phu̍k".) chhai Song-chú mien-chhièn.8 Song-chú â, yîn-vi Ngì ke phan-ton kûng-chṳn,Siak-ôn ke ngìn-mìn thâng-tó chhiu thai fôn-hí;lièn Yù-thai ke-têu sàng-sṳ ya chhin khoai-lo̍k.9 Song-chú â, Ngì he chṳ-kô ke,Ngì thúng-chhṳ thiên-ha;Ngì he chṳ-chûn vù-tui ke,Ngì chhêu-ko van-sṳ̀n.10 Song-chú thung-siak ke-têu nâu sià-ok ke ngìn( 88 97:10 "Song-chú thung-siak ke-têu nâu sià-ok ke ngìn": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Kin-oi Song-chú ke ngìn â, ngì-têu oi nâu sià-ok".);Kì pó-fu Kì chûng-sîm chṳ́-mìn ke sâng-miang,chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì fái-ngìn ke sṳ-li̍t.11 Ngi-ngìn voi tet-tó kông-mìn cheu-liong;sîm-kôn chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke voi thai fôn-hí.12 Ngi-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi yîn-vi Song-chú fôn-hí!Ngì-têu oi kám-chhia Kì, siung-chan Kì ke sṳn-miàng!
