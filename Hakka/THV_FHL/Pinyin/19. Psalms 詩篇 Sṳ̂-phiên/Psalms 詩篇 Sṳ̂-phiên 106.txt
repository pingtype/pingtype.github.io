1 Song-ti Yung Chhṳ̀-oi Tui-thai Kì Phoi-ngia̍k ke Chṳ́-mìn Hâ-li-lu-yá!Oi kám-chhia Song-chú, yîn-vi Kì he chṳ-san;Kì ke chhṳ̀-oi yún-yén chhòng-chhùn!2 Song-chú só-cho ke thai-sṳ, má-ngìn kóng tet-chhin?Yû má-ngìn nèn kóng-mìn Kì yit-chhiet ke mî-san?3 Hàng-sṳ kûng-phìn, yit-chhṳ̍t sṳ̂n-chông kûng-ngi ke,liá-chúng ke ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi!4 Song-chú â, Ngì hó khoán-thai Ngì chṳ́-mìn ke sṳ̀,khiù Ngì ku-ngiam ngài;Ngì chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu ke sṳ̀,khiù Ngì pông-chhu ngài.5 Khiù Ngì pûn ngài khon-tó Ngì sién-mìn ke hen-fuk,ya oi pûn ngài fûn-hióng Ngì chṳ́-mìn ke fôn-hí,pûn ngài thùng su̍k Ngì ke ngìn khiung-ha siung-chan Ngì.6 Ngài-têu thùng chú-siên yit-yong fam-liáu chhui;ngài-têu lûng-chúng chok-ok, cho fái-sṳ.7 Ngài-têu ke chú-siên chhai Âi-khi̍p ke sṳ̀,m̀ mìn-pha̍k Song-ti khì-meu ke chok-vì;kì-têu m̀ ki-tet Kì fûng-sṳn ke chhṳ̀-oi,fán-chón chhai Fùng-hói chhièn chhiu phoi-ngia̍k Chhiòn-nèn-chá( 94 106:7 "chhai ... Chhiòn-nèn-chá" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chhai hói-sùn, chhiu-he chhai Fùng-hói chhièn phoi-ngia̍k".).8 Chúng-he, Song-ti hàn-he yîn-vi Kì ke miàng kiu kì-têu,vi-tó oi hién-mìn Kì chhṳ-kâ ke thai khièn-nèn.9 Kì chit-phi Fùng-hói, hói-súi chhiu châu-thet;Kì tai kì-têu tui hói-tái hàng ko-hi, chhiong hàng châu-thi.10 Kì kiu kì-têu thot-lì yen-hen kì-têu ke ngìn;Kì tui sù-thi̍t ke sú-chûng kiu kì-têu chhut-lòi.11 Súi chiông sù-thi̍t yim-sí;lièn yit-sà ya mò chhûn.12 Ke-sṳ̀, kì-têu chang siông-sin Kì ke yin-hí;yîn-vi án-ngiòng, kì-têu chhong-kô siung-chan Kì.13 Kì-têu chhin-kiak chhiu m̀ ki-tet Kì ke chok-vì;kì-têu ngiàm-sṳ̀ chhiu m̀-hén tén-heu Kì ke chṳ́-yi.14 Chhai khóng-yâ, kì-têu chhûng-mân thâm-sṳ̍t ke sîm,chhai sâ-mo̍k, kì-têu sṳ-tham Song-ti.15 Kì cheu kì-têu só-khiù ke su-pûn kì-têu;chúng-he, Kì ya kong-hâ phiang-thung chhai kì-têu chûng-kiên.16 Chhai yàng-fòng chûng kì-têu chhit-tu Mô-sî,lâu Song-chú fûn-phe̍t vì-sṳn ke Â-lùn.17 Thi chhiu lie̍t-khôi, chiông Thai-thân thûn lo̍k-hi,chiông Â-pí-làn lâu kì chhiòn-kâ sâng-mài.18 Yû fó kong chhai kì-têu thùng-phân ke sṳ̂n-song,chiông ke-têu chok-ok ke ngìn sêu-sí chhiang-chhiang.19 Kì-têu chhai Hò-lie̍t-sân chu-chho ngiù-siong,khúi-pai ngìn ke sú só-cho ke ngiéu-siong.20 Kì-têu chiông Song-ti ke yùng-kông,von-cho sṳ̍t-chhó ke ngiù ke siong.21 Kì-têu m̀ ki-tet kiu kì-têu ke Song-ti,chhiu-he chhai Âi-khi̍p hàng thai-sṳ ke Song-ti.22 Kì sṳt chhai Hàm ke thi-fông hàng sṳ̀n-chiak,sṳt chhai Fùng-hói hàng tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sṳ.23 Song-ti kóng Kì oi sêu-me̍t Kì ke chṳ́-mìn ke-sṳ̀,Kì kién-sién ke phu̍k-ngìn Mô-sî khî chhai Kì mien-chhièn,thùng Kì chên-lun, khiù Kì sû-fì Kì ke thai fat-khién,chang kié-chhù Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn su sêu-me̍t ke ngùi-kî.24 Heu-lòi, kì-têu méu-sṳ ke mî-hó ke thi;kì-têu tui Song-ti ke yin-hí mò sin-sîm.25 Kì-têu chhai chhṳ-kâ ke chong-phùng tú mài-yen,m̀-hén thâng-chhiùng Song-chú ke fa.26 Kì chhiu ngiàm-ngiàm kín-ko kì-têu:Oi pûn kì-têu vang chhai khóng-yâ,27 pûn kì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn san-to lie̍t-koet,pûn kì-têu liù-mòng to ngoi-koet hi.28 Heu-lòi, Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn chhai Phî-ngí pai Pâ-li̍t,sṳ̍t ke-têu pai-ko sí-ngìn( 95 106:28 "sí-ngìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sí-sṳ̀n".) ke chi-vu̍t.29 Kì-têu ke hàng-vì ngiâ Song-chú thai fat-khién,chhiu yû vûn-yi̍t chhai kì-têu tông-chûng liù-hàng.30 Chúng-he Fî-nì-ha̍p hong-hí-lòi hìn-fa̍t chhui,án-ngiòng, vûn-yi̍t chang mò ki-siu̍k chhe hâ-hi.31 Liá-khien sṳ chhiu son-cho kì ke ngi,tui ke-sṳ̀ chhṳ̍t-to sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi.32 Kì-têu ya chhai Mí-li-pâ chhièn-súi ke-vi,kit Song-chú fat-khién, hoi Mô-sî su lièn-lui.33 Yîn-vi kì-têu chhiuk-sông Mô-sî ke sîm( 96 106:33 "Yîn-vi ... ke sîm" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Yîn-vi kì-têu vì-poi Song-chú ke Lìn".),hoi-tó kì kóng mò yin-kôi kóng ke fa.34 Kì-têu vì-poi Song-chú ke min-lin,mò chiông ke-têu ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn sêu-me̍t,35 fán-chón hi lâu kì-têu thûng-fûn,ho̍k kì-têu ke fûng-siu̍k si̍p-koan.36 Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn hàn hi-pai ngiéu-siong,liá chhiu-he kì-têu me̍t-mòng ke ngièn-yîn.37 Kì-têu chiông chhîn-sên ke chṳ́-ńg hî-sên,cho chi-vu̍t hien-pûn Kâ-nàm ke kúi-sṳ̀n.38 Kì-têu chhṳ̀ vù-kû ke ngìn,liù chhṳ-kâ chṳ́-ńg ke hiet,chiông kì-têu hien-pûn Kâ-nàm ke ngiéu-siong,ke thi-fông pûn só-liù ke hiet tá-lá-sap.39 Kì-têu ke hàng-vì ya chiông chhṳ-kâ tá-lá-sap,chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn cho phoi-ngia̍k pien-chiet ke ngìn.40 Só-yî, Song-chú tui kì-têu fat-khién;Kì chhin-nâu Kì chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-mìn.41 Kì sṳ́ kì-têu lo̍k chhai ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn ke sú-chûng;Kì yù-chhai kì-têu ke thi̍t-ngìn hot-chṳ kì-têu.42 Kì-têu pûn sù-thi̍t khî-ap,pûn sù-thi̍t khim-to tap-tap.43 Song-chú chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu chhin-tô pái,chúng-he kì-têu kie-mèu phoi-ngia̍k Kì,ham-lo̍k chhui-ok chûng, kín-lòi kín-chhṳ̂m.44 Chúng-he, Kì ku-ngiam kì-têu ke khú-nan;kì-têu yit khiù-ham, Kì chhiu thâng kì-têu.45 Vi-tó kì-têu, Song-chú sióng-hí Kì ke khie-yok;cheu Kì fûng-sṳn ke chhṳ̀-oi, Kì fì-sîm chón-yi.46 Kì sṳ́ chok kì-têu hi ke ngìn chhiòn-phu lìn-mén kì-têu.47 Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti â, chṳ́n-kiu ngài-têu!Khiù Ngì chiông ngài-têu tui lie̍t-koet chhi-si̍p hí-lòi,pûn ngài-têu yû-hó siung-chan Ngì ke sṳn-miàng,yung kám-chhia Ngì lòi cho ngài-têu ke yùng-kông!48 Ngien Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti su chhṳ̂n-siung,tui yún-yén to yún-yén!Ngien van-mìn thùng-sâng lòi-kóng:"Â-mén!"Hâ-li-lu-yá!
