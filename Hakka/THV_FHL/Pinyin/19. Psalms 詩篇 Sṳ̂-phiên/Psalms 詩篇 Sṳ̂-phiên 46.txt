1 Khó-lâ heu-thoi ke sṳ̂-kô, kâu-pûn Sṳn-kô Chhui-chóng, yung kô-yîm lòi-chhong.Song-ti Lâu Ngài-têu Thùng-chhai Song-ti he ngài-têu ke phit-nan-só lâu li̍t-liong,he ngài-têu fam-nan chûng khi̍p-sṳ̀ ke pông-chhu.2 Só-yî, sûi-yèn yû thai thi-thûng,kô-sân pên-thet, lo̍k-to hói-chûng,sûi-yèn hói-long thâu-thiên, khiùn-sân yèu-thung,ngài-têu ya m̀-kiâng.3 併於上節4 Yû yit-thiàu hò-pa chiông hí-lo̍k tai-to Song-ti ke sàng,to Chṳ-kô-chá he̍t ke sṳn-só.5 Song-ti chhai sàng-chûng, sàng chhiu m̀-voi vang-thet;thiên yit-kông, Song-ti ke yên-chhu chhiu voi to.6 Van-pâng sâu-thung, lie̍t-koet vang-thet;Song-ti hióng-lùi-kûng, thai-thi chhiu yùng-thet.7 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi lâu ngài-têu thùng-chhai;Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti he ngài-têu ke sàng-pó!8 Lòi â, lòi khon Song-chú ke chok-vì;khon Kì chhai thi-song só-cho ke khì-sṳ.9 Kì phìn-thin sṳ-kie ke chan-lon;Kì áu-thôn kiûng-chien, chiông chhiông chám-thôn, fó-sêu tún-phài.10 Kì kóng: "Tiâm-tiâm! Oi tî Ngài he Song-ti!Chhai van-koet chûng Ngài he chṳ-chûn ke,chhai chhiòn-thi song Ngài he chṳ-kô ke."11 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi lâu ngài-têu thùng-chhai;Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti he ngài-têu ke sàng-pó!
