1 Phien-ngâ-mén ke Heu-thoi Phien-ngâ-mén yû ńg-ke lai-é; kì-têu cheu thai-se sun-si he: Pí-lâ, Â-sṳ̍t-phe̍t, Â-ha̍p-lâ,2 Nò-ha̍p, Lâ-fap.3 Pí-lâ ke heu-thoi yû Â-thai, Kî-lâ, Â-pí-fut,4 Â-pí-sû, Nâi-man, Â-hò-â,5 Kî-lâ, Sṳ-fèu-sun, Fu-làn.6 Yî-fut ke heu-thoi yû Nâi-man, Â-hî-â, Kî-lâ. Kì-têu he he̍t chhai Kâ-pâ ke-têu kâ-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng, chúng-he pûn-ngìn pet-tó chang sái-to Mâ-nâ-hot he̍t. He Vû-sat lâu Â-hî-fut ke â-pâ Kî-lâ tai-liâng kì-têu sái ko-hi ke.7 併於上節8 Sâ-ha̍p-lièn ke lióng-ke pû-ngiòng — Fu-sṳ̂n lâu Pâ-lâ chhiòn-phu pûn kì lì-thet; heu-lòi kì he̍t chhai Mô-ap ke sṳ̀ thó Fo-tet. Kì-têu yû chhit-ke lai-é — Yok-pâ, Sé-pí-ngâ, Mí-sâ, Mâ-lâ-kôn,9 併於上節10 Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Sâ-kâ, Mí-mâ. Liá-têu ngìn chhiòn-phu sàng-cho kâ-chhu̍k chhu̍k-chóng.11 Fu-sṳ̂n vi kì kiung ke lióng-ke lai-é he Â-pí-thut lâu Yî-li-pâ-li̍t.12 Yî-li-pâ-li̍t yû sâm-ke lai-é — Hî-pak, Mí-sân, Sâ-ma̍k. Sâ-ma̍k hí Â-nò lâu Lò-tet liá lióng-chho sàng lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông. He̍t Chhai Kâ-thi̍t lâu Â-ngâ-lùn ke Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn13 Pí-li-â lâu Sṳ-mâ he he̍t chhai Â-ngâ-lùn-sàng ke chhu̍k-chóng; kì-têu lióng-ke kâ-chhu̍k kiuk-chéu Kâ-thi̍t ke ngìn-mìn.14 Pí-li-â ke heu-thoi yû Â-hî-yok, Sâ-sat, Yâ-li-ma̍t,15 Sî-pâ-thi-ngâ, Â-lâ-tet, Â-tet,16 Mí-kâ-le̍t, Yî-sṳ̂-pâ, Yok-ha̍p. He̍t Chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn17 Yî-li-pâ-li̍t ke heu-thoi yû Sî-pâ-thi-ngâ, Mí-sû-làn, Hî-sî-kî, Hî-pak,18 Yî-sṳ̂-mí-lòi, Yî-sṳ̂-li-â, Yok-pâ.19 Sṳ-mî ke heu-thoi yû Ngâ-kîm, Se-kî-li, Sat-tí,20 Yî-li-nâi, Sé-le̍t-thai, Yî-lie̍t,21 Â-thai-ngâ, Pí-lâ-ngâ, Sṳ̂n-lâ.22 Sâ-sat ke heu-thoi yû Yî-sṳ̂-pân, Hî-pak, Yî-lie̍t,23 Â-pak-tun, Se-kî-li, Ha̍p-nàn,24 Ha̍p-nâ-nì-ngâ, Yî-làn, Ôn-thò-thì-ngâ,25 Yî-fut-tí-ngâ, Pí-nú-yî-le̍t.26 Yâ-lò-hón ke heu-thoi yû Sân-sṳ-lòi, Sṳ-ha̍p-li, Â-thâ-li-ngâ,27 Ngâ-li-sî, Yî-li-â, Se-kî-li.28 Yî-song chhiòn-phu he kâ-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng; kì-têu chú-yeu ke heu-thoi he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng. He̍t Chhai Kî-phiên lâu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn29 Yâ-li( 19 8:29 "Yâ-li" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún (khon 9:35), Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò liá miàng.) khôi-sat Kî-phiên-sàng, he̍t chhai ke-vi. Kì ke pû-ngiòng ôn-to Mâ-kâ;30 kì ke chóng-chṳ́ ôn-to Â-pak-tun. Kì khì-thâ ke lai-é yû Sû-ngí, Kî-sṳ, Pâ-li̍t, Nì-ngí( 20 8:30 "Nì-ngí" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún (khon 9:36), Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò liá miàng.), Nâ-tap,31 Kî-tô, Â-hî-yok, Sat-kâ,32 Mí-kî-lò. Mí-kî-lò he Sṳ-mí-am ke â-pâ. Kì-têu ke heu-thoi he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, lì kì-têu pún chûng-chhu̍k ke khì-thâ kâ-chhu̍k mò kí-yén. Só-lò-vòng ke Kâ-chhu̍k33 Nì-ngí kiung Kî-sṳ; Kî-sṳ kiung Só-lò. Só-lò yû si-ke lai-é — Yok-nâ-tân, Ma̍k-kî-Sû-â, Â-pí-nâ-tha̍t, Yî-sṳ̂-Pâ-li̍t( 21 8:33 "Yî-sṳ̂-Pâ-li̍t" chhai Sat-mû-ngí Ki-ha 2:8 lâu khì-thâ kîn-vùn ôn-to "Yî-sṳ̂-pô-sat".).34 Yok-nâ-tân kiung Mí-li̍t-Pâ-li̍t( 22 8:34 "Mí-li̍t-Pâ-li̍t" chhai Sat-mû-ngí Ki-ha 4:4 lâu khì-thâ kîn-vùn ôn-to "Mí-fî-pô-sat".); Mí-li̍t-Pâ-li̍t kiung Mí-kâ.35 Mí-kâ yû si-ke lai-é — Pí-tûn, Mí-le̍t, Thâ-li-â, Â-ha̍p-sṳ̂.36 Â-ha̍p-sṳ̂ kiung Yâ-hò-â-tha̍t; Yâ-hò-â-tha̍t yû sâm-ke lai-é — Â-lâ-me̍t, Â-sṳ̂-mâ-vî, Sîm-li. Sîm-li kiung Mô-sat;37 Mô-sat kiung Pí-nì-â; Pí-nì-â kiung Lâ-fap; Lâ-fap kiung Yî-li-â-sat; Yî-li-â-sat kiung Â-sit.38 Â-sit yû liuk-ke lai-é — Â-sṳ̂-li-kôn, Pô-kî-lu, Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li, Sṳ-â-li-ngâ, Ngò-pâ-tí-ngâ, Ha̍p-nàn.39 Â-sit ke ló-thâi Yî-sat yû sâm-ke lai-é — Vû-làn, Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Yî-li-fap-lie̍t.40 Vû-làn ke-têu lai-é chhiòn-phu he yúng-kám ke chan-sṳ lâu kiûng-chien-sú. Kì ke lai-é lâu sûn-é chúng-khiung yû yit-pak ńg-sṳ̍p sà. Yî-song só-siá chhiòn-phu he Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke ngìn.
