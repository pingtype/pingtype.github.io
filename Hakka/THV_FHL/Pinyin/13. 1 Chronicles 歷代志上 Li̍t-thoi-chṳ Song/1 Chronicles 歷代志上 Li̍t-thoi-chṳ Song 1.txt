1 Â-tông to Â-pak-lâ-hón( Chhóng-sṳ Ki 5:1-32 10:1-32 11:10-26 ) Â-tông kiung Sai-thi̍t; Sai-thi̍t kiung Yî-nò-sṳ; Yî-nò-sṳ kiung Kâi-nàm;2 Kâi-nàm kiung Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t; Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t kiung Ngâ-lie̍t;3 Ngâ-lie̍t kiung Yî-nok; Yî-nok kiung Mâ-thú-sat-lâ; Mâ-thú-sat-lâ kiung Lâ-ma̍k;4 Lâ-ma̍k kiung Nò-â; Nò-â kiung sâm-ke lai-é — Sám, Hàm, Ngâ-fut.5 Ngâ-fut ke lai-é he: Kô-me̍t, Mâ-kok, Mâ-thoi, Ngâ-vàn, Thú-pâ, Mí-sat, Thì-lâ( 1 1:5 Thi 5, 8, 13, 17 tén chiet chûng ke miàng, yû-têu he thi-miàng, yû-têu he ngìn-miàng. Ke-têu thi-miàng ya he chú-siên ke miàng.).6 Kô-me̍t ke chṳ́-sûn he Â-sṳ̍t-kî-nâ-ngìn, Li-fap-ngìn, Thò-kâ-mâ-ngìn.7 Ngâ-vàn ke chṳ́-sûn he Yî-li-sâ-ngìn, Thâ-sṳ̂-ngìn, Kî-thì-ngìn, Lò-tet-ngìn.8 Hàm ke lai-é he: Kú-sṳ̍t, Âi-khi̍p, Fut, Kâ-nàm.9 Kú-sṳ̍t ke chṳ́-sûn he Sî-pâ-ngìn, Ha̍p-fî-lâ-ngìn, Sat-fut-thâ-ngìn, Lâ-mâ-ngìn, Sat-fut-thì-kâ-ngìn. Lâ-mâ ke chṳ́-sûn he Sṳ-pâ-ngìn, Tí-tan-ngìn.10 (Kú-sṳ̍t yû yit-ke lai-é miàng ôn-to Nèn-liu̍k; kì he li̍t-sṳ́ song thi-yit ke yîn-hiùng.)11 Âi-khi̍p ke heu-thoi he: Lu-tâi-ngìn, Â-nâ-mí-ngìn, Li-ha̍p-pí-ngìn, Nâ-fut-thú-hî-ngìn,12 Pha-sṳ̂-lû-se-ngìn, Kâ-sṳ̂-lu-hî-ngìn lâu Khiet-lî-thi̍t-ngìn. Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn he Khiet-lî-thi̍t-ngìn ke heu-thoi.13 Kâ-nàm kiung chóng-chṳ́ Sî-tun, yu kiung Het.14 Kâ-nàm ya he Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn, Â-mô-li-ngìn, Kiet-kâ-sat-ngìn,15 Hî-vi-ngìn, Â-kî-ngìn, Sî-nì-ngìn,16 Â-ngá-tí-ngìn, Sé-mâ-li-ngìn lâu Ha̍p-mâ-ngìn ke chú-siên.17 Sám ke lai-é he: Yî-làn, Â-sut, Â-fap-sat, Lu-tet, Â-làn, Vû-sṳ̂, Fu-le̍t, Kî-thiap, Mâ-sṳ̂.18 Â-fap-sat kiung Sâ-lâ; Sâ-lâ kiung Hî-pak.19 Hî-pak yû lióng-ke lai-é: yit-ke ôn-to Fap-le̍t( 2 1:19 "Fap-le̍t" yû "fûn-san" ke yi-sṳ.), yîn-vi chhai kì ke sṳ̀-thoi, ngìn-lui he fûn-san lòi-he̍t ke; kì ke hiûng-thi miàng-ham Yok-thân.20 Yok-thân ke chṳ́-sûn he: Â-mô-tap-ngìn, Sâ-lie̍t-ngìn, Ha̍p-sat-mâ-fî-ngìn, Yâ-lâ-ngìn,21 Ha̍p-tô-làn-ngìn, Vû-sat-ngìn, Tet-lâ-ngìn,22 Ngò-pâ-lu-ngìn, Â-pí-mâ-li-ngìn, Sṳ-pâ-ngìn,23 Â-fî-ngìn, Ha̍p-fî-lâ-ngìn, Yok-pâ-ngìn. Liá-têu ngìn chhiòn-phu he Yok-thân ke chṳ́-sûn.24 Tui Sám to Â-pak-làn ke heu-thoi ki chhai hâ-poi: Sám, Â-fap-sat, Sâ-lâ,25 Hî-pak, Fap-le̍t, Lâ-ǹg,26 Sî-lu̍k, Nâ-ho̍k, Thâ-lâ,27 Â-pak-làn (chhiu-he Â-pak-lâ-hón). Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li ke Heu-thoi28 ( Chhóng-sṳ Ki 25:12-16 ) Â-pak-lâ-hón yû lióng-ke lai-é — Yî-sat lâu Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li.29 Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li ke lai-é he hâ-poi sṳ̍p-ngi chûng-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng: Nì-pai-yok (Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li ke chóng-chṳ́), Kî-tha̍t, Â-tet-phe̍t, Mí-pí-sâm,30 Mí-sṳ̂-mâ, Thu-mâ, Mâ-sat, Ha̍p-tha̍t, Thì-mâ,31 Yî-thut, Nâ-fî-sṳ̂, Kî-tí-mâ.32 Â-pak-lâ-hón ke se-chiá Kî-thú-lâ vi kì kiung liuk-ke lai-é — Sîm-làn, Yok-sân, Mí-tan, Mí-thien, Yî-sṳ̂-pâ, Sû-â. Yok-sân yû lióng-ke lai-é — Sṳ-pâ lâu Tí-tan.33 Mí-thien yû ńg-ke lai-é — Yî-fap, Yî-fut, Ha̍p-nok, Â-pí-thai, Yî-le̍t-thai. Yî-song liá-têu chhiòn-phu he Kî-thú-lâ ke heu-thoi. Yî-so ke Heu-thoi34 ( Chhóng-sṳ Ki 36:1-19 ) Â-pak-lâ-hón ke lai-é Yî-sat yû lióng-ke lai-é — Yî-so lâu Yî-set-lie̍t( 3 1:34 "Yî-set-lie̍t" he Song-ti lâu Ngâ-kok só ôn ke miàng (khon Chhóng-sṳ Ki 32:28; 35:10).).35 Yî-so ke lai-é he Yî-li-fap, Liù-ngí, Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Ngâ-làn, Khó-lâ.36 Yî-li-fap he Thì-man, Â-mat, Sé-pô, Kâ-thán, Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂, Thìn-na̍p, Â-mâ-li̍t kok chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.37 Liù-ngí he Nâ-ha̍p, Chhia-lâ, Sâ-mâ, Mí-sat kok chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. Sî-ngí ke Heu-thoi38 ( Chhóng-sṳ Ki 36:20-30 )Yî-tûng thi-khî ngièn-siên ke chhu-mìn he Sî-ngí liá-têu lai-é ke heu-thoi:Lò-thân — Hò-li lâu Hî-man lióng chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. (Lò-thân yû yit-ke ló-moi miàng ôn-to Thìn-na̍p.)Sok-pâ — Â-le̍t-vùn, Mâ-nâ-hot, Yî-pâ-lu̍k, Sṳ-pô, Â-nàm tén chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.Chi-phien — kì yû lióng-ke lai-é Â-ngâ lâu Â-nâ. Â-nâ he Tí-sun ke â-pâ; Tí-sun he Hiûn-tan, Yî-sṳ-pân, Yit-làn, Kî-làn tén chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.Yî-chhat — Phit-hón, Sat-fân, Â-kôn tén chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.Tí-sân — Vû-sṳ̂ lâu Â-làn lióng chûng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.39 併於上節40 併於上節41 併於上節42 併於上節43 Yî-tûng Lie̍t-vòng( Chhóng-sṳ Ki 36:31-43 )Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn màng-yû kiûn-vòng yî-chhièn, hâ-poi liá-têu vòng chiap-tén thúng-chhṳ Yî-tûng:Thìn-ha̍p-pâ-ngìn Pí-ngí ke lai-é Pí-lâ,Pô-sṳ̂-lâ-ngìn Chhia-lâ ke lai-é Yok-pâ,Thì-man thi-khî ke ngìn Fu-sân,Â-vi-tet-ngìn Pí-tha̍t ke lai-é Ha̍p-tha̍t (kì chhai Mô-ap-thi tá-yàng Mí-thien-ngìn),Mâ-sṳ-li-kâ-ngìn Sông-lâ,thai hò-pa sùn ke Li-hò-pak-ngìn Seu-lò,Â-kiet-pô ke lai-é Pâ-le̍t-Ha̍p-nàm,Pâ-vû-ngìn Ha̍p-tha̍t (kì ke pû-ngiòng Mí-hî-thâ-phe̍t he Mâ-thi̍t-lie̍t ke moi-é — Mí-Sat-ha̍p ke sûn-ńg).44 併於上節45 併於上節46 併於上節47 併於上節48 併於上節49 併於上節50 併於上節51 Yî-tûng kok-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng he: Thìn-na̍p, Â-le̍t-ngá, Yâ-thiap,52 Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ, Yî-lâ, Pí-nun,53 Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂, Thì-man, Mí-pí-sat,54 Mâ-kî-thia̍p, Yî-làn.
