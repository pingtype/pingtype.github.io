1 Chi-sṳ̂ ke Chṳt-vu Â-lùn ke heu-thoi fûn-cho kí-chû. Â-lùn yû si-ke lai-é — Nâ-tap, Â-pí-fu, Yî-li-â-sat, Yî-thâ-mâ.2 Nâ-tap lâu Â-pí-fu pí kì-têu ke â-pâ siên sí, mò heu-thoi, só-yî kì-têu ke ló-thâi Yî-li-â-sat lâu Yî-thâ-mâ cho chi-sṳ̂.3 Thai-ví-vòng cheu Â-lùn heu-thoi ke chṳt-vu chiông kì-têu fûn-cho yî-ha kí-chû, chhiùng Yî-li-â-sat ke heu-thoi Sat-tuk lâu Yî-thâ-mâ ke heu-thoi Â-hî-mí-le̍t hia̍p-chhu Thai-ví chhú-lî liá-sṳ.4 Yî-li-â-sat heu-thoi ke nàm-ngìn cho chhu̍k-chóng ke pí Yî-thâ-mâ ke heu-thoi kha-tô, só-yî Yî-li-â-sat ke heu-thoi fûn-cho sṳ̍p-liuk chû; Yî-thâ-mâ ke heu-thoi fûn-cho pat-chû.5 Chúng-he kì-têu ke kûng-chok he yung chhû-chhiâm ke fông-sṳt fûn-phi ke, yîn-vi Yî-li-â-sat lâu Yî-thâ-mâ heu-thoi chhiòn-phu yû ngìn cho sṳn-só sṳ-vu lâu lìn-sin sên-fa̍t ke liâng-thô chá.6 Yî-li-â-sat lâu Yî-thâ-mâ ke heu-thoi lùn-liù chhû-chhiâm. Ke-sṳ̀, Li-vi-ngìn Nâ-thán-ngia̍p ke lai-é Sṳ-mâ-ngâ cho sû-ki thoi kì-têu tên-ki; vòng lâu vòng ke kôn-yèn, chi-sṳ̂ Sat-tuk, Â-pí-â-thâ ke lai-é Â-hî-mí-le̍t, chi-sṳ̂ kâ-chhu̍k lâu Li-vi kâ-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng cho chṳn-ngìn.7 Ngi-sṳ̍p si-chû fûn-phi chṳt-vu ke sun-si ki chhai hâ-poi:Thi-yit chhiâm, Yâ-hò-ngâ-li̍p;ngi, Yâ-thai-ngâ;8 sâm, Ha̍p-lìm;si, Sô-lìm;9 ńg, Mâ-kî-ngâ;liuk, Mí-ngâ-mìn;10 chhit, Ha̍p-kô-sṳ̂;pat, Â-pí-ngâ;11 kiú, Yâ-sû-â;sṳ̍p, Sṳ-kâ-nì;12 sṳ̍p-yit, Yî-li-â-sṳ̍t;sṳ̍p-ngi, Ngâ-kîm;13 sṳ̍p-sâm, Fù-pâ;sṳ̍p-si, Yâ-sṳ-pí-ap;14 sṳ̍p-ńg, Piak-kâ;sṳ̍p-liuk, Yîm-ma̍k;15 sṳ̍p-chhit, Hî-sit;sṳ̍p-pat, Ha̍p-phit-sit;16 sṳ̍p-kiú, Pí-thâ-hî-ngâ;ngi-sṳ̍p, Yî-sî-kiet;17 ngi-sṳ̍p yit, Ngâ-kîn;ngi-sṳ̍p ngi, Kâ-ma̍t;18 ngi-sṳ̍p sâm, Thi-lòi-ngâ;ngi-sṳ̍p si, Mâ-sî-â.19 Liá-têu ngìn he cheu kì-têu chhai Song-chú sṳn-thien ke chṳt-vu tên-ki ke; liá-têu chṳt-vu he kì-têu ke chú-siên Â-lùn cheu Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t Song-ti ke min-lin só-thin ke. Li-vi-ngìn ke Miàng-tân20 Li-vi kî-chhu̍k khì-thâ kâ-chhu̍k chhu̍k-chóng ke miàng-tân ki chhai hâ-poi:Am-làn chṳ́-sûn Sû-pâ-ngia̍p ke heu-thoi Yâ-hî-tí-â,21 Li-ha̍p-pí-ngâ ke heu-thoi Yî-sṳ-ngâ,22 Yî-sṳ̂-ha̍p chṳ́-sûn Sṳ-lò-mô ke heu-thoi Ngâ-ha̍p,23 Hî-pak-lùn ke si-ke lai-é, cheu thai-se sun-si phài-lie̍t: Yâ-li-ngâ, Â-mâ-li-â, Ngâ-ha̍p-sit, Yâ-kâ-mien,24 Vû-siet chṳ́-sûn Mí-kâ ke heu-thoi Sâ-me̍t,25 Vû-siet chṳ́-sûn Yî-sṳ-ngâ (Mí-kâ ke hiûng-thi) ke heu-thoi Sat-kâ-li-ngâ.26 Mí-lâ-li ke heu-thoi yû Mat-li, Mû-sṳ, lâu Ngâ-sî-ngâ.27 Ngâ-sî-ngâ ke lai-é chhiu-he Sok-hàm, Sat-khat, Yî-pí-li.28 Mat-li yû lióng-ke lai-é — Yî-li-â-sat lâu Kî-sṳ.Yî-li-â-sat mò lai-é; Kî-sṳ ke heu-thoi yû Yâ-lâ-me̍t.29 併於上節30 Mû-sṳ yû sâm-ke lai-é — Ma̍t-li̍t, Yî-tet, Yâ-li-mô.Yî-song he Li-vi-ngìn ke kâ-chhu̍k.31 Tak kâ-chhu̍k chhu̍k-chóng lâu kiâ chhu̍k-nui yit-ke heu-sâng ke hiûng-thi phiàng-tén vi kì-têu ke chṳt-vu chhû-chhiâm, chhiu-chhiong kì-têu ke chhîn-su̍k Â-lùn heu-thoi cho chi-sṳ̂ ke ngìn só-cho ke khiung-yong. Thai-ví-vòng, Sat-tuk, Â-hî-mí-le̍t, chi-sṳ̂ lâu Li-vi kâ-chhu̍k ke-têu chhu̍k-chóng chhiòn-phu he chṳn-ngìn.
