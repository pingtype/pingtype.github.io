1 Sṳn-thien Kô-sú Thai-ví-vòng lâu Li-vi-ngìn ke liâng-chhiu fûn-phai Â-sat, Hî-man, Yâ-thu-tun sâm-ke Li-vi chûng-chhu̍k chú-chhṳ̀ lî-pai ke sṳ. Kì-têu oi chiông Song-ti ke fa chhong chhut-lòi, yung su-khìm, chhit-hièn-khìm, lâu chhê-é cheu-ngo̍k. Yî-ha he su sién chú-chhṳ̀ lî-pai ke ngìn ke miàng-tân lâu chṳt-vu:2 Â-sat ke si-ke lai-é — Sat-khat, Yok-sit, Nì-tham-ngâ, Ngâ-sat-li-lâ, chiap-su Â-sat ke chṳ́-thô. Â-sat cheu vòng ke min-lin chiông Song-ti ke fa chhong chhut-lòi.3 Yâ-thu-tun ke liuk-ke lai-é — Kî-thai-li, Sî-li, Yâ-chhî-â, Sṳ-mî, Ha̍p-sâ-pí-ngâ, Mâ-thâ-thì-ngâ, chhai â-pâ chṳ́-thô hâ chiông Song-ti ke fa chhong chhut-lòi, yung su-khìm cheu-ngo̍k; kì-têu ya hiong Song-chú chhong siung-chan lâu kám-chhia ke sṳ̂-kô.4 Hî-man yû sṳ̍p-si ke lai-é; kì-têu he Pu-kî-ngâ, Mâ-tham-ngâ, Vû-siet, Se-pu-ngia̍p, Yâ-li-mô, Ha̍p-nâ-nì-ngâ, Ha̍p-nâ-nì, Yî-li-â-thâ, Kî-thai-li-thì, Lò-man-thì-Yî-chhia, Yok-sṳ̂-pí-kâ-sâ, Mâ-lò-thì, Hò-thì, Mâ-ha̍p-siu.5 Song-ti cheu Kì ke yin-hí, su vòng ke siên-kien Hî-man yû liá sṳ̍p-si ke lai-é, yu yû sâm-ke moi-é, sṳ́ Hî-man yû khièn-li̍t.6 Hî-man ke-têu lai-é chhai kì chṳ́-thô chṳ̂-ha, phi-ha̍p Song-chú sṳn-thien ke lî-pai, thàn-cheu su-khìm, chhit-hièn-khìm lâu chhê-é. Â-sat, Yâ-thu-tun, lâu Hî-man chhiòn-phu thâng vòng ke min-lin.7 Liá ngi-sṳ̍p si-ke ngìn chhiòn-phu he chôn-kâ, lâu kì-têu thùng-kûng ke Li-vi thùng-pâu ya he su-ko hiun-lien siung-chan Song-chú, yit-khiung yû lióng-pak pat-sṳ̍p pat-ngìn.8 Liá-têu ngìn mò-lun heu-sâng, ngièn-ló, chôn-kâ, ya-he chhû-ho̍k ke, chhiòn-phu yung chhû-chhiâm ke fông-sṳt kiet-thin só tâm-ngim ke chṳt-vu.9 Liá lióng-pak pat-sṳ̍p pat-ngìn cheu kâ-chhu̍k fûn-cho ngi-sṳ̍p si-chû, tak-chû sṳ̍p-ngi ke ngìn, yû yit-ke chû-chóng kón-lî.
