1 Yù-thai ke Heu-thoi Yî-set-lie̍t yû sṳ̍p-ngi ke lai-é — Lî-phien, Sî-mién, Li-vi, Yù-thai, Yî-sat-kâ, Sî-pu-lùn,2 Tan, Yok-sit, Phien-ngâ-mén, Nâ-fut-thâ-li, Kâ-tet, Â-sat.3 Yù-thai yû ńg-ke lai-é. Kì ke pû-ngiòng Kâ-nàm-ngìn Pâ-sû-â vi kì kiung sâm-ke lai-é — Ngí, Ngò-nàm, Sṳ-lâ. Chóng-chṳ́ Ngí fî-sòng fái, pûn Song-chú chhṳ̀-thet.4 Yù-thai ke sîm-khiû Thap-mâ vi kì kiung lióng-ke lai-é — Fap-le̍t-sṳ̂ lâu Chhia-lâ.5 Fap-le̍t-sṳ̂ yû lióng-ke lai-é — Hî-sṳ̂-lùn lâu Ha̍p-mû-le̍t.6 Kì ke ló-thâi Chhia-lâ yû ńg-ke lai-é — Sîm-li, Yî-tham, Hî-man, Kap-kok, Thai-lâ.7 Chhia-lâ ke yit-ke heu-thoi — Kâ-mí ke lai-é Â-kôn( 4 2:7 "Â-kôn" he cheu Yok-sû-â Ki 7:1, chhai liá-vi Hî-pak-lòi-vùn yi̍t-cho "Â-kot-ngí", yi-sṳ he "châi-fo".), yîn-vi chiông yin-kôi hien-pûn Song-ti ke chan-li-phín thêu-khong hí-lòi, hoi Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn tù-tó châi-fo.8 Yî-tham yû yit-ke lai-é, miàng ôn-to Â-sat-li-ngâ. Thai-ví-vòng ke Kâ-phú9 Hî-sṳ̂-lùn yû sâm-ke lai-é — Yâ-lâ-me̍t, Làn, Kâ-le̍t.10 Tui Làn to Yâ-sî ke kâ-phú ki chhai hâ-poi: Làn, Â-mí-nâ-tha̍t, Nâ-sun (Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k chûng ke yit-ke khiet-chhut ngìn-vu̍t),11 Sat-mùn, Pô-â-sṳ̂,12 Ngò-phi-tet, Yâ-sî.13 Yâ-sî yû chhit-ke lai-é, cheu thai-se ke sun-si he: Yî-li-ap, Â-pí-nâ-tha̍t, Sṳ-mí-â,14 Nâ-thán-ngia̍p, Lâ-thoi,15 Â-siên, Thai-ví.16 Yâ-sî yû lióng-ke moi-é — Sé-lu-ngâ lâu Oi-pí-kâi.Yâ-sî ke moi-é Sé-lu-ngâ yû sâm-ke lai-é — Â-pí-chhî, Yok-ap, Â-sat-het.17 Yâ-sî nang-ngoi yit-ke moi-é Oi-pí-kâi ka-pûn Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li-ngìn Yit-thiap, kiung yit-ke lai-é miàng ôn-to Â-mâ-sat. Hî-sṳ̂-lùn ke Heu-thoi18 Hî-sṳ̂-lùn ke lai-é Kâ-le̍t thó Â-sû-pâ, kiung yit-ke moi-é, miàng ôn-to Yâ-lio̍k( 5 2:18 "thó Â-sû-pâ, kiung ... Yâ-lio̍k" ya-he yi̍t-cho "thó Â-sû-pâ lâu Yâ-lio̍k".). Kì yû sâm-ke lai-é — Yâ-sat, Sok-pa, Ap-tûn.19 Â-sû-pâ sí-heu, Kâ-le̍t yu thó Yî-fap-thâ, kiung yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Fu-ngí.20 Fu-ngí ke lai-é he Vû-li, sûn-é he Pí-sat-lie̍t.21 Hî-sṳ̂-lùn liuk-sṳ̍p se ke sṳ̀ thó Mâ-kit ke moi-é, Kî-lie̍t ke chí-moi. Kì-têu yû yit-ke lai-é, miàng ôn-to Sî-kot;22 Sî-kot yû yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Ngài-ngí. Ngài-ngí chhai Kî-lie̍t kin-nui sṳt thúng-chhṳ ko ngi-sṳ̍p sâm chho sàng.23 Chúng-he heu-lòi Kî-sut lâu Â-làn-koet cham-liâng ke-vi ke liuk-sṳ̍p ke sàng-sṳ, chhiu-he Ngài-ngí lâu Kî-na̍p ke chhûn-chông, pin fu-khiun ke sàng-sṳ. Só-yû he̍t chhai ke só-chhai ke ngìn chhiòn-phu he Kî-lie̍t ke â-pâ Mâ-kit ke heu-thoi.24 Hî-sṳ̂-lùn chhai Kâ-le̍t-Yî-fap-thâ sí-heu, kì ke pû-ngiòng Â-pí-ngâ vi kì kiung Â-sṳ̂-fu( 6 2:24 "chhai ... Â-sṳ̂-fu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sí-heu, kì ke lai-é Kâ-le̍t thó â-pâ ke se-chiá Yî-fap-thâ; kì-têu kiung yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Â-sṳ̂-fu".). Â-sṳ̂-fu khôi-sat Thì-kô-â-sàng. Yâ-lâ-me̍t ke Heu-thoi25 Hî-sṳ̂-lùn ke chóng-chṳ́ Yâ-lâ-me̍t yû ńg-ke lai-é — Làn (chóng-chṳ́), Pu-nâ, Â-lièn, Â-siên, Â-hî-ngâ.26 Làn yû sâm-ke lai-é — Mâ-sṳ̂, Ngâ-mén, Yî-kiet. Yâ-lâ-me̍t nang-ngoi yit-ke pû-ngiòng ôn-to Â-thâ-lâ, kiung yit-ke lai-é miàng ôn-to Â-nàm.27 併於上節28 Â-nàm yû lióng-ke lai-é — Sâ-mâi lâu Ngâ-thai. Sâ-mâi ya yû lióng-ke lai-é — Nâ-tap lâu Â-pí-sut.29 Â-pí-sut thó Â-pí-hài, kiung lióng-ke lai-é — Â-phan lâu Mô-li.30 Â-pí-sut ke â-kô Nâ-tap yû lióng-ke lai-é — Sî-lie̍t lâu Â-phiên. Sî-lie̍t to-sí mò lai-é.31 Â-phiên kiung Yî-sṳ; Yî-sṳ kiung Sṳ-sân; Sṳ-sân kiung Â-lòi.32 Sâ-mâi ke ló-thâi Ngâ-thai yû lióng-ke lai-é — Yit-thiap lâu Yok-nâ-tân. Yit-thiap to-sí mò lai-é.33 Yok-nâ-tân yû lióng-ke lai-é — Pí-le̍t lâu Sat-sat. Yî-song chhiòn-phu he Yâ-lâ-me̍t ke heu-thoi.34 Sṳ-sân mò lai-é, chṳ́-yû moi-é. Kì yû yit-ke Âi-khi̍p ke phu̍k-ngìn miàng ôn-to Yâ-ha̍p.35 Sṳ-sân chiông yit-ke moi-é ka-pûn kì, kì-têu kiung yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Â-thai.36 Tui Â-thai to Yî-li-sâ-mâ ke kâ-phú ki chhai hâ-poi: Â-thai, Nâ-tân, Sat-pha̍t,37 Yî-fut-lâ, Ngò-phi-tet,38 Yâ-fu, Â-sat-li-ngâ,39 Hî-li-sṳ̂, Yî-li-â-sat,40 Sî-sṳ̂-mâi, Sâ-liùng,41 Yâ-kâ-mí-ngâ, Yî-li-sâ-mâ. Kâ-le̍t Khì-thâ ke Heu-thoi42 Yâ-lâ-me̍t ke ló-thâi Kâ-le̍t ke chóng-chṳ́ miàng ôn-to Mí-sâ. Mí-sâ kiung Sî-fut; Sî-fut kiung Mâ-li-sâ; Mâ-li-sâ kiung Hî-pak-lùn( 7 2:42 "Sî-fut kiung ... Hî-pak-lùn", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).43 Hî-pak-lùn yû si-ke lai-é — Khó-lâ, Thâ-phû-â, Li-hén, Sṳ-mâ.44 Sṳ-mâ he Lâ-hàm ke â-pâ, Yok-kôn ke â-kûng. Sṳ-mâ ke â-kô Li-hén kiung Sâ-mâi;45 Sâ-mâi kiung Mâ-yùn; Mâ-yùn kiung Pak-Siuk.46 Kâ-le̍t yû yit-ke se-chiá miàng ôn-to Yî-fap, vi kì kiung sâm-ke lai-é — Ha̍p-làn, Mô-sat, Kâ-chhia, Ha̍p-làn ke lai-é miàng ôn-to Kâ-khiok.47 (Yû yit-ke ngìn miàng ôn-to Ngâ-thoi; kì yû liuk-ke lai-é — Li-khien, Yok-thán, Kî-sân, Pí-li̍t, Yî-fap, Sâ-â-fut.)48 Kâ-le̍t nang-ngoi yit-ke se-chiá Mâ-kâ vi kì kiung lióng-ke lai-é — Sṳ-phe̍t lâu Thi̍t-ha̍p-nâ.49 Heu-lòi kì yu kiung lióng-ke lai-é — Sâ-â-fut lâu Sṳ-fap. Sâ-â-fut khôi-sat Ma̍k-mâ-nâ-sàng; Sṳ-fap khôi-sat Ma̍t-pí-nâ lâu Kî-pí-â sàng.Chhù liá yî-ngoi, Kâ-le̍t yû yit-ke moi-é ôn-to Ap-sat.50 Kâ-le̍t nang-ngoi yû-têu heu-thoi, ki chhai hâ-poi: Fu-ngí he Kâ-le̍t lâu kiâ pû-ngiòng Yî-fap-thâ ke chóng-chṳ́. Fu-ngí ke chóng-chṳ́ Sok-pâ khôi-sat Kî-lie̍t-Yâ-lìm;51 thi-ngi ke lai-é Sat-mâ khôi-sat Pak-li-hèn; thi-sâm ke lai-é Ha̍p-le̍t khôi-sat Pak-Kâ-tet.52 Khôi-sat Kî-lie̍t-Yâ-lìm ke ngìn Sok-pâ kiung Ha̍p-lò-yî; Mí-nú-ha̍p thi-khî yû yit-pan ke ngìn-mìn he Ha̍p-lò-yî ke chṳ́-sûn,53 Ha̍p-lò-yî ya he he̍t chhai Kî-lie̍t-Yâ-lìm ke kok chûng-chhu̍k — Yî-thiap-ngìn, Pu-thi̍t-ngìn, Sû-mâ-ngìn, Me̍t-lòi-ngìn ke chú-siên. (Só-lâ lâu Yî-sṳ̍t-thò sàng ke ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu he liá-têu chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn.)54 Khôi-sat Pak-li-hèn ke ngìn Sat-mâ he Nì-thò-fap-ngìn lâu Â-thâ-liu̍k-Pak-Yok-ap-ngìn ke chú-siên, ya he chhai Mâ-nâ-ha̍p ke Só-li chhu̍k-ngìn ke chú-siên.55 Thi̍t-lâ chhu̍k-ngìn, Sṳ-mí-ap chhu̍k-ngìn, lâu Sû-kap chhu̍k-ngìn he chôn-mùn chhâu-siá vùn-khien ke vùn-sû sṳ-kâ, he̍t chhai Ngâ-pí-sṳ̂-sàng. Kì-têu he Kî-nì-ngìn Li-kap kâ-chhu̍k ke chú-siên Ha̍p-ma̍t ke heu-thoi.
