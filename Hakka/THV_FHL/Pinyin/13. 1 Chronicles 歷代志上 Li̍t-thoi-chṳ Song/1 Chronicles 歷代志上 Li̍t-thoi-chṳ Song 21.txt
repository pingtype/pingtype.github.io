1 Thai-ví Tiám-son Ngìn-khiéu( Sat-mû-ngí Ki-ha 24:1-25 ) Sat-tan sióng-oi hoi Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, chhiuk-kit Thai-ví lòi phan-lî fu-khiéu thiàu-chhà.2 Thai-ví chhiu min-lin kiûn-chhui ke ngièn-soi Yok-ap lâu khì-thâ kôn-yèn kóng: "Ngì-têu oi to Yî-set-lie̍t tak só-chhai, tui chui nàm-phién ke Phe̍t-sṳ-pâ to chui pet-phién ke Tan, tiám-son ngìn-mìn; chón-lòi lâu ngài po-ko, ngài oi tî ngìn-mìn ke su-muk."3 Chúng-he Yok-ap en-kóng: "Ngien Song-chú sṳ́ Yî-set-lie̍t ke ngìn-mìn pí liá-ha ke ngìn-su tô yit-pak phi! Ngâ chú ngâ vòng, kì-têu chhiòn-phu kám m̀-he ngì ke phu̍k-ngìn? Ngì cho-má-ke khiòng-khiòng oi cho liá-sṳ? Cho-má-ke oi hoi chhiòn-mìn fam-chhui nè?"4 Chúng-he vòng kiên-chhṳ̀ oi Yok-ap fu̍k-chhiùng kì ke min-lin. Só-yî Yok-ap chhut-hi, chhiòn Yî-set-lie̍t koet hàng-to theu-theu, yèn-heu chón-to Yâ-lu-sat-lâng.5 Yok-ap hiong Thai-ví po-ko: Yî-set-lie̍t tai vú-hi ke chong-tên yû yit-pak yit-sṳ̍p van ngìn; Yù-thai tai vú-hi ke chong-tên yû si-sṳ̍p chhit-van ngìn.6 Yok-ap mò thiàu-chhà Li-vi kî-chhu̍k lâu Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke ngìn-su, yîn-vi kì tông-nâu vòng liá-ke min-lin.7 Song-ti ya mò fôn-hí liá tiám-son ngìn-mìn ke sṳ, só-yî Kì chit-fa̍t Yî-set-lie̍t.8 Thai-ví tui Song-ti kóng: "Ngài cho liá-sṳ fam thai-chhui lé! Khiù Ngì sa-miên phu̍k-ngìn ke ko-sṳt! Ngài ke hàng-vì sṳ̍t-chhai tông-ngong."9 Song-chú tui Thai-ví ke siên-kien Kâ-tet kóng:10 "Hi lâu Thai-ví kóng, Ngài yung sâm-ke châi-nan pûn kì sién yit-ke, kì sién má-ke, Ngài chhiu cho má-ke."11 Kâ-tet hi kien Thai-ví, chiông Song-chú kóng ke fa lâu kì kóng, yu mun Thai-ví: "Ngì oi sién nai-yong?12 Sâm-ngièn ke kî-fông nè? Ya-he sâm-ke ngie̍t pûn thi̍t-kiûn kiuk? Ya-he pûn Song-chú yung Kì ke kiam sṳ̂-hàng kûng-kit, sṳ́ ngì ke koet-kâ pot sâm-ngit ke vûn-é, Song-chú phai thiên-sṳ́ chhai Yî-set-lie̍t tak só-chhai chho-sṳ̀n sí-mòng nè? Sióng khon-é, oi ngài ngióng-pân fì-tap Song-chú."13 Thai-ví tui Kâ-tet kóng: "Ngài tông vì-nàn! Ngài mò-oi pûn-ngìn chhú-fa̍t. Ngài kâm-ngien pûn Song-chú chhṳ-kâ chit-fa̍t ngài, yîn-vi Song-chú yû fûng-sṳn ke lìn-mén."14 Só-yî, Song-chú kong vûn-é chhai Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn sṳ̂n-song, chhiu yû chhit-van ngìn sí-thet.15 Song-ti yu phai thiên-sṳ́ hi fí-me̍t Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, chúng-he thiên-sṳ́ oi chṳp-hàng ke sṳ̀, Song-chú kói-pien Kì chit-fa̍t ngìn-mìn ke sîm-yi, chhiu tui ke chṳp-hàng fí-me̍t ke thiên-sṳ́ kóng: "Sú thìn hâ-lòi, la lé!" Ke-sṳ̀, Song-chú ke thiên-sṳ́ chhai Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn Â-lò-nâ ke tá-ma̍k-chhòng.16 Thai-ví khon-tó Song-chú ke thiên-sṳ́ khî chhai pan khûng-chûng, sú-hong khià-tén kiam, oi fí-me̍t Yâ-lu-sat-lâng. Thai-ví lâu ke-têu chóng-ló chhiòn-phu chok mà-sâm, phu̍k chhai thi-song khúi-pai.17 Thai-ví khì-tó kóng: "Song-ti â, ngài chang he ke fam-chhui, cho m̀-chho̍k sṳ ke ngìn, he ngài ha-lin thiàu-chhà fu-khiéu ke. Liá-têu ngìn-mìn chhiong yòng-è, kì-têu me-mò cho má-ke. Song-chú — ngài ke Song-ti â, khiù Ngì chit-fa̍t ngài lâu ngài vuk-hâ ngìn, m̀-hó kong vûn-é pûn Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn."18 Só-yî, Song-chú ke thiên-sṳ́ lâu Kâ-tet kóng, ham kì min-lin Thai-ví to Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn Â-lò-nâ ke tá-ma̍k-chhòng hi, vi Song-chú hí yit-chho chi-thàn.19 Thai-ví cheu Kâ-tet fung Song-chú min-lin só-kóng ke fa sông-hi.20 Â-lò-nâ lâu kì si-ke lai-é tú-hó chhai tá-ma̍k-chhòng tá-ma̍k; kì-têu yit khon-tó thiên-sṳ́, ke si-ke lai-é chhiòn-phu chéu-hi khong hí-lòi.21 Â-lò-nâ khon-tó Thai-ví hiong kì liá-phién hàng ko-lòi, chhiu lì-khôi tá-ma̍k-chhòng, hàng-chhièn phu̍k chhai thi-song khúi-pai.22 Thai-ví tui Â-lò-nâ kóng: "Chhiáng ngì chiông tá-ma̍k-chhòng mai-pûn ngài, ngài hó vi Song-chú hí yit-chho chi-thàn, pûn pot-vûn thìn hâ-lòi, ngài voi cheu ka-chhièn fu-pûn ngì."23 Â-lò-nâ kóng: "Ngâ chú ngâ vòng, cho ngì nâ-hi! Ngì kak-yi má-ke chhiu cho má-ke. Liá-vi yû ngiù hó cho sêu-fa-chi, yu yû tá-ma̍k ke piông-é hó cho chhèu sêu, hàn-yû ma̍k-é hó cho su-chi. Ngài chiông liá-têu chhiòn-phu sung-pûn ngì."24 Chúng-he Thai-ví-vòng tui Â-lò-nâ kóng: "Cho-m̀-tet! Ngài yit-thin oi cheu la-ngiak ke ka-chhièn lâu ngì mâi; ngài mò-oi hiong Song-chú hien ngì ke tûng-sî, mò-oi hien pha̍k-pha̍k tet-lòi ke cho sêu-fa-chi."25 Só-yî Thai-ví yung liuk-pak chak kîm-pi hiong Â-lò-nâ mâi tá-ma̍k-chhòng.26 Thai-ví chhai ke-vi vi Song-chú hí yit-chho chi-thàn, hien sêu-fa-chi lâu phìn-ôn-chi. Thai-ví khì-tó Song-chú; Song-chú chhiu thâng kì ke khì-khiù, tui thiên-táng kong-fó hâ-lòi, chiông chi-thàn táng ke thèu-sâng sêu-thet.27 Song-chú fûn-fu thiên-sṳ́, thiên-sṳ́ chhiu chiông kiam sû hí-lòi.28 Thai-ví khon Song-chú chhai Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn Â-lò-nâ tá-ma̍k-chhòng thâng kì ke khì-tó, só-yî chhiu chhai ke-vi hien-chi.29 Mô-sî chhai khóng-yâ só-chho Song-chú ke sṳn-chong-phùng lâu hien sêu-fa-chi ke chi-thàn ke-sṳ̀ hàn chhai Kî-phiên kin-pai ke só-chhai.30 Chúng-he Thai-ví m̀-kám to ke-vi hi khiù-mun Song-ti, yîn-vi kì kiâng Song-chú thiên-sṳ́ ke kiam.
