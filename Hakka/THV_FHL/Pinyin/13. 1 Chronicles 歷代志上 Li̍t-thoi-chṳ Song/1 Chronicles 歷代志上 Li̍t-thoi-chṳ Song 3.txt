1 Thai-ví-vòng ke Chṳ́-ńgThai-ví chhai Hî-pak-lùn só-kiung ke lai-é, cheu chhut-se ke sun-si, miàng-è ki chhai hâ-poi:Am-nun — â-mê he Yâ-sṳ̂-lie̍t-ngìn Â-hî-nôn;Tan-yî-li — â-mê he Kâ-me̍t-ngìn Oi-pí-kâi;Ap-sâ-liùng — â-mê he Mâ-kâ, Kî-sut-vòng Tha̍t-mâi ke moi-é;Â-tô-nì-ngâ — â-mê he Ha̍p-khi̍p;Sṳ-fap-thì-ngâ — â-mê he Â-pí-thâ;Yî-thi̍t-ngiam — â-mê he Yî-kiet-lâ.2 併於上節3 併於上節4 Yî-song liuk-ke lai-é chhiòn-phu he Thai-ví chhai Hî-pak-lùn cho vòng ke chhit-ngièn pan khì-kiên kiung ke.Thai-ví chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng cho vòng sâm-sṳ̍p sâm-ngièn;5 chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng liá khì-kiên, kì kiung chhin-tô lai-é.Kì ke pû-ngiòng Pha̍t-sṳ-pâ he Â-mí-li ke moi-é, vi kì kiung si-ke lai-é — Sṳ-mí-â, Sok-pa, Nâ-tân, Só-lò-mùn.6 Kì nang-ngoi yû kiú-ke lai-é — Yit-hot, Yî-li-sû-â, Yî-fap-lie̍t,7 Nò-kâ, Nì-fî, Ngâ-fî-â,8 Yî-li-sâ-mâ, Yî-li-ngâ-thai, Yî-li-fap-lie̍t.9 Liá-têu lai-é yî-ngoi, Thai-ví hàn-yû tui se-chiá só-kiung ke lai-é. Kì ya yû yit-ke moi-é miàng ôn-to Thap-mâ. Só-lò-mùn-vòng ke Heu-thoi10 Só-lò-mùn ke heu-thoi, cheu fu-ne chhòn-hâ ke kâ-phú ki chhai hâ-poi: Só-lò-mùn, Lò-pô-ôn, Â-pí-ngâ, Â-sat, Yok-sâ-fap,11 Yok-hò-làn, Â-ha̍p-chhia, Yok-â-sṳ̂,12 Â-mâ-chhia, Â-sat-li-ngâ, Yok-thán,13 Â-ha̍p-sṳ̂, Hî-sî-kâ, Mâ-nâ-sî,14 Â-mùn, Yok-sî-â.15 Yok-sî-â yû si-ke lai-é — Yok-ha̍p-nàn, Yok-ngâ-kin, Sî-tí-kâ, Sâ-liùng.16 Yok-ngâ-kin yû lióng-ke lai-é — Yok-ngâ-kîn lâu Sî-tí-kâ. Yok-ngâ-kîn-vòng ke Heu-thoi17 Yok-ngâ-kîn pûn Pâ-pí-lùn-ngìn chok-chéu. Kì yû chhit-ke lai-é — Sat-lâ-thiet,18 Mâ-kî-làn, Pí-thai-ngâ, Sṳ-nâ-sat, Yâ-kâ-mí, Hò-sâ-mâ, Nì-thai-pí-ngâ.19 Pí-thai-ngâ yû lióng-ke lai-é — Só-lò-pâ-pak lâu Sṳ-mî. Só-lò-pâ-pak yû lióng-ke lai-é — Mí-sû-làn lâu Ha̍p-nâ-nì-ngâ, hàn-yû yit-ke moi-é Sṳ-lò-me̍t.20 Kì nang-ngoi yu yû ńg-ke lai-é — Ha̍p-sû-pâ, Â-het, Pí-li-kâ, Ha̍p-sat-tí, Yì-sâ-Hî-sit.21 Ha̍p-nâ-nì-ngâ yû lióng-ke lai-é — Pí-lâ-thì lâu Yâ-chhî-â. Yâ-chhî-â kiung Li-fap-ngâ; Li-fap-ngâ kiung Â-ngí-nàn; Â-ngí-nàn kiung Ngò-pâ-tí-â; Ngò-pâ-tí-â kiung Sṳ-kâ-nì.22 Sṳ-kâ-nì yû yit-ke lai-é Sṳ-mâ-ngâ, yû ńg-ke sûn-é — Ha̍p-thut, Yî-kap, Pâ-li-â, Nì-li-ngâ, Sâ-fap.23 Nì-li-ngâ yû sâm-ke lai-é — Yî-li-yok-nâi, Hî-sî-kâ, Â-sṳ̂-li-kôn.24 Yî-li-yok-nâi yû chhit-ke lai-é — Hò-thai-ngâ, Yî-li-â-sṳ̍t, Pí-lòi-ngâ, Â-kuk, Yok-ha̍p-nàn, Thi-lòi-ngâ, Â-nâ-nì.
