1 Pó-lò chhai Thiap-sat-lò-nì-kâ ke Kûng-chok Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, ngì-têu chhṳ-kâ tî, ngài-têu fóng-mun ngì-têu pin m̀-he mò-háu ke.2 Ngì-têu tî, ngài-têu hi ngì-têu ke-vi yî-chhièn, chhai Fî-li̍p-pí yí-kîn su pet-hoi, pûn-ngìn vú-yu̍k. Chúng-he, ngài-têu ke Song-ti su ngài-têu yúng-kám, sûi-yèn chhai tông-tô ke chú-tóng chûng, hàn-he chiông Kì ke fuk-yîm chhòn-pûn ngì-têu.3 Ngài-têu ke khien-miên m̀-he tui chho-ngu ya-he mò sùn-chṳn ke thung-kî lòi, ya m̀-he sióng-oi cha-khî má-ngìn.4 Tiên-to, Song-ti kháu-ngiam yu kién-sién ngài-têu, chiông chhòn fuk-yîm ke ngim-vu thok pûn ngài-têu. Só-yî, Song-ti oi ngài-têu kóng má-ke, ngài-têu chhiu kóng má-ke. Ngài-têu m̀-he oi pûn ngìn fôn-hí, he oi pûn ke chhat-ngiam ngài-têu nui-sîm ke Song-ti fôn-hí.5 Ngì-têu tî, ngài-têu chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt kóng fung-sṳ̀n ngì-têu ke fa, sîm ya mò chhùn thâm-ngiam; liá he Song-ti cho-tet vi ngài-têu cho kien-chṳn ke.6 Ngài-têu mò khiù ngìn ke chhṳ̂n-chan, mò-lun he ngì-têu ke, ya-he phe̍t-ngìn ke.7 Sṳ̂n-vì Kî-tuk ke sṳ́-thù, pún-lòi ngài-têu yû khièn hó yêu-khiù ngì-têu ke chûn-chhung; chúng-he, chhai ngì-têu chûng-kiên ke sṳ̀, ngài-têu yung vûn-yù khoán-thai ngì-têu, chhiong â-mê yông-yuk chṳ́-ńg yit-yong.8 Ngài-têu ki-yèn án-ngiòng siak ngì-têu, m̀ tân-chhiang fôn-hí thùng ngì-têu fûn-hióng Song-ti ke fuk-yîm, lièn ngài-têu ke sâng-miang ya kâm-ngien pûn ngì-têu, yîn-vi ngì-têu he ngài-têu só thung-siak ke!9 Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, ngì-têu yin-tông ki-tet, ngài-têu he ngióng-pân sat-mâng lò-luk lòi cho-se; ngài-têu ngit-ya kûng-chok, yîn-vi chhòn Song-ti ke fuk-yîm pûn ngì-têu ke sṳ̀-chiet, mò-oi kâ-thiâm ngì-têu ke fu-tâm.10 Ngì-têu he kien-chṳn-ngìn, Song-ti ya cho-tet cho-chṳn: Tui ngì-têu sin ke ngìn, ngài-têu ke cho-ngìn he ngióng-pân sùn-kiet, kûng-chṳn, mò hó hiàm.11 Ngì-têu tî, ngài-têu tui ngì-têu mî-yit-ke ngìn, chhiu-chhiong â-pâ tui chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-ńg yit-yong:12 Ngài-têu kú-li ngì-têu, ôn-ví ngì-têu; ngài-têu ya yit-chhṳ̍t khien-va ngì-têu, hàng-sṳ oi pûn Song-ti fôn-hí; Kì chhiu-he fû-seu ngì-têu lòi fûn-hióng Kì ke chú-khièn lâu yùng-kông ke Song-ti.13 Ngài-têu ya sòng-sòng vi liá-ke sṳ kám-chhia Song-ti: Thâng-tó ngài-têu só chhòn Song-ti ke chṳ̂n-tho, ngì-têu chhiu liâng-su, pin ngin-vì m̀-he ngìn ke fa, he khok-khok sṳ̍t-sṳ̍t tui Song-ti lòi ke chṳ̂n-tho; liá chṳ̂n-tho( 1 2:13 "liá chṳ̂n-tho" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Song-ti".) ya chhai ngì-têu sin ke ngìn chûng-kiên cho kûng.14 Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, ngì-têu só tù-tó ke, thùng ke-têu chhai Yù-thai thi-khî, Song-ti ke chung kau-fi, chhiu-he su̍k Kî-tuk Yâ-sû ke sin-thù só tù-tó ke khiung-yong. Ngì-têu pûn chhṳ-kâ ke thùng-pâu pet-hoi, chhiu-chhiong kì-têu pûn Yù-thai ke thùng-pâu pet-hoi khiung-yong.15 Ke-têu Yù-thai-ngìn hoi-sí Chú Yâ-sû lâu chung siên-tî, yu chiông ngài-têu kiuk chhut-hi. Kì-têu ke só cho, Song-ti chhin mò fôn-hí. Kì-têu yu lâu chung-ngìn tui-thi̍t.16 Lièn ngài-têu tui Ngoi-pâng-ngìn chhòn tho-lî lòi pûn Ngoi-pâng-ngìn tet-kiu, kì-têu ya oi fán-tui. Chhiong án-ngiòng, kì-têu ke chhui yit-chhṳ̍t chen-kâ to mân-mân; Song-ti ke thai fat-khién chui-mî lìm-to kì-têu lé! Pó-lò Hî-mong Chai Fóng-mun Kì-têu17 Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, ngài-têu su pet-hoi, thùng ngì-têu chhiam-sṳ̀ fûn-khôi, he sṳ̂n-thí fûn-khôi thin-thin, ngài-têu ke sîm pin mò lì-khôi ngì-têu. Ngài-têu fî-sòng sióng-ngiam ngì-têu, sióng-chhin phan-fap oi chai khon-tó ngì-têu!18 Ngài-têu chhin sióng-oi chai hi ngì-têu ke-vi! Ngài — Pó-lò yit-pái yu yit-pái tá-son oi chón-hi khon ngì-têu, chúng-he Sat-tan chú-tóng ngài-têu.19 Ngài-têu ke Chú Yâ-sû chai-lòi ke sṳ̀, chhai kì ke mien-chhièn, ngài-têu ke hî-mong lâu fôn-hí, pin só khoâ-khiéu ke miên-liù he má-ke nè? Kám m̀-he ngì-têu?20 Ngì-têu khok-sṳ̍t he ngài-têu ke kông-yùng, ngài-têu ke fôn-hí!
