1 Cho chú-ngìn ke, ngì-têu oi kûng-phìn ha̍p-lî lòi tui-thai nù-phu̍k; oi tî, ngì-têu chhai thiên-táng ya yû yit-vi Chú. Khien-miên ke Fa2 Ngì-têu oi pet-chhiet khì-tó, khì-tó ke sṳ̀ oi kín-siáng, ya oi chhùn kám-chhia ke sîm.3 Thùng-sṳ̀, ya chhiáng vi ngài-têu khì-tó, khiù Song-ti vi ngài-têu khôi chhòn fuk-yîm ke mùn, lòi kóng-lun Kî-tuk ke o-pi. Liá chhiu-he ngài chhô-kâm ke ngièn-yîn.4 Chhiáng vi ngài khì-tó, pûn ngài chhin chit-ngim chiông liá-ke o-pi kóng-to chhîn-chhîn chhú-chhú.5 Thùng m̀-he Kî-tuk-thù ke ngìn vông-lòi ke sṳ̀, ngì-têu oi pá-ak kî-fi, hó-hó yung ngì-têu ke chṳ-fi.6 Kóng-fa oi sòng-sòng fò-hi, chhiong yung yàm thiàu-mi, oi tî ngióng-pân fì-tap mî-yit-ke ngìn só thì-chhut ke mun-thì. Chui-mî ke Chhiáng-ôn7 Ngài chhai liá-vi ke yit-chhiet chhìn-hìn, ngài-têu chhîn-oi ke hiûng-thi Thûi-kî-kú voi siòng-se lâu ngì-têu kóng. Kì he yit-ke chûng-sîm ke kûng-ngìn, thùng ngài khiung-ha cho Chú ke phu̍k-ngìn.8 Ngài thi̍t-phe̍t phai kì hi ngì-têu ke-vi, po-ko ngài-têu liá-vi ke chhìn-hìn, hó pûn ngì-têu tet-tó kú-li.9 Thùng kì khiung-ha hi ke yû yit-ke chûng-sîm yu chhîn-oi ke hiûng-thi Â-nì-sî-mèu; kì he ngì-têu ke-vi ke ngìn. Kì lióng-sà voi chiông liá-vi yit-chhiet ke sṳ lâu ngì-têu kóng.10 Thùng ngài khiung-ha chhô-kâm ke Â-lî-tha̍t-kú tui ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn. Pâ-nâ-pâ ke péu-ló-thâi Mâ-khó ya tui ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn. (Koân-he Mâ-khó, ngài sṳt fûn-fu, kì na-he hi ngì-têu ke-vi, oi chiap-thai kì.)11 Phiên-miàng Yù-sṳ-tû ke Yâ-su ya tui ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn. Yù-thai-ngìn ke sin-thù tông-chûng, chṳ́-yû kì sâm-sà vi Song-ti ke koet lâu ngài thùng-kûng, tui ngài yû chhin-thai ke ôn-ví.12 Tui ngì-têu ke-vi lòi ke Yî-pâ-fut hiong ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn; kì ya he Kî-tuk Yâ-sû ke phu̍k-ngìn. Kì sòng-sòng vi ngì-têu pet-chhiet khì-tó, khiù Song-ti pûn ngì-têu sin-sîm kiên-thin, cho sṳ̀n-su̍k ke Kî-tuk-thù, vàn-chhiòn sun-fu̍k Kì ke chṳ́-yi.13 Kì vi ngì-têu, ya vi-tó Ló-tí-kâ lâu Hî-lâ-pô-lî ke hiûng-thi chí-moi sîn-khú cho-kûng; liá he ngài cho-tet chṳn-mìn ke.14 Ngài-têu só chhîn-oi ke Lu-kâ yî-sên lâu Tí-mâ lióng-sà ya hiong ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn.15 Chhiáng thoi ngài-têu tui Ló-tí-kâ ke hiûng-thi chí-moi, lâu Nèn-fap, hàn-yû chhai kì vuk-hâ ke kau-fi chhiáng-ôn.16 Ngì-têu siên-thu̍k liá-fûng sin-é heu, chhiáng chón-kâu pûn Ló-tí-kâ kau-fi siên-thu̍k; thùng-sṳ̀, ngì-têu ya oi thu̍k tui Ló-tí-kâ kau-fi chón pûn ngì-têu ke sin-é.17 Oi lâu Â-kî-pu kóng: "Ngì só liâng-su ke fu̍k-sṳ Chú ke chṳt-vu yit-thin oi chhin-li̍t vàn-sṳ̀n."18 Ngài — Pó-lò chhîn-pit tui ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn. Ngì-têu oi ki-tet ngài he tai-tén só-lien ke!Ngien Song-ti su ên-tién pûn ngì-têu!
