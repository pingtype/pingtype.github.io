1 Yêu-tai ke Pí-yi Song-chú án-ngiòng fûn-fu ngài: "Hi mâi yit-thiàu mà-sâ yêu-tai( 21 13:1 "yêu-tai": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chṳ́ tông-sṳ̀ ke vì-yêu-fu̍k, chhiu-he tú-tap-fu, vi-tó vì-tén ngìn ke ha-puk.) kiê hí-lòi; chúng-he m̀-hó chim chhai súi-chûng."2 Só-yî ngài cheu Song-chú ke fûn-fu mâi yit-thiàu yêu-tai kiê hí-lòi.3 Song-chú thi-ngi pái lâu ngài kóng:4 "Ngì to Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò( 22 13:4 "Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò": Chhai Hî-pak-lòi-vùn fat-yîm lâu Pâ-lâ siông-chhiong. Pâ-lâ su̍k Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k (chhâm-kháu Yok-sû-â Ki 18:23), lì Â-nâ-thut-sàng chhin khiûn. Yû ho̍k-chá ngin-vì Yâ-li-mí hi Pâ-lâ, Pâ-lâ ya siong-chṳ̂n Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò. Hâ-poi khiung-yong.) hi, chiông ngì só kiê ke yêu-tai khong chhai sa̍k-phung tú."5 Ngài chhiu cheu Song-chú ke min-lin to Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò hi, chiông yêu-tai khong chhai ke-vi.6 Ko-liáu yit-chham sṳ̀-kiên, Song-chú fûn-fu ngài: "Ngì to Yu-fat-lâ-tí-hò hi nâ ke-thiàu Ngài fûn-fu ngì khong hí-lòi ke yêu-tai."7 Ngài chhiu chón-to ke-vi, chiông khong-tén ke yêu-tai yet chhut-lòi; yêu-tai yí-kîn mièn-thet, yung m̀-tet lé.8 Chiap-tén, Song-chú yu lâu ngài kóng-fa. Kì kóng:9 "Ngài oi cheu án-ngiòng pho-fái Yù-thai lâu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ngìn-mìn ke thai kiêu-ngau.10 Liá-pân ngìn-mìn tông-fái, m̀-hén thâng-chhiùng Ngài ke min-lin. Kì-têu hàn-he ngang-kiáng sià-ok, hàn hi kin-pai, fu̍k-sṳ phe̍t ke sṳ̀n-mìn. Só-yî, kì-têu voi chhiong liá-thiàu yêu-tai án-ngiòng pien mò lu-yung.11 Chhiu-chhiong yêu-tai ngiàm-tén ngìn ke sṳ̂n-thí, Ngài pún-lòi oi só-yû Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn lâu Yù-thai-ngìn ngiàm-tén Ngài. Ngài oi kì-têu cho Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn, hó-pûn Ngài ke sṳn-miàng su chhṳ̂n-chan, tet yùng-yeu; chúng-he kì-têu m̀-hén thâng." Chiú-kông ke Pí-yi12 Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti lâu ngài án-ngiòng kóng: "Yâ-li-mí â, ngì oi tui Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn kóng: Tak-chak chiú-kông chhiòn-phu oi chông-mân chiú. Na-he kì-têu en-kóng: ‘Ngài-têu ngióng m̀-tî tak-chak chiú-kông oi chông-mân chiú?’13 yèn-heu, ngì chang lâu kì-têu kóng: Ngài — Song-chú oi yung chiú kon-chui liá thi-fông só-yû ke ngìn-mìn; Thai-ví vòng-chhèu ke kiûn-vòng, chi-sṳ̂, siên-tî, lâu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu voi chhiong sṳ̍t chiú-chui.14 Ngài oi tá-sui kì-têu, sṳ́ kì-têu chhiu-son he yà-ôi lâu chṳ́-ńg ya voi ngia̍k-tó, chhiong chiú-kông siông-kho̍k to sui-thet. Ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀ lìn-mén kì-têu, m̀ siak kì-têu, m̀ thùng-chhìn kì-têu; Ngài yit-thin oi sêu-me̍t kì-têu." Yâ-li-mí Kín-ko Kiêu-ngau ke Hàng-vì15 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, Song-chú yí-kîn án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.Ngì-têu oi khiâm-pî, oi chu-yi thâng Kì ke fa.16 Vû-am hàn-màng to yî-chhièn,ngì-têu hàn-màng chhai am-am ke chhṳ̂m-sân tiet-tó yî-chhièn,oi yùng-kông Song-chú — ngì-têu ke Song-ti.Song-chú hàn-màng chiông ngì-têu só phan-mong kekông-mìn pien-cho vû-am yî-chhièn,ngì-têu oi yùng-kông Song-chú — ngì-têu ke Song-ti.17 Na-he ngì-têu m̀-thâng,ngài oi yîn-vi ngì-têu ke kiêu-ngau thêu-kieu;ngài voi kieu-to muk-chṳp làm-kâm;yîn-vi Song-chú ke yòng-khiùn voi pûn-ngìn chok-chéu lé.18 Song-chú lâu ngài kóng: "Hi ham vòng lâu thai-heu( 23 13:18 "vòng lâu thai-heu" khó-nèn chṳ́ Yok-ngâ-kîn lâu Nì-fu-sṳ̂-thâ (chhâm-kháu Lie̍t-vòng Ki-ha 24:8).) khiâm-pî tui pó-chho táng hâ-lòi! Yîn-vi kì-têu thèu-nà táng chhin-chiâng ke miên-liù voi tiet hâ-lòi lé.19 Yù-thai nàm-phu tak-chak sàng-sṳ voi pûn thi̍t-ngìn vì-tén; mò-ngìn nèn thoi kì-têu kié-vì. Yù-thai ke ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu voi pûn-ngìn chok-chéu."20 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng â, ngì khon! Thi̍t-ngìn tui pet-fông lòi lô! Kâu-thok ngì khon-ku ke yòng-khiùn, ngì só khoâ-khiéu ke yòng-è liá-ha chhai nai-vi nè?21 Ngì só kiet-kâu, yit-chhṳ̍t khon-cho hó phèn-yû ke, fán-chón lòi chṳ̂n-fu̍k ngì, oi thúng-chhṳ ngì; ngì hàn-yû má-ke fa hó kóng nè? Ngì voi chhiong sán-fu chhṳn-thung án-ngiòng thung-khú.22 Na-he ngì sióng-oi tî, ngì cho-má-ke voi tù-tó liá-ke châi-nan, cho-má-ke khiùn-è pûn-ngìn hiên hí-lòi, pûn-ngìn vú-yu̍k, liá he yîn-vi ngì fam tông-tô chhui só ngiâ-lòi ke.23 Kú-sṳ̍t-ngìn kám-nèn kói-pien phì-fû ke set? Fâ-pau kám-nèn chhù-thet kì ke pân-tiám? Liá-têu sṳ na-he yû khó-nèn, ngì liá-têu cho-koan fái-sṳ ke ngìn chang yû khó-nèn cho hó-sṳ.24 Só-yî, Song-chú oi tá-san ngì-têu, chhiu-chhiong khóng-yâ ke fûng chiông phang-é chhôi-chéu.25 Kì kóng, liá he ngì ke po-yin. Kì kiet-thin án-ngiòng tui-fu ngì, yîn-vi ngì m̀ ki-tet Kì, fán-chón hi-sin ká-sṳ̀n.26 Song-chú oi chhîn-chhṳ hiên ngì ke khiùn-è, lòi-miâng ngì ke mien, sṳ́ ngì kien-seu.27 Yîn-vi Kì yû khon-tó ngì cho Kì só-nâu ke sṳ; khon-tó ngì chhai sân-táng, thièn-hong pai phe̍t ke sṳ̀n-mìn, chhiong fu-ngìn-kâ fam kiên-yìm, fat yìm-sâng, hàng m̀-tî kien-seu ke yìm-lon.Yâ-lu-sat-lâng â, ngì chhám lé! Ngì mò chhiang-li oi to kí-sṳ̀ nè?
