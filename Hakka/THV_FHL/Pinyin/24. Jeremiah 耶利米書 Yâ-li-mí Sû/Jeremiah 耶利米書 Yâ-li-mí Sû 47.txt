1 Yû-koân Fî-li-sṳ ke Yi-ngièn Chhai Âi-khi̍p-vòng kûng-tá Kâ-sat yî-chhièn, Song-chú tui siên-tî Yâ-li-mí kóng-hí yû-koân Fî-li-sṳ ke sṳ.2 Song-chú kóng:Khon â, hò-pa súi voi tui pet-fông chong hí-lòi,chho-sṳ̀n súi-châi.Thai-súi voi chim-thet chhiòn-thi lâu khì-chûng só-yû ke,thûn-thet sàng-sṳ lâu sàng-tú ke ngìn-mìn.Ngìn voi thai-sâng hêm kiu-miang;só-yû ke ngìn-mìn voi thai-kieu.3 Kì-têu voi thâng-tó chan-mâ pêu ke sâng,chan-chhâ lung-lung-kún, chhâ-lin-é lu̍k-lu̍k-kún ke sâng!Cho â-pâ ke sûng-sú sôn-ngiôn mò-li̍t,mò phan-fap khon-ku chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-ńg.4 Fí-me̍t yit-chhiet Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn ke ngit-è to lé;thèu-pái pông-chhu Thai-ngì, Sî-tun ke ngìn,oi sêu-me̍t kì-têu ke sṳ̀-kiên to lé.Ngài — Song-chú oi sêu-me̍t Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn,sêu-me̍t tui Khiet-lî-thi̍t-tó( 68 47:4 "Khiet-lî-thi̍t-tó" kú miàng ôn-to "Kâ-fî-thok-tó".) hói-sùn lòi ke chhûn-tó ke ngìn.5 Kâ-sat-ngìn thi-kông-thèu péu-sṳ pî-sông.Â-sṳ̍t-kî-lùn tiâm-tiâm chhiong sí-sàng yit-yong.Phìn-ngièn chhûn-tó ke Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn â,hàn-oi kot-sông chhṳ-kâ( 69 47:5 "kot-sông chhṳ-kâ" he yi-kau ke fûng-siu̍k (chhâm-kháu Sṳ̂n-min Ki 14:1).) pî-sông kí-kiú nè?6 Ngì-têu hêm kóng: "Song-chú ke tô-kiam â,ngì oi chhṳ̀ kí-kiú chang thìn nè?Sû-kiam ngi̍p tô-seu,pûn kì hiet-khun â!"7 Chúng-he, Ngài ham kì kûng-kit,kì ngióng cho-tet thìn nè?Ngài min-lin kì kûng-kit Â-sṳ̍t-kî-lùn,chhṳ̀-thet hói-sùn ke ngìn-mìn.
