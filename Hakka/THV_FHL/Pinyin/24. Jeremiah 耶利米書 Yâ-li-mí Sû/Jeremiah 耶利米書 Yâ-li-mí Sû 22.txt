1 Yâ-li-mí pûn Yù-thai Vòng-sṳt ke Kín-koSong-chú fûn-fu Yâ-li-mí hâ-to Yù-thai vòng-kiûng, hi lâu Thai-ví vòng-chhèu ke kiûn-vòng, kì ke kôn-yèn, pin tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng kok sàng-mùn chhut-ngi̍p ke ngìn-mìn kóng, Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:2 併於上節3 "Ngài — Song-chú min-lin ngì-têu chṳp-hàng kûng-chṳn ke sṳ́m-phan, sṳ̂n-chông chṳn-ngi, tui khî-fu ngìn ke sú-chûng kié-kiu pûn-ngìn ap-pet ke. Ngì-têu cho-m̀-tet fái khoán-thai ya-he ap-pet ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn, kû-yì lâu koá-fu; ya cho-m̀-tet chhai liá só-chhai liù mò-chhui ke ngìn ke hiet.4 Na-he ngì-têu chûn-sú Ngài ke min-lin, Thai-ví ke heu-thoi chhiu voi ki-siu̍k cho vòng. Kì-têu lâu kì-têu ke kôn-yèn, hàn-yû ngìn-mìn, chhiòn-phu cho-tet chhô chan-chhâ, khì-mâ, kîn-ko liá-têu sàng-mùn, ngi̍p-to vòng-kiûng.5 Na-he ngì-têu m̀ fu̍k-chhiùng Ngài ke min-lin, Ngài chṳ́-tén Ngài chhṳ-kâ ke miàng fat-sṳ, liá vòng-kiûng voi pien-cho fông-phû. Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.6 "Chhai Ngài khon-lòi, Yù-thai vòng-sṳt ke vòng-kiûng chhiong Kî-lie̍t, yu chhiong Lì-pâ-nun sân-táng án-chiâng; chúng-he Ngài oi sṳ́ kì pien-cho khóng-yâ, mò-ngìn he̍t ke sàng-sṳ.7 Ngài oi phai ngìn fí-me̍t kì. Kì-têu chhiòn-phu voi tai pú-thèu lòi, chám-thôn ke-têu chhin-chiâng ke hiông-pak-su chhû-é, fit-lo̍k fó-teu tú hi.8 "Heu-lòi, chhin-tô ngoi-koet ngìn voi kîn-ko liá só-chhai, thai-kâ voi mun, Song-chú cho-má-ke án-ngiòng tui-fu liá-chho thai-sàng?9 Yèn-heu kì-têu yu chhṳ-kâ en-kóng: Yîn-vi liá-chho sàng ke ngìn vì-poi Song-chú — kì-têu ke Song-ti lâu kì-têu li̍p ke yok, hi fu̍k-sṳ pai phe̍t ke sṳ̀n." Yû-koân Yok-ha̍p-sṳ̂10 Yù-thai ngìn-mìn â, m̀-hó vi sí-thet ke vòng kieu;m̀-hó vi-tó Yok-sî-â-vòng pî-sông.Oi vi kì ke lai-é Yok-ha̍p-sṳ̂ lòi kieu;kì pûn-ngìn chok-chéu, yún-yén m̀-voi chón-lòi,m̀-voi chai khon-tó chhṳ-kâ ke ku-hiông lé.11 Yû-koân sṳ̀n-chiap kiâ-pâ Yok-sî-â cho Yù-thai-vòng ke Yok-ha̍p-sṳ̂( 33 22:11 "Yok-ha̍p-sṳ̂" chhâm-kháu Lie̍t-vòng Ki-ha 23:30, ya ôn-to "Sâ-liùng", chhâm-kháu Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 3:15.), Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng: "Kì pûn-ngìn chok-chéu, lì-khôi liá só-chhai, yún-yén m̀-voi chón-lòi.12 Kì voi sí chhai pûn-ngìn chok-hi ke só-chhai, m̀-voi chai khon-tó liá-ke thi-fông lé." Yû-koân Yok-ngâ-kin13 Chhám lé, liá-sà ngìn yû châi-fo lé!Kì yung put-ngi ke sú-thon vi chhṳ-kâ hí vòng-kiûng,yung mò kûng-chṳn ke fông-fap hí tông-tô lèu-fòng;kì khiòng-pet ngìn-mìn cho pha̍k-kûng,m̀ fu kûng-chhièn.14 Chhám lé, liá-sà ngìn yû châi-fo lé!Kì kóng: "Ngài oi hí chhin ví-thai ke vòng-kiûng,hí chhin-fat ke lèu-fòng."Kì khôi tông-thai ke chhûng-mùn,yung hiông-pak-su cho thiên-fâ-pán,chhit thai-fùng ke yù-chhit.15 Kám-kóng ngì hí pí phe̍t-ngìn kha kô-kui ke hiông-pak-su vòng-kiûng,chhiu péu-sṳ ngì he yit-vi hièn-mìn ke kiûn-vòng he-mò?Ngiâ-pâ yit-sên-ngìn hióng-fuk, yu sṳ̍t yu lîm.Kì cho-ngìn cho-sṳ cheu kûng-phìn kûng-ngi lòi hàng;kì só-cho ke chhiòn-phu sun-li.16 Kì vi khiùng-khú mò-ngìn pông-chhu ke sṳ̂n-yên;kì só-cho ke yit-chhiet sun-li.Liá he péu-sṳ kì ngin-sṳt Song-chú.17 Chúng-he ngì tông thâm-sîm, tân-tân ku chhṳ-kâ ke li-yit;ngì liù mò-chhui ke ngìn ke hiet,yung phau-li̍t ap-chṳ ngìn-mìn.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.18 Só-yî, yû-koân Yok-sî-â ke lai-é Yù-thai-vòng Yok-ngâ-kin, Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Mò-ngìn voi vi kì ôi-sông kóng:"Âi-châi, ngài ke hiûng-thi chí-moi â, hàn-chhám ô!"Mò-ngìn voi vi kì sông-sîm kieu kóng:"Ngài ke chú! Ngài ke vòng â!"19 Kì voi pûn-ngìn thô-chéu,pûn-ngìn fit-to Yâ-lu-sat-lâng sàng-ngoi,chhiu-chhiong mài-chong yit-chak lì-è.20 Yû-koân Yâ-lu-sat-lâng Ngìn-mìn ke Miang-yun Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke ngìn-mìn â,sông-to Lì-pâ-nun sân-táng hi hêm!To Pâ-sân hi thai-sâng kieu!Tui Mô-ap ke sân-táng hêm!Yîn-vi ngì-têu ke thùng-mèn-koet chhiòn-phu pûn-ngìn tá-sû lô.21 Ngì-têu fàn-yùng ke sṳ̀ Song-chú kín-ko ngì-têu,chúng-he ngì-têu kóng: "Ngài-têu mò-oi thâng!"Ngì-têu chhiùng-se chhiu m̀ thâng-kóng;chhṳ-chhiùng m̀-sṳt chṳn-kîn fu̍k-chhiùng Song-chú.22 Ngì-têu ke liâng-chhiu voi pûn khòng-fûng kién-chéu;ngì-têu ke thùng-mèn-koet voi pûn-ngìn chok-chéu;ngì-têu chhiòn-phu voi tù-tó kien-seu, pûn-ngìn vú-yu̍k;yîn-vi ngì-têu cho án-tô ke fái-sṳ.23 Ngì-têu he̍t chhai yung Lì-pâ-nun hiông-pak-su hí ke vòng-kiûng,ngì-têu chhṳ-kâ láu-tó tông ôn-chhiòn,chúng-he thai-nàn lìm-to ke-sṳ̀,ngì-têu he ngióng-pân khó-lièn â!Ngì-têu voi chhiong kiung se-ngìn-è ke fu-ngìn-kâ án-ngiòng thai-sâng kín-chhên.24 Song-ti Sṳ́m-phan Yok-ngâ-kîn Song-chú tui Yù-thai-vòng Yok-ngâ-kin ke lai-é Yok-ngâ-kîn( 34 22:24 "Yok-ngâ-kîn" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Kô-nì-ngâ", 28 chiet khiung-yong.) kóng: "Ngài — yún-sên ke Song-ti fat-sṳ: Chhiu-son ngì he Ngài chṳn-sú só tai yin-é yung ke kim-chṳ́, Ngài ya oi chiông ngì pok-thet,25 kâu-pûn ngì só kiâng, oi sû ngiâ miang ke ngìn. Ngài oi chiông ngì kâu-pûn Pâ-pí-lùn-vòng Nì-pu-kap-nì-sat lâu kì ke kiûn-chhui.26 Ngài oi chiông ngì lâu ngiâ-mê kiuk-to ngoi-koet hi, to ke m̀-he ngì-têu chhut-se ke só-chhai hi; ngì-têu voi sí chhai ke-vi.27 Ngì-têu chhin-sióng oi chón-lòi, chúng-he yún-yén mò phan-fap chón-lòi."28 Yû-ngìn kóng: "Yok-ngâ-kîn-vòng kám m̀-he chhiong yit-chak mò-ngìn oi ke lan âng-é? Na-m̀-he, cho-má-ke kì lâu kì ke chṳ́-ńg chhiòn-phu pûn-ngìn fit-thet, kiuk-to ngoi-pâng hi nè?"29 Thai-thi â! Thai-thi â! Thai-thi â!Oi thâng Song-chú ke fa.30 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Oi chiông liá-ngìn tên-ki cho mò chṳ́-ńg,kì yit-sên-ngìn mò pan-tiám sṳ̀n-chhiu!Kì m̀-voi yû heu-thoi hó sṳ̀n-chiap Thai-ví ke vòng-vi;mò yit-ke voi cho vòng thúng-chhṳ Yù-thai.
