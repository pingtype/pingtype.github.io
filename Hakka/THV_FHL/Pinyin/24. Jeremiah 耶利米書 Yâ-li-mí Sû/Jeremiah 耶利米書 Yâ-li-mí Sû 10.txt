1 Pai Ngiéu-siong lâu Chṳn-kîn ke Kin-pai Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, ngì-têu oi thâng Song-chú hiong ngì-têu kóng ke fa.2 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Ngì-têu m̀-hó ho̍k lie̍t-koet ke fûng-siu̍k si̍p-koan,ya m̀-hó chhiong lie̍t-koet án-ngiòng pûn thiên-táng ke van-siong hak-tó.3 Kì-têu só sin-fung ke chhiòn he hî-khûng,put-ko he tui su-nà chûng chám hâ-lòi ke su-é,muk-siong sṳ̂-fu yung tô-é tiâu-khiet ke chok-phín,4 yèn-heu siông-kîm siông-ngiùn lòi tá-pan,yung tâng-é lâu chhùi-è lòi ku-thin,sṳ́ kì-têu m̀-voi vang-thet.5 Kì-têu ke ngiéu-siong chhiong koâ-thièn tú yung vò-kón chat ke ngìn:Kì-têu m̀-voi kóng-fa,m̀-voi hàng-lu, oi ngìn kông-tén hàng.Só-yî m̀-sṳ́ kiâng kì-têu!Kì-têu m̀-voi kong-châi,ya mò nèn-li̍t kong-fuk.6 Song-chú â, mò-ngìn lâu Ngì pí é-tet;Ngì tông ví-thai, Ngì ke sṳn-miàng thai-yû nèn-li̍t.7 Ngì he van-koet ke kiûn-vòng, má-ngìn kám m̀ kin-vi?Ngì yin-kôi su chûn-kin.Lie̍t-koet ke chṳ-fi ngìn lâu kiûn-vòng mò yit-sà lâu Ngì pí é-tet.8 Kì-têu chhiòn-phu ngong-ngong tong-tong;kì-têu tui su-é khat ke ngiéu-siong liâng-su hî-ká ke kau-hiun.9 Kì-têu ke ngiéu-siong siông-tén tui Thâ-sṳ̂ chin-khiéu ke ngiùn-è,lâu tui Vû-fap chin-khiéu ke kîm-é,chhiòn-phu he muk-siong lâu ngiùn-siong ke sú-kûng.Kì-têu chok làm-set, chṳ́-set ke sâm-fu,chhiòn-phu he sú-ngi chhin-hó ke ngìn chhiak ke.10 Song-chú â, chṳ́-yû Ngì he chṳ̂n-sṳ̀n,he yún-sên ke Song-ti, yún-yén ke kiûn-vòng.Ngì yit fat-khién, thai-thi chhiu kín-chûn;Ngì yit su-hi, van-koet chhiu khî m̀-he̍t.11 Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn â, ngì-têu oi hiong kì-têu siên-ko, ngiéu-siong m̀-he chhóng-chho thiên-thi ke sṳ̀n; kì-têu yit-thin voi tui thiên-thi kiên sêu-me̍t. Siung-chan Song-ti ke Sṳ̂12 Song-chú yung nèn-li̍t chhóng-chho thai-thi;Kì yung chṳ-fi kien-li̍p sṳ-kie,yung chhûng-mìn chán-khôi thiên-khûng.13 Kì yit-hióng lùi-kûng, thiên-khûng ke súi chhiu ho-ho-kún;Kì sṳ́ yùn tui thi-phìn-sien chhung-tén sông.Kì sṳ́ ngiap-lang chhai yí-chûng fat-kông;Kì sṳ́ fûng tui chhṳ-kâ ke chhông-khu chhôi-chhut.14 Ngìn tui liá kông-kín chhiòn-phu ngong-tong, mò chṳ-sṳt;tá-kîm sṳ̂-fu chho ngiéu-siong, fán-chón mò mien-chṳ́,yîn-vi kì-têu chho ke sṳ̀n-mìn he ká ke, m̀-voi théu-hi.15 Ngiéu-siong chhiòn he hî-khûng, he tông kien-seu ke tûng-sî;Song-chú sṳ́m-phan ke sṳ̀, kì-têu chhiòn-phu voi sêu-me̍t.16 Ngâ-kok ke Song-ti m̀-he chhiong liá-têu ngiéu-siong:Kì chhóng-chho van-vu̍t,kién-sién Yî-set-lie̍t cho chhṳ-kâ ke chṳ́-mìn.Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi he Kì ke miàng.17 Yi-ngièn Kì-têu voi Liù-mòng Su pâu-vì ke Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-ngìn â, chiông ngì-têu oi tai-chéu ke tûng-sî sû-hó.18 Yîn-vi Song-chú liá-ha oi chiông ngì-têu tui liá thú-thi fin chhut-hi. Kì oi kong châi-nan chhai ngì-têu sṳ̂n-hong, ngì-têu voi pûn-ngìn chok-chéu. Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.19 Yâ-li-mí thoi-péu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-ngìn kóng:( 17 10:19 "Yâ-li-mí ... kóng" ngièn-vùn mò liá-ki fa, chúng-he tui nui-yùng cho-tet án-ngiòng liáu-kié.)Chhám lé! Ngài ngióng-voi chho̍k-sông to án ngiàm-chhung?Chúng-he ngài ya voi sióng:"Liá ke thung-khú he ngài yit-thin oi ngiûn-su ke."20 Ngài ke chong-phùng pûn-ngìn pho-fái,chong-phùng ke sok-è chhiòn-phu thôn-thet.Chṳ́-ńg lì-khôi ngài chéu chhiang-chhiang lé;m̀-voi chai yû-ngìn lòi lâu ngài tap chong-phùng,ya mò-ngìn lòi lâu ngài tiau pu-liàm.21 Yîn-vi ngài-têu ke liâng-chhiu mò chṳ-sṳt;kì-têu m̀ chhìm-khiù Song-chú.Só-yî, kì-têu sṳt-phai,ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu si-san thet.22 Thâng â! Yû fûng-sâng chhòn-lòi kóng:"Yû khon-tó yit thai-khiùn kiûn-chhui tui pet-fông lòi,voi sṳ́ Yù-thai tak-chak sàng-sṳ fông-thet,sàng-cho yâ-kiéu ke teu."23 Song-chú â, ngài tî mò-ngìn nèn kiet-thin chhṳ-kâ ke miang-yun;mò-ngìn nèn ôn-phài chhṳ-kâ ke chhièn-thù.24 Song-chú â, khiù Ngì cheu kûng-tong lòi kón-kau ngài-têu;khiù Ngì m̀-hó chhai fat-khién chûng sêu-me̍t ngài-têu.25 Khiù Ngì chiông thai fat-khién tó chhai m̀ sṳ̀n-ngin Ngì ke lie̍t-koet,lâu m̀ kin-pai Ngì ke ngìn sṳ̂n-song.Yîn-vi kì-têu chhṳ̀ Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi;kì-têu chha̍t-tái fí-me̍t ngài-têu,sṳ́ ngài-têu ke kâ-yèn fông-fi.
