1 Yâ-li-mí lâu Chi-sṳ̂ Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí Chhûng-thu̍t Yîm-ma̍k ke lai-é, chúng-kón sṳn-thien ke chi-sṳ̂ Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí yit thâng-tó Yâ-li-mí siên-pu liá-têu fa,2 chhiu tá siên-tî Yâ-li-mí, chhai sṳn-thien pet-phién ke Phien-ngâ-mén mùn-nui yung kâ chiông kì só-tén.3 Thi-ngi chêu-sṳ̀n, Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí piong kì chhut-lòi. Yâ-li-mí chhiu tui kì kóng: "Song-chú pin mò chiông ngì ôn-to Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí. Kì pûn ngì ke miàng he ‘Si-vì Tet-ngìn-kiâng’.4 Yîn-vi Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng: ‘Ngài oi sṳ́ ngì chhṳ-kâ lâu ngì ke phèn-yû chhiòn-phu yîn-vi ngì kiâng-to mò-kat mò-sat; ngì voi chhîn-muk khon-tó kì-têu sí chhai thi̍t-ngìn ke tô-kiam hâ. Ngài voi chiông só-yû ke Yù-thai-ngìn kâu-pûn Pâ-pí-lùn-vòng thúng-chhṳ; kì voi chiông kì-têu chok-to Pâ-pí-lùn hi, ya-he chhṳ̀-sí kì-têu.5 Ngài oi pûn thi̍t-ngìn chhióng-kiap sàng-tú ke chhòi-fu, chiông sàng-tú chit-chhùn ke pó-vu̍t chhióng-chéu, lièn Yù-thai-vòng ke pó-pi ya chhióng-chéu. Kì-têu voi chiông tak-hong tûng-sî pân-to Pâ-pí-lùn hi.6 Lun-tó ngì, Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí, ngì lâu ngì chhiòn-kâ voi pûn-ngìn chok-to Pâ-pí-lùn hi. Ngì voi sí chhai ke-vi, chong chhai ke-vi; sṳ̀-sòng thâng ngì kóng ká yi-ngièn ke-têu phèn-yû ya voi lâu ngì tù-tó khiung-yong ke kiet-khiu̍k.’ " Yâ-li-mí Koai-yen Song-chú7 Song-chú â, Ngì met-sa ngài; ngài pûn Ngì met-sa tó.Ngì pí ngài kha-khiòng, Ngì yàng-ko ngài.Tak-sà chhiòn-phu met-sôi ngài;kì-têu kûi-ngit chiông ngài tông-cho seu-khô.8 Ngài tak-pái khôi-choi, thin-chho̍k oi thai-sâng hêm,thai-sâng kóng: Phau-li̍t! Fí-me̍t!Song-chú â, ngài siên-pu Ngì ke sin-sit,chúng-he ngiâ-lòi sâu-nat, méu-sṳ.9 Ngài na kóng: "Ngài mò-oi chai thì-hí Song-chú,mò-oi chai fung Kì ke miàng siên-kóng",Ngì ke fa chhiu voi chhiong fó chhai ngài sîm-tú sêu,chon-ngi̍p ngài ke kut-thèu.Ngài ngióng-pân tû ngiûn m̀-he̍t;ngài kô-put-chiông kóng chhut-lòi.10 Ngài thâng-tó chhin-tô ngìn se-sâng kóng:"Si-vì Tet-ngìn-kiâng" ke lòi lé;ngài-têu lòi ko kì!Lièn tî-kí ke phèn-yû ya chhiòn-phu tén-tén khon ngài lau-khui.Kì-têu kóng: "Mò yit-thin kì voi pûn-ngìn lau-tó!Ngài-têu chhiu yû khó-nèn yàng-ko kì, hiong kì po-sù."11 Chúng-he Song-chú â, Ngì lâu ngài khiung-ha;Ngì thai-yû nèn-li̍t, fî-sòng tet-ngìn-vi.Ap-pet ngài ke ngìn chhiòn-phu voi sṳt-phai;kì-têu ke yîm-mèu voi lau-khui.Kì-têu voi fî-sòng kien-seu, yîn-vi kì-têu sṳt-phai lé;ngìn yún-yén m̀-voi m̀ ki-tet kì-têu ke siû-yu̍k.12 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi â, Ngì ke chhài-kiet kûng-tong;Ngì khon-chhôn ngìn ke sîm-chhòng.Khiù Ngì pûn ngài khon-tó Ngì po-fu̍k ngài ke thi̍t-ngìn,yîn-vi ngài chiông ngài ke on-khien kâu-pûn Ngì.13 Oi chhong-kô siung-chan Song-chú!Oi chan-mî Song-chú!Kì kiu khiùng-khú-ngìn ke miang thot-lì fái-ngìn ke sú.14 Ngien ngài ke sâng-ngit su chu-chú!Ngien ngài chhut mû-thôi ke-ngit mò su chuk-fuk!15 Ngien ke hiong ngâ-pâ po sêu-sit ke ngìn su chu-chú!Kì tui ngâ-pâ po hó sêu-sit kóng:"Kiûng-hí ngì kiung lai-é!"16 Ngien ke-ngìn chhiong pûn Song-chú fí-me̍t ke sàng( 29 20:16 "pûn Song-chú fí-me̍t ke sàng" chṳ́ Só-tô-mâ lâu Ngò-mô-lâ, chhâm-kháu Chhóng-sṳ Ki 19:23-28.),tet m̀-tó Song-chú ke lìn-mén.Ngien kì chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu thâng-tó chhin-sê ke sâng,tông-chu-thèu thâng-tó chan-chên ke sê-sâng;17 yîn-vi Song-chú( 30 20:17 "Song-chú" ya-he yi̍t-cho "kì".) mò pûn ngài sí chhai mû-thôi,mò pûn mû-thôi sàng-cho ngài ke fùn-mu,ngâ-mê ke tú-sṳ́ chhiu yit-chhṳ̍t khoan-tén ngài.18 Ngài cho-má-ke oi chhut-se nè?Kám he vi-tó kîn-ngiam lò-khú, yù-sông,chhai kien-seu chûng kiet-suk ngài yit-sên ke ngièn-ngit mâ?
