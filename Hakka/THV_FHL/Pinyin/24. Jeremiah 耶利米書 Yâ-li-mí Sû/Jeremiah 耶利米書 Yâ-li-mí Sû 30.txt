1 Song-chú pûn Kì Chṳ́-mìn ke Yin-hí Song-chú ke fa lìm-to Yâ-li-mí, kóng:2 "Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti án-ngiòng kóng: Ngì oi chiông Ngài lâu ngì kóng-ko só-yû ke fa chhiòn-phu siá chhai sû tî-poi,3 yîn-vi sṳ̀-kiên voi-to, Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn tui Yù-thai lâu Yî-set-lie̍t pûn-ngìn chok-chéu ke, Ngài oi pûn kì-têu chón-to ku-hiông, chhùng-chṳ́n kâ-yèn. Ngài oi tai kì-têu chón-to Ngài su-pûn kì-têu chú-siên ke thú-thi; kì-têu voi chhùng-sîn tet-tó liá thú-thi. Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé."4 Song-chú tui Yî-set-lie̍t lâu Yù-thai ke ngìn-mìn kóng:5 Ngài thâng-tó voi pûn ngìn pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat ke sâng,liá he tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sâng, mò phìn-ôn ke sâng!6 Hi mun khon-é, he m̀-he yû nàm-ngìn oi kiung se-ngìn-è?Cho-má-ke Ngài khon-tó tak-ke nàm-ngìn yung sûng-sú nám-tén tú-sṳ́,chhiong he oi kiung ke fu-ngìn-kâ?Cho-má-ke tak-ke nàm-ngìn mien-set la̍k-chhiâng nè?7 Chhám lé! Tet-ngìn-kiâng ke ngit-è voi-to lé!Màng-sṳt yû án-ngiòng ke ngit-è;liá he Ngài chṳ́-mìn Ngâ-kok su-nan ke sṳ̀-khì,chúng-he kì-têu yit-thin voi tet-kiu.8 Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi án-ngiòng kóng: "To ke-ngit, Ngài oi áu-thôn koa chhai kì-têu kiáng-kîn ke ak, pâng-thôn kì-têu sṳ̂n-song ke só-lien, kì-têu m̀-voi chai cho ngoi-koet ngìn ke nù-chhòi.9 Kì-têu oi sṳ-fung Ngài — Song-chú, kì-têu ke Song-ti, lâu Ngài vi kì-têu hîn-hí lòi sṳ̀n-chiap Thai-ví ke vòng.10 Ngài ke phu̍k-ngìn Ngâ-kok â, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng;Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, m̀-sṳ́ chho̍k-kiâng.Ngài oi tui yén-yén ke thi-fông,ngì-têu pûn-ngìn chok-hi ke koet-kâ chiông ngì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn kiu chhut-lòi.Ngâ-kok oi chón-lòi ku-hiông,ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p, mò kiâng má-ke.11 Yîn-vi Ngài lâu ngì khiung-ha, oi chṳ́n-kiu ngì.Ngài oi chiông Ngài kiuk ngì hi ke ke-têu koet-kâ vàn-chhiòn sêu-me̍t;chúng-he Ngài m̀-voi sêu-me̍t ngì.Ngài oi yung kûng-chṳn kón-kau ngì,cho-m̀-tet m̀ chit-fa̍t ngì.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé."12 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Ngì ke su-sông mò phan-fap yî,sông-héu mò fap-thu yî-hó lé.13 Ngì ke on-é mò-ngìn voi thoi ngì chhut-thèu,chhiong mò yo̍k hó kò, mò yo̍k hó yî.14 Oi-siak ngì ke ngìn( 50 30:14 "Oi-siak ngì ke ngìn" chṳ́ "thùng-mèn-koet", chhâm-kháu 22:20, 22.) chhiòn-phu m̀ ki-tet ngì;kì-têu m̀-voi chai lòi koân-sîm ngì.Ngài oi chhiong kûng-kit thi̍t-ngìn án-ngiòng kûng-kit ngì,tông chhàn-ngiûn lòi chit-fa̍t ngì;yîn-vi ngì tông chit-ok, chhui tông chhûng.15 Cho-má-ke hàn vi ngì ke su-sông kín-kieu?Ngì ke sông-héu mò fap-thu yî-hó lé.Yîn-vi ngì tông chit-ok, chhui tông chhûng,Ngài chang án-ngiòng chit-fa̍t ngì.16 Chúng-he, thûn-sṳ̍t ngì ke, voi pûn-ngìn sṳ̍t-thet;ngì só-yû ke thi̍t-ngìn voi pûn-ngìn chok-chéu.Khî-fu ngì ke, voi pûn-ngìn khî-fu;chhióng ngì ke, voi pûn-ngìn chhióng.17 Sûi-yèn ngì ke thi̍t-ngìn kóng:"Siak-ôn he pûn-ngìn fit-thet ke, mò-ngìn koân-sîm ke";chúng-he Ngài oi yî ngì ke sông-héu,pûn ngì hó hí-lòi.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.18 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Khon â, Ngài oi fu̍k-hîn Ngâ-kok ke chong-phùng,ya oi lìn-mén kì ke kâ-yèn;Ngài oi sṳ́ kì chhùng-kien Yâ-lu-sat-lâng,chhai ngièn-lòi ke thi-fông hí vòng-kiûng.19 Kì-têu voi fat-chhut kám-chhia ke kô-sâng;kì-têu voi fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.Ngài oi sṳ́ kì-têu ke ngìn-khiéu chen-kâ, m̀-voi kám-séu.Ngài oi sṳ́ kì-têu yû chûn-yùng, m̀-voi chai su vú-yu̍k.20 Kì-têu ke chṳ́-sûn voi chhiong ló thèu-pái án mùng-ên,kì-têu ke koet-kâ thin-chho̍k voi chhai Ngài mien-chhièn khî hí-lòi;Ngài oi chit-fa̍t só-yû khî-fu kì-têu ke ngìn.21 Kì-têu chhṳ-kâ ngìn cho thúng-chhṳ-chá;kì-têu ke liâng-chhiu he pún-chhu̍k ngìn.Ngài ham kì to Ngài mien-chhièn lòi,kì chhiu lòi chhîn-khiun Ngài;mò su yêu-chhiáng ke,má-ngìn kám chhṳ-kâ lòi chiap-khiun Ngài nè?Kì-têu oi cho Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn;Ngài oi cho kì-têu ke Song-ti.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.22 併於上節23 Khon â, Song-chú ke thai fat-khién voi chhiong khòng-fûng fat-sên, chhiong liùng-kién-fûng án-ngiòng chhai fái-ngìn thèu-nà táng lit-lit-chón. Song-chú ke thai fat-khién m̀-voi sû chón-hi, chhṳ̍t-to Kì vàn-sṳ̀n chhṳ-kâ só-yû ke kie-va̍k. Ke-sṳ̀, ngì-têu chhiu voi mìn-pha̍k liá-sṳ.24 併於上節
