1 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn Chón Ku-hiông Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng: "To ke-sṳ̀, Ngài oi cho Yî-set-lie̍t tak-chhu̍k ke Song-ti; kì-têu oi cho Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn.2 Chhai khóng-yâ, Ngài lìn-mén ke-têu mò pûn tô-kiam chhṳ̀-sí ke ngìn. Chhai Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhin-sióng ôn-sit ke sṳ̀,3 Ngài tui yén-yén ke thi-fông( 51 31:3 "yén-yén ke thi-fông" ya-he yi̍t-cho "kú-sṳ̀".) hiong kì-têu( 52 31:3 "kì-têu" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "ngài".) hién-hien. Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, Ngài yún-yén thung-siak ngì-têu, Ngài hàn oi yung put-pien ke chhṳ̀-oi thung-siak ngì-têu.4 Ngài oi chhùng-sîn kien-li̍p ngì-têu. Ngì-têu voi chai nâ-hí lìn-kú, thai-sâng chhong-kô thiau-vú.5 Ngì-têu voi chhai Sat-mâ-li-â ke sân-táng chhùng-sîn chung phù-thò; chung ke ngìn voi hióng-su tó yèn-tú ke chhut-sán.6 Yû yit-ngit, sú-mong ke ngìn voi chhai Yî-fap-lièn sân-táng thai-sâng hêm: ‘Lòi ô! Ngài-têu to Siak-ôn hi chhèu-kien Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti.’ "7 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Ngì-têu oi vi Yî-set-lie̍t thai-sâng chhong-kô,vi chui ví-thai ke koet-kâ thai-sâng chhin-hêm,ngì-têu oi chhong siung-chan ke kô kóng:"Song-chú â, khiù Ngì chṳ́n-kiu Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn,chhiu-he Yî-set-lie̍t chhûn-tó ke ngìn-mìn."8 Khon â, Ngài oi tui pet-fông chiông kì-têu tai chón-lòi,tui thiên-ngài hói-kok chiông kì-têu chhi-si̍p hí-lòi.Mô-muk ke, pâi-kiok ke, yû sṳ̂n-hong ke, kiung se-ngìn-è ke,ya chhiòn-phu voi yê-kông tá-chhṳn khiung-ha chón-lòi.9 Khon â, Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn voi yit-lu kieu-tén chón-lòi;kì-têu voi thèn-tén Ngài hàng, theu-lu khì-tó.Ngài oi tai kì-têu kîn-ko hâi-súi piên,thu kì-têu hàng phiàng-phiàng ke chhṳ̍t-lu,sṳ́ kì-têu m̀-voi tiet-tó.Ngài he Yî-set-lie̍t ke â-pâ;Yî-fap-lièn he Ngài ke chóng-chṳ́.10 Song-chú kóng:Van-koet â, ngì-têu oi chu-yi thâng Ngài ke fa,ngì-têu oi chhai yén-yén ke hói-tó chhòn Ngài ke fa.Ngài kiuk-san Yî-set-lie̍t — Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn,chúng-he Ngài oi chai chhi-si̍p kì-têu;Ngài oi pó-fu kì-têu, chhiong mu̍k-ngìn pó-fu yòng-khiùn.11 Ngài oi kiu-su̍k Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi,tui khiòng-koet sú-chûng chiông kì-têu kiu chhut-lòi.12 Kì-têu voi chón-lòi, chhai Siak-ôn sân-táng fôn-hí chhong-kô;kì-têu voi mân-mien fôn-hí, hióng-su Ngài fûng-fu ke ên-fi:Chhiu-he ńg-kuk, sîn-chiú, kám-lám-yù, ngiù-yòng.Kì-têu voi chhiong súi-ngièn chhûng-chiuk ke thièn-yèn;m̀-voi chai sèu má-ke, khiam má-ke.13 Ke-sṳ̀, se-moi-é voi fôn-hí thiau-vú;ló-ngìn-kâ lâu heu-sâng-ngìn voi khiung-ha khoai-lo̍k.Ngài oi sṳ́ kì-têu ke pî-sông pien-cho fôn-hí,Ngài oi ôn-ví kì-têu, sṳ́ kì-têu ke yù-sèu pien-cho khoai-lo̍k.14 Ngài oi sṳ́ ke-têu chi-sṳ̂ yû fûng-chiuk ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t, sîm-mân yi-chiuk;Ngài oi sṳ́ Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn chhiòn mò khiam-khiet.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.15 Song-chú Lìn-mén Yî-set-lie̍t Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:Chhai Lâ-mâ yû thâng-tó ve-thiên ve-thi ke kieu-sâng;he Lùi-kiet vi kì ke chṳ́-ńg thai-kieu, m̀-hén chiap-su ôn-ví,yîn-vi kì-têu chhiòn-phu mò-thet-hi lé.16 Song-chú án-ngiòng kóng:M̀-hó kieu lé! Chhu̍t-châu muk-chṳp!Ngì ke sîn-khú voi tet-tó po-sóng;kì-têu voi tui thi̍t-ngìn ke koet-kâ chón-lòi.17 Ngì ke chhièn-thù chhûng-mân hî-mong;ngì ke chṳ́-ńg voi chón-lòi ku-hiông.Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.18 Ngài thâng-tó Yî-fap-lièn( 53 31:18 "Yî-fap-lièn" chṳ́-to pet-koet Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, 20 chiet khiung-yong.) pî-sông kóng:Song-chú â, ngài-têu chhiu-chhiong màng kâu-koâi ke se ngiù-kú,chúng-he Ngì hiun-lien ngài-têu, sṳ́ ngài-têu sun-fu̍k.Khiù Ngì tai ngài-têu chón-lòi, ngài-têu oi kûi-hiong Ngì,yîn-vi Ngì he Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti.19 Sûi-yèn ngài-têu phoi-ngia̍k lì-khôi Ngì,ngài-têu chit-sṳ̀ chhiu sióng-oi fì-thèu.Ngì ke kón-kau sṳ́ ngài-têu heu-fí, pî-sông to chhùi sîm-kôn;ngài-têu yîn-vi heu-sâng sṳ̀ ke chhui kám-kok chhin kien-seu, su-tó vú-yu̍k.20 Song-chú kóng:Yî-fap-lièn â, Ngì he Ngài chui-siak ke lai-é,he Ngài ke sîm-kôn pó-pi.Ngài tak-pái chit-phi ngì,hàn-he chhin ku-ngiam ngì( 54 31:20 "Ngài tak-pái ... ku-ngiam ngì" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Tak-pái thì-hí ngì ke miàng, Ngài ke sîm chhiu koa-tiau tén ngì".).Ngài siak ngì siak ngi̍p-kut;Ngài yit-thin voi lìn-mén ngì.21 Ngì oi chhap-hó chṳ́-lu-phêu, tun-hó lu-phài;ngì oi chhìm ngì hàng-ko ke ke-thiàu thai-lu.Ngài só siak ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn â, hó chón-lòi lé!Chón-to ngì-têu ke ku-hiông!22 Phoi-ngia̍k ke ngìn â, ngì lit hi lit chón oi to kí-sṳ̀ nè?Ngài chhai thi-song hàng yit-hong sîn ke sṳ:Fu-ńg oi pó-fu nàm-ngìn.23 Song-ti Chṳ́-mìn ke Kông-mìn Chhièn-thù Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi, Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti án-ngiòng kóng: "Ngài pûn chok-lòi ke chṳ́-mìn chón-to ku-hiông ke sṳ̀, kì-têu voi chhai Yù-thai koet-nui tak-chak sàng-sṳ chai kóng:‘Kûng-ngi ke chhu-só, Siak-ôn sṳn-sân â,ngien Song-chú su-fuk pûn ngì.’24 Yù-thai ke thú-thi lâu koet-nui tak-chak sàng-sṳ chiông-lòi voi yû chhin-tô ke nùng-mìn, lâu tai-tén yòng-khiùn ke mu̍k-yòng-ngìn khiung-ha he̍t chhai ke-vi.25 Ngài oi sṳ́ thiám ke ngìn chîn-sṳ̀n fî-fu̍k, sṳ́ yù-mun ke ngìn sîm-mân yi-chiuk.26 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng ngìn-mìn voi kóng( 55 31:26 "Yîn-vi ... voi kóng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Siên-tî kóng".): ‘Ngài soi-siáng ke sṳ̀, kám-kok soi-to chhin sûng-sóng.’27 "Ngài — Song-chú kóng, ngit-è voi-to, Ngài oi sṳ́ Yî-set-lie̍t lâu Yù-thai ke ngìn-khiéu chen-kâ, thèu-sâng hîn-vong.28 Ngài thèu-pái ngióng-pân yung-sîm chiông kì-têu pâng-thet, chhak-thet, thûi-fân, pho-fái, pin kong-châi; Ngài ya oi ngióng-pân yung-sîm châi-phì kì-têu, kien-li̍p kì-têu.29 Ke-sṳ̀, ngìn m̀-voi chai kóng:‘Yà-ôi sṳ̍t sôn phù-thò,sa sôn-tó chṳ́-ńg ke ngà-chhṳ́.’30 Fán-chón, má-ngìn sṳ̍t sôn phù-thò, chhiu sôn-tó má-ngìn ke ngà-chhṳ́; tak-sà oi yîn-vi chhṳ-kâ ke chhui lòi-sí."31 Song-chú kóng: "Ngit-è voi-to, Ngài oi lâu Yî-set-lie̍t pin Yù-thai ke ngìn-mìn li̍p sîn ke yok.32 M̀-chhiong Ngài khiên-tén kì-têu chú-siên ke sú tai-liâng kì-têu chhut Âi-khi̍p ke sṳ̀, lâu kì-têu só-li̍p ke yok. Chhai ke khie-yok chûng, Ngài he kì-têu ke ló-kûng, chúng-he kì-têu vì-poi ke-yok.33 To ke-ngit yî-heu, Ngài oi lâu Yî-set-lie̍t ngìn-mìn li̍p ke sîn-yok he án-ngiòng: Ngài oi chiông Ngài ke fap-li̍t piong-ngi̍p kì-têu ke sîm-tú, siá chhai kì-têu ke sîm-song. Ngài oi cho kì-têu ke Song-ti, kì-têu oi cho Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn.34 Kì-têu m̀-voi chai kâu lìn-sa lâu chhṳ-kâ ke hiûng-thi kóng: ‘Oi ngin-sṳt Song-chú’, yîn-vi tui chui-se ke to chui-thai ke, kì-têu lûng-chúng voi ngin-sṳt Ngài. Ngài oi sa-miên kì-têu ke chhui, m̀-voi chai ki-tet kì-têu ke ko-fam. Ngài — Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé."35 Song-chú chṳ́-thin ngit-thèu chhai ngit-sṳ̀-thèu fat-kông,phai ngie̍t-kông sên-é cho am-pû-thèu ke kông.Kì kiâu-thûng thai-hói, sṳ́ hói-long ho-ho-kún.Kì ke miàng he Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi.36 Kì kóng: Na-he liá-têu thin-li̍t chhai Ngài mien-chhièn fi-thet,Yî-set-lie̍t ke heu-thoi ya chhiu chhai Ngài mien-chhiènyún-yén m̀-voi sàng-cho koet-kâ.37 Song-chú yu án-ngiòng kóng:Na-he ngìn yû phan-fap liòng-chhut thiên yû kí-kô,chhet-chhut thi ke kîn-kî yû kí-chhṳ̂m,Ngài chang voi yîn-vi Yî-set-lie̍t ke heu-thoiyit-chhiet só-hàng ke lòi vok-thet kì-têu.38 Song-chú kóng: "Khon â, ngit-è voi-to, kûi-chho Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-sàng, oi vi Ngài chhùng-sîn kien-chho, tui Ha̍p-nàm-ngia̍p-lèu to Kok-mùn.39 Sàng ke sî-phién hàn-oi hiong-ngoi liòng-to Kâ-li̍p-sân, yu lit-to Kô-â.40 Kûi-chak tôi-tén sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ lâu chhṳ̀n-fôi ke sân-lak, hàn-yû Khi̍p-lùn-hâi to tûng-phién Mâ-mùn chón-kok yit-chhiet ke fông-thi, chhiòn-phu oi fûn-phe̍t cho-sṳn, kûi-ngi̍p Song-chú ke sṳn-sàng. Liá-sàng chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi pâng-thet, yún-yén m̀-voi vang-thet."
