1 M̀-hó lâu Sṳ-kie Cho Phèn-yû Ngì-têu chûng-kiên ke chhûng-thu̍t lâu yên-kâ he tui nai-vi lòi ke nè? He tui ngì-têu tî-poi siông-châng ke yu̍k-mong lòi ke!2 Ngì-têu thâm-thù, tet m̀-tó chhiu sióng-oi chhṳ̀ ngìn; ngì-têu chhit-tu, tet m̀-tó chhiu chhûng-thu̍t lâu yên-kâ. Ngì-têu tet m̀-tó, yîn-vi ngì-têu mò khiù Song-ti.3 Ngì-têu khiù ya tet m̀-tó, yîn-vi ngì-têu ke thung-kî m̀-chho̍k; ngì-têu só-khiù ke he sióng-oi long-fi chhai yen-lo̍k chûng.4 Ngì liá-têu mò chûng-sîm ke ngìn â! Kám-kóng ngì-têu m̀-tî thùng sṳ-kie cho phèn-yû chhiu-he thùng Song-ti cho tui-thi̍t mâ? Má-ngìn sióng-oi thùng sṳ-kie cho phèn-yû, chhiu-he cho Song-ti ke thi̍t-ngìn!5 Sṳn-kîn kóng: "Song-ti piong chhai ngài-têu tî-poi ke lìn yû khi̍t khiòng ke yu̍k-mong( 3 4:5 "Song-ti ... yu̍k-mong" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Song-ti tui Kì piong chhai ngài-têu tî-poi ke lìn siak-to ngi̍p-kut" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Song-ti piong chhai ngài-têu tî-poi ke Sṳn-Lìn fán-tui yu̍k-mong".)." Ngì-têu láu-tó liá-ki fa mò má-ke yi-sṳ mâ?6 Chúng-he, Song-ti só su ke ên-tién hàn-kha khiòng, chhiong Sṳn-kîn só kóng:Song-ti chú-tóng kiêu-ngau ke ngìn,su ên-tién pûn khiâm-pî ke ngìn.7 Só-yî, ngì-têu oi sun-chhiùng Song-ti. Oi tí-khong Mô-kúi, Mô-kúi chhiu voi sám-chéu.8 Ngì-têu chhîn-khiun Song-ti, Song-ti chhiu voi chhîn-khiun ngì-têu. Yû-chhui ke ngìn â, oi kiet-chhiang ngì-têu ke sú! Sâm-sîm lióng-yi ke ngìn â, oi kiet-chhiang ngì-têu ke sîm!9 Ngì-têu oi sông-sîm, pî-ôi, liù muk-chṳp; oi chiông seu-sâng pien-cho ôi-kieu, chiông fôn-hí pien-cho pî-sông!10 Oi chhai Chú ke mien-chhièn khiâm-pî, Kì chhiu voi kô-sṳ̂n ngì-têu. Mo̍k Phî-phìn Hiûng-thi Chí-moi11 Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, m̀-hó fu-siông phî-phìn. Phî-phìn ya-he lun-ton hiûng-thi chí-moi ke, chhiu-he phî-phìn, lun-ton fap-li̍t. Ngì na-he lun-ton fap-li̍t, ngì chhiu m̀-he hàng fap-li̍t ke ngìn, he cho sṳ́m-phan-chá.12 Song-ti he vì-yit ke li̍p-fap-chá lâu sṳ́m-phan-chá; chṳ́-yû Kì chang nèn chṳ́n-kiu, nèn fí-me̍t. Ngì he má-ngìn, ngióng-kám lun-ton phe̍t-ngìn â! Mo̍k Chhṳ-khoâ13 Oi se-ngi ô! Ngì-têu yû-ngìn kóng: "Kîm-pû-ngit ya-he thiên-kông-ngit, ngài-têu lòi-hi mêu-mêu sàng, chhai ke-vi he̍t yit ngièn, cho sên-lî chhon thai-chhièn."14 Sṳ-sṳ̍t-song, ngì-têu lièn thiên-kông-ngit voi ngióng-pân tû m̀ tî! Ngì-têu ke sâng-miang chhiong má-ke nè? He chhiong mùng-yên yit-yong, chhut-hien yit-ha-é chhiu m̀-kien-thet.15 Ngì-têu yin-tông án-ngiòng kóng: "Chú na hén, ngài-têu chhiu nèn sâng hâ-hi, cho liá-yong sṳ, cho ke-yong sṳ."16 Liá-ha, ngì-têu kin-yèn nâ phong-fûng ke sṳ lòi khoâ-khiéu; liá-chúng khoâ-khiéu he sià-ok ke.17 Só-yî, ngìn na tî san, m̀ hi hàng, chhiu-he kì ke chhui.
