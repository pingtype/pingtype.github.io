1 Sên-fa̍t chhai Kông-mìn chûng Ki-yèn ngì-têu he Song-ti só-siak ke chṳ́-ńg, ngì-têu chhiu yin-kôi háu-fap Song-ti.2 Ngì-têu ke hàng-sṳ cho-ngìn oi phìn oi-sîm, chhiong Kî-tuk siak ngài-têu, vi ngài-têu hî-sên sâng-miang, cho hiông-hiông ke kiûng-vu̍t lâu chi-vu̍t hien-pûn Song-ti yit-yong.3 Ke-têu kiên-yìm, ô-chô, ya-he thâm-sîm ke sṳ-chhìn, chhai ngì-têu chûng-kiên lièn thì-hí ya m̀-hó, yîn-vi ngì-têu he Song-ti ke sṳn-mìn.4 Ngì-têu ya m̀-hó kóng yìm-lon, hâm-chhê, ya-he ha-liù ke fa; tiên-to, ngì-têu oi kóng kám-chhia Song-ti ke fa.5 Yîn-vi ngì-têu khok-sṳ̍t tî, fam kiên-yìm, ô-chô, ya-he thâm-sîm ke ngìn (thâm-sîm lâu pai ngiéu-siong khiung-yong) chhie̍t-tui put-nèn liâng-su Kî-tuk lâu Song-ti koet tî-poi ke fuk-fun.6 M̀-hó pûn má-ngìn yung hî-ká ke fa phien-chéu; yîn-vi liá-têu sṳ, Song-ti voi tui ke-têu phoi-ngia̍k ke ngìn thai fat-khién.7 Só-yî, m̀-hó thùng liá-chúng ngìn kiet-kâu.8 Thèu-pái ngì-têu chhai vû-am chûng, chúng-he chhṳ-chhiùng sàng-cho Chú ke sin-thù, ngì-têu chhiu chhai kông-mìn chûng; án-ngiòng, ngì-têu ke sên-fa̍t yin-tông oi chhiong kông-mìn ke chṳ́-ńg.9 Kông-mìn só kiet fûng-sṳn ke kó-chṳ́ chhiu-he yit-chhiet ke liòng-san, kûng-ngi, lâu chṳ̂n-lî.10 Ngì-têu oi chhà-kháu má-ke he Chú só fôn-hí ke sṳ.11 M̀-hó cho phe̍t-ngìn chhai am-chûng só cho ke-têu mò li-yit ke sṳ-chhìn, fán-chón oi chiông ke-têu sṳ-chhìn hiên-chhut-lòi.12 (Kì-têu chhai am-chûng só-cho ke sṳ, lièn kóng-hí ya he chhin kien-seu!)13 Yit-chhiet sṳ-chhìn chhai kông-mìn chûng yit kûng-khôi, chṳ̂n-siong chhiu hién-mìn chhut-lòi;14 yîn-vi sṳ́ yit-chhiet hién-mìn chhut-lòi ke chhiu-he kông. Só-yî, sṳ̂-ngìn án-ngiòng kóng:Soi-muk ke ngìn â, hó siáng lé!Tui sí-ngìn chûng hong-hí-lòi!Kî-tuk chhiu oi kông-cheu ngì.15 Só-yî, ngì-têu ke hàng-vì oi se-ngi. M̀-hó chhiong mò chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn, oi chhiong yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn.16 Oi pá-ak kî-fi, yîn-vi liá-ha ke sṳ-thoi sià-ok.17 Só-yî, ngì-têu m̀-hó ngok-ngok tok-tok ko ngit-è; oi mìn-pha̍k Chú ke chṳ́-yi he má-ke.18 M̀-hó sṳ̍t-to chiú-chui, lîm thet-tô chiú voi hoi ngì-têu fong-thong; tiên-to, ngì-têu oi pûn Sṳn-Lìn chhûng-mân.19 Ngì-têu oi yung sṳ̂-phiên, sṳn-sṳ̂, lâu lìn-kô lòi tui-chhong, khiéu chhong sîm fò lòi chan-mî Chú.20 Fàm-sṳ oi fung ngài-têu Chú Yâ-sû Kî-tuk ke miàng, sòng-sòng kám-chhia Â-pâ Song-ti. Fû-chhî ke Koân-he21 Oi yîn-vi kin-vi Kî-tuk lòi pí-chhṳ́ sun-fu̍k.22 Cho pû-ngiòng ke, ngì-têu oi sun-chhiùng chhṳ-kâ ke ló-kûng, chhiong sun-fu̍k Chú yit-yong.23 Yîn-vi ló-kûng he pû-ngiòng ke thèu, chhiu-chhiong Kî-tuk he kau-fi ke thèu, ya he kau-fi chhiòn-thí ke Kiu-chú.24 Chhiong kau-fi sun-fu̍k Kî-tuk, cho pû-ngiòng ke ya yin-tông fàm-sṳ sun-chhiùng ló-kûng.25 Cho ló-kûng ke, ngì-têu oi siak chhṳ-kâ ke pû-ngiòng, chhiong Kî-tuk siak kau-fi, vi kau-fi hî-sên sâng-miang yit-yong.26 Kì án-ngiòng cho he oi thûng-ko chṳ̂n-tho lâu súi lòi kiet-chhiang kau-fi, pûn kì sàng-cho sṳn-kiet,27 hó hien-pûn chhṳ-kâ, cho yùng-kông ke kau-fi, chhiòn mò khiet-tiám, mò lá-sap, mò tá-chap, chhiu-he sùn-kiet vàn-chhiòn ke kau-fi.28 Khiung-yong, cho ló-kûng ke yin-tông siak chhṳ-kâ ke pû-ngiòng, chhiong siak chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̂n-thí yit-yong; siak pû-ngiòng chhiu-he siak chhṳ-kâ.29 Mò ngìn yen-hen chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̂n-thí, chúng-he pó-yông lâu cheu-ku, chhiong Kî-tuk tui-thai kau-fi yit-yong;30 yîn-vi ngài-têu he kì sṳ̂n-song ke kî-thí.31 Sṳn-kîn kóng: "Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngìn oi lì-khôi yà-ôi, lâu pû-ngiòng kiet-ha̍p, lióng-sà sàng-cho yit-thí."32 Liá-ke kîn-chiet khí-sṳ khi̍t-thai ke o-pi. Ngài tî kì he chṳ́ Kî-tuk lâu kau-fi ke koân-he lòi kóng ke,33 chúng-he ya cho-tet yung chhai ngì-têu chûng-kiên: Cho ló-kûng ke yin-tông siak pû-ngiòng, chhiong siak chhṳ-kâ; cho pû-ngiòng ke ya yin-tông kin-chhung ló-kûng.
