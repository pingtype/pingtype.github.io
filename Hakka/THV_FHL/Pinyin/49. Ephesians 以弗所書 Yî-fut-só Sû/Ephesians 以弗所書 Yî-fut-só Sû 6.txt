1 Yà-ôi lâu Chṳ́-ńg ke Koân-he Cho chṳ́-ńg ke, ngì-têu oi chhai Chú ke tî-poi thâng-chhiùng yà-ôi; liá he lî só tông-yèn ke.2 "Oi hau-kin yà-ôi, ngì chhiu voi sṳ-sṳ sun-li, chhai sṳ-kiên hióng-su chhòng-su!" Liá he thi-yit-thiàu tai yin-hí ke kie-min.3 併於上節4 Cho â-pâ ke, m̀-hó ngiâ ngì-têu ke chṳ́-ńg fat-khién; oi yung Chú ke kau-hiun lâu kín-kie lòi yông-yuk kì-têu. Chú-phu̍k ke Koân-he5 Cho nù-phu̍k ke, ngì-têu oi yung kin-vi ke sîm, kiâng-kiâng lu̍k-lu̍k, sṳ̀n-sṳ̀n sṳ̍t-sṳ̍t lòi fu̍k-chhiùng sṳ-kiên ke chú-ngìn, chhiong sṳ-fung Kî-tuk yit-yong;6 m̀-hó tân-chhiang chhai ngìn khon-tén ngì ke sṳ̀ chang án-ngiòng cho, chhiong he oi pûn ngìn fôn-hí thin-thin; ngì-têu oi chhiong he cho Kî-tuk ke phu̍k-ngìn, sṳ̀n-sîm chûn-hàng Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi yit-yong.7 Ngì-têu oi kâm-sîm lo̍k-yi chhin cho nù-phu̍k ke pún-fun, chhiong he fu̍k-sṳ Chú, m̀-he fu̍k-sṳ ngìn.8 Oi tî, mò-lun he nù-phu̍k ya-he chhṳ-yù-ngìn, Chú oi cheu tak-sà ke hó hàng-vì lòi po-tap kì.9 Cho chú-ngìn ke, ngì-têu ya oi hó-hó tui-thai nù-phu̍k, m̀-hó vî-hia̍p kì-têu. Oi tî, ngì-têu lâu ngì-têu ke nù-phu̍k thùng-su̍k yit-vi chhai thiên-táng ke Chú; Kì yung khiung-yong ke phêu-chún tui-thai mî-yit-ke ngìn. Song-ti só Su ke Chhiòn-fu Kiûn-chông10 Chui-heu, ngì-têu oi yí-kho Chú lâu kì ke thai-nèn thai-li̍t cho kiên-khiòng ke ngìn.11 Ngì-têu oi chok Song-ti só su ke chhiòn-fu kiûn-chông, pûn ngì-têu khî tet-he̍t, lòi tí-tóng Mô-kúi ke kúi-kie.12 Yîn-vi ngài-têu m̀-he thùng yû-hiet yû-ngiuk ke ngìn tui-khong; he thùng thiên-kie ke sià-lìn, chhiu-he liá vû-am sṳ-thoi ke chṳp-chṳn-chá, chóng-khièn-chá, lâu yí-chhiu-kiên sià-ok ke sṳ-li̍t chan-chên.13 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngì-têu oi yung Song-ti só su ke chhiòn-fu kiûn-chông lòi chông-phi chhṳ-kâ, hó chhai sià-ok ke ngit-è tí-tóng thi̍t-ngìn ke kûng-kit, piang to-tái, khî-chhai-chhai.14 Só-yî, ngì-têu oi khî-vún. Oi yung chṳ̂n-lî cho yêu-tai kiê-yêu, yung chṳn-ngi cho fu-sîm-kiang châ-hiûng,15 sùi-sṳ̀ chún-phi siên-yòng fò-phìn ke fuk-yîm, chhiong chok-tén hài yit-yong.16 Oi sòng-sòng khià-tén sin-sîm ke tún-phài, lòi tí-tóng ke sià-ok-chá sa-ko-lòi yit-chhiet ke fó-chien.17 Oi yung kiu-ên cho thèu-khûi, yung Song-ti ke fa cho Sṳn-Lìn só-su ke pó-kiam.18 Ngì-têu oi sun-chhiùng Sṳn-Lìn ke tai-liâng sùi-sṳ̀ khì-tó, khiù Song-ti pông-chhu ngì-têu; oi kín-siáng khì-tó, m̀-hó thìn. Ngì-têu oi sòng-sòng vi chung sṳn-thù khì-tó;19 ya oi vi ngài khì-tó, khiù Song-ti su ngài chṳ̂n-tho, pûn ngài hó-tám khôi-choi kóng-mìn fuk-yîm ke o-pi.20 Vi-tó cho fuk-yîm ke thai-sṳ́, ngài sa pûn-ngìn yung só-lien thò-tén. Ngì-têu oi khiù Chú su ngài yúng-hi, thai-tám kóng yin-tông kóng ke fa. Mun-heu lâu Chuk-fuk21 Ngài-têu só chhîn-oi, chûng-sîm sṳ-fung Chú ke hiûng-thi Thûi-kî-kú, kì voi chiông yit-chhiet koân-he ngài ke sṳ-chhìn lâu ngì-têu kóng, pûn ngì-têu tî ngài ke chhìn-hìn.22 Ngài thi̍t-phe̍t phai kì hi ngì-têu ke-vi, he oi po ngì-têu tî ngài-têu liá-vi ke chhìn-hìn, pûn ngì-têu tet-tó kú-li.23 Ngien Â-pâ Song-ti lâu Chú Yâ-sû Kî-tuk su phìn-ôn, yìn-oi, lâu sin-sîm pûn ngì-têu kok-vi hiûng-thi chí-moi!24 Ngien Song-ti ke ên-tién yún-yén lâu só-yû kin-oi ngài-têu Chú Yâ-sû Kî-tuk ke ngìn thùng-chhai!( 6 6:24 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Ngien Song-ti su ên-tién pûn só-yû yung yún-yén ke oi lòi kin-oi ngài-têu ke Chú Yâ-sû Kî-tuk ke ngìn!")
