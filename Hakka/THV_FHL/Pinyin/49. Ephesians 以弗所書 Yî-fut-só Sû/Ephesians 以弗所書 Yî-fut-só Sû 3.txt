1 Pó-lò Vi Ngoi-pâng-ngìn Kûng-chok Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngài — Pó-lò vi-tó ngì-têu Ngoi-pâng-ngìn ke yèn-ku cho Kî-tuk Yâ-sû ke siù-fam, vi ngì-têu khiù Song-ti.2 Ngì-têu yit-thin yû thâng-ko, vi-tó ngì-têu ke hó-chhu, Song-ti sṳ̂-ên chiông yit-hong chṳt-vu kâu-thok pûn ngài.3 Kì khí-sṳ ngài, pûn ngài tî Kì ke o-pi. (Ngài yí-kîn chiông liá o-pi kién-lio̍k siá hâ-lòi;4 ngì-têu thu̍k-liáu chhiu voi liáu-kié, ngài chhṳ̂m tî Kî-tuk ke o-pi.)5 Chhai yî-chhièn ke sṳ-thoi, Song-ti m̀-sṳt pûn má-ngìn tî liá-ke o-pi; chúng-he liá-ha, Kì yí-kîn thûng-ko Sṳn-Lìn khí-sṳ Kì ke sṳn sṳ́-thù lâu siên-tî lé.6 Liá o-pi ke nui-yùng he án-ngiòng: Thûng-ko fuk-yîm, Ngoi-pâng-ngìn oi thùng Yù-thai-ngìn khiung-ha fûn-hióng Song-ti ke chuk-fuk, thùng-su̍k yit-thí, ya thùng-hióng Song-ti chhai Kî-tuk Yâ-sû tî-poi só sṳ̍t-hien ke yin-hí.7 Song-ti sat-li̍p ngài chṳp-hàng liá chhòn fuk-yîm ke chṳt-vu; liá he cheu Kì só yun-hàng ke khièn-nèn su-pûn ngài ke thi̍t-phe̍t ên-su.8 Sûi-yèn ngài he só-yû sṳn-thù chûng chui mì-se ke, Song-ti hàn-he su ngài thi̍t-khièn, chiông fuk-yîm, chhiu-he Kî-tuk chhet m̀-theu ke fûng-sṳn, chhòn-pûn Ngoi-pâng-ngìn,9 pûn chhiòn ngìn-lui tî Song-ti ngióng-pân sṳ̍t-hàng Kì o-pi ke kie-va̍k. Chhai siên-chhièn ke sṳ-thoi, chhóng-chho van-vu̍t ke Song-ti chiông liá o-pi khong-tén,10 vi-tó oi chhai liá-ha ke sṳ-thoi, thûng-ko kau-fi, pûn thiên-kie ke chṳp-chṳn-chá lâu chóng-khièn-chá lûng-chúng voi ngin-sṳt Song-ti vù-khiùng ke chṳ-fi.11 Song-ti án-ngiòng cho he cheu Kì van-sṳ put-pien ke chṳ́-yi; liá chṳ́-yi yí-kîn chhai ngài-têu Chú Kî-tuk Yâ-sû ke sṳ̂n-song sṳ̍t-hien lé.12 Thûng-ko sin Kî-tuk( 5 3:12 "Thûng-ko sin Kî-tuk" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Thûng-ko Kî-tuk ke sin-sṳ̍t".), ngài-têu thùng kì kiet-lièn, chhiu cho-tet hó-tám lòi-to Song-ti ke mien-chhièn, chhiòn m̀-sṳ́ kiâng.13 Só-yî, chhiáng ngì-têu m̀-hó yîn-vi ngài vi ngì-têu su-khú chhiu fôi-sîm, fán-chón oi khon-cho he ngì-têu ke kông-yùng. Kî-tuk ke Thung-siak14 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngài chhai Â-pâ mien-chhièn khúi hâ-lòi khì-tó.15 Thiên-táng lâu thi-song só-yû ke kâ-chhu̍k chhiòn-phu he Kì ôn-miàng ke.16 Ngài khiù Song-ti, cheu Kì yùng-yeu ke fûng-sṳn, thûng-ko Sṳn-Lìn su ngì-têu li̍t-liong, pûn ngì-têu nui-chhai ke sâng-miang yúng-khien hí-lòi,17 sṳ́ Kî-tuk thûng-ko ngì-têu ke sin-sîm, he̍t chhai ngì-têu ke sîm-chûng. Ngài ya khì-khiù Song-ti, pûn ngì-têu chhai kì ke thung-siak chûng yû kîn ya yû kî,18 hó pûn ngì-têu thùng chung sṳn-thù khiung-ha thí-ngiam Kî-tuk ke thung-siak he ngióng-pân ke chhòng, fat, kô, chhṳ̂m.19 Ngien ngì-têu chṳn-kîn thí-ngiam-tó Kî-tuk ke chhêu-ko chṳ-sṳt só nèn liáu-kié ke thung-siak, hó sṳ́ ngì-têu chhûng-mân Song-ti yit-chhiet ke fûng-sṳn.20 Song-ti nèn yung yun-hàng chhai ngài-têu tông-chûng ke nèn-li̍t sṳ̀n-chhiu yit-chhiet, yén-yén chhêu-ko ngài-têu só khiù só sióng ke.21 Ngien Kì chhai kau-fi chûng pin chhai Kî-tuk Yâ-sû tî-poi tet-tó yùng-yeu, chhṳ̍t-to sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi, yún-yún yén-yén! Â-mén.
