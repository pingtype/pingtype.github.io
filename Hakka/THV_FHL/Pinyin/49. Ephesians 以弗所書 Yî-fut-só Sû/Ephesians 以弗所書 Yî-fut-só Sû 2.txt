1 Chhut-sí Ngi̍p-sên Thèu-pái, yîn-vi ngì-têu fam-chhui, phoi-ngia̍k Song-ti ke min-lin, ngì-têu tui lìn-sin lòi kóng he sí ke.2 Ke-sṳ̀, ngì-têu thèn liá sṳ-kiên sià-ok ke fûng-siu̍k, sun-chhiùng thiên-kie ke chóng-khièn-chá, chhiu-he liá-ha hot-chṳ ke-têu phoi-ngia̍k Song-ti min-lin ke ngìn ke sià-lìn.3 Khì-sṳ̍t, thèu-pái ngài-têu ya thùng kì-têu khiung-yong, fong-chiúng ngiuk-thí ke chhìn-yu̍k, thèn chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̂-yu̍k lâu yi-ngiam hi hàng. Só-yî, ngài-têu thùng phe̍t-ngìn khiung-yong, chu-thin oi su Song-ti ke chit-fa̍t.4 Chúng-he Song-ti yû fûng-sṳn ke lìn-mén; Kì tui ngài-têu ke thung-siak vù-khiùng vù-chhin.5 Ngài-têu ke lìn-sin chhai phoi-ngia̍k ke chhui-ok chûng sí-thet ke sṳ̀, Kì pûn ngài-têu thùng Kî-tuk khiung-ha sâng-ko-lòi; ngì-têu ke tet-kiu he yí-kho Song-ti ke ên-tién.6 Song-ti yu pûn ngài-têu chhai Kî-tuk Yâ-sû ke sâng-miang chûng thùng Kî-tuk khiung-ha fu̍k-fa̍t, thùng kì khiung-ha chhai thiên-táng chóng-khièn.7 Song-ti án-ngiòng cho he oi tui sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi péu-mìn Kì khi̍t fûng-fu ke ên-tién, chhiu-he Kì chhai Kî-tuk Yâ-sû tî-poi tui ngài-têu hién-hien chhut-lòi ke chhṳ̀-oi.8 Ngì-têu ke tet-kiu he kho Song-ti ke ên-tién, phìn sin-sîm lòi tet-tó ke; m̀-he yîn-vi ngì-têu ke hàng-vì, he Song-ti su-pûn ngì-têu ke.9 Ki-yèn m̀-he kho hàng-vì, ngì-têu chhiu mò má-ke hó khoâ-khiéu ke.10 Ngài-têu he Song-ti ke chok-phín, chhai Kî-tuk Yâ-sû tî-poi chho-sṳ̀n ke, muk-tit he oi ngài-têu hàng-san; liá he Song-ti chó chhiu kie-va̍k oi ngài-têu hi-cho ke. Chhai Kî-tuk tî-poi ke Ha̍p-yit11 Ngì-têu chhut-se chhiu-he Ngoi-pâng-ngìn ke, oi ki-tet yî-chhièn ke chhìn-hìn: Ngì-têu pûn chhṳ-chhṳ̂n su-ko kot-lî ke ngìn — chhiu-he ke-têu yung ngìn ke sú chhai chhṳ-kâ ngiuk-sṳ̂n hàng kot-lî ke Yù-thai-ngìn — chhṳ̂n-cho mò su kot-lî ke.12 Ke-sṳ̀, ngì-têu thùng Kî-tuk mò koân-he, he ngoi-ngìn, m̀-he Song-ti ke sién-mìn. Song-ti tui Kì ke chṳ́-mìn só yin-hí ke khie-yok, ngì-têu mò fun; ngì-têu chhai sṳ-kiên mò hî-mong, ya mò Song-ti.13 Ngì-têu thèu-pái he lì-khôi Song-ti yén-yén ke ngìn, chúng-he liá-ha thùng Kî-tuk Yâ-sû kiet-lièn khiung-ha, yu yîn-vi Kî-tuk liù-hiet lòi sí, yí-kîn chhîn-khiun Song-ti lé.14 Kî-tuk chhîn-chhṳ chiông fò-phìn su-pûn ngài-têu( 3 2:14 "chiông fò-phìn su-pûn ngài-têu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "he ngài-têu ke fò-phìn".); kì sṳ́ Yù-thai-ngìn lâu Ngoi-pâng-ngìn ha̍p-yit, yung chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̂n-thí chhak-thet ke hoi kì-têu yên-kâ fûn-lie̍t ke chhiòng.15 Kì fi-chhù Yù-thai fap-li̍t ke kie-min lâu kau-thiàu, muk-tit he oi pûn liá lióng-chúng ngìn thûng-ko kì ke sâng-miang sàng-cho yit-chúng sîn-ngìn, lòi sṳ̀n-chhiu fò-phìn.16 Thûng-ko chhai sṳ̍p-sṳ-ka-táng ke sí, Kî-tuk chhù-thet yên-sù; thûng-ko sṳ̍p-sṳ-ka, kì pûn lióng-phién ke ngìn sàng-cho yit-thí, lâu Song-ti fò-hó.17 Án-ngiòng, Kî-tuk lòi chhòn fò-phìn ke fuk-yîm pûn só-yû ke ngìn, pâu-hàm ngì liá-têu thèu-pái lì-khôi Song-ti yén-yén ke Ngoi-pâng-ngìn lâu chhîn-khiun Song-ti ke Yù-thai-ngìn.18 Yîn-vi ngài-têu mò-lun he Yù-thai-ngìn ya-he Ngoi-pâng-ngìn, lûng-chúng he thûng-ko Kî-tuk, su khiung yit-vi Sṳn-Lìn ke kám-thung, lòi-to Â-pâ Song-ti ke mien-chhièn.19 Só-yî, ngì-têu Ngoi-pâng-ngìn mò chai he ngoi-ngìn, ya m̀-he sâng-fun-ngìn; ngì-têu he Song-ti chṳ́-mìn ke thùng-pâu, he Song-ti yit-kâ ke ngìn.20 Ngì-têu thùng Yù-thai-ngìn khiung-yong, ya he hí chhai sṳ́-thù lâu siên-tî ke thi-kî táng-kô, Kî-tuk Yâ-sû chhṳ-kâ he vuk-tung lièn-kiet ke thai-liòng( 4 2:20 "vuk-tung lièn-kiet ke thai-liòng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "kî-sa̍k" ya-he "vuk-tung ke kiúng-sîm-sa̍k".).21 Yí-kho kì, kûi-chho kien-chuk chhiu fu-siông kiet-lièn, chhiam-chhiam khóng-thai, sàng-cho Chú ke sṳn-thien.22 Yí-kho kì, ngì-têu thai-kâ ya khiung-ha pûn kì kien-chho, sàng-cho Song-ti thûng-ko Sṳn-Lìn he̍t ke só-chhai.
