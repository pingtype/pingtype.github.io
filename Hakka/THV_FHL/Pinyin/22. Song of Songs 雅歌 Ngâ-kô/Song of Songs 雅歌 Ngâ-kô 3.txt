1 Ngài am-pû-thèu soi chhai mìn-chhòng táng,chhìm ngài ke oi-ngìn;ngài chhìm kì, chúng-he chhìm m̀-tó.2 Ngài sióng: "Ngài oi hong-hí-lòi hàng-theu ke-sàng,chhôn-ko thai-kiê séu-hong,hi chhìm ngài ke oi-ngìn."Ngài chhìm kì, chúng-he chhìm m̀-tó.3 Sàng-tú ke-têu sùn-lò chóng-kâng ke ngìn tù-tó ngài,ngài chhiu mun: "Ngì-têu yû khon-tó ngài ke oi-ngìn mò?"4 Ngài yit lì-khôi kì-têu, chhiu chhìm-tó kì.Ngài chiông kì lâi-tén, m̀-hén piong kì chéu,yit-chhṳ̍t tai kì ngi̍p ngâ-mê ke vuk-hâ,chui-mî ngi̍p-to ngài chhut-se ke fòng-kiên.5 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke se-moi-é â, chhiáng tap-yin ngài,chhiáng chṳ́-tén lìn-yòng lâu lu̍k-mà fat-sṳ;mo̍k chhàu ngài-têu, m̀-hó met-sa ngài-têu,pûn ngài-têu ke oi-chhìn chhṳ-yù fat-chán.( 8 3:5 Chhâm-kháu 2:7; 8:4.)6 p (Sîn-ngiòng) Ke tui khóng-yâ sông-lòi, khon chhiong yên-chhû,yung sên-lî ngìn mai ke nen-hiông lâu mu̍t-yo̍k hiûn-to hiông-hiông ke, he má-ngìn nè?7 Khon nâ, ke he Só-lò-mùn ke khieu-é,si-vì yû liuk-sṳ̍p ke sṳ-pîn pó-fu,he Yî-set-lie̍t chui yû-siu ke yúng-sṳ.8 Kì-têu chhiòn-phu tông voi sṳ́-kiam;kì-têu lûng-chúng tông voi siông-chhṳ̀.Tak-sà yêu-kiên phi-tai li-kiam,fòng-phi am-pû-thèu ke ngùi-hiám.9 Só-lò-mùn-vòng yung Lì-pâ-nun ke su-é,vi chhṳ-kâ chṳ-chho khieu-é.10 Khieu-é ke chhû-é yung ngiùn-è cho ke,khieu-tái( 9 3:10 "khieu-tái" ya-he yi̍t-cho "khieu-é táng", ngièn-vùn mò chhîn-chhú.) yung kîm-é cho ke;chhô-thiap yung chṳ́-set ke liau-é cho ke,he Yâ-lu-sat-lâng se-moi-é yung oi-chhìn chṳt hí-lòi ke.11 Siak-ôn ke se-moi-é â, lòi khon Só-lò-mùn-vòng!Kì thèu-nà tai-tén kôn-miên,he kì kiet-fûn ke-ngit,kì fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k ke-ngit,kiâ-mê vi kì tai ke.
