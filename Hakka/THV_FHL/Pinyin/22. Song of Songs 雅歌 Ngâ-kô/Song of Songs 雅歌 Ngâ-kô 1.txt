1 Só-lò-mùn ke sṳ̂-kô( 1 1:1 "Só-lò-mùn ke sṳ̂-kô" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Hien-pûn Só-lò-mùn ke sṳ̂-kô" ya-he "Yû-koân Só-lò-mùn ke sṳ̂-kô".), he sṳ̂-kô chûng chui chiâng ke sṳ̂-kô. Thi-yit Sú2 p (Sîn-ngiòng) Ngien kì yung kì ke choi-sùn chîm ngài!Ngì ke oi-chhìn pí hó-chiú kha-hiông.3 Ngì ke sṳ̂n-thí yû hiông-kâu ke hiông-mi;ngì ke miàng chhiong hiông-kâu fat-chhut chhîn-hiông,tông-yèn se-moi-é chhiòn-phu án oi-mu ngì.4 Ngien ngì khiên-tén ngài, ngài-têu kiak-kiak lòi-hi chéu;cho ngài ke kiûn-vòng, thu ngài ngi̍p ngì ke kiên-fòng.Ngài-têu oi khiung-ha lòi hióng-su fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k;yû ngì ke oi-chhìn, ngài kha-yàng hióng-su hó-chiú.Nàn-koai se-moi-é chhiòn-phu án oi-mu ngì!5 Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke se-moi-é â,ngài sûi-yèn kha vû, chúng-he tông-chiâng( 2 1:5 "ngài ... chiâng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ngài yu kha vû, yu he tông-chiâng".),chhiong Kî-tha̍t ke vû yòng-mô chong-phùng,chhiong Só-lò-mùn kiûng-chûng ke pu-liàm.6 M̀-hó yîn-vi ngài án vû chhiu khon-khiâng ngài,he ngit-thèu chiông ngài sai-to án vû ke â!Yîn-vi ngài khiung-ôi ke hiûng-thi hiong ngài fat-khién,kâm ngài chhai phù-thò-yèn kûng-chok,ngài lièn chhṳ-kâ ke phù-thò-yèn( 3 1:6 "phù-thò-yèn" khó-nèn chṳ́-to se-moi-é ke sṳ̂n-thí.) sa mò cheu-ku tó!7 Ngài ke oi-ngìn â, chhiáng lâu ngài kóng,ngì chhai nai-vi chóng-yòng?Tông-chu chhai nai-vi pûn yòng hiet-khun?Ngài cho má-ke oi chhai ngì thùng-phân ke yòng-khiùn sùn,chhiong êm-tén mien-sâ ke ngìn kín chhìm ngì nè?( 4 1:7 "chhiong êm-tén ... nè?" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhiong chhông-kî hàng-lòi hàng-hi kín chhìm ngì nè?" Ngièn-vùn mò chhîn-chhú.)8 p (Sîn-lòng) Chui-chiâng ke se-moi-é â,ngì na m̀-tî ke só-chhai,thèn yòng-khiùn ke kiok-chiak hi,chhai chóng-yòng ke chong-phùng sùn,pûn ngì ke sân-yòng sṳ̍t chhó.9 Ngài ke chhìn-moi â,ngì chhiong Âi-khi̍p-vòng chan-chhâ ke chun-mâ án-chiâng.10 Ngì lióng-phién ke mien-kap-lón yîn-vi koa ngí-vàn fî-sòng chiâng,kiáng-kîn tai-tén pha̍t-lien ya chhin-chiâng.11 Ngài-têu lòi vi ngì tá kîm lien-é,vi ngì siông ngiùn-è ke chông-sṳt-phín.12 p (Sîn-ngiòng) Ngài ke kiûn-vòng chhô-si̍t ke sṳ̀,ngài ke hiông-súi fat-chhut hiông-mi.13 Ngài ke chhìn-lòng sòng-sòng kho chhai ngài ke hiûng-chhièn;kì chhiu-chhiong ngài tai-tén yit-thoi mu̍t-yo̍k án-hiông.14 Ngài ke chhìn-lòng chhiong yit-chhon fung-siên-fâ,khôi chhai Yún-Kî-tí ke phù-thò-yèn tú.15 p (Sîn-lòng) Ngài ke chhìn-moi â, ngì sṳ̍t-chhai fî-sòng chiâng!Ngì ke muk-chû chhiong ngie̍t-kap-è án vûn-yù.16 p (Sîn-ngiòng) Chhìn-lòng â, ngì tông sṳ̂-vùn, tông tet-ngìn-siak!Ngài-têu cho-tet yung chhiâng-chhó lòi-chún mìn-chhòng,17 yung hiông-pak-su cho vuk-táng ke tung-liòng,yung chhiùng-su chún-cho vuk-táng ke kok-è.
