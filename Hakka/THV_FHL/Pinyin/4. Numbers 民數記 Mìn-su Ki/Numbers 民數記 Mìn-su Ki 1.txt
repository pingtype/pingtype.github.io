1 Yî-set-lie̍t ke Thi-yit Pái Ngìn-khiéu Thiàu-chhà Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn lì-khôi Âi-khi̍p heu ke thi-ngi ngièn ngi-ngie̍t chhû-yit, Song-chú chhai Sî-nai khóng-yâ, chhai Kì ke sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi tui Mô-sî kóng:2 "Ngì lâu Â-lùn oi thiàu-chhà Yî-set-lie̍t chhiòn fi-chung ke ngìn-khiéu, oi yit chûng-chhu̍k yit chûng-chhu̍k, yit kâ-chhu̍k yit kâ-chhu̍k thiàu-chhà, chiông ngi-sṳ̍p se yî-song voi siông-chhṳ̀ ke nàm-ngìn yit-sà yit-sà, cheu kì-têu ke kiûn-chhui lie̍t-chhut miàng-tân lòi.3 併於上節4 Ngì-têu oi tui tak kî-chhu̍k chhìm yit-ke chhu̍k-chóng lòi pông-chhu ngì-têu."5 Yî-ha he tak kî-chhu̍k vi liá-hong kûng-chok só-sién chhut-lòi ke liâng-chhiu:Lî-phien kî-chhu̍k — Sṳ-tiû-ngí ke lai-é Yî-li-siuk;Sî-mién kî-chhu̍k — Sû-li-sâ-thoi ke lai-é Sṳ-lu-me̍t;Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k — Â-mí-nâ-tha̍t ke lai-é Nâ-sun;Yî-sat-kâ kî-chhu̍k — Sû-ap ke lai-é Nâ-thán-ngia̍p;Sî-pu-lùn kî-chhu̍k — Hî-lùn ke lai-é Yî-li-ap;Yok-sit ke chṳ́-sûn lióng-ke kî-chhu̍k:Yî-fap-lièn kî-chhu̍k — Â-mí-fut ke lai-é Yî-li-sâ-mâ;Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k — Pí-thai-siuk ke lai-é Kâ-mâ-lie̍t;Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k — Kî-tô-nì ke lai-é Â-pí-tan;Tan kî-chhu̍k — Â-mí-sâ-thoi ke lai-é Â-hî-yî-chhia;Â-sat kî-chhu̍k — Ngò-làn ke lai-é Pha-kiet;Kâ-tet kî-chhu̍k — Tiû-ngí ke lai-é Yî-li-ngâ-sat;Nâ-fut-thâ-li kî-chhu̍k — Yî-nàm ke lai-é Â-hî-lâ. Yî-song ke ngìn he tui fi-chung sién chhut-lòi ke, tak-sà cho pún kî-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng, chhiòn-phu he Yî-set-lie̍t kiûn-chhui ke liâng-chhiu.6 併於上節7 併於上節8 併於上節9 併於上節10 併於上節11 併於上節12 併於上節13 併於上節14 併於上節15 併於上節16 併於上節17 Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn tet-tó liá sṳ̍p-ngi sà ke pông-chhu,18 chhiu chhai ngi-ngie̍t chhû-yit chhi-si̍p chhiòn fi-chung, cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k ke sun-si tên-ki chhiòn-phu ngìn-mìn. Só-yû ngi-sṳ̍p se yî-song ke nàm-ngìn yit-sà yit-sà chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi son ngìn-su,19 cheu Song-chú só min-lin ke. Án-ngiòng, Mô-sî chhai Sî-nai khóng-yâ tên-ki chhiòn-phu ngìn-mìn.20 Ngi-sṳ̍p se yî-song voi siông-chhṳ̀ ke nàm-ngìn, cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k ke sun-si, yit-sà yit-sà chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi son ngìn-su; Yî-set-lie̍t ke chóng-chṳ́ Lî-phien ke yit kî-chhu̍k phài thèu-chhièn. Tak kî-chhu̍k ke chúng ngìn-su ki chhai hâ-poi:Lî-phien kî-chhu̍k — si-van liuk-chhiên ńg-pak ngìn;Sî-mién kî-chhu̍k — ńg-van kiú-chhiên sâm-pak ngìn;Kâ-tet kî-chhu̍k — si-van ńg-chhiên liuk-pak ńg-sṳ̍p ngìn;Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k — chhit-van si-chhiên liuk-pak ngìn;Yî-sat-kâ kî-chhu̍k — ńg-van si-chhiên si-pak ngìn;Sî-pu-lùn kî-chhu̍k — ńg-van chhit-chhiên si-pak ngìn;Yok-sit ke chṳ́-sûn lióng-ke kî-chhu̍k:Yî-fap-lièn kî-chhu̍k — si-van làng ńg-pak ngìn;Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k — sâm-van lióng-chhiên lióng-pak ngìn;Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k — sâm-van ńg-chhiên si-pak ngìn;Tan kî-chhu̍k — liuk-van lióng-chhiên chhit-pak ngìn;Â-sat kî-chhu̍k — si-van yit-chhiên ńg-pak ngìn;Nâ-fut-thâ-li kî-chhu̍k — ńg-van sâm-chhiên si-pak ngìn.21 併於上節22 併於上節23 併於上節24 併於上節25 併於上節26 併於上節27 併於上節28 併於上節29 併於上節30 併於上節31 併於上節32 併於上節33 併於上節34 併於上節35 併於上節36 併於上節37 併於上節38 併於上節39 併於上節40 併於上節41 併於上節42 併於上節43 併於上節44 Yî-song ke nàm-ngìn chhiòn-phu he Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn pin thoi-péu Yî-set-lie̍t sṳ̍p-ngi kî-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-chóng tên-ki hâ-lòi ke.45 Yî-set-lie̍t chhiòn fi-chung ngi-sṳ̍p se yî-song voi siông-chhṳ̀ ke nàm-ngìn, chhiòn-phu he cheu kâ-chhu̍k tên-ki hâ-lòi son ngìn-su ke.46 Chúng-khiung ngìn-su chhiu-he liuk-sṳ̍p van sâm-chhiên ńg-pak ńg-sṳ̍p ngìn.47 Li-vi-ngìn mò lâu khì-thâ kî-chhu̍k khiung-ha tên-ki,48 yîn-vi Song-chú sṳt tui Mô-sî kóng:49 "Ngì thiàu-chhà voi siông-chhṳ̀ ke ngìn-su ke sṳ̀, m̀-hó pâu-hàm Li-vi kî-chhu̍k.50 Ngì oi phai Li-vi-ngìn kón-lî yok-khui ke sṳn-chong-phùng lâu khì-chûng ke yung-phín; kì-têu oi pân yun liá-têu tûng-sî, chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi sṳ-fung. Kì-têu ke chong-phùng oi tap chhai sṳn-chong-phùng ke si chû-vì.51 Ngì-têu tak-pái sái-yàng ke sṳ̀, Li-vi-ngìn oi chhak-hâ sṳn-chong-phùng, chhai sîn ke yàng-thi chhùng-sîn tap hí-lòi. Só-yû khì-thâ ke ngìn na-he chiap-khiun sṳn-chong-phùng, yit-thin oi chhú sí-hìn.52 "Khì-thâ ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn oi ôn-yàng, tak-sà chhai kì só-su̍k ke thai-khì hâ, cheu kiûn-chhui lòi ôn-phài.53 Chúng-he Li-vi-ngìn oi chhai yok-khui ke sṳn-chong-phùng ke si chû-vì ôn-yàng sú-ví, miên-tet yû-ngìn hàng-khiûn sṳn-chong-phùng, ngiâ Ngài chit-fa̍t Yî-set-lie̍t fi-chung."54 Só-yû Song-chú min-lin Mô-sî ke fa, Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhiu cheu án-ngiòng hi-cho.
