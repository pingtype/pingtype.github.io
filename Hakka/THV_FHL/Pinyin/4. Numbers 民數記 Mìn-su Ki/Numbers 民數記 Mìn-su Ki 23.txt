1 Pâ-làn tui Pâ-le̍t kóng: "Ngì oi chhai liá-vi vi ngài hí chhit-chho chi-thàn, yu vi ngài chún-phi chhit-chak ngiù-kú lâu chhit-chak yòng-kú."2 Pâ-le̍t cheu kì fûn-fu hi-cho; kì lâu Pâ-làn chhai tak-chho chi-thàn hien yit-chak ngiù-kú lâu yit-chak yòng-kú.3 Pâ-làn tui Pâ-le̍t kóng: "Ngì khî chhai ngì ke sêu-fa-chi sùn-thèu; ngài hi khon Song-chú oi kien ngài mò. Kì khí-sṳ ngài má-ke, ngài yit-thin lâu ngì kóng." Só-yî Pâ-làn tân-sà sông-to sân-táng.4 Song-ti hiong Pâ-làn hién-hien; Pâ-làn kóng: "Ngài yí-kîn hí chhit-chho chi-thàn, chhai tak-chho chi-thàn hien yit-chak ngiù-kú lâu yit-chak yòng-kú."5 Song-chú lâu Pâ-làn kóng yin-kôi kóng ke fa, yèn-heu ham kì to-chón Pâ-le̍t ke-vi hi, chiông Song-chú ke fa lâu Pâ-le̍t kóng.6 Pâ-làn chón-hi, fat-hien Pâ-le̍t lâu só-yû Mô-ap ke liâng-chhiu hàn khî chhai sêu-fa-chi sùn-thèu.7 Pâ-làn chhiu kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Mô-ap-vòng Pâ-le̍t tai ngài chhut Si-li-â,tai ngài chhut tûng-phién ke sân.Kì kóng: ‘Lòi â, vi ngài chu-chú Ngâ-kok;lòi â, vi ngài chu-ma Yî-set-lie̍t.’8 Song-ti mò chu-chú ke, ngài ngióng cho-tet chu-chú?Song-chú mò chu-ma ke, ngài ngióng cho-tet chu-ma?9 Ngài tui kô-sân táng khon kì-têu;ngài tui sân-kông táng kien kì-têu.Kì-têu he yit-ke lâu van-mìn mò khiung-yong ke koet-kâ;kì-têu pí phe̍t ke koet-kâ hàn-kha mùng-fuk.10 Ngâ-kok ke chṳ́-sûn chhiong nài-sâ án-ngiòng;Yî-set-lie̍t ke heu-thoi tô-to mò fap-thu kie-son.Pûn ngài chhiong Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn án-ngiòng kiet-suk yit-sên;ngài ngien chhiong ngi-ngìn án-ngiòng phìn-ôn ko-sṳ̂n."11 Pâ-le̍t tui Pâ-làn kóng: "Ngì tui ngài cho ke he má-ke? Ngài tai ngì to liá-vi lòi chu-chú ngài ke thi̍t-ngìn, ngì fán-chón sa chuk-fuk kì-têu!"12 Pâ-làn en-kóng: "Ngài tân-chhiang cho-tet kóng Song-chú oi ngài kóng ke fa!" Pâ-làn Thi-ngi Pái Yi-ngièn13 Pâ-le̍t tui Pâ-làn kóng: "Ngì thèn ngài to nang-ngoi yit-ke thi-fông hi, tui ke-vi ngì chṳ́-yû khon tet-tó phu-fun ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn. Ngì oi chhai ke-vi vi ngài chu-chú kì-têu!"14 Kì tai Pâ-làn to Pí-sṳ̂-kâ-sân táng-kô ke Só-fî-thièn, chhai ke-vi ya hí chhit-chho chi-thàn, chhai tak-chho chi-thàn hien yit-chak ngiù-kú lâu yit-chak yòng-kú.15 Pâ-làn tui Pâ-le̍t kóng: "Ngì khî chhai ngì ke sêu-fa-chi sùn-thèu, ngài to ke-phién hi kien Song-ti."16 Song-chú hiong Pâ-làn hién-hien, lâu kì kóng yin-kôi kóng ke fa, yèn-heu ham kì to-chón Pâ-le̍t ke-vi hi, chiông Song-chú ke fa lâu Pâ-le̍t kóng.17 Pâ-làn chón-hi, fat-hien Pâ-le̍t lâu Mô-ap ke-têu liâng-chhiu hàn khî chhai sêu-fa-chi sùn-thèu. Pâ-le̍t mun kì Song-chú kóng má-ke fa,18 Pâ-làn chhiu kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Lòi â, Sî-pat ke lai-é Pâ-le̍t;ngì lòi-thâng ngài oi kóng ke fa.19 Song-ti lâu ngìn mò khiung-yong, Kì m̀-kóng fâ-liâu;Kì m̀-chhiong phû-thûng ngìn, Kì m̀-voi kói-pien chú-yi.Kì tap-yin ke sṳ, yit-thin cho-to;Kì yit kóng-fa, sṳ chhiu vàn-sṳ̀n.20 Ngài fung Chú min-lin lòi chuk-fuk;Song-ti su ke ên-tién, ngài mò fap-thu sû chón-lòi.21 Ngài khon-chhut Ngâ-kok ke chiông-lòi,Yî-set-lie̍t mò châi-nan, ya-mò châi-fo.Song-chú — kì-têu ke Song-ti lâu kì-têu thùng-chhai;kì-têu fôn-hí khin-chuk Song-chú cho kì-têu ke vòng.22 Song-ti thu kì-têu chhut Âi-khi̍p;Kì tá-thui Âi-khi̍p, chhiu-chhiong yâ-ngiù án yû li̍t.23 Fap-su̍t mò fap-thu hoi Ngâ-kok;puk-koa ya mò phan-fap hoi Yî-set-lie̍t.Liá-ha, ngìn oi thàm-lun Ngâ-kok,chhiu-he thàm-lun Yî-set-lie̍t, kóng:‘Khon nâ, Song-ti sṳ̀n-chhiu ví-thai ke sṳ!’24 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhiong mû-sṳ̂ khî hí-lòi,chhiu-chhiong kûng-sṳ̂ hong hí-lòi chhìm-sṳ̍t;kì na mò sṳ̍t-thet lia̍p-vu̍t ke ngiuk,mò lîm-tó lia̍p-vu̍t ke hiet, kì chhie̍t-tui m̀-hén thìn."25 Pâ-le̍t tui Pâ-làn kóng: "Ngì m̀ chu-chú Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, chṳ-séu ya m̀ yin-kôi chuk-fuk kì-têu â!"26 Pâ-làn en-kóng: "Ngài m̀-he yû lâu ngì kóng-ko, ngài yit-thin oi cheu Song-chú só-kóng ke fa cho!" Pâ-làn Thi-sâm Pái Yi-ngièn27 Pâ-le̍t tui Pâ-làn kóng: "Thèn ngài lòi! Ngài oi tai ngì to nang-ngoi yit-ke thi-fông; yû khó-nèn Song-ti voi pûn ngì chhai ke-vi vi ngài chu-chú kì-têu."28 Pâ-le̍t chhiu tai Pâ-làn to ke yén-yén khon tet-tó khóng-yâ ke Phî-ngí-sân táng-kô.29 Pâ-làn tui Pâ-le̍t kóng: "Ngì oi chhai liá-vi vi ngài hí chhit-chho chi-thàn, yu vi ngài chún-phi chhit-chak ngiù-kú lâu chhit-chak yòng-kú."30 Pâ-le̍t cheu Pâ-làn ke fa hi-cho, chhai tak-chho chi-thàn hien yit-chak ngiù-kú lâu yit-chak yòng-kú.
