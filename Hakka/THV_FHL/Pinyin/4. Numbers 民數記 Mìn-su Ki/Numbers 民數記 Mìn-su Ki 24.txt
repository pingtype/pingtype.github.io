1 Pâ-làn yí-kîn khon-chhut Song-chú oi kì chuk-fuk Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, só-yî kì liá-pái mò-chhiong yî-chhièn án-ngiòng hi khiù seu-thèu. Kì ke mien chón-hiong khóng-yâ,2 khon-tó Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn tak kî-chhu̍k ôn-yàng chhai ke-vi. Song-ti ke Lìn lìm-to kì,3 kì chhiu kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Pí-ngí ke lai-é Pâ-làn ke yi-ngièn,ke khon chhin chhîn-chhú( 21 24:3 "khon chhin chhîn-chhú" ya-he yi̍t-cho "muk-chû sap-tén".) ke ngìn kóng:4 Ngài thâng-tó Song-ti só kóng ke fa,ngài khon-tó ke tui Chhiòn-nèn-chá lòi ke yi-siong,ngài chhiu phu̍k hâ-hi, muk-chû tá-khôi.5 Ngâ-kok â, ngì ke chong-phùng chhin chiâng!Yî-set-lie̍t â, ngì ke yàng-chong ya tông hó khon!6 Kì-têu chhiong yit-phài yit-phài ke chûng-su,chhiong hò-pa sùn ke fâ-yèn,chhiong Song-chú chhîn-sú chung ke chhṳ̀m-hiông su,ya chhiong súi-sùn ke hiông-pak-su.7 Súi voi tui kì-têu ke thúng liù chhut-lòi;kì-têu ke chúng-chṳ́ voi ve chhai sṳt-yun ke thièn-tú.Kì-têu ke vòng voi pí Â-kap ví-thai;kì ke koet voi hiong si-fông fat-chán.8 Song-ti thu kì-têu chhut Âi-khi̍p,Kì tá-thui Âi-khi̍p, chhiu-chhiong yâ-ngiù án yû li̍t.Kì-têu voi thûn-me̍t sù-thi̍t,chak-sui kì-têu ke kut-thèu,áu-thôn kì-têu ke chien.9 Liá-ke koet-kâ chhiong kûng-sṳ̂ phu̍k-tén;chhiong mû-sṳ̂ soi-tén, mò ngìn kám chhàu-tó kì.Chuk-fuk Yî-set-lie̍t ke, yit-thin yû fuk-hi;chu-chú Yî-set-lie̍t ke, yit-thin yû châi-nan."10 Pâ-le̍t thai fat-khién kha̍k-tén khièn-thèu, tui Pâ-làn kóng: "Ngài ham ngì lòi chu-chú ngài ke sù-thi̍t, ngì tiên-to sâm-pái chuk-fuk kì-têu.11 Ngì chón-hi! Ngài sióng-oi thai-thai tap-chhia ngì, chúng-he Song-chú m̀ pûn ngì tet-tó."12 Pâ-làn en-kóng: "Ngài kám m̀-he tui ngì phai-lòi ke sṳ́-chá kóng:13 ‘Chhiu-son Pâ-le̍t chiông vòng-kiûng só-yû ke kîm-ngiùn pûn ngài, ngài ya cho-m̀-tet cheu chhṳ-kâ ke yi-sṳ lòi vì-poi Song-chú ke min-lin. Ngài tân-chhiang cho-tet kóng Song-chú oi ngài kóng ke fa.’ " Pâ-làn Chui-heu ke Yi-ngièn14 Pâ-làn tui Pâ-le̍t kóng: "Ngài liá-ha oi chón-to ngài thùng-pâu ke-vi hi. Lì-khôi yî-chhièn, ngài oi kín-ko ngì, pûn ngì tî chiông-lòi Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn oi ngióng-pân tui-thai ngì ke ngìn-mìn."15 Só-yî Pâ-làn kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Pí-ngí ke lai-é Pâ-làn ke yi-ngièn,ke khon chhin chhîn-chhú( 22 24:15 Khon 24:3.) ke ngìn kóng:16 Ngài thâng-tó Song-ti só kóng ke fa,tet-tó tui Chṳ-kô-chá lòi ke tî sṳt;ngài khon-tó ke tui Chhiòn-nèn-chá lòi ke yi-siong,ngài chhiu phu̍k hâ-hi, muk-chû tá-khôi.17 Ngài khon yén-yén ke chiông-lòi;ngài khon-tó yit-sà ngìn.Yû yit-lia̍p sên-é oi tui Ngâ-kok chhut-lòi;yû yit-ke kiûn-vòng oi tui Yî-set-lie̍t hîn-hí.Kì oi tá-sui Mô-ap ke liâng-chhiu,fí-me̍t só-yû ke Sî-ngí-ngìn.18 Kì oi chṳ̂n-fu̍k Yî-tûng,cham-liâng Sî-ngí, liá sù-thi̍t ke thú-thi.Yî-set-lie̍t voi sòng-sòng tet-sṳn.19 Yû yit-sà oi tui Ngâ-kok chhut-lòi chóng-khièn,chiông sàng-tú chhûn-tó ke ngìn chhù chhiang-chhiang."20 Heu-lòi, Pâ-làn chhai yi-siong chûng khon-tó Â-mâ-li̍t-ngìn, chhiu kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Â-mâ-li̍t pún-he chui khiòng-thai ke koet-kâ,chúng-he chui-heu yit-thin voi me̍t-mòng."21 Pâ-làn chhai yi-siong chûng khon-tó Kî-nì-ngìn, chhiu kóng-chhut yî-ha ke yi-ngièn:"Ngì he̍t ke só-chhai pún-lòi he ôn-chhiòn,chhiong cho chhai ngàm-sa̍k piak-táng ke teu.22 Chúng-he ngì-têu Kî-nì-ngìn voi sêu-me̍t;Â-sut-ngìn voi chiông ngì-têu chok-chéu."23 Pâ-làn yu kóng yi-ngièn:"Ôi! Song-ti hàng liá-têu sṳ, má-ngìn voi sâng hâ-hi nè?( 23 24:23 "Ôi ... hâ-hi nè?" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Chhi-si̍p chhai pet-fông ke-têu ngìn he má-ngìn?")24 Tui Kî-thì yû chhîm-lio̍k-chá chhô-sòn lòi;kì-têu voi chṳ̂n-fu̍k Â-sut lâu Hî-pak,chúng-he kì-têu ya yit-thin voi sêu-me̍t."25 Yèn-heu, Pâ-làn chhiu chón vuk-hâ, Pâ-le̍t ya chón-hi.
