1 Kô-hot Chûng-chhu̍k ke Ngim-vu Song-chú fûn-fu Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn2 cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k thiàu-chhà Li-vi-ngìn Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn-khiéu,3 chiông só-yû sâm-sṳ̍p se to ńg-sṳ̍p se chûng-kiên, voi chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi kûng-chok ke nàm-ngìn chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi.4 Song-chú chṳ́-sṳ Mô-sî, Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi oi chôn-mùn cho lâu chṳ-sṳn-vu̍t yû-koân ke kûng-chok.5 Chhai sái-yàng ke sṳ̀, Â-lùn lâu kì ke-têu lai-é oi chin-ngi̍p sṳn-chong-phùng, chiông kak-tén yok-khui ke chong-man nâ hâ-lòi, khièm chhai yok-khui táng-kô.6 Kì-têu hàn-oi yung chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau khièm chhai táng-kô, chai khièm yit-te sùn làm-set ke pu-é, yèn-heu chiông kong-é chhôn ko-hi.7 Kì-têu oi phû yit-te làm-set ke pu-é chhai kiûng-piáng ke chok-táng, chai chiông phàn-è, vón, kin-sṳ̀n yung ke chiú-fù lâu chiú-pî chhiòn-phu piong chhai táng-kô. Chok-táng oi sṳ̀-sòng phài-lie̍t kiûng-piáng.8 Kì-têu hàn-oi yung yit-te chhṳ̂m-fùng set ke pu-é khièm chhai liá-têu tûng-sî táng-kô, chai khièm yit-chhèn chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau, yèn-heu chiông kong-é chhôn ko-hi.9 Kì-têu oi yung làm-set ke pu-é khièm tên-thòi, tên, tên-fâ chién-tô, tên-phàn, lâu só-yû chông kám-lám-yù ke yung-phín;10 yu oi yung chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau chiông liá-têu tûng-sî pâu hí-lòi, piong chhai kông-ka táng-kô.11 Chai-lòi, kì-têu oi phû yit-te làm-set ke pu-é chhai kîm hiông-thàn táng, pin yung chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau khièm-tén, yèn-heu chiông kong-é chhôn ko-hi.12 Oi yung làm-pu chiông só-yû sṳn-só tî-poi ke yung-phín pâu hí-lòi, pin yung chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau khièm-tén, piong chhai kông-ka táng-kô.13 Oi chiông chi-thàn táng ke fôi sû hí-lòi, yung chṳ́-set ke pu-é khièm chhai chi-thàn táng,14 chiông só-yû chi-thàn ke fu-khien, chhiu-he fó-fôi-phàn, ngiuk-chhâ, chhán-è lâu fó-tín chhiòn-phu piong chhai táng-kô, yèn-heu oi yung chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau khièm chhai táng-kô, chiông kong-é chhôn ko-hi.15 Sái-yàng ke sṳ̀, Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k oi tén Â-lùn lâu kì ke-têu lai-é chiông liá-têu sṳn-vu̍t lâu só-yû ke làng-khien khièm-hó yî-heu, chang-lòi kông. Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn chhie̍t-tui cho-m̀-tet miâ-tó liá-têu sṳn-vu̍t, na-he miâ-tó chhiu voi sí.Sái sṳn-chong-phùng ke sṳ̀, Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k oi fu-chit kông liá-têu tûng-sî.16 Chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn ke lai-é Yî-li-â-sat oi fu-chit chóng-kón kûi-chak sṳn-chong-phùng, pin tên-yù, hiông, su-chi, sṳn-yù, lâu sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi só-yû fûn-phe̍t vì-sṳn kûi-pûn Song-chú ke tûng-sî.17 Song-chú tui Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn kóng:18 "M̀-hó pûn Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn hàng-khiûn liá-têu chṳ-sṳn vu̍t, miên-tet kì-têu sí-thet.19 Vi-tó phit-miên fat-sên liá-chúng sṳ, Â-lùn lâu kì ke-têu lai-é oi chin-ngi̍p sṳn-chong-phùng, fûn-phai kì-têu tak-sà ke kûng-chok, lâu kì-têu sot-mìn só oi kông ke he má-ke.20 Chúng-he, Kô-hot-ngìn ngi̍p sṳn-chong-phùng, m̀-hó hi khon-tó sṳn-vu̍t, miên-tet kì-têu voi sí." Kiet-sun Chûng-chhu̍k ke Ngim-vu21 Song-chú min-lin Mô-sî,22 oi cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k thiàu-chhà Li-vi-ngìn( 3 4:22 "Li-vi-ngìn" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò liá kí-chak sṳ.) Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn-khiéu,23 chiông só-yû sâm-sṳ̍p se to ńg-sṳ̍p se chûng-kiên, voi chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi kûng-chok ke nàm-ngìn chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi.24 Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k oi fu-chit kông yî-ha ke tûng-sî:25 Sṳn-chong-phùng nui ke phùng-táng lâu ngoi-chhèn ke táng-koi, khièm chhai táng-kô chhin-chiâng ke phì-liau, mùn-liàm,26 sṳn-chong-phùng lâu chi-thàn chû-vì thìn-yen ke chong-man lâu sok-è, thìn-yen thai-mùn ke mùn-liàm, lâu liá-têu tûng-sî ke fu-khien chhiòn-phu piong hó-se. Kì-têu oi kón-lî só-yû lâu liá-têu tûng-sî yû-koân ke sṳ-vu.27 Mô-sî lâu Â-lùn oi kam-tuk Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn fu-chit kông liá-têu tûng-sî, chóng-tén kì-têu pân-yun Â-lùn lâu kì ke-têu lai-é phai kì-têu pân-yun ke vu̍t khien.28 Liá-têu kûng-chok he Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi yin-kôi cho ke sṳ. Kì-têu oi chhai chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn ke lai-é Yî-thâ-mâ ke chṳ́-fî hâ-poi chṳp-hàng ngim-vu. Mí-lâ-li Chûng-chhu̍k ke Ngim-vu29 Song-chú oi Mô-sî cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k thiàu-chhà Li-vi-ngìn( 4 4:29 Khon 4:22.) Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k ke ngìn-khiéu,30 chiông só-yû sâm-sṳ̍p se to ńg-sṳ̍p se chûng-kiên, voi chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi kûng-chok ke nàm-ngìn chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi.31 Kì-têu oi fu-chit kông sṳn-chong-phùng ke kut-ka, vàng-kong, chhû-é, kî-chho,32 lâu sṳn-chong-phùng chû-vì thìn-yen ke chhû-é, kî-chho, tâng-é, sok-è, pin liá-têu tûng-sî ke làng-khien. Tak-sà oi fu-chit kông chṳ́-thin ke tûng-sî.33 Liá-têu he Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi yin-kôi cho ke sṳ. Kì-têu oi chhai chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn ke lai-é Yî-thâ-mâ ke chṳ́-fî hâ-poi chṳp-hàng ngim-vu. Li-vi-ngìn ke Ngìn-khiéu Thiàu-chhà34 Mô-sî, Â-lùn lâu fi-chung ke liâng-chhiu chûn-cheu Song-chú ke min-lin thiàu-chhà Li-vi sâm-chak chûng-chhu̍k Kô-hot, Kiet-sun, Mí-lâ-li ke ngìn-khiéu. Kì-têu cheu chûng-chhu̍k, kâ-chhu̍k, chiông só-yû sâm-sṳ̍p se to ńg-sṳ̍p se chûng-kiên, voi chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi kûng-chok ke nàm-ngìn chhiòn-phu tên-ki hâ-lòi:Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k, lióng-chhiên chhit-pak ńg-sṳ̍p ngìn;Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k, lióng-chhiên liuk-pak sâm-sṳ̍p ngìn;Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k, sâm-chhiên lióng-pak ngìn;chúng ngìn-su, pat-chhiên ńg-pak pat-sṳ̍p ngìn.35 併於上節36 併於上節37 併於上節38 併於上節39 併於上節40 併於上節41 併於上節42 併於上節43 併於上節44 併於上節45 併於上節46 併於上節47 併於上節48 併於上節49 Cheu Song-chú pûn Mô-sî ke min-lin, tak-sà chhiòn-phu tên-ki lé; chûn-cheu Song-chú thûng-ko Mô-sî só min-lin ke, tak-sà chhiòn-phu yû chṳ́-thin ke ngim-vu, yû-têu fu-chit sṳ-fung, yû-têu fu-chit kông.
