1 Sṳ̍p-ngi ke Tham-é( Sṳ̂n-min Ki 1:19-33 ) Song-chú tui Mô-sî kóng:2 "Ngì oi tui sṳ̍p-ngi kî-chhu̍k chûng kok sién-chhut yit-ke liâng-chhiu, phai kì-têu hi tá-tham Kâ-nàm-thi, chhiu-he Ngài oi su-pûn Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn ke thú-thi."3 Mô-sî chhiu thâng-chhiùng Song-chú ke min-lin, tui Pâ-làn khóng-yâ phai-chhut sṳ̍p-ngi ke liâng-chhiu. Mô-sî chiông Nun ke lai-é Hò-hî-â kói-miàng cho Yok-sû-â.17 Mô-sî phai kì-têu chhut-hi ke sṳ̀, fûn-fu kì-têu kóng: "Tui liá-vi hiong-pet hàng, to Kâ-nàm-thi ke nàm-phién, yèn-heu chin-ngi̍p sân-khî.18 Khon ke só-chhai he ngióng-pân ke thi-fông, yû kí-tô ngìn he̍t chhai ke-vi, ngìn-mìn he khiòng ya-he ngio̍k.19 Khon ke thi-fông he hó ya-he fái, ngìn-mìn he he̍t chhai sân-yâ ya-he he̍t chhai yû fòng-ví ke sàng-sṳ.20 Khon ke-vi ke thú-thi yû phì mò? Yû su-é mò? Ngì-têu oi chhin-liong chiông ke-vi chhut-sán ke kó-chṳ́ tai-têu chón-lòi." (Ke-sṳ̀ tú-hó he phù-thò sîn-chhut ke sṳ̀-chiet.)21 Só-yî, kì-têu hiong-pet hàng, tui nàm-phién Chhìm ke khóng-yâ yit-chhṳ̍t tá-tham to pet-phién Ha̍p-mâ ai-héu fu-khiun ke Li-ha̍p.22 Kì-têu siên chin-ngi̍p ke-thi ke nàm-phu, yèn-heu lòi-to Hî-pak-lùn. He̍t chhai ke-vi ke ngìn yû Â-hî-man-chhu̍k, Sṳ-chhî-chhu̍k, Tha̍t-mâi-chhu̍k; kì-têu he kô-thai ke ngìn Â-na̍p-chhu̍k ke heu-thoi. (Hî-pak-lùn-sàng pí Âi-khi̍p ke Só-ôn chó chhit-ngièn hí ke.)23 Kì-têu lòi-to Yî-sṳ̍t-kok sân-lak, chhai ke-vi chám-hâ yit-chiet phù-thò su-kî, táng-kô yû yit thai-chhon phù-thò, oi lióng-sà yung kong-é chang kông tet-hí. Kì-têu ya tai-têu sa̍k-liù lâu vù-fâ-kó chón-lòi.24 (Ke thi-fông ôn-to Yî-sṳ̍t-kok( 14 13:24 "Yî-sṳ̍t-kok" yi-sṳ he "yit thai-chhon".) sân-lak, yîn-vi Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhai ke-vi chám-hâ yit thai-chhon phù-thò.)25 Tham-é chhai ke-thi tá-tham si-sṳ̍p ngit yî-heu,26 chhiu chón-to Pâ-làn khóng-yâ ke Kâ-tâi-sṳ̂, chiông kì-têu só khon-tó ke hiong Mô-sî, Â-lùn lâu Yî-set-lie̍t chhiòn fi-chung po-ko, thùng-sṳ̀ chiông tai chón-lòi ke kó-chṳ́ pûn thai-kâ khon.27 Kì-têu lâu Mô-sî kóng: "Ngài-têu tá-tham ngì phai ngài-têu hi ke thi-fông, fat-hien ke-he liù-nen-lâu-me̍t, chhin-phì ke thú-thi; liá chhiu-he ke thi-fông só chhut-sán ke kó-chṳ́.28 Chúng-he ke thi-fông ke ngìn-mìn tông yúng-chong; kì-têu ke sàng tông-thai tông kiên-ku. Hàn-yû, ngài-têu chhai ke-vi khon-tó kô-thai ke ngìn Â-na̍p-chhu̍k ke heu-thoi.29 Â-mâ-li̍t-ngìn he̍t chhai nàm-phién; Het-ngìn, Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn lâu Â-mô-li-ngìn he̍t chhai sân-khî; Kâ-nàm-ngìn he̍t chhai Thi-chûng-hói sùn lâu Yok-tan hò-sùn."30 Kâ-le̍t ham ke-têu tui Mô-sî mài-yen ke ngìn tiâm-tiâm, kóng: "Ngài-têu liá-ha yin-kôi sông-hi cham-liâng ke-phién thú-thi; ngài-têu yû nèn-li̍t chṳ̂n-fu̍k."31 Chúng-he, ke-têu lâu Kâ-le̍t khiung-ha hi ke ngìn kóng: "Cho-m̀-tet! Ngài-têu mò nèn-li̍t hi kûng-tá kì-têu; ke-vi ke ngìn pí ngài-têu khiòng-chong."32 Kì-têu chhiu chhai Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn tông-chûng chhòn-chhut yû-koân ke thi-fông mò khok-sṳ̍t ke po-ko. Kì-têu kóng: "Ngài-têu só tá-tham, ke thi-fông ke chhut-sán hàn m̀-la pún-thi ngìn sṳ̍t. Ngài-têu só khon-tó ke mî-yit ke ngìn chhiòn-phu tông kô-thai.33 Ngài-têu chhai ke-vi hàn khon-tó kô-thai ke ngìn Â-na̍p-chhu̍k ke heu-thoi; ngài-têu kám-kok chhṳ-kâ chhiong chhó-máng án-se, chhai kì-têu ngién-chûng, ngài-têu ya khok-sṳ̍t he án-ngiòng."
