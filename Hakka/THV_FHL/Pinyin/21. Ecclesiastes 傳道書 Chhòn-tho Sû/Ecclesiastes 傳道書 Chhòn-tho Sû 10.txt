1 Sí vû-yìn sṳ́ kûi-âng ke hiông-súi chhu-thet;yit-si̍t-é ngong-tong voi pho-fái chṳ-fi lâu chûn-yùng.2 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke sîm-kôn chṳn-chhṳ̍t;ngong-tong ngìn ke sîm-sṳ̂ phiên-chhâ.3 Ngong-tong ngìn lièn hàng-lu ke sṳ̀ ya mò thin-sin,thai-kâ yit-ha chhiu khon-chhut kì ke ngong-tong.4 Na-he chóng-khièn-chá tui ngì fat-khién, m̀-hó lì-khôi ngì ke chṳt-vi;ngì na lâng-chhin vûn-fò, chhiu-son ngiàm-chhung ke ko-sṳt ya voi tet-tó sa-miên.5 Ngài yu khon-tó ngit-thèu hâ yit-khien mò kûng-tong ke sṳ,liá he thúng-chhṳ-chá ke m̀-chho̍k:6 Kì pûn chhin-tô ngong-tong ngìn chhô kô-vi;fu-chiuk ke ngìn fán-chón chhô tâi-vi.7 Ngài khon-tó nù-chhòi khì-mâ,vòng-chṳ́ fán-chón chhiong nù-chhòi hàng-lu.8 Yet ham-chiáng ke, chhṳ-kâ yû khó-nèn tiet lo̍k-hi;chhak vì-chhiòng ke, yû khó-nèn pûn thu̍k-sà ngâu-tó.9 Tá-sa̍k ke, yû khó-nèn pûn sa̍k-thèu sông-tó;pho-chhèu ke, yû khó-nèn pûn chhèu-sak tá-sông.10 Pú-thèu thun-thet na mò nò-li, chhiu oi sṳ́ kha-thai li̍t;yung chṳ-fi cho-sṳ, chang voi sṳ̀n-kûng.11 Màng ngiam-chu chhiu pûn-sà ngâu-tó,án-ngiòng ngiam-chu yû má-ke yung nè?12 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke fa tai-lòi ên-fi;ngong-tong ngìn ke fa hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.13 Ngong-tong ngìn yit khôi-choi chhiu kóng ngong-fa,kóng to-mî he hoi ngìn ke tiên-fa.14 Ngong-tong ngìn kóng tông-tô fa;chúng-he ngìn m̀-tî chiông-lòi voi yû má-ke sṳ,kì ko-sṳ̂n yî-heu ke sṳ má-ngìn yû phan-fap lâu kì kóng nè?15 Ngong-tong ngìn piang-miang cho-se,lièn ngi̍p-sàng ke lu ya m̀-tî.16 Kiûn-vòng hàn-se, thai-sṳ̀n tá-chó chhiu yen-lo̍k,koet-kâ chhiu yû châi-fo lé.17 Kiûn-vòng he chûn-kui ke ngìn,kôn-yèn cheu-sṳ̀ lîm-sṳ̍t pú-chhûng thí-li̍t, m̀-voi chiú-chui,ke koet-kâ chhiu yû fuk-hi lé.18 Yîn-vi lân-sṳ̂, vuk-táng voi lap-thet;yîn-vi sú-lân, vuk chhiu voi leu.19 Pài yèn-si̍t sṳ́ ngìn fôn-seu,sṳ̍t mî-chiú sṳ́ ngìn khoai-lo̍k,yû-chhièn nèn sṳ́ van-sṳ thûng.20 M̀-hó phî-phìn kiûn-vòng, sîm-tú koai-yen ya cho-m̀-tet;lièn chhai fòng-kiên tú ya cho-m̀-tet chu-ma yû-chhièn ngìn.Khûng-chûng ke tiâu-é voi chiông ngì ke fa chhòn chhut-hi,yû yi̍t-kap ke voi chiông ngì ke fa po chhut-lòi.
