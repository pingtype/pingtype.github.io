1 Ngìn-sên ke Khûng-hî Chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng cho vòng, Thai-ví ke lai-é, Chhòn-tho-chá( 1 1:1 "Chhòn-tho-chá" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Thó-sṳ̂", 1:2, 12 khiung-yong.) ke fa.2 Chhòn-tho-chá kóng: Khûng-hî( 2 1:2 "Khûng-hî" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Mò yung" ya-he "Mò yi-sṳ"; nang-ngoi ya-he yi̍t-cho "Hièn-mì" ya-he "Tông-nàn liáu-kié", chhai pún-sû sòng-sòng chhut-hien.), khûng-hî, sṳ̍p-fûn khûng-hî,van-sṳ chhiòn-phu he khûng-hî.3 Ngìn ke yit-sên, chhai ngit-thèu hâ lò-lò luk-luk,to-tái yû má-ke ka-chhṳ̍t?4 Yit-thoi ko-hi, yit-thoi yu lòi,sṳ-kie hàn-he khiung-yong.5 Ngit-thèu chhut-lòi, ngit-thèu lo̍k-sân,tông-kiak to-chón kì chhut-lòi ke thi-fông.6 Fûng hiong-nàm chhôi, yu chón hiong-pet,chhôi-lòi chhôi-hi, hàn-he chón-to ngièn-lòi ke lu-sien.7 Kông-hò liù-ngi̍p thai-hói, hói yit-chhṳ̍t m̀-voi mân;kông-hò tui-nai liù chhut-lòi, hàn-he to-chón hi.8 Ngìn yîn-vi van-sṳ chhâu-fàn:Kóng-fa, ya kóng m̀-voi so̍t;muk-chû khon, khon m̀-voi páu;ngí-kûng thâng, thâng m̀-voi la.9 Fat-sên ko ke sṳ hàn-he voi fat-sên;cho-ko ke sṳ hàn-he chai cho.Ngit-thèu hâ mò yit-hong sîn ke sṳ.10 Yû nai-hong sṳ ngìn cho-tet kóng: "Khon â, liá he sîn ke"?Sṳ-sṳ̍t song, chhai ngài-têu chhut-se yî-chhièn chó chhiu yû lé.11 Kú-chó ke sṳ-thoi, mò ngìn ki-tet;chiông-lòi ke sṳ-thoi, heu-lòi ke ngìn ya m̀-voi ki-tet.12 Chhòn-tho-chá ke Kîn-ngiam Ngài he Chhòn-tho-chá; ngài chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng cho-ko Yî-set-lie̍t ke vòng.13 Ngài chôn-sîm yung chṳ-fi chhìm-khiù, ngiên-kiu thiên-ha yit-chhiet fat-sên ke sṳ.Song-ti pûn sṳ-kiên ngìn ke chhûng-tâm ngióng-voi án chhûng!14 Ngài khon-ko ngit-thèu hâ fat-sên ke yit-chhiet; chhiòn-phu he khûng-hî, he chok-fûng.15 Vân ke mò phan-fap pien chhṳ̍t;mò ke cho-m̀-tet son yû.16 Ngài sîm-tú án-ngiòng sióng: "Ngài ke chṳ-fi kô-ko só-yû yî-chhièn thúng-chhṳ ko Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke ngìn. Ngài ke sîm liáu-kié tông-tô chṳ-fi lâu chṳ-sṳt."17 Ngài yung-sîm oi fûn-phe̍t chṳ-fi lâu ngong-tong, chṳ-sṳt lâu tiên-khòng, chang tî liá ya he chok-fûng.18 Chṳ-fi ye̍t-kô, fàn-lò ye̍t-tô;chṳ-sṳt ye̍t-tô, sông-sîm ye̍t-thai.
