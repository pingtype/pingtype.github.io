1 Ngài yu khon-tó ngit-thèu hâ só-yû mò kûng-phìn ke sṳ. Pûn-ngìn khî-fu ke liù muk-chṳp, mò-ngìn ôn-ví kì-têu. Kì-têu tet m̀-tó ôn-ví, yîn-vi khî-fu kì-têu ke ngìn yû-khièn yû-sṳ.2 Ngài hiûn-mu ke-têu sí-thet ke ngìn, kì-têu pí hàn sâng ke ngìn kha hen-fuk.3 Chúng-he, màng chhut-se, màng khon-ko ngit-thèu hâ só fat-sên mò kûng-phìn ke sṳ ke ngìn, pí yî-song lióng-chúng ngìn hàn-kha hen-fuk.4 Ngài yu fat-hien: Ngìn lò-luk kûng-chok, chûi-khiù kûng-miàng, he yîn-vi oi lâu lìn-sa kin-chên. Liá ya he khûng-hî, ya he chok-fûng.5 Yû ngìn kóng: "Ngong-ngìn hàn chhok-chhok, ngo-sí chhṳ-kâ."6 Chúng-he, yit-sú púng mân-mân, phìn-ôn hióng-su, yàng-ko sûng-sú púng mân-mân, lò-luk chok-fûng.7 Ngài yu khon-tó ngit-thèu hâ yû-têu khûng-hî ke sṳ:8 Yû ngìn kû-tân yit-sà, mò lai-é, ya mò hiûng-thi, chúng-he kûi-ngit lò-luk, chhṳ-chhiùng m̀-sṳt mân-chiuk só tet-tó ke chhòi-fu. Kì kóng: "Ngài sîn-sîn khú-khú, hî-sên hióng-su, to-tái he vi má-ngìn nè?" Liá ya he khûng-hî, ya tông chhôi-ko.9 Lióng-sà pí yit-sà kha-hó, yîn-vi lióng-sà ha̍p-chok háu-kó kha-hó.10 Yit-sà tiet-tó, nang-ngoi yit-sà cho-tet fù kì hong hí-lòi. Na-he kû-tân yit-sà, tiet-tó ke sṳ̀ mò-ngìn fù kì hong hí-lòi, kì chhiu tông chhôi-ko lé.11 Lióng-sà khiung-ha soi kha sêu-nôn, yit-sà chhṳ-kâ soi ngióng-voi sêu nè?12 Lióng-sà thòn-kiet nèn tá-yàng yit-sà ke kûng-kit, tân-thu̍k yit-sà tí-khong chhiu mò pá-ak. Sâm-kú ke sok-è mò án-kiak lâi-thôn.13 Tui kâm-ngiu̍k chhut-lòi ke heu-sâng ngìn yû khó-nèn cho vòng, chhut-sṳ̂n khiùng-khú ke ya yû khó-nèn cho vòng( 4 4:13-14 "chhut-sṳ̂n ... cho vòng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhut-sṳ̂n cho vòng ke ya yû khó-nèn pien khiùng-khú".); khiùng-khú chúng-he yû chṳ-fi ke heu-sâng ngìn, yàng-ko ngièn-ló ke sṳ̀ m̀-hén chiap-su khien-va ke ngong-tong vòng.14 併於上節15 Ngài fat-hien he̍t chhai liá sṳ-kiên, yû yit thai-khiùn ngìn thèn-tén ke( 5 4:15 "ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "nang-ngoi yit-ke".) heu-sâng ngìn fa̍t-thung; kì hong hí-lòi cho vòng, chiông khiu vòng von-thet.16 Liá-ke sîn vòng sûi-yèn yû phan-fap kón-lî chhin-tô ke ngìn-mìn; chúng-he heu-lòi ke ngìn hàn-he m̀ mân-yi kì ke thúng-chhṳ. Liá ya he khûng-hî, ya he chok-fûng.
