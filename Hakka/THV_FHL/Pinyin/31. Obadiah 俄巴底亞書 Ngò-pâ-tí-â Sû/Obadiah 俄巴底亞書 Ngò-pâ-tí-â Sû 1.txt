1 Song-chú Oi Chit-fa̍t Yî-tûng Hâ-poi he Ngò-pâ-tí-â só khon-tó ke yi-siong, he Chṳ-kô ke Song-chú lun Yî-tûng ke fa.Song-chú chhâi-phai sṳ́-chá to lie̍t-koet hi;ngài-têu thâng-tó sṳ́-chá só-chhòn ke sin-sit.Kì kóng: Hong hí-lòi ô!Ên-têu oi lâu Yî-tûng siông-chhṳ̀!2 Song-chú tui Yî-tûng kóng:Ngài oi sṳ́ ngì chhai lie̍t-koet chûng sàng-cho chui-ngio̍k ke,pûn-ngìn thai-thai khon-khiâng.3 Ngì kiêu-ngau ke sîm khî-phien chhṳ-kâ.Ngì ke kîn-sàng he ngàm-sa̍k hí ke sàng-pó;ngì he̍t chhai kô-sân táng,só-yî ngì chhiu tui chhṳ-kâ kóng:"Má-ngìn yû phan-fap chiông ngài lâi hâ-lòi?"4 Ngì sûi-yèn chhiong thai yeu-phò pî chhin-kô,cho-teu chhai sên-sṳ̀n kiên,Ngài ya oi chiông ngì lâi hâ-lòi.5 Chhe̍t-é lòi, ya-he khiòng-tho am-pû-thèu lòi,kì-têu voi nâ-chéu só-oi ke tûng-sî thin-thin;lòi chak phù-thò ke ngìn,kì-têu hàn-voi liù têu-é;chúng-he ngì ke sù-thi̍t voi chiông ngì chhióng-to chhiang-chhiang.6 Yî-so ke chṳ́-sûn â,ngì khong ke chhòi-pó yí-kîn pûn-ngìn sêu chhut-lòi chhióng-chéu!7 Lâu ngì kiet-yok ke chhiòn-phu chhut-mai ngì,chiông ngì kiuk-chhut chhṳ-kâ ke koet-kâ;lâu ngì siông-hó ke, liá-ha chṳ̂n-fu̍k ngì;sṳ̍t ngiâ fan ke ngìn, sat kî-koân ham-hoi ngì.Kì-têu chhṳ́-seu ngì kóng:"Ngì ke chhûng-mìn chhai nai-vi?"8 Song-chú kóng:Ngài chit-fa̍t Yî-tûng ke-ngit,Ngài oi chhù-thet kì-têu ke chhûng-mìn-ngìn,tui Yî-so-sân sêu-me̍t kì-têu ke chṳ-fi-ngìn.9 Thì-man ke chan-sṳ voi kiâng-to voi sí,Yî-so-sân ke ngìn chhiòn-phu voi pûn-ngìn chhṳ̀ chhiang-chhiang.10 Yî-tûng Su Chit-fa̍t ke Ngièn-yîn Yîn-vi ngì tui ngì ke hiûng-thi Ngâ-kok( 1 1:10 "ngì ke hiûng-thi Ngâ-kok": Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn he Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi, Yî-tûng-ngìn he Ngâ-kok ke â-kô Yî-so ke heu-thoi.) án chhàn-ngiûn,ngì voi pûn siû-yu̍k châ-tén, yún-yén pûn-ngìn sêu-me̍t.11 Thi̍t-ngìn ngi̍p Ngâ-kok ke sàng-mùn lòi chhióng kì ke sṳ̀,ngì khî chhai sùn-thèu khon nau-ngie̍t;ngoi-chhu̍k-ngìn ngiâm-khiêu lòi pûn tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng chhióng-tó ke chhòi-pó sṳ̀,ngì tông-chhiong he lâu kì-têu khiung-tóng ke ngìn.12 Ngì ke hiûng-thi tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi muk-chû kîm-kîm khon-tén kì-têu.Yù-thai-ngìn me̍t-mòng ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi chhiong tông fôn-hí.Kì-têu tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi kóng sâu-nat ke fa.13 Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi ngi̍p kì-têu ke sàng-mùn.Kì-têu tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi muk kîm-kîm khon kì-têu su-khú.Kì-têu tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,ngì mò yin-kôi chhióng kì-têu ke chhòi-sán.14 Ngì mò yin-kôi khî chhai chhâ-lu-héu,chok ke-têu thò-nan ke ngìn.Ngì mò yin-kôi chhai kì-têu tù-tó châi-nan ke-ngit,chiông chhûn-tó ke ngìn kâu-pûn sù-thi̍t.15 Song-ti Oi Sṳ́m-phan Van-koet Ngài — Song-chú sṳ́m-phan van-koet ke ngit-è voi to lé.Yî-tûng â, ngì voi cheu ngì só-cho ke tet-tó po-yin;ngì só-cho ke, yit-thin voi kûi-to ngì ke thèu-nà-táng.16 Chhiong Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn chhai Ngài ke sṳn-sân( 2 1:16 "Ngài ke sṳn-sân": Chṳ́ Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, Siak-ôn-sân.) lîm khú-pî,van-koet oi lîm hàn-kha khú ke pî;kì-têu oi lîm-liáu yu lîm, lîm-to châu-châu,yèn-heu kì-têu chhiu sêu-sṳt thet.17 Yî-set-lie̍t ke Tet-sṳn Chúng-he Siak-ôn sân-táng yit-thin voi yû-ngìn thò-nan chhut-lòi;liá-sân voi sàng-cho sṳn-sân.Ngâ-kok-kâ voi chhùng-sîn tet-tó ngièn-lòi ke sán-ngia̍p.18 Ngâ-kok-kâ voi sàng-cho thai-fó,Yok-sit-kâ voi sàng-cho fó-yam,kì-têu oi chiông Yî-so-kâ sêu-thet,chhiong fó sêu vò-kón.Yî-so ke heu-thoi mò liù pan-sà.Ngài he Song-chú, Ngài án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.19 Yù-thai nàm-phién ke ngìn oi cham-liâng Yî-so-sân;sî-phién sân-kiok hâ ke ngìn oi kûng-chhí Fî-li-sṳ.Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn oi cham-liâng Yî-fap-lièn lâu Sat-mâ-li-â ke thú-thi;Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn oi tet-tó Kî-lie̍t.20 Pet-koet Yî-set-lie̍t ke liù-mòng kiûn-chhui,oi chón-lòi chṳ̂n-fu̍k Fî-nì-kî, chhṳ̍t-to Sat-le̍t-fap;tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng liù-mòng to Sat-thi̍t-sṳ̂ ke ngìn,oi cham-liâng Yù-thai nàm-phu ke sàng-sṳ.21 Ke-têu tá-yàng ke ngìn oi sông-hi Siak-ôn-sân,thúng-chhṳ Yî-so-sân( 3 1:21 "sông-hi Siak-ôn-sân, thúng-chhṳ Yî-so-sân" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sông-hi Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, thúng-chhṳ Yî-tûng".).Song-chú chhṳ-kâ oi cho vòng.
