1 Hî-mong ke Fa Ngì-têu ke Song-ti kóng:Ngì-têu oi ôn-ví Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn,oi ôn-ví kì-têu.2 Oi kú-li Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke ngìn-mìn,lâu kì-têu kóng: Kì-têu ke khú-nan yí-kîn la lé;kì-têu ke chhui yí-kîn tet-tó sa-miên( 80 40:2 "kì-têu ke chhui yí-kîn tet-tó sa-miên" ya-he yi̍t-cho "kì-têu yí-kîn vi chhṳ-kâ ke hàng-vì su-tó po-yin lé".).Ngài — Song-chú yí-kîn kâ-phi chit-fa̍t kì-têu yit-chhiet ke chhui.3 Yû yit-ke sâng hêm-kóng:Oi chhai fông-phû vi Song-chú yi-phi tho-lu( 81 40:3 "Yû yit-ke sâng ... tho-lu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Chhai khóng-yâ yû ngìn hêm-kóng: Oi chún-phi hó-se Song-chú ke tho-lu".);oi chhai sâ-mo̍k vi ngài-têu ke Song-ti khôi thai-lu.4 Só-yû ke sân-lak chhiòn-phu oi thùn-mân;thai-se sân-kông chhiòn-phu oi siok-phiàng.Kô-tâi mò-phiàng ke thi oi sàng-cho phiàng-phiàng;sân-thi oi sàng-cho phìn-ngièn.5 Ke-sṳ̀, Song-chú ke yùng-kông oi hién-mìn chhut-lòi;chhiòn ngìn-lui yit-thin voi khiung-ha khon-tó.Yîn-vi Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.6 Yû yit-ke sâng hêm-kóng: Hi po sêu-sit!Ngài mun: Po má-ke sêu-sit?Oi siên-pu kóng: Tak-ke ngìn lûng-chúng chhiong chhó khiung-yong;kì-têu chhin-chiâng chhiong yâ-thi ke fâ.7 Song-chú chhôi yit-chhṳn fûng,chhó chhiu khû-châu, fâ ya chhia-thet.Ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai lâu fâ-chhó tông siông-chhiong;8 chhó voi châu-thet, fâ voi chhia-thet,chúng-he ngài-têu Song-ti ke fa yún-yén m̀-voi kói-pien!9 Siak-ôn nâ, oi sông kô-sân;oi po hó sêu-sit!Yâ-lu-sat-lâng â, oi thai-sâng hêm;oi po hó sêu-sit!( 82 40:9 "Siak-ôn nâ ... sêu-sit!" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Oi sông kô-sân, hiong Siak-ôn po hó sêu-sit! Oi thai-sâng hêm, hiong Yâ-lu-sat-lâng po hó sêu-sit!")Oi thai-sâng hêm, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng.Lâu Yù-thai ke thai-se chhûn-chông kóng:"Ngì-têu ke Song-ti voi lòi lé!"10 Khon nâ, Chṳ-kô ke Song-chú oi tai-tén khièn-piang lòi;Kì oi yung khièn-nèn chhṳ-lî thiên-ha.Kì oi sṳ̂-hàng po-yin;Kì oi chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu.11 Kì oi chhiong mu̍k-ngìn chóng yòng-khiùn;Kì oi chhi-si̍p se yòng-è,yung sûng-sú chiông kì-têu nám chhai hiûng-chhièn;Kì oi yung chhṳ̀-oi tai-liâng vi-nen ke yòng-mà.12 Mò Ngìn lâu Song-ti Pí É-tet Má-ngìn cho-tet yung sú pâ-chóng liòng hói-súi?Má-ngìn cho-tet yung sú-chṳ́ liòng kô-thiên?Má-ngìn cho-tet yung téu liòng thai-thi ke nài?Má-ngìn cho-tet yung thiên-phìn chhṳn sân-liâng?13 Má-ngìn thòn tet-chhut Song-chú ke sîm-yi?Má-ngìn yû chṳ̂-kiet kâu Kì? Chṳ́-thô Kì?14 Kám yû-ngìn sṳt kâu Kì, chṳ́-thô Kì,sṳ́ Kì tî kûng-chṳn ke lu,sṳ́ Kì tet-tó chṳ-sṳt,chṳ́-sṳ Kì thûng-tha̍t ke tho?15 Chhai Song-chú lòi-khon, lie̍t-koet he súi-thúng chûng ke yit-ti̍t súi,he thiên-phìn táng ke nài-fún;hói-tó he chhṳn-phàn táng ke chhṳ̀n-fôi thin-thin.16 Lì-pâ-nun ke su-nà m̀-la cho hien-chi ke chhèu;ke-vi ke chéu-chhu m̀-la cho sêu-fa-chi.17 Lie̍t-koet tui Kì lòi-kóng m̀-son má-ke,chhiong mò chhùn-chhai ke tûng-sî, chhiu-he hî-khûng.18 Má-ngìn lâu Song-ti pí é-tet?Ngì-têu oi yung má-ke thoi-péu Kì ke hìn-siong?19 Kì mò chhiong ngiéu-siong;sṳ̂-fu chho-hó ngiéu-siong heu,yu vi kì pâu kîm-sṳ̂n,yèn-heu koa kí-thiàu ngiùn-lien.20 Ke mâi m̀-hí kîm-ngiùn ke ngìnchhiu yung m̀-voi mut-thet ke su-thèu.Kì chhìm-lòi yit-ke sú-ngi tông-hó ke sṳ̂-fu,tiâu-khiet yit-ke m̀-voi vang-thet ke ngiéu-siong.21 Ngì-têu kám m̀-tî? Ngì-têu kám mò thâng-tó?M̀-he yû-ngìn chó chhiu lâu ngì-têu kóng-ko?Ngì-têu kám m̀-tî liá sṳ-kie he ngióng-pân khôi-sṳ́?22 Song-ti chhô chhai thai-thi ke kiûng-chhông táng-kô;chhai Kì lòi-khon, thi-song ke ngìn chhiong chhó-máng yit-yong.Kì hiên-khôi thiên chhiong tá-khôi pu-liàm,chhiong chán-khôi ngìn só-he̍t ke chong-phùng.23 Kì sêu-me̍t yû khièn-sṳ ke kiûn-vòng,sṳ́ thi-song ke sṳ́m-phan-kôn pien mò thet-hi.24 Kì-têu chhiong hàn tông se-thèu ke chhṳ̍t-vu̍t,chang fat-ngà sâng-kîn.Song-chú chhôi yit-chhṳn fûng, kì-têu chhiu chhiòn-phu khû-châu;thai-fûng yit-kién, kì-têu chhiu-chhiong vò-kón pûn fûng chhôi-san.25 Má-ngìn cho-tet lâu sṳ̀n-sṳn ke Song-ti pí nè?Sṳ-kiên kám yû-ngìn chhiong Kì mò?26 Ngì-têu ngo-thèu khon thiên-táng!Má-ngìn chhóng-chho liá-têu sên-sṳ̀n?He Song-ti! Kì tai-liâng kì-têu chhiong tai-liâng kiûn-chhui.Kì tî kì-têu ke su-muk;Kì yit-ke yit-ke tiám-miàng.Kì ke khièn-nèn khi̍t-thai mò-pí,lièn yit-ke ya m̀-voi m̀ kien-thet.27 Yî-set-lie̍t â, ngì cho-má-ke koai-yen:Láu-tó Song-chú m̀-tî ngì ke kân-khú?Ngin-vì Song-ti mò koân-sîm ngì só-su ke yên-vóng?28 Ngì kám m̀-tî? Ngì kám mò thâng-kóng?Song-chú he yún-sên ke Song-ti!Kì chhóng-chho chhiòn sṳ-kie.Kì m̀-voi khioi, ya m̀-voi thiám;Kì ke chṳ-fi kô-chhṳ̂m, ngìn mò-fap liáu-kié.29 Khioi ke ngìn, Kì su li̍t-liong pûn kì-têu;thiám ke ngìn, Kì su hi-li̍t pûn kì-têu.30 Heu-sâng ngìn voi khioi ya voi thiám;khiòng-chong ke chhiâng-ngièn ya voi tiet-tó vang hâ-hi.31 Chúng-he yí-kho Song-chú ke ngìn,voi chhùng-sîn tet-tó li̍t-liong.Kì-têu voi chhiong ên-tiâu tá-khôi yi̍t-kap pî-to kô-kô;kì-têu chéu m̀-voi khioi,hàng ya m̀-voi thiám.
