1 Song-chú ke Phu̍k-ngìn Song-chú kóng:Khon nâ, Ngài ke phu̍k-ngìn, Ngài fù-chhṳ̀ kì;Ngài kién-sién kì, kì he Ngài sîm só fôn-hí ke.Ngài chiông Ngài ke Lìn su-pûn kì;kì oi vi van-koet tai-lòi chṳn-ngi.2 Kì m̀-voi chhin-hêm, ya m̀-voi chhàu-nau;kì m̀-voi chhai kiê-hong thai-sâng kóng-fa.3 Chak-fái ke lù-chuk, kì m̀-voi chiông kì áu-thôn;voi vû ke tên-fó, kì m̀-voi chiông kì phùn-thet.Kì oi chûng-sîm chiông chṳn-ngi tai-lòi.4 Kì m̀-voi sṳt-mong ya m̀-voi sṳt-chṳ;kì oi chhai thi-song kien-li̍p chṳn-ngi;hói-tó ke ngìn lûng-chúng ngióng-mong kì ke fap-li̍t.5 Song-ti chhóng-chho chû-thiên, phû-sat kiûng-chhông;Kì chho thai-thi lâu thi-song só-yû ke sâng-vu̍t;Kì su-pûn ngìn sâng-miang, sṳ́ ngìn voi théu-hi.Chú Song-ti hiong Kì ke phu̍k-ngìn án-ngiòng siên-pu:6 Ngài — Song-chú cheu kûng-ngi lòi fû-seu ngì;Ngài voi khiên-tén ngì ke sú, pó-sú ngì.Ngài oi thûng-ko ngì lâu sṳ-kiên-ngìn li̍p-yok( 86 42:6 "Ngài oi ... li̍p-yok" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Ngài oi sṳ́ ngì cho sṳ-kiên-ngìn ke chûng-pó".);Ngài oi sṳ́ ngì sàng-cho van-koet ke kông.7 Ngì oi hi khôi mô-muk ke muk-chû,thu chhô-kâm ke ngìn chhut kâm-ngiu̍k,sṳ́ vû-am thi-lò chûng ke ngìn tet chhṳ-yù.8 Ngài he Song-chú, liá he Ngài ke miàng.Ngài thin-chho̍k m̀ chiông Ngài ke yùng-yeu kûi-pûn ká sṳ̀n,ya m̀ chiông oi pûn Ngài ke chhṳ̂n-chan kûi-pûn tiâu-khiet ke ngiéu-siong.9 Khon nâ, Ngài só yi-ngièn ke sṳ yí-kîn sṳ̍t-hien.Liá-ha Ngài oi lâu ngì-têu kóng-têu sîn ke sṳ,chhai sṳ fat-sên yî-chhièn, Ngài siên lâu ngì-têu kóng-mìn.10 Chan-mî ke Sṳ̂-kô Oi hiong Song-chú chhong yit-sú sîn-kô;chhiòn sṳ-kie oi siung-chan Kì!Thai-hói lâu khì-chûng ke sâng-vu̍t chhiòn-phu oi chan-mî Kì;hói-tó lâu tó-song ke ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu oi siung-yòng Kì!11 Sâ-mo̍k lâu khì-chûng ke sàng-sṳ chhiòn-phu oi thai-sâng chhong-kô;Kî-tha̍t ke ngìn-mìn oi chan-mî Kì;he̍t chhai Sî-lâ ke ngìn chhiòn-phu oi fôn-fû,chhai sân-táng thai-sâng hêm.12 Hói-tó ke ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu oi yùng-kông Song-chú;kì-têu oi siung-chan Kì!13 Song-chú chhiong yúng-sṳ chhut-hi siông-chhṳ̀;Kì kiûn-chông chok-hó, kit-thung ngie̍t-sîm hi.Kì thai-sâng chhin-hêm, chhut-chan kûng-kit thi̍t-ngìn.14 Song-chú Yin-hí Pông-chhu Kì ke Chṳ́-mìn Song-ti kóng:Ngài yí-kîn chhin-kiú mò chhut-sâng,tiâm-tiâm mò-tap Ngài ke chṳ́-mìn.Liá-ha Ngài oi hàng-thung lé,chhiong kiung-chṳ́ ke fu-ngìn-kâ chhin-miang-é hêm.15 Ngài oi sṳ́ thai-se sân-kông fông-fi,sṳ́ chhó-muk khû-châu.Ngài oi sṳ́ hò-pa pien-cho sâ-phû( 87 42:15 "sâ-phû" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "hói-tó".),sṳ́ pî-thòng châu-thet.16 Ngài oi thu mô-muk ke chṳ́-mìn,hàng m̀-sṳt hàng ke lu;Ngài oi sṳ́ kì-têu mien-chhièn ke vû-am pien-cho kông-mìn,sṳ́ vân-vat ke lu pien phiàng-phiàng ke thai-lu.Ngài oi sṳ̍t-hien liá-têu sṳ, chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi vok-thet kì-têu.17 Ke-têu siông-sin ká sṳ̀n ke ngìn yit-thin voi tá-sû;yí-kho ngiéu-siong ke ngìn yit-thin voi tù-tó kien-seu.18 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn M̀ Su-kau Song-chú kóng:Ngì-têu ngí-lûng ke â, oi thâng!Ngì-têu chhiâng-miâng ke â, oi khon!19 Sṳ-song yû má-ngìn pí Ngài ke phu̍k-ngìn hàn-kha chhiâng-miâng?Yû má-ngìn pí Ngài ke sṳ́-chá hàn-kha ngí-lûng?To-tái yû má-ngìn pí Ngài kién-sién ke chṳ́-mìn, Song-chú ke phu̍k-ngìn hàn-kha chhiâng-miâng?20 Yî-set-lie̍t â, ngì khon-ko chhin-tô, chúng-he khon m̀ chhîn-chhú;ngì thâng-ko chhin-tô, chúng-he thâng mò liáu-kié.21 Song-chú yit-sîm oi sṳ̂n-chông chṳn-ngi;Kì fôn-hí sṳ́ fap-li̍t tet-tó chûn-chhung, fat-yòng kông-thai.22 Chúng-he Kì ke chṳ́-mìn pûn-ngìn chhióng-kiap;kì-têu pûn-ngìn koân chhai thi-lò tú,pûn-ngìn khong chhai kâm-ngiu̍k chûng.Kì-têu pûn-ngìn chhióng, mò-ngìn kiu;kì-têu pûn-ngìn chok-chéu, mò-ngìn piong kì-têu chón-lòi.23 Ngì-têu tông-chûng yû-ngìn oi thâng liá-têu fa mò?Chhiùng-kîm yî-heu voi chu-yi thâng mò?24 He má-ngìn chiông Yî-set-lie̍t kâu-pûn chhîm-lio̍k-chá?He má-ngìn chiông Ngâ-kok kâu-pûn chhióng-kiap ke ngìn?Kám m̀-he Song-chú, yîn-vi ngài-têu chhiòn-phu tet-chhui Kì.Ngài-têu m̀-hén hàng Kì ke tho-lu;ngài-têu mò chûn-sú Kì ke fap-li̍t.25 Só-yî, Kì hiong ngài-têu thai fat-khién,sṳ́ ngài-têu su-tó chan-chên ke thung-khú.Chan-chên vì-khun ngài-têu, ngài-têu hàn-he mò ho̍k-tó kau-hiun;lie̍t-fó sêu-tó ngài-têu, ngài-têu hàn-he mò pan-tiám kám-kok.
