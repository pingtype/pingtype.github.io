1 Pâ-pí-lùn Lòi ke Sṳ́-chá( Lie̍t-vòng Ki-ha 20:12-19 ) Liá-sṳ̀, Pâ-lâ-tan ke lai-é Pâ-pí-lùn-vòng Mí-lò-tha̍t-Pâ-lâ-tan thâng-tó Hî-sî-kâ-vòng pot-phiang, yu fì-fu̍k khien-không, thi̍t-phe̍t sung-lòi mun-ôn sin lâu lî-vu̍t.2 Hî-sî-kâ fôn-ngiàng sṳ́-chá, yu hiong kì-têu tién kì ke chhòi-fu — kîm, ngiùn, hiông-liau, hiông-súi lâu só-yû ke kiûn-phi; kì chiông chhiòn-koet tak só-chhai ke pó-khu lâu kì vòng-kiûng tú só-yû ke chhiòn-phu pûn kì-têu khon.3 Sṳ-heu, siên-tî Yî-soi-â hi kien Hî-sî-kâ-vòng, mun kì: "Liá-têu ngìn tui nai-vi lòi? Kì-têu lâu ngì kóng má-ke?"Hî-sî-kâ en-kóng: "Kì-têu he tui yit-ke tông-yén ke koet-kâ Pâ-pí-lùn lòi kien ngài."4 Yî-soi-â mun: "Kì-têu chhai vòng-kiûng tú khon-tó má-ke?"Hî-sî-kâ en-kóng: "Ngài só-yû ke, kì-têu chhiòn-phu khon-tó; ngài chiông pó-khu ke kok-yong tûng-sî chhiòn-phu pûn kì-têu khon."5 Só-yî, Yî-soi-â tui Hî-sî-kâ kóng: "Ngì oi thâng Song-chú — Van-kiûn Ngièn-soi ke fa:6 ‘Sṳ̀-kiên yit-thin voi to, ngì vòng-kiûng tú só-yû ke tûng-sî, lâu ngiâ chú-siên liù-hâ lòi ke tak-hong tûng-sî, chhiòn-phu voi pûn-ngìn pân-to Pâ-pí-lùn hi, mò liù pan-hong.’ Liá he Song-chú kóng ke.7 ‘Lièn ngì ke heu-thoi yû-têu ya voi pûn-ngìn chok-hi, chhai Pâ-pí-lùn vòng-kiûng tú cho thai-kam.’ "8 Hî-sî-kâ sióng, liá yi-ngièn he kóng-tó chhai kì hàn-sâng ke ngit-è, kì hàn cho-tet hióng-su fò-phìn lâu ôn-chhiòn, só-yî kì en Yî-soi-â kóng: "Ngì só-chhòn Song-chú ke fa tông-hó!"
