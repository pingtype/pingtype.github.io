1 Song-chú Oi Chit-fa̍t Thai-thi Song-chú oi fí-me̍t thai-thi, sṳ́ kì fông-liòng. Kì oi chiông thú-thi pién-chón ko-lòi, chiông ngìn-mìn fûn-san chhut-hi.2 Mò-lun ngìn-mìn ya-he chi-sṳ̂, nù-chhòi ya-he chú-ngìn, mâi ke ya-he mai ke, chai-chú ya-he khiam-chai ke, khiùng-khú ke ya-he fu-chiuk ke, chhiòn-phu voi su-tó khiung-yong ke miang-yun.3 Thú-thi fông-liòng, yit-si̍t-é chhut-sán ya mò. Song-chú án-ngiòng siên-pu lé.4 Thú-thi pien chhin-chheu chhin-châu; chhiòn sṳ-kie phai-fái thet-hi; lièn sṳ-song chhô thai-vi ke ngìn ya pien fú-phai.5 Thi-song ke ngìn m̀ chûn-sú fap-li̍t, vì-fán Song-ti só-li̍p yún-yén ke yok, tá-me̍t kì-têu só-he̍t ke thú-thi.6 Só-yî, Song-ti chu-chú thú-thi, sṳ́ kì phai-fái. Ngìn-mìn fu-chhut kì-têu só-cho só-hàng ke thoi-ka. Thi-song ke ngìn chhiong pûn-fó sêu, chhûn hâ-lòi ke ngìn ye̍t-lòi ye̍t-séu lé.7 Phù-thò thèn khû-châu, sîn-chiú ya chhin-séu. Sîm-chûng fôn-lo̍k pien-cho pî-sông;8 khìm-sâng, kú-sâng chhiòn-phu thìn-thet; fôn-lo̍k ke sâng-yîm ya mò lé.9 Mò-ngìn chai lîm-chiú chok-lo̍k, mò-ngìn sòng tet-chhut chiú ke kâm-thiàm.10 Sàng-tú yit-phién fun-lon, kâ-kâ fu-fu mùn só-to hèn-hèn, kiâng ngìn thêu ngi̍p-hi.11 Yû-ngìn chhai kiê-hong chhàu oi lîm-chiú; yit-chhiet ke fôn-lo̍k mò-thet hi, thi-song mò má-ke sên-chhi.12 Sàng-tú fông-liòng, sàng-mùn vàn-chhiòn fí-fái.13 Sṳ-song lie̍t-koet chhiòn-phu voi yû khiung-yong ke châi-nan, chhiu-chhiong sû-sṳ̀n heu, kám-lám su-táng mò kám-lám, phù-thò thèn-hong mò phù-thò.14 Châi-nan ko-heu hàn-sâng ke ngìn voi fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k chhong-kô. Sî-fông ke ngìn oi chhṳ̂n-chan Song-chú ke ví-thai;15 tûng-fông ke ngìn oi siung-chan Kì; he̍t chhai hói-tó ke ngìn ya oi chan-mî Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti.16 Tui thiên-ngài hói-kok ngài-têu thâng-tó yû-ngìn chhong-kô, kóng: "Yùng-yeu kûi-pûn kûng-ngi ke Song-chú( 54 24:16 "kûng-ngi ke Song-chú" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "kûng-ngi chá".)."Chúng-he ngài ngin-vì ngài voi yû châi-nan lé, ngài fái lé! Ngài chhám lé! Kúi-cha ke ngìn kín-lòi kín kúi-cha; kì-têu ke hàng-vì tet-ngìn-kiâng to chṳ-khi̍t.17 Thi-song ke ngìn-mìn â, yû châi-nan, ham-chiáng, lò-mióng tén-tén ngì-têu.18 Sám-ko châi-nan ke ngìn voi tiet-lo̍k ham-chiáng; phà-chhut ham-chiáng ke ngìn voi pûn lò-mióng chhàn-tó; yîn-vi thiên-táng ke súi-mùn chhiòn tá khôi-lòi, thi ke kîn-kî ya kín-chûn.19 Thi-mien voi lie̍t-khôi, sui-thet, pên-thet.20 Thai-thi voi yèu-thung, chhiong lîm chiú-chui ke ngìn vang-lòi vang-hi, chhiong fûng-chhâi chûng ke màu-liàu yèu-yèu pài-pài. Thai-thi pûn chhûng-chhui chak-to lap-thet, yún-yén m̀-voi chai khî hí-lòi.21 To ke-ngit, Song-chú oi chit-fa̍t thiên-kie ke chóng-khièn-chá lâu thi-song ke lie̍t-vòng.22 Kì-têu voi chhiong fam-ngìn án-ngiòng pûn-ngìn ham ko-lòi, kiuk-ngi̍p thi-lò. Song-chú oi chiông kì-têu koân chhai ke-vi, tén-to chit-fa̍t ke sṳ̀-chiet lòi-to.23 Ngie̍t-kông voi kien-seu to m̀-voi fat-kông, ngit-thèu voi fái-se to vû-thet; yîn-vi Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi voi chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, chhai Siak-ôn sân-táng chóng-khièn; mìn-kiên ke liâng-chhiu chhiòn-phu voi khon-tó Kì ke yùng-kông.
