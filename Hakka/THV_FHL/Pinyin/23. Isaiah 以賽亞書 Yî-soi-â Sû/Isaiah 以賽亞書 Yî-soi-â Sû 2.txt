1 Yún-yén ke Fò-phìn( Mì-kâ Sû 4:1-3 ) Hâ-poi he Song-ti pûn Â-mô-sṳ̂ ke lai-é Yî-soi-â ke yi-siong, he yû-koân Yù-thai lâu Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke sin-sit.2 To chui-mî ke ngit-è,Song-chú sṳn-thien ke sân thin-chho̍k voi kien-li̍p chhai khiùn-sân ke táng-kô,kô-kô chhai-song, chhêu-ko chung-sân.Van-koet chhiòn-phu voi liù hiong liá sân.3 Chhin-tô mìn-chhu̍k voi chhi-si̍p chhièn-lòi, kóng:Lòi â, ngài-têu khiung-ha sông-hi Song-chú ke sân( 4 2:3 "Song-chú ke sân" chṳ́ Siak-ôn-sân, liá sân he Yâ-lu-sat-lâng-sàng ke yit phu-fun, sṳn-thien chhai liá sân-táng.),khiung-ha lòi-hi Ngâ-kok Song-ti ke sṳn-thien.Kì voi chṳ́-kau ngài-têu yin-kôi hàng ke tho;ngài-têu ya oi hàng Kì só chṳ́-sṳ ke lu.Song-chú ke kau-hiun tui Siak-ôn fat-chhut;Kì ke sin-sit tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng chhòn chhut-lòi.4 Kì voi kié-kiet koet-chi kiên ke kiu-fun,thiàu-kié mìn-chhu̍k kiên ke chên-tôn.Kì-têu voi chiông tô-kiam tá-cho lài-thèu,chiông màu-chhiông tá-cho liàm-è.Koet-chi kiên m̀-voi yû siông-chhṳ̀,kì-têu ya m̀-sṳ́ chai ho̍k-si̍p chan-chên.5 Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi â! Lòi â, ngài-têu chhai Song-chú ke kông-mìn chûng hàng! Kiêu-ngau ke Thin-chho̍k Voi Sṳt-phai6 Song-ti â, Ngì yí-kîn fit-thet Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn — Ngâ-kok ke heu-thoi; yîn-vi koet-nui tak só-chhai yû tui tûng-fông lâu Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn lòi ke sià-su̍t; ngìn-mìn chhiòn-phu thèn ngoi-chhu̍k ke fûng-siu̍k.7 Kì-têu koet-nui kîm-ngiùn mân-thi, chhòi-pó tô-to son m̀-chhut; kì-têu kin-nui mân-phien he chan-mâ, hàn-yû son m̀-chhin ke chan-chhâ.8 Kì-têu ke só-chhai chhûng-mân ngiéu-siong; kì-têu pai chhṳ-kâ yung-sú tiâu-khiet ke tûng-sî.9 Pî-chhien ngìn khúi-pai, chûn-kui ngìn ya khúi-hâ, chhṳ-ngien phai-fái. Song-chú â, khiù Ngì m̀-hó sa-miên kì-têu!10 Kì-têu yin-tông oi sám-to ngàm-sa̍k hâ, khong-to thi-lùng tú, sióng-oi sám Song-chú ke fat-khién lâu Kì vî-ngiàm ke yùng-kông.11 Ngit-è chiông-to, kiêu-ngau ke ngìn voi su ap-chṳ, chhṳ-thai ke ngìn voi kong-cho pî-chhien; chṳ́-yû Song-chú su chûn-kin.12 Chhai ke-ngit, Song-chú — Van-kiûn ke Ngièn-soi oi chiông yû khièn-sṳ, chhṳ-khoâ lâu kiêu-ngau ke ngìn kong-cho pî-chhien.13 Kì oi chám-thet Lì-pâ-nun kô-thai ke hiông-pak-su, fí-thet Pâ-sân ke siong-su;14 siok-phiàng thai-se sân-kông,15 súng-vang kô-thap lâu kiên-ku ke sàng-chhiòng;16 chhṳ̀m-thet Thâ-sṳ̂ chhin-chiâng ke thai-sòn.17 Chhai ke-ngit, kiêu-ngau ke ngìn voi su ap-chṳ, chhṳ-thai ke ngìn voi kong-cho pî-chhien, chṳ́-yû Song-chú su chûn-kin.18 Ngiéu-siong ya voi pûn Kì vàn-chhiòn chhù-thet.19 Song-chú yèu-thung thi-kî ke sṳ̀, ngìn oi sám-to ngàm-sa̍k hâ, khong-to thi-lùng tú, sióng-oi sám Song-chú ke fat-khién lâu Kì vî-ngiàm ke yùng-kông.20 To ke-ngit, kì-têu oi te̍p-thet chhṳ-kâ chṳ-chho ke kîm-ngiùn ngiéu-siong, fit-pûn thièn-chhú lâu phi̍t-phò-è.21 Song-chú yèu-thung thi-kî ke sṳ̀, ngìn oi khong chhai sân-lùng ya-he ngàm-sa̍k ke phung-tú, sióng-oi sám Song-chú ke fat-khién lâu Kì vî-ngiàm ke yùng-kông.22 M̀-hó vá-kho sṳ-kiên ngìn, yîn-vi kì-têu voi sí, kì-têu son má-ke nè?
