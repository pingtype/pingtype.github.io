1 Song-chú sṳ́ Kì ke Chṳ́-mìn Tet-sṳn Ke-ngit, ngìn-mìn oi chhai Yù-thai-thi chhong liá-sú kô:Ngài-têu ke sàng-chhiòng ngióng-pân kiên-ku!Song-ti chhîn-chhṳ pó-fu liá-ke sàng!2 Sàng-mùn tá khôi-lòi!Pûn chṳn-ngi ke koet-kâ ngi̍p-lòi!Pûn chûng-sin ke ngìn-mìn lo̍k-lòi!3 Song-chú â, Ngì chiông vàn-chhiòn ke phìn-ônsu-pûn sîm-yi kiên-khiòng ke ngìn,su-pûn sin-kho Ngì ke ngìn.4 Oi yún-yén sin-kho Song-chú,yîn-vi Song-chú he yún-kiú ke phàn-sa̍k.5 Kì sṳ́ kiêu-ngau ke ngìn khiâm-pî;Kì pho-fái kiên-ku ke sàng-sṳ, chhak-thet sàng-chhiòng,chhak-to phiàng-phiàng, sàng-cho nài-fún.6 Pûn-ngìn khî-fu ke voi khî chhai chôn-chiet tôi táng-kô;khiùng-khú ke ngìn voi chien-tha̍p vang hâ-lòi ke sàng-chhiòng.7 Kûng-ngi ke Song-chú â,Ngì sṳ́ kûng-phìn ke ngìn hàng phiàng-lu;Ngì sṳ́ chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn hàng chhṳ̍t-lu.8 Song-chú â, ngài-têu tén-heu Ngì, sun-chhiùng Ngì ke chṳ́-yi;Ngì ke miàng lâu miàng-mong he ngài-têu sîm-lìn ke ngien-mong.9 Chhai am-pû-sṳ̀ ngài hiûn-mu Ngì,ngài chhiòn-sîm chhiòn-yi chhìm-khiù Ngì.Chhai Ngì sṳ́m-phan sṳ-kie ke sṳ̀-chiet,thi-song ke van-mìn chhiu ho̍k-si̍p chṳn-ngi.10 Sûi-yèn Ngì yung yìn-chhṳ̀ tui-thai fái-ngìn,kì-têu hàn-he m̀-hén ho̍k-cho san-sṳ;chhai kûng-ngi ke thi kì-têu hàng-sṳ put-ngi,kì-têu chhiòn-mò chu-yi tó Ngì ke vî-ngiàm.11 Song-chú â, Ngì ke sù-thi̍t m̀-tî Ngì oi chit-fa̍t kì-têu.Pûn kì-têu khon-tó Ngì án ngie̍t-sîm siak Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn lòi kám-kok kien-seu;pûn kì-têu su-tó Ngì chó chhiu vi Ngì thi̍t-ngìn chún-phi hó ke chit-fa̍t.12 Song-chú â, khiù Ngì sṳ́ ngài-têu hîn-vong;ngài-têu ke sṳ̀n-chhiu chhiòn-phu he Ngì sóng-su ke.13 Song-chú — ngài-têu ke Song-ti â,ngài-têu sṳt pûn phe̍t ke chú kón-chṳ;chúng-he Ngì chang-he ngài-têu ke Chú.14 Kì-têu yí-kîn sí-thet, pien-cho yîm-fùn lé, mò khó-nèn fán-sâng;kì-têu pûn Ngì chit-fa̍t, vàn-chhiòn sêu-me̍t, m̀-voi yû-ngìn ki-ngiam.15 Song-chú â, Ngì sṳ́ ngài-têu ke koet-kâ hîn-vong,sṳ́ ngài-têu ke koet-mìn kín tô, thú-thi kín fat,yîn-vi án-ngiòng, Ngì ke miàng chhiu tet-tó yùng-kông.16 Song-chú â, Ngì sṳt chit-fa̍t Ngì ke chṳ́-mìn;chhai khú-nan chûng kì-têu chhiòn-sîm khiù-ham Ngì( 56 26:16 "chhiòn-sîm khiù-ham Ngì" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).17 Song-chú â, Ngì sṳ́ ngài-têu thai-sâng chhin-hêm, chhin-sê,chhiong sên-sán ke fu-ngìn-kâ chhai chhṳn-thung chûng kín-chhên.18 Ngài-têu sṳt chhiong sên-sán án-ngiòng chhin-hêm, chhin-sê,só kiung ke put-ko he fûng thin-thin.Ngài-têu m̀-sṳt vi koet-kâ tá-yàng chan-chên;ngài-têu m̀-sṳt sṳ́ sṳ-kiên ngìn sṳt-lo̍k tiet-tó( 57 26:18 "ngài-têu m̀-sṳt sṳ́ ... tiet-tó" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ngài-têu m̀-sṳt hiong sṳ-kiên ngìn kung-hien má-ke", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).19 Chúng-he ngài-têu sí-thet ke thùng-pâu voi fu̍k-fa̍t!Kì-têu ke sṳ̂n-thí voi chai tet-tó sâng-miang!Soi chhai fùn-mu chûng ke ngìn â, oi fán-sâng thai-sâng fôn-chhong!Chhiu-chhiong thi-song chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu ke lu-súi tai-lòi sâng-miang,Song-chú oi pûn sí-thet ke ngìn chai fán-sâng ko-lòi.20 Sṳ́m-phan lâu Fu̍k-hîn Ngài ke thùng-pâu, ngi̍p ngì-têu ke vuk-tú hi! Mùn koân-hó, chhiam-sṳ̀ khong hí-lòi, chhṳ̍t-to Song-ti ke nu-hi sêu-thet.21 Song-chú oi tui Kì só he̍t ke thiên-táng kong-lìm, chit-fa̍t sṳ-kiên ngìn ke chhui. Ngìn-kiên ke hiûng-sat chhiòn-phu voi hién-lu chhut-lòi; thi mien-song m̀-voi chai khong-tén pûn-ngìn chhṳ̀-sí ke sṳ̂-thí.
