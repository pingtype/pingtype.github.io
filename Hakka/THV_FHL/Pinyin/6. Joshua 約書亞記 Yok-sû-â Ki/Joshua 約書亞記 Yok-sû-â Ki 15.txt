1 Yù-thai Kî-chhu̍k Pûn-tó ke Thú-thi Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k ke-têu kâ-chhu̍k só pûn-tó ke thú-thi ki chhai hâ-poi:Kì-têu ke thú-thi hiong-nàm chhṳ̍t-to Yî-tûng piên-kie, to Chhìm ke khóng-yâ chui nàm-phién.2 Liá nàm-phién thi-kie he tui Sí-hói nàm-phién khôi-sṳ́,3 hiong-nàm tui Â-khiet-lâ-pîn lu-héu to Chhìm, yu tui Kâ-tâi-sṳ̂-Pâ-nì-â nàm-fông hiong-pet, kîn-ko Hî-sṳ̂-lùn, chhṳ̍t-to Â-tha̍t-ngí, chai chón-hiong Kap-kâ,4 chhṳ̍t-to Ap-mùn, chai sun Âi-khi̍p piên-kin ke hò-pa to Thi-chûng-hói. Liá chhiu-he Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k nàm-phién ke thi-kie.5 Tûng-phién ke thi-kie he Sí-hói, hiong-song to Yok-tan-hò liù-to Sí-hói ke ngi̍p-héu.Pet-phién ke thi-kie tui ke ngi̍p-héu khôi-sṳ́,6 chhṳ̍t-to Pak-Hot-lâ, theu-to ke voi khon-tó Yok-tan-hò hò-sùn phìn-ngièn ke sân-liâng pet-phién, hiong-pet to Pô-hón-sa̍k (Pô-hón he Lî-phien ke lai-é),7 yu tui Â-kot sân-lak to Tí-piak, hiong-pet to Kit-kap (Kit-kap mien-tui hò-kuk nàm-phién ke Â-tû-mìn lu-héu). Yèn-heu thi-kie theu-to Yún-Sṳ-ma̍k, thûng-to Yún-Lò-kiet,8 sun Hiûn-nun-chṳ́ sân-lak to sân nàm-phién ke Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-sàng (chhiu-he Yâ-lu-sat-lâng), tui liá-vi sông-to Hiûn-nun sân-lak sî-phién ke sân-táng, chhiu-he Li-fa̍t-yîm sân-lak chui pet-phién,9 yu theu-to Nì-fut-tô-â-chhièn, to Yî-fut-lùn-sân fu-khiun ke sàng-chṳ́n, yèn-heu chón-hiong Pâ-lâ (yu ôn-to Kî-lie̍t-Yâ-lìm),10 lit-ko Pâ-lâ sî-phién to Yî-tûng sân-khî, lièn-to Yâ-lìm-sân pet-phién (yu ôn-to Kî-sat-lùn-sân), hiong-hâ to Pak-Sṳ-ma̍k, ko Thìn-nâ,11 yèn-heu hiong Yî-kiet-lùn pet-phién ke sân, chai chón-hiong Sṳ̂-kî-lùn, kîn-ko Pâ-lâ-sân to Ngâ-pí-ngiap, chhṳ̍t-to Thi-chûng-hói.12 Thi-chûng-hói chhiu-he Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k sî-phién ke piên-kie.Yù-thai ke-têu kâ-chhu̍k ke ngìn chhiu-he he̍t chhai liá thi-khî.13 Kâ-le̍t Cham-liâng Hî-pak-lùn lâu Tí-piak( Sṳ-sṳ̂ Ki 1:11-15 ) Yok-sû-â chûn-cheu Song-chú ke min-lin chiông Yù-thai yit phu-fun thú-thi pûn-pûn Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k Yâ-fù-nì ke lai-é Kâ-le̍t. Kâ-le̍t tet-tó Hî-pak-lùn, chhiu-he su̍k Â-na̍p kiâ-pâ ke Â-pâ-sàng.14 Kâ-le̍t chiông Â-na̍p sâm-ke lai-é ke heu-thoi kiuk-chhut sàng; chhiu-he Sṳ-chhî-chhu̍k, Â-hî-man-chhu̍k, lâu Tha̍t-mâi-chhu̍k.15 Kâ-le̍t yu tui ke-vi kûng-tá Tí-piak ke ngìn-mìn. Liá-sàng yî-chhièn ôn-to Kî-lie̍t-Sî-fut.16 Kâ-le̍t kóng: "Má-ngìn cham-liâng Kî-lie̍t-Sî-fut, ngài chhiu chiông ngài ke moi-é Ap-sat ka-pûn kì!"17 Kâ-le̍t ke ló-thâi Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂ ke lai-é Ngò-thò-ngiap cham-liâng ke-sàng; só-yî Kâ-le̍t chiông moi-é Ap-sat pûn kì cho pû-ngiòng.18 Kiet-fûn ke-ngit, Ngò-thò-ngiap sô-súng Ap-sat( 4 15:18 "Ngò-thò-ngiap sô-súng Ap-sat" he kîn-kí yit-pún kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Ap-sat sô-súng Ngò-thò-ngiap".) hi hiong kiâ-pâ thó yit-te thièn-thi. Ap-sat yit hâ lì-è, Kâ-le̍t chhiu mun kì oi má-ke,19 kì kóng: "Khiù ngì sung ngài yit-ke lî-vu̍t; yîn-vi ngì pûn ngài ôn-kî chhai châu-thi, khiù ngì ya pûn ngài chhièn-súi." Só-yî Kâ-le̍t chiông song chhièn-súi lâu ha chhièn-súi chhiòn-phu pûn kì. Yù-thai Kî-chhu̍k ke Sàng-chṳ́n20 Yî-ha he Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k ke-têu kâ-chhu̍k tet-tó ke sán-ngia̍p.21 Chui nàm-phién vá-khiun Yî-tûng piên-kin ke sàng-chṳ́n yû: Kap-siet, Yî-tet, Ngâ-kû-ngí,22 Kî-nâ, Tí-mô-nâ, Â-thai-tha̍t,23 Kî-tâi-sṳ̂, Ha-só, Yî-thì-nàm,24 Sî-fut, Thì-lien, Pí-â-liu̍k,25 Ha-só-Ha̍p-thai-thâ, Kâ-lio̍k-Hî-sṳ̂-lùn (chhiu-he Ha-só),26 Â-man, Sṳ-mâ, Mô-lâ-thai,27 Ha̍p-sat-Kâ-thai, Het-sṳ̍t-mùn, Pak-Pha-lie̍t,28 Ha̍p-sat-Sû-â, Phe̍t-sṳ-pâ, Pí-sṳ̂-yok-thâ,29 Pâ-lâ, Yî-yîn, Yî-sêm,30 Yî-le̍t-tô-la̍p, Kî-sṳt, Hò-ngí-mâ,31 Sé-kiet-lâ, Ma̍k-mâ-nâ, Sâm-sat-nâ,32 Li-pâ-vut, Sṳ̍t-hiûn, Â-yîn lâu Lìm-mùn, chúng-khiung ngi-sṳ̍p kiú-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.33 Chhai sî-phién kô-ngièn ke sàng-chṳ́n yû: Yî-sṳ̍t-thò, Só-lâ, Â-sṳ̍t-nâ,34 Sat-nò-â, Yún-Kôn-nèn, Thâ-phû-â, Yî-nàm,35 Yâ-ma̍t, Â-thu-làn, Sô-kô, Â-sî-kâ,36 Sâ-lâ-yîm, Â-tí-thâ-yîm, Kî-tí-lâ lâu Kî-tí-lò-thâ-yîm, chúng-khiung sṳ̍p-si chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.37 Yu-yû Sé-nàm, Ha̍p-thai-sâ, Ma̍k-thai-Kâ-tet,38 Tí-lièn, Mí-sṳ̂-pâ, Yok-thiap,39 Lâ-kit, Pô-sṳ̂-kâ, Yî-kî-lùn,40 Kâ-pún, Lâ-man, Kî-thì-li,41 Kî-tâi-lò, Pak-Thai-kún, Nâ-mâ lâu Mâ-kî-thai, chúng-khiung sṳ̍p-liuk chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.42 Yu-yû Li̍p-nâ, Yî-thiap, Â-sân,43 Yit-fut-thâ, Â-sṳ̍t-nâ, Nì-sit,44 Kî-yî-lâ, Â-kiet-sit lâu Mâ-li-sâ, chúng-khiung kiú-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.45 Yu-yû Yî-kiet-lùn lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông,46 tui Yî-kiet-lùn to Thi-chûng-hói, vá-khiun Â-sṳ̍t-thut só-yû ke chhûn-chông.47 Yu-yû Â-sṳ̍t-thut lâu Kâ-sat, lièn-thùng fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông, chhṳ̍t-to Âi-khi̍p piên-kin ke hò-pa lâu Thi-chûng-hói sùn.48 Chhai sân-khî yû: Sâ-me̍t, Ngâ-thì-ngí, Sô-kô,49 Thai-nâ, Kî-lie̍t-Sat-nâ (chhiu-he Tí-piak),50 Â-nâ-pak, Yî-sṳ̍t-thì-mo̍k, Â-ngiam,51 Kô-sân, Hò-lùn lâu Kî-lò, chúng-khiung sṳ̍p-yit chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.52 Yu-yû Â-lâ, Thu-mâ, Yî-sân,53 Ngâ-nùng, Pak-Thâ-phû-â, Â-fî-kâ,54 Fèn-thâ, Kî-lie̍t-Â-pâ (chhiu-he Hî-pak-lùn), lâu Sé-ngí, chúng-khiung kiú-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.55 Yu-yû Mâ-yùn, Kâ-me̍t, Sî-fut, Vû-thâ,56 Yâ-sṳ̂-lie̍t, Yok-thien, Sat-nò-â,57 Kâi-yún, Kî-pí-â lâu Thìn-nâ, chúng-khiung sṳ̍p-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.58 Yu-yû Ha̍p-fut, Pak-Siuk, Kî-thut,59 Mâ-la̍p, Pak-Â-nok lâu Yî-le̍t-thì-kiûn, chúng-khiung liuk-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.60 Yu-yû Kî-lie̍t-Pâ-li̍t (chhiu-he Kî-lie̍t-Yâ-lìm) lâu Lâ-pâ lióng-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.61 Chhai khóng-yâ yû: Pak-Â-lâ-pâ, Me̍t-tên, Sî-kâ-kâ,62 Ni̍t-sân, Yàm-sàng lâu Yún-Kî-tí, chúng-khiung liuk-chho sàng, lâu fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.63 Yù-thai-ngìn mò fap-thu chiông he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn kiuk-chéu, só-yî Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn hàn-he lâu Yù-thai-ngìn khiung-ha he̍t chhai ke-vi, chhṳ̍t-to kîm-pû-ngit.
