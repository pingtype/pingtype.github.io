1 Li-vi-ngìn ke Sàng Li-vi kok kâ-chhu̍k ke-têu chhu̍k-chóng hi kien chi-sṳ̂ Yî-li-â-sat, Nun ke lai-é Yok-sû-â, lâu Yî-set-lie̍t kok kî-chhu̍k, kok kâ-chhu̍k ke-têu liâng-chhiu,2 chhai Kâ-nàm-thi ke Sṳ-lò tui kì-têu kóng: "Song-chú sṳt thûng-ko Mô-sî chhòn-hâ min-lin, oi pûn-têu sàng pûn ngài-têu he̍t, hàn-oi yû chhó-phû hó hiuk ngài-têu ke thèu-sâng."3 Só-yî, Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chhiu cheu Song-chú ke min-lin, tui kì-têu chhṳ-kâ ke thú-thi chûng chiông yî-ha só ki ke sàng-chṳ́n lâu chhó-phû pûn-pûn Li-vi-ngìn:4 Li-vi kî-chhu̍k ke Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k chui-siên pûn-tó sàng-chṳ́n. Chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn kâ-chhu̍k ke heu-thoi tui Yù-thai, Sî-mién, lâu Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó sṳ̍p-sâm chho sàng.5 Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k khì-thâ ke kâ-chhu̍k tui Yî-fap-lièn, Tan, lâu sî Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó sṳ̍p-chho sàng.6 Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k tui Yî-sat-kâ, Â-sat, Nâ-fut-thâ-li, lâu chhai Pâ-sân ke tûng Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó sṳ̍p-sâm chho sàng.7 Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k tui Lî-phien, Kâ-tet, lâu Sî-pu-lùn kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó sṳ̍p-ngi chho sàng.8 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn cheu Song-chú thûng-ko Mô-sî chhòn-hâ ke min-lin, yung chhû-chhiâm ke phan-fap chiông liá-têu sàng-chṳ́n lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû fûn-phi pûn Li-vi-ngìn.9 Yî-ha liá-têu sàng he tui Yù-thai lâu Sî-mién kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn chhut-lòi,10 pûn Â-lùn ke heu-thoi. Â-lùn su̍k Li-vi kî-chhu̍k, Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k. Kì-têu chui-siên pûn-tó sàng-chṳ́n.11 Kì-têu tet-tó ke sàng yû Â-pâ (Â-pâ he Â-na̍p ke â-pâ), liá-ha ôn-to Hî-pak-lùn, chhai Yù-thai sân-khî; kì-têu ya pûn-tó Â-pâ chû-vì ke chhó-phû.12 Chúng-he su̍k liá-sàng ke thièn-thi lâu chhûn-chông, yí-kîn pûn-pûn Yâ-fù-nì ke lai-é Kâ-le̍t cho sán-ngia̍p.13 Chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn ke heu-thoi, chhù-liáu pûn-tó Hî-pak-lùn (thò-sám-sàng chṳ̂-yit) yî-ngoi, hàn pûn-tó yî-ha liá-têu sàng: Li̍p-nâ,14 Ngâ-thì-ngí, Yî-sṳ̍t-thì-mo̍k,15 Hò-lùn, Tí-piak,16 Â-yîn, Vû-thâ lâu Pak-Sṳ-ma̍k, chúng-khiung kiú-chho Yù-thai lâu Sî-mién kî-chhu̍k ke sàng, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.17 Kì-têu ya tui Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng, chhiu-he Kî-phiên, Kâ-pâ,18 Â-nâ-thut, Â-le̍t-mùn, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.19 Chi-sṳ̂ Â-lùn ke heu-thoi chúng-khiung pûn-tó sṳ̍p-sâm chho sàng, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.20 Li-vi ke Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k khì-thâ ke kâ-chhu̍k tui Yî-fap-lièn kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng,21 chhiu-he Yî-fap-lièn sân-khî ke Sṳ-kiam (thò-sám-sàng chṳ̂-yit), Kî-set,22 Kî-pak-siên, Pak-Fò-lùn, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.23 Kì-têu tui Tan kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi ya pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Yî-li-thì-kî, Kî-pí-tun,24 Â-ngâ-lùn, Kâ-thi̍t-Lìm-mùn, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.25 Kì-têu tui sî Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó lióng-chho sàng: Thâ-na̍p, Kâ-thi̍t-Lìm-mùn, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.26 Kô-hot chûng-chhu̍k khì-thâ ke kâ-chhu̍k chúng-khiung pûn-tó sṳ̍p-chho sàng, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.27 Li-vi ke Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k tui tûng Mâ-nâ-sî kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó lióng-chho sàng: Pâ-sân ke Kô-làn (thò-sám-sàng chṳ̂-yit), Pí-Sṳ̂-thì-lâ, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.28 Kì-têu tui Yî-sat-kâ kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Kî-san, Thai-pí-lâ,29 Yâ-ma̍t, Yún-Kôn-nèn, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.30 Kì-têu tui Â-sat kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi ya pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Mí-sâ-le̍t, Ap-tun,31 Het-kap, Li-ha̍p, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.32 Kì-têu tui Nâ-fut-thâ-li kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó sâm-chho sàng: Kâ-li-li ke Kî-tâi-sṳ̂ (thò-sám-sàng chṳ̂-yit), Ha̍p-ma̍t-Tô-ngí, Kâ-ngí-thán, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.33 Kiet-sun chûng-chhu̍k kok kâ-chhu̍k chúng-khiung pûn-tó sṳ̍p-sâm chho sàng, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.34 Khì-thâ ke Li-vi-ngìn, Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k, tui Sî-pu-lùn kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Yok-ngiam, Kâ-ngí-thâ,35 Tên-nâ, Nâ-ha̍p-lâ, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.36 Kì-têu tui Lî-phien kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Pí-sit, Ngâ-chha̍p,37 Kî-tí-mo̍k, Mí-fap-ap, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.38 Kì-têu tui Kâ-tet kî-chhu̍k ke thú-thi pûn-tó si-chho sàng: Kî-lie̍t ke Lâ-ma̍t (thò-sám-sàng chṳ̂-yit), Mâ-ha̍p-ngiam,39 Hî-sṳ̍t-pún, Ngâ-chhia, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû.40 Khì-thâ ke Li-vi-ngìn, Mí-lâ-li chûng-chhu̍k chúng-khiung pûn-tó sṳ̍p-ngi chho sàng.41 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn tui kì-têu ke thú-thi chúng-khiung fûn-chhut si-sṳ̍p pat-chho sàng, lâu kok-sàng fu-khiun ke chhó-phû pûn Li-vi-ngìn.42 Liá-têu sàng si chû-vì yû chhó-phû, tak-chho sàng chhiòn-phu he án-ngiòng. Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn Tet-tó Thú-thi Cho Sán-ngia̍p43 Án-ngiòng, Song-chú cheu Kì hiong Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn ke chú-siên só yin-hí ke, chiông só-yû ke thú-thi su-pûn kì-têu. Kì-têu cham-liâng ke-têu thú-thi, chhiu chhai ke-vi ôn-kî.44 Song-chú su-pûn kì-têu kin-nui thai-phìn, chhiu-chhiong Kì tui kì-têu ke chú-siên só yin-hí ke. Kì-têu ke sù-thi̍t mò yit-ke yû phan-fap lâu kì-têu chok-tui, yîn-vi Song-chú chiông só-yû ke thi̍t-ngìn chhiòn-phu kâu chhai Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn sú-chûng.45 Song-chú yin-hí su-fuk pûn Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn ke fa, yit-ki ya mò leu-thet, chhiòn-phu yû sṳ̍t-hien.
