1 Yî-so ke Heu-thoi( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 1:34-37 ) Yî-so ke heu-thoi ki chhai hâ-poi; Yî-so yu ôn-to Yî-tûng.2 Yî-so lâu Kâ-nàm ńg-chṳ́ kiet-fûn; kì thó Het-ngìn Yî-lùn ke moi-é Â-thai, yu thó Hî-vi-ngìn Chi-phien ke sûn-ńg — Â-nâ ke moi-é Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ,3 yu thó Yî-sṳ̍t-mâ-li ke moi-é — Nì-pai-yok ke ló-moi Pâ-sṳ̍t-mat.4 Â-thai kiung Yî-li-fap; Pâ-sṳ̍t-mat kiung Liù-ngí;5 Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ kiung Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Ngâ-làn, Khó-lâ. Yî-so liá-têu lai-é chhiòn-phu he chhai Kâ-nàm chhut-se ke.6 Yî-so tai kì ke thai-phò, se-chiá, chṳ́-ńg lâu kì chhiòn-kâ ngìn, pin kì só-yû ke ngiù-yòng, thèu-sâng lâu chhai Kâ-nàm tet-tó ke chhòi-vu̍t, lì-khôi kiâ ló-thâi Ngâ-kok, hi phe̍t ke thi-fông.7 Yîn-vi kì lióng-kâ yû tông-tô ngiù-yòng lâu chhin-tô chhòi-sán, kì-têu cho lî-hak ke thi-fông m̀-la fat, mò fap-thu khiung-ha he̍t,8 Yî-so chhiu lì-khôi ke thi-fông, hi-he̍t chhai Yî-tûng sân-khî; Yî-so yu ôn-to Yî-tûng.9 Yî-ha he Yî-tûng-ngìn ke chú-siên Yî-so ke heu-thoi.10 Yî-so lai-é ke miàng ki chhai hâ-poi: Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Â-thai kiung yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Yî-li-fap. Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Pâ-sṳ̍t-mat kiung yit-ke lai-é ôn-to Liù-ngí.11 Yî-li-fap ke lai-é chhiu-he: Thì-man, Â-mat, Sé-pô, Kâ-thán, Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂.12 Yî-li-fap ke se-chiá Thìn-na̍p kiung yit-ke lai-é Â-mâ-li̍t. Yî-song he Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Â-thai ke chṳ́-sûn.13 Liù-ngí ke lai-é chhiu-he: Nâ-ha̍p, Chhia-lâ, Sâ-mâ, Mí-sat. Yî-song he Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Pâ-sṳ̍t-mat ke chṳ́-sûn.14 Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ he Chi-phien ke sûn-ńg — Â-nâ ke moi-é; kì lâu Yî-so kiung ke lai-é chhiu-he: Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Ngâ-làn, Khó-lâ.15 Yî-ha he Yî-so heu-thoi ke kî-chhu̍k. Yî-so ke chóng-chṳ́ Yî-li-fap he Thì-man, Â-mat, Sé-pô, Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂,16 Khó-lâ, Kâ-thán, Â-mâ-li̍t kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. Liá-têu kî-chhu̍k he̍t chhai Yî-tûng, chhiòn-phu he Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Â-thai ke heu-thoi.17 Yî-so ke lai-é Liù-ngí he Nâ-ha̍p, Chhia-lâ, Sâ-mâ, Mí-sat kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. Liá-têu kî-chhu̍k he̍t chhai Yî-tûng, chhiòn-phu he Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng Pâ-sṳ̍t-mat ke heu-thoi.18 Yâ-vû-sṳ̂, Ngâ-làn, Khó-lâ kok-chhu̍k he Yî-so ke pû-ngiòng — Â-nâ ke moi-é Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ ke heu-thoi.19 Yî-song liá-têu kî-chhu̍k chhiòn-phu he Yî-so ke heu-thoi; Yî-so yu ôn-to Yî-tûng. Sî-ngí ke Heu-thoi20 ( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 1:38-42 )Yî-tûng thi-khî ngièn-chhu-mìn ke chú-siên chhiòn-phu he Hò-li-ngìn Sî-ngí ke heu-thoi. Kì-têu he: Lò-thân, Sok-pâ, Chi-phien, Â-nâ, Tí-sun, Yî-chhat, Tí-sân.21 併於上節22 Lò-thân he Hò-li, Hî-man lióng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. (Lò-thân yû yit-ke ló-moi miàng ôn-to Thìn-na̍p.)23 Sok-pâ he Â-le̍t-vùn, Mâ-nâ-hot, Yî-pâ-lu̍k, Sṳ-pô, Â-nàm kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.24 Chi-phien yû lióng-ke lai-é, chhiu-he Â-ngâ lâu Â-nâ. (Tông-ngièn chhai khóng-yâ thoi â-pâ chóng lì-è, chhìm-tó vûn-chhièn ke, chhiu-he liá-ke Â-nâ.)25 Â-nâ he Tí-sun ke â-pâ; Â-nâ yû yit-ke moi-é miàng ôn-to Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ.26 Tí-sun he Hiûn-tan, Yî-sṳ-pân, Yit-làn, Kî-làn kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.27 Yî-chhat he Phit-hón, Sat-fân, Â-kôn kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.28 Tí-sân he Vû-sṳ̂ lâu Â-làn lióng-chhu̍k ke chú-siên.29 Yî-tûng thi-khî Hò-li-ngìn ke kok-chhu̍k chhiu-he: Lò-thân, Sok-pâ, Chi-phien, Â-nâ, Tí-sun, Yî-chhat, Tí-sân. Yî-tûng Lie̍t-vòng30 併於上節31 ( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 1:43-54 ) Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn hàn-màng yû kiûn-vòng yî-chhièn, chhai Yî-tûng cho-vòng ke, ki chhai hâ-poi:32 Pí-ngí ke lai-é Pí-lâ chhai Yî-tûng cho-vòng, kì ke kîn-sàng ôn-to Thìn-ha̍p-pâ.33 Pí-lâ sí-heu, Pô-sṳ̂-lâ-ngìn Chhia-lâ ke lai-é Yok-pâ chiap-tén kì cho-vòng.34 Yok-pâ sí-heu, Thì-man thi-khî ke ngìn Fu-sân chiap-tén kì cho-vòng.35 Fu-sân sí-heu, Pí-tha̍t ke lai-é Ha̍p-tha̍t chiap-tén kì cho-vòng; liá Ha̍p-tha̍t chhiu-he chhai Mô-ap-thi tá-yàng Mí-thien-ngìn ke, kì ke kîn-sàng ôn-to Â-vi-tet.36 Ha̍p-tha̍t sí-heu, Mâ-sṳ-li-kâ-ngìn Sông-lâ chiap-tén kì cho-vòng.37 Sông-lâ sí-heu, thai hò-pa ke Li-hò-pak-ngìn Seu-lò chiap-tén kì cho-vòng.38 Seu-lò sí-heu, Â-kiet-pô ke lai-é Pâ-le̍t-Ha̍p-nàm chiap-tén kì cho-vòng.39 Â-kiet-pô ke lai-é Pâ-le̍t-Ha̍p-nàm sí-heu, Ha̍p-tha̍t chiap-tén kì cho-vòng; kì ke kîn-sàng ôn-to Pâ-vû; kì ke pû-ngiòng ôn-to Mí-hî-thâ-phe̍t, he Mâ-thi̍t-lie̍t ke moi-é — Mí-Sat-ha̍p ke sûn-ńg.40 Yî-so he Thìn-na̍p, Â-le̍t-ngá, Yâ-thiap,41 Â-hò-li-pâ-mâ, Yî-lâ, Pí-nun,42 Kî-na̍p-sṳ̂, Thì-man, Mí-pí-sat,43 Mâ-kî-thia̍p lâu Yî-làn liá-têu Yî-tûng-ngìn kok-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. Kok-chhu̍k só-he̍t ke thi-khî, yung kok-chhu̍k ke miàng lòi ôn-miàng.
