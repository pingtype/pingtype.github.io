1 Song-ti chhai Pak-thi̍t-li Su-fuk Ngâ-kok Song-ti tui Ngâ-kok kóng: "Ngì oi to Pak-thi̍t-li hi, he̍t chhai ke-vi. Ngì oi chhai ke-vi vi Ngài hí yit-chho chi-thàn. Ngài chhiu-he ngì thò-phit ngiâ-kô Yî-so ke sṳ̀, hiong ngì hién-hien ke Song-ti."2 Ngâ-kok chhiu tui kì ke kâ-chhu̍k lâu só-yû thèn kì khiung-ha hi ke ngìn kóng: "Oi chiông ngì-têu tai-lòi ke-têu ngoi-chhu̍k sṳ̀n-siong te̍p-thet; oi kiet-chhiang chhṳ-kâ, von chhiang-li ke sâm-fu.3 Ngài-têu oi lì-khôi liá-vi hi Pak-thi̍t-li; ngài oi chhai ke só-chhai vi Song-ti hí yit-chho chi-thàn, yîn-vi ngài tù-tó khun-nàn ke sṳ̀, Song-ti pông-chhu ngài; ngài liù-long ke sṳ̀, Kì lâu ngài khiung-ha."4 Kì-têu chhiu chiông só-yû ke ngoi-chhu̍k sṳ̀n-siong lâu chhṳ-kâ só tai ke ngí-vàn kâu-pûn Ngâ-kok; Ngâ-kok chiông liá-têu tûng-sî mài chhai Sṳ-kiam fu-khiun ke siong-su hâ.5 Ngâ-kok lâu kì ke-têu lai-é chhut-fat ke sṳ̀, Song-ti pûn si chû-vì sàng-chṳ́n ke ngìn fî-sòng chho̍k-kiâng, m̀-kám tûi kì-têu.6 Ngâ-kok lâu kûi-kâ ngìn lòi-to Kâ-nàm-thi ke Lu-sṳ̂, chhiu-he Pak-thi̍t-li ke só-chhai.7 Kì chhai ke-vi hí yit-chho chi-thàn, chiông ke thi-fông ôn-to Yî-le̍t-Pak-thi̍t-li( 68 35:7 "Yî-le̍t-Pak-thi̍t-li" yi-sṳ he "Pak-thi̍t-li ke Song-ti".), yîn-vi kì thò-phit kiâ-kô ke sṳ̀, Song-ti chhai ke-vi chhîn-chhṳ hiong kì hién-hien.8 Li-pak-kâ ke nen-ôi Tí-pô-lâ sí-thet, mài chhai Pak-thi̍t-li nàm-phu ke siong-su hâ, só-yî ke-thèu su-é ôn-to "ôi-sông ke siong-su".9 Ngâ-kok tui Mî-sok-put-tha̍t-mí-â chón-lòi yî-heu, Song-ti chai yit-pái hiong kì hién-hien, su-fuk pûn kì.10 Song-ti tui kì kóng: "Ngì ke miàng he Ngâ-kok, chúng-he chhiùng-kîm yî-heu ngì oi ôn-to Yî-set-lie̍t." Án-ngiòng, Song-ti chhiu lâu kì kói miàng ôn-to Yî-set-lie̍t.11 Song-ti tui kì kóng: "Ngài he chhiòn-nèn ke Song-ti; Ngài oi pûn ngì kiung tô-tô chṳ́-ńg. Tông-tô koet-kâ oi tui ngì ke chṳ́-sûn hîn-hí; ngì oi cho chhin-tô kiûn-vòng ke chú-siên.12 Ngài oi chiông Ngài su-pûn Â-pak-lâ-hón lâu Yî-sat ke thú-thi su-pûn ngì lâu ngì ke heu-thoi."13 Liá-têu fa kóng-so̍t, Song-ti chhiu lì-khôi kì.14 Ngâ-kok chhai Song-ti tui kì kóng-fa ke thi-fông tun yit-chak sa̍k-thèu cho ki-ngiam-pî, chhai ke táng-kô lìm-chiú lâu kám-lám-yù.15 Ngâ-kok chiông ke thi-fông ôn-to Pak-thi̍t-li, yîn-vi Song-ti chhai ke-vi lâu kì kóng-fa. Lùi-kiet Nàn-sán Sí-thet16 Ngâ-kok lâu kì ke kâ-chhu̍k tui Pak-thi̍t-li chhut-fat. Kì-têu lòi-to lì Yî-fap-thâ hàn-yû yit-chham lu ke thi-fông, Lùi-kiet sán-khì to lé, chhin nàn-sán.17 Thung-to ngiûn m̀-he̍t ke sṳ̀, sán-phò lâu kì kóng: "M̀-sṳ́ kiâng, ngì yu kiung yit-ke lai-é."18 Tông Lùi-kiet voi thôn-hi ke sṳ̀-chiet, kì lâu ô-nga-é ôn-miàng Phien-Ngò-nì( 69 35:18 "Phien-Ngò-nì" yi-sṳ he "sṳ́ ngài kân-khú ke lai-é".); chúng-he Ngâ-kok lâu kì ôn-to Phien-ngâ-mén( 70 35:18 "Phien-ngâ-mén" yi-sṳ he "ngài yu-sú ke lai-é" ya-he "hó-yun ke lai-é".).19 Lùi-kiet sí-thet, mài chhai oi-hi Yî-fap-thâ ke lu-sùn; Yî-fap-thâ chhiu-he Pak-li-hèn.20 Ngâ-kok chhai kì ke fùn-mu tun yit-te pî-sa̍k, chhṳ̍t-to kîm-pû-ngit Lùi-kiet ke mu-pî hàn-chhôi.21 Yî-set-lie̍t ki-siu̍k kì ke lu-chhàng, chhai Yî-tet-thap ke nàm-phién tap chong-phùng. Ngâ-kok ke Lai-é22 ( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 2:1-2 ) Yî-set-lie̍t hàn he̍t chhai ke thi-khî ke sṳ̀, kì ke chóng-chṳ́ Lî-phien lâu kiâ-pâ ke se-chiá Phit-lâ soi-muk. Yî-set-lie̍t ya thâng-tó liá-khien sṳ( 71 35:22 Yû-têu kú-kién kâ "fî-sòng fat-khién".).Ngâ-kok yû sṳ̍p-ngi ke lai-é.23 Li-â kiung ke lai-é yû:Ngâ-kok ke chóng-chṳ́ Lî-phien,hàn-yû Sî-mién, Li-vi, Yù-thai, Yî-sat-kâ, Sî-pu-lùn.24 Lùi-kiet kiung ke lai-é yû:Yok-sit, Phien-ngâ-mén.25 Lùi-kiet ke sṳ́-ńg Phit-lâ kiung ke lai-é yû:Tan, Nâ-fut-thâ-li.26 Li-â ke sṳ́-ńg Sit-pha kiung ke lai-é yû:Kâ-tet, Â-sat. Ngâ-kok liá-têu lai-é chhiòn-phu he chhai Mî-sok-put-tha̍t-mí-â chhut-se ke.27 Yî-sat Ko-sṳ̂n Ngâ-kok lòi-to Man-li, to kiâ-pâ Yî-sat ke-vi; Man-li chhiu-he Hî-pak-lùn, he Â-pak-lâ-hón lâu Yî-sat he̍t-ko ke thi-fông.28 Yî-sat hióng-su yit-pak pat-sṳ̍p se.29 Kì sṳ̍t-to chhin-tô se, chhin-ló, chang thôn-hi sí-thet, chón-to kiâ chú-chûng ke-vi hi. Kì ke lai-é Yî-so lâu Ngâ-kok chiông kì mài-chong.
