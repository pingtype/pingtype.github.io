1 Nò-â ke Heu-thoi( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 1:5-23 ) Yî-ha he Nò-â ke lai-é Sám, Hàm lâu Ngâ-fut ke heu-thoi. Thai-súi yî-heu kì-têu chhiòn-phu yû kiung lai-é.2 Ngâ-fut ke lai-é he: Kô-me̍t, Mâ-kok, Mâ-thoi, Ngâ-vàn, Thú-pâ, Mí-sat, Thì-lâ( 11 10:2 Thi 2, 6, 15, 22 tén chiet chûng ke miàng, yû-têu he thi-miàng, yû-têu he ngìn-miàng. Ke-têu thi-miàng ya he chú-siên ke miàng.).3 Kô-me̍t ke chṳ́-sûn he Â-sṳ̍t-kî-nâ-ngìn, Li-fap-ngìn, Thò-kâ-mâ-ngìn.4 Ngâ-vàn ke chṳ́-sûn he Yî-li-sâ-ngìn, Thâ-sṳ̂-ngìn, Kî-thì-ngìn, Lò-tet-ngìn.5 Kì-têu he he̍t chhai Thi-chûng-hói hói-sùn lâu hói-tó mìn-chhu̍k ke chú-siên. Ngâ-fut ke heu-thoi kok-chhu̍k chhai chhṳ-kâ ke phu-lo̍k, koet-kâ he̍t, sṳ́-yung chhṳ-kâ ke ngî-ngièn.6 Hàm ke lai-é he: Kú-sṳ̍t, Âi-khi̍p, Fut, Kâ-nàm.7 Kú-sṳ̍t ke chṳ́-sûn he Sî-pâ-ngìn, Ha̍p-fî-lâ-ngìn, Sat-fut-thâ-ngìn, Lâ-mâ-ngìn, Sat-fut-thì-kâ-ngìn. Lâ-mâ ke chṳ́-sûn he Sṳ-pâ-ngìn lâu Tí-tan-ngìn.8 Kú-sṳ̍t yû yit-ke lai-é miàng ôn-to Nèn-liu̍k; kì he li̍t-sṳ́ song thi-yit ke yîn-hiùng.9 Yîn-vi yû Song-chú ke pông-chhu, kì sàng-cho tá-lia̍p yîn-hiùng. Só-yî, yû siu̍k-ngî kóng: "Ngien Song-chú pûn ngì sàng-cho chhiong Nèn-liu̍k án-ngiòng ke tá-lia̍p yîn-hiùng."10 Pún-lòi, kì ke vòng-koet pâu-hàm Pâ-pí-lùn, Yî-li̍t, Â-kap; liá sâm-ke thi-fông chhai Sṳ-nâ ke só-chhai.11 Kì tui liá thi-fông chhut-lòi, hi Â-sut, hí Nì-nì-mì-sàng, Li-hò-pak-sàng, Kâ-lâ-sàng,12 yu chhai Nì-nì-mì lâu Kâ-lâ thai-sàng chûng-kiên hí Li-siên-sàng.13 Âi-khi̍p ke heu-thoi he: Lu-tâi-ngìn, Â-nâ-mí-ngìn, Li-ha̍p-pí-ngìn, Nâ-fut-thú-hî-ngìn,14 Pha-sṳ̂-lû-se-ngìn, Kâ-sṳ̂-lu-hî-ngìn lâu Khiet-lî-thi̍t-ngìn. Fî-li-sṳ-ngìn he Khiet-lî-thi̍t-ngìn ke heu-thoi.15 Kâ-nàm kiung chóng-chṳ́ Sî-tun, yu kiung Het.16 Kâ-nàm ya he Yâ-pu-sṳ̂-ngìn, Â-mô-li-ngìn, Kiet-kâ-sat-ngìn,17 Hî-vi-ngìn, Â-kî-ngìn, Sî-nì-ngìn,18 Â-ngá-tí-ngìn, Sé-mâ-li-ngìn, Ha̍p-mâ-ngìn ke chú-siên. Kâ-nàm-ngìn kok-chhu̍k fûn-phu chhin-fat;19 kì-têu ke thi-kie tui Sî-tun hiong-nàm to Kâ-sat fu-khiun ke Kî-lâ-ngí, hiong-tûng to Só-tô-mâ, Ngò-mô-lâ, Ap-mâ lâu Lâ-sâ fu-khiun ke Sé-pién.20 Yî-song he Hàm ke chṳ́-sûn, kok-chhu̍k chhai chhṳ-kâ ke phu-lo̍k, koet-kâ he̍t, sṳ́-yung chhṳ-kâ ke ngî-ngièn.21 Sám he Ngâ-fut ke thai-kô, kì he Hî-pak( 12 10:21 "Hî-pak" chhiu he Hî-pak-lòi-ngìn ke chú-siên.) chṳ́-sûn ke chú-siên.22 Sám ke lai-é he: Yî-làn, Â-sut, Â-fap-sat, Lu-tet, Â-làn.23 Â-làn ke chṳ́-sûn he: Vû-sṳ̂-ngìn, Fu-le̍t-ngìn, Kî-thiap-ngìn, Mâ-sṳ̂-ngìn.24 Â-fap-sat kiung Sâ-lâ; Sâ-lâ kiung Hî-pak.25 Hî-pak kiung lióng-ke lai-é: yit-ke ôn-to Fap-le̍t( 13 10:25 "Fap-le̍t" yû "fûn-san" ke yi-sṳ.), yîn-vi chhai kì ke sṳ̀-thoi, ngìn-lui he fûn-san lòi-he̍t ke; kì ke hiûng-thi miàng-ham Yok-thân.26 Yok-thân ke chṳ́-sûn he: Â-mô-tap-ngìn, Sâ-lie̍t-ngìn, Ha̍p-sat-mâ-fî-ngìn, Yâ-lâ-ngìn,27 Ha̍p-tô-làn-ngìn, Vû-sat-ngìn, Tet-lâ-ngìn,28 Ngò-pâ-lu-ngìn, Â-pí-mâ-li-ngìn, Sṳ-pâ-ngìn,29 Â-fî-ngìn, Ha̍p-fî-lâ-ngìn, Yok-pâ-ngìn. Liá-têu ngìn chhiòn-phu he Yok-thân ke chṳ́-sûn.30 Liá-têu ngìn he̍t tûng-phién ke sân-thi, tui Mí-sâ yit-chhṳ̍t to Sî-fat.31 Liá-têu ngìn he Sám ke chṳ́-sûn, kok-chhu̍k chhai chhṳ-kâ ke phu-lo̍k, koet-kâ he̍t, sṳ́-yung chhṳ-kâ ke ngî-ngièn.32 Yî-song he Nò-â sâm-ke lai-é ke chûng-chhu̍k, cheu kok kî-chhu̍k li̍p-koet. Thai-súi heu, thi-song ke koet-kâ he tui liá-têu chûng-chhu̍k fûn chhut-lòi ke.
