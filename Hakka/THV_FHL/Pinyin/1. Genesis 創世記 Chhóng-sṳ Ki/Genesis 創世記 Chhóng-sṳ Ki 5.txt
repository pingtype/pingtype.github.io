1 Â-tông ke Heu-thoi( Li̍t-thoi-chṳ Song 1:1-4 ) Liá he Â-tông heu-thoi ke chhu̍k-phú. (Song-ti chhóng-chho ngìn ke sṳ̀, he cheu Song-ti ke yong-sṳt chho kì.2 Kì chhóng-chho kì-têu, yû-nàm yû-ńg. Kì-têu su-chho ke sṳ̀, Song-ti su-fuk pûn kì-têu, chiông kì-têu ôn-cho "ngìn".)3 Â-tông yit-pak sâm-sṳ̍p se ke sṳ̀, kiung yit-ke lai-é, hìn-siong, yong-sṳt lâu kì chhṳ-kâ chhin siông-chhiong, chhiu lâu kì ôn-to Sai-thi̍t.4 Â-tông kiung Sai-thi̍t yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.5 Â-tông sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak sâm-sṳ̍p se chang ko-sṳ̂n.6 Sai-thi̍t yit-pak làng ńg-se ke sṳ̀, kiung Yî-nò-sṳ.7 Sai-thi̍t kiung Yî-nò-sṳ yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak làng chhit-ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.8 Sai-thi̍t sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak yit-sṳ̍p ngi-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.9 Yî-nò-sṳ kiú-sṳ̍p se ke sṳ̀, kiung Kâi-nàm.10 Yî-nò-sṳ kiung Kâi-nàm yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak yit-sṳ̍p ńg-ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.11 Yî-nò-sṳ sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak làng ńg-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.12 Kâi-nàm chhit-sṳ̍p se ke sṳ̀, kiung Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t.13 Kâi-nàm kiung Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak si-sṳ̍p ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.14 Kâi-nàm sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak yit-sṳ̍p se chang ko-sṳ̂n.15 Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t liuk-sṳ̍p ńg-se ke sṳ̀, kiung Ngâ-lie̍t.16 Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t kiung Ngâ-lie̍t yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak sâm-sṳ̍p ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.17 Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t sṳ̍t-to pat-pak kiú-sṳ̍p ńg-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.18 Ngâ-lie̍t yit-pak liuk-sṳ̍p ngi-se ke sṳ̀, kiung Yî-nok.19 Ngâ-lie̍t kiung Yî-nok yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ pat-pak ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.20 Ngâ-lie̍t sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak liuk-sṳ̍p ngi-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.21 Yî-nok liuk-sṳ̍p ńg-se ke sṳ̀, kiung Mâ-thú-sat-lâ.22 Yî-nok kiung Mâ-thú-sat-lâ yî-heu, lâu Song-ti thùng-hàng sâm-pak ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.23 Yî-nok chhai-sṳ sṳ̍t-to sâm-pak liuk-sṳ̍p ńg-se.24 Yî-nok lâu Song-ti khiung-ha hàng, yî-heu Song-ti chiông kì tai-chéu, kì chhiu mò-chhôi lé.25 Mâ-thú-sat-lâ yit-pak pat-sṳ̍p chhit-se ke sṳ̀, kiung Lâ-ma̍k.26 Mâ-thú-sat-lâ kiung Lâ-ma̍k yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ chhit-pak pat-sṳ̍p ngi-ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.27 Mâ-thú-sat-lâ sṳ̍t-to kiú-pak liuk-sṳ̍p kiú-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.28 Lâ-ma̍k yit-pak pat-sṳ̍p ngi-se ke sṳ̀, kiung yit-ke lai-é,29 lâu kì ôn-to Nò-â( 9 5:29 "Nò-â" yû "ôn-ví" ke yi-sṳ.), kóng: "Yîn-vi Song-chú chu-chú thú-thi, pûn ngài-têu lò-luk kâng-chung, liá-ke lai-é voi pûn ngài-têu chhai sîn-khú chûng tet-tó ôn-ví."30 Lâ-ma̍k kiung Nò-â yî-heu, hàn chhai-sṳ ńg-pak kiú-sṳ̍p ńg-ngièn, yu sên-yông chṳ́-ńg.31 Lâ-ma̍k sṳ̍t-to chhit-pak chhit-sṳ̍p chhit-se chang ko-sṳ̂n.32 Nò-â ńg-pak se ke sṳ̀, kiung Sám, Hàm lâu Ngâ-fut.
