1 Tí-pô-lâ lâu Pâ-lâ ke Kô Ke-ngit, Tí-pô-lâ lâu Â-pí-nò-êm ke lai-é Pâ-lâ chhong liá-sú kô:2 Oi siung-chan Song-chú!Yî-set-lie̍t ke kiûn-chóng kiet-sîm siông-chhṳ̀,ngìn-mìn chhṳ-ngien chhiùng-kiûn.3 Kiûn-vòng â, ngì-têu oi thâng!Liâng-chhiu â, chet-ngí lòi thâng!Ngài oi hiong Song-chú thàn-khìm chhong-kô,oi kô-siung Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti.4 Song-chú â! Ngì lì-khôi Sî-ngí-sân,Ngì tui Yî-tûng chhut-fat,thai-thi kín-chûn, thiên-táng lo̍k-yí,yùn-chhèn tó-súi.5 Chhai Sî-nai ke Song-chú — Yî-set-lie̍t ke Song-ti mien-chhièn,khiùn-sân chhiòn-phu kín-chûn.6 Chhai Â-nâ ke lai-é Sân-kâ ke sṳ̀-thoi,chhai Ngâ-yi ke ngit-è,sên-lî ngìn m̀-kám kîn-ko liá-vi,chhut-ngoi ngìn lit-to phe̍t-thiàu lu hi.7 Tí-pô-lâ â, Yî-set-lie̍t ke chhûn-chông mò-ngìn kám he̍t,tak só-chhai khûng-khûng, chhṳ̍t-to ngì lòi;ngì lòi-cho Yî-set-lie̍t ke â-mê.8 Yî-set-lie̍t von sîn ke sṳ̀n-mìn,sàng-mùn chhiu yû chan-chên.Si-van Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn chûng,khon m̀-tó tún-phài lâu chhiông.9 Ngài hiong Yî-set-lie̍t ke chṳ́-fî-kôn chûng-sîm,chṳ̂-chhṳ̀ Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn ke chṳ-ngien kiûn.Oi siung-chan Song-chú!10 Ngì-têu khì pha̍k-lì ke, chhô chhai mâ-ôn táng ke,hàng-lu ke chhiòn-phu oi chhòn-yòng.11 Thâng â, chhai chiáng-sùn yû ngìn( 10 5:11 "ngìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sa-chien-sú".) chhòn-chhut fôn-fû!Kì-têu chhòn-kóng Song-chú ke sṳn-li;khin-chuk Yî-set-lie̍t tet-sṳn chón-lòi.Ke-sṳ̀ Song-chú ke chṳ́-mìn hàng-to sàng-mùn fôn-fû:12 Tí-pô-lâ â, hîn-hí hîn-hí!Hîn-hí chhong-kô, yúng-kám hiong-chhièn!Â-pí-nò-êm ke lai-é Pâ-lâ â, hong hí-lòi!Chiông ngì chok-tó ke ngìn tai-chéu!13 Chûng-sin ke ngìn hiong kì-têu ke liâng-chhiu hàng-tén hi;Song-chú ke chṳ́-mìn to Kì( 11 5:13 "Kì" he kîn-kí yit-pún kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "ngài".) mien-chhièn chún-phi siông-chhṳ̀.14 Kì-têu tui Yî-fap-lièn lòi( 12 5:14 "Kì-têu tui Yî-fap-lièn lòi" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "tui Yî-fap-lièn kì-têu ke kîn".), chin-ngi̍p sân-lak( 13 5:14 "sân-lak" he kîn-kí yit-pún kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "Â-mâ-li̍t".),thèn chhai Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ngìn ke heu-poi.Yû chṳ́-fî-kôn tui Mâ-kit chhut-lòi;yû kiûn-kôn tui Sî-pu-lùn hâ-lòi.15 Yî-sat-kâ ke liâng-chhiu lâu Tí-pô-lâ khiung-ha lòi;Yî-sat-kâ lòi, Pâ-lâ ya lòi;kì-têu thèn kì chin-ngi̍p sân-lak.Chúng-he Lî-phien kî-chhu̍k yi-kien kóng m̀-fò;kì-têu mò fap-thu cho kiet-thin.16 Ngì-têu cho-má-ke thèn chhai yòng-khiùn heu-poi?Oi thâng mu̍k-ngìn hêm khiùn-yòng he-mò?He â, Lî-phien kî-chhu̍k yi-kien kóng m̀-fò;kì-têu mò fap-thu kiet-thin.17 Kî-lie̍t-ngìn he̍t chhai Yok-tan-hò tûng-phién;Tan kî-chhu̍k cho-má-ke liù chhai sòn-táng?Â-sat kî-chhu̍k he̍t chhai hói-sùn;kì-têu chhai hói-kóng sùn thìn-thò.18 Chúng-he Sî-pu-lùn-ngìn kám piang-miang m̀-kiâng sí;Nâ-fut-thâ-li-ngìn chhai chan-chhòng ya he án-ngiòng.19 Chhai Mí-kit-tô hò-sùn ke Thâ-na̍p,chung-vòng chhiòn-phu lòi liá-vi siông-chhṳ̀.Kâ-nàm só-yû ke vòng lòi chan-chên;chúng-he kì-têu mò tai-chéu ngiùn-è.20 Sên-é tui thiên-táng lòi yên-chhu,pî ko-lòi kûng-tá Sî-sî-lâ.21 Kî-sun ke hò-súi chhûng-tén hâ —kip-liù chiông kì-têu tá-chéu.Ngài oi chhièn-chin, piang-miang hiong-chhièn!22 Ke-sṳ̀ chan-mâ pêu-tén chéu,kiok-thài sâng ti̍t-ti̍t ta̍t-ta̍t.23 Song-chú ke thiên-sṳ́ kóng: Oi chu-chú Mí-lò-sṳ̂,oi thai-thai lòi chu-chú he̍t chhai ke-vi ke ngìn-mìn.Kì-têu mò lòi pông-chhu Song-chú,m̀ lòi then-sú kûng-tá khiòng-thi̍t.24 Kî-nì-ngìn Hî-pak ke pû-ngiòng Ngâ-yi,chhai fu-ngìn-kâ chûng chui su ngìn chhṳ̂n-chan;pí he̍t chong-phùng ke fu-ńg kha mùng-fuk.25 Sî-sî-lâ hiong kì thó-súi, kì pûn kì ngiù-nen;kì yung kui-chhung ke vón chông nen-yù pûn kì.26 Ngâ-yi chó-sú nâ chong-phùng ke tâng-é,yu-sú nâ-tén sṳ̂-fu yung ke chhùi-è;kì chhin-chhùi Sî-sî-lâ, tá-lan kì ke thèu-nà,chhiuk-chhôn kì ke thai-yòng-hiet.27 Chhai Ngâ-yi kiok-chhièn, Sî-sî-lâ kiû hí-lòi,yu vang chón-hi, vang-to chhṳ̍t-chhṳ̍t;Sî-sî-lâ kiû hí-lòi, yu vang chón-hi,kì chhiu thôn-hi.28 Sî-sî-lâ ke â-mê kín-khon chhûng-mùn ngoi-poi;chhai kak-è chhûng-mùn ke heu-poi thai-sâng kín-mun:Kì ke chan-chhâ cho-má-ke án-kiú màng-chón?Kì ke chan-mâ cho-má-ke hàn-m̀ chón-lòi?29 Kì chui chhûng-mìn ke kiûng-ńg ôn-ví kì,kì ya he chhṳ-kâ mun chhṳ-kâ en kóng:30 Kì-têu yit-thin he chhióng tông-tô tûng-sî, tông pûn chan-li-phín,mò yit-thin yit-ke pîn-é pûn-tó yit, lióng ke se-moi-é.Khó-nèn Sî-sî-lâ pûn-tó ke he chhái-set ke ngoi-sâm;kì thin-chho̍k voi tai siu-fâ vì-kîn chón-lòi pûn vòng-heu.31 Song-chú â, ngien Ngì ke sù-thi̍t chhiòn-phu án-ngiòng me̍t-mòng!Ngien Ngì ke phèn-yû chhiong ngit-thèu án kông-mìn! Án-ngiòng liá thi-fông thai-phìn si-sṳ̍p ngièn.
