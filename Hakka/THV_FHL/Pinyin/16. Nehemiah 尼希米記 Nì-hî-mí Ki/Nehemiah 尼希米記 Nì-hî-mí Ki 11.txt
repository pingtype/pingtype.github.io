1 He̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Ngìn Ngìn-mìn ke liâng-chhiu chhiòn-phu he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng; khì-thâ ke ngìn-mìn chhû-chhiâm, sṳ̍p-ke kâ-chhu̍k sién-chhut yit-kâ he̍t chhai sṳn-sàng Yâ-lu-sat-lâng, chhûn ke kiú-kâ chhiu he̍t chhai khì-thâ sàng-chṳ́n.2 Chhṳ-ngien he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke ngìn, chhiòn-phu tet-tó chung-ngìn ke chhṳ̂n-chan.3 Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, chi-sṳ̂, Li-vi-ngìn, sṳn-thien kûng-ngìn, lâu Só-lò-mùn phu̍k-ngìn ke heu-thoi he̍t chhai Yù-thai-sén khì-thâ sàng-chṳ́n, chhiu-he kì-têu chhṳ-kâ chú-kûng ngia̍p ke só-chhai.Hâ-poi he he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke-têu Yù-thai-sén liâng-chhiu ke miàng-tân:4 Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-ngìn yû:Â-thâ-ngá — kì he Vû-sî-ngâ ke lai-é, Sat-kâ-li-ngâ ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Â-mâ-li-ngâ, Sṳ-fap-thì-ngâ, lâu Mâ-le̍t-lie̍t; kì-têu chhiòn-phu he Yù-thai ke lai-é Fap-le̍t-sṳ̂ ke heu-thoi.5 Mâ-sî-ngâ — kì he Pâ-liu̍k ke lai-é, Kuk-Hò-sî ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Ha̍p-sai-â, Â-thai-ngâ, Yok-ngâ-li̍p, lâu Sat-kâ-li-ngâ; kì-têu chhiòn-phu he Yù-thai ke lai-é Sṳ-lò-nì ke heu-thoi.6 Fap-le̍t-sṳ̂ ke heu-thoi yû si-pak liuk-sṳ̍p pat-miàng yúng-sṳ he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng.7 Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke chhu̍k-ngìn yû:Sat-lu — kì he Mí-sû-làn ke lai-é, Yok-ya̍p ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Pí-thai-ngâ, Kô-lai-ngâ, Mâ-sî-ngâ, Yî-thiet lâu Yâ-chhî-â.8 Kâ-pai lâu Sat-lòi — kì-têu he Sat-lu ke khiun-chhîn( 19 11:8 "khiun-chhîn" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "yî-heu".).Chúng-khiung yû kiú-pak ngi-sṳ̍p pat ke Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng.9 Se-kî-li ke lai-é Yok-ngí he kì-têu ke kam-tuk. Ha̍p-sî-nú-â ke lai-é Yù-thai he liá sàng-tú ke fu kam-tuk.10 Chi-sṳ̂ yû:Yok-ngâ-li̍p ke lai-é Yâ-thai-ngâ, hàn-yû Ngâ-kîn.11 Thai-chi-sṳ̂ Sî-lòi-ngâ — kì he Hî-le̍t-kâ ke lai-é, Mí-sû-làn ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Sat-tuk, Mí-lâ-yok lâu Â-hî-thut.12 Liá chûng-chhu̍k chúng-khiung yû pat-pak ngi-sṳ̍p ngi-miàng chhai sṳn-thien tông-chṳt.Â-thai-ngâ — kì he Yâ-lò-hón ke lai-é, Pí-lâ-li ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Am-sé, Sat-kâ-li-â, Pâ-sṳ̂-fu-ngí lâu Mâ-kî-ngâ.13 Liá chûng-chhu̍k chúng-khiung yû lióng-pak si-sṳ̍p ngi-miàng he kâ-chhu̍k-chóng.Â-mâ-soi — kì he Â-sat-lie̍t ke lai-é, Â-ha̍p-soi ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Mí-sṳ̍t-li-ma̍t lâu Yîm-ma̍k.14 Liá chûng-chhu̍k yû yit-pak ngi-sṳ̍p pat-miàng he tông-voi siông-chhṳ̀ ke yúng-sṳ. Kì-têu ke kam-tuk he Sat-pâ-thi-ngia̍p; kì he Ha̍p-kî-tô-lìm ke lai-é.15 Li-vi-ngìn yû:Sṳ-mâ-ngâ — kì he Ha̍p-sut ke lai-é, Ap-li-kâm ke sûn-é. Kì ke chú-siên hàn-yû Ha̍p-sâ-pí-ngâ lâu Pu-nì.16 Sâ-pí-thai lâu Yok-sat-pha̍t — kì-têu he chhin yû-siu ke Li-vi-ngìn, kam-tuk Song-ti sṳn-thien ngoi ke sṳ-chhìn.17 Mâ-thâ-nì — kì he Mí-kâ ke lai-é, Sat-tí ke sûn-é, Â-sat ke heu-thoi. Kì tai-liâng sṳn-thien sṳ̂-pân chhong kám-chhia ke khì-tó.Pat-pu-kâ — kì he Mâ-thâ-nì ke chhu-sú.Ap-thai — kì he Sâ-mû-â ke lai-é, Kâ-lâ ke sûn-é, Yâ-thu-tun ke heu-thoi.18 Li-vi-ngìn chúng-khiung yû lióng-pak pat-sṳ̍p si-miàng he̍t chhai sṳn-sàng Yâ-lu-sat-lâng.19 Sṳn-thien sú-ví yû:Â-kuk, Tha̍t-mùn, lâu kì-têu ke chhîn-su̍k chúng-khiung yit-pak chhit-sṳ̍p ngi-miàng.20 Khì-thâ ke Yî-set-lie̍t-ngìn, chi-sṳ̂, Li-vi-ngìn liù chhai Yù-thai ke khì-thâ sàng-chṳ́n, kì-têu ke ló-vuk.21 Sṳn-thien kûng-ngìn he̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Ngò-fî-le̍t; Sî-ha̍p lâu Kî-sṳ̂-thiap kón-lî kì-têu.22 He̍t chhai Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ke Li-vi-ngìn, kì-têu ke kam-tuk he Ha̍p-sâ-pí-ngâ ke sûn-é, Pâ-nì ke lai-é Vû-sî. Kì ke chú-siên he Mâ-thâ-nì lâu Mí-kâ, su̍k Â-sat chûng-chhu̍k, chhiu-he chhai Song-ti ke sṳn-thien fu-chit yîm-ngo̍k ke chûng-chhu̍k.23 Vòng yû min-lin, kûi-thin liá chûng-chhu̍k yin-kôi ngióng-pân lùn-liù tai-liâng tak-ngit ke sṳn-thien yîm-ngo̍k.24 Mí-sṳ-sat-phe̍t ke lai-é Pí-thâ-hî-ngâ su̍k Chhia-lâ chûng-chhu̍k, Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k; kì pông-chhu Pô-sṳ̂ fòng-ti phan-lî Yù-thai-ngìn ke sṳ. Khì-thâ Sàng-chṳ́n ke Ngìn25 Chhin-tô ngìn he̍t chhai kì-têu nùng-chhûn fu-khiun ke hiông-chṳ́n. Yù-thai kî-chhu̍k ke ngìn he̍t chhai Kî-lie̍t-Â-pâ, Tí-pún, Ya̍p-kap-siet, lâu liá-têu sàng fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông.26 Kì-têu ya yû-ngìn he̍t chhai Yâ-sû-â, Mô-lâ-thai, Pak-Pha-lie̍t,27 Ha̍p-sat-Sû-â, Phe̍t-sṳ-pâ lâu chû-vì ke chhûn-chông.28 Kì-têu he̍t chhai Sé-kiet-lâ-sàng, chhai Mí-kô-nâ lâu chû-vì ke chhûn-chông,29 chhai Yîm-Lìm-mùn, Só-lâ, Yâ-ma̍t,30 Sat-nò-â, Â-thu-làn, lâu liá-têu hiông-chṳ́n fu-khiun ke chhûn-chông. Kì-têu he̍t chhai Lâ-kit lâu fu-khiun ke nùng-chhûn, Â-sî-kâ lâu chû-vì ke chhûn-chông. Ya chhiu-he kóng, Yù-thai-ngìn he̍t ke só-chhai he tui Phe̍t-sṳ-pâ chhṳ̍t-to Hiûn-nun sân-lak.31 Phien-ngâ-mén kî-chhu̍k ke ngìn he̍t chhai Kâ-pâ, Me̍t-mat, Ngie, Pak-thi̍t-li lâu chû-vì ke chhûn-chông,32 Â-nâ-thut, Nò-pak, Â-nàn-ngâ,33 Ha-só, Lâ-mâ, Kî-thâ-yîm,34 Ha̍p-thia̍p, Sé-phiên, Nì-pat-lâ,35 Lò-tet, Â-nò, lâu Sṳ̂-fu ke sân-lak.36 Yû-têu sṳt he̍t chhai Yù-thai thú-thi ke Li-vi-ngìn su-phai lâu Phien-ngâ-mén-ngìn khiung-ha he̍t.
