Ngài ya fûn-fu kì-têu oi tui Yâ-lu-sat-lâng ngìn-mìn tông-chûng chṳ́-phai sú-ví, yit phu-fun ngìn khon-sú thi̍t-phe̍t ke thi-tiám, khì-thâ ke chhiu chhai chhṳ-kâ vuk-hâ chû-vì sùn-lò.
