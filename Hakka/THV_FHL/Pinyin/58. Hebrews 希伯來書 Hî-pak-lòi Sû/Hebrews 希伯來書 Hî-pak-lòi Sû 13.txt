1 Ngióng-pân Tet Song-ti ke Fôn-hí Ngì-têu oi yit-chhṳ̍t chhiong hiûng-thi chí-moi án-ngiòng siông-siak.2 M̀-hó m̀ ki-tet chiap-thai ngoi-hiông-ngìn hi ngì-têu vuk-hâ. Sṳt yû-ngìn án-ngiòng hàng, vù-yi-chûng sa chiap-thai-tó thiên-sṳ́.3 Oi koân-sîm ke-têu chhô-kâm ke ngìn, chhiong-he ngì-têu ya thùng kì-têu khiung-ha chhô-kâm. Oi koân-sîm ke-têu chhai fam-nan chûng ke ngìn, chhiu-chhiong ngì-têu ya chhai fam-nan chûng yit-yong.4 Mî-yit-ke ngìn lûng-chúng oi chûn-chhung fûn-yîn; fû-chhî oi pí-chhṳ́ chûng-sṳ̍t. Song-ti voi sṳ́m-phan ke-têu fong-thong lâu yìm-lon ke ngìn.5 Ngì-têu ke ngìn-sên m̀-hó chúng-he sióng-oi chhon-chhièn; oi mân-chiuk chhṳ-kâ só yû ke. Yîn-vi Song-ti sṳt kóng: "Ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi lì-khôi ngì, yún-yén m̀-voi fit-thet ngì."6 Só-yî, ngài-têu cho-tet thai-tám kóng:Chú he ngài ke pông-chhu,ngài m̀-sṳ́ kiâng;ngìn nèn tui ngài ngióng-pân nò?7 Ngì-têu oi ki-ngiam thèu-pái tai-liâng ngì-têu, chhòn Song-ti ke chṳ̂n-tho pûn ngì-têu ke ngìn. Hó-hó sióng khon-é, kì-têu he ngióng-pân sên-fa̍t, ngióng-pân sí; ngì-têu oi háu-fap kì-têu ke sin-sîm.8 Yâ-sû Kî-tuk chhô-pû-ngit, kîm-pû-ngit, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yén, ya m̀-voi kói-pien.9 M̀-hó pûn kok-yong khì-koai ke ho̍k-sot chiông ngì-têu tai chéu-chán-hi. Ngì-têu ke sîm oi yí-kho Song-ti ke ên-tién lòi tet-tó kiên-ku, m̀-hó yí-kho sṳ̍t-vu̍t ke li-kûi; cheu ke-têu li-kûi hi-hàng ke ngìn pin mò tet-tó má-ke hó-chhu.10 Chhai sṳn-chong-phùng tî-poi sṳ-fung ke Yù-thai chi-sṳ̂, mò khièn hó sṳ̍t ngài-têu chi-thàn-táng ke chi-vu̍t.11 Yù-thai ke thai-chi-sṳ̂ chiông thèu-sâng ke hiet tai-ngi̍p chṳ-sṳn-só, hien-cho su̍k-chhui-chi; chúng-he ke-têu thèu-sâng ke sṳ̂n-thí he chhai yàng-ngoi sêu.12 Só-yî, vi-tó oi yung chhṳ-kâ ke hiet lòi kiet-chhiang ngìn ke chhui, Yâ-sû chhiu su-khú, sí chhai sàng-ngoi.13 Án-ngiòng, ngài-têu lòi-hi yàng-ngoi chhiu-khiun kì, fûn-tâm kì só-su ke vú-yu̍k.14 Yîn-vi chhai sṳ-kiên, ngài-têu mò yún-yén chhùn-chhai ke sàng; ngài-têu só chûi-khiù ke he chiông-lòi ke sàng.15 Só-yî, ngài-têu yin-kôi thûng-ko Yâ-sû, sòng-sòng yung siung-chan cho chi-vu̍t lòi hien pûn Song-ti, chhiu-he yung ngài-têu ke choi ko-pha̍k Kì ke miàng.16 Chúng-he, ya m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet hàng-san lâu pí-chhṳ́ fûn-hióng, yîn-vi liá-chúng ke chi-vu̍t he Song-ti só fôn-hí ke.17 Oi fu̍k-chhiùng ngì-têu ke liâng-chhiu, thâng kì-têu ke chṳ́-thô. Kì-têu yit-chhṳ̍t khon-ku ngì-têu ke lìn-fùn, yîn-vi kì-têu oi tui Song-ti fu-chit, chhiong oi kâu-chong yit-yong. Ngì-têu thâng-chhiùng kì-têu, kì-têu chhiu voi cho-to chhin fôn-hí; na mò, kì-têu voi lâng-sîm théu thai-hi, liá tui ngì-têu yû má-ke li-yit nè?18 Chhiáng ngì-têu ki-siu̍k thoi ngài-têu khì-tó. Ngài-têu chhṳ-sin liòng-sîm chhîn-pha̍k, lo̍k-yi sùi-sṳ̀ sùi-thi hàng chṳn-lu.19 Ngài thi̍t-phe̍t oi chhiáng ngì-têu thoi ngài khì-tó, pûn ngài chhin-kiak to-chón ngì-têu ke-vi hi. Khì-tó20 Song-ti yí-kîn sṳ́ ngài-têu ke Chú Yâ-sû tui sí fu̍k-fa̍t. Kì thûng-ko Yâ-sû só liù ke hiet yin-chṳn liáu yún-yén ke yok, sṳ́ kì sàng-cho chung yòng-khiùn ke Thai-mu̍k-chá. Ngien su phìn-ôn ke Song-ti sṳ̀n-chhiòn ngì-têu, pûn ngì-têu hàng tak-yong ke hó-sṳ, lòi chûn-hàng Kì ke chṳ́-yi! Ngien Kì thûng-ko Yâ-sû Kî-tuk chhai ngài-têu sṳ̂n-song sṳ̀n-chhiu Kì só fôn-hí ke sṳ! Ngien yùng-yeu kûi-pûn Kî-tuk, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén! Â-mén. Kiet-mî ke Fa21 併於上節22 Hiûng-thi chí-moi â, ngài khiù ngì-têu nai-sîm thâng ngài ke khien-miên; yîn-vi ngài siá pûn ngì-têu ke sin-é, pin m̀-he chhin chhòng.23 Ngài po ngì-têu tî, ngài-têu ke hiûng-thi Thì-mô-thai yí-kîn tet-tó sṳt-fong lé. Kì na-he kha chó lòi, ngài voi thùng kì khiung-ha hi khon ngì-têu.24 Chhiáng thoi ngài-têu tui ngì-têu só-yû ke liâng-chhiu lâu chung sṳn-thù chhiáng-ôn. Tui Yi-thai-li lòi ke ngìn chhiòn-phu ya tui ngì-têu chhiáng-ôn.25 Ngien Song-ti su ên-tién pûn ngì-têu thai-kâ!
