1 Fap-li̍t pin m̀-he chiông-lòi ke-têu mî-hó sṳ-vu̍t ke chṳ̂n-siong, he ke-têu sṳ-vu̍t ke yáng-siong thin-thin. Khiung-yong ke chi-vu̍t tak-ngièn yit-chhṳ̍t hien-song, fap-li̍t yu ngióng-pân cho-tet thûng-ko liá-têu chi-vu̍t sṳ́ lòi-to Song-ti mien-chhièn ke ngìn sàng-cho vàn-chhiòn nè?2 Na-he yû khó-nèn, yit-chhiet ke hien-chi chó chhiu thìn-chṳ́ lô! Yîn-vi ke-têu kin-pai Song-ti ke ngìn, ki-yèn chhui yí-kîn tet-tó kiet-chhiang, chhiu m̀-voi chai yû chhui ke kám-kok lé.3 Sṳ-sṳ̍t-song, tak-ngièn ke hien-chi fán-chón sṳ́ ngìn sióng-hí chhṳ-kâ ke chhui,4 yîn-vi ngiù-kú lâu sân-yòng ke hiet mò fap-thu chhù-thet ngìn ke chhui.5 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, Kî-tuk lòi-to sṳ-kiên ke sṳ̀, tui Song-ti kóng:Ngì mò-oi chi-vu̍t lâu kiûng-vu̍t;Ngì yí-kîn vi ngài chún-phi sṳ̂n-thí.6 Ngì ya mò kak-yi sêu-fa-chi lâu su̍k-chhui-chi.7 Só-yî ngài kóng:Khon â, ngài lòi lé!Song-ti â, ngài lòi he oi chûn-hàng Ngì ke chṳ́-yi,chhiu-chhiong Fap-li̍t-sû só ki-chai koân-he ngài ke sṳ.8 Yit khôi-sṳ́, kì kóng: "Ngì mò-oi, ya mò kak-yi chi-vu̍t lâu kiûng-vu̍t, sêu-fa-chi lâu su̍k-chhui-chi." Sûi-yèn liá-têu chi-vu̍t he cheu fap-li̍t ke kûi-thin só-hien ke, kì hàn án-ngiòng kóng.9 Chiap-tén, kì yu kóng: "Khon â, ngài lòi lé, he oi chûn-hàng Ngì ke chṳ́-yi." Khó-kien, Song-ti chhí-sêu siên-chhièn yit-chhiet ke hien-chi, oi yung Kî-tuk ke hî-sên lòi thoi-von.10 Cheu Song-ti liá-ke chṳ́-yi, Yâ-sû Kî-tuk hien chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̂n-thí, yit-pái chhiu yèn-mân, pûn ngài-têu sṳn-fa lòi kûi Chú.11 Mî-yit-ke Yù-thai ke chi-sṳ̂ tak-ngit khî-tén sṳ-fung Song-ti, sòng-sòng hien khiung-yong ke chi-vu̍t; chúng-he liá-têu chi-vu̍t yún-yén mò fap-thu chhù-thet ngìn ke chhui.12 Chúng-he, Kî-tuk hien su̍k-chhui-chi yit-pái, yún-yén chhiu yû-háu, yèn-heu chhô chhai Song-ti ke yu-phién,13 tén-heu Song-ti pûn kì ke thi̍t-ngìn hòng-fu̍k chhai kì ke kiok-hâ.14 Yîn-vi kì hien-chi yit-pái, ke-têu sṳn-fa kûi Chú ke ngìn chhiu yún-yén vàn-chhiòn.15 Sṳn-Lìn ya tui ngài-têu cho-chṳn; Kì siên kóng:16 Chú án-ngiòng kóng:Chhai chiông-lòi ke-têu ngit-è,Ngài oi lâu kì-têu li̍p liá-chúng ke yok:Ngài oi chiông Ngài ke fap-li̍t piong-ngi̍p kì-têu ke sîm,siá chhai kì-têu ke thèu-nó.17 Yèn-heu, Kì kóng: "Ngài mò-oi chai ki-tet kì-têu ke chhui-ok lâu ko-fam."18 Ki-yèn liá-têu chhui tet-tó sa-miên, chhiu mò sî-yeu chai yû su̍k-chhui-chi lé. Ngài-têu lòi Chhîn-khiun Song-ti19 Só-yî, hiûng-thi chí-moi â, thûng-ko Yâ-sû liù-hiet to sí, ngài-têu chhiu cho-tet ôn-sîm ngi̍p-hi chṳ-sṳn-só.20 Yâ-sû yí-kîn vi ngài-têu khôi yit-thiàu yu sîn yu sâng ke lu; liá-thiàu lu theu-ko pu-liàm, chhiu-he thûng-ko kì ke sṳ̂n-thí.21 Ki-yèn ngài-têu yû yit-ke thai-chi-sṳ̂ chóng-kón Song-ti ke kâ-thìn,22 án-ngiòng, ngài-têu yin-kôi yung sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke sîm, kiên-thin ke sin-sîm, yung yí-kîn kiet-chhiang, mò sià-ok ke liòng-sîm, lâu chhiang-súi sé-ko ke sṳ̂n-thí, lòi chhîn-khiun Song-ti.23 Ngài-têu oi kiên-thin chhṳ̀-sú ngài-têu só ko-pha̍k ke phan-mong, yîn-vi yin-hí ngài-têu ke Song-ti he sin-sṳ̍t ke.24 Ngài-têu oi pí-chhṳ́ koân-sîm, kit-fat oi-sîm, miên-li hàng-san.25 Yû-têu ngìn piong-thet chhâm-kâ chhi-fi ke si̍p-koan; m̀-hó ho̍k kì-têu, fán-chón oi fu-siông khien-miên. Ngì-têu ki-yèn tî Chú ke ngit-è voi to lé, hàn-kha oi án-ngiòng.26 Yîn-vi ngài-têu ngin-sṳt chṳ̂n-lî yî-heu, na-he hàn ku-yi fam-chhui, chhiu m̀-voi chai yû su̍k-chhui ke chi-vu̍t lé;27 chṳ́-yû kiâng-kiâng lu̍k-lu̍k tén-heu sṳ́m-phan lâu ke oi sêu-me̍t tui-thi̍t Song-ti ke ngìn ke lie̍t-fó!28 Vì-fam Mô-sî fap-li̍t ke, yû lióng-sâm-ke ngìn cho-chṳn, chhiu yit-thin oi chhú sí-hìn, tet m̀-tó lìn-mén.29 Án-ngiòng, chien-tha̍p Song-ti ke Lai-é, khon-khiâng Song-ti li̍p-yok ke hiet — chhiu-he kiet-chhiang kì ke chhui ke hiet, yu vú-yu̍k su ên-tién ke Lìn, liá-chúng ngìn voi ngióng-pân nè? Sióng khon-é, kì kám m̀-he voi su-tó hàn-kha chhûng ke hìn-fa̍t mâ?30 Ngài-têu tî he má-ngìn kóng: "Sṳ̂n-yên chhai Ngài, Ngài yit-thin oi po-yin"; yu kóng: "Chú oi sṳ́m-phan Kì ke chṳ́-mìn."31 Lo̍k chhai yún-sên Song-ti ke sú-chûng he ngióng-pân tet-ngìn-kiâng â!32 Oi ki-tet vông-sṳ̀ ke ngit-è. Song-ti kông-cheu ngì-têu yî-heu, sûi-yèn tù-tó chhin-tô thung-khú, chúng-he ngì-têu chhai fún-teu-chûng pin mò sṳt-phai.33 Yû-sṳ̀, ngì-têu sṳt tông-chung pûn-ngìn vú-yu̍k lâu khú-thu̍k; yû-sṳ̀, ya sṳt thùng ke-têu tù-tó liá-têu khú-nan ke ngìn khî khiung-ha.34 Ngì-têu fûn-tâm chhô-kâm ke ngìn ke khú-nan. Chhòi-sán pûn-ngìn chhióng-chéu, ngì-têu ya fôn-hí ngiûn-su; yîn-vi ngì-têu tî, ngì-têu yû hàn-kha hó yu chhòng-chhùn ke sán-ngia̍p.35 Só-yî, ngì-têu ke yúng-hi m̀-hó mò-thet-hi; liá yúng-hi voi tai pûn ngì-têu khi̍t-thai ke chióng-sóng.36 Vi-tó sṳ̍t-hàng Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi pin liâng-su Kì ke yin-hí, ngì-têu thin-chho̍k oi ngiûn-nai.37 Yîn-vi Sṳn-kîn kóng:Kâ yit-ha-é,ke oi lòi ke chhiu voi to,m̀-voi yèn-chhàn.38 Su̍k Ngài ke ngi-ngìn voi yîn-vi sin tet-tó sâng-miang.Chúng-he, kì na thui-heu,Ngài chhiu m̀ fôn-hí kì.39 Hó-tet ngài-têu m̀-he thui-heu chṳ-to me̍t-mòng ke ngìn; ngài-têu he yû sin-sîm lòi tet-kiu ke ngìn.
