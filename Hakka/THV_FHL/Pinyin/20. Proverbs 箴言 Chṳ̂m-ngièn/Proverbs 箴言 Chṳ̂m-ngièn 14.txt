1 Chṳ-fi ke fu-ńg kien-li̍p kâ-thìn;ngong-tong ke fu-ngìn chhîn-sú chhak-san.2 Hàng-vì chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn kin-vi Song-chú;hàng-sṳ kúi-cha ke ngìn chhiu méu-sṳ Kì.3 Ngong-ngìn yîn-vi kiêu-ngau tô-choi su piên-tá;chṳ-fi ngìn ke ngièn-ngî voi pó-fu chhṳ-kâ.4 Mò ngiù hó kâng-chung, ńg-kuk khiam-sû;yû ngiù then chhut-li̍t, ńg-kuk fûng-tên.5 Sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke chṳn-ngìn m̀-voi kóng fâ-liâu;cho ká kien-chṳn ke chôn-mùn kóng pha̍k-chhe̍t.6 Ngau-man ngìn chhìm chṳ-fi chúng-he chhìm m̀-tó;mìn-lî ke ngìn kha yùng-yi tet-tó chṳ-sṳt.7 Ngì oi yén-lì ngong-tong ngìn,yîn-vi tui kì ke choi tet m̀-tó chṳ-sṳt.8 Chhûng-mìn ngìn ke chṳ-fi pûn kì tî tông-hàng ke lu;ngong-tong ngìn ngong-to chhṳ-kâ phien chhṳ-kâ.9 Ngong-tong ngìn chiông vàn chhui-chai khon-cho hó-seu( 28 14:9 "chiông vàn chhui-chai khon-cho hó-seu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sâu-nat su̍k-ko-sṳt-chi".);chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn hiáu-tet fu-siông sṳ̂-ên.10 Sîm-kôn-tú ke sèu-khú chhṳ-kâ chang tî;sîm-kôn-tú ke hí-lo̍k phe̍t-ngìn mò-fun.11 Sià-ok ngìn ke fòng-vuk yit-thin vang-thet;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke vuk-hâ thin-chho̍k hîn-vong.12 Yû yit-thiàu lu khon-lòi chhin-chhṳ̍t,chúng-he chui-mî theu-to sí-mòng.13 Fôn-seu tông-chûng, sîm ya voi sông-thung;fôn-lo̍k ko-hi, hàn-he voi yù-sèu.14 Ok( 29 14:14 "Ok" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chṳ́-to "Sîm-chûng poi-tho ke ngìn".) yû ok-po;san yû san-po.15 Ngong-chhṳ̍t ngìn sṳ-sṳ siông-sin;chîn-mìn ngìn phu-phu séu-sîm.16 Chṳ-fi ngìn hàng-sṳ séu-sîm, yén-lì fo-tôn;ngong-tong ngìn lû-fû yu ngang-kiáng.17 Tông-kiak pot-at ke ngìn cho-sṳ fù-thù;sîm-fài kúi-kie ke ngìn chhin tet-ngìn-nâu.18 Ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn chhṳ-kâ sṳ̀n-su ngong-tong ke kiet-kó;thûng-tha̍t ke ngìn tet-tó chṳ-sṳt cho kì ke miên-liù.19 Fái-ngìn chhai hó-ngìn mien-chhièn tâi-thèu;sià-ok ngìn hòng-fu̍k chhai chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn mùn-héu.20 Khiùng-khú ngìn lièn lìn-sa tû nâu kì;hó-ngiak ngìn phèn-yû kín-lòi kín-tô.21 Méu-sṳ lìn-sa ke yû-chhui;lìn-mén khiùng-khú ke yû-fuk.22 Sióng-cho fái-sṳ ke, kám m̀-voi ham-ngi̍p mì-thù?Sióng-oi hàng-san ke, tet-tó yìn-chhṳ̀ lâu chûng-sṳ̀n.23 Sat-mâng kûng-chok, tai-lòi chhòi-fu,kûi-ngit khûng-thàm, chṳ-to khiùng-khú.24 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke chhòi-fu cho kì-têu ke miên-liù;ngong-tong ngìn ke ngì-ngong kiet-khiu̍k hàn-he ngì-ngong.25 Sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke kien-chṳn kiu ngìn sâng-miang;hî-ká ke kien-chṳn voi sông-hoi ngìn.26 Kin-vi Song-chú ke chhiu yû yí-kho;kì ke chṳ́-ńg ya yû phit-nan-só.27 Kin-vi Song-chú he sâng-miang ke chhièn-ngièn,voi sṳ́-ngìn lì-khôi sí-mòng ke lò-mióng.28 Ngìn-mìn tô he kiûn-vòng ke kông-yùng;ngìn-mìn séu siong-chṳ̂n vòng-sṳt sôi-phai.29 Mò sùi-phien fat-khién, thai-yû chhûng-mìn;thet yùng-yi pot-at, kâ-thiâm ngong-tong.30 Sîm-phìn hi-fò sṳ̂n-thí không-khien;chhit-tu hoi-ngìn sṳ̂n-sîm phai-fái.31 Khî-fu khiùng-khú ngìn he vú-yu̍k chhóng-chho Chú;lìn-mén kân-khú ke ngìn chhiu-he chûn-kin Chú.32 Sià-ok ngìn chhai fam-nan chûng thin-chho̍k voi kang-tó( 30 14:32 "Sià-ok ngìn ... kang-tó" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Sià-ok ngìn voi pûn chhṳ-kâ ke vàng-hàng kang-tó".);chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn lìm-chûng ke sṳ̀ hàn yû-hó yí-kho( 31 14:32 "chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ... yí-kho" yû-têu kú yi̍t-pún yi̍t-cho "chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn yîn chhṳ-kâ sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t pit-mùng pó-sú".).33 Chṳ-fi chhai chhûng-mìn ngìn ke sîm-chûng ôn-kî,chhai ngong-tong ngìn sîm-chûng mò hién-mìn chhut-lòi( 32 14:33 "mò hién-mìn chhut-lòi" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "oi hién-mìn chhut-lòi".).34 Chṳn-ngi sṳ́ koet-kâ khiòng-sṳn;chhui-ok sṳ́ mìn-chhu̍k mùng-siû.35 Mìn-lî ke phu̍k-ngìn mùng kiûn-vòng pô-chióng;hàng-sṳ kien-seu ke thin-chho̍k su chit-fa̍t.
