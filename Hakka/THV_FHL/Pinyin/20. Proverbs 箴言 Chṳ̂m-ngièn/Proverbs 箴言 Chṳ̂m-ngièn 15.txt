1 Yin-tap vûn-fò, phìn-sit fun-nu;ngièn-ngî chhû-phau, ngiâ-ngìn fat-khién.2 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke sa̍t-mà chhòn-kóng chṳ-sṳt;ngong-tong ngìn ke choi-sùn thu-chhut ngong-fa.3 Song-chú ke muk-chû khon-tó tak só-chhai;fái-ngìn lâu hó-ngìn Kì lûng-chúng kam-chhat.4 Vûn-fò ke sa̍t-mà tai-pûn ngìn sâng-miang( 33 15:4 "Vûn-fò ... sâng-miang" chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Yî-chhṳ ke sa̍t-mà he sâng-miang-su".);kiên-cha ke fa-ngî voi hoi-ngìn sîm-sui.5 Ngong-tong ngìn khon-khiâng â-pâ ke kón-kau;thâng-chhiùng khien-va ke chhiu-he chîn-mìn ngìn.6 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn kâ-chûng chhòi-sán tô-tô;sià-ok ngìn tet-li tai-lòi fàn-lò.7 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke choi siên-yòng chṳ-sṳt;ngong-tong ngìn ke sîm m̀-he án-ngiòng.8 Song-chú chhin-nâu sià-ok ngìn ke hien-chi;Kì tông fôn-hí chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke khì-tó;9 Song-chú chhin-nâu sià-ok ngìn ke hàng-vì;Kì tông fôn-hí chûi-khiù chṳn-ngi ke ngìn.10 Poi-lì chṳn-tho, oi su ngiàm-ngiàm ke chit-fa̍t;khî-chhie̍t khien-va, yit-thin voi sóng-sṳt sâng-miang.11 Yîm-kiên lâu me̍t-mòng tû chhai Song-chú kam-chhat hâ;sṳ-kiên-ngìn ke sîm ngióng-pân nèn khong tet-he̍t nè?12 Ngau-man ngìn mò-oi thâng khien-va;kì mò-oi chhîn-khiun chṳ-fi ngìn.13 Sîm-chûng hí-lo̍k, mien tai seu-yùng;sîm-tú yù-sèu, sîm-sṳ̀n sún-sông.14 Chhûng-mìn ngìn ke sîm chhìm-khiù chṳ-sṳt;ngong-tong ngìn ke choi sài-sṳ̍t hâm-chhê.15 Khun-khú ke ngìn m̀-tet chu-am;lo̍k-sien ke ngìn tak-ngit phông-phai.16 Nèn-khó chhòi-sán mò-tô, kin-vi Song-chú,yàng-ko kâ-chhòi van-kon, fàn-lò put-ôn.17 Tân-chhiang chhû-chhà thâm-fan, thai-kâ siông-siak,yàng-ko chiú-ngiuk mân-chok, pí-chhṳ́ yên-kâ.18 Phì-hi fó-phau ngiâ-hí chên-tôn;sîm-phìn hi-fò phìn-sit fûn-chên.19 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn ke lu net-teu set-set;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke tho phiàng-phiàng sun-sun.20 Chṳ-fi ke lai-é pûn yà-è fôn-hí;ngong-ngong ke lai-é khon-khiâng kiâ ôi-é.21 Mò chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn khon ngì-ngong cho khoai-lo̍k;yû kien-sṳt ke ngìn cheu-tén chṳn-chhṳ̍t hàng-sṳ.22 Mò siên chhâm-siòng, yit-thin sṳt-phai;thai-kâ ngiên-kiu, chang voi sṳ̀n-kûng.23 Hiáu-tet yin-tui, pûn-ngìn sóng-khoai;kóng-fa ha̍p-sṳ̀, sṳ̍t-chhai hàn-hó.24 Chhûng-mìn ngìn tui sâng-miang ke lu sông-hi,oi yén-yén lì-khôi hâ-poi ke yîm-kiên.25 Song-chú oi chhak kiêu-ngau ngìn ke vuk;Kì oi pó-sú koá-fu ke kâ-sán.26 Song-chú chhin-nâu sià-ok ke yîm-mèu;Kì tông fôn-hí sùn-kiet ke ngièn-ngî.27 Thâm-oi vàng-chhòi voi hoi-tó chhṳ-kâ ke kâ-thìn;khî-chhie̍t put-ngi ke fùng-pâu voi hióng-su chhòng-su.28 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn sâm-sṳ̂ yî-heu chang tap-yin ngìn;sià-ok ngìn ke choi sùi-phien thu-chhut fái-fa.29 Song-chú lì-khôi sià-ok ngìn yén-yén;Kì oi thâng chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke khì-tó.30 Fôn-thèu hí-mien, ngìn-sîm mân-chiuk;thâng hó sêu-sit, kîn-kut sóng-khoai.31 Thâng-chhiùng chho-chhiu ngìn sâng-miang ke chit-phi,chhiu voi he̍t chhai chṳ-fi ngìn ke chûng-kiên.32 Khî-chhie̍t kón-kau, he khon-khiâng chhṳ-kâ;thâng-chhiùng khien-va, chhiu tet-tó chṳ-fi.33 Chṳ-fi kâu ngìn kin-vi Song-chú;chûn-yùng yî-chhièn pit-yû khiâm-pî.
