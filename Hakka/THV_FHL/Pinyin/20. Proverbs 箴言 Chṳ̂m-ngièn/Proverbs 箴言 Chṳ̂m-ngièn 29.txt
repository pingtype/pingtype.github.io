1 Ye̍t su chit-fa̍t chhiu ye̍t ngang-kiáng ke ngìn,voi chit-sṳ̀ phai-fai, mò fap-thu hó-yî.2 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chen-kâ, ngìn-mìn fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k;sià-ok ngìn chṳp-chṳn, ngìn-mìn pî-sông tho-than.3 Oi-mu chṳ-fi ke, sṳ́ â-pâ fôn-hí;kiet-kâu kî-ńg ke, chhòi-sán liáu chhiang-chhiang.4 Kiûn-vòng hàng-sṳ kûng-ngi, koet-kâ chhiu kiên-thin;na-he thâm-vû fú-phai, koet-kâ chhiu me̍t-mòng.5 Fàm he ô-mì fung-sṳ̀n lìn-sa ke,chhiu-he sat lò-mióng pan kì( 68 29:5 "kì" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhṳ-kâ".) ke kiok.6 Sià-ok ngìn fam-chhui, ham-ngi̍p lò-mióng;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhong-kô, fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.7 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chûn-chhung khiùng-khú ngìn ke khièn-li;sià-ok ngìn chhiu mò án-ngiòng ke thùng-chhìn-sîm.8 Ngau-man ngìn chhai sàng-tú chṳ-chho fun-lon;chṳ-fi ngìn phìn-sit pak-siang ke fun-nu.9 Chṳ-fi ngìn khúng-ko ngong-ngìn vàn-chhiòn mò-háu;ngong-ngìn m̀-he fat-khién chhiu thai-seu thai-nau.10 Hau chhṳ̀-ngìn ke chhin-hen vàn-chhiòn ngìn,me sióng-oi hoi chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke miang.11 Ngong-tong ngìn at-tó chhiu piang chhut-lòi;chṳ-fi ngìn hiáu-tet khúng-chṳ phì-hi.12 Chṳp-chṳn-chá na fôn-hí thâng phong-fûng ke fa,kì ke phu-ha yit-thin voi pien-cho kiên-sṳ̀n.13 Khiùng-khú ngìn lâu khî-fu ngìn ke chhai sṳ-kiên tù-tó,Song-chú pûn lióng-sà ke muk-chû phiàng-phiàng khon-tet-tó.14 Kiûn-vòng yung kûng-chṳn phan-ton khiùng-khú ngìn,kì ke vòng-vi chhiu voi yit-chhṳ̍t kiên-thin.15 Yung chuk-siû kón-kau nèn kâ-thiâm chṳ-fi;fong-thong ke chṳ́-ńg sṳ́ â-mê kien-seu.16 Sià-ok ngìn kín tô, chhui-ok kín chen-kâ;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn voi khon-tén kì-têu sôi-phai.17 Oi kón-kau ngì ke lai-é,kì chhiu sṳ́ ngì yû phìn-ôn,ya pûn ngì ke sîm khoai-lo̍k.18 Na mò Song-ti ke yi-siong, ngìn-mìn chhiu lám-sám lòi;chûn-sú Song-ti kau-sṳ ke, sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!19 Tân-tân yung kóng-fa, kón-kau phu̍k-ngìn mò-háu;sûi-yèn kì liáu-kié, hàn-he mò-oi chhap ngì.20 Ngì khon-tó kóng-fa sùi-phien ke ngìn mò?Ngong-tong ngìn pí kì hàn-kha yû hî-mong.21 Ngìn chiông phu̍k-ngìn chhiùng-se chiúng-to thai,liá phu̍k-ngìn to-mî chhiu voi pa-cham kì ke chhòi-sán( 69 29:21 "pa-cham kì ke chhòi-sán" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sṳ́ kì chhôi-ko" ya-he "pien lai-é", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).22 Yùng-yi fat-khién ke voi ngiâ-hí siông-châng,chhin-kiak fat phì-hi ke tô-tô fam-chhui.23 Ngìn ke kiêu-ngau sṳ́ kì pî-mì;sîm chhùn khiâm-pî su-ngìn chûn-chhung.24 Ngìn lâu chhe̍t-é kiet-tóng, chhiu-he hoi chhṳ-kâ;kì chhai fap-thìn fat-sṳ, m̀-kám kóng sṳ̍t-fa.25 Ngìn só-kiâng ke, voi sàng-cho kì ke ham-chiáng;sin-kho Song-chú, kì chhiu tet ôn-yèn chhṳ-chhai.26 Ngìn-ngìn sióng khiù thúng-chhṳ-chá ke ên-fi;khì-sṳ̍t chṳ́-yû Song-chú sṳ̂n-chông chṳn-ngi.27 Ngi-ngìn nâu put-ngi ke ngìn;ok-ngìn hen chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.
