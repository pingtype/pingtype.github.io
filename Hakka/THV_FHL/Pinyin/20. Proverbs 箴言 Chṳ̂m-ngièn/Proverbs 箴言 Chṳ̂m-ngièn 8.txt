1 Chṳ-fi Siung Thâng ô, chṳ-fi fû-ham,chhûng-mìn chhut-sâng kín-hêm.2 Kì chhai sân-kông-táng ke lu-sùn,yu chhai sṳ̍p-sṳ lu-héu khî-tén;3 kì chhai sàng-mùn héu,chhai sàng-mùn sùn, thai-sâng hêm-kóng:4 "Chung-ngìn â, ngài hiong ngì-têu fû-ham;ngài hiong sṳ-kiên-ngìn chhut-sâng chhin-hêm.5 Ngì-têu ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn â,oi ho̍k kha-chîn têu-é!Ngì-têu ngong-tong ke ngìn â,oi kha sông-kâu têu-é!6 Thâng ô, yîn-vi ngài oi kóng kô-kui ke fa;ngài lâu ngì-têu kóng ke he chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke sṳ.7 Ngài choi só-kóng ke lûng-chúng he chṳ̂n-lî;ngài chhie̍t-tui mò-oi kóng sià-ok ke fa.8 Ngài ke fa lûng-he chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t mò-ká,mò vân-khiuk, mò koâi-phiak.9 Tui chhûng-mìn ke ngìn lòi-kóng,yit-chhiet chhîn-chhú;tui thûng-tha̍t ke ngìn lòi-kóng,chhiòn-phu chṳn-khok.10 Yin-tông tho̍k ngài ke kau-hiun,mò-oi nâ pha̍k-ngiùn;yin-tông sién chṳ-sṳt,mò-oi nâ vòng-kîm;11 yîn-vi chṳ-fi hó-ko chû-pó;ngì só sióng-oi ke mò yit-hong pí-et-tet.12 "Ngài he Chṳ-fi, ngài yû kien-sṳt;ngài yû chṳ-sṳt lâu chṳn-khok ke phan-ton li̍t.13 Kin-vi Song-chú chhiu-he yen-hen sià-ok;kiêu-ngau, áu-màn, ok-pa lâu fâ-liâu,ngài chhiòn-phu chhin-nâu.14 Ngài yû kie-va̍k lâu chṳ̂n chṳ-sṳt;ngài yû chhûng-mìn, ngài yû nèn-li̍t.15 Ngài pông-chhu kiûn-vòng thúng-chhṳ,hia̍p-chhu chóng-khièn-chá thin kûng-tong ke fap-li̍t.16 Só-yû ke thúng-chhṳ-chá lûng-chúng yí-kho ngài;chṳp-chṳn-chá lâu sṳ́m-phan-kôn thûng-ko ngài chóng-khièn.17 Siak ngài ke, ngài ya siak kì;pet-chhiet chhìm ngài ke, yit-thin chhìm tet-tó.18 Hó-ngiak lâu hó miàng-sâng he ngài sóng-su;fu-kui lâu sṳ̀n-kûng ya chhai ngài sú-chûng.19 Ngài só kiet-chhut ke kó-chṳ́ yàng-ko sùn-kîm;ngài ke chhut-sán yàng-ko chui-hó ke ngiùn-è.20 Ngài chhai chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke lu-song hàng,chhai kûng-ngi ke lu-hong chhièn-chin.21 Ngài su chhòi-fu pûn siak ngài ke ngìn;ngài sṳ́ kì-têu ke chhông-khu mân-mân.22 Chṳ-fi ke Hí-thèu "Song-chú chhai thai-chhû chhóng-chho ke sṳ̀,Kì chhai hí-thèu chhóng-chho van-vu̍t yî-chhièn,yí-kîn yû ngài( 13 8:22 "yí-kîn yû ngài" ya-he yi̍t-cho "siên chhóng-chho ngài".).23 Chhai thai-chhû, thai-thi màng sṳ̀n-hìn yî-chhièn,tui chhin kú-chó, ngài yí-kîn su sat-li̍p.24 Thai-hói hàn-màng chhut-hien,thai-súi ke chhièn-ngièn màng-yû yî-chhièn,ngài yí-kîn kiung chhut-lòi.25 Thai-sân hàn-màng kiên-thin,se-sân hàn-màng yû yî-chhièn,ngài yí-kîn kiung chhut-lòi.26 Ke-sṳ̀, Song-chú hàn-màng chho thai-thi lâu thièn-yèn,lièn yit-kha̍k nài tû hàn-màng yû.27 Kì sat-li̍p thiên-khûng,Kì chhai hói-mien thin thi-phìn-sien ke sṳ̀,ngài yí-kîn chhai ke-vi.28 Kì chhai thiên-khûng pu-chṳ yùn,chhai hói-tái khôi-fat súi-ngièn,29 Kì vi thai-hói thin-chhut kie-han,pûn hói-súi m̀-voi chhêu-chhut fam-vì;Kì vi thai-thi kien-li̍p kîn-kî,ke-sṳ̀, ngài yí-kîn chhai ke-vi.30 Ngài chhai Kì ke sùn-thèu chhiong yit-ke kûng-chhàng-sṳ̂( 14 8:30 "kûng-chhàng-sṳ̂" ya-he yi̍t-cho "se-ngìn-è".) tak-ngit pûn Kì fôn-hí;ngài yit-chhṳ̍t chhai Kì mien-chhièn chhin khoai-lo̍k,31 tông fôn-hí Kì ke sṳ-kie,chhin fôn-hí sṳ-song ke ngìn.32 "Lai-é â, ngì-têu liá-ha oi thâng ngài ke fa;chûn-sú ngài tho ke ngìn, chhiu voi tet-tó hen-fuk.33 Oi thâng-chhiùng ngài ke kau-hiun sàng-cho yû chṳ-fi,m̀-hó fut-lio̍k kì.34 Thâng-chhiùng ngài ke fa,tak-ngit chhai ngài mùn-héu ngióng-mong,chhai ngài vuk-hâ mùn-piên tén-heu ke,liá-ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!35 Yîn-vi chhìm-tó ngài ke,chhiu-he chhìm-tó sâng-miang;kì ya voi tet-tó Song-chú ke ên-fi.36 Mò chhìm-tó ngài ke,chhiu-he chhṳ-kâ hoi chhṳ-kâ;yen-hen ngài ke,lûng-chúng he sióng-oi sí ke ngìn."
