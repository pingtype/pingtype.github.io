1 Oi Sin-kho Song-chú Lai-é â, m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet ngài ke kau-sṳ;ngì oi sṳ̀-sṳ̀ sióng-hí ngài kâu ngì ke kie-min.2 Kì-têu voi pûn ngì hióng-su chhòng-su,si-kui phìn-ôn.3 M̀-hó pûn chûng-sṳ̀n, sin-sṳ̍t lì-khôi ngì;oi chiông liá-têu thak chhai kiáng-hong, khat chhai sîm-pán.4 Án-ngiòng, ngì chhai Song-ti lâu ngìn mien-chhiènchhiu voi tet-tó fôn-hí lâu hó miàng-sâng.5 Ngì oi chôn-sîm lòi sin-kho Song-chú,m̀-hó yí-kho chhṳ-kâ ke chhûng-mìn.6 Ngì hàng-sṳ cho-ngìn chhiòn-phu oi ngióng-mong Chú,Kì chhiu voi chṳ́-sṳ ngì hàng chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke lu.7 M̀-hó láu-tó chhṳ-kâ yû chṳ-fi;oi kin-vi Song-chú, yén-lì sià-ok.8 Án-ngiòng cho, chhiu voi yî-chhṳ ngì ke sṳ̂n-thí,chṳ̂-yông ngì ke kîn-kut.9 Ngì oi yung chhòi-sán lâu thièn-yèn chui-hó ke chhut-sánhien-pûn Song-chú, péu-sṳ tui Kì ke chûn-kin;10 án-ngiòng, ngì ke chhông-khu chhiu voi chhûng-mân ńg-kuk,chiú-thúng chông ke sîn-chiú voi tô-to nêm-chhut-lòi.11 Lai-é â, m̀-hó khon-khiâng Song-chú ke kón-kau,ya m̀-hó nâu Kì ke chit-phi;12 yîn-vi Song-chú kón-kau Kì só thung-siak ke ngìn,chhiu-chhiong â-pâ kón-kau só fôn-hí ke chṳ́-ńg.13 Chṳ-fi ke Ka-chhṳ̍t Chhìm-tó chṳ-fi, tet-tó chhûng-mìn ke,liá-têu ngìn sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi ô!14 Chṳ-fi pí mâi-mai chhon-tó ke ngiùn-è kha-yû ka-chhṳ̍t,pí vòng-kîm só-sâng ke li-sit kha-tô hó-chhu.15 Chṳ-fi pí chû-pó hàn-kha pó-pi;ngì só kak-yi ke mò yit-hong lâu kì pí-et-tet.16 Chṳ-fi ke yu-sú yû chhòng-su,chó-sú yû fu-kui lâu chûn-yùng.17 Chṳ-fi pûn ngì sên-fa̍t khoai-lo̍k,tai-liâng ngì hàng phìn-ôn ke lu.18 Tui kiún-sú kì ke ngìn, kì chhiong sâng-miang-su;liù-tet kì he̍t ke ngìn, chhiu voi yû fuk-hi.19 Song-chú yung chṳ-fi ku-thin thai-thi;Kì sṳt yung chhûng-mìn ôn-sat thiên-khûng.20 Kì yung chṳ-sṳt pûn chhṳ̂m-yên yúng-chhut thai-súi,pûn vû-yùn kong-hâ kâm-lìm.21 Lai-é â, oi kiún-sú chṳ̂n chṳ-fi lâu kien-sṳt,m̀-hó pûn kì-têu lì-khôi ngì ke mien-chhièn;22 kì-têu voi pûn ngì ke sâng-miang tet-tó ngièn-hi,ya sàng-cho ngì kiáng-hong ke pó-pi.23 Ngì chhiu voi hàng phìn-ôn ke lu,ngì ke kiok ya m̀-voi tiet-tó.24 Ngì voi ôn-sîm soi-muk,yit-kau soi-to thiên-kông.25 Ngì m̀-sṳ́ kiâng fut-yèn-kiên lòi-to ke châi-nan,ya m̀-sṳ́ sèu ke lìm-to fái-ngìn ke châi-fo;26 yîn-vi Song-chú he ngì só yí-kho ke;Kì m̀-voi pûn ngì tiet-lo̍k lò-mióng chûng.27 Oi chhin-li̍t pông-chhu sî-yeu pông-chhu ke ngìn;ngì na yû li̍t-liong, m̀-hó thôi-chhṳ̀ hàng-san.28 Ngì liá-ha yû li̍t-liong pông-chhu lìn-sa, chhiu m̀-hó tui kì kóng:"Chón-hi, thiên-kông-ngit chang lòi, ngài chhiu voi pûn ngì."29 Lìn-sa chhai ngì fu-khiun ôn-kî,chhiu m̀-hó sat-kie lòi-hoi kì.30 Phe̍t-ngìn m̀-sṳt sông-hoi ngì,mo̍k vù-ku lâu kì siông-châng.31 M̀-hó hiûn-mu khiòng-phau ke ngìn,m̀-hó thèn kì só-hàng ke lu;32 yîn-vi Song-chú chhin-nâu vàng-hàng ke ngìn,chúng-he Kì chhîn-khiun chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.33 Song-chú chu-chú sià-ok ngìn ke kâ-thìn;Kì su-fuk pûn chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke vuk-hâ.34 Kì sâu-nat ke-têu sâu-nat phe̍t-ngìn ke,chúng-he su ên-tién pûn khiâm-pî ke ngìn.35 Chṳ-fi ngìn voi tet-tó hó miàng-sâng;chúng-he ngong-tong ngìn kâ-thiâm kien-seu.
