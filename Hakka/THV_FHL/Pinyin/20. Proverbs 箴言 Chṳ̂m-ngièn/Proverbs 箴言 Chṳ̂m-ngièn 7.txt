1 Lai-é â, oi chûn-sú ngài ke fa,m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet ngài ke kie-min.2 Chûn-sú ngài ke kie-min,ngì chhiu voi yû fûng-sṳn ke sâng-miang;oi sú ngài ke kau-sṳ,chhiong pó-fu chhṳ-kâ ke muk-chû-yìn.3 Oi thak chhai ngì ke sú-chṳ́-thèu,oi khat chhai ngì ke sîm-pán hong.4 Oi tui Chṳ-fi kóng: "Ngì he ngài ke chí-moi";oi chiông kien-sṳt chún-cho ngì ke chhîn-ngìn.5 Kì-têu chhiu voi pông-chhu ngì yén-lì yìm-fu,lì-khôi phe̍t-ngìn choi-thiàm sa̍t-va̍t ke pû-ngiòng.6 Thì-fòng Pûn Set Mì Yû yit-pái, ngài chhai vuk-tú tui chhûng-mùn khon hâ-hi,7 khon-tó chhin-tô ngong-chhṳ̍t ke heu-sâng-ngìn;ngài thi̍t-phe̍t chu-yi khì-chûng yit-ke thèu-nó kién-tân ke.8 Kì chhai kiê-lu khiun chón-kok ke thi-fông,hàng-ngi̍p yit-ke yìm-fu he̍t ke hong-tú,9 ngit-thèu lo̍k-sân heu, thiên am lé,chhai am sò-sò tông-chûng,hàng-khiûn kì ke mùn-héu.10 Ngài khon-tó yit-ke fu-ńg chhut-lòi ngiàng-chiap kì,tá-pan chhiong chhông-kî, yû kúi-cha ke sîm-sṳ̂.11 Kì yit-liu-lî fong-thong m̀-tî kien-seu,sṳ̀-sòng chhai kiê-hong hàng-lòi hàng-hi,12 yû-sṳ̀ chhai kiê-thèu hong-mî,yû-sṳ̀ chhai sṳ-chhòng tú yín-yu ngìn.13 Kì lâi-tó ke heu-sâng-ngìn, lâu kì chîm-choi,m̀-tî kien-seu tui kì kóng:14 "Ngài kîm-pû-ngit vàn-ngien hien-chi,vuk-hâ hàn-chhûn chhin-tô sên-ngì,15 só-yî ngài chhut-lòi chhìm ngì,chhin sióng-oi lâu ngì kien-mien,mò sióng-tó chhai liá-vi tù-tó ngì!16 Ngài yí-kîn nâ Âi-khi̍p chhái-set chhòng-tânphû-hó ngài ke mìn-chhòng,17 yung mu̍t-yo̍k, chhṳ̀m-hiông, kui-phì,chiông mìn-chhòng hiûn-to hiông-hiông.18 Lòi â, ngài-têu hióng-su oi-chhìn,chhin-chhìn fôn-lo̍k, chhṳ̍t-to thiên-kông.19 Ngài ke ló-kûng mò-chhôi vuk-hâ,hi chhin-yén ke thi-fông lî-hàng.20 Kì tai yit thai-pit chhièn hi,to ngie̍t-pan chang voi chón-lòi."21 Ńg-ngìn yung ô-mì fung-sṳ̀n yín-yu kì,yung choi-thiàm sa̍t-va̍t ke fa mì-fe̍t kì.22 Heu-sâng-ngìn chit-sṳ̀ thèn-tén kì hàng,chhiong ngiù pûn-ngìn khiên-hi thù-chái-chhòng,yu chhiong lu̍k-é tiet-lo̍k ham-chiáng tú( 12 7:22 "chhiong lu̍k-é tiet-lo̍k ham-chiáng tú" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhiong ngong-ngìn tai-tén só-lien hi su chit-fa̍t", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.),23 chhṳ̍t-to li-chien sa-chhôn kì ke sîm-kôn;kì chhiong tiâu-é pî-ngi̍p lò-mióng,vàn-chhiòn m̀-tî chhṳ-kâ ke sâng-miang chhai ngùi-hiám chûng.24 Lai-é â, ngì-têu liá-ha oi thâng-chhiùng ngài,oi chu-yi thâng ngài só-kóng ke fa.25 M̀-hó pûn yìm-fu mì-fe̍t ngì-têu ke sîm;m̀-hó ngong-sṳ̀n ngong-sṳ̀n thèn-tén kì hi.26 Kì yí-kîn sṳ́ hó-tô ngìn vang-tó,yû chhin-tô nàm-ngìn pûn kì hoi-sí.27 Ngì hi kiâ vuk-hâ,he hàng vòng-chhièn lu,lo̍k sí-mòng yîm-kiên.
