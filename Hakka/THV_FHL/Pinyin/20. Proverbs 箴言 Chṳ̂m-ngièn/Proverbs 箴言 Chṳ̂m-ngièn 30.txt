1 Â-kú-ngí ke Chṳ̂m-ngièn Hâ-poi he Ngâ-kî ke lai-é Â-kú-ngí ke chṳ̂m-ngièn; liá-ngìn tui Yî-thiet lâu Vû-kap kóng:( 70 30:1 "liá-ngìn tui Yî-thiet lâu Vû-kap kóng:" ya-he yi̍t-cho "liá-ngìn án-ngiòng kóng: ‘Song-ti â, ngài tông khioi; Song-ti â, ngài tông khioi; ngài mò pan-si̍t li̍t!’ " ya-he "liá-ngìn án-ngiòng kóng: ‘Song-ti mò lâu ngài thùng-chhai; Song-ti mò lâu ngài thùng-chhai; ngài mò má-ke pông-chhu!’ ")2 Ngài chhiong thèu-sâng, mò phe̍t-ngìn án chhûng-mìn;sṳ-kiên-ngìn ke chṳ-fi, ngài lûng-chúng mò.3 Ngài m̀-sṳt ho̍k-ko chṳ-fi,ya m̀ ngin-sṳt Chṳ-sṳn-chá.4 Má-ngìn sông-ko thiên-táng yu hâ-lòi?Má-ngìn nèn yung sú pâ-chóng chok-fûng?Má-ngìn yû phan-fap yung-sâm pâu-súi?Yû má-ngìn thin-ko thi ke kie-han?Kì he má-ngìn, miàng ôn-to má-ke?Kiâ lai-é ke miàng ôn-to má-ke?Ká-sṳ́ ngì tî, pai-thok lâu ngài kóng!5 Song-ti ke fa tak-ki chhiòn-phu he sùn-kiet ke;Kì chhiong tún-phài pó-fu só-yû thèu-kho Kì ke.6 Kì só-kóng ke fa-ngî, ngì cho-m̀-tet kâ-thiâm má-ke,kiâng-pha Kì voi chit-phi ngì, chṳ́-chhut ngì he fâ-liâu-kú.7 Song-ti â, yû lióng-hong sṳ-chhìn,khiù Ngì chhai ngài màng-sí yî-chhièn tap-yin ngài:8 Khiù Ngì sṳ́ hî-ká lâu fâ-liâu yén-lì ngài;sṳ́ ngài m̀-voi thet-khú ya m̀-voi thet-pot;su-pûn ngài só sî-yeu ke yím-sṳ̍t chhiu hó,9 kiâng-pha ngài thet hó-ngiak, voi m̀-ngin Ngì,chhiu kóng: "Song-chú he má-ngìn nè?"yu kiâng-pha ngài thet-khú, voi hi thêu-nâ,chhiu siû-yu̍k ngài Song-ti ke miàng.10 M̀-hó tui cho chú-ngìn ke phî-phìn kì ke phu̍k-ngìn,kiâng-pha phu̍k-ngìn chu-ma ngì, ngì ya oi su chit-fa̍t.11 Yû-têu ngìn chu-ma yà-è,ya m̀-hén chuk-fuk â-mê.12 Yû-têu ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ chhin chhiang-li;khì-sṳ̍t mân-sṳ̂n mò sé-chhiang chhin ô-chô.13 Yû-têu ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ chhin vàn-mî,chúng-he kì ke muk-chû hién-chhut kiêu-ngau.14 Yû-têu ngìn chiâm-ngà li-chhṳ́,oi thûn-sṳ̍t thi-song ke khun-khú ngìn lâu sṳ-kiên ke phìn-khiùng ngìn.15 Fù-khì yû lióng-ke moi-é,kín-kóng: "Pûn ngài! Pûn ngài!"M̀-voi mân-chiuk ke yû sâm-hong,lièn m̀-kóng "La" ke khiung-yû si-hong,16 chhiu-he: Yîm-kiên,m̀-voi kiung ke fu-ngìn-kâ,thiên-hôn mò-súi ke thú-thi,m̀-voi kóng "La lé!" ke fó.17 Sâu-nat â-pâ, méu-sṳ yu m̀ thâng-chhiùng â-mê ke,kiâ muk-chû voi pûn sân-lak ke vû-â tuk chhut-lòi, pûn se ên-tiâu sṳ̍t-thet.18 Ngài sióng m̀-kié khì-meu ke sṳ yû sâm-hong,lièn ngài só m̀ liáu-kié ke khiung-yû si-hong,19 chhiu-he: Ên-tiâu chhai khûng-chûng pî ke lu,sà-kô chhai thai-sa̍k-táng phà ke lu,sòn-è chhai hói-song hàng ke lu,nàm-ńg kâu-vông ke lu.20 Yìm-fu ke lu he án-ngiòng:Kì sṳ̍t-liáu, chiông choi yit-chhu̍t chhiu kóng:"Ngài mò cho fái-sṳ!"21 Sṳ́ thai-thi yèu-thung ke yû sâm-hong,lièn thi khâi m̀-yàng ke khiung-yû si-hong,22 chhiu-he: Nù-chhòi cho-vòng,ngong-tong ngìn tet-tó páu,23 tông tet-ngìn-nâu ke se-moi-é chhut-ka,sṳ́-ńg châng-to thèu-kâ-ngiòng ke thi-vi.24 Thi-song yû si-chúng se ke thung-vu̍t,chúng-he fî-sòng chhûng-mìn:25 Ngie-kûng m̀-he yû li̍t ke thung-vu̍t,chúng-he kì tî ha-thiên yi-phi sṳ̍t-vu̍t;26 sa̍k-fôn m̀-he khiòng-chong ke thung-vu̍t,chúng-he kì voi chhai ngàm-sa̍k chûng cho-teu;27 chhó-máng mò vòng,chúng-he kì-têu voi pài-chhṳn chhièn-chin;28 piak-sà, ngìn yung sú chok tet-tó kì,chúng-he kì voi he̍t chhai vòng-kiûng tú.29 Kiok-phu vî-fûng ke yû sâm-hong,lièn hàng-thung vî-fûng ke khiung-yû si-hong,30 chhiu-he: Chhu-lui chûng chui khiòng-chong,mò kiâng má-ke ke sṳ̂-é;31 chhin fûng-sṳ̀n ke kiê-kûng;sân-yòng-kú;lâu thúng-liâng thai-kiûn ke kiûn-vòng.32 Ká-sṳ́ ngì cho ngong-tong ke sṳ,chhṳ-kô chhṳ-thai, ya-he yîm-mèu fái-sṳ,chhiu oi yung sú êm-choi!33 Luk ngiù-nen, voi tet-tó nen-yù;tá phi-kûng, phi-kûng voi chhut-hiet;liàu ngìn fat-khién, voi ngiâ-hí chên-tôn.
