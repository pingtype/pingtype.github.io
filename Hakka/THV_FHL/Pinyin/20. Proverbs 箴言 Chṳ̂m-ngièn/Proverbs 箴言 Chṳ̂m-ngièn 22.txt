1 Hó miàng-sâng yàng-ko kâ-ngia̍p thai;su chûn-chhung khiòng-ko kîm-ngiùn tô.2 Hó-ngiak ngìn lâu khiùng-khú ngìn khiung-yong,lióng-chúng ngìn chhiòn-he Song-chú chho ke.3 Chîn-mìn ke ngìn khon-tó ngùi-hiám chhiu sám-khôi;ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn chhṳ̍t-thèu chhṳ̍t-lu su châi-hoi.4 Chhùn-sîm khiâm-pî, kin-vi Song-chú,chhiu voi fu-chiuk, kông-yùng, chhòng-su.5 Kiên-cha ngìn ke lu chhûng-mân net-è lâu lò-mióng;sióng pó-sú chhṳ-kâ sâng-miang ke chhiu oi yén-lì.6 Kau-yông hiun-lien se-ngìn-è hàng chṳn-lu,chhiùng-se to-ló chhiu m̀-voi phiên-chhâ.7 Khiùng-khú ngìn pûn hó-ngiak ngìn kón-hot;khiam-chai ke he chai-chú ke nù-chhòi.8 Ve put-ngi ke chúng-chṳ́, thin-chho̍k sû châi-fo;kì khî-ap ke sṳ-li̍t, chó-man voi sṳt-háu.9 Không-khói kiu-chi ngìn ke pit-thin tet chuk-fuk;yîn-vi kì chiông sṳ̍t-vu̍t pûn-pûn khiùng-khú ngìn.10 Chiông ngau-man ngìn kiuk-chéu chhiu voi sêu-chhù kiu-fun;chên-chhàu lâu siû-yu̍k ke sṳ-chhìn ya voi so̍t-thet.11 Sîm-tú sùn-kiet kóng-fa vûn-yù ke ngìn,kiûn-vòng yit-thin voi lâu kì cho phèn-yû.12 Song-chú ke muk-chû khon-ku chṳ̂n-lî;Kì thûi-fân kiên-cha ngìn ke ngièn-lun.13 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn kóng: "No-poi yû sṳ̂-é!Ngài hi kiê-hong voi pûn kì sṳ̍t-thet!"14 Yìm-lon fu-ńg ke choi-mâ chhiong ham-chiáng;Song-chú só-nâu ke ngìn voi tiet lo̍k-hi.15 Ngì-ngong chhàn-tén se-ngìn-è ke sîm;yung chuk-siû-é kón-kau chang kié tet-khôi.16 Pok-siok khiùng-khú ngìn lòi fat-chhòi ke,lâu sung-lî pâ-kiet hó-ngiak ngìn ke,to-mî pit-thin voi liáu-to chhiang-chhiang.17 Chṳ-fi ke Fa Sâm-sṳ̍p Thiàu Ngì oi ngin-chṳ̂n thâng chṳ-fi ngìn ke fa,chôn-sîm liâng-su ngài só-chhòn ke chṳ-sṳt.18 Ngì na ki chhai sîm-tú, sùi-sṳ̀ kóng-chhut yín-yung,liá chhiu-he chhin fôn-hí ke sṳ.19 Ngài kîm-pû-ngit chiông liá-têu fa thi̍t-phe̍t chṳ́-kau ngì,hó-pûn ngì chhiòn-sîm yí-kho Song-chú.20 Ngài vi ngì siá-hâ sâm-sṳ̍p thiàu,khien-va lâu chṳ-sṳt ke chṳ̂m-ngièn,21 yung án-ngiòng lòi pûn ngì tî má-ke he chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t khó-kho ke fa,hó-pûn ngì yung sin-sṳ̍t ke fa fì-fu̍k phai ngì chhut-lòi ke ngìn.22 2 (1) Khiùng-khú ngìn mò nèn-li̍t pó-fu kì chhṳ-kâ,ngì cho-m̀-tet kho-se chhiu chhióng kiâ tûng-sî;ya m̀-hó chhai fap-yen( 50 22:22 "fap-yen": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "sàng-mùn héu".) ap-pet khun-khú ngìn.23 Song-chú oi vi kì-têu lòi sṳ̂n-yên;na-yû má-ngìn hoi kì-têu ke miang,Song-chú oi sû ke-têu ngìn ke miang.24 2 (2) M̀-hó lâu hau fat-khién ke ngìn cho phèn-yû,ya m̀-hó lâu sin-thí lie̍t ke ngìn lòi-vông,25 miên-tet ngì chhe-tó kì ke fái si̍p-koan,chhiu tiet-lo̍k lò-mióng lòi hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.26 2 (3) M̀-hó sùi-phien sṳ̀n-tâm chit-ngim vi-ngìn tâm-pó;ya m̀-hó vi phe̍t-ngìn ke chai-vu cho tâm-pó-ngìn.27 To-sṳ̀ ngì na-he mò-chhièn hó vàn-ngìn,lièn ngì ke mìn-chhòng ya voi pûn-ngìn kông-chéu!28 2 (4) Chú-siên só-thin ke ló thi-kie,ngì cho-m̀-tet chiông kì sái-chéu.29 2 (5) Ngì khon-ko cho-sṳ chîn-mìn ke ngìn mò?Kì voi khî chhai kiûn-vòng mien-chhièn fu̍k-sṳ,m̀-he fu̍k-sṳ phû-phû thûng-thûng ke ngìn.
