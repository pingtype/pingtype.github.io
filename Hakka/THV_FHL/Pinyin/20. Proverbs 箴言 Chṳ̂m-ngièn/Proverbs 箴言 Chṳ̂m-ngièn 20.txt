1 Thâm-chiú hoi-ngìn ngau-man, ngiàm-chiú hoi-ngìn fù-nau;hau chiú hàng-chhâ lu ke ngìn, sṳ̍t-chhai mò chṳ-fi.2 Vòng ke fat-khién chhiong sṳ̂-é thai-sâng sê;ngiâ vòng su-hi sóng-sṳt chhṳ-kâ sâng-miang.3 Ngìn nèn phit-miên fûn-chên chhiu-he kông-yùng;ngong-ngìn chhiòn-phu chhin-hau ngiâ-ngìn yên-kâ.4 Lân-sṳ̂ ke kâng-chung ngìn mò cheu chiet-hi;sû-sṳ̀n ke sṳ̀ kì chhiu yit-vù só-tet.5 Ngìn ke sîm-sṳ̂ chhiong chhṳ̂m-chiáng tú ke súi,chhûng-mìn ngìn chang tî ngióng-pân tá chhut-lòi.6 Chhin-tô ngìn chhṳ-khoâ cho-ngìn chûng-sṳ̀n,chúng-he má-ngìn chhìm tet-tó chṳn-kîn sin-sṳ̍t ke ngìn nè?7 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn hàng-sṳ vàn-chhiòn sùn-chṳn,kì ke chṳ́-sûn he ngióng-pân yû fuk-hi!8 Kiûn-vòng chhô chhai sṳ́m-phan-thòi táng-kô,kiâ muk-chû kam-chhat yit-chhiet sià-ok.9 Má-ngìn kám kóng: "Ngài yí-kîn sé-chhiang ngài ke sîm,ngài he sùn-kiet mò-chhui ke"?10 Yung lióng-chúng chhṳn-é, lióng-yong sṳ̂n-téu lòi phien-ngìn,liá he Song-chú chhin-nâu ke.11 Se-ngìn-è ke phín-hen he sùn-kiet he chṳn-chhṳ̍t,tui kì ke hàng-vì me khó-yî khon tet-chhut lòi.12 Voi thâng ke ngí-kûng, voi khon ke muk-chû,lióng-yong tû he Song-chú só chhóng-chho ke.13 M̀-hó thâm-soi, chang m̀-voi pien khiùng-khú;sat-mâng cho-se, chhiu voi tet-tó páu-chiuk.14 Mâi-fo hiàm-fo: "M̀-hó! M̀-hó!"Mâi-chón chhiu kóng: "Chhin-hó! Chhin-hó!"15 Vòng-kîm van-liông, chû-pó mân-siông,m̀-tong yit-ki chṳ-fi liòng-ngièn.16 Má-ngìn vi sâng-fun ngìn tâm-pó,ngì chhiu oi nâ kì( 45 20:16 "kì" chṳ́-to "Má-ngìn".) ke ngoi-sâm cho tí-ap;má-ngìn vi ngoi-chhu̍k ngìn( 46 20:16 "ngoi-chhu̍k ngìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "mò chṳn-tông ke fu-ńg".) tâm-pó,ngì chhiu oi ham kì sṳ̀n-tâm yit-chhiet heu-kó.17 Phien-lòi ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t thi̍t-phe̍t hó-sṳ̍t;ko-heu chhiu kám-kok mân-choi sâ-sa̍k.18 Yû kie-va̍k ke sṳ yit-thin sṳ̀n-kûng;mò chhet-lio̍k ke chan-chên m̀-hó tá.19 Tô-kóng hàn-fa, nàn-sú pi-me̍t;tô-choi phèn-yû, m̀-hó kiet-kâu.20 Chu-ma yà-ôi ke ngìn,kì ke sâng-miang voi chhiong tên-fó chhai vû-am chûng vû-thet.21 Thet-chó ki-sṳ̀n chú-kûng-ngia̍p,chui-mî tui kì m̀-he fuk.22 Ngì m̀-hó kóng: "Ngài oi po-sù!"Ngì oi tén-heu Song-chú, Kì voi vi ngì sṳ̂n-yên.23 Mò-chún ke chhṳn-thò, Song-chú chhin-nâu;kúi-cha ke chhṳn-é, sṳ̍p-fûn m̀-hó.24 Ngìn ke kiok-phu he Song-chú só ôn-phài ke;má-ngìn nèn tî kì ngìn-sên ke tho-lu nè?25 Hiong Song-ti hí-ngien chhièn oi sâm-sṳ̂,miên-tet heu-lòi voi ham-ngi̍p lò-mióng.26 Chṳ-fi ke vòng poi-chhut fái-ngìn,chhiu yung lu̍k-chhu̍k lòi-chan kì-têu.27 Song-chú ke tên kam-chhat ngìn ke sîm( 47 20:27 "Song-chú ... sîm" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Ngìn ke liòng-sîm he Song-chú ke tên".),chhà-mìn sîm-tái yit-chhiet ke ngiam-thèu.28 Yìn-chhṳ̀ lâu sin-sṳ̍t he kiûn-vòng ke pó-chông;kì ya yung yìn-chhṳ̀ vún-ku kì ke chṳn-khièn.29 Sṳ̂n-khiòng li̍t-chong he heu-sâng-ngìn ke yùng-yeu;pha̍k thèu-nà-mô he ló-ngìn-kâ ke chûn-yùng.30 Ngiàm-hìn chhù-thet ngìn ke chhui-ok;piên-tá sé-chhiang ngìn ke nui-sîm.
