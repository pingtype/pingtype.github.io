1 Chṳ-fi ke lai-é thâng-chhiùng â-pâ ke kau-hiun;chúng-he ngau-man ngìn m̀-hén thâng-ngìn ke chit-phi.2 Choi chhut liòng-ngièn ke ngìn hióng-su mî-fuk;kiên-cha ngìn ke yu̍k-mong chhiu-he khiòng-phau.3 Kiún-sṳ́m kóng-fa, pó-chhiòn sâng-miang;hau-kóng thai-fa, chhṳ-chhí me̍t-mòng.4 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn nong-sióng chúng-he yit-vù só-tet;sat-mâng ngìn ke sîm-ngien voi tet-tó mân-chiuk.5 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhin-nâu fâ-liâu ke fa;sià-ok ngìn liù chhu-miàng yu kien-seu.6 Chṳn-ngi pó-fu hàng-vì sùn-chṳn ke;chúng-he sià-ok sṳ́ chhui-ngìn vang-tó.7 Yû-ngìn ká-pot, khì-sṳ̍t mò pan-ti̍t;yû-ngìn cha-khiùng, khì-sṳ̍t chṳ̂n hó-ngiak.8 Yû-chhièn ngìn yung-chhièn su̍k-miang;khiùng-khú ngìn miên-su khiúng-hak.9 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke kông chhin mìn-liong;sià-ok ngìn ke tên voi vû-thet.10 Kiêu-ngau chṳ́-voi ngiâ-hí chên-tôn;chiap-su khien-va chang-he chṳ-fi.11 Vàng-chhòi voi chhiong súi-liù-sâ( 25 13:11 "voi chhiong súi-liù-sâ" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "chhiu voi mò-thet".);hiet-hon chhièn ngit-ngit chen-kâ.12 Ngien-mong mò sṳ̍t-hien, voi hoi-ngìn sông-sîm;mî-mung yit sṳ̀n-chṳ̂n, chhiu-chhiong sâng-miang-su.13 Méu-sṳ kau-hiun, chhṳ-chêu châi-fo;thâng-chhiùng kie-min, voi yû hó-po.14 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke kau-sṳ he sâng-miang ke chhièn-ngièn,yû khó-nèn sṳ́-ngìn lì-khôi sí-mòng ke lò-mióng.15 Mìn-lî ngìn to-chhu su-ngìn chûn-kin;kiên-cha ngìn ke lu chhun-phu nàn-hàng.16 Chhûng-mìn ngìn tak-sà kho chṳ-sṳt hàng-sṳ;ngong-tong ngìn péu-hien kì m̀-tî thèu-thiên.17 Kiên-cha ke sṳ́-chá tai-lòi châi-fo;chûng-sîm ke sṳ́-chá tai-lòi fò-phìn.18 Khî-chhie̍t kón-kau, tai-lòi khiùng-khú lâu kien-seu;khiâm-pî chiap-su khien-va, voi su-ngìn chûn-kin.19 Ngien-mong sṳ̍t-hien, sîm-tú chhiu tông kâm-thiàm;lì-khôi sià-ok, he ngong-ngìn só-nâu ke.20 Thùng chṳ-fi ngìn khiung-ha hàng, nèn-tet chṳ-fi;lâu ngong-tong ngìn cho phèn-yû, voi su lièn-lui.21 Châi-fo kín-tûi chhui-ngìn;ngi-ngìn pit-tet san-po.22 Hó-ngìn chiông sán-ngia̍p liù-pûn chṳ́-chṳ́ sûn-sûn;chhui-ngìn chit-chhùn chhièn-chhòi fán-chón kûi ngi-ngìn.23 Khiùng-ngìn ke thièn-thi, voi ńg-kuk fûng-tên;put-ngi yit lòi-to, chhiu pok-siok chhiang-chhiang.24 M̀ kón-kau chṳ́-ńg, chhiu-he mò siak kì-têu( 26 13:24 "M̀ kón-kau ... kì-têu" chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Mò yung kun-é, chhiu-he nâu lai-é".);siak chṳ́-ńg, chhiu yin-kôi oi chu-yi( 27 13:24 "chu-yi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sùi-sṳ̀" ya-he "thì-chó" ya-he "ngiàm-ngiàm".) kón-kau.25 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn sṳ̍t-to sîm-mân yi-chiuk;sià-ok ngìn fán-chón yâu-chhòng ngo-tú.
