1 Fôn-hí su kón-kau ke, chhiu-he oi-mu chṳ-sṳt;tông-nâu ngìn chit-phi ke, chhiu-he hâm-chhê ke ngìn.2 Song-chú sṳ̂-ên pûn liòng-san ke ngìn;Kì hìn-fa̍t kúi-kie tô-tôn ke ngìn.3 Ngìn yí-kho ok-hàng mò-fap khî-vún;ngi-ngìn ke kîn-kî m̀-voi yèu-thung.4 Hièn-fi ke pû-ngiòng he kiâ ló-kûng thèu-nà-táng ke miên-liù;m̀-tî kien-seu ke pû-ngiòng chhiong chhong-fû kut-chûng ke thu̍k-liù.5 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke kie-va̍k kûng-chṳn;sià-ok ngìn ke kie-mèu kiên-cha.6 Sià-ok ngìn ke fa chhiong lò-mióng voi hoi ngìn;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn só-kóng ke fa voi chṳ́n-kiu ngìn.7 Sià-ok ngìn yit vang, vang-to tap-tap;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke kâ, khî-to vún-vún.8 Ngìn tet-tó chhṳ̂n-chan he yîn-vi yû chṳ-fi;sîm-su̍t sià-ok ke chhiu voi pûn-ngìn méu-sṳ.9 Thi-vi sûi-yèn tâi chúng-he yû phu̍k-ngìn( 21 12:9 "chúng-he yû phu̍k-ngìn" yû-têu kú-kién yi̍t-cho "chúng-he chhṳ-sṳ̍t khì-li̍t".),yàng-ko ngang-tién phài-chhòng ngo-tén tú-sṳ́.10 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn thí-sit thèu-sâng ke sî-yeu;sià-ok ngìn ke lìn-mén hàn-he chhàn-ngiûn.11 Sat-mâng kâng-chung ke, liòng-sṳ̍t chhûng-chiuk;chûi-khiù hî-yùng ke, chhiòn-mò sòng-sṳt.12 Sià-ok ngìn thâm fái-ngìn chhióng-tó ke chhièn-chhòi( 22 12:12 "fái-ngìn chhióng-tó ke chhièn-chhòi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "put-ngi ke chhièn-chhòi".);chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke kîn tai-lòi hó ke kiet-kó( 23 12:12 "tai-lòi hó ke kiet-kó" ya-he yi̍t-cho "tang-to tông-chhṳ̂m".).13 Sià-ok ngìn yîn-vi kì fam-chhui ke fa tiet-lo̍k ham-chiáng;chúng-he chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn thin-chho̍k voi ôn-yèn thot-lì fam-nan.14 Ngìn yîn-vi kóng-fa kiet ke kó-chṳ́ chhûng-mân fuk-hi;ngìn yîn-vi sú só-cho ke voi tet hó ke po-sóng.15 Ngong-tong ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ só-cho ke chhin-chho̍k;chúng-he chṳ-fi ngìn voi thâng phe̍t-ngìn ke khien-va.16 Ngong-tong ngìn yùng-yi pot-at;chîn-mìn ngìn ngiûn-su vú-yu̍k.17 Kóng sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t fa, chông-hién kûng-ngi;cho ká kien-chṳn, hién-chhut kúi-cha.18 Lám-sám kóng-fa chhiong li-kiam chhiuk-sông ngìn;chṳ-fi ngìn ke sa̍t-mà nèn yî-chhṳ ngìn.19 Sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke ngièn-ngî yún-yén su ki-ngiam;phien-ngìn ke fâ-liâu chhin-kiak chhiu mò-háu.20 Sióng cho fái-sṳ ke, sîm-chhùn kúi-cha;khien-ngìn fò-mu̍k ke, chhûng-mân hí-lo̍k.21 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn m̀-voi yû châi-hoi;sià-ok ngìn yit-chhṳ̍t yû fo-fam.22 Kóng fâ-liâu ke choi-sùn, Song-chú chhin-nâu;hàng-vì sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke, he Chú só fôn-hí.23 Chhûng-mìn ngìn mò tién chhṳ-kâ chṳ-sṳt;ngong-tong ngìn siên-chhòn kì ke hâm-chhê.24 Sat-mâng ngìn ke sú voi chóng-khièn;lân-sṳ̂ ngìn voi pien-cho khú-li̍t.25 Ngìn-sîm yù-li, chhiong khâi chhûng-tâm;yit-ki hó-fa, sṳ́ ngìn fôn-lo̍k.26 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn vi lìn-sa tai-lu;sià-ok ngìn hoi kì ngi̍p mì-thù.27 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn mò mí chú;sat-mâng ngìn voi fu-chiuk.( 24 12:27 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn mò lia̍p-vu̍t hó phoi; sat-mâng ngìn voi yû pó-kui ke chhòi-vu̍t.")28 Chṳn-ngi ke lu tai-lòi sâng-miang;liá-thiàu lu-song pin-mò sí-mòng.
