1 Song-chú sùi-yi chṳ́-fî kiûn-vòng ke sîm,chhiu chhiong Kì chón-pien hò-liù ke fông-hiong.2 Ngìn khon chhṳ-kâ só-hàng ke lûng-chúng chho̍k,chúng-he Song-chú chhà-ngiam kì ke thung-kî.3 Hàng yìn-ngi lâu kûng-phìn,pí hien-chi kien-mùng Song-chú chiap-na̍p.4 Méu-sṳ ke muk-chû, kiêu-ngau ke sîm,fái-ngìn só kâng-chung ke( 48 21:4 "só kâng-chung ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ke tên".), chhiòn he chhui.5 Sat-mâng kie-va̍k ke, yit-thin voi hó-ngiak;hàng-sṳ kip-sin ke, pit chṳ-to khiùng-khú.6 Cha-khî phien-lòi ke chhòi-pó,chhiu chhiong phêu-san ke yùn-yên,yu chhiong sí-mòng ke lò-mióng.7 Sià-ok ngìn ke khiòng-phau hàng-vì chiông chhṳ-kâ thô-lui to-sí,yîn-vi kì-têu m̀-hén cheu kûng-phìn hàng-sṳ.8 Fam-chhui ke ngìn, lu he vân-khiuk;sùn-kiet ke ngìn, hàng-vì chṳn-chhṳ̍t.9 Kâm-ngien tân-thu̍k chhai vuk-táng ke yit-kok,mò-oi lâu phat-fu khiung-ha he̍t thai-vuk.10 Sià-ok ngìn kín-sióng cho fái-sṳ;tui lìn-sa mò lìn-mén ke sîm.11 Ngau-man ngìn su-tó chit-fa̍t,ngong-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhiu tet chṳ-fi;chṳ-fi ngìn tet-tó kau-yuk,kì chhiu voi chen-kâ chṳ-sṳt.12 Kûng-ngi-chá kam-chhat fái-ngìn ke kâ;kì oi sṳ́ sià-ok ke ngìn me̍t-mòng.13 Ngí-kûng set-tén m̀-thâng khiùng-khú ngìn ôi-khiù ke,kì chiông-lòi khiù pông-chhu ya mò-ngìn oi tap kì.14 Am-chûng sung lî-vu̍t nèn phìn-sit fun-nu;thiap heu-sú ya khó-yî ap-chṳ lie̍t-nu.15 Sṳ̍t-hàng kûng-ngi, ngi-ngìn fôn-hí,chok-ok ke ngìn, kiâng-to voi-sí.16 Ngìn na lì-khôi mìn-lî ke lu,voi hi thùng yîm-fùn khiung-ha chhu.17 Fôn-hí yen-lo̍k, pit-chṳ phìn-khiùng;thâm-sṳ̍t chiú-ngiuk, put-nèn fu-chiuk.18 Sià-ok ngìn sióng-oi ham-hoi chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn,châi-fo fán-chón lìm-to chhṳ-kâ sṳ̂n-hong.( 49 21:18 Pún-chiet chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he: "Sià-ok ngìn cho chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke su̍k-ka; kiên-cha ngìn ya thoi-von chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.")19 Kâm-ngien he̍t chhai phiên-phit mò-ngìn tai ke khóng-yâ,mò-oi lâu hau yên-kâ ke phat-fu khiung-ha he̍t.20 Chṳ-fi ngìn vuk-hâ chit-chhùn pó-vu̍t lâu yù;ngong-tong ngìn yit-ha tet-tó chhiu yung chhiang-chhiang.21 Chhin-sîm chhin-yi oi hàng kûng-ngi lâu yìn-chhṳ̀,chhiu voi tet-tó chhòng-su kûng-ngi lâu chûn-yùng.22 Kho chṳ-fi chang nèn cham-liâng yúng-sṳ chóng ke sàng,chiông kì-têu só yí-kho ke sàng-pó chhióng ko-lòi.23 Ngìn nèn kiún-sṳ́m kì só-kóng ke fa,voi pó-sú chhṳ-kâ miên-su châi-nan.24 Kiêu-ngau yu sâu-nat ngìn ke, ôn-to "Ngau-man ngìn";kì hàng-sṳ cho-ngìn, sṳ̀-sòng ko-thèu kiêu-ngau.25 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn ke nong-sióng voi hoi-sí kì,yîn-vi kì ke sú m̀ ngien-yi cho-kûng;26 kì kûi-ngit thâm-sîm to mò han-thu;chúng-he ngi-ngìn sṳ̂-sá, mò ngat-chhat.27 Song-chú chhin-hen fái-ngìn ke hien-chi;hò-khóng kì chhùn fái-yi lòi-hien nè!28 Cho ká kien-chṳn ke ngìn, kì yit-thin voi thai sṳt-phai;chhîn-ngí thâng sṳ̍t-fa ke, kì ke kien-chṳn voi sṳ̀n-kûng.29 Sià-ok ngìn cha-yi yû tám-liong;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn hàng-sṳ yû kháu-li.30 Ngìn ke chṳ-fi, chhûng-mìn, mèu-lio̍k,lûng-chúng mò fap-thu lâu Song-chú tui-khong.31 Ngìn vi-tó siông-chhṳ̀ ke-ngit chêu-pîn mâi-mâ,chúng-he sû-yàng chhiòn yù Song-chú lòi kiet-thin.
