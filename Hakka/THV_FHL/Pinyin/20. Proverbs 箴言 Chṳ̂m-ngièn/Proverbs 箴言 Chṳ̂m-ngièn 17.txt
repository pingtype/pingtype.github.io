1 Yû yit-héu fan hó-sṳ̍t, chhiòn-kâ phìn-ôn,yàng-ko mân-chok phông-phai, siông-châng pi-poi.2 Chîn-mìn ke phu̍k-ngìn voi kón chú-ngìn mò chhiong-yong ke chṳ́,hàn yû-hó chhai kì-têu ke hiûng-thi chûng pûn-tó kâ-ngia̍p.3 Kîm-ngiùn su lù-fó thon-lien;ngìn-sîm pûn Song-chú kháu-ngiam.4 Cho-fái ke thâng-chhiùng kiên-cha ke fa;fâ-liâu-kú hau-thâng hoi-ngìn ke fa.5 Sâu-nat khiùng-khú ngìn ke, he vú-yu̍k chho kì ke Chú;chhṳ́-seu phe̍t-ngìn su-khú ke, nàn-miên oi su chit-fa̍t.6 Chṳ́-sûn he ló-ngìn-kâ ke miên-liù;yà-ôi he se-ngìn-è ke kông-yùng.7 Ngong-tong ngìn kóng vùn-ngá ke fa mò ha̍p-nì;kô-kui ngìn kóng fâ-liâu hàn-kha mò ha̍p-sṳt.8 Put-ngi ke fùng-pâu, sung ke ngìn khon-cho van-nèn;kì láu-tó yit-chhiet kie-mèu ke lûng-chúng voi thûng.9 Sa-miên ko-chho, chhiuk-chin yû-oi;ki-ngiam khiu-hen, pho-fai yû-chhìn.10 Tui mìn-lî ke ngìn kóng yit-ki chit-phi ke fa,pí tá ngong-tong ngìn yit-pak ha hàn-kha yû-háu.11 Sià-ok ke ngìn chṳ́-yû chhùn-sîm phoi-ngia̍k;ngiàm-li ke sṳ́-chá voi lòi tui-fu kì.12 Nèn-khó tù-tó sṳt-thet yùng-chṳ́ ke yùng-mà,m̀-oi tù-tó fù-cho lon-lòi ke ngong-ngìn.13 Yî-ok po-san ke ngìn,kâ-thìn put-lì châi-fo.14 Siông-châng yit khôi-sṳ́ chhiu-chhiong thai-súi chhôn-pok;só-yî siông-chhàu màng pau-fat chhièn oi thìn-chṳ́.15 Phan ok-ngìn vì-ngi, thin ngi-ngìn vì-ok,liá lióng-hong tû he Song-chú só-nâu ke.16 Ngong-tong ngìn nâ-chhièn mâi chṳ-fi kám yû-yung?Kì kîn-pún mò-sîm lòi ho̍k-si̍p!17 Phèn-yû sṳ̀-sòng pí-chhṳ́ siông-siak;hiûng-thi chhut-se fûn-tâm khú-nan.18 Chṳ́-yû mò sòng-sṳt ke ngìn,chang voi lâu lìn-sa tâm-pó.19 Hau fam-chhui ke ngìn fôn-hí fûn-chên;tién fûng-sṳ̀n ke ngìn( 38 17:19 "tién fûng-sṳ̀n ke ngìn" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "kô-li̍p kâ-mùn ke ngìn".) ngiâ-lòi châi-fo.20 Sîm-su̍t mò-chang ke, chhìm m̀-tó hen-fuk;kóng-fa kiên-cha ke, sòng tù-tó châi-nan.21 Kiung ngong-tong ke lai-é, hoi â-mê yù-sông;phoi-ngia̍k ke lai-é, hoi â-pâ mò fôn-hí.22 Fôn-hí ke sîm he yit-thiap liòng-yo̍k;yù-sèu ke lìn hoi kut-thèu châu-thet.23 Fái-ngìn am-chûng pûn-ngìn sû-mâi,hoi chṳn-ngi mò fap-thu sṳ̂n-chông.24 Chhûng-mìn ngìn mien-hiong chṳ-fi;ngong-tong ngìn ngo-thèu nong-sióng.25 Ngong-tong ke lai-é hoi â-pâ yù-sèu,me hoi â-mê thung-khú.26 Hìn-fa̍t mò-chhui ke ngìn he m̀-chho̍k ke;piên-tá chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke liâng-chhiu me m̀-chho̍k.27 Mìn-lî ngìn kóng-fa se-ngi;chhûng-mìn ngìn thèu-nó lâng-chhin.28 Hâm-chhê ke ngìn na mò kóng-fa, ya son yû chṳ-fi;kì na-he tiâm-tiâm mo̍k khôi-choi, ya son yû chhûng-mìn.
