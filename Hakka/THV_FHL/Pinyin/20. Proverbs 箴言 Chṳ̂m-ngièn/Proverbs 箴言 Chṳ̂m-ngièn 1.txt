1 Chṳ̂m-ngièn ke Ka-chhṳ̍t Thai-ví ke lai-é, Yî-set-lie̍t-vòng Só-lò-mùn ke chṳ̂m-ngièn:2 Liá-têu chṳ̂m-ngièn voi pûn ngìn ngin-sṳt chṳ-fi,tet-tó kau-hiun, lî-kié kiet-ngièn ke yi-sṳ.3 Kì-têu voi kâu ngìn ngióng-pân ko mìn-chṳ ke sên-fa̍t,ngióng-pân cho kûng-ngi, kûng-phìn lâu chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn.4 Kì-têu voi pûn ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn( 1 1:4 "ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn": Chhai pún-sû he chṳ́ tân-sùn vù-tî, yùng-yi hàng-chhâ lu ke ngìn.) pien-cho chîn-mìn,sṳ́ heu-sâng-ngìn yû chṳ-sṳt, hiáu-tet fûn-phe̍t sṳ-fî.5 Yû chṳ-fi ke thâng-tó, chhiu kien-kâ yû ho̍k-mun,mìn-lî ke ngìn thâng-tó, chhiu tet-tó yîn-thô,6 lòi liáu-kié chṳ̂m-ngièn lâu pí-yi,lòi mìn-pha̍k chṳ-fi ngìn ke fa lâu liang-é.7 Kin-vi Song-chú he chṳ-sṳt ke khôi-thèu( 2 1:7 "Kin-vi ... khôi-thèu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Chṳ-sṳt ke chhung-tiám chhiu-he kin-vi Song-chú".).Ngong-tong ngìn( 3 1:7 "Ngong-tong ngìn": Chhai pún-sû m̀-tân-chhiang chṳ́ "ngong-é", ya sòng-sòng chṳ́ tho-tet song yû khiam-khiet lâu phiên-chhâ ke ngìn; yû-sṳ̀ ya-he yi̍t-cho "hâm-chhê ke ngìn".) khon-khiâng chṳ-fi lâu kau-hiun.8 Khien-va Heu-sâng-ngìn Lai-é â( 4 1:8 "Lai-é â": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Ngài ke lai-é â", ya he tông-sṳ̀ sîn-sâng tui ho̍k-sâng chhîn-me̍t ke chhṳ̂n-fû; ya yû hien-thoi yi̍t-pún fân-cho "heu-sâng-ngìn â". Pún-sû sòng-sòng chhut-hien.), oi thâng ngiâ-pâ ke kau-hiun,m̀-hó lì-khôi ngiâ-mê ke kau-sṳ;9 yîn-vi kì-têu chhiong-he ngì só-tai chhin-chiâng ke mo-é,chhiong-he ngì kiáng-hong pó-pi ke pha̍t-lien.10 Lai-é â, na-he yû fái-ngìn lòi yín-yu ngì,chhiên-van m̀-hó thèn kì-têu.11 Na-he kì-têu kóng:"Khiung-ha lòi-hi, mài-fu̍k chhṳ̀ ko-lu-ngìn,mò-lun kì he má-ngìn, m̀-sṳ́ lî-yù, cheu tá!12 Ngài-têu oi chhiong yîm-kiên chiông kì-têu sâng-sâng thûn lo̍k-hi,vàn-chhiòn sṳ̍t-thet, chhiu-chhiong ngìn tiet-lo̍k chhṳ̂m-hâng tú!13 Ngài-têu voi tet-tó kok-chúng ke chhòi-pó,vuk-tú voi chông-mân chhióng-lòi ke tûng-sî!14 Lòi teu-kú ô,thai-kâ khiung-ha yung yit-chak chhièn-thoi!"15 Lai-é â, m̀-hó lâu kì-têu khiung-ha hàng,oi kim-chṳ́ ngì ke kiok hàng kì-têu ke lu;16 kì-têu ke kiok chéu-tén hi hàng sià-lu,kì-têu kiak-kiak sióng-oi hoi-ngìn liù-hiet.17 Tiâu-é khon-tén ke sṳ̀, lòi-sat lò-mióng,liá he mò-chhái kûng ke;18 chúng-he liá-têu fái-ngìn só-sat ke lò-mióng,he liù chhṳ-kâ ke hiet, hoi chhṳ-kâ ke miang.19 Yit-chhiet thâm-chhòi ke ngìn só tù-tó ke( 5 1:19 "tù-tó ke" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "hàng ke lu".) chhiu-he án-ngiòng;thâm-chhòi ke sîm chhióng-chéu ke he kì-têu ke miang.20 Chṳ-fi ke Fû-sâng Chṳ-fi( 6 1:20 Chhai pún-sû chok-chá chiông "Chṳ-fi" hìn-yùng chhiong yit-ke "Fu-ńg".) chhai kiê-hong fû-ham,chhai thai-chung chhòng-só kín-hêm,21 chhai nau-ngie̍t ke kiê-thèu thai-sâng sê,chhai sàng-mùn phòng-piên kín chhut-sâng kóng:22 "Ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn â,ngì-têu fôn-hí ngong-chhṳ̍t oi to kí-sṳ̀?Ngau-man ngìn( 7 1:22 "Ngau-man ngìn": Chṳ́ yu kiêu-ngau yu hau sâu-nat ngìn ke ngìn, chhâm-kháu 21:24; pún-sû sòng-sòng chhut-hien.) fôn-hí ngau-man,ngong-tong ngìn nâu chṳ-sṳt oi to kí-kiú?23 Ngì-têu oi thâng ngài ke khien-va;ngài oi chiông ngài ke sîm-sṳ̂ tui ngì-têu hién-mìn,chiông ngài ke fa kâu ngì-têu mìn-pha̍k.24 Ngài ham ngì-têu, ngì-têu m̀-thâng;ngài ya̍k ngì-têu, ngì-têu m̀-tap;25 fán-chón khon-khiâng ngài yit-chhiet ke khien-va,m̀ ngien-yi chiap-su ngài ke chit-phi.26 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng,ngì-têu tù-tó châi-nan ke sṳ̀,ngài chhiu oi seu ngì-têu;ngì-têu kiâng-to pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat,ngài ya oi sâu-nat ngì-têu.27 Chho̍k-kiâng chhiong thai-fûng lìm-to ngì-têu,châi-nan chhiong khòng-fûng lòi-to,kip-nan thung-khú kong-hâ ke sṳ̀,ngài hàn-he oi sâu-nat ngì-têu.28 "Ke-sṳ̀, kì-têu voi khiù-ham ngài,chúng-he ngài mò-oi en;kì-têu voi chhìm ngài,chúng-he chhìm m̀-tó.29 Kì-têu nâu chṳ-sṳt,ya m̀-sṳt kiet-sîm kin-vi Song-chú;30 kì-têu chhiùng-lòi m̀-thâng ngài ke khien-va,fán-chón khon-khiâng ngài yit-chhiet ke chit-phi.31 Só-yî, kì-têu oi sṳ̍t chhṳ-kâ só-kiet ke kó-chṳ́,pûn chhṳ-kâ só-sat ke kie-mèu chhang-to páu-páu.32 Ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn yîn-vi hàng vâi-lu sóng-sṳt sâng-miang;ngong-tong ngìn yîn-vi sùi-phien ko-ngit hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.33 Chúng-he thâng-chhiùng ngài ke, voi ôn-chhiòn ko-ngit;kì-têu voi hióng-su phìn-ôn, m̀-kiâng châi-fo."
