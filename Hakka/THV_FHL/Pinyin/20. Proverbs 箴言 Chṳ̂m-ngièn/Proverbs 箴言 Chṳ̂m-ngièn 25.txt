1 Só-lò-mùn ke Chṳ̂m-ngièn Thi-ngi Si̍p Yî-ha só-siá ke ya he Só-lò-mùn ke chṳ̂m-ngièn,he Yù-thai-vòng Hî-sî-kâ kiûng-thìn ke ngìn só-chhâu ke.2 Chiông sṳ-chhìn khong-hí-lòi he Song-ti ke yùng-yeu;chiông sṳ-chhìn chhà chhîn-chhú he kiûn-vòng ke kông-yùng.3 Kiûn-vòng ke sîm chhin-nàn liáu-kié;chhiong thiên án-kô, chhiong thi án-phûn.4 Siên chhù-hi ngiùn-è ke chha̍p-chṳt,ngiùn-siong chang-nèn tá-cho tûng-sî;5 siên chhù-hi kiûn-vòng mien-chhièn ke kiên-sṳ̀n,chṳn-fú chang nèn khî chhai chṳn-ngi táng-kô.6 Ngì m̀-hó chhai vòng mien-chhièn tién sâ-phi,m̀-hó chhai thai-kôn ke vi-é khî-tén.7 Tén ngìn lòi tui ngì kóng: "Chhiáng song-chhô",yàng-ko ngìn lòi-hêm ngì ngiong-vi pûn thai ngìn-vu̍t.8 M̀-hó sùi-phien chhut-thìn cho-chṳn;na yû phe̍t-ngìn chṳ́-chhut ngì ke chṳn-ngièn chho-ngu, sṳ́ ngì kien-seu,ngì oi ngióng-pân chang hó nè?9 Ngì lâu lìn-sa fat-sên chên-chṳp,chui-hó sṳ̂-ha fò-kié;m̀-hó siet-leu phe̍t-ngìn ke pi-me̍t,10 miên-tet thâng-tó ke ngìn chhiu vú-yu̍k ngì,ngì ke chhu-miàng chhiu mò fap-thu chhù-thet.11 Yit-ki fa kóng-to tông hó-se,chhiu-chhiong kîm phìn-kó piong chhai ngiùn-phàn tú.12 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke khien-va tui thâng-chhiùng ke ngìn lòi-kóng,pí kîm ngí-vàn lâu sùn-kîm ke chông-sṳt-phín kha-yû ka-chhṳ̍t.13 Chûng-sṳ̀n ke sṳ́-chá pûn chhâi-phai kì ke ngìn sîm-sṳ̀n sóng-khoai,chhiu-chhiong ngie̍t-thiên kot-vò sṳ̀-chiet ke liòng-súi yit-yong.14 Khoâ-khiéu oi sung-lî, mò sṳ̍t-hàng ke,chhiu-chhiong yû-fûng yû-yùn, mò lo̍k-yí.15 Nai-sîm ke khien-va voi sot-fu̍k chóng-khièn ke ngìn;vûn-yù nèn yàng-ko kông-khiòng( 57 25:15 "vûn-yù nèn yàng-ko kông-khiòng" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "vûn-yù ke sa̍t-mà nèn tá-sui kut-thèu".).16 Chhìm-tó phûng-thòng sṳ̍t-la chhiu hó;sṳ̍t thet-tô ngì chhiu voi éu-thet.17 M̀-hó sòng-sòng ko-kâ chhìm lìn-sa,lò-káu kì thet-tô kì voi nâu ngì.18 Cho ká kien-chṳn ham-hoi lìn-sa,chhiu-chhiong kun-é, tô-kiam lâu li-chien voi hoi-sí ngìn.19 Fam-nan ke sṳ̀, thèu-kho mò chûng-sṳ̀n ke ngìn,chhiu-chhiong chu-ngà ngâu tûng-sî, pâi-kiok hàng-lu.20 Tui sông-sîm ke ngìn chhong-kô,chhiu-chhiong lâng-thiên thot kiâ sâm,yu-chhiong chhai sông-héu ve-yàm( 58 25:20 "chhai sông-héu ve-yàm": Yû kú yi̍t-pún fân-cho "chhai sông-héu tó sôn-chhṳ", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "chhai fat-fún song tó sôn-chhṳ".).21 Ngì ke thi̍t-ngìn na-he tú-kî, oi nâ-fan pûn kì sṳ̍t,na-he choi-châu, oi nâ-súi pûn kì lîm;22 ngì án-ngiòng hàng voi sṳ́ kì kien-seu to mò-kat mò-sat( 59 25:22 "sṳ́ kì ... mò-sat" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "chiông fó-than tôi chhai kì ke thèu-nà táng-kô".);Song-chú ya voi chióng-sóng ngì.23 Sô-súng sṳ-fî ngiâ-hí nu-hi,chhiu-chhiong pet-fûng tai-lòi thai-yí.24 Kâm-ngien tân-thu̍k chhai vuk-táng ke yit-kok,mò-oi lâu phat-fu khiung-ha he̍t thai-vuk.25 Thâng-tó yén-fông lòi ke hó sêu-sit,chhiu-chhiong choi-châu ke sṳ̀ sṳ̍t liòng-súi.26 Ngi-ngìn chhai sià-ok ngìn mien-chhièn tâi-thèu ngiong-phu,chhiong luk-vùn ke chhièn-súi, met ô-chô ke chiáng-súi.27 Sṳ̍t thet-tô phûng-thòng m̀-hó;tién fûng-sṳ̀n ya he yû-hoi.28 M̀ hiáu-tet chiet-chṳ ke ngìn,chhiong chhak-thet vì-chhiòng ke sàng.
