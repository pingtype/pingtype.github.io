1 Hàng-vì mò hó-hiàm ke khiùng-khú ngìn,yàng-ko kóng-fa kúi-cha ke ngong-é.2 Ngie̍t-sîm mò chṳ-sṳt chhin m̀-hó;hàng-thung thet-kiak yùng-yi hàng-chho.3 Ngìn ke ngì-ngong phai-fai kì ke chhièn-thù;kì ke sîm-kôn fán-chón mài-yen Song-chú.4 Chhièn-chhòi tô phèn-yû chen-kâ;khiùng-khú ngìn phèn-yû yén-lì.5 Cho ká kien-chṳn ke, voi su chit-fa̍t;hau-kóng fâ-liâu ke, nàn-thò chhui-chit.6 Thi-vi kô-kui ke, tô-ngìn khiù kì ke ên-chhìn;thai-fông sṳ̂-sá ke, thai-kâ lâu kì cho phèn-yû.7 Ngìn khiùng hiûng-thi me m̀-tap,phèn-yû chéu-to hàn-kha yén;kiuk-tén hi khôi-sâng chhin-hêm,kì-têu yí-kîn chéu chhiang-chhiang( 40 19:7 "kiuk-tén ... chhiang-chhiang" ya-he yi̍t-cho "kì chhìm-khiù yû-ngì, chúng-he nai-vi tû chhìm m̀-tó".)!8 Tet-tó chṳ-fi ke, oi-siak sâng-miang;chhṳ̀-sú chhûng-mìn ke, hen-fuk vù-khiùng.9 Cho ká kien-chṳn ke, voi su chit-fa̍t;hau-kóng fâ-liâu ke, chhṳ-chhí me̍t-mòng.10 Ngong-ngìn hióng ôn-lo̍k he mò ha̍p-sṳt;nù-chhòi kón thèu-kâ kha mò sṳt-tông.11 Ngìn yû kien-sṳt, mò sùi-phien fat-khién;ngièu-sa ko-sṳt, chhṳ-kâ yû yùng-kông.12 Kiûn-vòng fat-khién, chhiong ho-ho-kún ke sṳ̂-é;kiûn-vòng sṳ̂-ên, chhiong chhiâng-chhó song ke kâm-lu.13 Ngong-tong ke lai-é sàng-cho â-pâ ke châi-fo;chhàu-nau ke pû-ngiòng chhiong vuk-táng leu-yí kín-tit.14 Fòng-vuk lâu chhòi-sán he chú-siên liù-hâ ke;hièn-fi ke pû-ngiòng he Song-chú só-su ke.15 Lân-sṳ̂ ke ngìn hau soi-muk;hàn chhok-chhok ke ngo tú-sṳ́.16 Chûn-sú Song-ti kie-min ke, voi pó-sú sâng-miang;khon-khiâng Song-chú tho-lu ke, yit-thin voi me̍t-mòng.17 Kiu-chi khiùng-ngìn, chhiu-he chia-chhièn pûn Song-chú;kì ke hó hàng-vì, Song-chú voi po-tap kì.18 Hàn-yû phan-mong ke sṳ̀, oi kón-kau ngì ke chṳ́-ńg;chúng-he m̀-hó thet-ngiàm, kiâng-pha hoi kì-têu yit-sên( 41 19:18 "chúng-he ... yit-sên" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ngì ke sîm m̀-hó yù-chhai kì-têu sí-thet".).19 Phì-hi phau-lie̍t ke ngìn, pit-thin su hìn-fa̍t( 42 19:19 "pit-thin su hìn-fa̍t" ya-he yi̍t-cho "pûn kì chhṳ-sṳ̍t ok-kó".);ngì na oi kiu kì, pit-sî yit-kiu chai-kiu.20 Oi thâng-ngìn khien-va, chiap-su chṳ́-thô;ngì chui-heu voi sàng-cho chṳ-fi ngìn.21 Mèu-sṳ chhai ngìn,sṳ̀n-sṳ chhai Thiên( 43 19:21 "Thiên" ngièn-vùn he "Song-chú".).22 Cho-ngìn chûng-sṳ̀n he ngìn só hot-mu ke( 44 19:22 "Cho ngìn ... hot-mu ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Thâm-sîm he ngìn ke siû-chhṳ́".);khiùng-khú ngìn yàng-ko fâ-liâu-kú.23 Kin-vi Song-chú ke ngìn, hióng-su chhòng-su;kì voi yún-kiú ôn-kî, yén-lì châi-nan.24 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn chhûn-sú nâ phàn-tú ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t,lân-to lièn kiû-sú sung lo̍k-choi me m̀-hén.25 Chit-tá ngau-man ngìn, ngong-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhiu ho̍k-tó chîn-mìn;khien-va chhûng-mìn ngìn, kì chhiu tet-tó chṳ-sṳt.26 Lai-é na khú-thu̍k yà-è, kiuk-chéu ôi-é,he tai-lòi kien-seu, siû-yu̍k ke put-hau chṳ́.27 Lai-é â, ngì na m̀ chiap-su kón-kau,chhiu voi sṳt-lo̍k chṳ-sṳt ke ngièn-ngî.28 Kiên-cha ke chṳn-ngìn khon kûng-ngi cho seu-fa;sià-ok ke ngìn chiông put-ngi sṳ̍t-to mân-choi.29 Sṳ́m-phan he vi ngau-man ngìn lòi chún-phi ke;piên-hìn he vi ngong-ngìn ke poi-nòng sat ke.
