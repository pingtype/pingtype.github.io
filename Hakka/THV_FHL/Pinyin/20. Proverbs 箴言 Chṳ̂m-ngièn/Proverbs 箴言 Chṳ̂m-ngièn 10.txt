1 Só-lò-mùn ke Chṳ̂m-ngièn Thi-yit Si̍p Hâ-poi he Só-lò-mùn ke chṳ̂m-ngièn:Chṳ-fi ke lai-é pûn â-pâ khoai-lo̍k;ngong-tong ke lai-é hoi â-mê yù-sèu.2 Put-ngi ke chhièn-chhòi mò má-ke hó-chhu;kûng-ngi chang-nèn kiu ngìn thot-lì sí-mòng.3 Song-chú m̀-voi sṳ́ chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn tú-sṳ́ yâu;Kì sṳ́ sià-ok ngìn só sióng-mong ke lo̍k-khûng.4 Lân-sṳ̂ ke sú hoi-ngìn khiùng-khú;sat-mâng ke sú tai-lòi fu-chiuk.5 Cheu sṳ̀-chiet lòi sû-kot ke, he chhûng-mìn ke lai-é;sû-sṳ̀n ke sṳ̀ soi-muk ke, he kien-seu ke lai-é.6 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke thèu-táng yû chuk-fuk;sià-ok ngìn ke choi khong-tén chhàn-phau( 15 10:6 "khong-tén chhàn-phau" ya-he yi̍t-cho "ngiâ-tó châi-fo".).7 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn voi pûn-ngìn ki-ngiam;sià-ok ngìn voi liù-hâ chhu-miàng.8 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke sîm fu̍k-chhiùng min-lin;lám-sám kóng-fa ke, chhṳ-ngiâ sṳt-phai.9 Hàng chṳn-lu ke, sên-fa̍t ôn-ôn vún-vún;hàng vâi-lu ke, yit-thin hién-lu ngièn-hìn.10 Yung muk-chû tá am-ho ke, ngiâ-hí mà-fàn;lám-sám kóng-fa ke, chhṳ-ngiâ sṳt-phai( 16 10:10 "lám-sám ... sṳt-phai" yû-têu kú yi̍t-pún fân-cho "thán-pha̍k khien-va ke, chhiuk-chin fò-phìn".).11 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke choi he sâng-miang-chhièn;sià-ok ngìn ke choi khong-tén chhàn-phau.12 Sù-hen ngiâ-hí chên-tôn;yìn-oi châ-koi ko-chho.13 Mìn-lî ngìn ke choi kóng-chhut chṳ-fi ke fa;mò sòng-sṳt ke ngìn poi-nòng oi su piên-tá.14 Chṳ-fi ngìn ngin-chṳ̂n khip-sû chṳ-sṳt;ngong-tong ngìn khôi-choi chhiu ngiâ châi-fo.15 Chhòi-fu he yû-chhièn ngìn ke sàng-pó;phìn-khiùng he khiùng-khú ngìn ke lan-thân.16 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke kûng-ka he sâng-miang;sià-ok ngìn ke sû-ngi̍p he sí-mòng( 17 10:16 "sí-mòng" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "chhui".).17 Chûn-sú kau-hiun ke, hàng sâng-miang ke lu;khî-chhie̍t chit-phi ke, chhiu chéu-ngi̍p mì-thù.18 Sîm-tú khong-tén yen-hen ke he lau-é;sô sṳ-fî kóng yèu-ngièn ke he ngong-é.19 Tô-ngièn tô-ngî nàn-miên fam-chhui;yok-suk choi-sùn chhiu-he chṳ-fi.20 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke fa chhiong thi̍t-sién ke ngiùn-è;sià-ok ngìn ke sîm-sṳ̂ mò pan-si̍t ka-chhṳ̍t.21 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke fa voi châi-phì chhin-tô ngìn;ngong-tong ngìn yîn mò sòng-sṳt hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.22 Song-chú su-fuk, sṳ́ ngìn hó-ngiak;chhṳ-kâ lò-luk, chhòi-fu yû han( 18 10:22 "chhṳ-kâ ... yû han" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kì pin-mò kâ-song yù-li".).23 Ngong-tong ngìn cho fái-sṳ chhiong chún-káu ke;chhûng-mìn ke ngìn khon chṳ-fi cho khoai-lo̍k.24 Sià-ok ngìn só-kiâng ke phiên-phiên lìm-to kì;Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke ngien-mong thin-chho̍k voi tha̍t-sṳ̀n.25 Fûng-chhâi yit ko, sià-ok ngìn chhiu mò-thet;chúng-he, chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn voi yún-yén khî-chhai.26 Chhiáng lân-sṳ̂ ngìn hi cho-se, voi pûn kì at-sí,chhiong ngà-chhṳ́ lìm-tó sôn-chhṳ, muk-chû hiûn-tó yên.27 Kin-vi Song-chú, yèn-ngièn yit-su;sià-ok ke ngìn, miang-tón fuk-pho̍k.28 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke hî-mong tai-lòi hí-lo̍k;sià-ok ngìn ke khì-thai pien-cho khûng-khûng.29 Song-chú ke tho he sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ngìn ke pó-chông,chúng-he sṳ́ chok-ok ke ngìn chṳ-to me̍t-mòng.30 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn yún-yén m̀-voi liù-lì sṳt-só;sià-ok ngìn yit-thin mò-fap ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p.31 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke fa-ngî tai-lòi chṳ-fi;sià-ok ngìn ke sa̍t-mà voi su kot-thôn.32 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn voi kóng sṳt-tông ke fa;sià-ok ngìn kóng-fa chhûng-mân khî-phien.
