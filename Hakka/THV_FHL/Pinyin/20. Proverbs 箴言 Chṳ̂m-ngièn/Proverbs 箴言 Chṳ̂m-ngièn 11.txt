1 Song-chú chhin-nâu mò-chún ke chhṳn-é;Kì tông fôn-hí kûng-tong ke chhṳn-thò.2 Kiêu-ngau lòi, kien-seu thèn-tén lòi;yû khiâm-pî, chhiu voi yû chṳ-fi.3 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn yû sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t yîn-thô kì-têu;kiên-cha ke ngìn yîn hî-ká chṳ-to me̍t-mòng.4 Chhai sṳ́m-phan ke ngit chhòi-sán mò má-ke yung;vì-yû kûng-ngi nèn kiu ngìn thot-lì sí-mòng.5 Mò hó-hiàm ke ngìn yû chṳn-ngi vi kì phû phiàng-phiàng ke lu;sià-ok ngìn pûn kì chhṳ-kâ só-cho ke fái-sṳ lòi kang-tó.6 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke ngi-hi voi chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu;kiên-cha ngìn ke thâm-sîm voi hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.7 Sià-ok ngìn yit sí, kì ke hî-mong chhiu sêu-me̍t;kì só yí-kho ke sṳ-li̍t ya pien-cho khûng-khûng.8 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn nèn tui fam-nan chûng tet-kiu;sià-ok ngìn fán-chón thoi kì chéu ngi̍p-hi.9 Mò kin-khièn ke ngìn yung kì ke fa hoi-sí lìn-sa;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn yîn-vi yû chṳ-sṳt chhiu voi tet-kiu.10 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn hióng-fuk, chhiòn-sàng khoai-lo̍k;sià-ok ngìn me̍t-mòng, thai-kâ fôn-hí.11 Yîn-vi chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn tai-lòi chuk-fuk,sàng-sṳ chhiu hîn-vong;yîn-vi sià-ok ngìn só-kóng ke fa,sàng-sṳ chhiu fí-me̍t.12 Sâu-nat lìn-sa ke chhin-mò sòng-sṳt;mìn-lî ke ngìn m̀-voi kâ kóng-fa.13 Hau chhòn hàn-fa ke ngìn, m̀-voi sú pi-me̍t;sîm-tú sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke ngìn, chhiu cho-tet sin-lai.14 Chhet-lio̍k séu, koet-kâ thin-chho̍k sôi-phai;chhâm-mèu tô, ngìn-mìn chhiu yû ôn-chhiòn.15 Vi sâng-fun-ngìn tâm-pó, voi su khûi-sún;khî-chhie̍t cho tâm-pó ke, phìn-ôn mò-sṳ.16 Hièn-fi ke fu-ńg su-ngìn kin-chhung;khiòng-phau ke nàm-ngìn tet-tó chhièn-chhòi.17 Yìn-chhṳ̀ ke ngìn chhṳ-kâ tet hó-chhu;chhàn-ngiûn ke ngìn fán-chón hoi chhṳ-kâ.18 Sià-ok ngìn só-chhon ke, he hî-ká ke kûng-ka;ve kûng-ngi chúng-chṳ́ ke, tet chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t ke po-sóng.19 Chhin-li̍t hàng-san, pit-tet sâng-miang;chûi-khiù sià-ok, pit-chṳ sí-mòng.20 Sîm-kôn sià-ok ke, Song-chú chhin nâu;hàng-vì mò hó-hiàm ke, Chú chhin fôn-hí.21 Sià-ok ngìn yit-thin voi su-tó hìn-fa̍t;chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke chṳ́-sûn pit-mùng chṳ́n-kiu( 19 11:21 "chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ... chṳ́n-kiu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn yit-thin voi tet-tó théu-piong".).22 Tông-chiâng chúng-he mò kien-sṳt ke fu-ńg,chhiu-chhiong chû phi-khûng khien-tén ke kîm-vàn.23 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke sîm-ngien kiet-chhut san-kó;sià-ok ngìn ke hî-mong ngiâ-hí fun-nu.24 Lo̍k-san hau-sṳ̂, kín-lòi kín fu-chiuk;ngat-chhat ko-thu, fán-chón pien phìn-khiùng.25 Không-khói sṳ̂-sá, kien-kâ fat-tha̍t;sṳ́-ngìn mân-chiuk, chhṳ-kâ mùng-fuk.26 Tún-liòng m̀-mai, su-ngìn chu-chú;kâm-ngien mai-liòng, ngìn-ngìn chuk-fuk.27 Hot-mu liòng-san, pit-tet ên-fi;chhìm-khiù sià-ok, fo-fam lìm-thèu.28 Yí-kho chhièn-chhòi, chhiong chhiû-thiên lo̍k-ya̍p;ngi-ngìn hîn-vong, chhiong chhiâng-ya̍p meu-sṳn.29 Káu-lon kâ-thìn ke voi ki-sṳ̀n khûng-khûng ke fûng;ngong-tong ngìn voi sàng-cho mìn-lî ngìn ke nù-phu̍k.30 Ngi-ngìn ke kó-chṳ́ chhiu-he sâng-miang-su;chṳ-fi ngìn tông-tet ngìn-sîm( 20 11:30 "chṳ-fi ... ngìn-sîm" yû-têu kú yi̍t-pún yi̍t-cho "khiòng-phau-ngìn ham-hoi sâng-miang".).31 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhai sṳ-kiên yû po-yin,hò-khóng sià-ok ngìn lâu chhui-ngìn nè!
