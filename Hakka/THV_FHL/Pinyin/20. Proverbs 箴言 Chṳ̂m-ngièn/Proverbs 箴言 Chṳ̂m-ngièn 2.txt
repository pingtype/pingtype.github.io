1 Chṳ-fi ke Kiet-kó Lai-é â, ngì oi chiap-su ngài ke fa,oi chiông ngài ke min-lin ki chhai sîm-tú.2 Oi chet-ngí lòi thâng chṳ-fi,oi chôn-sîm lòi khiù chhûng-mìn.3 Oi thai-sâng chûi-khiù kien-sṳt,oi chhut-sâng chhìm-khiù chhûng-mìn.4 Oi chhiong chhìm kîm-ngiùn án ngie̍t-sîm,oi chhiong chhìm khong-tén ke chhòi-pó án ngin-chṳ̂n.5 Án-ngiòng, ngì chhiu voi hiáu-tet kin-vi Song-chú,liáu-kié oi ngióng-pân ngin-sṳt Song-ti.6 Yîn-vi sóng-su chṳ-fi ke chhiu-he Song-chú,chṳ-sṳt lâu chhûng-mìn lûng he tui Kì lòi ke.7 Kì vi chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn chhùn-liù chṳ̂n chṳ-fi,Kì cho hàng-vì sùn-chṳn ke ngìn ke tún-phài.8 Kì pó-sú ngi-ngìn ke tho,pó-fu khièn-sṳ̀n ngìn ke lu.9 Yèn-heu ngì chhiu voi liáu-kié kûng-ngi, kûng-phìn lâu chṳn-chhṳ̍t,tî yit-chhiet tông-hàng ke lu.10 Chṳ-fi voi chin-ngi̍p ngì ke sîm-tú,chṳ-sṳt voi sṳ́ ngì ke lìn khoai-lo̍k.11 Kien-sṳt voi pí-yu ngì,chhûng-mìn voi pó-sú ngì,12 oi sṳ́ ngì thot-lì fái-ngìn ke lu,lì-khôi kóng-fa mò sṳ-fî ke ngìn;13 ke-têu ngìn lì-khôi chṳn-tho,chôn-mùn hàng vû-am ke lu,14 fôn-hí cho fái-sṳ,chok-ok lòi khoai-lo̍k;15 kì-têu hàng vân-khiuk ke lu,sòng-sòng cho kiên-cha ke sṳ.16 Chṳ-fi voi kiu ngì thot-lì yìm-fu,chhiu-he chhin voi yín-yu ngì, choi-thiàm sa̍t-va̍t ke fu-ńg.17 Kì lì-khôi heu-sâng sṳ̀ só ka ke ló-kûng,m̀ ki-tet kì chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn só-li̍p ke fûn-yok( 8 2:17 "m̀ ki-tet ... fûn-yok" ya-he yi̍t-cho "m̀ ki-tet kì ke Song-ti só-li̍p ke khie-yok".).18 Hi kì vuk-hâ chhiu voi chhṳ̀m-lùn to-sí;hàng kì ke lu chhiu voi tiet-lo̍k yîm-kiên.19 Hi kì ke-vi ke ngìn mò yit-sà voi to-chón-lòi;kì-têu ya mò fap-thu chai chhìm-tó sâng-miang ke lu.20 Só-yî, ngì oi hàng hó-ngìn ke tho,oi kiún-sṳ́m chûn-hàng ngi-ngìn ke lu.21 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn voi chhai sṳ-song ôn-kî;hàng-vì mò hó-hiàm ke ngìn voi chhai thi-song chhòng-su.22 Chúng-he fái-ngìn voi tui sṳ-song chién-chhù;kiên-cha ke ngìn voi tui thi-song pâng-thet.
