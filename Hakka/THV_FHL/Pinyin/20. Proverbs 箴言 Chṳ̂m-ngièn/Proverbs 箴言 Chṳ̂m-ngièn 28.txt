1 Sià-ok ngìn sûi-yèn mò-ngìn tûi ya chhai thò-miang;chúng-he chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhiong sṳ̂-é mò kiâng má-ke.2 Koet-kâ chhûng-mân chhui-ok, chóng-khièn-chá chhiu kín-von;koet yû hièn-mìn liâng-chhiu, chṳn-khièn chang-nèn chhòng-kiú.3 Thai-kôn( 67 28:3 "Thai-kôn" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Khiùng-ngìn".) khî-fu khiùng-khú ngìn,chhiong thai-súi tá-chéu ńg-kuk.4 M̀ sú fap-li̍t ke kóng fái-ngìn tông-voi;chûn-sú fap-li̍t ke lâu kì-têu tui-khong.5 Fái-ngìn m̀-tî má-ke he chṳn-ngi;chhìm-khiù Song-chú ke mìn-pha̍k yit-chhiet.6 Hàng-vì sùn-chṳn ke khiùng-khú ngìn,yàng-ko kiên-cha ke hó-ngiak ngìn.7 Chûn-sú fap-li̍t ke chhiu-he chhûng-mìn ke lai-é;lâu hau-sṳ̍t lân-cho ke ngìn kiet phèn-yû, voi sṳ́ kiâ-pâ kien-seu.8 Kho kô li-sit sàng-cho hó-ngiak ngìn,kì ke chhièn-chhòi to-mî voi lo̍k chhai lìn-mén khiùng-khú ngìn ke sú-chûng.9 Ngìn na-he set-ngí m̀-hén thâng kau-sṳ,lièn kì ke khì-tó Song-ti ya chhin-nâu.10 Yín-yu chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn hàng sià-ok ke lu,liá-ngìn voi tiet-lo̍k chhṳ-kâ ke ham-chiáng;chúng-he sùn-kiet ke ngìn voi tet fuk-fun.11 Hó-ngiak ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ chhin-yû chṳ-fi;mìn-lî ke khiùng-ngìn khon-chhôn kì ke hî-ká.12 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chóng-khièn, ngìn-ngìn thai fôn-hí;sià-ok ngìn hîn-hí, sà-sà chéu-hi khong.13 Chiông chhṳ-kâ chhui-ko châ-tén ke, m̀-voi sṳ̀n-kûng;sṳ̀n-ngin ko-sṳt lòi fí-kói ke, Song-ti voi lìn-mén kì.14 Sòng-sòng kin-vi Song-chú sṳ̍t-chhai yû fuk-hi;ngang-sîm ngang-hok ke ngìn voi ham-lo̍k châi-fo.15 Fái ke chṳp-chṳn-chá ap-pet khiùng-khú ngìn,chhiu-chhiong thai-sâng ho-ho-kún ke sṳ̂-é,ya chhiong tú tông-yâu yu tông-ok ke yùng.16 Mò mìn-lî ke chṳp-chṳn-chá voi sàng-cho phau-kiûn;mò thâm put-ngi ke chhièn-chhòi chang voi hióng chhòng-su.17 Fam chhṳ̀-ngìn chhui ke, thin-chho̍k thò-miang to-sí;mò-lun ngì he má-ngìn, m̀-hó pông-chhu kì.18 Hàng-vì vàn-chhiòn, tet-tó chṳ́n-kiu;cho-ngìn kiên-cha, chhin-kiak tiet-tó.19 Sat-mâng kâng-chung, liòng-sṳ̍t chhûng-chiuk;long-fi kông-yîm, nàn-miên khiùng-khú.20 Sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke ngìn chhûng-mân hen-fuk;sióng fat vàng-chhòi nàn-miên su-fa̍t.21 Cho-m̀-tet phiên-sîm lòi khon ngìn ke chhìn-mien;chúng-he yû-ngìn vi yit-te piáng hi fam-fap.22 Ku chhon-chhièn sióng kiak-kiak fat-chhòi ke,m̀-tî khiùng-khú thèn-tén chhiu to-lòi.23 Khien-va ngìn ke, to-mî voi tet-tó fôn-ngiàng,kha-yàng ke kóng-fa ô-mì fung-sṳ̀n ngìn ke.24 Thêu-nâ yà-ôi ke, yu kóng: "Liá m̀-he chhui",liá-chak ngìn lâu khiòng-tho mò má-ke chhâ-phe̍t.25 Chhṳ-sṳ̂ ke ngìn ngiâ-hí fûn-chên;yí-kho Song-chú tit-khok fûng-sṳn.26 Sîm-kôn tân-tân kho chhṳ-kâ ke, liá chhiu-he ngong-ngìn;cheu chṳ-fi hàng-sṳ cho-ngìn ke, voi tet-tó chṳ́n-kiu.27 Kiu-chi khiùng-khú ke, m̀-voi khiet-fa̍t;cha m̀-tî ke, voi tô su chu-chú.28 Sià-ok ngìn chóng-khièn, sà-sà chéu-hi khong;kì-têu me̍t-mòng, chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn chhiu chen-kâ.
