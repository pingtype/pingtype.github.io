1 Li-mu-yî-le̍t-vòng Â-mê ke Kau-hiun Hâ-poi he Li-mu-yî-le̍t-vòng ke fa, he kì ke â-mê kau-hiun kì ke chṳ̂m-ngièn:2 Ngài ke lai-é, ngài chhîn-sên ke lai-é,ngài khì-tó hí-ngien tet lòi ke lai-é,ngài oi ngióng-pân kâu ngì chang-hó nè?3 Ngì m̀-hó chhai ńg-ngìn sṳ̂n-song hô-fi ngì ke chîn-li̍t;liá-chúng sṳ sṳt hoi chhin-tô kiûn-vòng sṳt-phai.4 Kiûn-vòng cho-m̀-tet ô,Li-mu-yî-le̍t â,kiûn-vòng cho-m̀-tet lîm-chiú;vòng-chṳ́ cho-m̀-tet thâm ngiàm-chiú.5 Kì-têu na-he lîm-chiú, voi m̀ ki-tet koet-fap,ya voi pok-siok yit-chhiet khun-khú ngìn ke khièn-li.6 Ngiàm-chiú he pûn mien-tui sí ke ngìn,phù-thò-chiú he pûn sông-sîm ke ngìn;7 pûn kì-têu lîm, pûn kì-têu m̀ ki-tet chhṳ-kâ ke khiùng-khú;pûn kì-têu m̀-sṳ́ kín-sióng chhṳ-kâ ke put-hen.8 Ngì oi vi mò-khièn chhut-sâng ke ngìn kóng-fa,ya oi vi yit-chhiet chhôi-ko ke ngìn sṳ̂n-yên.9 Ngì oi thoi kì-têu chhut-sâng, oi kûng-tong phan-ton,oi vì-fu phìn-khiùng ngìn lâu kân-khú ngìn ke khièn-li.10 Hièn-fi ke Pû-ngiòng Hièn-fi ke pû-ngiòng sṳ̍t-chhai chhin nàn-tet ô!Kì ke ka-chhṳ̍t pí chû-pó hàn-kha kui-chhung!11 Kì ke ló-kûng cho-tet fong-sîm sin-lai kì,sû-ngi̍p thin-chho̍k m̀-voi m̀-la yung.12 Kì yit-sên-ngìn sṳ́ ló-kûng tet-tó hó-chhu,yit-chhṳ̍t m̀-voi sṳ́ kì yû má-ke sún-hoi.13 Kì chhìm yòng-mô lâu mà-sâ,fôn-hí sat-mâng cho sâm-fu.14 Kì ya chhiong sông-sòn khiung-yong,tui yén-fông yun liòng-sṳ̍t lòi.15 Kì thiên hàn màng-kông chhiu hong-chhòng,vi chhiòn-kâ ngìn chún-phi sṳ̍t-vu̍t,chṳ́-tiám fûn-phi sṳ́-ńg kûng-chok.16 Kì khon chung-yi ke thièn-thi chhiu mâi hâ-lòi,yung chhṳ-kâ ke li̍t-liong kâng-chung phù-thò-yèn.17 Kì kiê-hèn yêu-tai, sat-mâng cho-se,sûng-sú chhin chhut-li̍t, lò-luk kûng-chok.18 Kì tî chhṳ-kâ só kîn-yìn ke yû-chhièn chhon,vuk-hâ ke tên-fó tiám kông-kông cho-to pan-ya.19 Kì vi chhṳ-kâ fóng-sien,yu vi chhṳ-kâ kâng-pu.20 Kì thai-fông kiu-chi phìn-khiùng ngìn,chhûn sûng-sú pông-chhu khun-khú ngìn.21 Lo̍k-siet thiên kì m̀-sṳ́ vi vuk-hâ ngìn sèu,yîn-vi chhiòn-kâ ngìn yû ko-tûng ke sâm-fu.22 Kì vi chhṳ-kâ cho-hó siu-fâ thán-è,chok mà-sâ lâu chṳ́-set pu cho ke sâm.23 Kì ke ló-kûng su-ngìn chûn-chhung, he thi-fông liâng-chhiu,chhai sàng-mùn-héu thùng pún-thi ke chóng-ló khiung-ha chhô.24 Kì cho yu-mà-pu ke sâm hi-mai,yu cho yêu-tai mai-pûn sên-lî-ngìn.25 Nèn-li̍t lâu chûn-kui cho kì ke sâm-fu;sióng-tó chiông-lòi kì chhiu fôn-thèu hí-mien.26 Kì kóng ke fa-ngî tông-yû chṳ-fi,só-kóng ke he chhṳ̀-oi ke kau-hiun.27 Kì koân-sîm kâ-vu,m̀-hén sṳ̍t hàn-fan.28 Kì ke chṳ́-ńg thai-sâng kóng, kì he yû fuk-hi ke ngìn;kì ke ló-kûng ya ô-nó kì, kóng:29 "Hièn-fi ke fu-ńg yû chhin-tô,chúng-he ngì chhêu-ko kì-têu sà."30 Mî-mau he kho m̀-he̍t ke, ngìn chiâng ya he chhiam-sṳ̀ ke;chṳ́-yû kin-vi Song-chú ke fu-ńg yin-tông su chhṳ̂n-chan.31 Ngien kì hióng-su lò-luk só-tet ke;ngien kì ke sṳ̀n-chhiu kûng-khôi péu-yòng.
