1 Oi Chhìm-khiù Chṳ-fi Lai-é â, oi thâng â-pâ ke kau-hiun;ngì-têu oi chôn-sîm lòi tet-tó chhûng-mìn.2 Ngài pûn ngì-têu ke kau-hiun lûng-chúng he hó ke,só-yî, ngì-têu m̀-hó phiên-lì ngài ke kau-sṳ.3 Ngài chhai â-pâ mien-chhièn he hau-chṳ́,chhai â-mê ngién-chûng he tet-ngìn-siak ke tân-tên-chṳ́;4 ke-sṳ̀, ngâ-pâ chhiu kâu ngài kóng:"Ngì ke sîm oi ki-tet ngài ke fa;oi chûn-sú ngài ke kie-min,ngì chhiu voi yû fûng-fu ke sâng-miang.5 Oi chhìm-khiù chṳ-fi, oi chûi-khiù chhûng-mìn;mo̍k m̀ ki-tet ya-he vì-poi ngài ke fa.6 Mo̍k lì-khôi chṳ-fi, kì voi pó-fu ngì;ngì siak kì, kì yit-thin voi pó-sú ngì.7 Chṳ-fi he chui chhung-yeu ke, só-yî oi chhìm-khiù chṳ-fi( 9 4:7 "Chṳ-fi he ... chṳ-fi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Chṳ-fi ke khôi-thèu he chhìm-khiù chṳ-fi".);ngì oi yung ngì só-yû ke yit-chhiet lòi tet-tó chhûng-mìn.8 Ngì khon-chhung chṳ-fi, chṳ-fi chhiu voi pûn ngì kô-sṳ̂n;ngì nám-tén chṳ-fi, kì chhiu voi pûn ngì yû chûn-yùng.9 Chṳ-fi voi chiông chiâng mo-é tai chhai ngì thèu-nà-táng,chiông kông-yùng ke miên-liù sung-pûn ngì."10 Oi Hàng Chṳ-fi ke Tho Lai-é â, thâng ngài kóng, chiap-su ngài ke fa,ngì chhiu voi hióng-su chhòng-su.11 Ngài yí-kîn kâu ngì hàng chṳ-fi ke tho,yí-kîn tai-liâng ngì hàng chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke lu.12 Ngì hàng ke sṳ̀, phu-phu sun-li;ngì chéu ke sṳ̀, m̀-voi tiet-tó.13 Oi vún-vún chok-he̍t kau-hiun, m̀-hó piong-thet kì;oi kiún-sú kì, yîn-vi kì he ngì ke sâng-miang.14 M̀-hó hàng sià-ok ngìn ke tho;ya m̀-hó tha̍p fái-ngìn ke lu.15 Oi sám-khôi, cho-m̀-tet kîn-ko!Oi chón-sṳ̂n lì-khôi, sám ko-hi!16 Fái-ngìn mò cho-têu fái-sṳ, chhiu soi m̀-lo̍k kau;kì-têu na mò hoi-ngìn tiet-tó, chhiu soi m̀-tet;17 yîn-vi sià-ok he kì-têu sṳ̍t ke fan,chhàn-phau he kì-têu lîm ke chiú.18 Ngi-ngìn ke lu chhiong tá-chó ke kông,kín-lòi kín kông, chhṳ̍t-to tông-chu-thèu.19 Sià-ok ngìn ke tho chhiong am-pû-thèu ke vû-am;kì-têu tiet-tó, hàn m̀-tî pûn má-ke kang-tó.20 Lai-é â, oi chu-yi thâng ngài ke fa,oi chet-ngí thâng ngài ke kau-hiun.21 Mo̍k pûn kì-têu tui ngiâ mien-chhièn sám-chéu,oi yit-chhṳ̍t ki chhai ngì ke sîm-kôn tú;22 yîn-vi chhìm-tó kì-têu ke, chhiu-he tet-tó sâng-miang,chhiòn-sṳ̂n ya voi tet-tó yî-chhṳ.23 Ngì oi kiún-sṳ́m ngì ke sîm-sṳ̂ chhêu-ko yit-chhiet;yîn-vi sâng-miang ke chhièn-ngièn he tui sîm yúng-chhut.24 Oi chhù-thet vân-khiuk ke fa,oi yén-lì fâ-liâu ke choi.25 Ngì ke muk-chû oi tân-tân hiong-chhièn khon;ngì ke muk-chû oi chhṳ̍t-chhṳ̍t khon thèu-chhièn.26 Oi siû-phiàng( 10 4:26 "siû-phiàng" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chu-yi".) ngì ke kiok só-hàng ke lu,ngì só-cho ke yit-chhiet chhiu voi vún-tong.27 M̀-hó hiong-chó fe̍t hiong-yu phiên-chéu;oi sṳ́ ngì ke kiok yén-lì sià-ok.
