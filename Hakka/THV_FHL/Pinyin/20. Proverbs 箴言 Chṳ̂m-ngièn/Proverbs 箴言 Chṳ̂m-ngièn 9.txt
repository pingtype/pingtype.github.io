1 Chṳ-fi lâu Ngong-tong Chṳ-fi hí chhṳ-kâ ke vuk-è,tun chhit-kî chhû-tún,2 chhṳ̀ ngiù-yòng, thiàu mî-chiú, phan chok-si̍t.3 Kì chhâi-phai sṳ́-ńg chhut-hi chheu-ngìn,chhṳ-kâ chhai sàng-tú chui-kô ke thi-fông hêm-kóng:4 "Ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn â, to ngài liá-vi lòi!"Kì yu tui mò chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn kóng:5 "Lòi â, lòi sṳ̍t ngài ke phông-phai,lòi lîm ngài thiàu-chṳ ke mî-chiú.6 Oi lì-khôi ngong-chhṳ̍t, hó-hó sên-fa̍t,oi hàng chhûng-mìn ke tho-lu."7 Kiu-chṳn ngau-man ngìn ke, voi ngiâ-tó siû-yu̍k;chit-phi sià-ok ngìn ke, voi sông-hoi chhṳ-kâ.8 M̀-hó chit-phi ngau-man ngìn ke ko-chho,yîn-vi kì voi hen ngì.Ngì na-he chit-phi yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn,kì chhiu voi kin-oi ngì.9 Khien-va chṳ-fi ngìn, kì chhiu kien-kâ yû chṳ-fi;kau-thô chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn, kì chhiu voi chen-kâ ho̍k-mun.10 Kin-vi Song-chú he chṳ-fi ke khôi-thèu;ngin-sṳt Chṳ-sṳn-chá chhiu-he chhûng-mìn.11 Chṳ-fi voi chen-kâ ngì ke ngit-chṳ́,ya voi sṳ́ ngì hióng-su chhòng-su.12 Ngì na yû chṳ-fi, tet-tó hó-chhu ke he ngì chhṳ-kâ;ngì na chhùn ngau-man, sún-sṳt he ngì chhṳ-kâ lòi tâm-tông.13 Ngong-tong ke Fu-ńg chhàu-chhàu nau-nau,kì he tông-ngong, má-ke tû m̀-tî.14 Kì chhô chhai chhṳ-kâ vuk-hâ mùn-héu,chhô chhai sàng-tú chui-kô ke thi-fông,15 tui ke-têu ko-lu ke ngìn,tui tông kón-lu ke ngìn hêm-kóng:16 "Ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn â, to ngài liá-vi lòi!"Kì yu tui mò chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn kóng:17 "Thêu-lòi ke súi kha-thiàm,thêu-sṳ̍t ke piáng kha-hiông."18 Chúng-he ngong-ngìn m̀-tî yû yîm-fùn chhai kì ke-vi;kì ke ngìn-hak yí-kîn tiet-lo̍k yîm-kiên ke chhṳ̂m-lak.
