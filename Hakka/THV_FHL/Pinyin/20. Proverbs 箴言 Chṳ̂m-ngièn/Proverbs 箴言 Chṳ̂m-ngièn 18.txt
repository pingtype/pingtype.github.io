1 Kû-ló ke ngìn koân-sîm chhṳ-kâ thin-thin;phe̍t-ngìn hó ke yi-kien, kì chúng-oi fán-tui.2 Ngong-tong ngìn mò fôn-hí chṳ-sṳt;tân-tân hau tién chhṳ-kâ thin-thin.3 Sià-ok lòi, vú-yu̍k thèn-tén lòi;kien-seu to, siû-chhṳ́ khiung-ha to.4 Khiéu-chûng ke ngièn-ngî chhiong thai-hói án-chhṳ̂m;chṳ-fi ke chhièn-ngièn chhiong hâi-súi yúng-liù.5 Chiúng-yùng sià-ok he put-kûng;yên-vóng vù-kû me put-ngi.6 Ngong-ngìn kóng-fa yîn-hí chên-tôn;kì yit khôi-choi chhiu ngiâ-ngìn tá.7 Ngong-tong ngìn ke choi phai-fai kì chhṳ-kâ;kiâ choi-sùn he kì sâng-miang ke ham-chiáng.8 Poi-heu ke hàn-fa chhiong sṳ̍t phông-phai;yit lo̍k-choi chhiu thûn-to chhòng-tú hi.9 Lân-sṳ̂ cho-se ke lâu ngièn-hàng phai-fai ke, he hó hiûng-thi.10 Song-chú ke miàng he kiên-ku ke sàng-pó;ngi-ngìn chéu ngi̍p-hi chhiu tet-tó ôn-chhiòn.11 Hó-ngiak ngìn chiông kì ke chhòi-fu khon-cho sàng-pó,láu-tó he kô-thai kiên-ku ke thùng-chhiòng thiet-piak.12 Phai-fai siên-chhièn, ngìn-sîm kiêu-ngau;chûn-yùng yî-chhièn, pit-yû khiâm-pî.13 Màng thâng chhîn-chhú, chhiu chhióng-siên fì-tap,liá he ngong-tong, voi tù-tó kien-seu.14 Khiù sên-chhùn ke sîm-chṳ, nèn ngiûn-su phiang-thung;yi-chṳ na-he sêu-chhṳ̀m, má-ngìn ngai tet-ko?15 Chhûng-mìn ngìn ke sîm oi tet chṳ-sṳt;chṳ-fi ngìn ke ngí-kûng khiù chṳ-sṳt.16 Ngìn ke lî-vu̍t vi kì khôi-lu,thu kì to thai ngìn-vu̍t mien-chhièn.17 Siên ko-chhong ke, khon-chhiong yû-lî;tui-fông lòi-to, chhà-chhut sṳ-fî.18 Chhû-chhiâm khó-yî phìn-sit yên-kâ,ya nèn kié-khôi lióng-khiòng chhàn-teu.19 Hiûng-thi kiet yên-sù, tông-chhiong kiên-ku ke sàng-chhiòng chhin-nàn pho;án-ngiòng ke chên-chhàu, chhiong sàng-pó chhôn-tén ke mùn chhin-nàn thûng.20 Ngìn kóng-fa só tai-lòi ke kiet-kó,thai-thai yáng-hióng kì ke yit-sên;ngìn ke fa só sán-sên ke kó-háu,voi sṳ́ kì sṳ̍t-to páu-páu.21 Fe̍t-sâng fe̍t-sí, chhai sa̍t-mà ke khièn-ha;hau kóng-fa ke, heu-kó chhṳ-kâ sṳ̀n-su.22 Thó-tó hó pû-ngiòng sṳ̍p-fûn hen-fuk,ya he tet-tó Song-chú ke ên-fi.23 Phìn-khiùng ngìn ke khién-khiù, tâi-sâng hâ-hi;yû-chhièn ngìn ke fì-tap, sṳ̍p-fûn lû-fû.24 Chiú-ngiuk phèn-yû, chhìn-pho̍k ko-chṳ́( 39 18:24 "Chiú-ngiuk ... ko-chṳ́" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Lám-sám kâu phèn-yû ke, chhṳ-chhí phai-fai", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.);tî-kí phèn-yû, yàng-ko hiûng-thi.
