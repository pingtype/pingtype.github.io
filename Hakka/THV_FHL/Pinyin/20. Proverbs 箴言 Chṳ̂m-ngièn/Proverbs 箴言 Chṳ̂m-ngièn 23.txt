1 2 (6) Ngì lâu thai ngìn-vu̍t khiung-chok sṳ̍t-fan ke sṳ̀-chiet,oi ki-tet chhô chhai ngiâ mien-chhièn ke he má-ngìn.2 Na-he ngì fan-liong thai,chhiu oi yok-suk chhṳ-kâ( 51 23:2 "chhiu oi yok-suk chhṳ-kâ" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "chhiu oi khià tô-é piong chhai ngì ke hèu-lièn song".).3 M̀-hó thâm-sṳ̍t kì ke phông-phai;kiâng-pha liá he kì ke yîm-mèu.4 2 (7) Ngì oi chhûng-mìn têu-é,mo̍k ko-lò, sí-oi chhièn.5 Chhièn-chhòi yit-ngiap-muk chhiu sêu-sṳt;yîn-vi chhièn-chhòi voi sâng yi̍t-kap,chhiu chhiong yeu-phò pî-to thiên-táng.6 2 (8) Mo̍k sṳ̍t ngat-chhat ngìn ke fan,m̀-hó thâm kì ke phông-phai.7 Kì kóng: "M̀-sṳ́ se-ngi, sṳ̍t â, lîm â",chúng-he kì sîm-tú nang-ngoi yû tá-son.Kì cho-ngìn, sîm-tú chôn-mùn kie-kau chhièn( 52 23:7 "Kì cho-ngìn ... kie-kau chhièn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kì sióng má-ke, cho-ngìn chhiu-he án-ngiòng".).8 Ngì só-sṳ̍t ke yit-si̍t tûng-sî voi éu chhut-lòi;ngì só-kóng ke chiâng-fa ya voi pien-cho khûng-khûng.9 2 (9) Ngì m̀-hó kóng-fa pûn ngong-ngìn thâng;kì voi méu-sṳ ngì chṳ-fi ke fa.10 2 (10) M̀-hó sái kú-sṳ̀ ke thi-kie,m̀-hó cham kû-yì ke thièn-thi;11 yîn-vi kì-têu ke Pó-fu-chá thai-yû nèn-li̍t,Kì oi cho ngì ke tui-thèu, vi kì-têu sṳ̂n-yên.12 2 (11) Oi chôn-sîm chiap-su kau-hiun,ngin-chṳ̂n thâng chṳ-sṳt ke fa.13 2 (12) M̀-hó m̀ kón-kau se-ngìn-è;yung chuk-siû-é m̀-voi tá-sí;14 ngì yung chuk-siû-é tá kì,chang voi kiu kì ke sâng-miang.15 2 (13) Lai-é â, ngì na yû chṳ-fi,ngài he ngióng-pân fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k!16 Ngì yit-kóng chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke fa,ngài ke sîm-kôn chhiu tông-thiong!17 2 (14) Ngì m̀-hó chhit-tu chhui-ngìn,oi ngit-ya kin-vi Song-chú;18 án-ngiòng, ngì ke chhièn-thù chhiu kông-mìn,ngì ke chiông-lòi ya m̀-voi chhie̍t-mong.19 2 (15) Lai-é â, thâng ngài ke fa lòi tet-tó chṳ-fi;oi liù-sîm hàng chṳn-lu.20 Hau lîm-chiú ke, thâm-sṳ̍t ngiuk ke,m̀-hó thùng kì-têu cho phèn-yû;21 yîn-vi hau-chiú thâm-sṳ̍t ke, voi pien khiùng-khú ngìn;hau soi-muk ke ngìn, yit-thin voi chok lan sâm-fu.22 2 (16) Yin-kôi thâng-chhiùng kiung ngì ke yà-ôi;chhiu-son ló lé, ya m̀-hó khon-khiâng kì-têu.23 Ngì oi fu thoi-ka lòi tet-tó chṳ̂n-lî, chṳ-fi, kau-hiun lâu kien-sṳt;chhiên-van m̀-hó chiông kì-têu mai-thet.24 Ngi-ngìn ke yà-è, thin-chho̍k voi fî-sòng khoai-lo̍k;lai-é yû chṳ-fi, kiung kì ke chhiu chhin fôn-hí.25 Yin-tông pûn ngì ke yà-ôi khoai-lo̍k;oi pûn kiung ngì ke â-mê fôn-hí!26 2 (17) Lai-é â, oi chhiòn-sîm hiong ngài ho̍k-si̍p,oi chu-yi khon ngài ke mù-yong.27 Kî-ńg he vù-tái chhṳ̂m-hâng;yìm-fu he chṳ-miang ham-chiáng.28 Kì-têu chhiong mài-fu̍k ke khiòng-tho,sṳ́ kien-tô ke ngìn pien kiên-cha.29 2 (18) Má-ngìn yû châi-fo?Má-ngìn yû yù-sông?Má-ngìn yû siông-châng?Má-ngìn yû mài-yen?Má-ngìn vù-ku su-sông?Má-ngìn muk-chû chhak-chhak?30 Chhiu-he ke-têu liù-lièn chui-hiông,hau-chiú thâm-pî ke ngìn.31 M̀-hó thâm-oi pî-chûng yu-fùng yu-chiâng,chhai hèu-lièn chûng yu-sùn yu-sun ke chiú.32 Chui-heu chiú voi chhiong sà-kô chhàn ngì,chhiong thu̍k-sà ngâu ngì.33 Ngì voi muk fâ-fâ, thèu fûn-fûn, lon kóng-fa.34 Ngì voi chui-to fèu-fèu, chhiong chhai hói-chûng,yu chhiong soi chhai sòn ke ngùi-kôn táng-kô.35 Ngì voi kóng:"Ngài pûn-ngìn chhùi, m̀-voi thung,pûn-ngìn tá, mò kám-kok.Ngài ngióng siáng m̀ ko-lòi?Ngài oi chai lîm yit-pî!"
