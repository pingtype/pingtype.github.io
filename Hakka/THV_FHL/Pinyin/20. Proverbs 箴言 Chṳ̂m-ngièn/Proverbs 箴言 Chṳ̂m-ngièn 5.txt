1 Oi Yén-lì Yìm-lon Lai-é â, oi khon-chhung ngài ke chṳ-fi,oi ngin-chṳ̂n thâng ngài chhûng-mìn ke fa-ngî;2 án-ngiòng, ngì chhiu voi kiún-sṳ́m hàng-sṳ,ngì ke choi-sùn voi mân-yû chṳ-sṳt.3 Yìm-fu ke choi-sùn tit-hâ phûng-thòng,kì ke choi pí kám-lám-yù kha-va̍t;4 chúng-he chui-mî kì pí vòng-lièn kha fú,ya pí lióng-piên ke tô-kiam hàn-kha li.5 Kì ke kiok hàng sí-mòng ke lu;kì ke kiok-phu chhṳ̍t-hiong yîm-kiên.6 Kì mò chu-yi hàng sâng-miang ke lu,kiok-phu hàng-chhâ, chhṳ-kâ me m̀-tî.7 Lai-é â, liá-ha oi thâng-chhiùng ngài;ngì-têu m̀-hó vì-poi ngài ke fa.8 Oi yén-yén lì-khôi yìm-fu!Chhiên-van m̀-hó hàng-khiûn kì ke mùn-héu!9 Kiâng-pha ngì voi chiông hó miàng-sâng sung-pûn phe̍t-ngìn,chiông chhiâng-chhûn ke ngièn-ngit kâu-pûn hiûng-ok ke ngìn;10 kiâng-pha sâng-fun-ngìn chhiu lòi chhióng-chéu ngì ke chhòi-sán,ngì lò-luk tet-lòi ke voi lo̍k-ngi̍p ngoi-ngìn ke kâ.11 Ngì lìm-chûng ke sṳ̀, hiet-hi sêu-chhin,voi kín-chhên mò-thìn, kóng:12 "Ngài cho-má-ke án yen-hen kón-kau?Ngài ke sîm ngióng-voi méu-sṳ khien-va?13 Ngài tông-sṳ̀ m̀-thâng sṳ̂-fu ke fa,vàn-chhiòn m̀-tap ngài ke-têu sîn-sâng.14 Ngài liá-ha chhai kûng-khiung chhòng-só chung-ngìn ke mien-chhièn,kien-seu to voi sí!"15 Ngì oi lîm chhṳ-kâ pî-thòng chûng ke súi,lîm ngì chhṳ-kâ chiáng-tú liù-chhut ke súi.( 11 5:15 Pún-chiet ke yi-sṳ he "Ngì oi tui chhṳ-kâ ke pû-ngiòng chûng-sṳ̍t".)16 Ngì ke chhièn-ngièn kám cho-tet liù-to kiê-hong?Ngì ke chhièn-súi kám cho-tet yúng-to kóng-chhòng?17 Ngì ke chhièn-ngièn oi tân-tân kûi-su̍k ngì;chhie̍t-tui cho-m̀-tet lâu ngoi-ngìn chhâm-chha̍p.18 Ngien ngì ke chhièn-ngièn mùng-fuk;ngien ngì fôn-hí heu-sâng sṳ̀ só-thó ke pû-ngiòng.19 Kì chhiong tet-ngìn-siak yu tông-chiâng ke lu̍k-é;ngien kì ke hiûng-fài sṳ̀-sòng pûn ngì sîm-mân yi-chiuk,ngien kì ke oi-chhìn yit-chhṳ̍t sṳ́ ngì sṳ̀n-fùn tiên-to.20 Lai-é â, cho-má-ke oi pûn phe̍t ke fu-ngìn-kâ mì-tó?Cho-má-ke oi nám yìm-fu ke hiûng-fài?21 Ngìn só-hàng ke tho, chhai Song-chú ngién-chhièn;ngìn yit-chhiet ke lu, Kì lûng-chúng kam-chhat.22 Sià-ok ngìn ke chhui chhiong lò-mióng,chiông chhṳ-kâ khún-to hèn-hèn;kì só-fam ke chhui chhiong sok-è,chiông chhṳ-kâ chhàn-to sí-sí.23 Kì yîn-vi m̀-su kón-kau, chhiu sóng-sṳt sâng-miang;yîn-vi ngong-tong chṳ-khi̍t, kì pit-thin hàng-chhâ lu.
