1 Kien-tô ke Khien-va Lai-é â, ngì na tap-yin thoi lìn-sa cho tâm-pó,ya-he thoi m̀-sṳt ke ngìn ak-sú chhiâm-yok vàn-chai,2 ngì chhiu pûn chhṳ-kâ kóng-ko ke fa chhàn-tó,pûn chhṳ-kâ tap-yin ke fa lap-tó.3 Lai-é, ngì ki-yèn lo̍k chhai lìn-sa ke sú-chûng,oi án-ngiòng lòi-kiu chhṳ-kâ:Oi kiak-kiak khiâm-pî hâ-lòi,khiù tui-fông kié-chhù khie-yok.4 Ngì cho-m̀-tet soi-muk,ya m̀-hó tuk-muk-soi.5 Oi kiu chhṳ-kâ,chhiong lu̍k-é sám-khôi tá-lia̍p ngìn ke sú,chhiong tiâu-é thot-lì chok tiâu-é ke sú.6 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn â, oi hi chhìm ngie-kûng;chhà-khon kì-têu ngióng-pân sên-fa̍t,hó sàng-cho yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn.7 Ngie-kûng mò liâng-chhiu,mò kôn-chóng, mò thúng-chhṳ-chá;8 chúng-he, kì-têu chhai ngie̍t-thiên yi-phi sṳ̍t-vu̍t,chhai sû-kot ke sṳ̀-chiet tún-chit liòng-sṳ̍t.9 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn â, ngì oi soi-to kí-sṳ̀?Ngì kí-tô tiám-chûng chang oi hong-chhòng nè?10 Ngì kóng: "Pûn ngài tuk-kû hiû-sit yit-ha,soi kâ yit-ha-é, nám-tén sú hiet-khun yit-ha."11 Chúng-he, phìn-khiùng voi fut-yèn-kiên chhiong khiòng-tho lòi chhióng ngì;khiet-fa̍t voi chhiong thú-fî lòi kûng-tá ngì.12 Ke-têu m̀ cho chṳn-tông sṳ-ngia̍p ke fái-ngìn,hàng-lòi hàng-hi kóng fâ-liâu-fa khî-phien ngìn.13 Kì-têu yung muk-chû, sú-kiok,tá am-ho lòi khî-phien ngì.14 Kì-têu ke fái sîm-su̍t sṳ̀-sṳ̀ sat-kie,chṳ-chho fûn-chên.15 Só-yî, châi-nan voi kiak-kiak lìm-to kì-têu;kì-têu voi fut-yèn phai-fái, mò-fap hó-kiu.16 Song-chú só yen-hen ke yû liuk-hong,lièn Kì só-nâu ke khiung-yû chhit-hong,17 chhiu-he: kiêu-ngau ke muk-chû,kóng phong-fûng ke sa̍t-mà,sat-hoi vù-kû ke sú,18 kúi-kie tô-tôn ke sîm,pûn-chéu sià-lu ke kiok,19 phong-fûng kóng yèu-ngièn cho ká kien-chṳn,chhai phèn-yû chûng-kiên kâu-sô sṳ-fî.20 Kín-kie Yìm-lon Lai-é â, oi kiún-sú ngiâ-pâ ke kie-min,m̀-hó lì-khôi ngiâ-mê ke kau-sṳ;21 oi yit-chhṳ̍t khat chhai sîm-tú,koa chhai ngì ke kiáng-kîn hong.22 Kì-têu ke kau-hiun voi tai-liâng ngì só-hàng ke lu,am-pû-sṳ̀ pó-sú ngì,ngit-sṳ̀-thèu kâu-tai ngì.23 Yîn-vi kie-min he tên, kau-sṳ he kông,kau-hiun lâu chit-phi voi chṳ́-sṳ ngì ngìn-sên ke tho-lu,24 voi pó-sú ngì yén-lì fái fu-ngìn-kâ,lâu choi-thiàm sa̍t-va̍t ke yìm-fu.25 Ngì sîm-chûng m̀-hó pûn kì ke mî-set mì-tó,ya m̀-hó pûn kì ke ngién-koái chok-hi;26 yîn-vi kî-ńg voi hoi ngì to chṳ́-chhûn yit-te piáng,phe̍t-ngìn ke pû-ngiòng ya voi chiông ngì ke ló-miang sû-thet.27 Ngìn na-he chiông fó khong chhai fài-chûng,kám m̀-voi sêu-thet chhṳ-kâ ke sâm?28 Ngìn chhai sêu-tén ke fó-than chûng hàng,kiok kám yû khó-nèn m̀-voi sêu-sông?29 Thùng lìn-sa ke pû-ngiòng khiung-ha soi ke, ya chhiong án-ngiòng;chhîn-khiun phe̍t-ngìn ke pû-ngiòng ke, yit-thin voi su chit-fa̍t.30 Chhe̍t-é yîn-vi tú-kî thêu-nâ sṳ̍t-vu̍t chhûng-kî,ngìn kha m̀-voi méu-sṳ kì;31 chúng-he pûn-ngìn chok-tó, chhiu oi phòi-ngìn chhit-phi,chiông kì kâ-chûng só-yû ke, chhiòn-phu phòi chhut-lòi.32 Thùng phe̍t-ngìn ke pû-ngiòng thûng-kiên sṳ̍t-chhai tông-ngong;án-ngiòng cho ke ngìn thin-chho̍k voi hoi-sí chhṳ-kâ.33 Kì yit-thin voi su siû-yu̍k pûn-ngìn tá;kì ke kien-seu yún-yén ya sé m̀-chhiang;34 yîn-vi chhit-tu voi sṳ́ ló-kûng thai fat-khién,po-sù ke sṳ̀, kì yit-thin m̀-voi liù-chhìn.35 Kì mò-oi chiap-su châ-siû-fi,kha-tô lî-vu̍t kì ya m̀-sû.
