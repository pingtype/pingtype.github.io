1 Hó miàng-sâng phi ngong-tong ngìn tông m̀ tên-tui,chhiong ha-thiên lo̍k-siet chhiû-sû lo̍k-yí khiung-yong.2 Mò lî ke chu-chú chhiong vuk-yàm-tiâu lâu yen-é pî-lòi pî-hi,m̀-voi lo̍k chhai ngìn ke sṳ̂n-hong.3 Yung phì-piên tá-mâ, yung choi-lap sṳ́-lì,oi yung kun-é tá ngong-tong ngìn ke poi-nòng.4 M̀-hó cheu ngong-ngìn ke ngong-fa en kì,kiâng-pha ngì ya thùng kì khiung-yong.5 Oi cheu ngong-ngìn ke ngong-fa tù kì,miên-tet kì láu-tó chhṳ-kâ yû chṳ-fi.6 Ham ngong-ngìn chhòn-fa ke,chhiu-chhiong chám-thôn chhṳ-kâ ke kiok, chêu-lòi sún-hoi.7 Chhiong khiò-è ke kiok chhiòn-mò lu-yung,ngong-ngìn kóng chṳ̂m-ngièn ya he án-ngiòng.8 Chiông chûn-yùng pûn ngong-ngìn,chhiong sa̍k-thèu thak chhai thàn-kiûng song, sa m̀ chhut-hi.9 Ngong-tong ngìn kóng chṳ̂m-ngièn,chhiong chiú-chui ke ngìn nâ-tén net-su-khoá lon-tá ngìn.10 Sùi-phien chhiáng ngong-ngìn lâu ko-lu ngìn cho-kûng ke,voi sṳ́ só-yû ke thùng-kûng chhiòn-phu su sún-hoi( 60 26:10 "voi sṳ́ ... su sún-hoi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhiu-chhiong kiûng-chien-sú lon-sa, sông-tó chung-ngìn", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.).11 Ngong-tong ngìn yit-pái yu yit-pái cho ngong-se,chhiong kiéu fì-thèu sṳ̍t kì só éu chhut-lòi ke.12 Ngì khon chhṳ-kâ láu-tó yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn mâ?Lièn ngong-tong ke ngìn pí kì hàn-kha yû hî-mong.13 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn liù-to vuk-hâ nong-sióng kóng:"Lu-hong yû sṳ̂-é, kiê-hong yû sṳ̂-é!"14 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn lai-chhòng fân-lòi fân-hi,chhiu-chhiong mùn-kau-é chón-lòi chón-hi.15 Lân-sṳ̂ ke ngìn chhûn-sú to phàn-tú nâ sṳ̍t-vu̍t,lân-to lièn kiû-sú sung lo̍k-choi ya hiàm fi-hi.16 Lân-sṳ̂ ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ pí chhit-ke chhin voi yin-tui ngìn ke hàn-kha yû chṳ-fi.17 Hi chhap mò kôn-sa̍p chhṳ-kâ ke chên-chhàu,chhiu-chhiong khiú-tó ko-lu-kiéu ke ngí-kûng.18 Phien lìn-sa yèn-heu kóng: "Ngài he lâu ngì tú-káu ke ô!"liá-ngìn chhiu-chhiong tiên-é, chiông chṳ-miang ke fó-pá lâu chien sa chhut-hi.19 併於上節20 Mò chhèu lòi sêu, fó thin-chho̍k voi vû-thet;mò ngìn chhòn-fa, yên-kâ chhiu thìn-hâ-lòi.21 Hau yên-kâ ke chhiu voi san-thung fûn-chên,chhiu-chhiong fó-tôi thiâm-than, fó-song kâ-chhèu.22 Poi-heu ke hàn-fa chhiong sṳ̍t phông-phai;yit lo̍k-choi chhiu thûn-to chhòng-tú hi.23 Choi-thiàm sa̍t-va̍t chúng-he sîm-kôn sià-ok,chhiu-chhiong chhû fì-è pâu yit-chhèn pha̍k-ngiùn.24 Thi̍t-ngìn kóng fung-sṳ̀n ngìn ke fa,chúng-he sîm-tú khong-tén kiên-cha.25 Sâng-yîm sûi-yèn vûn-yù, m̀-hó siông-sin kì,yîn-vi kì ke sîm-kôn-tú chhûng-mân ok-thu̍k( 61 26:25 "chhûng-mân ok-thu̍k" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "yû chhit-hong khó-vu ke sṳ".).26 Sûi-yèn kì chhiên-fông pak-kie oi chiông kì ke yen-hen khong-hí-lòi,chúng-he kì ke sià-ok voi chhai chung-ngìn mien-chhièn hién-lu chhut-lòi.27 Yet ham-chiáng ke, chhṳ-kâ voi tiet lo̍k-hi;fân sa̍k-thèu ke, voi pûn sa̍k-thèu khám-tó.28 Hî-ká ke sa̍t-mà hoi kì só-nâu ke ngìn;ô-mì fung-sṳ̀n ke choi voi tai-lòi phai-fai.
