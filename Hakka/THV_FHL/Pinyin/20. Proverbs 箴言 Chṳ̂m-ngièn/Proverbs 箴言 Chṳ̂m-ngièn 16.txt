1 Mèu-sṳ chhai ngìn;sṳ̀n-sṳ chhai Thiên.( 34 16:1 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Sîm-chûng tá-son chhai-fû ngìn; khiéu ke yin-tui chhai-yì Sṳ̀n". Mò-lun "Thiên" ya-he "Sṳ̀n", Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Song-chú".)2 Ngìn láu-tó chhṳ-kâ só-cho ke chhiòn chho̍k;chúng-he Song-chú sṳ́m-chhat kì ke thung-kî.3 Ngì só-cho ke sṳ oi kâu-thok Song-chú;ngì só kie-va̍k ke thin-chho̍k voi sṳ̀n-kûng.4 Song-chú só-chho ke he oi tha̍t-tó kok-chúng ke muk-tit;sṳm-chṳ sià-ok ngìn me he vi fam-nan ke ngit chho ke.5 Song-chú tông-nâu só-yû kiêu-ngau ke ngìn;kì-têu chhie̍t-tui mò-fap sám-phit chit-fa̍t.6 Chûng-sṳ̀n sin-sṳ̍t, chhui tet sa-miên;kin-vi Song-chú, yén-lì fái-sṳ( 35 16:6 "yén-lì fái-sṳ" ya-he yi̍t-cho "miên-su châi-fo".).7 Hàng-vì na tet Song-chú fôn-hí,Chú sṳ́ sù-thi̍t sàng-cho phèn-yû.8 Kâm-ngien hàng kûng-ngi, sû-ngi̍p séu,yàng-ko hàng put-ngi, vàng-chhòi tô.9 Ngìn-sîm kie-va̍k chhṳ-kâ ke tho-lu;chúng-he Song-chú yîn-tai kiâ kiok-phu.10 Kiûn-vòng ke fa yû Sṳ̀n khí-sṳ;kì ke phan-ton m̀-voi chhâ-chho.11 Kûng-phìn ke chhṳn lâu pong su̍k-yì Song-chú;thoi-tú ke chhṳn-thò chhiòn-he Kì cho ke.12 Kiûn-vòng mò fap-thu ngiûn-su sià-ok ke sṳ( 36 16:12 "Kiûn-vòng ... ke sṳ" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Kiûn-vòng cho ok-sṳ he put-nèn ngiûn-su ke".),yîn-vi chṳn-khièn he kho chṳn-ngi cho kîn-kî.13 Kiûn-vòng fôn-hí thâng chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke fa;kì chhin-siak kóng sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t fa ke ngìn.14 Kiûn-vòng ke fun-nu chhiong sí-mòng ke sṳ́-chá;yû chṳ-fi ke voi phìn-sit vòng ke su-hi.15 Kiûn-vòng ke seu-yùng sṳ́ ngìn yû sâng-miang;kì ke sṳ̂-ên chhiong yùn tai-lòi chhûn-yí.16 Tet chṳ-fi yàng-ko tet vòng-kîm;sién chhûng-mìn yàng-ko sién pha̍k-ngiùn.17 Chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn ke tho-lu yén-lì sià-ok;kiún-sṳ́m hàng chṳn-lu nèn pó-chhiòn sâng-miang.18 Kiêu-ngau chhai phai-fai thèu-chhièn;chhṳ-thai chhai tiet-tó yî-chhièn.19 Chhùn-sîm khiâm-pî lâu khiùng-khú ngìn lòi-vông,yàng-ko lâu kiêu-ngau ngìn phiàng-pûn chhióng-lòi ke chhièn-chhòi.20 Thâng-chhiùng kau-hiun, pit-tet hó-chhu;sin-kho Song-chú, sṳ̍t-chhai yû-fuk.21 Sîm-chûng yû chṳ-fi, chhṳ̂n-cho thûng-tha̍t ngìn;kóng-fa ye̍t vùn-ngá, ye̍t sṳ́ ngìn sîm-fu̍k.22 Ngìn yû chṳ-fi chhiu yû sâng-miang ke chhièn-ngièn;ngì-ngong chhiu-he pûn ngong-tong ngìn ke chit-fa̍t.23 Chṳ-fi ngìn ke sîm sṳ́ kì ke choi thûng-tha̍t,yu pûn kì kóng ke fa kha sṳ́ ngìn sîm-fu̍k.24 Chhîn-chhiet ke ngî-ngièn, chhiong phûng-thòng khiung-yong,sṳ́ sîm-lìn kâm-thiàm, pûn sṳ̂n-thí khiòng-chong.25 Yû yit-thiàu lu khon-lòi chhin-chhṳ̍t;chúng-he chui-mî theu-to sí-mòng.26 Kûng-ngìn lò-luk vi khiéu-puk;khiéu-puk chhûi kì tô lò-luk.27 Lù-màn ngìn thù-mèu ok-sṳ;kóng-fa chhiong fó sêu-sông ngìn.28 Pân-nung sṳ-fî, chṳ-chho fûn-chên;poi-heu chung-sông, pho-fai yû-chhìn.29 Khiòng-phau ngìn mì-fe̍t lìn-sa,yín-yu kì hi hàng sià-lu.30 Muk-chû lit-lit-chón, sîm-su̍t m̀-hó;chhṳ̀m-thu̍k m̀ kóng-fa, sióng-cho fái-sṳ.31 Pha̍k thèu-nà-mô he kông-yùng ke miên-liù,kîn-ko kûng-ngi ke sên-fa̍t chang tet-tó.32 Mò sùi-phien fat-khién, yàng-ko yúng-sṳ;nèn kón-chṳ chhṳ-kâ, yàng-ko chhióng sàng.33 Chhû-chhiâm chhai ngìn;kiet-thin chhai Sṳ̀n( 37 16:33 "Sṳ̀n" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "Song-chú".).
