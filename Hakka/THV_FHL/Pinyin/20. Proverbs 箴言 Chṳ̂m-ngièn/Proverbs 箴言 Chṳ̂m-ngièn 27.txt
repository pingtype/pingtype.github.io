1 M̀-hó vi thiên-kông-ngit lòi khoâ-khiéu,yîn-vi yit-ngit voi fat-sên má-ke sṳ ngì tû m̀-tî.2 M̀-hó kóng chhṳ-kâ án-voi, oi tén phe̍t-ngìn khoâ-chióng ngì;m̀-hó chhṳ-khoâ, oi tén m̀ su̍k-sṳ ke ngìn chhṳ̂n-chan ngì.3 Sa̍k-thèu lâu sâ-é lióng-hong chhin-chhûng,chúng-he ngong-tong ngìn ngiâ-hí ke fam-nan hàn-kha chhûng.4 Fat-khién voi hoi-ngìn, pot-at voi sông-ngìn,chhit-tu na lòi-to, má-ngìn tong tet-he̍t?5 Kûng-khôi ke chit-phiyàng-ko khong-tén ke oi-sîm.6 Phèn-yû sông-hoi ngì he chhut-yì chûng-sṳ̀n;sù-thi̍t fán-chón lâu ngì lièn-lièn chîm-choi.7 Sṳ̍t-páu ke ngìn, lièn phûng-thòng kì me-mò sióng sṳ̍t;tú-kî ke ngìn, lièn fú ke ya kám-kok chhin-thiàm.8 Lì-kâ chhut-ngoi liù-long ke ngìn,chhiu-chhiong tiâu-é lì-teu yén-pî.9 Hiông-yù lâu hiông-liau pûn-ngìn ke sîm tông sóng-khoai;phèn-yû chṳ̂n-sîm ke khien-va ya he án kâm-thiàm( 62 27:9 "ya he án kâm-thiàm" ya-he yi̍t-cho "yàng-ko chhṳ-kâ ke chú-yi".).10 M̀-hó fit-thet ngì ke phèn-yû lâu ngiâ-pâ ke phèn-yû;tù-tó châi-nan ke sṳ̀ m̀-sṳ́ hiong chhîn-ngìn khiù pông-chhu,yîn-vi yén-chhîn put-yì khiun-lìn.11 Lai-é â, oi cho chṳ-fi ngìn lòi pûn ngài fôn-hí,pûn ngài yû-fa hó-en sâu-nat ngài ke ngìn.12 Chîn-mìn ke ngìn khon-tó ngùi-hiám chhiu sám-khôi;ngong-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn chhṳ̍t-thèu chhṳ̍t-lu su châi-hoi.13 Má-ngìn vi sâng-fun ngìn tâm-pó,ngì chhiu oi nâ kì( 63 27:13 "kì" chṳ́-to "Má-ngìn".) ke ngoi-sâm cho tí-ap;má-ngìn vi ngoi-chhu̍k ngìn( 64 27:13 "ngoi-chhu̍k ngìn" he kîn-kí yit-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "mò chṳn-tông ke fu-ńg".) tâm-pó,ngì chhiu oi ham kì sṳ̀n-tâm yit-chhiet heu-kó.14 Tá-chó hong-chhòng chhàu-siáng phèn-yû,thai-sâng vi kì chuk-fuk,tông-chhiong he chu-chú kì.15 Ngiàm-ngiàm ngiam-ngiam ke pû-ngiòng,lâu ti̍t-ti̍t ta̍p-ta̍p ke lo̍k-yí-thiên khiung-yong;16 sióng-oi kim-chṳ́ kì ke,chhiong oi làn-chú khòng-fûng,ya chhiong yu-sú kha̍k-yù.17 Phèn-yû fu-siông ngiên-kiu( 65 27:17 "ngiên-kiu" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "nò-mien".),chhiong thiet nò-thiet ye̍t-nò ye̍t-li.18 Cheu-ku vù-fâ-kó-su ke, yû vù-fâ-kó hó-sṳ̍t;fu̍k-sṳ chú-ngìn ke, ya nèn tet-tó chûn-yùng.19 Chhin-súi nèn cheu-mien, mien-mien siông-tui;ngìn-sîm tui ngìn-sîm, ya he án-ngiòng.( 66 27:19 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Súi-mien cheu-chhut ke he chhṳ-kâ ke mien, nui-sîm fán-yang ke he chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t ke ngì.")20 Yîm-kiên lâu me̍t-mòng ke chhṳ̂m-yên yit-chhṳ̍t thùn m̀-voi mân;ngìn ke yu̍k-mong ya khiung-yong yún-yén mò phan-fap mân-chiuk.21 Lù-fó thon-lien kîm-ngiùn,chhṳ̂n-chan kháu-ngiam ngìn-phín.22 Ngì chhiu-he chiông ngong-ngìn piong chhai chûng-khiû tú, lâu ma̍k-é khiung-ha chûng-sui,ngong-tong hàn-he lì m̀-khôi kì.23 Oi séu-sîm khon-hó ngì ke yòng-khiùn,oi se-ngi cheu-ku ngì ke thèu-sâng;24 yîn-vi chhièn-chhòi m̀-he yún-kiú nèn pó-chhùn,miên-liù ya m̀-voi chhùn-to sṳ-sṳ thoi-thoi.25 Châu-chhó kot-thet, nun-chhó chhut-hien,sân-piên yâ-chhó sû-si̍p ke sṳ̀,26 ngì cho-tet chién yòng-mô cho sâm-fu,yung mai sân-yòng ke chhièn mâi thú-thi;27 sân-yòng-nen voi la ngì yit-kâ ngìn sṳ̍t,yu hó vì-chhṳ̀ ngì sṳ́-ńg ke sên-fa̍t.
