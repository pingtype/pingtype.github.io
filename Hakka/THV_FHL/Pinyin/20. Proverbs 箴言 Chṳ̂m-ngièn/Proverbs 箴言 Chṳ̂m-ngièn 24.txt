1 2 (19) M̀-hó chhit-tu fái-ngìn,m̀-hó sióng-oi thùng kì-têu cho-phân;2 yîn-vi kì-têu ke sîm kie-mèu hiûng-phau ke sṳ,kì-têu só-kóng ke chhiòn he sông-hoi ngìn ke fa.3 2 (20) Kâ-thìn he yung chṳ-fi lòi kien-chho,he thûng-ko liáu-kié tet-tó vún-ku;4 yîn-vi yû chṳ-sṳt,vuk-tú ya chhûng-mân kok-chúng chṳ̂n-khì mî-hó ke pó-vu̍t.5 2 (21) Yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn thai-yû nèn-li̍t;yû chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn li̍t-song kâ-li̍t.( 53 24:5 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho: "Chṳ-fi yàng-ko khiòng-chong; chṳ-sṳt pí li̍t-liong kha chhung-yeu.")6 Siông-chhṳ̀ oi yû hó kie-mèu;chhâm-mèu tô chhiu nèn tá-yàng.7 2 (22) Chṳ-fi he chhêu-ko ngong-ngìn só-nèn liáu-kié ke;kì-têu chhai fi-ngi chûng( 54 24:7 "fi-ngi chûng": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "sàng-mùn héu".) m̀-kám khôi-choi kóng-fa.8 2 (23) Kie-mèu cho fái-sṳ ke,chhiu ôn-to yîm-mèu-kâ.9 Ngong-tong ngìn ke kie-mèu chhiu-he chhui-ok;ngau-man ngìn chhin tet-ngìn-nâu.10 2 (24) Ngì chhai fam-nan ke sṳ̀ na-he sṳt-chṳ mò-tám,ngì ke li̍t-liong chhiu chhin yû-han.11 2 (25) Ngìn vù-khû phan sí-hìn, ngì oi hi chhióng-kiu;kì pûn-ngìn lâi-hi oi chhṳ̀, ngì oi lâi chón-lòi.12 Ngì na-he kóng: "Liá sṳ-chhìn ngài-têu m̀-tî";kám-kóng ke kam-chhat ngìn-sîm ke Song-ti voi m̀-tî he-mò?Pó-fu ngì sâng-miang ke, Kì fî-sòng chhîn-chhú!Kì oi cheu tak-sà só-hàng ke lòi po-yin.13 2 (26) Lai-é â, oi sṳ̍t phûng-thòng, yîn-vi tui ngì yû hó-chhu;sṳ̍t phûng-teu tit-hâ ke phûng-thòng, ngì voi kám-kok kâm-thiàm.14 Ngì ya oi tî, chṳ-fi tui ngì ke sîm-lìn ya he án-ngiòng;ngì na chhìm-tó, chhièn-thù chhiu kông-mìn,ngì ke chiông-lòi ya m̀-voi chhie̍t-mong.15 2 (27) Ok-kun â, m̀-hó mài-fu̍k kûng-kit ngi-ngìn ke kâ;m̀-hó pho-fai kì ke vuk;16 yîn-vi ngi-ngìn sûi-yèn tiet-tó chhit-pái, kì hàn voi hong-hí-lòi;chúng-he, châi-fo yit-to, sià-ok ke ngìn chhiu voi vang-to tap-tap.17 2 (28) Khon-tó ngì ke sù-thi̍t tiet-tó, m̀-hó fôn-hí;kì kang-tó ke sṳ̀, ngì ke sîm m̀-hó khoai-lo̍k;18 kiâng-pha Song-ti khon-tó ngì ke fái-sîm chhiu mò fôn-hí,yèn-heu chiông Kì ke nu-hi tui sù-thi̍t sṳ̂n-hong sái-chéu.19 2 (29) M̀-hó yîn-vi fái-ngìn sên-fa̍t hó-se lòi pot-at;ya m̀-hó chhit-tu sià-ok ke ngìn;20 yîn-vi fái-ngìn mò chhièn-thù,sià-ok ke ngìn mò chṳ́-mong( 55 24:20 "sià-ok ke ngìn mò chṳ́-mong" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "sià-ok ngìn ke tên voi vû-thet".).21 2 (30) Lai-é â, oi kin-vi Song-chú, chûn-kin kiûn-vòng;ya m̀-hó thùng phoi-ngia̍k ke ngìn khiung-ha kiet-kâu,22 yîn-vi liá-têu ngìn yit-ha-é chhiu voi me̍t-mòng.Ngì kám m̀-tî Song-ti lâu kiûn-vòng voi kong châi-nan he-mò?23 Khì-thâ Chṳ-fi ke Fa Hâ-poi ya he chṳ-fi ngìn ke chṳ̂m-ngièn:Sṳ́m-phan ke sṳ̀ m̀-hó khon ngìn ke chhìn-mien.24 Má-ngìn tui fái-ngìn kóng: "Ngì mò-chhui",van-mìn voi chu-chú kì, lie̍t-koet voi kûng-kit kì.25 Chúng-he chit-fa̍t fam-chhui ke voi fàm-sṳ sun-li;kok-yong ke chuk-fuk ya voi lìm-to kì sṳ̂n-hong.26 Sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t ke fì-tap péu-sṳ chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t ke yû-chhìn( 56 24:26 "péu-sṳ chṳ̂n-sṳ̍t ke yû-chhìn" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "chhiong lâu ngìn chîm-choi".).27 Oi siên yû mèu-sên ke pá-vok,thièn-yèn ya oi chṳ́n-lî hó-se,chang lòi hí-vuk, sṳ̀n-kâ li̍p-ngia̍p.28 M̀-hó sùi-phien cho-chṳn lòi-hoi lìn-sa;ya m̀-hó yung-choi khî-phien ngìn.29 M̀-hó kóng: "Ngìn tui ngài án-ngiòng,ngài ya oi tui kì án-ngiòng!Ngài oi cheu kì só-hàng ke po-fu̍k kì!"30 Ngài lu-ko lân-sṳ̂ ngìn ke thièn-thi,mò sòng-sṳt ke ngìn ke phù-thò-yèn;31 khon net-è tông-tô,chha̍p-chhó mân-phien he,sa̍k-chhiòng ya pên-thet.32 Ngài kín-khon kín-sióng ke sṳ̀,chhiu tet-tó yit-ke kau-hiun:33 Ngì soi kâ yit-ha-é,tuk-kû hiû-sit yit-ha-é,nám-tén sú hiet-khun yit-ha-é;34 chúng-he phìn-khiùng voi fut-yèn-kiên chhiong khiòng-tho lòi chhióng ngì,khiet-fa̍t voi chhiong thú-fî lòi kûng-tá ngì.
