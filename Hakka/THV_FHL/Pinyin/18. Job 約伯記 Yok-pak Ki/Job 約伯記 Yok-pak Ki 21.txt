1 Yok-pak en-kóng:2 Chhiáng ngì-têu ngin-chṳ̂n thâng ngài ke fa;liá he ngì-têu cho-tet pûn ngài vì-yit ke ôn-ví.3 Pai-thok, chhiáng pûn ngài yit-ke kóng-fa ke kî-fi,ngài kóng-so̍t, ngì-têu chang lòi chhṳ́-seu ngài hàn m̀-voi man.4 Ngài só au ke pin m̀-he tui ngìn;só-yî ngài chang voi án chho̍k-kip â!5 Khon-tén ngài, ngì-têu kám m̀-voi pot chhṳ̂-kiâng?Ngì-têu kám m̀-he hak-to mò-fa kóng!6 Sióng-tó ngài só tù-tó ke yit-chhiet,ngài chhiu kiâng-to kûi-sṳ̂n pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat.7 Fái-ngìn cho-má-ke ki-siu̍k sâng hâ-hi?Cho-má-ke hióng chhòng-su, hîn-vong, fat-tha̍t?8 Kì-têu m̀ tân-chhiang chṳ́-sûn mân-thòng,hàn chhîn-muk khon-tén heu-thoi chóng-thai.9 Kì-têu ke vuk-hâ phìn-ôn, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng má-ke;Song-ti chit-fa̍t ke kun-é mò lìm-to kì-têu.10 Kì-têu ke ngiù-kú chhòn-chúng yit-thin sṳ̀n-kûng;kì-têu ke ngiù-mà kiung-chṳ́ m̀-voi lau-thôi.11 Kì-têu ke chṳ́-ńg chhut-hi liau, chhṳ-yù chhṳ-chhai chhiong yòng-khiùn;kì ke-têu se-ngìn-è fôn-hí to kín-chéu, kín-thiàu.12 Kì-têu fò-tén lìn-kú lâu khìm lòi chhong-kô;kì-têu thèn-tén phùn-tha̍k ke sâng lòi fôn-fû.13 Kì-têu yit-sên-ngìn fûng-fûng fu-fu,phìn-phìn ôn-ôn ngi̍p-to fùn-mu( 32 21:13 "fùn-mu" ngièn-vùn chhṳ̍t-yi̍t he "yîm-kiên".) hi.14 Fái-ngìn tui Song-ti kóng: "Yén-yén lì-khôi ngài-têu!Ngài-têu yit-sên-ngìn mò-oi chhìm Ngì ke chṳ́-yi.15 Chhiòn-nèn-chá he má-ngìn, yû má-ke hó kin-pai?Ngài-têu hiong Kì khì-tó yu yû má-ke hó-chhu?"16 Fái-ngìn khon chhṳ-kâ ke sṳ̀n-kûng he tá-piang lòi ke;chúng-he liá-chúng sióng-fap ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi chan-sṳ̀n.17 Kám-kóng fái-ngìn ke tên sṳt vû-thet mâ?Kám-kóng châi-nan sṳt lìm-to kì-têu mâ?Song-ti sṳt fat-khién chit-fa̍t fái-ngìn mâ?18 Kì kám sṳt chiông kì-têu chhôi-san, chhiong thai-fûng chhôi-san vò-kón?Kì kám sṳt chiông kì-têu kién-chéu, chhiong khòng-fûng kién-chéu lùng-hông?19 Ngì-têu kóng, yîn-vi fái-ngìn chit-ok, Song-ti thin-chho̍k voi chit-fa̍t kì-têu ke chṳ́-ńg;ngài ngin-vì, Song-ti oi chit-fa̍t chhui-ngìn pún-sṳ̂n,pûn kì-têu sṳ̀n-tâm chhṳ-kâ ke chhui-ko chang chho̍k.20 Chhui-ngìn yin-kôi sṳ̀n-su chhṳ-kâ oi su ke chit-fa̍t;pûn kì-têu chhîn-sṳ̂n thí-ngiam Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke fat-khién.21 Ngìn chhai sṳ-kiên ke ngièn-ngit voi kiet-suk ke sṳ̀,hàn nèn cheu-ku chhṳ-kâ chṳ́-ńg ke hen-fuk mâ?22 Lièn chhô thai-vi ke ngìn ya voi pûn Song-ti sṳ́m-phan,ngìn ke chṳ-sṳt ngióng yû khó-nèn lòi kâu Song-ti nè?23 Yû-têu ngìn yit-sên-ngìn yúng-khien,to sí hióng-su phìn-ôn hen-fuk.24 Kì-têu ke tú-sṳ́ koe̍t-koe̍t;kut-súi ya tet-tó chṳ̂-yun.25 Yû-têu ngìn to sí sîm-kôn thung-khú,yit-sên-ngìn m̀-sṳt hióng-su khoai-lo̍k.26 Chúng-he liá lióng-chúng ngìn khiung-yong voi sí, oi mài,sṳ̂n-sṳ̂ khiung-yong voi chhai nài-kak tú pûn chhùng sṳ̍t.27 Ngài tî ngì-têu sîm-tú só-sióng ke,tî ngì-têu ham-hoi ngài ke yîm-mèu.28 Ngì-têu mun: "Ke-chak pa-vòng he̍t chhai nai-vi?Ke-chak fái tûng-sî ke vuk-hâ chhai nai-vi?"29 Ngì-têu kám m̀-sṳt mun ko-lu ngìn?Ngì-têu kám m̀-tî kì-têu tai chón-lòi ke po-ko?30 Chhai Song-ti fat-khién chit-fa̍t ngìn ke ngit-è,fái-ngìn sòng-sòng sám-chéu, tiên-to mò sṳ-chhìn.31 Kám-yû má-ngìn tông-mien chit-phi fái-ngìn?Ya-he cheu kì só-cho ke po-fu̍k kì?32 Chhiu-he kì pûn-ngìn kông-to mu-thi,chong chhai yû-ngìn chóng ke fùn-mu sṳ̀,33 yû yit thai-chhṳn ke ngìn hi sung-chong,lièn mu-thi ke nài ya chhin châu-sóng.34 Ngì-têu ngióng hàn kóng khûng-fa lòi ôn-ví ngài!Ngì-têu kóng ke fa tak-ki tû he hâm-chhê!
