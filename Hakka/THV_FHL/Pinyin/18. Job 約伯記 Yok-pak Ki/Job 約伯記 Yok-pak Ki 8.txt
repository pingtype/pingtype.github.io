1 Chiap-tén, Sû-â-ngìn Pí-le̍t-tha̍t en-kóng:2 Liá-chúng fa ngì oi kóng-to kí-sṳ̀ nè?Ngì kóng ke chhiong fûng-chhâi án-ngiòng lon chhôi!3 Kám-kóng Song-ti voi tiên-to sṳ-fî mâ?Kám-kóng Chhiòn-nèn-chá voi vì-poi kûng-ngi?4 Ngì ke chṳ́-ńg yit-thin he tet-chhui Kì,só-yî Kì chhiu sṳ́ kì-têu su po-yin.5 Ngì yin-kôi mien-hiong Song-ti,pet-chhiet khién-khiù Chhiòn-nèn-chá.6 Na-he ngì án sùn-kiet chṳn-chhṳ̍t,Song-ti yit-thin voi pông-chhu ngì,fì-fu̍k ngì ke thièn-yèn kâ-sṳt.7 Ngì hí-chhû sûi-yèn mì-se,to-mî pit-thin thai hîn-vong.8 Chhiáng ngì chhà-khon kú-thoi ke chṳ-fi,ngiên-kiu ngài-têu chú-siên ke kîn-ngiam.9 Ngài-têu ke sâng-miang tông-tón, yu múng-múng túng-túng;ngit-è kín ko chhiong yáng-è, yit-ha chhiu mò-thet.10 Ngài-têu ke chú-siên kám m̀-voi chṳ́-kau ngì?Oi thâng kì-têu tui sîm-tú só-kóng ke fa.11 Phû-chhó mò nài kám voi sâng?Lù-chuk mò súi kám voi vong?12 Súi yit châu-thet, kì-têu chui-siên sí-thet;na-he hàn-nun, kot hâ-lòi ya mò-yung.13 Mò kin-khièn ke ngìn lâu phû-chhó khiung-yong;m̀ ki-tet Song-ti, chhiu mò hî-mong lé.14 Kì-têu sin-lai ke voi thôn ke sṳ̂,kì-têu yí-kho ke he tî-tû mióng.15 Kì-têu pen-tén mióng-è, chúng-he tî-tû mióng kám m̀-voi lan-thet?Kì-têu khiú-tén mióng-è, tî-tû mióng kám-nèn pûn kì-têu yí-sṳ?16 Fái-ngìn chhiong ngit-thèu hâ ke chha̍p-chhó,thèn-ya̍p chhin-chiâng, chhe-to mân-yèn.17 Kì ke kîn chhàn-tén ngàm-sa̍k,tâng-ngi̍p sa̍k-phung tú.18 Chúng-he, na-he chiông kì pâng chhut-lòi,mò ngìn tî kì sṳt chhai ke-vi sâng-kîn.19 Khon â, liá chhiu-he fái-ngìn chhai sṳ-kiên ke hí-lo̍k;yî-heu phe̍t-ngìn voi thoi kì-têu ke thi-vi.20 Chúng-he, Song-ti yún-yén m̀-voi fit-thet vàn-chhiòn ngìn,ya chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi pông-chhu fái-ngìn.21 Kì hàn-oi sṳ́ ngì khôi-sâng thai-seu;Kì hàn-oi sṳ́ ngì fôn-hí na-thiên.22 Kì voi sṳ́ hen ngì ke ngìn kien-seu;Kì voi pûn fái-ngìn ke kâ sêu-me̍t.
