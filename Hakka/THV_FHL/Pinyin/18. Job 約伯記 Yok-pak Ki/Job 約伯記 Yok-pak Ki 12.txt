1 Yok-pak en-kóng:2 Ngì-têu khok-sṳ̍t he chui yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn ô!Ngì-têu yit sí, chṳ-fi chhiu voi khiung-ha me̍t-thet!3 Chúng-he ngài lâu ngì-têu khiung-yong yû chhûng-mìn,ngài ke chṳ-fi pin m̀-voi kha sû ngì-têu.Ngì-têu só-kóng ke, yû má-ngìn m̀-tî nè?4 Liá-ha lièn ngài ke phèn-yû ya sâu-nat ngài;ngài chṳn-chhṳ̍t chhîn-pha̍k, kì-têu hàn sâu-nat ngài;chúng-he Song-ti ya sṳt thâng ngài ke khì-tó.5 Mò châi-fo ke ngìn khon-khiâng tù-tó châi-fo ke ngìn;liá-chúng ngìn sòng-sòng tá-kit ke voi tiet-tó ke ngìn.6 Khiòng-tho ke chong-phùng fán-chón vún-ku,ngiâ Song-ti fat-khién ke ngìn fán-chón phìn-ôn;kì-têu chiông pún-sṳ chún-cho sṳ̀n-mìn( 18 12:6 "kì-têu ... sṳ̀n-mìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "khì-sṳ̍t he Song-ti yù-chhai kì-têu".).7 Ngì hi chhiáng-kau chéu-chhu, kì-têu voi kâu ngì;ngì hi mun khûng-chûng ke fî-niâu, kì-têu voi lâu ngì kóng.8 Chhiu-he lâu thai-thi kóng, kì ya voi kâu ngì,hói-tú ke ǹg-è ya voi lâu ngì sot-mìn.9 He Song-chú ke sú chhóng-chho kì-têu,kì-têu tông-chûng kám yû m̀-tî ke?10 Sâng-vu̍t ke miang chhiòn-phu chhai Song-ti ke sú-chûng;ngìn ke théu-hi ya chhai Kì ke chóng-kón chṳ̂-chûng.11 Ngí-kûng oi fûn-phe̍t fa-ngî ke nui-yùng,chhiu-chhiong sa̍t-mà chhṳ sṳ̍t-vu̍t ke chṳ̂-mi.12 Ló-ngièn ngìn yû chṳ-fi,chúng-he Song-ti yû chṳ-fi yu yû nèn-li̍t.Ló-ngìn-kâ yû kien-sṳt,chúng-he Song-ti yû kien-sṳt yu yû sṳ̍t-hàng ke nèn-li̍t.13 併於上節14 Song-ti chhak-thet ke, má-ngìn yû phan-fap chhùng-kien?Kì koân-kim ke ngìn, má-ngìn yû phan-fap sṳt-fong?15 Song-ti m̀ lo̍k-yí, chhiu voi yû thiên-hôn;Kì piong-khôi súi-ngièn, thi chhiu cho thai-súi.16 Song-ti yû khièn-nèn, chhiùng-thèu to-mî yàng-ko yit-chhiet;phien-ngìn ke lâu pûn-ngìn phien ke, chhiòn chhai Kì sú-chûng.17 Kì chhióng-chéu mèu-sṳ ke chṳ-fi,yu sṳ́ sṳ́m-phan-kôn chhiong ngong-é.18 Kì fi-thet kiûn-vòng ke khièn-piang,yu sṳ́ kì-têu sàng-cho fam-ngìn.19 Kì chhióng-chéu chi-sṳ̂ ke chṳt-vu;Kì thûi-fân kha̍k thai-khièn ke ngìn.20 Kì sṳ́ sin-yung ke ngìn mò-fa kóng;Kì chhióng-chéu ló-ngìn-kâ ke chṳ-fi.21 Kì sṳ́ chûn-kui ke ngìn kien-seu,sṳ́ yû khièn-sṳ ke ngìn sôi-phai.22 Kì sṳ́ o-meu tui vû-am chûng hién-mìn chhut-lòi;Kì sṳ́ vû-am hién-chhut kông-mìn.23 Kì sṳ́ lie̍t-koet khiòng-thai, hîn-vong,yèn-heu yu tá-kit kì-têu, sêu-me̍t kì-têu.24 Kì sṳ́ ngìn-mìn ke liâng-chhiu ngì-ngong,pûn kì-têu mì-sṳt chhai fun-lon chûng;25 kì-têu chhiong sṳ̍t chiú-chui ke ngìn,chhai vû-am chûng phiên-lòi phiên-hi.
