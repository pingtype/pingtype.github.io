1 Ngìn chhai sṳ-kiên, chhiu chhiong cho-pîn pà chhûng-tâm,ngit-ngit ko chhiong kûng-ngìn lò-khú ke sên-fa̍t,2 chhiong nù-chhòi hot-mu ngit-thèu lo̍k-sân,chhiong chhiáng ke kûng-ngìn tén fat kûng-chhièn.3 Ngài chu-thin ko khun-khú ke ngit-ngie̍t;tak am-pû chṳ́-yû yù-sèu thung-khú.4 Ngài vang lo̍k-hi soi-muk, kám-kok am-pû-sṳ̀ ngióng-voi án-chhòng,fân ko-lòi fân ko-hi, yit-chhṳ̍t to thiên-kông soi m̀-lo̍k kau.5 Ngài kûi-sṳ̂n sâng-chhùng, kung-nùng;phì-ngiuk sû-héu yu lan-thet.6 Ngài ke ngit-è pí sô kha-kiak,chhiòn-phu chhai sṳt-mong chûng ko-thet.7 Song-ti â, khiù Ngì ki-tet,ngài ke sâng-miang chhiu chhûn-tó yit-héu hi;ngài m̀-voi chai khon-tó hen-fuk ke ngit-è.8 Ngì liá-ha khon-tén ngài, mò kí-kiú voi khon ngài m̀-tó;na-he sióng-oi chhìm ngài, ke-sṳ̀ ngài yí-kîn mò-chhôi lé.9 Chhiong yùn san-thet yit-yong,ngìn yit sí chhiu m̀-voi to-chón-lòi;10 kì m̀-voi chai chón-to kiâ vuk-hâ,ku-hiông ke ngìn ya m̀ ki-tet kì.11 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngài mò-oi chai tiâm-tiâm!Ngài oi kóng-chhut ngài sîm-lìn ke yù-sèu;ngài oi tho-than ngài nui-sîm ke thung-khú.12 Song-ti â, Ngì cho-má-ke kam-sṳ ngài?Kám-kóng Ngì chiông ngài chún-cho hói-koai mâ?13 Ngài sông mìn-chhòng sióng hiet-khun yit-ha,hî-mong voi kám-khiâng ngài ke thung-khú;14 Ngì sa yung ok-mung lòi sṳ́ ngài chho̍k-kiâng,yung tet-ngìn-kiâng ke yi-siong lòi-hak ngài.15 Só-yî, ngài kâm-ngien káng-sí,kha-yàng chhûn yit-sṳ̂n kut-thèu.16 Ngài tông sióng-oi sí, ngài ke sâng-miang mò pan-tiám sên-chhi;khiù Ngì mo̍k chhap ngài, ngài ke ngìn-sên vàn-chhiòn mò yi-ngi.17 Ngìn son má-ke, Ngì án khon-chhûng kì?Ngì ngióng voi chiông kì piong chhai sîm-song?18 Ngì tak chêu-sṳ̀n kam-chhat kì,sṳ̀-sṳ̀ khiet-khiet kháu-ngiam kì.19 Ngì oi to kí-sṳ̀ chang hén piong-ko ngài,pûn ngài thûn yit-ha héu-lân hó-mò?20 Kam-chhat ngìn ke Chú â,kám-kóng ngài ke chhui ngoi-tó Ngì he-mò?Cho-má-ke chiông ngài chún chien-pá lòi sa?Ngài sàng-cho Ngì án-thai ke chhûng-tâm mâ?( 11 7:20 "Ngài sàng-cho ... chhûng-tâm mâ?" he cheu yit-têu Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhâu-pún lâu kú yi̍t-pún, thai-phu-fun ke Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhâu-pún siá "sṳ́ ngài yam-hi chhṳ-kâ ke sâng-miang?")21 Ngì ngióng m̀ sa-miên ngài ke ko-sṳt?Ngì ngióng m̀ chhù-thet ngài ke chhui-ok?Ngài mò kí-kiú chhiu voi ngi̍p fùn-mu;Ngì chhìm ngài, ngài yí-kîn mò-chhôi lé.
