1 Chiap-tén, Sû-â-ngìn Pí-le̍t-tha̍t en-kóng:2 Song-ti chóng thai-khièn, van-mìn oi kin-vi Kì;Kì chhai thiên-táng kien-li̍p fò-phìn ke chṳt-si.3 Má-ngìn son tet-chhut Kì ke thiên-kiûn yû kí-tô?Yû má-ke só-chhai Kì ke kông cheu m̀-tó nè?4 Chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn má-ngìn he chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke nè?Tui fu-ńg kiung ke ngìn nai-yû chhiang-li ke nè?5 Chhai Song-ti khon-lòi, chhiu-son ngie̍t-kông ya mò-kông,sên-é ya mò-mìn;6 hò-khóng sṳ-kiên ngìn chhiong chhùng-è,chhai Song-ti khon-lòi son má-ke nè?
