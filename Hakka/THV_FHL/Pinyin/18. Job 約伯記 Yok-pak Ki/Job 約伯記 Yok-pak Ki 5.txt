1 Yok-pak â, cho ngì chhin-hêm, khon yû má-ngìn en ngì nè;ngì voi hiong nai-ke thiên-sṳ́ khiù pông-chhu nè?2 Pot-at voi hoi-sí ngong-tong ngìn;chhit-tu voi sṳ́ vù-tî ke ngìn mò-miang.3 Ngài khon-ko ngong-tong ngìn tá-piang oi ôn-kî lo̍k-ngia̍p,chúng-he châi-nan fut-yèn-kiên lìm-to kì ke vuk-hâ;4 kì ke chṳ́-ńg mò má-ke ôn-chhiòn,chhai fap-thìn pûn-ngìn khî-fu, mò-ngìn thoi kì-têu kóng-fa.5 Kì ke sû-sṳ̀n pûn tú-kî ke ngìn chhióng-hi sṳ̍t,lièn ke-têu net-teu tú ke ya chak-hi sṳ̍t chhiang-chhiang;kì ke chhòi-sán pûn kî-hot ke ngìn thûn-thet.6 Châi-fo m̀-voi tui nài-kak tú sâng chhut-lòi;khú-nan ya m̀-he tui nài-hâ pau chhut-lòi.7 Ngìn yit chhut-se chhiu voi yû khú-nan,chhiu-chhiong fó-fâ tui fó pî chhut-lòi.8 Na-he ngài, ngài oi chhìm-khiù Song-ti,hiong Kì sṳ̂n-su ngài ke mun-thì.9 Kì só-cho ke thai-sṳ ngài-têu m̀ liáu-kié;Kì khì-meu ke chok-vì son m̀-chhut án-tô.10 Kì lo̍k-yí chhai thi-mien;Kì sung-súi to thièn-tú.11 Song-ti sṳ́ pî-mì ke ngìn kô-sṳ̂n hí-lòi,chiông ôi-sông ke ngìn tai-to ôn-vún só-chhai.12 Kì pho-fái káu-koai ngìn ke kie-mèu,sṳ́ kì-têu sú só-cho ke m̀-voi sṳ̀n-kûng.13 Kì sṳ́ chhûng-mìn ngìn ham-ngi̍p chhṳ-kâ ke kúi-kie,Kì sṳ́ káu-va̍t ngìn ke yîm-mèu chhiòn-phu sṳt-phai.14 Kì-têu ngit-sṳ̀-thèu tù-tó vû-am,tông-chu-thèu chhiong am-pû-sṳ̀ miâ-lòi miâ-hi.15 Song-ti chṳ́n-kiu khiùng-khú ngìnthot-lì khiòng-phau ngìn ke thúng-chhṳ.16 Kì sṳ́ khiùng-khú ngìn yû phan-mong,pûn sià-ok ke ngìn mò fa kóng.17 Song-ti só kón-kau ke ngìn yû fuk-hi;Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke chit-phi m̀-hó khon-khiâng.18 Song-ti sông-tó ngìn, ya voi lâu ngìn pâu-hó;Kì tá-sông ngìn, Kì ke sú ya voi yî-hó.19 Kì sṳ̀-sṳ̀ khiet-khiet oi chṳ́n-kiu ngì;Kì voi yit-chhṳ̍t kiu ngì lì-khôi châi-nan.20 Kî-fông ke sṳ̀, Kì oi kiu ngì thot-lì sí-mòng;chan-chên ke sṳ̀, Kì voi kiu ngì lì-khôi tô-kiam.21 Kì voi pó-fu ngì, sṳ́ ngì m̀-voi su fí-pong,châi-fo lìm-to ke sṳ̀, ngì ya m̀-sṳ́ chho̍k-kiâng.22 Kî-fông, phau-lon chûng, ngì hàn-he mân-mien seu-yùng;yâ-chhu ke sông-hoi, ngì ya vàn-chhiòn m̀-sṳ́ kiâng.23 Ngì kâng-chung ke thièn-thi m̀-voi yû sa̍k-thèu;yâ-thi ke chhuk-sâng ya m̀-voi sông-hoi ngì.24 Ke-sṳ̀ ngì voi tî, he̍t chhai ngiâ chong-phùng tú yû phìn-ôn;ngì chhà-khon ngì ke yòng-khiùn, lièn yit-chak ya mò sṳt-thet.25 Ngì voi khon-tó ngiâ chṳ́-sûn hîn-vong,chhiong chhó-phiàng ke chhiâng-chhó án meu-sṳn.26 Ngì voi chhin chhòng-su mân-chiuk chang kûi-thú,chhiong ma̍k-é sṳ̀n-su̍k cheu-sṳ̀ hó sû-sṳ̀n.27 Yok-pak â, liá he ngài-têu só chhà-kháu ke chṳ̂n-lî;vi-tó ngì ke hó-chhu, ngì yin-kôi oi thâng.
