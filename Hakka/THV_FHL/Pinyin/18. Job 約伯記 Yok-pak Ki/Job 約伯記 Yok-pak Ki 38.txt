1 Song-chú Fì-tap Yok-pak Heu-lòi, Song-chú tui thai-fûng tông-chûng fì-tap Yok-pak kóng:2 Ngì he má-ngìn, kám fài-ngì Ngài ke chṳ́-yi?Ngì ngióng voi kóng án mò chṳ-sṳt ke fa â?3 Khî hí-lòi, chhiong yit-ke yúng-kám ke ngìn;Ngài mun ngì, ngì lòi chṳ́-kau Ngài.4 Ngài chhóng-chho sṳ-kie ke sṳ̀, ngì chhai nai-vi?Ngì na-he chṳn-kîn án chhûng-mìn, kóng khon-é!5 Ngì kám tî he má-sà thin kì ke thai-se?Má-sà chhai kì ke táng-kô lâi-sien chhet-liòng?6 Thai-thi ke kî-chhú( 53 38:6 "kî-chhú" khon 9:6.) ôn chhai nai-vi?Sṳ-kie ke kî-sa̍k má-sà piong ke?7 Ke-ngit thiên-kông-sên khiung-ha chhong-kô;thiên-táng chung sṳ̀n-chṳ́( 54 38:7 "thiên-táng chung sṳ̀n-chṳ́" khon 1:6.) thai-sâng fôn-fû.8 Thai-hói( 55 38:8 "Thai-hói" khon 26:12.) tui thi-nài-hâ pau chhut-lòi ke sṳ̀,má-sà koân súi-mùn, pûn kì thìn hâ-lòi nè?9 He Ngài yung yùn châ-koi thai-hói,chiông kì pâu chhai thai mùng-yên chûng.10 Ngài vi hói-súi thin-hó kie-han,pin ôn súi-mùn yu chhôn hí-lòi.11 Ngài lâu kì kóng: Ngì to liá-vi chhiu hó, cho-m̀-tet chhêu-ko;ngì hiûng-khòng ke thai-long yit-thin oi chhai liá-vi thìn hâ-lòi.12 Yok-pak â, chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu ke ngit-kông tui nai-vi chhut-lòi?Ngì yit-sên-ngìn tông-chûng kám sṳt tui kì ha-ko min-lin?13 Ngì kám yû phan-fap fûn-fu ngit-thèu cheu thai-thi,chiông khong chhai vû-am chûng ke fái-ngìn kiuk chhut-lòi?14 Ngit-thèu kín-cheu, thi-song ke sân-kông sân-lak pien-hìn,chhiong chho̍k chhut-lòi ke mù-è, chhiong chap-hó ke sâm-fu.15 Thai-kông yit-cheu, ke-têu fái-ngìn chhiu m̀-kám lon-lòi,kì-têu khià hí-lòi ke sú m̀-kám kûng-kit ngìn.16 Ngì sṳt lo̍k-hi ko hói-tái mò?Ngì sṳt chhai chhṳ̂m-yên tú hàng mò?17 Yû-ngìn chṳ́-sṳ ngì sí-mòng ke mùn mò?Ngì kám sṳt khon-ko yîm-kiên ke mùn mò?18 Ngì tî liá sṳ-kie yû kí-thai mò?Na-he ngì tî chhiu lâu Ngài kóng â!19 Ngì tî kông tui nai-vi lòi mò?Vû-am ke ngièn-thèu chhai nai-vi?20 Ngì voi tai kì-têu chón ngièn-vi hi mò?Ngì tî tai kì-têu chón-hi ke lu mò?21 Liá-têu sṳ ngì yin-tông tî, yîn-vi ngì án-ló lé;sṳ-kie su chhóng-chho ke sṳ̀, ngì yí-kîn chhut-se lé!22 Ngì kám sṳt ngi̍p-hi piong-siet ke chhông-khu,ya-he khon-ko pên-pho̍k ke chhông-khu mò?23 Ngài tún liá-têu,chún-phi chhai kong châi-nan lâu chan-chên ke ngit-è lòi yung.24 Kông he tui nai-vi san khôi-lòi?Tûng-fûng tui nai-vi chhôi ko-lòi?25 Má-sà vi thai-yí fûn súi-lu?Má-sà vi ngiap-lang khôi lu-sien?26 Má-sà lo̍k-súi chhai mò-ngìn he̍t ke só-chhai?Chhai mò-ngìn to ke fông-thi?27 Má-sà chṳ̂-yun thiên-hôn ke thi,sṳ́ thú-thi yu sâng-chhut chhiâng-chhó?28 Yí-súi yû â-pâ mò?Lu-súi he má-sà kiung ke nè?29 Má-sà he pên ke â-mê?Thiên-táng ke sông he má-ngìn kiung ke?30 Má-sà sṳ́ súi pien chhiong sa̍k-thèu án-ngang?Sṳ́ hói-mien kiet-pên?31 Ngì yû phan-fap chiông Mâu-sên thak khiung-ha mò?Ngì yû phan-fap kié-khôi Lia̍p-fu-sên ke tai-é mò?32 Ngì yû phan-fap cheu-sṳ̀ tai khiùn-sên mò?Ngì voi thu thai-se Pet-téu sên mò?33 Ngì tî thiên-thí yun-hàng ke kûi-chet mò?Ngì voi kiet-thin thai-thi ke fap-chet mò?34 Ngì yû phan-fap sṳ́ yùn thâng ngì fat-chhut ke min-lin mò?Ngì voi ham thai-yí chhiong tó-tén hâ-lòi ke súi mò?35 Na-he ngì hiong ngiap-lang fat-chhut min-lin,kì voi to ngì ke mien-chhièn en-kóng: "Ngài chhai liá-vi" mò?36 Má-sà pûn chû-lu niâu yû chṳ-fi kóng Nì-lò-hò kí-sṳ̀ voi cho thai-súi?Má-sà su chhûng-mìn pûn kiê-kûng thài kóng voi thiên-kông lé?( 56 38:36 Pún-chiet ya-he yi̍t-cho "Má-sà chiông chṳ-fi piong chhai fài-chûng? Má-sà chiông chhûng-mìn piong chhai sîm-nui?")37 Má-sà yû chṳ-fi hi-son yùn-thòn yû kí-lùi?Má-sà yû phan-fap sṳ́ yùn pien-yí tó-tén hâ-lòi?38 Má-sà yû phan-fap sṳ́ fôi-chhṳ̀n kiet-khi̍t?Má-sà yû phan-fap sṳ́ nài-fún kiet-thòn?39 Sṳ̂-é chhai lùng-tú khù-tén,ya-he chhai set-teu tú phu̍k-tén;ngì yû phan-fap vi sṳ̂-mà chok-tó lia̍p-vu̍t mò?Ngì yû phan-fap sṳ́ se sṳ̂-é sṳ̍t-páu mò?40 併於上節41 Vû-â ngo-to pî-lòi pî-hi,se vû-â hiong Ngài khiù sṳ̍t-vu̍t,ke-sṳ̀ má-sà hi vi-páu kì-têu nè?
