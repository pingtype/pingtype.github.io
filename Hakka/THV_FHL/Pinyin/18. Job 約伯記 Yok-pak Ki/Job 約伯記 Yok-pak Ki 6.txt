1 Yok-pak en-kóng:2 Ngien yû ngìn chiông ngài ke yù-sèu chhṳn khon-é,yû ngìn chiông ngài ke châi-nan piong chhai thiên-phìn;3 kì-têu pí hói-sùn ke sâ hàn-kha chhûng;só-yî, m̀-hó koai ngài kóng-fa chhû-siu̍k.4 Chhiòn-nèn ke Song-ti yung chien sa-tó ngài;chien-thèu ke thu̍k chhe-to ngài sṳ̂n-sîm-lìn.Song-ti yung kok-chúng tet-ngìn-kiâng ke châi-nan tá-kit ngài.5 Yâ-lì yû chhiâng-chhó chhiu mân-chiuk;ngiù yû chhó hó-sṳ̍t chhiu tiâm-tiâm.6 Sṳ̍t-vu̍t mò-hàm mò-mi ngióng sṳ̍t-tet?Lón-pha̍k kám-yû má-ke mi-si nè?7 Liá-chúng sṳ̍t-vu̍t ngài mò-oi sṳ̍t,yit-ha lo̍k-choi chhiu sióng-oi éu.8 Ngien Song-ti su-pûn ngài só khì-khiù ke,sóng-su ngài só phan-mong ke.9 Ngien Song-ti chiông ngài chak-mièn,chhut-sú chiông ngài chién-chhù!10 Án-ngiòng, ngài hàn-he voi tet-tó ôn-ví;khú-nan chhûng-to voi sí, ngài hàn voi fôn-hí,yîn-vi ngài chhiùng-lòi m̀-sṳt vì-poi sṳ̀n-sṳn Song-ti ke min-lin.11 Ngài nai-yû ngièn-hi kín tén-tén hâ-hi nè?Ngài m̀-tî kiet-khiu̍k, oi ngióng-pân ngiûn-nai nè?12 Kám-kóng ngài he sa̍k-thèu cho ke?Kám-kóng ngài ke sṳ̂n-thí he thùng tá ke?13 Ngài sṳ̍t-chhai mò phan-fap hó kiu chhṳ-kâ;ngài m̀-tî oi hi nai-vi khiù pông-chhu.14 Ke-têu mò yung chhṳ̀-oi tui-thai phèn-yû ke,chhiu he vok-thet tui Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke kin-vi( 10 6:14 "Ke-têu ... kin-vi" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Put-kón ngài he m̀-he vok-thet tui Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke kin-vi, chhai án-ngiòng ke fam-nan chûng ngài sî-yeu chûng-sîm ke phèn-yû.").15 Ngài ke hiûng-thi â, ngì-têu chhiong hâi-súi kho m̀-tet,yit-ha mò lo̍k-yí, súi chhiu châu-thet.16 Hòn-thiên lo̍k-siet ke sṳ̀,hâi-súi chhiu kiet-pên.17 Chhai ngie̍t-thiên sî-yeu súi ke sṳ̀,kì yu châu-thet.18 Kûi-chhui sên-lî ngìn chhìm súi-ngièn sa mì-lu,kì-têu hàng m̀-chho̍k lu chhiu sí chhai khóng-yâ.19 Kûi-chhui Thì-mâ ke sên-lî ngìn hi chhìm súi;Sṳ-pâ ke lî-hak kín-tén ya kín hî-mong.20 Kì-têu ke phan-mong pien-cho thung-khú;kì-têu chhìm m̀-tó súi chhiu sṳt-mong.21 Tui ngài lòi-kóng, ngì-têu chhiu-chhiong liá hâi-súi;khon-tó ngài ke chhìn-khóng, chhiu chho̍k-kiâng to-thui.22 Ngài kám sṳt hiong ngì-têu khiù má-ke lî-vu̍t,ya-he oi ngì-têu vi ngài hi sû-mâi ngìn?23 Ya-he yêu-khiù kiu ngài lì-khôi sù-thi̍t ke sú,ya-he su̍k ngài lì-khôi ke-têu khiòng-phau ke sú?24 Hó â, chhiáng ngì kâu ngài, ngài voi tiâm-tiâm thâng;sṳ́ ngài liáu-kié chhṳ-kâ nai-vi m̀-chho̍k.25 Kóng-fa kûng-tong chhiu voi sṳ́ ngìn sin-fu̍k;chúng-he ngì-têu to-tái chit-phi má-ke?26 Ngài chhie̍t-mong ke fa ngì-têu khon-cho chhiong fûng;cho-má-ke hàn sióng-oi chit-phi kói-chṳn ngài?27 Ngì-têu chhû-chhiâm, oi tet-tó kû-yì lòi cho nù-chhòi,sióng-oi chhon-chhièn, ngì-têu hàn-kám chhut-mai hó phèn-yû.28 Chhiáng ngì-têu mien-tui-mien lòi khon ngài,ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi kóng fâ-liâu.29 Ngì-têu oi fì-sîm chón-yi, m̀-hó án mò kûng-phìn;oi chai sióng khon-é, yîn-vi ngài chṳn-chhṳ̍t mò hî-ká.30 Ngài ke sa̍t-mà kám sṳt kóng fâ-liâu,ngài kám m̀-voi fûn-phe̍t sṳ-fî mâ?
