1 Chiap-tén, Nâ-mâ-ngìn Só-fap en-kóng:2 Liá yit thai-tôi ke hâm-chhê fa mò-ngìn en he-mò?Tô-choi ke ngìn kám chhiu chṳn-mìn kì yû-lî he-mò?3 Yok-pak â, ngì lon kóng-fa, mò-ngìn nèn fì-tap ngì he-mò?Ngì sâu-nat Song-ti ke sṳ̀, mò-ngìn kám chit-phi ngì he-mò?4 Ngì láu-tó chhṳ-kâ ke tho-lî chhiòn-phu chho̍k,ngì chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn he chhiang-chhiang li-li.5 Chúng-he, ngien Song-ti en ngì;ngien Kì khôi-choi kûng-kit ngì.6 Kì voi chṳ́-sṳ ngì chṳ-fi ke kok-chúng o-meu;yû-têu sṳ-chhìn thet-chhṳ̂m, ngìn mò fap-thu liáu-kié.Oi tî, Song-ti mò chhiòn-phu son ngì ke chhui-ko!7 Ngì yû phan-fap liáu-kié Song-ti ke o-meu mò?Ngì kám khon tet-theu Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke vù-han mò?8 Kì ke chṳ-fi( 17 11:8 "ke chṳ-fi" Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò liá kí-chak sṳ.) pí thiên hàn-kha kô, ngì miâ m̀-tó;pí yîm-kiên hàn-kha chhṳ̂m, só-yî ngì m̀-voi liáu-kié;9 pí thi hàn-kha chhòng,pí hói hàn-kha fat.10 Na-he Song-ti oi chok ngì hi-koân, sṳ́m-phan ngì,má-ngìn yû phan-fap chú-tóng Kì nè?11 Kì chhiu tî nai-têu ngìn hî-hî ká-ká;Kì khon chhîn-chhú kì-têu ke fái hàng-vì.12 Yâ lì-è kiung chhut-lòi chhiu voi sun-fu̍k mâ?Hâm-chhê ke ngìn ya mò fap-thu pien chhûng-mìn.13 Yok-pak â, ngì oi chhùn-sîm chṳn-chhṳ̍t;ngì oi khià-sú khì-khiù Song-ti.14 Oi chha̍t-tái chhù-thet ngì sú-hong ke sià-ok;m̀-hó pûn put-ngi liù chhai ngì ke vuk-hâ.15 Án-ngiòng, ngì voi khî-to chhai-chhai, m̀-kiâng má-ke;chhiu cho-tet kông-mìn chṳn-thai lòi mien-tui sṳ-kie.16 Ngì voi m̀ ki-tet ngì só-yû ke châi-fo,chhiu-son sióng-hí, ya voi chhiong súi liù-tén chéu.17 Ngì ke ngìn-sên voi pí tông-chu ke ngit-thèu kha-kông,chhiu-son yû vû-am, hàn voi chhiong chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu án-kông.18 Ngì voi hióng-su ôn-chhiòn, yîn-vi yû phan-mong;Song-ti oi pó-fu ngì, sṳ́ ngì ôn-sîm soi.19 Ngì soi hâ-hi, m̀-sṳ́ kiâng yû thi̍t-ngìn;chhin-tô ngìn voi lòi hiong ngì khiù pông-chhu.20 Chúng-he fái-ngìn muk-chû fâ-fâ,chhìm m̀-tó thò-chéu ke chhut-lu;kì-têu ke hî-mong chhiu-he sí.
