1 Yî-li-fu yu tui Yok-pak kóng:2 Ngì kín kóng chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn mò má-ke chhui;ngì láu-tó chhṳ-kâ án-ngiòng kóng yû-lî he-mò?3 Ngì mun Song-ti: "Ngài ke chhui lâu Ngì yû má-ke koân-he?Ngài na mò fam-chhui yû má-ke hó-chhu?"4 Ngài liá-ha oi fì-tap ngì,ya fì-tap ngì ke-têu phèn-yû.5 Ngì ngo-thèu khon thiên,khon ngì thèu-nà táng ke yùn yû kí-kô.6 Ngì na-he fam-chhui, tui Song-ti yû má-ke yáng-hióng?Chhiu-son ngì kín fam-chhui, kám-voi sông-hoi tó Kì mò?7 Chhiu-son ngì kûng-chṳn, tui Song-ti yû má-ke pông-chhu?Ngì cho ke hó-sṳ, kám tui Kì yû li-yit he-mò?8 Ngì ke chhui chṳ́-yû hoi-tó lâu ngì khiung-yong ke ngìn-lui su-khú;ngì ke hó hàng-vì ya chṳ́-yû pûn sṳ-kiên ngìn tet-tó li-yit.9 Ngìn su-tó ap-pet ke sṳ̀, chhiu kín ôi-khiù;kì-têu pûn-ngìn khî-fu, chhiu hi khiù pông-chhu.10 Chúng-he mò-ngìn kóng: "Chhóng-chho ngài ke Song-ti chhai nai-vi?Kì sṳ́ ngài chhai am-pû-sṳ̀ hàn voi chhong-kô.11 Song-ti kau-thô ngài-têu, su-pûn ngài-têu chṳ-fi,yén-yén chhêu-ko só-yû ke fî-niâu lâu chéu-chhu."12 Kì-têu ôi-khiù, chúng-he Song-ti mò en,yîn-vi kì liá-têu fái-ngìn chhin kiêu-ngau.13 Kì-têu hî-ká ke khiù-ham mò háu-kó,Song-ti m̀-thâng, Chhiòn-nèn-chá ya m̀-chhap.14 Yok-pak â, hò-khóng ngì kóng ngì khon m̀-tó Song-ti;ngì ke on-é pài chhai Kì mien-chhièn, oi nai-sîm tén.15 Ngì láu-tó Song-ti m̀-voi chit-fa̍t;ngì ngin-vì Kì m̀-voi chhap chhui-ok.16 Ngì kóng án-ngiòng ke fa mò-tap mò-toi,kóng án tô, m̀-tî chhṳ-kâ kóng má-ke.
