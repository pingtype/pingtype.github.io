1 Yî-li-fu ki-siu̍k kóng:2 Ngì-têu yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn chhiáng thâng ngài ke fa;ngì-têu yû chṳ-sṳt ke ngìn chu-yi thâng ngài kóng.3 Ngí-kûng oi fûn-phe̍t fa-ngî ke nui-yùng,chhiu-chhiong sa̍t-mà chhṳ sṳ̍t-vu̍t ke chṳ̂-mi.4 Sṳ-fî oi ên-têu chhṳ-kâ lòi phan-ton;san-ok ya oi ên chhṳ-kâ lòi kiet-thin.5 Yok-pak sṳt kóng: "Ngài mò-chhui,chúng-he Song-ti mò yung kûng-tong tui-thai ngài.6 Sûi-yèn ngài chṳn-chhṳ̍t yû-lî, sa son-cho kóng fâ-liâu!Ngài mò-chhui, chúng-he su-tó ke sông-hoi mò hó yî."7 Ngì-têu khon-ko chhiong Yok-pak liá-chúng ngìn mò?Kì sâu-nat Song-ti, chhiong sṳ̍t-súi án sùi-phien.8 Kì chhin fôn-hí lâu fái-ngìn lòi-vông;kì lâu cho fái-sṳ ke ngìn khiung-ha.9 Kì kóng: "Cho pûn Song-ti fôn-hí,liá kám-yû má-ke li-yit nè?"10 Ngì-têu mìn-lî ke ngìn, oi thâng ngài kóng:Song-ti chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi cho fái-sṳ!Chhiòn-nèn-chá chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi put-ngi!11 Kì cheu ngìn ke hàng-vì po-yin ngìn,cheu ngìn só yin-tet ke tui-thai ngìn.12 Chṳn-kîn, Song-ti chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi cho fái-sṳ;Chhiòn-nèn-chá chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi tiên-to sṳ-fî.13 Song-ti kám-yû tui má-ngìn liâng-su khièn-li̍t?Kám-yû má-ngìn sat-li̍p Kì kón-lî sṳ-kie?14 Na-he Song-ti kiet-sîm sû-chón sâng-miang,na-he Kì sû-fì hi-sit,15 só-yû hiet-hi ke chhiòn-phu voi sêu-me̍t,sṳ-kiên ngìn lûng-chúng voi pien-chón nài-fún.16 Ngì na-he mìn-lî, chhiáng thâng ngài kóng,chhiáng chu-yi thâng ngài só-kóng ke fa.17 Song-ti chóng-khièn, chhie̍t-tui mò khó-nèn yen-hen kûng-phìn!Kì yû kûng-ngi, khièn-nèn, ngì oi thin Kì yû-chhui mâ?18 Song-ti cho-tet tui kiûn-vòng kóng: Ngì mò ha-lo̍k!Kì ya cho-tet tui kui-chhu̍k kóng: Ngì he fái-ngìn!19 Kì m̀-voi tiên-to sṳ-fî, tui thúng-chhṳ-chá phiên-sîm,ya m̀-voi pâ-kiet yû-chhièn ngìn, méu-sṳ khiùng-khú ngìn;yîn-vi ngìn-lui chhiòn-phu he Kì chhîn-sú só chho ke.20 Ngìn khó-nèn chhai am-pû-thèu fut-yèn-kiên sí-thet.Song-ti yit kûng-kit, chhiu chiông kì-têu sêu me̍t-thet;Kì m̀-sṳ́ chhut-sú, chhiu chhù-thet chui-khiòng ke ngìn.21 Kì chu-yi ngìn só-hàng ke tho-lu;Kì kam-chhat tak-sà ngìn ke kiok-phu.22 Chhiu-son he vû-am lâu sí ke yîm-yáng,ya mò fap-thu chiông chhui-ngìn khong hí-lòi.23 Song-ti m̀-sṳ́ lâu ngìn thin ngit-è,ham ngìn to Kì mien-chhièn su sṳ́m-phan.24 Kì m̀-sṳ́ thiàu-chhà chhiu chhù-thet khiòng-ngìn,Kì sat-li̍p phe̍t-ngìn lòi thoi-lî kì-têu.25 Kì chó chhiu chu-yi kì-têu ke hàng-vì,yit am-pû chiông kì-têu thûi-fân sêu-me̍t.26 Kì chhai chung-ngìn mien-chhièn piên-tá kì-têu,he vi-tó kì-têu só cho ke fái-sṳ,27 yîn-vi kì-têu m̀ sun-chhiùng Kì,méu-sṳ Kì yit-chhiet ke min-lin.28 Khiùng-khú ngìn pûn kì-têu pet-to hi khiù Song-ti;Song-ti yû thâng-tó kì-têu khiù-ham ke ôi-sâng.29 Mò-lun he koet-kâ ya-he ngìn-mìn,Song-ti na-he tiâm-tiâm, má-ngìn cho-tet koai-yen Kì nè?Na-he Kì êm-mien m̀-chhap, má-ngìn nèn tet-tó pông-chhu?30 Kì sṳ́ mò kin-khièn ke ngìn mò fap-thu cho vòng,miên-tet ngìn-mìn su-tó châi-nan.31 Ngióng yû-ngìn tui Song-ti kóng:"Ngài yí-kîn su-tó chit-fa̍t,m̀-voi chai-ko fam-chhui lé.32 Ngài mò khon-tó ke, Ngì lâu ngài kóng â;chhiu-son ngài yû-chhui, m̀-voi chai fam lé."33 Ngì ki-yèn fán-tui Song-ti ke chok-vì,Kì kám oi cheu ngì ke yêu-khiù sṳ̂-hàng po-yin?Oi cho kiet-thin ke he ngì, m̀-he ngài;chiông ngì ke sióng-fap lâu ngài-têu kóng â!34 Só-yû mìn-lî ke ngìn voi thùng-yi ngài ke fa;só-yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn thâng-tó yit-thin voi kóng:35 "Yok-pak kóng-fa mò chṳ-sṳt;kì ke fa chhiòn-mò yi-ngi."36 Ngien Yok-pak chha̍t-tái su-tó sṳ-ngiam,yîn-vi kì fì-tap tông-chhiong fái-ngìn.37 Kì m̀ tân-chhiang fam-chhui, sṳ-heu hàn ki-siu̍k phoi-ngia̍k;kì chhai thai-kâ mien-chhièn kâ kóng-fa vú-yu̍k Song-ti.
