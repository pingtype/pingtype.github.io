1 Yî-li-fu ki-siu̍k kóng:2 Chhiáng ngì nai-sîm thâng ngài kié-sṳt;ngài oi vi Song-ti kóng kâ-têu.3 Ngài só tî ke fam-vì tông-fat, oi nâ-lòi yín-yung;ngài oi sot-mìn ngài ke Chhóng-chho Chú he kûng-chṳn ke.4 Ngài só-kóng ke fa chṳn-kîn m̀-he phùn kiê-kôi;chhai ngì mien-chhièn ke he thai yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn.5 Khon â! Song-ti thai yû nèn-li̍t,chúng-he Kì m̀-voi méu-sṳ ngìn;Kì ke sîm-sṳ̂ fî-sòng chîn-mìn.6 Kì mò-oi yù-chhai fái-ngìn sâng hâ-hi;Kì oi vi khiùng-khú ngìn sṳ̂n-chông chṳn-ngi.7 Kì khon-ku chṳn-chhṳ̍t ngìn, yu sat-li̍p kì-têu chhô thai-vi;chhiu-chhiong kiûn-vòng án-ngiòng, kì-têu yún-yén su-ngìn chûn-kin.8 Ngìn na-he pûn lien-é khún-tén,pûn khú-nan ke sok-è thak-tén,9 Song-ti oi chṳ́-chhut kì-têu só-fam ke chhui-ok,sṳ́ kì-têu tî chhṳ-kâ ke kiêu-ngau lâu chhṳ-thai.10 Kì sṳ́ kì-têu ke ngí-kûng thâng-tó kín-ko,fûn-fu kì-têu lì-khôi sià-ok ke tho-lu.11 Na-he kì-têu sun-fu̍k Song-ti, sṳ-fung Kì,kì-têu chhiu nèn hióng-su phìn-ôn ke ngit-è,kì-têu ke ngìn-sên ya voi mân-yû hen-fuk.12 Na mò sun-fu̍k, kì-têu voi mò-chṳ mò-sṳt,yèn-heu chhiu kîn-ko sí-hò, chin-ngi̍p yîm-kiên.13 M̀ kin-vi Song-ti ke ngìn su-tó Song-ti chit-fa̍t;kì-têu kín-sióng kín-khién, chúng-he m̀ khiù Kì pông-chhu.14 Kì-têu chhin heu-sâng chhiu sí-thet,sâng-miang chhai vû-ve( 48 36:14 "vû-ve" ngièn-vùn chṳ́ meu-tú ke nàm-chhông.) chûng sóng-thet.15 Chúng-he, Song-ti yung khú-nan lòi kiu khú-nan chûng ke ngìn,yung châi-nan sṳ́ ngìn ke ngí-kûng thâng tet-ngi̍p Kì ke fa.16 Song-ti tui châi-nan chûng yîn-tai ngì chhut-lòi,sṳ́ ngì chin-ngi̍p tông fat tông phìn-ôn ke thi,sṳ́ ngì chok-si̍t pài mân-mân, sṳ̍p-fûn phông-phai.17 Liá-ha ngì tù-tó fái-ngìn ke po-yin;ngì yí-kîn su-tó kûng-ngi ke sṳ́m-phan.18 Oi se-ngi, m̀-hó pûn ngìn ke chhòi-pó yín-yu;ya m̀-hó pûn-ngìn thiap heu-sú su-tó koái-phien( 49 36:18 "Oi se-ngi ... koái-phien" ya-he yi̍t-cho "M̀-hó yîn-vi tông-at chhiu vú-man Song-ti; m̀-hó yîn-vi tông-pot chhiu hàng phiên chhâ-lu".).19 Ngì ke chhòi-fu( 50 36:19 "chhòi-fu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "khiù-ham".) tui châi-nan yû má-ke yung?Ngì só-yû ke sṳ-li̍t kám-voi pông-chhu ngì?20 M̀-sṳ́ phan-mong ke am-pû lòi-to;m̀-sṳ́ kín-tén chung-ngìn voi sêu-me̍t.21 Se-ngi ô, m̀-hó hiong sià-ok hàng-tén hi;ngì ke khú-nan kín-ko ngì oi lì-khôi kì.22 Oi ki-tet Song-ti ke khièn-li̍t yû kí-thai;kám-yû chhiong Kì án ví-thai ke chṳ́-thô chá?23 Má-ngìn cho-tet chṳ́-thin Kì oi cho má-ke?Má-ngìn nèn chit-phi Kì kóng: "Ngì cho m̀-chho̍k"?24 Kì só-cho ke yún-yén su-tó siung-chan;ngì ya m̀-hó m̀ ki-tet chan-mî Kì.25 Van-mìn khon-tó Kì ke só-cho;sṳ-ngìn tui tông-yén lòi chhà-khon( 51 36:25 "sṳ-ngìn ... chhà-khon" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chúng-he mò-ngìn vàn-chhiòn liáu-kié".).26 Song-ti yû kí ví-thai, ngài-têu mò fap-thu liáu-kié;ngài-têu ya son m̀-chhut Kì to-tái yû kí-tô se.27 Song-ti tui thi-song khi̍p-sû súi-hi,chiông kì pien-cho yùn-tú ke yí-súi.28 Yí-tiám tui yùn lo̍k-hâ,chhiu chṳ̂-yun chhiòn ngìn-lui.29 Má-ngìn mìn-pha̍k Song-ti ngióng-pân san-pu yùn-chhái?Ngióng-pân tui Kì he̍t ke hàng-kiûng fat-chhut lùi-sâng?30 Kì yung ngiap-lang cheu thiên-khûng,yung hói-súi châ-koi hói-tái.31 Kì chhiu-he án-ngiòng chhṳ-lî van-mìn,yu su kì-têu fûng-fu ke sṳ̍t-vu̍t.32 Kì chhîn-sú chóng-kón ngiap-lang,min-lin kì hi-tá muk-phêu.33 Kì tá lùi-kûng péu-sṳ voi lo̍k thai-yí,lièn thèu-sâng me chhîn-chhú tî oi sám-khôi.
