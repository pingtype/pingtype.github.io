1 Ngì nèn yung ǹg kiêu-é chiông hói-koai( 61 41:1 "hói-koai" ya-he yi̍t-cho "thai ngo̍k-ǹg", 41:9, 12 khiung-yong. Chhâm-kháu 3:8.) lâi hí-lòi,ya-he yung sok-è hi thak kì ke sa̍t-mà mò?2 Ngì yû phan-fap yung sok-è khien kì ke phi-khûng,ya-he yung kiêu-é chhôn-ko kì ke hâ-ngâm mò?3 Kì voi kín-khiù ngì piong kì chéu,ya-he khiù ngì lìn-mén kì mò?4 Kì kám voi lâu ngì li̍p-yok,hén yún-yén fu̍k-sṳ ngì mò?5 Ngì kám voi chiông kì chún tiâu-é káu mò?Ngì nèn chiông kì thò-tén, pûn ngì ke se-â-moi-é káu mò?6 Tá-ǹg ke nèn chiông kì chún fo-vu̍t lòi lun-ka mò?Sên-lî ngìn kám nèn chiông kì pho-khôi lòi mai mò?7 Ngì kám nèn yung chok-ǹg ke chhòng-chhiông chhiuk kì ke phì,ya-he yung ǹg-chhâ chhiuk-chhôn kì ke thèu-nà mò?8 Ngì sióng-oi chiông kì khim-tén, chai yit-pái chhiu m̀-kám lé;ngì yún-yén m̀-voi m̀ ki-tet lâu kì piang he ngióng-pân tet-ngìn-kiâng.9 Khon-ko hói-koai ke ngìn chhiòn-phu hak-tó;mò ngìn kám chṳ-fu̍k kì.10 Kì yit su-tó chhiuk-kit, chhiu ok khià-khià;má-ngìn kám khî chhai kì ke mien-chhièn nè?11 Chhiu-chhiong án-ngiòng, thiên-ha van-vu̍t lûng-chúng he Ngài ke â!Má-ngìn lâu Ngài chok-tui, hàn-oi ham Ngài phòi kì nè?12 Kóng-tó hói-koai, Ngài m̀-kóng ngì kám voi chhîn-chhú?Ngì kám tî kì kí yû-li̍t, he ngióng-pân khiòng-thai?13 Má-ngìn yû phan-fap pok kì ke ngoi-sâm,ya-he chhiuk-chhôn kì só-chok ke thiet-kap( 62 41:13 "thiet-kap" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "mâ-hàm-thiet".)?14 Má-ngìn yû phan-fap ham kì choi tá khôi-lòi?Kì ke ngà-chhṳ́ si chû-vì chhin tet-ngìn-kiâng.15 Kì ke poi-nòng( 63 41:15 "poi-nòng" he kîn-kí yû-têu kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "khoâ-yeu" ya-he "kiêu-ngau".) yû yit-phài yit-phài ke lìn-kap,hèn-hèn ngiàm khiung-ha, chhiong sa̍k-thèu án-ngang.16 Tak-phién lìn-kap siông-lièn khiung-ha,lièn khûng-hi tû theu m̀ lo̍k-hi.17 Kì-têu fu-siông ngiàm-to tông-he̍t,mò má-ke nèn chiông kì-têu pak-khôi.18 Kì yit-ha tá ha̍p-chhiú chhiu voi fat-chhut kông;muk-chû chhiong thiên-mùng-kông ke ngit-thèu án-kîm.19 Kì ke choi voi phùn-fó,yû fó-fâ pî chhut-lòi.20 Kì ke phi-khûng ya voi phùn-yên chhut-lòi,chhiong vo̍k-thèu hâ sêu lù-chuk-chhó yîn-hí ke yên.21 Kì phùn ke hi sṳ́ fó chho̍k hí-lòi;fó chhiu tui kì ke choi phun chhut-lòi.22 Kì ke kiáng-kîn tông-ngang yu fî-sòng yû-li̍t;tù-tó kì ke chhiòn-phu kiâng-to thiau hí-lòi.23 Kì ke ngoi-hok tông kiet-sṳ̍t,ngang-to mò ngìn pâng-tet thîn-thûng.24 Kì ke sîm ngang-to chhiong sa̍k-thèu,chhiong mo-sa̍k án-ngang.25 Kì yit hong-hí-lòi, lièn yúng-sṳ( 64 41:25 "yúng-sṳ" ya-he yi̍t-cho "sṳ̀n-mìn".) ya chho̍k-kiâng,mò-kat mò-sat, tá-lón-thui.26 Tô-kiam mò fap-thu sông-hoi kì;chhiông lâu chien tui kì ya mò-háu.27 Tui kì lòi-kóng, thiet chhiong châu-chhó,thùng ya chhiong mut-thet ke su-é.28 Kiûng-chien mò fap-thu chiông kì kiuk-chéu;nâ sa̍k-thèu te̍p kì, chhiong vò-kón tá-tó chhiòn mò ngai-tó.29 Chhai kì khon-lòi, kun-é chhiu-chhiong vò-kón;kì seu pî ko-lòi ke chhiông, lièn khon tû m̀-khon.30 Kì ke tú-sṳ́ phì chhiong tông-chiâm ke ngá-sak;kì-têu chhiong thiet-phà án-ngiòng kín-phà thi-nài.31 Kì luk thai-hói, súi chhiu kín-kún,chhiong vo̍k-thèu tú ke yù chhin-kún.32 Kì yit chhiù-ko, súi-mien chhiu-chhiong voi fat-kông;kì sṳ́ hói-súi liù-hâ pha̍k-set ke súi-phô.33 Thi-song mò khì-thâ thung-vu̍t nèn lâu kì pí;kì má-ke tû m̀-kiâng.34 Lièn chui kô-thai ke thung-vu̍t kì ya khon-khiâng;kì he só-yû yâ-chhu ke vòng.
