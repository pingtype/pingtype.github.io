1 Heu-sâng-ngìn Yî-li-fu Kóng ke Fa( 32:1-37:24 ) Yîn-vi Yok-pak kiên-chhṳ̀ chhṳ-kâ mò-chhui, liá sâm-ke ngìn chhiu mò chai en kì.2 Chúng-he yû yit-ke ngìn chhai sùn-thèu, kì ôn-to Yî-li-fu (Yî-li-fu he Pu-sî heu-thoi Làn-chhu̍k ngìn Pâ-lâ-kâ ke lai-é); yîn-vi Yok-pak ngin-vì chhṳ-kâ mò-chhui yu koai-yen Song-ti, kì ngiûn m̀-he̍t chhiu hiong Yok-pak fat-khién.3 Kì ya hiong Yok-pak ke sâm-ke phèn-yû fat-khién, ngin-vì kì-têu m̀-tî oi ngióng-pân fì-tap Yok-pak, ngang-kóng Yok-pak m̀-chho̍k( 47 32:3 "ngang-kóng Yok-pak m̀-chho̍k" yû-têu Hî-pak-lòi-vùn kú chhâu-pún ya khó-yî yi̍t-cho "fán-chón sa pien-cho Song-ti mò-lî".).4 Yîn-vi Yî-li-fu pí kì-têu heu-sâng, m̀-kám chhióng-tén lâu Yok-pak kóng-fa;5 khon-tó kì-têu sâm-sà mò fa hó en Yok-pak, chhiu tông-at.6 Só-yî, Yî-li-fu chhiu kóng:Ngài chui heu-sâng, ngì-têu he chóng-pi;pún-lòi ngài m̀-kám kóng ngâ yi-kien.7 Ngài sióng, ngài yin-kôi oi ngiong chóng-pi kóng-fa,pûn ngièn-ló ke chhòn-su kì-têu ke chṳ-fi.8 Khì-sṳ̍t, chṳ́-yû chhiòn-nèn Song-ti ke Lìnchang nèn chiông chṳ-fi su-pûn sṳ-kiên ngìn.9 Ngièn-ki thai mò yit-thin chhiu kha yû chṳ-fi;kha tô-se ya mò yit-thin mìn-pha̍k sṳ-fî.10 Só-yî, ngài chhiáng ngì-têu thâng ngài kóng;ngài ya oi kóng ngài ke yi-kien.11 Ngì-têu ke thàm-lun ngài chhiòn-phu ngin-chṳ̂n thâng,kín tén ngì-têu voi kóng-chhut mìn-chṳ ke fa.12 Ngài chu-yi thâng ngì-têu ke fa,fat-hien ngì-têu chhiòn-phu sṳt-phai;lièn yit-sà tû au Yok-pak m̀-yàng.13 Ngì-têu ngióng-pân cho-tet kóng, yí-kîn chhìm-tó chṳ-fi nè?Song-ti chang yû phan-fap sot-fu̍k Yok-pak, ngìn cho m̀-tó.14 Yok-pak m̀-he hiong ngài chên-lun;ngài me m̀-voi chhiong ngì-têu án-ngiòng en kì.15 Yok-pak â, kì-têu tui ngì mò fa hó-en;kì-têu mò phan-fap pûn ngì má-ke tap-on.16 Kì-têu khî chhai ke-vi, yí-kîn mò má-ke fa hó-kóng lé;kì-têu tiâm-tiâm m̀ kóng-fa, ngài hàn-oi tén-tén hâ-hi mâ?17 Cho-m̀-tet, ngài liá-ha oi fì-tap ngì;ngài oi chiông ngài ke sióng-fap lâu ngì kóng.18 Ngài yû tông-tô fa yit-thin oi kóng,ngài ke sîm pet ngài oi kóng chhut-lòi.19 Na-he ngài m̀ chhìm kî-fi chiông fa kóng chhut-lòi,ngài voi chhiong chông-mân sîn-chiú ke phì-thoi pau-thet.20 Ngài ngiûn m̀-he̍t oi kóng-fa;ngài yit-thin oi piang chhut-lòi.21 Ngài chhai ngì-têu ke phien-lun chûng,m̀-voi phiên-sîm, pâ-kiet má-ngìn.22 Ngài m̀-tî oi ngióng-pân fung-sṳ̀n ngìn;na-he ngài án-ngiòng cho, Chhóng-chho Chú yit-thin voi ngiàm-sṳ̀ chit-fa̍t ngài.
