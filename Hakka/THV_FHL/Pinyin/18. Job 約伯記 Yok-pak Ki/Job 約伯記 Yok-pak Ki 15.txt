1 Thi-ngi Pái Chên-lun( 15:1-21:34 ) Chiap-tén, Thì-man-ngìn Yî-li-fap en Yok-pak kóng:2 Chhûng-mìn ngìn voi kóng khûng-hî ke fa mò?Kì kám voi kín kóng phong-fûng ke fa mò?3 Kì chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi chhiong ngì kóng mò li-yit ke fa,m̀-voi kóng mò ka-chhṳ̍t ke fa vi chhṳ-kâ phien-lun.4 Chhiong ngì án-ngiòng, oi ngióng-pân kin-vi Song-ti?Ngìn nai yû khó-nèn chhai Kì mien-chhièn khì-tó?5 Ngì só-kóng ke fa hién-chhut ngì ke sià-ok;ngì sióng yung kúi-cha ke fa pó-fu chhṳ-kâ.6 Ngióng oi-sṳ́ ngài lòi chṳ́-chhut ngì ke m̀-chho̍k?Ngì ke fa yí-kîn thin ngì chhṳ-kâ yû-chhui.7 Ngì láu-tó chhṳ-kâ he sṳ-kiên thi-yit ke chhut-se ke ngìn?Song-ti chhóng-chho kô-sân ke sṳ̀ ngì yí-kîn chhai-chhòng he-mò?8 Kám-kóng ngì thâng-ko Song-ti ke kie-va̍k mâ?Ngìn-lui ke chṳ-fi oi ngì chang yû he-mò?9 Yû má-ke sṳ ngì tî, chúng-he ngài-têu m̀-tî?Yû má-ke sṳ ngì mìn-pha̍k, ngài-têu m̀ mìn-pha̍k?10 Ngài-têu he lâu thèu-nà-mô pha̍k ke ló-ngìn-kâ ho̍k ke;kì-têu pí ngiâ-pâ hàn-kha ló nè!11 Ngì ngióng-pân oi khî-chhie̍t Song-ti ke ôn-ví nè?Ngì khon-khiâng ngài-têu vûn-fò ke khien-va he-mò?12 Ngì ke sîm cho-má-ke mò phìn-ôn?Ngì ke muk-chû cho-má-ke fat-fó?13 Ngì án-ngiòng he hiong Song-ti fat-khién nè!Ngì ngióng cho-tet tui Kì kóng ke-chúng fa?14 Sṳ-kiên ngìn he má-ke, kám yû-ngìn chṳn-kîn chhiang-li?Fu-ngìn-kâ só kiung ke, kám yû-ngìn khó-nèn mò-chhui?15 Song-ti lièn ke-têu thiên-sṳ́ ya m̀ sin-ngim;chhai Kì khon-lòi, thiên me-mò chhiang-li.16 Hò-khóng fam-chhui chhiong lîm-súi ke sṳ-kiên ngìn;ngìn ngióng-pân tet-ngìn-nâu, ngióng-pân phai-fái â!17 Yok-pak â, ngì oi thâng ngài ke khien-va;ngài oi chiông ngài ke kîn-ngiam lâu ngì kóng.18 Liá-têu he chú-siên chhòn hâ-lòi ke chṳ̂n-lî,ke-têu chṳ-fi ngìn chhiòn mò pó-liù kâu ngài.19 Kì-têu ke thú-thi mò pûn ngoi-chhu̍k thúng-chhṳ;ya mò ngoi-chhu̍k ngìn lâu kì-têu he̍t khiung-ha.20 Fái-ngìn yit-sên ke ngit-è su-chhin thung-khú,khiòng-phau ngìn yit-sên ke ngièn-ngit ya khiung-yong.21 Tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sâng sòng-sòng chhai kiâ ngí-kûng tú,kì láu-tó ôn-chhiòn ke sṳ̀, khiòng-tho chhiu lòi-to.22 Kì m̀ sin cho-tet tui vû-am thò-chéu,yîn-vi yû tô-kiam tén-tén oi chhṳ̀ kì,23 ên-tiâu tén-tén oi sṳ̍t kì ke sṳ̂-thí( 22 15:23 "ên-tiâu ... sṳ̂-thí" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn khó-yî yi̍t-cho "kì liù-long khiù sṳ̍t, kóng: Nai-vi yû sṳ̍t-vu̍t nè?".).Kì tî thèu-chhièn chṳ́-yû vû-am ke ngit-è;24 châi-nan, khun-khú chhiong yit-ke khiòng-thai ke vòng,tén-tén oi lòi hak kì, oi kûng-tá kì.25 Yîn-vi kì fán-khong Song-ti,kiêu-ngau méu-sṳ Chhiòn-nèn-chá.26 Kì ngang-kiáng m̀-hén sun-fu̍k,khià tún-phài kûng-kit Song-ti.27 Kì ke mien-phì yù chi-chi;kì ke yêu-khiên tú yê-yê.28 Kì chhiong yit-ke ngìn chhióng-tó sàng-sṳ,cham-liâng mò ngìn he̍t ke vuk-è,heu-lòi ke chan-chên hàn-he voi tá-fái liá-têu sàng-sṳ lâu vuk-è.29 Kì fûng-fu ke chhòi-sán liù m̀-voi kiú,lièn sán-ngia̍p ke yáng-è ya voi mò-thet.30 Kì mò fap-thu tui vû-am thò-chéu;kì oi chhiong su-kî pûn fó sêu-thet;kì ke fâ( 23 15:30 "fâ" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "choi".) pûn fûng chhôi m̀ kien-thet.31 Kì na-he yí-sṳ hî-ká khî-phien chhṳ-kâ,chiông-lòi hî-ká voi sàng-cho kì ke po-yin.32 Ngit-khì màng to, kì voi siên châu-thet( 24 15:32 "voi siên châu-thet" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "voi chhûng-chiuk".),chhiong su-kî châu-thet, m̀-voi chhiâng-liu̍k.33 Kì voi chhiong phù-thò-su, phù-thò màng-siuk chhiu lo̍k-lia̍p,yu chhiong kám-lám-su, yit khôi-fâ ngiàm-sṳ̀ chhiu chhia-thet.34 Mò kin-vi Song-ti ke ngìn chiông-voi mò chṳ́-sûn;kho vâi-kô lòi hí ke vuk voi pûn-fó sêu-thet.35 Liá-têu ngìn sat kie-mèu, yu cho fái-sṳ;sîm-kôn-tú só yi-phi ke he kúi-cha.
