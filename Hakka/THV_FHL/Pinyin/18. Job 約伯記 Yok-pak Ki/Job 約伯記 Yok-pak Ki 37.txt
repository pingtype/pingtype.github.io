1 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, ngài ke sîm po̍k-po̍k thiàu,khiòng-khiòng voi thiàu lì-khôi kì ke ngièn-vi.2 Thâng â, ngì-têu chhiòn-phu oi thâng Song-ti ke sâng,thâng Kì choi-tú só fat-chhut lung-lung-kún ke sâng.3 Kì chhâi ngiap-lang chhôn-ko thiên-khûng,tui thiên liá-phién ngiap-to ke-phién.4 Sùi-heu, ngìn thâng-tó kì lung-lung-kún ke sâng —lùi-kûng vî-ngiàm ke hióng-sâng,thùng-sṳ̀ ngiap-lang ya kín ngiap mò-thìn.5 Song-ti fat-chhut khì-meu ke lùi-sâng;Kì cho ke thai-sṳ, ngài-têu mò-fap lî-kié.6 Kì min-lin siet lo̍k chhai thi-song,ya sṳ́ thai-súi lo̍k-to mò-thìn.7 Kì thìn-chṳ́ ngìn ke kûng-chok,sṳ́ ngìn tî Kì ke só-cho.8 Yâ-chhu chéu-ngi̍p lùng tî-poi,khong chhai kì-têu ke khûng-tú.9 Fûng-chhâi tui nàm-fông chhut,hòn-liù tui pet-fông lòi.10 Song-ti phùn-hi, súi chhiu kiet-tung,yit thai-phién ke súi chhiu kiet-cho pên.11 Kì sṳ́ yùn chhûng-mân súi-hi,sṳ́ ngiap-lang tui yùn fat-chhut.12 Kì-têu thèn-tén Song-ti ke chṳ́-fî yù-hàng chón-thung,chhai chhiòn sṳ-kie chûn-hàng Song-ti yit-chhiet ke min-lin.13 Song-ti lo̍k-súi chṳ̂-yun thi-mien,hién-mìn Kì tui ngìn ke chhṳ̀-oi;Kì ya lo̍k thai-súi chit-fa̍t ngìn.14 Yok-pak â, chu-yi thâng ô!Chhiáng ôn-chhin yit-ha-é, sṳ̂-kháu Song-ti khì-meu ke só-cho.15 Ngì kám tî Song-ti ngióng-pân ha min-lin,ngióng-pân sṳ́ ngiap-lang tui yùn-chûng fat-sa?16 Ngì kám tî mân-yû chṳ-fi Song-ti khì-meu ke só-cho,ngióng-pân sṳ́ yùn phèu chhai thiên-khûng chûng mâ?17 Ngì kám tî nàm-fûng chhôi-lòi, thai-thi chhiu sêu-nôn,liá-sṳ̀ ngì chok ke kí-liâng sâm voi ngie̍t hí-lòi?18 Ngì voi hia̍p-chhu Song-ti phû-khôi kiûng-chhông,sṳ́ kì chhiu-chhiong thùng-kiang án-ngang he-mò?19 Chhiáng ngì kâu ngài-têu lâu Song-ti oi kóng má-ke;ngài-têu ngong-ngong tong-tong, m̀-tî oi ngióng-pân kóng.20 Ngài m̀-kám yêu-khiù lâu Song-ti kóng-fa;kám yû-ngìn sióng-oi chhṳ-kâ chhìm sí-lu?21 Sṳ-sṳ̍t song, fûng yit-chhôi, yùn chhiu san,thiên-táng kông to fî-sòng án chhiuk-muk;mò-ngìn nèn ngo-thèu hi khon ke kông.22 Pet-fông yû kîm-set ke thai-kông chhut-hien;Song-ti ke vî-ngiàm sṳ́ ngài-têu kin-vi.23 Chhiòn-nèn-chá thai-yû nèn-li̍t, ngài-têu mò fap-thu chiap-khiun;chúng-he Kì yû kûng-ngi lâu chṳn-chhṳ̍t, m̀-voi khî-fu ngìn.24 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, thai-kâ lûng-chúng kin-vi Kì;chúng-he Kì m̀-tap chhṳ-chok chhûng-mìn ke ngìn( 52 37:24 "chúng-he ... ke ngìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chúng-he lièn yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn ya khon m̀-tó Kì" ya-he "yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn chhiòn-phu ngióng-mong Kì" ya-he "Kì kám m̀-voi khon-ku yû chṳ-fi ke ngìn?".).
