1 Siung-chan Chṳ-fi( 44 28:1 Hî-pak-lòi-vùn mò chu-mìn siá chhai 28 chông yû má-ngìn tui má-ngìn kóng-fa. Yû-têu ho̍k-chá ngin-vì liá-chông he Yok-pak chhṳ-kâ só sṳ̂-kháu ke fa.) Ngiùn yû sên-sán ke khóng-chhòng;kîm yû thì-lien ke só-chhai.2 Ngìn tui thi-nài-hâ yet-chhut thiet,ya tui sa̍k-thèu tú thì-lien thùng.3 Ngìn chéu-to chui-am ke só-chhai hi chhái-khóng,kì to thi-chhèn chhin chhṳ̂m ke só-chhai hi chhìm,oi tui vû-am chûng chiông khóng-sa̍k yet chhut-lòi.4 Kì chhiu chhai mò-ngìn he̍t ke thi-fông khôi-khóng;chhai hán-tet yû-ngìn to ke só-chhai kûng-chok,chhai khóng-hâng tî-poi tiau-tén yèu-lòi yèu-hi.5 Thi-mien chhut-sán liòng-sṳ̍t,thi tái-hâ sa ngie̍t-to chhiong fó-lù.6 Sa̍k-thèu tú khong-tén làm-pó-sa̍k,thi-nài-hâ yû kîm-sâ.7 Ên-tiâu m̀-tî ngi̍p khóng-chhòng ke lu;yeu-phò ke muk-chû ya mò khon-ko.8 Hiûng-ok ke yâ-chhu m̀-sṳt tha̍p-ko,hiûng-mâng ke sṳ̂-é ya m̀-sṳt kîn-ko.9 Ngìn chho̍k-khôi chhin-ngang ke sa̍k-thèu,chiông kûi-chak sân pién chón-lòi.10 Kì chhai ngàm-sa̍k tông-chûng yet sân-lùng,fat-hien lùng-tú tak-yong ke pó-sa̍k.11 Kì chiông súi ngièn-thèu tù-tén,sṳ́ pó-chhòng hién-lu chhut-lòi.12 Chúng-he, oi hi nai-vi chhìm chṳ-fi nè?Hi nai-vi chang ho̍k tet-tó chhûng-mìn nè?13 Chṳ-fi ke ka-chhṳ̍t mò-ngìn tî,chhai sṳ-kiên mò-ngìn chhìm tet-tó.14 Chhṳ̂m-yên kóng: Chṳ-fi mò chhai ngài liá-vi;thai-hói kóng: Chṳ-fi mò lâu ngài khiung-ha.15 Kîm-é mâi m̀-tó chṳ-fi;kha-tô ke ngiùn-è ya von m̀-tó.16 Sùn-kîm lâu kui-chhung ke chû-pó,khiung-yong ya lâu kì pí m̀-tet.17 Vòng-kîm lâu pó-ngiu̍k mò phan-fap lâu kì pí;sùn-kîm ke hi-mén ya von m̀-tó.18 Chṳ-fi ke ka-chhṳ̍t yàng-ko sân-fù lâu súi-chîn;yû chṳ-fi yén-yén chhêu-ko tet-tó fùng-pó-sa̍k.19 Kú-sṳ̍t ke vòng-ngiu̍k lâu kì ke ka-chhṳ̍t pí m̀-tet;chui-sùn ke kîm-é ya mò fap-thu lâu kì pí-ka.20 Án-ngiòng, chṳ-fi ke ngièn-thèu chhai nai-vi?Hi nai-vi chang ho̍k tet-tó chhûng-mìn nè?21 Thung-vu̍t ke muk-chû khon m̀-tó kì;khûng-chûng ke fî-niâu ya khon m̀-tó.22 Chhiu-son fí-me̍t lâu sí-mòng ya sṳ̀n-ngin,chṳ́-yû thâng-ko kì ke fûng-sâng thin-thin.23 Tân-tân Song-ti tî ke-thiàu lu,tî oi hi nai-vi chhìm chṳ-fi;24 yîn-vi Kì ke muk-chû khon tet-tó thi-khi̍t,Kì chhà-khon phû-thiên-ha ke van-sṳ van-vu̍t.25 Song-ti su vî-li̍t pûn fûng,vi thai-hói kiet-thin súi-liong,26 kûi-thin lo̍k-yí ke sṳ̀-chiet,thin hióng lùi-kûng ke lu-sien,27 ke-sṳ̀ Kì khon-tó chṳ-fi, chhet-liòng kì,kiên-thin kì, yu kam-chhat kì ke ka-chhṳ̍t.28 Song-ti hiong sṳ-kiên ngìn kóng:"Kin-vi Song-chú chhiu-he chṳ-fi;lì-khôi sià-ok chhiu-he chhûng-mìn."
