1 Chiap-tén, Sû-â-ngìn Pí-le̍t-tha̍t en Yok-pak kóng:2 Ngì séu kóng-têu fa hó-mò?Ngì ôn-chhin hâ-lòi sióng, ngài-têu chang yû phan-fap lâu ngì kóng-fa.3 Ngì láu-tó ngài-têu chhiong thèu-sâng-é?Ngài-têu chhiong khìm-chhu án ngong he-mò?4 Ngì ke fat-khién sông-hoi ngì chhṳ-kâ thin-thin;kám-kóng yîn-vi ngì fat-khién thi voi fông-thet?Song-ti yin-tông yì-sân lòi mân-chiuk ngì mâ?5 Fái-ngìn ke kông yit-thin voi vû-thet;kì ke fó chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi chai chho̍k.6 Kì chong-phùng tú ke tên thin-chho̍k pien-am;koa chhai táng-kô ke tên ya voi vû-thet.7 Kì vún-thin ke kiok-phu khôi-sṳ́ yèu-yèu pái-pái;kì pûn chhṳ-kâ ke kúi-kie pan-tó.8 Kì chhṳ-kâ ham-ngi̍p lò-mióng tú;kì ke kiok pûn mióng-è chhàn-tén.9 Lò-mióng thak-tó kì ke kiok-châng;kî-koân chiông kì kia̍p-to hèn-hèn.10 Yû ham-chiáng mài chhai thi-nài-hâ;kì hàng ke lu phû-tén lò-mióng.11 Khiúng-pu tui chû-vì kûng-kit kì,phu-phu pet-tén kì ke kiok-châng.12 Kì yî-chhièn hó-ngiak, liá-ha khiòng-khiòng voi ngo-tó;châi-fo yit-chhṳ̍t chhai kì ke sṳ̂n-piên tén-tén kì.13 Chṳ-miang ke phiang-thung chhàn-sṳ̂n;kì ke sú-kiok mièn-mièn lok-lok.14 Kì tui ôn-kî ke chong-phùng tú pûn-ngìn thô chhut-lòi,yèn-heu pûn-ngìn tai-to yîm-kiên ke vòng ke-vi hi.15 Kì ke chong-phùng yung liù-vòng( 27 18:15 "liù-vòng": Kú-thoi ke sṳ-kie chiông liù-vòng cho sêu-thu̍k, yung-lòi kiet-chhiang piong-ko sṳ̂-thí ke fòng-kiên.) sêu-thu̍k,yèn-heu chhiu kâu-pûn phe̍t-ngìn hi he̍t.16 Kì hâ-poi ke su-kîn khû-châu;kì táng-kô ke kî-ya̍p châu-thet.17 Thi-song mò ngìn voi ki-tet kì;thùng-pâu m̀ ki-tet kì ke miàng.18 Kì oi tui sṳ-kiên phî kiuk chhut-hi,tui kông-mìn chûng phî kiuk-ngi̍p vû-am.19 Kì chhai pún-chhu̍k chûng mò chṳ́ mò sûn;kì he̍t ke só-chhai mò liù pan-sà.20 Sî-fông ke ngìn thâng-tó kì ke miang-yun kám-kok chho̍k-kiâng;tûng-fông ke ngìn tî kì só tù-tó ke chhiu hak-tó.21 Liá chhiu-he fái-ngìn ke kiet-khiu̍k;m̀ kin-vi Song-ti ke ngìn chui-mî chhiu-he án-ngiòng.
