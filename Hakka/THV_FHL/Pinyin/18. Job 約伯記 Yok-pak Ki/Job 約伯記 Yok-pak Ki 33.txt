1 Yok-pak â, liá-ha chhiáng thâng ngài ke fa;chu-yi thâng ngài só-oi kóng ke yit-chhiet.2 Ngài liá-ha khôi-choi,yung sa̍t-mà kóng-fa.3 Ngài oi kóng-chhut chṳ̂n-sîm fa;ngài kóng ke he sṳ̀n-sṳ̍t fa.4 Ngài he Song-ti ke Lìn chhóng-chho ke;Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke hi pûn ngài sâng-miang.5 Ngì na-he yû fa oi kóng,cho ngì thì chhut-lòi fán-pok.6 Ngài chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn lâu ngì khiung-yong;ngài me he yung nài chho ke.7 Só-yî ngì m̀-sṳ́ kiâng ngài;ngài m̀-voi pûn ngì ap-li̍t.8 Ngì kóng ke liá-têu fa,ngài chhîn-ngí thâng-tó lé.9 Ngì kóng: "Ngài mò-chhui, mò cho má-ke m̀-chho̍k;ngài chhîn-pha̍k, mò fam má-ke chhui-ngiak.10 Chúng-he, Song-ti chhìm kî-fi tá-kit ngài,chiông ngài khon-cho sù-thi̍t.11 Kì yung thiet-lien só-tén ngài ke sûng-kiok;Kì chu-yi khon ngài só-yû ke hàng-tha̍p."12 Chúng-he Yok-pak â, ngài lâu ngì kóng, ngì m̀-chho̍k lé;mò-lun má-ke ngìn, Song-ti pí kì hàn-kha ví-thai.13 Ngì cho-má-ke koai-yen Song-ti,kóng Kì m̀ en ngì ke sṳ̂n-su?14 Song-ti yit-chhṳ̍t yung kok-chúng fông-fap kóng-fa,chúng-he mò-ngìn chu-yi thâng Kì kóng má-ke.15 Am-pû-thèu ngìn tông hó-soi ke sṳ̀-chiet,Song-ti thok mung chhai yi-siong chûng kóng-fa.16 Kì tá-khôi ngìn ke ngí-kûng,yung kín-ko lòi hak kì-têu.17 Song-ti kóng-fa, ham ngìn lì-khôi chhui-ok,sṳ́ kì-têu m̀-voi chhṳ-kô chhṳ-thai.18 Kì m̀-ngien khon-tó kì-têu lo̍k yîm-kiên;Kì oi chṳ́n-kiu kì-têu thot-lì sí-mòng.19 Song-ti yung phiang-thung lòi chit-fa̍t ngìn ke ko-sṳt,pûn ngìn phiang-to soi chhai mìn-chhòng, lòi kón-kau kì.20 Pot-phiang ke ngìn má-ke tû m̀-sióng sṳ̍t,lièn chhin-hó sṳ̍t ke ya chhiòn mò vi-khiéu.21 Kì chheu kia̍p-kia̍p, mò pan-tiám ngiuk,kiâ kut-thèu chhiòn-phu thu̍t chhut-lòi.22 Kì ke lìn-fùn chiap-khiun yîm-fú;kì ke sâng-miang lì sí mò-yén.23 Song-ti ke chhiên-van thiên-sṳ́ tông-chûng,fe̍t-chá yû yit-vi chhòn-fa ke,lòi chṳ́-sṳ ngìn yin-kôi chhin ke pún-fun.24 Thiên-sṳ́ lìn-mén kì, kóng: "Piong kì chéu!Kì m̀-sṳ́ lo̍k-hi sí ke chhṳ̂m-hâng;ngài yû-chhièn su̍k-chón kì ke chhṳ-yù."25 Kì ke sṳ̂n-thí voi fî-fu̍k to chhiong se-ngìn-è;kì voi pien-to chhiong heu-sâng ke sṳ̀ án yúng-khien.26 Kì khì-tó, Song-ti chhiu voi fôn-hí en kì;kì oi yung khoai-lo̍k ke sîm kin-pai Song-ti;Song-ti oi lâu kì fî-fu̍k hó ke koân-he.27 Kì voi kûng-khôi sṳ̀n-ngin:"Ngài yû fam-chhui, tiên-to sṳ-fî;chúng-he Song-ti pin-mò cheu ngài ke chhui lòi chit-fa̍t ngài.28 Kì kiu ngài, sṳ́ ngài m̀-sṳ́ lo̍k sí ke chhṳ̂m-hâng;sṳ́ ngài ke sâng-miang hàn cho-tet khon-tó kông-mìn."29 Song-ti yit-pái yu yit-pái,vi ngìn cho liá yit-chhiet sṳ,30 oi kiu ngìn lì-khôi sí ke chhṳ̂m-hâng,sṳ́ kì hióng-su sâng-miang ke kông-mìn.31 Liá-ha, Yok-pak â, ngì oi thâng ngài ke fa,ngì oi tiâm-tiâm, hó-hó thâng ngài kóng-fa.32 Ngì na-he yû fa oi kóng, chhiu cho-tet en ngài;ngì chhin-liong kóng, ngài ngien-yi chiông ngì tông-cho yû-lî ke.33 Na-he ngì m̀-kóng, chhiu tiâm-tiâm thâng;ngài oi chiông chṳ-fi kau-thô ngì.
