1 Thi-sâm Pái Chên-lun( 22:1-27:23 ) Chiap-tén, Thì-man-ngìn Yî-li-fap en Yok-pak kóng:2 Ngìn tui Song-ti kám-yû má-ke lu-yung nè?Chui chhûng-mìn ke tui Song-ti yû hó-chhu mò?3 Ngì chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke hàng-vì voi pûn Chhiòn-nèn-chá fôn-hí mò?Ngì ke liòng-san kám voi pûn Song-ti tet-tó li-yit mâ?4 Song-ti kám voi yîn-vi ngì kin-vi Kìlòi chit-fa̍t ngì, sṳ́m-phan ngì mâ?5 M̀-voi! Kì chit-fa̍t ngì he yîn-vi ngì ke chhui chhin-chhûng,ngì ke chhui-ok tô-tôn.6 Ngì vi-tó oi hiong hiûng-thi thó-chai,pet kì chiông sâm-fu thot-to kông-kông.7 Choi-châu ke ngìn ngì m̀ pûn kì súi lîm;tú-sṳ́ yâu ke ngìn ngì m̀ pûn kì sṳ̍t.8 Ngì li-yung ngì ke sṳ-li̍t lâu thi-vicham-liâng só-yû ke thú-thi.9 Ngì ham koá-fu khûng-sú chón-hi;ngì tá-thôn kû-yì ke sú-kut.10 Yîn-vi án-ngiòng, liá-ha ngì chû-vì yû ham-chiáng;tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sṳ voi fut-yèn-kiên pâu-vì ngì.11 Vû-am châ-tén ngì, sṳ́ ngì khon m̀-tó;thai-súi voi chim-thet ngì!12 Song-ti kám m̀-he chhai kô-thiên ke táng-kô?Kì tâi-thèu hâ-lòi khon chui-kô ke sên-é.13 Chúng-he ngì thai-tám mun: "Song-ti tî má-ke nè?Kì kám voi chhôn-ko yùn-chhèn lòi sṳ́m-phan ngài-têu?"14 Ngì láu-tó yû vû-yùn châ-tén Kì, sṳ́ Kì khon m̀-tó;ngì ngin-vì Kì chhai kiûng-chhông táng-kô hàng-lòi hàng-hi thin-thin.15 Fái-ngìn hàng-ko ke ló lu,ngì oi thèn-tén hàng he-mò?16 Kì-têu ke sí-khì hàn-màng to,chhiu pûn thai-súi lièn-kîn tá-chéu.17 Kì-têu tui Song-ti kóng: "Ngì sám-khôi!Chhiòn-nèn-chá nèn nâ ngài-têu ngióng-pân?"18 Chúng-he, sṳ́ kì-têu hîn-vong ke he Song-ti;fái-ngìn ke sióng-fap, ngài mò fap-thu chan-sṳ̀n.19 Ngi-ngìn khon-tó kì-têu ke kiet-khiu̍k chhiu fôn-hí;vù-kû ke ngìn yit khon-tó chhiu chhṳ́-seu kì-têu.20 Fái-ngìn ke chhòi-sán chhiòn-phu sêu-me̍t thet-hi,chhûn-tó ke yit-chhiet ya pûn fó-sêu chhiang-chhiang.21 Só-yî, Yok-pak â, ngì oi sun-chhiùng Song-ti, lâu Kì fò-hó,án-ngiòng, Kì chhiu voi su-fuk pûn ngì.22 Ngì oi chiap-su Kì ke kau-hiun,chiông Kì ke fa ki chhai sîm-tú.23 Ngì na-he chón-to Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke-vi,chhù-thet ngì vuk-hâ só-yû put-ngi ke hàng-vì,Song-ti chhiu voi sṳ́ ngì hîn-vong.24 Oi chiông ngì ke vòng-kîm vok chhai nam-nài tú,chiông chîn-kîm fit-to châu-thet ke hò-pa tái.25 Chhiòn-nèn-chá chhiu voi cho ngì kui-chhung ke vòng-kîm,ya voi chhiong pha̍k-ngiùn kô-kô vi ngì tún hí-lòi.26 Án-ngiòng, ngì chhiu voi ngióng-mong Chhiòn-nèn-chá,thùng-sṳ̀ voi tui Song-ti tet-tó hí-lo̍k.27 Ngì hiong Kì khì-tó, Kì voi thâng ngì ke khì-khiù;ngì ya oi vàn ngì só-hí ke ngien.28 Ngì só-cho ke kûng-chok chhiòn-phu voi sṳ̀n-kûng;ngì só-hàng ke lu yit-thin kông-mìn chhan-lan.29 Song-ti tá-vang kiêu-ngau ke ngìn,Kì oi chṳ́n-kiu khiâm-pî ke ngìn.30 Ngì mò chhui( 33 22:30 "mò chhui" he kîn-kí yit kú yi̍t-pún, Hî-pak-lòi-vùn he "yû chhui".), Kì chhiu kiu ngì;ngì cho ngìn chṳn-chhṳ̍t, chhiu voi tet-tó chṳ́n-kiu.( 34 22:29-30 Liá lióng chiet ya cho-tet fân-yi̍t: "Ngìn na vang hâ-hi, ngì hiong Song-ti kóng: ‘Oi chiông kì-têu kô-sṳ̂n!’ Song-ti chhiu voi chṳ́n-kiu ke-têu khiâm-pî ke ngìn. 30 Ngìn na yû-chhui, Song-ti hàn oi kiu kì; yîn-vi ngì cho-ngìn chṳn-chhṳ̍t, kì yit-thin voi tet-kiu" (chhâm-kháu 42:7-9).)
