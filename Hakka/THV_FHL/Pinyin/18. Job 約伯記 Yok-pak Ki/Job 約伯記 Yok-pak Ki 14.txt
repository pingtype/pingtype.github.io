1 Ngài-têu chhut-se chhiu-he ngiôn-ngio̍k ke,ko-tén tón-chhiam, khú-nan ke sên-fa̍t.2 Ngài-têu chhiong fâ-khôi án-ngiòng, chhin-kiak chhiu chhia-thet;yu chhiong yáng-è án-ngiòng, yit-ha-é chhiu chéu-thet.3 Song-ti â, chhiong ngài án-ngiòng ke ngìn,Ngì hàn hén pak-muk khon ngài yit-ha,tai ngài ngi̍p fap-yen su sṳ́m-phan mò?4 Ô-chô ngìn kám voi chhut chhiang-li ke tûng-sî?M̀-voi, chhie̍t-tui mò khó-nèn!5 Ngìn ke su-miang chó chhiu chu-thin hó-se;kì chhai-sṳ ke ngit-è he Ngì kiet-thin.Ngì thin ke kie-han mò-ngìn nèn chhêu-ko.6 Khiù Ngì mo̍k khon-tén kì, mo̍k kón kì( 21 14:6 "mo̍k kón kì" he kîn-kí yit Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhâu-pún, thai-phu-fun ke Hî-pak-lòi-vùn chhâu-pún siá "sṳ́ kì hiet-khun".),pûn kì chhiong chhiáng-lòi ke kûng-ngìn cho-vàn kì ke kûng.7 Su-é na-he pûn-ngìn chám hâ-lòi,su-thèu hàn-yû sâng-ngà pau-sún ke hî-mong.8 Chhiu-son kîn chhai thi nài-hâ ló-thet,su-thèu chhai nài-tú châu-thet,9 súi-fûn chhûng-chiuk, hàn-he voi sâng-ngà pau-sún,chhiong sîn chung ke su-mèu pau-sún yit-yong.10 Chúng-he ngìn yit sí, chhiu-he kì ke kiet-khiu̍k;kì thôn-hi yî-heu, oi to nai-vi hi nè?11 Chhiu-chhiong fù mò súi,hò-pa súi châu-thet,12 ngìn yit sí, chhiu m̀-voi fán-sâng;thiên lap hâ, kì ya m̀-voi hong,chhṳ̍t-chhṳ̍t soi, ham ya m̀-voi siáng.13 Ngien Ngì chiông ngài khong chhai yîm-kiên,khong-to Ngì ke nu-hi sêu-thet,chang thin ngit-khì lòi ki-ngiam ngài.14 Ngìn na sí-thet, hàn-voi fán-sâng mâ?Ngài chhiu oi tén-to sṳt-fong ke-ngit,ngiûn-to châi-nan ke ngit-è ko-hi.15 To ke-sṳ̀, Ngì yit ham, ngài chhiu voi en Ngì;Ngì voi fôn-hí Ngì sú só chhóng-chho ke ngài.16 Ke-sṳ̀, Ngì voi chhà-khon ngài ke kiok-phu,chúng-he Ngì m̀ chûi-kiu ngài ke ko-fam.17 Ngì voi chiông ngài ke ko-sṳt fûng-ngi̍p thoi-tú,chiông ngài só-yû ke chhui-ok chhiòn-phu châ-koi.18 Chhiu-chhiong sân yit pên-thet,ngàm-sa̍k ya sái-chéu hi,19 liù súi nò-sún sa̍k-thèu,thai-súi chhûng-chéu thú-thi;Ngì ya án-ngiòng fí-me̍t ngìn ke hî-mong.20 Ngì yún-yén yàng-ko ngìn, sṳ́ kì sí-thet,sṳ́ kì mien-mau kói-pien, chiông kì kiuk-chéu.21 Kiâ lai-é tet-tó hó miàng-sâng, kì m̀-tî;kì-têu kong-cho pî-mì, kì ya mò kám-kok.22 Kì chṳ́ tî chhṳ-kâ sṳ̂n-hong ke thung-khú;kì chṳ́ yû vi chhṳ-kâ yen-than pî-ôi!
