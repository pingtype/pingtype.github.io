1 Song-chú ki-siu̍k lâu Yok-pak kóng:2 Hau-au ke Yok-pak â, ngì oi lâu Chhiòn-nèn-chá lî-lun;ngì kám lâu Ngài phien-pok, liá-ha ngì cho-tet en Ngài â.3 Yok-pak fì-tap Song-chú kóng:4 Ngài son má-ke?( 58 40:4 "Ngài son má-ke?" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Ngài án mì-se.") Hàn oi ngióng-pân en Ngì nè?Ngài êm-tén choi, m̀-kám chai kóng lé.5 Kóng yit-pái chhiu la lé;ngài mò-oi kâ-kóng lé.6 Só-yî, Song-chú tui thai-fûng tông-chûng lâu Yok-pak kóng:7 Khî hí-lòi, chhiong yit-ke yúng-kám ke ngìn;Ngài mun ngì, ngì lòi chṳ́-kau Ngài.8 Ngì sióng-oi chṳn-mìn Ngài mò kûng-tong, thin Ngài yû-chhui,lòi hién-chhut chhṳ-kâ chang chho̍k he-mò?9 Ngì yû chhiong Ngài án-thai ke nèn-li̍t mò?Ngì voi fat-chhut tá lùi-kûng ke sâng mò?10 Ngì na-he voi, chhiu hi yung chûn-kui lâu chông-ngiàm lòi tá-pan,yung chûn-ngiàm lâu kông-yùng cho sâm-fu.11 Ngì khon ke-têu kiêu-ngau ke ngìn;ngì hi thai fat-khién, met-sôi kì-têu â!12 Ngì hi chiông ngì só khon-tó ke-têu kiêu-ngau ke ngìn lâi hâ-lòi;chiông cho fái-sṳ ke ngìn chien-tha̍p chhai kì-têu ke pún-thi.13 Hi chiông kì-têu chhiòn-phu mài-chong;chiông kì-têu khún chhai yîm-kiên â!14 Na-he án-ngiòng, Ngài chhiu voi chan-mî ngì,sṳ̀n-ngin ngì chhṳ-kâ yû nèn-li̍t tet-tó sṳn-li( 59 40:14 "sṳn-li" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chṳ́n-kiu".).15 Khon ke án-thai ke hò-mâ( 60 40:15 "hò-mâ": Yû-ngìn ngin-vì liá he chṳ́ yit-chúng chhòn-sot chûng thi-song ke thai koai-vu̍t.) â;Ngài chho kì, ya chho ngì.Kì chhiong ngiù án-ngiòng sṳ̍t-chhó;16 kì ke hi-li̍t chhai tông-chap ke yêu-kiên!Kì ke nèn-li̍t chhai kiet-sṳ̍t ke tú-sṳ́!17 Kì ke mî khieu hí-lòi chhiong hiông-pak-su;kì thai kiok-pí ke ngiuk ngióng-pân yúng-khien!18 Kì ke kut-thèu chhiong thùng-kón;kì ke kiok chhiong thiet kun-é.19 Ngài só chhóng-chho ke pak-chhu chûng kì chhin thi̍t-phe̍t;chṳ́-yû kì ke Chhóng-chho-chá chang-nèn tá-yàng kì.20 Chhai yâ-chhu fa̍t-thung ke sân-lìm chûng,yû sâng tông-tô kì oi sṳ̍t ke chhó.21 Kì soi chhai lièn-fâ ya̍p hâ-poi,phu̍k chhai lù-chuk lâu nam-nài tú.22 Lièn-fâ ke ya̍p cho-tet lâu kì châ-liòng,hò-pa sùn ke liû-su ya vì-tén kì.23 Hò-pa cho thai-súi ke sṳ̀, kì m̀-voi kín-chông;Yok-tan-hò ke súi chong-to kì ke choi-sùn, kì ya ôn-yèn chhṳ-chhai.24 Má-ngìn nèn hi êm kì ke muk-chû chok kì?Má-ngìn nèn yung kiêu-é khien kì ke phi-khûng?
