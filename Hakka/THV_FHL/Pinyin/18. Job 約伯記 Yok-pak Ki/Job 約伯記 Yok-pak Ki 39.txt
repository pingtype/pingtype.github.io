1 Ngì tî yâ-sân-yòng kí-sṳ̀ kiung chṳ́ mò?Ngì khon-ko yâ-lu̍k ngióng-pân kiung chṳ́ mò?2 Kì-têu fài-thôi yû kí-kiú?Ngì tî kì-têu kí-sṳ̀ voi kiung mò?3 Ngì tî kì-têu su sên-sán ke thung-khú oi kí-kiú,chang khù lo̍k-hi chiông chṳ́ kiung hâ-lòi mò?4 Kì-têu ke se-chṳ́ chhai yâ-ngoi chhiam-chhiam thai,heu-lòi chhiu chéu-thet mò-chai chón-lòi.5 Má-ngìn chiông yâ-lì piong-chéu?Má-ngìn kié-khôi thak-tén kì-têu ke sok-è?6 Ngài chiông khóng-yâ pûn kì-têu he̍t,sṳ́ kì-têu he̍t chhai yàm-thi.7 Kì-têu fî-sòng nâu sàng-sṳ ke chhàu-nau,mò-oi pûn-ngìn thai-sâng tot-lòi tot-hi.8 Sân-yâ mân-phien tû he kì-têu ke mu̍k-chhòng;kì-têu chhai ke-vi chhìm chhiâng-set ke sṳ̍t-liau.9 Yâ-ngiù hén pûn ngì sṳ́ mò?Kì hén chhai ngì ke ngiù-làn ko-ya mò?10 Ngì yû phan-fap yung sok-è lâi yâ-ngiù lài-thièn,ya-he kâm( 57 39:10 "kâm" ya-he yi̍t-cho "miên-kióng" ya-he "khiòng-pet".) kì hi thièn-tú thô thiet-phà mò?11 Ngì cho-tet yí-sṳ kì ke hi-li̍t tông-thai,chiông chhin-chhûng ke kûng-chok kâu-pûn kì cho mò?12 Ngì nèn yí-sṳ kì chiông sû-sṳ̀n tai-chón vuk-hâ,thoi ngì sû vò-thàng ke kuk mò?13 Thò-niâu voi tá-khôi yi̍t-kap chhin ya̍k,chúng-he kì m̀-voi chhiong pha̍k-kon án-ngiòng pî.14 Thò-niâu sâng-lón chhai thi-song,sṳ́ lón tet-tó nài-sâ ke sêu-hi.15 Kì m̀-tî yû khó-nèn voi pûn kiok tha̍p-lan,ya-he pûn yâ-chhu tha̍p-thet.16 Kì tui-thai se tiâu-chṳ́ chhiu-chhiong m̀-he kì chhṳ-kâ ke,ya m̀ koân-sîm chhṳ-kâ ke sîn-khú he m̀-he mò chhái-kûng;17 yîn-vi Ngài mò pûn kì chṳ-fi,ya mò chiông ngu-sin su-pûn kì.18 Chúng-he kì tá-khôi yi̍t-kap yit-chéu,chhiu chhṳ́-seu mâ-é lâu khì-mâ ke ngìn.19 Yok-pak â, he ngì sṳ́ mâ-é án-chap mò?He ngì pûn kì án-chiâng ke chûng-mô he-mò?20 He ngì sṳ́ kì-têu chhiong chhó-máng thiau-lòi thiau-hi,sṳ́ kì-têu sê hí-lòi pûn ngìn tông chho̍k-kiâng mò?21 Kì-têu fôn-hí chhut thai-li̍t khie̍t-nài;kì-têu siông-châng pi-poi sông chan-chhòng.22 Kì-têu chhṳ́-seu tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sṳ, má-ke tû m̀-kiâng;tô-kiam ya m̀-voi sṳ́ kì-têu to-thui.23 Chan-sṳ só tai ke chien-thoi khin-khin khiang-khiang;màu lâu chhiông chhai ngit-thèu hâ kîm-kông sám-sám.24 Chan-mâ hí sin-thí, kit-thung to hiong-chhièn pêu;yòng-kok yit-phùn, kì-têu chhiu chhung-tén sông-chhṳn.25 Thâng-tó yòng-kok sâng, kì-têu chhiu kín-sê phun-hi;kì-têu yén-yén chhiu phi-tó chan-chên ke hi-fun,ya thâng tet-tó kiûn-kôn thai-sâng hêm ke min-lin.26 Ên-tiâu tá-khôi yi̍t-kap hiong-nàm pî;kám kóng liá he ngì kâu kì ke mâ?27 Thai ên-tiâu pî-kô, chhai sân-piak cho-teu;liá kám he cheu ngì ke min-lin?28 Kì chhai pên-kông táng hiet-khun,chhai chiâm-chiâm ke ngàm-sa̍k táng cho sàng-pó.29 Kì tui ke táng-kô khon hâ-hi,chhìm-khon yû má-ke hó-sṳ̍t mò.30 Yû sṳ̂-thí ke só-chhai ên-tiâu chhiu chhi-si̍p,lièn kì ke se ên-tiâu ya thèn-lòi sṳ̍t-hiet.
