1 Thi-yit Pái Chên-lun( 4:1-14:22 ) Thì-man-ngìn Yî-li-fap en Yok-pak kóng:2 Ngìn oi lâu ngì kóng-fa, ngì mò nai-sîm lòi thâng he-mò?Chúng-he má-ngìn ngiûn tet-he̍t m̀ kóng-fa nè?3 Ngì sṳt kâu-ko chhin-tô ngìn,sṳ́ ngiôn-ngio̍k ke sú yû li̍t.4 Ngì ke fa sṳt pûn voi tiet-tó ke ngìn tet-tó kú-li,sṳ́ sôi-ngio̍k ke chhit-thèu yúng-khien hí-lòi.5 Liá-ha châi-fo lìm-to ngì sṳ̂n-song,ngì chhiu kiâng-to mò yúng-hi tâm-tông.6 Ngì ke kin-khièn mò tai-pûn ngì sin-sîm he-mò?Ngì mò hó-hiàm ke hàng-vì mò tai-pûn ngì hî-mong he-mò?7 Ngì sióng khon â: Nai yû mò-chhui ke ngìn me̍t-mòng?Nai yû chṳn-chhṳ̍t ke ngìn hàng-to chhie̍t-lu hi nè?8 Cheu ngài khon, kâng sià-ok, ve thu̍k ke ngìn,kì-têu só sû-sṳ̀n ke chhiu-he ok-thu̍k.9 Song-ti ke théu-hi sṳ́ kì-têu me̍t-mòng;Song-ti ke fat-khién sṳ́ kì-têu sêu-me̍t.10 Thai sṳ̂-é ke ho-sâng chhiòn-phu tiâm-thet;se sṳ̂-é ke ngà-chhṳ́ chhiòn-phu khau-thet.11 Ló sṳ̂-é mò hó-sṳ̍t chhiu ngo-sí;se sṳ̂-é ya chhiòn-phu san chhiang-chhiang.12 Yû yit-pái ngài am-chûng thâng-tó sêu-sit,ngài ngí-kûng thâng-tó chhin-se ke sâng;13 thai-kâ tông hó-soi ke sṳ̀,yû yi-siong lòi káu-chhau ngài.14 Ngài kiâng-to pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat;ngài chhiòn-sṳ̂n ke kut-thèu ki̍t-ki̍t-chûn.15 Yit-chhṳn fûng chhôi-ko ngài ke mien,sṳ́ ngài kûi-sṳ̂n hí hòn-mô-kû.16 Ngài khon-tó yit-chúng hìn-siong thìn chhai ngài mien-chhièn,chúng-he ngin m̀-chhut kì he má-ke.Liá-sṳ̀, chhai tiâm-chhin chûng, ngài thâng-tó chhin-se ke sâng-yîm kóng:17 Chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn, má-ngìn khâm-tet chhṳ̂n-cho ngi-ngìn?( 8 4:17 "Chhai Song-ti ... ngi-ngìn?" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Yû má-ngìn pí Song-ti kha kûng-chṳn?")Chhai chhóng-chho Chú mien-chhièn, yû má-ngìn he sùn-kiet ke?( 9 4:17 "Chhai chhóng-chho Chú ... sùn-kiet ke?" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Yû má-ngìn pí chhóng-chho Chú kien sùn-kiet?")18 Lièn thiên-táng ke phu̍k-ngìn, Song-ti ya yí-sṳ m̀-tet;Kì sṳt chṳ́-chhut ke-têu thiên-sṳ́ ke ko-sṳt.19 Ngìn he yung nài cho ke, chhiong he̍t chhai nài-vuk tú,pí chu-chhùng kha yùng-yi chak-sí;kám-kóng Song-ti cho-tet yí-sṳ ngài-têu?20 Ngìn chêu-sṳ̀n-thèu hàn-sâng, lìm-am-é chhiu sí-thet;kì yún-yén me̍t-mòng, ya mò ngìn chhap kì!21 Kiâ chong-phùng ke sok-è pâng thôn-thet;kì sí, ya he mò chṳ-fi lòi sí.
