1 Chhiòn-nèn-chá cho-má-ke m̀ chiông sṳ́m-phan ke ngit-khì thin hâ-lòi?Cho-má-ke m̀-pûn ngin-sṳt Kì ke ngìn khon-tó chṳn-ngi lìm-to?2 Yû-têu ngìn sái thi-kie lòi chhîm-cham thú-thi,yû-têu ngìn chhióng phe̍t-ngìn ke yòng-khiùn lòi-hiuk.3 Yû-têu ngìn chhióng-chéu kû-yì ke lì-è,khiòng-lâi koá-fu ke ngiù-è cho tí-ap.4 Kì-têu khiòng-pet phìn-khiùng ngìn lì-khôi thai-lu,ke-vi ke khiùng-khú ngìn hak-to chéu-hi khong.5 Khiùng-khú ngìn chhiong yâ-lì án-ngiòng, sî-yeu hi khóng-yâ chhìm sṳ̍t-vu̍t;mò khì-thâ thi-fông hó chhìm sṳ̍t-vu̍t lòi-hiuk kì-têu ke chṳ́-ńg.6 Kì-têu mò-nai-hò chhai phe̍t-ngìn ke thièn-thi sû-kot( 36 24:6 "Kì-têu ... sû-kot": Khó-nèn chṳ́ khiùng-khú ngìn, chhṳ-kâ ke thièn-thi pûn-ngìn phien-hi yî-heu, yu pûn-ngìn khiòng-pet vi phe̍t-ngìn kûng-chok.),chhai sià-ok ngìn ke phù-thò-yèn chak phù-thò lòi sṳ̍t.7 Kì-têu tá-chhak-pok soi, yîn-vi mò sâm hó-chok;hòn-thiên ke am-pû-sṳ̀, ya mò phî-kut hó-koi.8 Kì-têu chhai sân-táng pûn lo̍k thai-súi tuk-to làu-làu,mò má-ke só-chhai hó sám, chhiu chiâm chhai ngàm-sa̍k hâ.9 Fái-ngìn chiông koá-fu nám-tén ke se-chṳ́ chhióng-chéu,oi chiông khiùng-khú ngìn ke chṳ́-ńg chok-hi tù-chai.10 Khiùng-ngìn to-chhu liù-long, mò sâm hó-chok;ngo tú-sṳ́, hàn oi lâu ngìn pà kuk-pâu.11 Kì-têu chhai fái-ngìn ke vì-chhiòng nui cha kám-lám-yù;kín tha̍p phù-thò lòi cho chiú, hàn-he mò-fap chṳ́-hot.12 Sàng-tú tông-tô voi sí ke ngìn kín-chhên,yû chhûng-sông ke ngìn hêm kiu-miang,chúng-he Song-ti m̀-tap ke-têu ngìn ke khì-tó.13 Yû-têu ngìn m̀ chiap-su kông;m̀ ngin-sṳt kông-mìn ke tho,ya m̀ hàng kông-mìn ke lu.14 Thiên màng-kông chhṳ̀-ngìn ke chhiu hong-chhòng;kì-têu chhut-hi sat-hoi khiùng-khú ngìn,am-pû-thèu yu chhut-hi cho chhe̍t-é.15 Yìm-lon ke ngìn tén-to thôn-vû,yu chiông chhṳ-kâ ke mien châ-tén,kóng: "Mo̍k pûn ngìn khon-tó chang hó!"16 Am-pû-thèu chhe̍t-é ngi̍p ngìn vuk-hâ,kì-têu ngit-sṳ̀-thèu chhiu khong hí-lòi,yîn-vi kì-têu tông-kiâng khon-tó kông.17 Am-pû-sṳ̀ chhiong he kì-têu ke chêu-sṳ̀n,tông koan-se tet-ngìn-kiâng ke am-pû-thèu.(Só-fap khôi-choi kóng:)( 37 24:18 "Só-fap khôi-choi kóng" ngièn-vùn mò liá-ki fa. Yû-têu ho̍k-chá ngin-vì 18-25 chiet he Só-fap kóng ke, chúng-he ya yû ho̍k-chá ngin-vì hàn-he Yok-pak ke fa.)18 Fái-ngìn yit-ha-é chhiu pûn thai-súi tá-chéu.Kì-têu ke thú-thi su-tó Song-ti chu-chú;só-yî mò ngìn chón phù-thò-yèn hi kûng-chok.19 Chhiu-chhiong thiên-hôn lâu ngie̍t-thiên voi chiông pên-siet yùng-thet,yîm-kiên ya khiung-yong voi chiông fam-chhui ke ngìn thûn-thet.20 Lièn kiung kì-têu ke â-mê ya m̀ ki-tet kì-têu;kì-têu voi pûn chhùng-è sṳ̍t-thet, mò-ngìn ki-tet kì-têu;fái-ngìn voi chhiong su-é chám thôn-thet.21 Liá he yîn-vi kì-têu fái khoán-thai koá-fu,tui m̀-voi kiung ke fu-ńg chhiòn mò oi-sîm.22 Song-ti yung Kì ke thai-nèn sêu-me̍t khiòng-phau ke ngìn;Kì yit hàng-thung, fái-ngìn ke sin-miang chhiu mò thet-hi.23 Song-ti chhiam-sṳ̀ pûn kì-têu phìn-ôn, mò sèu-fàn,chúng-he Kì ke muk-chû khon kì-têu ke só-hàng só-cho.( 38 24:22-23 Ngièn-vùn mò chhîn-chhú. Ya-he yi̍t-cho: "Song-ti yung nèn-li̍t yèn-chhòng khiòng-phau ngìn ke sâng-miang; ke sin-miang nàn pó ke ngìn hàn-he hîn-vong. (23) Song-ti sṳ́ kì-têu ôn-vún, kì-têu chhiu yû só yí-kho; Song-ti ke muk-chû ya khon-ku kì-têu ke tho-lu.")24 Ke-têu fái-ngìn sûi-yèn yit-sṳ̀ tông hîn-vong,chúng-he yit-ha-é chhiu-chhiong chhó-muk châu-thet,yu chhiong vò-é chó-man oi pûn-ngìn kot-thet.25 Má-ngìn nèn chṳn-mìn liá m̀-he sṳ-sṳ̍t?Má-ngìn kám kóng ngài ke fa mò kîn-kí?
