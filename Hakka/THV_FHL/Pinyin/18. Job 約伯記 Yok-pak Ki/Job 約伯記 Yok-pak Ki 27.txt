1 Yok-pak chiap-tén yu kóng:2 Song-ti mò yung kûng-tong tui-thai ngài;Chhiòn-nèn-chá sṳ́ ngài sîm-chûng kân-khú.Ngài chṳ́-tén yún-sên ke Song-ti fat-sṳ:3 Tông ngài ke miang hàn chhôi,Song-ti pûn ngài hàn-yû yit-héu hi,4 ngài ke choi-sùn chhie̍t-tui m̀ kóng pha̍k-chhe̍t,ngài ke sa̍t-mà chhie̍t-tui m̀ kóng fâ-liâu.5 Ngài chhie̍t-tui m̀ sṳ̀n-ngin ngì-têu yû-lî;ngài to sí me oi kiên-chhṳ̀ ngài mò-chhui.6 Ngài khok-sin chhṳ-kâ mò m̀-chho̍k, m̀-voi piong-thet liá li̍p-chhòng;ngài hàn chhai sṳ-kiên ke ngit-è, ngài kiên-chhṳ̀ liòng-sîm chhîn-pha̍k.7 Ngien ngài ke thi̍t-ngìn tù-tó fái-ngìn yin-kôi su ke po-yin;ngien kûng-kit ngài ke chhiong put-ngi ke ngìn án-ngiòng su hìn-fa̍t.8 M̀ kin-vi Song-ti ke ngìn yû má-ke hó hî-mong nè?Song-ti oi sû kì ke miang, kì hàn-yû má-ke phan-mong?9 Châi-nan lìm-to kì sṳ̂n-song ke sṳ̀,Song-ti kám oi thâng kì ke khiù-ham?10 Kì kám voi yîn-vi Chhiòn-nèn-chá lòi hí-lo̍k?Kì kám voi sòng-sòng hiong Song-ti lòi khì-tó?11 Ngài oi chṳ́-kau ngì-têu Song-ti ke khièn-nèn yû kí-thai;ngài ya oi lâu ngì-têu sot-mìn Chhiòn-nèn-chá ke chṳ́-yi.12 Ngì-têu tak-sà kám m̀-he chhiòn-phu khon-ko mâ!Cho-má-ke kín kóng mò-tap mò-toi ke fa nè?(Só-fap kóng:)( 41 27:13 "Só-fap kóng" ngièn-vùn mò liá-ki fa. Yû-têu ho̍k-chá ngin-vì 13-23 chiet he Só-fap kóng ke, chúng-he ya yû ho̍k-chá ngin-vì hàn-he Yok-pak ke fa.)13 Song-ti pûn fái-ngìn só-thin ke miang-yun,Chhiòn-nèn-chá oi hìn-fa̍t chhàn-phau ke ngìn,chhiu-he án-ngiòng:14 Kì chhiu-son yû chhin-tô lai-é, tak-ke ya voi pûn-ngìn chhṳ̀-sí;kì ke chṳ́-sûn ya voi sṳ̍t m̀-páu.15 Ke-têu chhûn-tó ke ngìn voi pot-vûn sí-thet;kì-têu ke koá-fu ya m̀-voi vi kì-têu kieu.16 Fái-ngìn sûi-yèn ngiùn-è tô-to son m̀-chhut,yu hàn-yû chok m̀-thet án-tô ke sâm-fu,17 chúng-he ngi-ngìn oi lòi chok kì ke sâm-fu,mò-chhui ke ngìn oi lòi pûn kì ke ngiùn-è.18 Fái-ngìn ke vuk chhiong tiâu-teu( 42 27:18 "tiâu-teu" ya-he yi̍t-cho "chhùng-teu" ya-he "tî-tû mióng".) mò kiên-ku,ya chhiong nù-chhòi sú-mong yung ke màu-liàu.19 Chúng-yû yit-ngit, hi soi ke sṳ̀ hàn tông-pot,soi-siáng ke sṳ̀, kì ke chhòi-fu chhiu mò lé.20 Tet-ngìn-kiâng ke sṳ chhiong thai-súi kiuk-tó kì,thai fûng-chhâi chhai am-pû-thèu chiông kì chhôi-chéu.21 Tûng-fûng yit-chhôi,chiông kì tui vuk-hâ chhôi-chéu.22 Khòng-fûng oi kûng-kit kì, chhiòn-mò liù-chhìn;kì piang-miang chéu, sióng-oi chéu-chhut fûng-chhâi ke fam-vì.23 Ngìn( 43 27:23 "Ngìn" ya-he yi̍t-cho "Fûng"; ya yû ho̍k-chá ngin-vì khó-nèn chṳ́ "Song-ti" (chhâm-kháu 27:13).) oi phok-sú lòi sâu-nat kì,chhut-sâng kiuk kì lì-khôi pún-thi.
