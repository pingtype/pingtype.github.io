1 Yok-pak en-kóng:2 Ngì tui ngài liá vù-nèn ke ngìn pông-chhu hàn-tô ô!Ngài liá sú-kut mò-li̍t ke ngìn, ngì hàn-hén chṳ́n-kiu!3 Chhiong ngài án mò chṳ-fi ke ngìn,ngì hàn pûn ngài án pó-kui ke chṳ́-kau!4 Chúng-he ngì sióng, yû má-ngìn voi thâng ngì ke fa?To-tái má-ngìn kám-thung ngì kóng án-ngiòng ke fa?(Pí-le̍t-tha̍t kóng:)( 39 26:5 "Pí-le̍t-tha̍t kóng" ngièn-vùn mò liá-ki fa. Yû-têu ho̍k-chá ngin-vì 5-14 chiet he Pí-le̍t-tha̍t kóng ke, chúng-he ya yû ho̍k-chá ngin-vì hàn-he Yok-pak ke fa.)5 Chhai thi tái-hâ ke chhṳ̂m súi-chûng,sí-ngìn ke yîm-fùn pi̍t-pi̍t-chhat.6 Yîm-kiên chhai Song-ti mien-chhièn hién-mìn;thi-ngiu̍k ya mò phan-fap châ tet-he̍t.7 Song-ti phû-sat pet-khi̍t ke thiên-khûng,chiông thi-khiù koa chhai thai-khûng tông-chûng.8 Song-ti tún-súi chhai vû-yùn tông-chûng,yùn mò yîn-vi súi chhûng chhiu lie̍t-thet.9 Kì chiông yùn phû-sat chhai ngie̍t-kông( 40 26:9 "ngie̍t-kông" ya-he yi̍t-cho "pó-chho", ha yit-ki khiung-yong.) ke mien-táng,yung yùn chiông kûi-chak ngie̍t-kông châ-to me̍t-me̍t.10 Kì chhai hói-mien va̍k thi-phìn-sien,sàng-cho kông lâu am ke kie-han.11 Kì yit chṳ́-chit thiên ke chhû-é,kì-têu chhiu kiâng-to kín-chûn.12 Kì yung thai-nèn sṳ́ thai-hói phìn-chhin hâ-lòi,Kì yung chhûng-mìn tá-sui hói-koai Lâ-ha̍p-pak.13 Kì phùn yit-héu hi, yùn chhiu khôi, thiên chhiu chhîn;Kì ke sú chhṳ̀-thet chéu tông-kiak ke hói-sà.14 Khon nâ, liá-têu he Song-ti kûng-chok ke yit phu-fun,ngài-têu chṳ́-yû thâng-tó Kì tông-se ke sâng thin-thin.Kì thai-nèn ke lùi-sâng má-ngìn nèn mìn-pha̍k nè?
