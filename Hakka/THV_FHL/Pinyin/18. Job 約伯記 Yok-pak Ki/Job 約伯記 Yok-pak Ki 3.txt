1 Yok-pak Chu-chú Chhṳ-kâ ke Sâng-ngit Chui-heu, Yok-pak khôi-choi chu-chú chhṳ-kâ ke sâng-ngit.2 Ngien ngài chhut-se ke-ngit,lâu ngài sàng-cho nàm-thôi ke am-pû mò thet-hi.3 併於上節4 Ngien ke-ngit pien-cho vû-am;ngien Song-ti mo̍k ki-ngiam ke-ngit,mo̍k pûn kông cheu kì.5 Ngien kì sàng-cho vû-am sí-yîm ke yit-ngit.Ngien vû-yùn châ-koi kì,ngien thiên-kiéu sṳ̍t-ngit lòi-hak kì.6 Ngien ke am-pû pûn vû-am chok-chéu,mò son chhai ngièn-tú ke ngit-è lâu ngie̍t-fun chûng.7 Ngien ke am-pû mò sên-yuk ke sṳ,ya mò fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k ke sâng.8 Ngien ke-têu voi hêm-siáng hói-koai( 7 3:8 "hói-koai": Khó-nèn chṳ́ thai ngo̍k-ǹg (khon 41:1), ya-he kú sṳ̀-thoi Khiun-tûng chhòn-sot chûng ke thai koai-vu̍t (khon Sṳ̂-phiên 74:14; Yî-soi-â Sû 27:1). Tông-sṳ̀ pien sià-su̍t ke voi hêm kì thûn-sṳ̍t ngit-thèu ya-he ngie̍t-kông lòi yîn-hí ngit-sṳ̍t ngie̍t-sṳ̍t.) ke ngìn,ke-têu pien sià-su̍t ke ngìn chhut-lòi chu-chú ke am-pû.9 Ngien ke am-pû ke thiên-kông-sên pien-am;ya khon m̀-tó thiên-kông ngit-chhut.10 Yîn-vi ke am-pû mò chiông fài ngài ke chṳ́-kiûng mùn koân-thet,sa hoi ngài tù-tó án-tô khú-nan.11 Cho-má-ke ngài m̀ yit chhut-se chhiu thôn-hi?Ya-he màng chhut mû-thôi chhiu sí-thet?12 Cho-má-ke ngâ-mê chiông ngài nám chhai chhit-thèu táng?Cho-má-ke kì yû nen pûn ngài sṳ̍t?13 Na-he ngài ke-sṳ̀ sí-thet,liá-ha chó chhiu ôn-sit,14 lâu sṳ-kiên vi chhṳ-kâ chhùng-hí vòng-kiûng ke kiûn-vòng, kôn-yèn khiung-ha soi,15 lâu kûi-vuk chông-mân kîm-ngiùn ke vòng-chṳ́ khiung-ha ôn-sit,16 lâu lau-thôi, màng kien kông ke ô-nga-é khiung-yong.17 Chhai fùn-mu tú, fái-ngìn thìn-chṳ́ chok-ok,chhâu-lò ke ngìn tet-tó ôn-sit.18 Lièn pûn-ngìn chok-hi ke ngìn ya hióng-su chhṳ-yù,m̀-voi chai thâng-tó kam-kûng hot-hot tot-tot;19 mò fûn kui-chhien thai-se, chhiòn-phu chhai ke-vi,lièn nù-chhòi ya tet-tó chhṳ-yù.20 Cho-má-ke pûn kiên-khú ke ngìn hàn-yû ngit-kông?Cho-má-ke pûn yù-sông ke ngìn hàn-yû sâng-miang?21 Ke-têu ngìn sióng-oi sí, phiên-phiên m̀-voi sí;kì-têu sióng chhìm sí, yàng-ko chhìm chhòi-pó.22 Kì-têu ngi̍p-to fùn-mu,chang yû chṳn-kîn ke hí-lo̍k.23 Chhièn-thù mòng-mòng ke ngìn,si-mien pûn Song-ti vì-khun tén,cho-má-ke yû kông su-pûn kì?24 Ngài théu thai-hi chún-fan sṳ̍t;ngài kín-chhên chhiong súi liù m̀-voi thôn.25 Ngài chui kiâng ke sṳ chhiòn-phu chhut-hien;só-kiâng ke sṳ phiên-phiên lìm-to ngài.26 Ngài mò phìn-ôn, tet m̀-tó ôn-sit;chúng-he, chhâu-fàn m̀-sṳt thìn-ko.
