1 Hâ-poi he Song-chú hiong siên-tî Ha̍p-pâ-kuk só khí-sṳ ke sin-sit. Ha̍p-pâ-kuk Vi Mò Kûng-phìn Koai-yen2 Song-chú â, ngài oi khiù Ngì pông-chhu kí-kiú, Ngì chang voi thâng? Ngài oi thai-sâng hêm Ngì kí-kiú, Ngì chang voi kiu ngài-têu lì-khôi khiòng-phau nè?3 Ngì cho-má-ke oi ngài khon-tó án chhui-ok ke sṳ? Ngì ngióng-pân cho-tet yù-chhai liá-chúng fái-sṳ fat-sên nè? Chhai ngài mien-chhièn chhiòn-phu he fí-fái, khiòng-phau, tak só-chhai lûng-chúng he siông-châng, siông-tá.4 Fap-li̍t mò-háu, mò sṳ̍t-hàng chhut-lòi; chṳn-ngi yit-chhṳ̍t tet m̀-tó sṳ̂n-chông. Fái-ngìn khî-fu ngi-ngìn, chṳn-ngi su vâi-khiuk. Song-chú ke Fì-tap5 Song-chú tui Kì ke chṳ́-mìn kóng: "Chu-yi khon ngì-têu si chû-vì ke koet-kâ, ngì-têu voi tông chho̍k-kiâng. Ngài oi chhai ngì-têu ke sṳ̀-thoi cho yit-khien sṳ, ngì-têu thâng-tó ke sṳ̀ voi m̀-kám siông-sin.6 Ngài oi sṳ́ tông-hiûng, chhàn-phau ke Pâ-pí-lùn-ngìn khiòng-sṳn hí-lòi. Kì-têu oi chṳ̂n-fu̍k sṳ-kie, cham-liâng phe̍t-ngìn ke thú-thi.7 Kì-têu vî-vú chhàn-ngiûn, chhin tet-ngìn-kiâng; kì-têu muk-chûng mò-ngìn, chhṳ-kâ ke yi-sṳ tông-cho fap-li̍t.8 "Kì-têu ke mâ pí pau-é chéu kha-kiak, pí tú-kî ke sài-lòng hàn-kha ok. Kì-têu ke khì-pîn tui yén-fông lòi; kì-têu pêu-tén lòi, chhiu-chhiong yeu-phò chhûng hâ-lòi kia̍p sṳ̍t-vu̍t.9 "Kì-têu ke kiûn-chhui vî-fûng lím-lím hiong chhièn-chin, só to ke thi-fông, tak-sà kiâng-to voi-sí( 1 1:9 "só to ke ... voi-sí": Hî-pak-lòi-vùn ke yi-sṳ mò chhîn-chhú.). Kì-têu chok-tó ke ngìn chhiong sâ-é án-tô.10 Kì-têu sâu-nat kiûn-vòng lâu kôn-chóng. Só-yû ke fòng-phi chhiòn-phu tóng kì-têu m̀-he̍t; kì-têu hí thú-tôi, chiông sàng-pó kûng hâ-lòi.11 Kì-têu chhiong fûng-chhâi án-ngiòng so ko-hi; liá-têu chhui-ngìn chiông chhṳ-kâ ke li̍t-liong chún-cho sṳ̀n-mìn." Ha̍p-pâ-kuk Yu Koai-yen Song-ti12 Song-chú â, Ngì tui thai-chhû chhiu-he Song-ti. Ngì he ngài ke Song-ti, he sṳ̀n-sṳn ke, yún-yén ke. Song-chú — ngài ke pó-fu-chá â, Ngì kién-sién Pâ-pí-lùn-ngìn, sṳ́ kì-têu khiòng-chong, lòi chit-fa̍t ngài-têu.13 Chúng-he, Ngì ngióng-pân cho-tet yù-chhai liá-têu phoi-ngia̍k, sià-ok ke ngìn nè? Ngì ke muk-chû tông sṳn-kiet; Ngì chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi khon-tén chhui-ok, yù-chhai ngìn-mìn cho fái-sṳ. Án-ngiòng, kì-têu fí-me̍t pí chhṳ-kâ kha kûng-chṳn ke ngìn, Ngì cho-má-ke m̀ kóng-fa nè?14 Ngì ngióng-pân cho-tet chiông ngìn-mìn chún-cho hói-tú ke ǹg, ya-he yit-khiùn mò liâng-chhiu chṳ́-fî ke phà-chhùng nè?15 Pâ-pí-lùn-ngìn yung kiêu-é kiêu-ngìn, chhiong tiau ǹg-è khiung-yong; kì-têu yung mióng-è mióng-ngìn, mióng-tó ngìn chhiu fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k!16 Kì-têu sṳm-chṳ pai kì-têu ke mióng-è, hiong mióng-è hien-chi sêu-hiông, yîn-vi ke-têu mióng-è tai-pûn kì-têu fûng-fu ke sên-fa̍t lâu sṳ̍t-vu̍t.17 Kám-kóng oi yù-chhai kì-têu yit-chhṳ̍t chiông mióng-è piang-chhiang, chhṳ̀ van-mìn, mò pan-tiám lìn-mén ke sîm mâ?
