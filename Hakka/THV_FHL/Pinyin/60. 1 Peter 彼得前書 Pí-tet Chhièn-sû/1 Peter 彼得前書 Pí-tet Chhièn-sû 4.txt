1 Sên-fa̍t ke Kói-pien Kî-tuk ki-yèn chhai ngiuk-thí su-khú, ngì-têu chhiu yin-kôi yung liá-chúng ke sîm-chṳ chông-phi chhṳ-kâ, yîn-vi chhai ngiuk-thí-song su-ko khú ke ngìn, kì thùng chhui-ok ke koân-he yí-kîn chhiet-thôn;2 chhiùng-kîm yî-heu, ngì-têu chhai sṳ-kiên ke ngit-è oi sun-chhiùng Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi, m̀-hó chai pûn ngìn ke yu̍k-mong hot-chṳ.3 Vông-sṳ̀ ngì-têu long-fi kông-yîm, hàng mò sin ke ngìn fôn-hí cho ke kok-chúng sṳ, chhiong yìm-lon, sṳ̂-yu̍k, hau-chiú, yen-lo̍k, chiú-chui, lâu Song-ti fap-li̍t só kim-chṳ́ ke pai ngiéu-siong.4 Liá-ha ngì-têu mò chai lâu kì-têu khiung-yong ko ke-chúng fong-thong, mò chiet-chṳ ke sên-fa̍t, kì-têu kám-kok chhin khì-koai, chhiu vú-yu̍k ngì-têu.5 Chúng-he, kì-têu yit-thin oi tui ke sṳ́m-phan sâng-ngìn lâu sí-ngìn ke Song-ti kâu-chong.6 Chhiu-he yîn-vi án-ngiòng, fuk-yîm ya sṳt chhòn pûn sí-ngìn, pûn kì-têu sûi-yèn ngiuk-thí thùng khì-thâ ke ngìn khiung-yong su sṳ́m-phan, chúng-he lìn-fùn yû-hó chhiong Song-ti án-ngiòng sâng-tén. Cho Song-ti Ên-su ke Kón-kâ7 Van-sṳ ke kiet-khiu̍k khiûn lé. Ngì-têu oi kiún-sṳ́m chiet-chṳ, kín-siáng khì-tó.8 Chui chhung-yeu ke he oi pí-chhṳ́ ngie̍t-sṳ̀n siông-siak, yîn-vi oi-siak chang nèn châ-koi tô-tô ke chhui.9 Thai-kâ oi pí-chhṳ́ chiap-thai, m̀-hó mài-yen.10 Ki-yèn tak-sà he Song-ti kok-yong ên-su ke hó kón-kâ, chhiu oi cheu só liâng-su ke chúng-chúng ên-su, pí-chhṳ́ fu̍k-vu.11 Kóng-tho ke, oi cheu Song-ti ke fa lòi kóng; fu̍k-vu ke, oi yung Song-ti só su ke li̍t-liong lòi fu̍k-vu, pûn van-hong sṳ lûng-chúng thûng-ko Yâ-sû Kî-tuk kûi yùng-kông pûn Song-ti. Ngien yùng-kông lâu khièn-nèn chhiòn-phu kûi-pûn Kì, chhṳ̍t-to yún-yún yén-yén! Â-mén. Ngiûn-su Khú-nan ke Kî-tuk-thù12 Chhîn-oi ke phèn-yû â, ngì-têu tù-tó chhiong fó án-ngiòng ke kháu-ngiam, m̀-sṳ́ khì-koai, chhiong-he tù-tó má-ke hî-khì ke sṳ.13 Tiên-to, ngì-têu yin-kôi chiông fûn-tâm Kî-tuk ke khú-nan khon-cho khoai-lo̍k ke sṳ, hó pûn ngì-têu chhai kì ke yùng-kông hién-hien ke sṳ̀ lòi fôn-hí khoai-lo̍k.14 Ngì-têu na yîn-vi thèn Kî-tuk su vú-yu̍k, chhiu yû fuk-hi lé! Liá péu-sṳ yùng-kông ke Sṳn-Lìn, chhiu-he Song-ti ke Lìn, chhai ngì-têu ke sṳ̂n-song.15 Chúng-he, ngì-têu tông-chûng na yû-ngìn su-khú, m̀-hó he yîn-vi hiûng-sat, cho chhe̍t, fam hìn-sṳ on-khien, ya-he hau chhap hàn-sṳ.16 Su-khú na-he yîn-vi cho Kî-tuk-thù, m̀-hó khon-cho kien-seu, fán-chón oi yîn-vi yû liá-ke miàng-chhṳ̂n lòi yùng-kông Song-ti.17 Sṳ́m-phan ke sṳ̀-chiet to lé, Song-ti kâ-nui ke chṳ́-ńg oi siên su sṳ́m-phan. Ki-yèn sṳ́m-phan oi tui ngài-têu khôi-sṳ́, án-ngiòng, m̀ sin-chhiùng Song-ti fuk-yîm ke ngìn kiet-khiu̍k voi ngióng-pân nè?18 Chhiong Sṳn-kîn só kóng:Lièn ngi-ngìn tet-kiu ya án mò kién-tân,m̀ kin-khièn ke chhui-ngìn voi ngióng-pân nè?19 Só-yî, ke-têu yîn-vi Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi su-khú ke ngìn, oi yit-sîm hàng-san, chiông chhṳ-kâ kâu-thok ke sin-sṳ̍t ke Chhóng-chho Chú.
