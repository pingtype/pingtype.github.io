1 Sâng ke Sa̍k-thèu lâu Sṳn-kiet ke Koet Só-yî, ngì-têu oi chhù-thet yit-chhiet ke sià-ok, kúi-cha, ká-mo vì-san, chhit-tu, lâu yit-chhiet fí-pong ngìn ke fa;2 oi chhiong chang chhut-se ke ô-nga-é, hot-mu sùn-chhiang ke lìn-nen, pûn ngì-têu thûng-ko kì lòi chóng-thai chṳ-to tet-kiu.3 Chhiong Sṳn-kîn só kóng: "Ngì-têu yí-kîn sòng-ko Chú chhṳ̀-oi ke chṳ̂-mi."4 Oi chhîn-khiun Chú, kì he sâng ke sa̍k-thèu; sûi-yèn kì pûn-ngìn khî-chhie̍t, chúng-he Song-ti kién-sién kì, khon kì cho pó-pi ke.5 Ngì-têu ya oi chhiong sâng ke sa̍k-thèu, su kien-chho sàng-cho su̍k-lìn ke sṳn-thien, chhai ke-vi cho sṳn-kiet ke chi-sṳ̂, thûng-ko Yâ-sû Kî-tuk lòi hien Song-ti só fôn-hí su̍k-lìn ke chi-vu̍t.6 Yîn-vi Sṳn-kîn kóng:Ngài tho̍k yit-te kui-chhung ke sa̍k-thèu,lòi cho Siak-ôn fòng-kok ke sa̍k-thèu;sin-kho kì ke, chhie̍t-tui m̀-voi sṳt-mong.7 Tui ngì-têu sin ke ngìn lòi kóng, liá sa̍k-thèu he fî-sòng pó-kui ke; chúng-he, tui ke-têu m̀ sin ke ngìn:Nài-súi sṳ̂-fu só fit-thet ke liá-te sa̍k-thèu,yí-kîn sàng-cho thi-kî chûng chui chhung-yeu ke( 2 2:7 "thi-kî chûng chui chhung-yeu ke" ya-he yi̍t-cho "vuk-tung ke kiúng-sîm-sa̍k".)!8 Sṳn-kîn nang-ngoi yu kóng:Liá-te sa̍k-thèu voi pan ngìn ke kiok,he voi sṳ́-ngìn tiet-tó ke thai sa̍k-thèu! Kì-têu pan-tó lé, yîn-vi kì-têu m̀ sin chṳ̂n-tho; liá ya he Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi.9 Chúng-he, ngì-têu he su kién-sién ke yit-chhu̍k, he Vòng ke chi-sṳ̂, sṳn-kiet ke koet, Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn. Song-ti fû-seu ngì-têu lì-khôi vû-am, chin-ngi̍p Kì khì-meu ke kông-mìn, lòi siên-yòng Kì o-meu ke chok-vì.10 Ngì-têu yî-chhièn m̀-he Song-ti ke chṳ́-mìn,chúng-he liá-ha he Kì ke chṳ́-mìn;yî-chhièn mò tet-tó Song-ti ke lìn-mén,liá-ha yí-kîn tet-tó Kì ke lìn-mén.11 Song-ti ke Nù-phu̍k Chhîn-oi ke phèn-yû â, ngì-têu chhai sṳ-kiên he lî-hak, he phit-nan ke ngìn! Ngài khien ngì-têu, m̀-hó fong-chiúng ngiuk-thí ke chhìn-yu̍k; chhìn-yu̍k chhiùng-lòi chhiu-he kûng-kit lìn-fùn ke.12 Chhai Ngoi-pâng-ngìn tông-chûng, ngì-têu yin-tông yû tôn-chang ke phín-hen, pûn ke-têu kóng ngì-têu fái-fa, chit-phi ngì-têu cho fái-sṳ ke ngìn, yîn-vi khon-tó ngì-têu ke hó hàng-vì, chhai Chú chai-lòi ke-ngit, voi kûi yùng-kông pûn Song-ti.13 Vi-tó Chú ke yèn-ku, ngì-têu oi sun-chhiùng sṳ-kiên só-yû ke chóng-khièn-chá, chhiu-he chhai-song ke kiûn-vòng,14 pin kì só phai-lòi chit-fa̍t chok-ok, chióng-li hàng-san ke kôn-chóng.15 Yîn-vi Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi he oi ngì-têu yung hó hàng-vì lòi set-sí ke-pân fù-thù-ngìn vù-tî ke choi.16 Ngì-têu oi cho chhṳ-yù-ngìn! Chúng-he m̀-hó yung chhṳ-yù cho sià-ok ke chia-khiéu; tiên-to, ngì-têu oi cho Song-ti ke nù-phu̍k.17 Ngì-têu oi chûn-chhung mî-yit-ke ngìn; siak sin Chú ke hiûng-thi chí-moi; kin-vi Song-ti; chûn-chhung kiûn-vòng. Kî-tuk Ngiûn-su Khú-nan ke Póng-yong18 Cho nù-phu̍k ke, ngì-têu oi sun-chhiùng chú-ngìn, ya oi tui kì-têu péu-hien sṳ̍p-fûn ke kiûng-kin; m̀ tân-chhiang tui hó-sîm vûn-yù ke chú-ngìn oi án-ngiòng, tui chhû-phau ke chú-ngìn ya oi án-ngiòng.19 Na-he ngì-têu khon liá he Song-ti ke chṳ́-yi, kâm-ngien ngiûn-su m̀ yin-kôi sṳ̍t ke khú, Song-ti yit-thin voi su-fuk pûn ngì-têu.20 Na-he ngì-têu yîn-vi fam-chhui lòi ngiûn-su chit-tá, yû má-ke kông-yùng nè? Vì-yû yîn-vi hàng-san lòi ngiûn-su khú-nan ke ngìn chang voi tet-tó Song-ti ke su-fuk.21 Song-ti fû-seu ngì-têu ke muk-tit chhiu-he chhai liá-vi; yîn-vi Kî-tuk vi ngì-têu su-khú, liù-hâ póng-yong pûn ngì-têu, oi pûn ngì-têu thèn kì ke kiok-phu hàng.22 Kì mò fam-ko chhui;mò ngìn thâng-ko kì kóng fâ-liâu.23 Kì pûn-ngìn yu̍k-ma mò en-choi, pûn-ngìn ngio̍k-thai ya mò kóng khiúng-hak ke fa, chṳ́-yû ngióng-mong ke cheu kûng-ngi sṳ́m-phan ngìn ke Chú.24 Kî-tuk chhai sṳ̍p-sṳ-ka-táng chhîn-sṳ̂n tâm-tông ngài-têu ke chhui, pûn ngài-têu ki-yèn chhai chhui-ok-song sí, chhiu vi-tó kûng-ngi lòi sâng. Yîn-vi kì só-su ke piên-sông, ngì-têu chhiu tet-tó yî-chhṳ.25 Yî-chhièn ngì-têu chhiong mì-lu ke yòng, chúng-he liá-ha yí-kîn fì-thèu, thèn-tén ngì-têu lìn-fùn ke mu̍k-chá lâu kam-fu-ngìn lé.
