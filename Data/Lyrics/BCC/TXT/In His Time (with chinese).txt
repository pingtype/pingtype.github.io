在主裡的時刻，在神時刻他使萬物美好。Zài zhǔ lǐ de shíkè, zài shén shíkè tā shǐ wànwù měihǎo.In His time, In His time,He makes all things beautiful, in His time.求主每天提醒我，求主每天教導我，一切事物都有神的時刻。Qiú zhǔ měitiān tíxǐng wǒ, qiú zhǔ měitiān jiàodǎo wǒ, yīqiè shìwù dōu yǒu shén de shíkè.Lord, please show me every dayAs You're teaching me Your way,That You do just what You say in Your time.在主裡的時刻，在神時刻你使萬物美好。Zài zhǔ lǐ de shíkè, zài shén shíkè nǐ shǐ wànwù měihǎoIn Your time, in Your time;You make all things beautiful in Your time.主願我所獻生命，主願我所唱詩歌，都能蒙我親愛主所喜悅。Zhǔ yuàn wǒ suǒ xiàn shēngmìng, zhǔ yuàn wǒ suǒ chàng shīgē, dōu néng méng wǒ qīn'ài zhǔ suǒ xǐyuè.Lord, my life to You I bring;May each song I have to singBe to You a lovely thing in Your time.
