Praise the Lord!Praise, O servants of the Lord, praise the name of the Lord!你們要讚美耶和華。耶和華的僕人哪！你們要讚美，要讚美耶和華的名。Blessed be the name of the Lord    from this time forth and forevermore!From the rising of the sun to its setting,    the name of the Lord is to be praised!耶和華的名是應當稱頌的，從現在直到永遠。從日出之地到日落之處，耶和華的名都該受讚美。The Lord is high above all nations,    and his glory above the heavens!Who is like the Lord our God,    who is seated on high,耶和華高過萬國，他的榮耀超越諸天。誰像耶和華我們的神呢？他坐在至高之處，who looks far down    on the heavens and the earth?He raises the poor from the dust    and lifts the needy from the ash heap,他俯首垂顧天上和地下的事。他從灰塵中抬舉貧寒人，從糞堆中提拔窮乏人，to make them sit with princes,    with the princes of his people.He gives the barren woman a home,    making her the joyous mother of children.Praise the Lord!他使他們和權貴同坐，就是和他子民中的權貴同坐，他使不能生育的婦人安居家中，成了有許多兒女的快樂母親。     你們要讚美耶和華。
