At the crossI know a place, a wonderful place有一地方，是奇妙地方，Where accused and condemned凡被定罪的人，Find mercy and grace,在那裡蒙恩；Where the wrongs we have done我們一切過犯，And the wrongs done to us我們一切傷害，Were nailed there with Him都與他同釘There on the cross.在十架上。At the cross, (echo)在十架 (在十架)   He (You) died for our sin.他(你)為我贖罪；   At the cross, (echo)在十架 (在十架)   He (You) gave us life again.他(你)賜我新生命。
