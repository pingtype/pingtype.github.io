Article 1
These standards are authorized by Article 21, Paragraph 3 of the People with Disabilities Rights Protection Act. Article 2
Municipal and county (city) competent authorities holding health checkups and healthcare services for people with disabilities shall inform about checkup times and locations through written notifications or public notices and shall have copies sent to related organizations for the disabled. Additionally, assistance shall be provided to people with disabilities for the checkups depending on actual needs. Article 3
Items of health checkups and healthcare services for people with disabilities are as shown in the attached table. Municipal and county (city) competent authorities may select from items listed in the attached table and hold health checkups and healthcare services for people with disabilities according to their financial situations and the needs of people with disabilities.
