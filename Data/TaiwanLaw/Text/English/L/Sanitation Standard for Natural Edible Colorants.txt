Article 1
The Standards are prescribed in accordance with the provisions of Article 17 of the Act Governing Food Sanitation. Article 2
Solvents for the extraction of natural edible colorants shall meet the following requirements.(1) Water, ethanol, and vegetable oil, etc..(2) Solvents permitted for use under the regulations set in “Scope and Application Standard of Food Additives”. Article 3
Excipients and other additives used in natural edible colorants shall under the regulations set in “Scope and Application Standard of Food Additives”. Article 4
