Article 1
These Regulations are established in accordance with the provisions of Paragraph 2 of Article 35 of the Act Governing Food Safety and Sanitation (hereinafter referred to as “Act”). Article 2
The terms used in these Regulations are defined as follows:1.“Systematic inspection” means inspecting the food safety management system and the supervisory measures taken by the governments of the exporting country (territory).2.“Inspection authority” means the Food and Drug Administration, Ministry of Health and Welfare.3.“Document review” means reviewing the documentation relating to the food safety management system in the exporting country (territory) provided by the governmental authority of the exporting country (territory).4.“On-site inspection” means the inspection authority designates inspectors to conduct the systematic inspection of the food safety management system in the exporting country (territory). Article 3
