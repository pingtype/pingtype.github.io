Article 1
These Regulations are prescribed pursuant to Article 29, Paragraph 2 of the Artificial Reproduction Act (herein referred to as this Act). Article 2
Children conceived through artificial reproduction or their statutory agents (herein referred to as applicants) may apply to the competent authority for an Certificate of Birth through Artificial Reproduction (Attached Form 1) where one of the conditions prescribed in the various subparagraphs of Article 29, Paragraph 1 of this Act is satisfied.
