Article 1
These Standards are adopted pursuant to the provisions of Article 7 and Article 10 of the Charges and Fees Act. Article 2
These Standards are applicable to the following:I. Medicament advertisements, including drug and medical device advertisements, that apply for review in accordance with the regulations of the Pharmaceutical Affairs Act.II. Cosmetic advertisements that apply for review in accordance with the regulations of the Statute for Control of Cosmetic Hygiene. Article 3
