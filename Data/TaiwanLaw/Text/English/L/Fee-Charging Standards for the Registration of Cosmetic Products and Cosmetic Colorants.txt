Article 1
These Standards are adopted pursuant to the provisions of Paragraph 3 of Article 7, Paragraph 2 of Article 8, Paragraph 3 of Article 16, Paragraph 2 of Article 17, and Article 33-1 of the Statute for Control of Cosmetic Hygiene and Article 10 of the Charges and Fees Act. Article 2
The standards for charging fees to register cosmetic products and cosmetic colorants are as follows:I. The review fee charged for registering new cosmetics containing medicine and new cosmetic colorants shall be NT6,000 per application.II. The review fee charged for registering cosmetics containing medicine and cosmetic colorants shall be NT2,000 per application.III. The review fee to register changes of cosmetics containing medicine and cosmetic colorants:(I) For change of product name, packaging, usage, business applicant, or manufacturer, NT,500 shall be charged per application.(II) For change of ingredient or item, NT,000 shall be charged per application.IV. The review fee charged for registering license extension of cosmetics containing medicine and cosmetic colorants shall be NT,500 per application.V. The review fee charged for registering package separation or package alteration of imported cosmetics shall be NT,000 per application.VI. For reissuing lost licenses, approved documents of label or package insert for cosmetics containing medicine and cosmetic colorants, NT,500 shall be charged per application.VII. The review fee charged for regulatory determination of the property of cosmetic products shall be NT,500 per application.VIII. The fee charged for a license or certificate shall be NT,500 each. Article 3
These Standards shall be implemented from 1 July 2015.
