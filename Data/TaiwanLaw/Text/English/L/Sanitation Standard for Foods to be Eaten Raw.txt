Article 1
This Standard is prescribed in accordance with the provisions of Article 17 of the Act Governing Food Sanitation. Article 2
Foods to be eaten raw means such foods to be served after preparing treatments like washing and peeling. Article 3
Products shall have their original pleasant flavor or color. No putridity, discoloration, off odor and flavor, contamination, moulds, foreign matter or parasite shall be found. Article 4
