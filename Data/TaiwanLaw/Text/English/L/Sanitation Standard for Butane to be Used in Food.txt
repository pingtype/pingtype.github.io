Article 1
This Standard is prescribed in accordance with the provisions of Article 17 of the Act Governing Food Sanitation. Article 2
Butane referred in this standard includes both n-butane (CAS Reg. No. 106-97-8) and isobutane (CAS Reg. No. 75-28-5).The formula of the chemical is indicated as C4H10, and its molecular weight is 58.12. Article 3
