Article 1
The Standards are prescribed in accordance with the provisions of Article 17 of the Act Governing Food Sanitation. Article 2
This Standard applies to the edible algae foods, including blue algae, green algae, brown algae and red algae. Article 3
The requirements of microbiological limits in algae foods vary with different products, i.e. fresh or processed ones.The different products are subject to the “Sanitation Standard for General Foods”, “Sanitation Standard for Foods to be Eaten Raw” or “Sanitation Standard for Ready-to-Eat (RTE) Foods”. Article 4
