Article 1
The Regulations are pursuant to Paragraph 1, Article 39 of the Statute for Prevention and Control of Infectious Animal Disease and Article 21-1 of the Plant Protection and Quarantine Act. Article 2
Animals, plants and their products (regulated articles, hereinafter) carried by passenger or crew of vehicles, vessels and aircrafts (inbound/outbound personnel, hereinafter) or sent by mail, within the specified variety and quantity of the Annex, are eligible for the Regulations.Regulated articles not in compliance with the preceding Paragraph are subject to general import/export quarantine inspection process.
