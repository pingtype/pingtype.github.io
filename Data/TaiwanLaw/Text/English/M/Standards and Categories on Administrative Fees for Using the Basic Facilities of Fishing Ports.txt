Article 1
The standards have are made pursuant to Paragraph 2 of Article 12 of the Fishing Port Act. Article 2
The categories and rates of administrative fees for the use of the fishing ports general facilities (thereinafter referred to as “administrative fees”) are as follows: 1.Recreational vessels: daily collection in a range of NT0 to NT20 on one tonnage (vessel’s gross weight) basis.2.Government vessels: free of charge3.Transport boats, workboats or other vessels: daily collection in a range of NT to NT4 on one tonnage basis. Nevertheless, daily collection for workboats providing service for fishing vessels entering and leaving ports may be halved.4.Operators of fishing ports for specific uses such as refueling, water supplying, ice supplying, repairs and maintenance, etc.: monthly collection in a range of NT00 to NT000 on one meter (vessel length) basis.5.Operators of fishing ports designated for angling: monthly collection in a range of NT00 to NT00 on one meter basis.The actual rates of the fees to be collected shall be decided upon by competent authority in charge of each fishing port within the abovementioned range. The fees collected for Subparagraphs 1 and 3 of Paragraph 1 shall be calculated according to the vessel’s gross tonnage. In case of vessels berthing less than one day, the fees should be collected as for one day. Article 3
Fishing port administrative fees shall be collected by the competent authority in charge or by the commissioned agency. The handling charges paid to the commissioned agency shall be equivalent to 5% of the total amount of fees collected, which the competent authority in charge shall include the amount allotted for this purpose in the budget. Article 4
This Act shall become effective as of the date of its promulgation.
