Article 1
These standards are promulgated in accordance with Article 50-1 of the Radio and Television Act. Article 2
The approval fee of these standards shall be calculated in New Taiwan Dollars. The fees are as follows:I. Application fee for the establishment of an FM radio station (B) or sub-units: NTD150,000 per application.II. Application fee for the establishment of an FM radio station (A): NTD50,000 per application. Article 3
According to the notice from the competent authority, there shall be no additional charges for applications submitted within the deadline. Article 4
Once paid, the approval fees, including those that have been mistakenly paid or over paid, shall not be refunded. Article 5
These standards shall become effective from the day of their promulgation
