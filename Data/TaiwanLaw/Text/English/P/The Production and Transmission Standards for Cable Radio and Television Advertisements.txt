Article 1
These standards are promulgated according to Paragraph 2, Article 50 of the Cable Radio and Television Act. Article 2
The cable radio and television advertisements in these standards shall refer to the images, sounds and contents transmitted by the system operators to promote commodities, concepts, services, or images. Article 3
The contents of the advertisements shall not1. Violate compulsory or prohibitive regulations under the law;2. Impair the physical or mental health of children or juveniles; or3.
