Article 1
These Standards are enacted in accordance with Subparagraph 2, Paragraph 1, Article 10 of the Charges and Fees Act. Article 2
Users of Each Coast National Scenic Area campgrounds (Xiaoyeliu. Ruishui, Shitiping) must observe campground regulations. For both wooden tent platforms and wooden rooftop tent spaces, rental rates are NT,000 per night on holidays/weekends and NT00 per night on weekdays. Article 3
The fees stipulated in these Standards will be reviewed every three years, with consideration given to such factors as changes in costs and in the consumer price index. Article 4
These Standards shall be enforced from the date of promulgation.
