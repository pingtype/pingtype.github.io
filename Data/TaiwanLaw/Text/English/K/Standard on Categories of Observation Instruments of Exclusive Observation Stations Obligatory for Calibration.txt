Article 1
This Standard is enacted in accordance with the terms in the fifth paragraph of Article 20, Meteorological Act. Article 2
The categories of observation instruments of exclusive observation stations obligatory for calibration are as follows:1.air pressure;2.temperature;3.humidity;4.precipitation;5.wind direction;6.wind speed;7.sunshine duration; and 8.solar radiation. Article 3
This Standard shall become effective on the date of promulgation.
