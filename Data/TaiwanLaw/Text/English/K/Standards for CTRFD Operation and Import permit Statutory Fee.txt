Article 1
These standards are promulgated pursuant to Article 70 of the Telecommunications Act. Article 2
The controlled telecommunications radio-frequency devices（hereinafter as CTRFD） statutory fees include CTRFD operation certificate (hereinafter as operation certificate),import permit and examinations. Article 3
The National Communications Commission (hereinafter as “NCC”) shall charge the fee as attached table “CTRFD Operation and Import Permit Statutory Fee”（hereinafter as Statutory Fee Table）with respect to every application for operation certificate. Those who shall apply for reissue or replacement operation certificate to the DGT.DGT shall charge the operation certificate fee as Statutory Fee Table.The aforementioned fee for CTRFD operation certificate has already included examination.
