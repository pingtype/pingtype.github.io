Article 1
The present Regulations are prescribed pursuant to Article 22 of The Law of Ships. Article 2
The owner of a Republic of China (ROC) -flag ship shall apply to the shipping administration authority for registration of the ship, and issuance of the Certificate of Vessel's Nationality.The Certificate of Vessel's Nationality shall be produced in the form as specified in Appendix 1.
