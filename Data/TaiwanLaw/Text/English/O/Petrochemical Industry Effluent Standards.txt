Article 1
These Standards are determined pursuant to Article 7, Paragraph 2 of the Water Pollution Control Act. Article 2
Terms used in these Standards are defined as follows:I. "High-nitrogen processes" means the following nitrogen-including processes, where the volume of process wastewater exceeds 40% of the permit-approved effluent volume at the discharge point: A. Nitrogen trifluoride and electronic-grade liquid ammonia manufacturing process.B. Methyl methacrylate (MMA) chemical manufacturing process.C. Acrylonitrile manufacturing process.D. Acrylonitrile-butadiene copolymer (AB) chemical manufacturing process.E. Acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene copolymer (ABS) chemical manufacturing process.F. Acrylonitrile styrene copolymer (AS) chemical manufacturing process.G. Caprolactam manufacturing process.H. Ammonium sulfate chemical manufacturing process.I. Polyamide plastic (nylon) manufacturing process.II. "Newly-established enterprises" means those enterprises for which planning had not been completed as of December 1, 2011, or for which planning had been completed, but project bid request procedures had not yet been completed as of that time.III. "Existing enterprises" means those enterprises that had been completed, were in construction, or for which project bid request procedures had been completed as of December 1, 2011. Article 3
These Standards are applicable to petrochemical enterprises announced as regulated in the enterprise classification and definitions of the Water Pollution Control Act. Article 4
