Article 1
These standards are determined pursuant to Article 11, Paragraph 1 of the Noise Control Act Article 2
Testing of civil aircraft noise levels shall be performed in accordance with effective perceived noise (EPN), A-weighting maximum noise level, or sound exposure level (SEL) regulations in ISO-3891. The units employed are EPN dB, LAmax dB, and SEL dB, respectively. Article 3
