Article 1
The Standards enacted herein are pursuant to Article 27, Paragraph 2 of The Tobacco and Alcohol Administration Act. Article 2
The Standards is applicable to entire alcohol products containers, including the capping of covers, pads and stoppers. Article 3
The plastic alcohol products containers shall not be recycled for reuse. Article 4
The alcohol products containers shall not have any of the following circumstances; unacceptable discoloration, unusual odor and flavor, contamination, mould, contain foreign matter or fibre fall off. Article 5
