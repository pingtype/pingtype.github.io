Article 1
These standards are duly enacted in accordance with Article 41 of the Act Governing the Administration of Professional Engineering Consulting Firms (hereinafter referred to as the Act) and Article 10, Paragraph 1 of the Charges and Fees Act. Article 2
To apply for professional engineering consulting firm permission in accordance with Article 9 of the Act shall pay the review fee of NT00; the same amount shall be charged in the case of applying for altering the permission in accordance with Article 15, Paragraph 1 of the Act.When an application for permission or altering the permission is not accepted by the competent authority or the applicant has voluntarily withdrawn before being reviewed, the paid fee shall be refunded. Article 3
To apply for professional engineering consulting firm registration certificate, firms shall pay the certificate fee in accordance with the following provisions:1. For issuing registration certificate under the provisions of Article 10 of the Act：NT,000.2. For renewing registration certificate under the provisions of Article 15, Paragraph 2 of the Act：NT,000.3. For reissuing or renewing registration certificate under the provisions of Article 9 of Enforcement Rules of the Act：NT,000.When an application of issuing, renewing or reissuing registration certificate is rejected, withdrawn or not accepted, the paid fee shall be refunded. Article 4
These standards shall take effect from the date of promulgation.
