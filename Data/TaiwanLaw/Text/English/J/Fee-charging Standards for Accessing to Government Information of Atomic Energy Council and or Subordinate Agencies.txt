Article 1
This Standard is enacted according to paragraph 2 of Article 22 of the Freedom of Government Information Law (hereinafter referred to as the "Law") and Article 10 of Charges and Fees Low. Article 2
The fee for browsing, transcribing, or photographing government information is NT0 for every two hours. Time spent less than two hours is counted as two hours. Article 3
The fee for duplicating or copying government information shall be charged in accordance with the Duplicating or Copying the Government Information Fee Standard Table as set forth in the attachment.The items undefined in the above Fee Standard Table shall be charged in accordance with the actual costs of duplication or copying.
