Article 1
These standards are enacted in accordance with Article 24 of the Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Industries Governance Act. Article 2
A refrigeration and air conditioning enterprise shall pay a review fee of NT00 to request the central competent authority to process any of the following applications:1. Application for a permit.2. Application for issuance, alteration or replacement of a refrigeration and air conditioning enterprise registration certificate. Article 3
A refrigeration and air conditioning enterprise shall pay a certificate fee of NT,500 to the central competent authority for its application for issuance, alteration, replacement or reissuance of a refrigeration and air conditioning enterprise registration certificate. Article 4
These standards shall come into force from the date of promulgation.
