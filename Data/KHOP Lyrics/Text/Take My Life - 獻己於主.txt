
Take  My  Life  -    獻  己  於  主    
Take  My  Life  -    xiàn   jǐ   yú   zhǔ     
Take  My  Life  -    offer   self   in   master     


Frances Havergal  
交  我  一生  為  祢  用  願  為主  分別  自己    
jiāo   wǒ   yī shēng   wèi   ní   yòng   yuàn   wéi zhǔ   fēn bié   zì jǐ     
mix   I   lifetime   do   you   use   desire   Mainly   respectively   self     
Take my life and let it be consecrated, Lord, to Thee

交  時間  給  祢  治理  變為  讚美  永不  息    
jiāo   shí jiān   gěi   ní   zhì lǐ   biàn wèi   zàn měi   yǒng bù   xī     
mix   time   give   you   Governance   Change to   praise   Never   rest     
Take my moments and my days let them flow in ceaseless praise

我  交  雙手  主  使用  因被  祢  大  愛  感動    
wǒ   jiāo   shuāng shǒu   zhǔ   shǐ yòng   yīn bèi   ní   dà   ài   gǎn dòng     
I   mix   Hands   master   use   Due to being   you   big   love   Moving     
Take my hands and let them move at the impulse of Thy love

願  我  腳  為  祢  所用  快  步  腳  行蹤  佳美    
yuàn   wǒ   jiǎo   wèi   ní   suǒ yòng   kuài   bù   jiǎo   xíng zōng   jiā měi     
desire   I   foot   do   you   Used   rapid   step   foot   whereabouts   Good beauty     
Take my feet and let them be swift and beautiful for Thee


使我  口中  常  歌頌  讚美  榮耀  我  君王    
shǐ wǒ   kǒu zhōng   cháng   gē sòng   zàn měi   róng yào   wǒ   jūn wáng     
Make me   Mouth   common   Praise   praise   glory   I   king     
Take my voice and let me sing always, only for my King

使我  唇  間  滿  主  言  各處  宣揚  祢  福音    
shǐ wǒ   chún   jiān   mǎn   zhǔ   yán   gè chù   xuān yáng   ní   fú yīn     
Make me   lips   interval   fill   master   words   throughout   To promote   you   Gospel     
Take my lips and let them be filled with messages from Thee

願  恭敬  獻  我  金銀  不  存  錢財  為  己  用    
yuàn   gōng jìng   xiàn   wǒ   jīn yín   bù   cún   qián cái   wèi   jǐ   yòng     
desire   Respectful   offer   I   Gold and silver   no   exist   money   do   self   use     
Take my silver and my gold not a mite would I withhold

將我  智慧  及  才能  任憑  主意  為  祢  用    
jiāng wǒ   zhì huì   jí   cái néng   rèn píng   zhǔ yì   wèi   ní   yòng     
Will me   wisdom   extend   Ability to   despite   idea   do   you   use     
Take my intellect and use every power as You choose

quán   xiàn shàng   jiāo gěi   ní     
whole   Offer   To give to   you     
Take my life it’s all for Thee


願  將  己  意  交給  祢  不再  恣意  只為  己    
yuàn   jiāng   jǐ   yì   jiāo gěi   ní   bù zài   zì yì   zhǐ wèi   jǐ     
desire   will   self   thought   To give to   you   no longer   Wantonly   Just for   self     
Take my will and make it Thine it shall be no longer mine

將我  心  全  交給  祢  全  心願  為主  寶座    
jiāng wǒ   xīn   quán   jiāo gěi   ní   quán   xīn yuàn   wéi zhǔ   bǎo zuò     
Will me   heart   whole   To give to   you   whole   wish   Mainly   throne     
Take my heart it is Thine own it shall be thy royal throne

使我  愛  如火  燒  焚獻  主  腳  前  永  留存    
shǐ wǒ   ài   rú huǒ   shāo   fén xiàn   zhǔ   jiǎo   qián   yǒng   liú cún     
Make me   love   Such as fire   burn   Burned   master   foot   forward   long   Retained     
Take my love, my Lord, I pour at Your feet it’s treasure store

將我  自己  獻給  祢  一生一世  來  愛  祢    
jiāng wǒ   zì jǐ   xiàn gěi   ní   yī shēng yī shì   lái   ài   ní     
Will me   self   Dedicated to   you   For the rest of my life   come   love   you     
Take myself and I will be ever only all for Thee
