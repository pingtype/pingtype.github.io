
Worth  It  All  -    唯有  祢  配得      
Worth  It  All  -    wéi yǒu   ní   pèi de       
Worth  It  All  -    Only   you   worthy       


Ben Cantelon
獻  我  所有  在  祢  面前  更深  地  尋求    
xiàn   wǒ   suǒ yǒu   zài   ní   miàn qián   gēng shēn   dì   xún qiú     
offer   I   all   in   you   before   Deeper   earth   seek     
All I am, Lord here before You reaching out for more 

祢  的  應許  永不  改變  祢  是  我  獎賞    
ní   de   yīng xǔ   yǒng bù   gǎi biàn   ní   shì   wǒ   jiǎng shǎng     
you   of   Promise   Never   change   you   is   I   Reward     
You're the promise never failing You are my reward


我  願  放棄  全  所有  只為  要  得著  祢    
wǒ   yuàn   fàng qì   quán   suǒ yǒu   zhǐ wèi   yào   de zháo   ní     
I   desire   give up   whole   all   Just for   need   Get it   you     
I let go of all I have just to have all of You

無論  付  多大  代價  我  要  跟隨  祢    
wú lùn   fù   duō dà   dài jià   wǒ   yào   gēn suí   ní     
regardless   give   How big   cost   I   need   follow   you     
And no matter what the cost I will follow You

為  祢  失去  的  一切  在  祢  都  尋  回    
wèi   ní   shī qù   de   yī qiè   zài   ní   dōu   xún   huí     
do   you   lose   of   all   in   you   all   seek   return     
Jesus, everything I've lost I have found in You

當  我  到達  了  終點  我  說  唯有  祢  配得    
dāng   wǒ   dào dá   le   zhōng diǎn   wǒ   shuō   wéi yǒu   ní   pèi de     
just   I   Arrivals   is   end   I   speak   Only   you   worthy     
When I finally reach the end, I'll say You are worth it all


世上  沒有  任何  珍寶  能夠  滿足  我    
shì shàng   méi yǒu   rèn hé   zhēn bǎo   néng gòu   mǎn zú   wǒ     
The world   No   any   treasure   were able   Satisfy   I     
There's no riches Earthly treasure that will satisfy

所有  渴望  只為  耶穌  點燃  我  的  心    
suǒ yǒu   kě wàng   zhǐ wèi   yē sū   diǎn rán   wǒ   de   xīn     
all   desire   Just for   Jesus   ignite   I   of   heart     
Every longing for You, Jesus Set this heart on fire


當  我  進入  祢  榮耀  之中  萬有  都  屈膝  祢  面前    
dāng   wǒ   jìn rù   ní   róng yào   zhī zhōng   wàn yǒu   dōu   qū xī   ní   miàn qián     
just   I   enter   you   glory   Among them   Everything   all   Knees   you   before     
When I'm there in Your glorious presence Every knee is bowed before You

聽見  天上  高聲  歡唱  唯有  祢  配得    
tīng jiàn   tiān shàng   gāo shēng   huan chàng   wéi yǒu   ní   pèi de     
hear   Heaven   loudly   Singing   Only   you   worthy     
Hear the sound of heaven singing You are worth it all

天使  呼喊  聖哉  聖哉  眾聖  徒  唱  配得  配得    
tiān shǐ   hū hǎn   shèng zāi   shèng zāi   zhòng shèng   tú   chàng   pèi de   pèi de     
angel   Shouting   Holy   Holy   Public sanctuary   disciple   sing   worthy   worthy     
All the saints cry Holy, Holy Angels singing worthy, worthy

直到  永遠  我  要  頌揚  唯有  祢  配得    
zhí dào   yǒng yuǎn   wǒ   yào   sòng yáng   wéi yǒu   ní   pèi de     
until   forever and always   I   need   Praise   Only   you   worthy     
Forever I will shout Your praises You are worth it all
