
New  Doxology  -    新  三  一  頌    
New  Doxology  -    xīn   sān   yī   sòng     
New  Doxology  -    new   three   one   laud     


Gateway Worship
讚美  我  神  萬福  之  源    
zàn měi   wǒ   shén   wàn fú   zhī   yuán     
praise   I   god   Wanfu   it   spring     
Praise God from Whom all blessings flow

天下  萬物  都  當  歌頌    
tiān xià   wàn wù   dōu   dāng   gē sòng     
The world   All things   all   just   Praise     
Praise Him, all creatures here below

天上  萬軍  也  讚  主  名    
tiān shàng   wàn jūn   yě   zàn   zhǔ   míng     
Heaven   Million army   also   praise   master   name     
Praise Him above, ye heavenly hosts

同心  讚美  父子  聖靈    
tóng xīn   zàn měi   fù zǐ   shèng líng     
concentric   praise   Father and son   The Holy Spirit     
Praise Father, Son and Holy Ghost


讚美  讚美  讚美  救主  我  神    
zàn měi   zàn měi   zàn měi   jiù zhǔ   wǒ   shén     
praise   praise   praise   Savior   I   god     
Praise God, praise God, praise God, Who saved my soul

讚美  讚美  讚美  歸於  永福  泉源    
zàn měi   zàn měi   zàn měi   guī wū   yǒng fú   quán yuán     
praise   praise   praise   Attribution   Yongfu   source     
Praise God, praise God, praise God from Whom all blessings flow


天上  地上  都  當  宣告    
tiān shàng   dì shàng   dōu   dāng   xuān gào     
Heaven   On the ground   all   just   Declare     
Let earth and heavenly saints proclaim

我  主  榮耀  已經  來到    
wǒ   zhǔ   róng yào   yǐ jīng   lái dào     
I   master   glory   already   come     
The power and might of His great Name

萬  膝  跪拜  尊崇  主  名    
wàn   xī   guì bài   zūn chóng   zhǔ   míng     
innumerable   knee   bow down   Respected   master   name     
Let us exalt on bended knee

讚美  歸於  父子  聖靈    
zàn měi   guī wū   fù zǐ   shèng líng     
praise   Attribution   Father and son   The Holy Spirit     
Praise God, the Holy Trinity


我  王  寶座  超乎  一切    
wǒ   wáng   bǎo zuò   chāo hu   yī qiè     
I   king   throne   Beyond   all     
Praise to the King, His throne transcends

祂  的  國度  永遠  掌權    
tā   de   guó dù   yǒng yuǎn   zhǎng quán     
He   of   country   forever and always   In power     
His crown and Kingdom never end

被殺  羔羊  配得  讚美    
bèi shā   gāo yáng   pèi de   zàn měi     
Killed   The lamb   worthy   praise     
Now and throughout eternity

直到  永恆  都  當  敬畏    
zhí dào   yǒng héng   dōu   dāng   jìng wèi     
until   eternal   all   just   Awe     
I'll praise the One Who died for me
