ní   shì   wǒ   yǒng yuǎn   de   jiù ēn   pán shí     
you   is   I   forever and always   of   salvation   Rocks     
You are the Rock of my salvation

ní   shì   wǒ   shēng mìng   lì liàng

You are the strength of my life

make   I   trouble   central   at any time   help
