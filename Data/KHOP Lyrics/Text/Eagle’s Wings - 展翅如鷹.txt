我  在  此  等候  求  祢  充滿  我    
wǒ   zài   cǐ   děng hòu   qiú   ní   chōng mǎn   wǒ     
I   in   this   waiting   seek   you   full   I     
Here I am waiting, abide in me I pray

我  在  此  渴慕  渴慕  祢    
wǒ   zài   cǐ   kě mù   kě mù   ní     
I   in   this   Thirsty   Thirsty   you     
Here I am longing for you

主  使我  屈膝  藏  在  祢  愛  裡    
zhǔ   shǐ wǒ   qū xī   cáng   zài   ní   ài   lǐ     
master   Make me   Knees   hide   in   you   love   inside     
Hide me in your love, bring me to my knees

Jesus   desire   More   understanding   you     
May I know Jesus more and more

To charge   fill   I   I   whole   man   you   In charge     
Come live in me , all my life take over

xiàng   wǒ   chuī qì   shǐ wǒ   xīng qǐ     
toward   I   Inflated   Make me   Rise     
Come breathe in me , and I will rise
