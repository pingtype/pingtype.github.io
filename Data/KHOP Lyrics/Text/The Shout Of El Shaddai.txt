
The Shout Of El Shaddai

Ed Kerr and Paul Wilbur 
你  聽見  嗎  ？  大  響聲  在  遠方    
nǐ   tīng jiàn   ma   ？  dà   xiǎng shēng   zài   yuǎn fāng     
you   hear   ?   ？  big   sound   in   distance     
Can you hear it? Thunder in the distance

當  我們  敬拜  猶大  獅子  發聲    
dāng   wǒ men   jìng bài   yóu dà   shī zi   fā shēng     
just   us   Worship   Judah   lion   Sound     
When we worship, the lion of Judah roars

堅固  營壘  四圍  環繞    
jiān gù   yíng lěi   sì wéi   huán rào     
Sturdy   camp   Surrounded by   surround     
Strongholds crumble all a round us

在  主  同在  中都  倒塌    
zài   zhǔ   tóng zài   zhōng dōu   dǎo tā     
in   master   together   In the middle   collapse     
In the presence of our Lord


勝利  在  我們  當中  齊  來呼  喊    El  Shaddai  
shèng lì   zài   wǒ men   dāng zhōng   qí   lái hū   hǎn     El  Shaddai  
victory   in   us   among   even   To call   shout     El  Shaddai  
There’ll be victory in the camp at the shout of El Shaddai

眾  仇敵  必定  逃跑  躲避  祂  目  中  火光    
zhòng   chóu dí   bì dìng   táo pǎo   duǒ bì   tā   mù   zhōng   huǒ guāng     
masses   Enemies   Must be   Escape   To avoid   He   eye   central   Fire     
Every enemy will flee from the fire in His eyes

主恩  惠  禧  年來  到  被擄  者  都  得著  釋放    
zhǔ ēn   huì   xǐ   nián lái   dào   bèi lǔ   zhě   dōu   de zháo   shì fàng     
Lord grace   favor   happiness   Years   arrive   Captive   he who   all   Get it   freed     
Every captive will be freed in this year of jubilee

當  我們  齊  呼喊    El  Shaddai  
dāng   wǒ men   qí   hū hǎn     El  Shaddai  
just   us   even   Shouting     El  Shaddai  
When we hear the shout of El Shaddai
