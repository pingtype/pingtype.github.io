
The  Stand  -    我  站立    
The  Stand  -    wǒ   zhàn lì     
The  Stand  -    I   Stand up     


Hillsong 
世界  在  祢  寶座  前  我  主  祢  永遠  掌權    
shì jiè   zài   ní   bǎo zuò   qián   wǒ   zhǔ   ní   yǒng yuǎn   zhǎng quán     
world   in   you   throne   forward   I   master   you   forever and always   In power     
You stood before creation eternity in Your hand

是  祢  創造  天地萬物  我  靈  敬畏  祢    
shì   ní   chuàng zào   tiān dì wàn wù   wǒ   líng   jìng wèi   ní     
is   you   create   Heaven and earth all things   I   spirit   Awe   you     
You spoke the earth into motion My soul now to stand

祢  接納  我  的  過錯  背起  羞愧  的  十字架    
ní   jiē nà   wǒ   de   guò cuò   bèi qǐ   xiū kuì   de   shí zì jià     
you   Accepted   I   of   fault   Stand up   ashamed   of   cross     
You stood before my failure and carried the cross for my shame

我  的  罪  在  祢  肩膀  上  我  靈  敬畏  祢    
wǒ   de   zuì   zài   ní   jiān bǎng   shàng   wǒ   líng   jìng wèi   ní     
I   of   crime   in   you   shoulder   top   I   spirit   Awe   you     
My sin weighed upon Your shoulders My soul now to stand


走在  祢  救恩  道路  永  活  聖靈  居住  我  心    
zǒu zài   ní   jiù ēn   dào lù   yǒng   huó   shèng líng   jū zhù   wǒ   xīn     
Walking in   you   salvation   the way   long   live   The Holy Spirit   live   I   heart     
So I'll walk upon salvation Your Spirit alive in me

生命  要  彰顯  祢  應許  我  靈  敬畏  祢    
shēng mìng   yào   zhāng xiǎn   ní   yīng xǔ   wǒ   líng   jìng wèi   ní     
life   need   Highlight   you   Promise   I   spirit   Awe   you     
This life to declare Your promise My soul now to stand

該說  些  什麼  該做  些  什麼    
gāi shuō   xiē   shén me   gāi zuò   xiē   shén me     
That said   little   what   Do so   little   what     
So what can I say and what could I do

只能  將我  心  喔  主  全  獻上  給  祢    
zhǐ néng   jiāng wǒ   xīn   ō   zhǔ   quán   xiàn shàng   gěi   ní     
Can only   Will me   heart   cry   master   whole   Offer   give   you     
But offer this heart, O God completely to You


我  站立  舉手  來  全  心  敬拜    
wǒ   zhàn lì   jǔ shǒu   lái   quán   xīn   jìng bài     
I   Stand up   Raise your hand   come   whole   heart   Worship     
So I'll stand with arms high and heart abandoned

敬畏  那位  捨  生命  的  主    
jìng wèi   nà wèi   shě   shēng mìng   de   zhǔ     
Awe   Who   discard   life   of   master     
In awe of the One who gave it all

喔  主  我  的  一切都  屬於  祢    
ō   zhǔ   wǒ   de   yī qiè dōu   shǔ wū   ní     
cry   master   I   of   everything is   belong   you     
I'll stand My soul, Lord to You surrendered

我  完全  降服    
wǒ   wán quán   xiáng fú     
I   complete   surrender     
All I am is Yours
