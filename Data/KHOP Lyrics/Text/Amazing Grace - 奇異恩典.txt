
Amazing  Grace  奇異  恩典    
Amazing  Grace  qí yì   ēn diǎn     
Amazing  Grace  Strange   grace     


Chris Tomlin
奇異  恩典  何等  甘甜  我  罪  巳  得  赦免    
qí yì   ēn diǎn   hé děng   gān tián   wǒ   zuì   sì   dé   shè miǎn     
Strange   grace   What is it?   Sweet   I   crime   morning   obtain   pardon     
Amazing grace! How sweet the sound that saved a wretch like me

如此  恩典  使我  敬畏  使我  心得  安慰    
rú cǐ   ēn diǎn   shǐ wǒ   jìng wèi   shǐ wǒ   xīn dé   ān wèi     
in this way   grace   Make me   Awe   Make me   Experience   comfort     
'Twas grace that taught my heart to fear and grace my fears relieved

恩  主  應許  愛我  真切  使我  今  得  盼望    
ēn   zhǔ   yīng xǔ   ài wǒ   zhēn qiè   shǐ wǒ   jīn   dé   pàn wàng     
kindness   master   Promise   Love me   True   Make me   now   obtain   Hope     
The Lord has promised good to me, His word my hope secures

世界  即將  化為烏有  日頭  不再  閃耀    
shì jiè   jí jiāng   huà wéi wū yǒu   rì tou   bù zài   shǎn yào     
world   Coming soon   Naught   Sun   no longer   shine     
The earth shall soon dissolve like snow The sun forbear to shine

將來  禧  年  聖徒  歡聚  恩  光  愛  誼  千年    
jiāng lái   xǐ   nián   shèng tú   huān jù   ēn   guāng   ài   yì   qiān nián     
future   happiness   year   saint   Gather together   kindness   light   love   friendship   millennium     
When we've been there ten thousand years bright shining as the sun


前  我  失喪  今  被  尋  回  瞎眼  今  得  看見    
qián   wǒ   shī sàng   jīn   bèi   xún   huí   xiā yǎn   jīn   dé   kàn jiàn     
forward   I   Lost   now   by   seek   return   Blind   now   obtain   see     
I once was lost, but now am found was blind, but now I see

初  信之  時  即  蒙恩  惠  真是  何等  寶貴    
chū   xìn zhī   shí   jí   méng ēn   huì   zhēn shi   hé děng   bǎo guì     
beginning   Letter of the letter   time   promptly   Grace   favor   Really   What is it?   valuable     
How precious did that grace appear the hour I first believed

是  我  盾牌  是  我  產業  是  我  生命  保障    
shì   wǒ   dùn pái   shì   wǒ   chǎn yè   shì   wǒ   shēng mìng   bǎo zhàng     
is   I   Shield   is   I   industry   is   I   life   Guarantee     
He will my shield and portion be as long as life endures

但  那  呼  召我  來  的  神  將與  我  永  同在    
dàn   nà   hū   zhào wǒ   lái   de   shén   jiāng yǔ   wǒ   yǒng   tóng zài     
only   that   exhale   Call me   come   of   god   Will be with   I   long   together     
But God who called me here below will be forever mine

喜樂  頌讚  在  父  座  前  深  望  那日  快  現    
xǐ lè   sòng zàn   zài   fù   zuò   qián   shēn   wàng   nà rì   kuài   xiàn     
Joyful   Praise   in   father   seat   forward   deep   expect   That day   rapid   appear     
we've no less days to sing God's praise than when we first begun


枷鎖  脫落  我  得  自由    
jiā suǒ   tuō luò   wǒ   dé   zì yóu     
shackles   Fall off   I   obtain   free     
My chains are gone I've been set free

我  神  我  救主  已  救贖  我    
wǒ   shén   wǒ   jiù zhǔ   yǐ   jiù shú   wǒ     
I   god   I   Savior   already   Salvation   I     
My God, my Savior has ransomed me

祂  的  憐憫  如同  江河    
tā   de   lián mǐn   rú tóng   jiāng hé     
He   of   mercy   as   Rivers     
And like a flood, His mercy reigns

無盡  的  愛  奇妙  恩典    
wú jìn   de   ài   qí miào   ēn diǎn     
Endless   of   love   wonderful   grace     
Unending love amazing grace
