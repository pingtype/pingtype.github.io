
Worthy  Of  It  All  -    祢  配得  所有      
Worthy  Of  It  All  -    ní   pèi de   suǒ yǒu       
Worthy  Of  It  All  -    you   worthy   all       


David Brymer
眾聖  徒和  天使  俯伏  祢  座  前    
zhòng shèng   tú hé   tiān shǐ   fǔ fú   ní   zuò   qián     
Public sanctuary   And only   angel   Fall down   you   seat   forward     
All the saints and angels bow before Your throne

眾  長老  放下  冠冕  在  神  羔羊  座  前  敬拜    
zhòng   zhǎng lǎo   fàng xià   guān miǎn   zài   shén   gāo yáng   zuò   qián   jìng bài     
masses   Elders   lay down   Crowned   in   god   The lamb   seat   forward   Worship     
All the elders cast their crowns before the Lamb of God and sing

master   you   worthy   all   master   you   worthy   all     
You're worthy of it all You're worthy of it all

萬有  從  祢  而來  萬有  也  歸向  祢    
wàn yǒu   cóng   ní   ér lái   wàn yǒu   yě   guī xiàng   ní     
Everything   from   you   Come from   Everything   also   Return to   you     
For from You are all things and to You are all things

祢  真  配得  榮耀    
ní   zhēn   pèi de   róng yào     
you   real   worthy   glory     
You deserve the glory
