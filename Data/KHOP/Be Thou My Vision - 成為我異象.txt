Be Thou my Vision 成為我異象Be Thou my Vision, O Lord of my heart求我⼼中王成為我異象Naught be all else to me, save that Thou art我別無愛慕唯主我景仰Thou my best Thought by day or by night充滿我思想我⼼響往Waking or sleeping, Thy presence my light睡著或睡醒慈容是我光Be Thou my Wisdom and Thou my true Word成為我智慧成為我箴⾔I ever with Thee and Thou with me, Lord我願常跟隨祢在我⾝邊Thou my great Father, I, Thy true son祢是我聖⽗我是祢⼦Thou in me dwelling and I with Thee one祢常居我⼼我與祢合⼀Be Thou my battle Shield, Sword for the fightBe Thou my Dignity Thou, my DelightThou, my soul’s Shelter Thou my high Tow’rRaise Thou me heav’nward O Pow’r of my pow’rRiches I heed not nor man’s empty praise我不求虛名也不求富有Thou mine Inheritance now and always祢是我基業從今到永久Thou and Thou only first in my heart惟祢在我⼼永遠居⾸O Sovereign of Heaven, my Treasure Thou art天上⼤君王祢是我萬有High King of Heaven, my victory won天上⼤君王光明的太陽May I reach Heaven’s joys, O bright Heav’n’s Sun容我享天樂我已打勝仗Heart of my own heart, whate’er befall我⼼屬祢⼼永無變更Still be my Vision, O Ruler of all萬有的主宰成為我異象
