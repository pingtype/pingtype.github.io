This map isn't too interesting even for hot springs fans. The data is static, decades old, already available elsewhere, and not even 100% accurate. However, for map hackers the map does show one way of presenting fairly large data sets in the Google Maps API, which gets way too slow past a couple hundred markers. I work around that problem here using two techniques:

  • Only the currently visible markers actually exist. Markers gets created and removed dynamically as you pan and zoom.
  • At wide zoom levels where too many markers would be displayed, the map automatically groups individual markers into cluster markers.
