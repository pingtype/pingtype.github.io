Dinbendon Map in Pingtype

Dinbendon is a group-order tool for tea and lunchboxes in Taiwan.

I downloaded their entire database, translated it from Chinese to English using Pingtype and made this map.

Please be patient while the map loads. It takes about 1 minute to show all 20,952 stores.

If the translations are wrong, please use the edit function of Pingtype to update the dictionary.
