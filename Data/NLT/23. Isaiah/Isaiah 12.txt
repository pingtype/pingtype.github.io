[Songs of Praise for Salvation] 1 In that day you will sing: "I will praise you, O Lord ! You were angry with me, but not any more. Now you comfort me.2 See, God has come to save me. I will trust in him and not be afraid. The Lord   God  is my strength and my song; he has given me victory."3 With joy you will drink deeply from the fountain of salvation!4 In that wonderful day you will sing: "Thank the Lord ! Praise his name! Tell the nations what he has done. Let them know how mighty he is!5 Sing to the Lord , for he has done wonderful things. Make known his praise around the world.6 Let all the people of Jerusalem[a] shout his praise with joy! For great is the Holy One of Israel who lives among you."       Footnotes:a.   12:6 Hebrew Zion.
